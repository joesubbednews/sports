Brackley Town v Boston United

National League North
Saturday, 4 May 2024 @ 3.30pm

Brackley Town host Boston United on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the National League North.

Brackley have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Pilgrims side who have lost their last two matches, and collected nine points from the last possible 18.

Brackley are third in the table after 46 games, of which they have won 25 and drawn 10, earning 85 points.

Boston are three places behind Town in sixth, with 21 wins and 12 draws putting them on 75 points.

In the last 10 years, Brackley and Boston have played each other on 19 occasions. Brackley won 10 of them, Boston six, and they drew three times.

On average, the Saints scored 1.5 goals and the Pilgrims 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 6, when Brackley won 2-1 away.

With 68 goals in 46 games so far this season, United are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

The Saints are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

Brackley's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 win against Chorley.

Boston drew 0-0 with Scunthorpe United last time out, on April 27.
