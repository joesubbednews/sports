Blyth Spartans v Scunthorpe United

National League North | Croft Park
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Tuesday's late match between Blyth Spartans and Scunthorpe United promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of National League North's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Croft Park, Blyth Spartans and the Iron sit joint-third and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 45 and 55 goals respectively.

Striker another leads the way for the home side, having bagged eight goals in their 29 games to date.

And another has been the main man in the opponents' penalty box for Scunthorpe, with 14 goals.

Scunthorpe are second in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn six, earning 54 points.

Blyth Spartans are 11 places behind United in 13th, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in National League North, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the Iron's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Blyth Spartans's eight.

Blyth Spartans's last match was on December 26, a 3-1 win against South Shields.

Scunthorpe beat Farsley Celtic 2-0 last time out, also on December 26.
