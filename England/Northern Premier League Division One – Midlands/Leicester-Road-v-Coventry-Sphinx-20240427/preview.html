Leicester Road v Coventry Sphinx

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Coventry Sphinx face a challenge away against high-flying Leicester Road on Saturday.

Coventry Sphinx are 17th in the Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands table, and have picked up six wins and 13 draws in their 35 games to date.

Leicester Road, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 67 points, having won 19 and drawn 10 of their first 36 matches, and are 16 points behind table-toppers Spalding United.

With 35 goals in 35 games so far this season, Coventry Sphinx are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Leicester Road, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Leicester Road's 11.

Leicester Road's last match was on April 20, a 1-1 draw against Cambridge City.

Coventry Sphinx drew 1-1 with Boldmere St. Michaels last time out, also on April 20.
