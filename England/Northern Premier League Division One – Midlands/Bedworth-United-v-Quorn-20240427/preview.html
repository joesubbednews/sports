Bedworth United v Quorn

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bedworth United face Quorn on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Bedworth are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Quorn team who have won three and lost two over the same number of games.

With 70 goals in 34 games so far this season, Quorn are scoring more than average in the league with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Bedworth, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away team are sixth in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn seven, earning 58 points.

United are four places behind Quorn in 10th, with 14 wins and eight draws putting them on 50 points.

Bedworth's last match was on April 13, a 1-1 draw against Rugby Town.

Quorn beat Lye Town 4-0 last time out, on April 20.
