Coleshill Town v Leicester Road

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leicester Road travel to Coleshill Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Spalding United 2-0 away.

Coleshill also lost their last match, 4-0 against Bedworth United.

With 49 goals in 34 games so far this season, Coleshill are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 75 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Leicester Road, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away team are fifth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn nine, earning 60 points.

Town are nine places behind Leicester Road in 14th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Leicester Road's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Coleshill's seven.

In the last three years, Coleshill and Leicester Road have played each other on three occasions. Coleshill won one of them and Leicester Road the other.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Leicester Road won 3-2 at home.
