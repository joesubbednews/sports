Walsall Wood v AFC Rushden and Diamonds

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling AFC Rushden and Diamonds face Walsall Wood away on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Shepshed Dynamo on April 20, Rushden & Diamonds sit 19th in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

They travel to play a Walsall Wood side ninth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 4-0 against Rugby Town.

With 32 goals in 35 games so far this season, Rushden & Diamonds are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 72 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Walsall Wood are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Walsall Wood's nine.
