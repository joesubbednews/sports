Anstey Nomads v Boldmere St. Michaels

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Boldmere St. Michaels come to play Anstey Nomads on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.
