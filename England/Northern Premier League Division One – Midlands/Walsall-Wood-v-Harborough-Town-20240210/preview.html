Walsall Wood v Harborough Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Harborough Town visit Walsall Wood on Saturday on the back of six consecutive wins in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

It means Harborough have picked up the maximum 18 points from their last six games, and they face a Walsall Wood side who lost their last match, and have collected 11 points from the last possible 18.

With 57 goals in 25 games so far this season, Harborough are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Walsall Wood are also above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are second in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn three, earning 57 points.

The hosts are seven places behind the away team in ninth, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

Walsall Wood's last match was on February 3, a 2-1 loss against Loughborough Dynamo.

Harborough beat Bedworth United 2-0 last time out, also on February 3.
