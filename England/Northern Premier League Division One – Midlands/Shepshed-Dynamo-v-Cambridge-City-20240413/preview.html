Shepshed Dynamo v Cambridge City

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | The Dovecote
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Shepshed Dynamo and Cambridge City will both be looking for points in Saturday's kick-off at the Dovecote to end their losing streaks.

The two teams have both gone three Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands games without a win. Shepshed Dynamo have picked up just three points from their last six games, while Cambridge have earned just four.

Cambridge are 16th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Shepshed Dynamo are one place ahead of City in 15th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

In the last five years, Shepshed Dynamo and Cambridge have played each other on five occasions. Shepshed Dynamo won one of them, Cambridge three, and they drew once.

On average, Shepshed Dynamo scored 1.8 goals and Cambridge 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Cambridge won 4-1 at home.

With 39 goals in 32 games so far this season, the home team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Shepshed Dynamo's last match was on April 6, a 3-1 loss against Rugby Town.

Cambridge lost 4-0 against Boldmere St. Michaels last time out, also on April 6.
