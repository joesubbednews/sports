Bedworth United v Spalding United

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bedworth United face Spalding United on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands games.

Bedworth have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Coventry Sphinx on January 1.

They face a Spalding side who have won four and lost one over that time.

Spalding are top of the table after 26 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn four, earning 58 points.

Bedworth are 12 places behind Spalding United in 13th, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.

With 56 goals in 26 games so far this season, the away side are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Bedworth United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last five years, Bedworth and Spalding have played each other on five occasions. They won one each, and they drew three times.

On average, Bedworth scored 0.8 goals and Spalding 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Spalding won 1-0 at home.

Bedworth's last match was on February 3, a 2-0 loss against Harborough Town.

Spalding beat Gresley 4-0 last time out, also on February 3.
