Shepshed Dynamo v Sporting Khalsa

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | The Dovecote
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sporting Khalsa make the journey to the Dovecote to play Shepshed Dynamo on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Sporting's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

Shepshed Dynamo are 15th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won nine and drawn six, earning 33 points.

Sporting are four places ahead of the home team in 11th, with 15 wins and five draws putting them on 50 points.

With 39 goals in 34 games so far this season, Shepshed Dynamo are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 59 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last five years, Shepshed Dynamo and Sporting have played each other on five occasions. Shepshed Dynamo won three of them and Sporting two.

On average, Shepshed Dynamo scored 1.8 goals and Sporting 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Sporting won 2-0 at home.

Shepshed Dynamo's last match was on April 20, a 1-0 loss against AFC Rushden and Diamonds.

Sporting lost 7-1 against Spalding United last time out, also on April 20.
