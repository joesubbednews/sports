Leicester Road v Sporting Khalsa

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sporting Khalsa travel to Leicester Road on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Bedworth United 3-0 away.

Leicester Road, meanwhile, won their last match, 3-1 against Rugby Town.

With 52 goals in 31 games so far this season, Leicester Road are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Sporting, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are fourth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn eight, earning 59 points.

The away team are four places behind Leicester Road in eighth, with 14 wins and four draws putting them on 46 points.

Leicester Road are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Sporting's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the home side's 10.

In the last three years, Leicester Road and Sporting have played each other on three occasions. Sporting won all of them.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when Sporting won 3-1 at home.
