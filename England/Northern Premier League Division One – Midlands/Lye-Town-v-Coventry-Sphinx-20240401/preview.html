Lye Town v Coventry Sphinx

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Coventry Sphinx travel to Lye Town on Monday in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Sutton Coldfield Town 1-0 at home.

Lye, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-0 against Rugby Town.

Coventry Sphinx are 17th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won six and drawn 11, earning 29 points.

Lye are 11 places ahead of the visitors in sixth, with 16 wins and six draws putting them on 54 points.

Town are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Coventry Sphinx's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the home team's 10.

With 31 goals in 31 games so far this season, Coventry Sphinx are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Lye are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.
