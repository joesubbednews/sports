Loughborough Dynamo v Rugby Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Nanpantan Sports Ground
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Rugby Town travel to Nanpantan Sports Ground looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands games against Loughborough Dynamo on Saturday.

Rugby have lost four and drawn one matches since they last earned three points – against Gresley on December 26.

They face a Loughborough Dynamo side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

Rugby are 19th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won five and drawn two, earning 17 points.

Loughborough Dynamo are 14 places ahead of Town in fifth, with 13 wins and five draws putting them on 44 points.

With 26 goals in 25 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Loughborough Dynamo's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 loss against Sporting Khalsa.

Rugby lost 2-0 against AFC Rushden and Diamonds last time out, on January 20.
