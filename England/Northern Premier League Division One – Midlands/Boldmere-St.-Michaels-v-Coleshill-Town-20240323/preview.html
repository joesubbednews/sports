Boldmere St. Michaels v Coleshill Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Boldmere St. Michaels and Coleshill Town both come into Saturday's kick-off in poor form, having picked up one and four points respectively in their last six games.

Boldmere St. Michaels have drawn just one of the last six, while Coleshill have taken one win and one draw.

With 47 goals in 31 games so far this season, Coleshill are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 69 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Boldmere St. Michaels, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are 14th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won nine and drawn five, earning 32 points.

The hosts are one place ahead of the away side in 13th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

In the last three years, Boldmere St. Michaels and Coleshill have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Boldmere St. Michaels won 4-0 away.

Boldmere St. Michaels's last match was on March 16, a 2-1 loss against Anstey Nomads.

Coleshill drew 1-1 with Gresley last time out, also on March 16.
