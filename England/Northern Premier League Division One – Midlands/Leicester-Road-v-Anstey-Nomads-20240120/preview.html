Leicester Road v Anstey Nomads

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of Northern Premier League Division One Midlands's top sides face each other in Saturday's kick-off, when fourth-placed Leicester Road host third-placed Anstey Nomads.

Leicester Road have picked up 13 wins and seven draws from 24 games so far this season, and sit three points below the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Anstey Nomads, meanwhile, have won 14 and drawn seven of 22, picking up 49 points.

With 53 goals in 22 games so far this season, Anstey Nomads are the league's highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Leicester Road are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Leicester Road's 13.

Leicester Road's last match was on January 13, a 3-1 win against Sutton Coldfield Town.

Anstey Nomads beat Spalding United 5-0 last time out, also on January 13.
