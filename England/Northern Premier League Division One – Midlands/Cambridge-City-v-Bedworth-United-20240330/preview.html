Cambridge City v Bedworth United

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | ProEdge Westwood Road
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bedworth United travel to Cambridge City on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Sporting Khalsa 3-0 at home.

Cambridge, meanwhile, drew their last match, 2-2 against Coventry Sphinx.

In the last 10 years, Cambridge and Bedworth have played each other on seven occasions. Cambridge won one of them, Bedworth two, and they drew four times.

On average, Cambridge scored 1.6 goals and Bedworth 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 3, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Cambridge are 16th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Bedworth are four places ahead of City in 12th, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

With 37 goals in 33 games so far this season, United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The home team are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Cambridge are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Cambridge's seven.
