Coleshill Town v Coventry Sphinx

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Coventry Sphinx make the journey to play Coleshill Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Coventry Sphinx's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

Coventry Sphinx are 17th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won four and drawn eight, earning 20 points.

Coleshill are three places ahead of the away team in 14th, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

With 21 goals in 23 games so far this season, Coventry Sphinx are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

Coleshill's last match was on January 20, a 4-3 loss against Cambridge City.

Coventry Sphinx lost 3-0 against Sporting Khalsa last time out, on January 13.
