Spalding United v Lye Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Sir Halley Stewart Field
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of Northern Premier League Division One Midlands's meanest defences go head-to-head at Sir Halley Stewart Field on Saturday, when Spalding United host Lye Town.

Only one side – Harborough Town – has conceded fewer goals than Spalding to date: the home side have let in just 25 goals in 26 games.

Lye have conceded 30 goals in 25 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Spalding are top of the table after 26 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn four, earning 58 points.

Lye are five places behind United in sixth, with 13 wins and five draws putting them on 44 points.

The hosts are in fantastic form in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Spalding's 15.

Spalding's last match was on February 3, a 4-0 win against Gresley.

Lye beat Sutton Coldfield Town 1-0 last time out, also on February 3.
