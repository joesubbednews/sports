Harborough Town v Quorn

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Harborough Town and Quorn promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Harborough and Quorn sit third and fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 67 and 66 goals respectively.

Harborough are second in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 23 and drawn five, earning 74 points.

Quorn are four places behind Town in sixth, with 16 wins and seven draws putting them on 55 points.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Harborough's 11.

Harborough's last match was on April 6, a 1-0 win against Sporting Khalsa.

Quorn lost 2-1 against Spalding United last time out, also on April 6.
