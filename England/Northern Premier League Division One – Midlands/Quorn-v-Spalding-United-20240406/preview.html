Quorn v Spalding United

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Sutton Park
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Quorn and Spalding United promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Sutton Park, Quorn and Spalding sit fourth and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 65 and 75 goals respectively.

Spalding are top of the table after 33 games, of which they have won 23 and drawn five, earning 74 points.

Quorn are five places behind United in sixth, with 16 wins and seven draws putting them on 55 points.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

But with five wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is even better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Quorn's 15.

Quorn's last match was on Monday, a 1-0 win against Shepshed Dynamo.

Spalding beat Leicester Road 2-0 last time out, also on Monday.
