Spalding United v Coleshill Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Sir Halley Stewart Field
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Spalding United host Coleshill Town at Sir Halley Stewart Field on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Spalding have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Coleshill side who have lost their last five matches, and collected three points from the last possible 18.

With 88 goals in 36 games so far this season, Spalding are the league's highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Coleshill, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

United are top of the table after 36 games, of which they have won 26 and drawn five, earning 83 points.

Town are 13 places behind the home team in 14th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last five years, Spalding and Coleshill have played each other on five occasions. They won two each, and they drew once.

On average, Spalding scored 1.2 goals and Coleshill 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Spalding won 3-2 away.

Spalding's last match was on April 20, a 7-1 win against Sporting Khalsa.

Coleshill lost 2-1 against Anstey Nomads last time out, also on April 20.
