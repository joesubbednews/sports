Boldmere St. Michaels v Harborough Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Harborough Town visit Boldmere St. Michaels on Saturday on the back of six consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

It means Harborough have picked up the maximum 18 points from their last six games, and they face a Boldmere St. Michaels side who drew their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

With 57 goals in 25 games so far this season, Harborough are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Boldmere St. Michaels are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Town are second in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn three, earning 57 points.

The hosts are 10 places behind the away side in 12th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

In the last three years, Boldmere St. Michaels and Harborough have played each other on three occasions. Harborough won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Harborough won 2-0 at home.

Boldmere St. Michaels's last match was on February 3, a 1-1 draw against Shepshed Dynamo.

Harborough beat Bedworth United 2-0 last time out, also on February 3.
