Sporting Khalsa v Anstey Nomads

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Anstey Nomads face Sporting Khalsa on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Anstey Nomads are unbeaten in 10, with seven wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Sporting team who have won four and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 56 goals in 24 games so far this season, Anstey Nomads are the league's highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Sporting, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The visitors are third in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn eight, earning 53 points.

The home team are five places behind Anstey Nomads in eighth, with 12 wins and four draws putting them on 40 points.

Sporting's last match was on January 27, a 4-0 loss against Quorn.

Anstey Nomads beat Shepshed Dynamo 2-1 last time out, also on January 27.
