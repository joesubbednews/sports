Corby Town v Gresley

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Steel Park
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Corby Town face Gresley on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Corby are unbeaten in six, with four wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Gresley team who have won one and drawn two over the same number of games.

Gresley are bottom of the table after 35 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

Corby are 12 places ahead of the visitors in eighth, with 14 wins and 11 draws putting them on 53 points.

With 34 goals in 35 games so far this season, Gresley are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 88 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Corby and Gresley have played each other on three occasions. Corby won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Corby won 6-1 away.

Corby's last match was on April 20, a 2-2 draw against Loughborough Dynamo.

Gresley lost 3-1 against Leicester Road last time out, on April 13.
