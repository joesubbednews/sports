Rugby Town v AFC Rushden and Diamonds

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Butlin Road
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match at Butlin Road sees the league's two bottom sides face each other, as Rugby Town host rock-bottom AFC Rushden and Diamonds.

Rugby have picked up 17 points from their first 17 Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands games, with five wins and two draws.

That is 12 points more than Rushden & Diamonds have collected, having won one and drawn two.

Rugby are in bad form in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

But with no wins and a draw over that period, Rushden & Diamonds's form is even worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to Town's four.

With 15 goals in 22 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Rugby's last match was on January 13, a 1-1 draw against Corby Town.

Rushden & Diamonds drew 2-2 with Bedworth United last time out, also on January 13.
