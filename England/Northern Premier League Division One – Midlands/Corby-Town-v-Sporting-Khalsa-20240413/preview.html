Corby Town v Sporting Khalsa

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Steel Park
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Corby Town host Sporting Khalsa at Steel Park on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Corby have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Sporting side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 64 goals in 34 games so far this season, Corby are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Sporting, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Corby and Sporting have played each other on five occasions. Corby won two of them, Sporting one, and they drew twice.

On average, Corby scored 3.0 goals and Sporting 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Corby won 6-0 away.

The visitors are eighth in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn five, earning 50 points.

Town are one place behind Sporting in ninth, with 13 wins and 10 draws putting them on 49 points.

Corby's last match was on April 6, a 1-0 win against Sutton Coldfield Town.

Sporting lost 1-0 against Harborough Town last time out, also on April 6.
