Walsall Wood v Coleshill Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Coleshill Town make the journey to play Walsall Wood on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Coleshill's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

With 46 goals in 31 games so far this season, Walsall Wood are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Coleshill are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Town are 14th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn five, earning 35 points.

The hosts are two places ahead of the away team in 12th, with 14 wins and six draws putting them on 48 points.

Walsall Wood's last match was on April 6, a 2-1 win against Coventry Sphinx.

Coleshill lost 5-3 against Leicester Road last time out, also on April 6.
