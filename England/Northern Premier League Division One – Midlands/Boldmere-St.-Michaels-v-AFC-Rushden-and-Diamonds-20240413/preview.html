Boldmere St. Michaels v AFC Rushden and Diamonds

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Boldmere St. Michaels host AFC Rushden and Diamonds on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Boldmere St. Michaels have picked up nine points from their last six games, and they face a Rushden & Diamonds side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

Rushden & Diamonds are bottom of the table after 33 games, of which they have won six and drawn four, earning 22 points.

Boldmere St. Michaels are seven places ahead of the visitors in 13th, with 13 wins and four draws putting them on 43 points.

With 29 goals in 33 games so far this season, Rushden & Diamonds are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 70 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The home side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Boldmere St. Michaels's last match was on April 6, a 4-0 win against Cambridge City.

Rushden & Diamonds lost 3-0 against Anstey Nomads last time out, also on April 6.
