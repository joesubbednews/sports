Quorn v AFC Rushden and Diamonds

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Sutton Park
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Quorn host AFC Rushden and Diamonds on Saturday at Sutton Park in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

In their last league match, on March 16, Quorn lost to Sutton Coldfield Town 5-3 away.

Rushden & Diamonds also lost, 4-0 at home against Lye Town.

Rushden & Diamonds are bottom of the table after 29 games, of which they have won five and drawn three, earning 18 points.

Quorn are 13 places ahead of the visitors in seventh, with 14 wins and seven draws putting them on 49 points.

With 25 goals in 29 games so far this season, Rushden & Diamonds are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 59 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Quorn are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Rushden & Diamonds's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Quorn's 13.
