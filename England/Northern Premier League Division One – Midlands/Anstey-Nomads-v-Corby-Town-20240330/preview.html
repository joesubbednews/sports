Anstey Nomads v Corby Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Anstey Nomads host Corby Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

In their last league match, on March 16, Anstey Nomads beat Boldmere St. Michaels 2-1 at home.

Corby also won, 3-0 at home against Walsall Wood on March 23.

With 67 goals in 28 games so far this season, Anstey Nomads are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Corby are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are third in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn eight, earning 62 points.

Town are eight places behind Anstey Nomads in 11th, with 11 wins and 10 draws putting them on 43 points.

Anstey Nomads are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.

Over the last two years, Anstey Nomads and Corby have played each other twice. Anstey Nomads won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on October 4, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
