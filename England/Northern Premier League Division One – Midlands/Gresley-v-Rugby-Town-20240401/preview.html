Gresley v Rugby Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | The Moat Ground
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Monday's match at the Moat Ground sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table Gresley host 18th-placed Rugby Town.

Gresley have picked up 19 points from their first 19 Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands games, with four wins and seven draws.

That is two points less than Rugby have collected, having won six and drawn three.

Gresley are in terrible form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Rugby's form is better – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the home side's one.

With 31 goals in 32 games so far this season, Gresley are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 84 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Gresley's last match was on Saturday, a 5-1 loss against Boldmere St. Michaels.

Rugby lost 2-0 against Lye Town last time out, also on Saturday.
