Leicester Road v Rugby Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Rugby Town come to play Leicester Road on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Leicester Road have three wins and a draw.

Rugby are 18th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won six and drawn three, earning 21 points.

Leicester Road are 13 places ahead of Town in fifth, with 16 wins and eight draws putting them on 56 points.

With 32 goals in 30 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 68 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Leicester Road's last match was on March 16, a 2-1 win against Shepshed Dynamo.

Rugby lost 5-1 against Spalding United last time out, also on March 16.
