Walsall Wood v Bedworth United

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bedworth United travel to Walsall Wood on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Corby Town 2-0 at home.

Walsall Wood, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-0 against Lye Town.

With 33 goals in 31 games so far this season, Bedworth are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Walsall Wood, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, United's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Walsall Wood's eight.

Walsall Wood are ninth in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

The away side are three places behind the home team in 12th, with 10 wins and seven draws putting them on 37 points.
