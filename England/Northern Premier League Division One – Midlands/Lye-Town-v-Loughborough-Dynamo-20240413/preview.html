Lye Town v Loughborough Dynamo

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Loughborough Dynamo come to play Lye Town on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Lye have three wins and a draw.

With 65 goals in 34 games so far this season, Loughborough Dynamo are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Lye, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The away side are fifth in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn six, earning 60 points.

Town are two places behind Loughborough Dynamo in seventh, with 16 wins and seven draws putting them on 55 points.

Lye's last match was on April 6, a 1-0 loss against Gresley.

Loughborough Dynamo lost 2-1 against Bedworth United last time out, also on April 6.
