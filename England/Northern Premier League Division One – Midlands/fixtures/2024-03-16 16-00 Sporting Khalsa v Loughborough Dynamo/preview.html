Sporting Khalsa v Loughborough Dynamo

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Loughborough Dynamo travel for Saturday's match against Sporting Khalsa looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Loughborough Dynamo, who sit fourth in the league after 29 games, fell to a 3-2 home defeat to Quorn on March 2.

They face a Sporting side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Lye Town, and who sit seventh in the table.

With 58 goals in 29 games so far this season, Loughborough Dynamo are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Sporting, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Sporting and Loughborough Dynamo have played each other on six occasions. Sporting won three of them, Loughborough Dynamo two, and they drew once.

On average, Sporting scored 2.3 goals and Loughborough Dynamo 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 23, when Sporting won 2-0 away.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Sporting's 10.
