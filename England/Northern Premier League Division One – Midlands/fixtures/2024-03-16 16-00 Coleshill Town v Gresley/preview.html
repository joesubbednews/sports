Coleshill Town v Gresley

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Gresley make the journey to play Coleshill Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Gresley's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

Gresley are bottom of the table after 29 games, of which they have won four and drawn six, earning 18 points.

Coleshill are six places ahead of the visitors in 14th, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

With 29 goals in 29 games so far this season, Gresley are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 77 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

In the last three years, Coleshill and Gresley have played each other on three occasions. Coleshill won one of them and Gresley the other.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when Gresley won 3-0 at home.

Coleshill's last match was on March 2, a 4-0 loss against AFC Rushden and Diamonds.

Gresley lost 2-1 against Quorn last time out, on February 17.
