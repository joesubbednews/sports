Coventry Sphinx v Harborough Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Sphinx Drive
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Coventry Sphinx face a challenge against high-flying Harborough Town at Sphinx Drive on Saturday.

Coventry Sphinx are 17th in the Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands table, and have picked up five wins and 10 draws in their 27 games to date.

Harborough, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 63 points, having won 20 and drawn three, and are one point behind table-toppers Spalding United.

Coventry Sphinx are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Harborough's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

With 60 goals in 27 games so far this season, Town are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Coventry Sphinx, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Coventry Sphinx's last match was on February 24, a 3-0 loss against Corby Town.

Harborough beat Leicester Road 2-0 last time out, also on February 24.
