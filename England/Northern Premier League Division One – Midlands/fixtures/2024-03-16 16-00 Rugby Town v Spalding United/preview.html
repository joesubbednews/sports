Rugby Town v Spalding United

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Butlin Road
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Rugby Town face a challenge against high-flying Spalding United at Butlin Road on Saturday.

Rugby Town are 18th in the Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands table, and have picked up six wins and three draws in their 29 games to date.

Spalding, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 64 points, having won 20 and drawn four.

With 64 goals in 29 games so far this season, Spalding are the league's highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Rugby, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, United's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

Rugby's last match was on February 24, a 2-0 loss against Coleshill Town.

Spalding beat Boldmere St. Michaels 3-0 last time out, on March 2.
