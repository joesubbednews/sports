Loughborough Dynamo v Coventry Sphinx

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Nanpantan Sports Ground
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Coventry Sphinx face a challenge away against high-flying Loughborough Dynamo at the Nanpantan Sports Ground on Saturday.

Coventry Sphinx are 17th in the Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands table, and have picked up five wins and 11 draws in their 29 games to date.

Loughborough Dynamo, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 59 points, having won 18 and drawn five of their first 30 matches, and are eight points behind table-toppers Harborough Town.

With 28 goals in 29 games so far this season, Coventry Sphinx are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Loughborough Dynamo, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home side are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Loughborough Dynamo's 12.

Loughborough Dynamo's last match was on March 16, a 3-2 win against Sporting Khalsa.

Coventry Sphinx drew 2-2 with Cambridge City last time out, also on March 16.
