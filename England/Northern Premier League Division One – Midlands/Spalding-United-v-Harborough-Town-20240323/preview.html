Spalding United v Harborough Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Sir Halley Stewart Field
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

The Northern Premier League Division One Midlands's top two sides face each other at Sir Halley Stewart Field in Saturday's kick-off, when second-placed Spalding United host Harborough Town.

Spalding have picked up 21 wins and four draws from 30 games so far this season, and sit on the same number of points as the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Harborough, meanwhile, have won 21 and drawn four of 29, picking up 67 points.

With 69 goals in 30 games so far this season, Spalding are the league's highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Harborough are also above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

United are in fantastic form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

But with five wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is even better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 15.

In the last three years, Spalding and Harborough have played each other on three occasions. Spalding won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Spalding's last match was on March 16, a 5-1 win against Rugby Town.

Harborough drew 1-1 with Corby Town last time out, also on March 16.
