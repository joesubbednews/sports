AFC Rushden and Diamonds v Lye Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Hayden Road
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling AFC Rushden and Diamonds face Lye Town at Hayden Road on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-0 victory over Coleshill Town on March 2, Rushden & Diamonds sit 19th in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

They travel to play a Lye side seventh in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-0 against Walsall Wood, on March 9.

With 25 goals in 28 games so far this season, Rushden & Diamonds are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Lye, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Rushden & Diamonds's 13.
