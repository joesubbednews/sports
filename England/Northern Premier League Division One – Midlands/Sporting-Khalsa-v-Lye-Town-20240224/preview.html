Sporting Khalsa v Lye Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lye Town travel to Sporting Khalsa on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Corby Town 1-1 at home.

Sporting, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-1 against Cambridge City.

With 38 goals in 26 games so far this season, Lye are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Sporting are also average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are seventh in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn six, earning 45 points.

The home team are one place behind the visitors in eighth, with 13 wins and four draws putting them on 43 points.

Sporting are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Lye's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Sporting's 10.
