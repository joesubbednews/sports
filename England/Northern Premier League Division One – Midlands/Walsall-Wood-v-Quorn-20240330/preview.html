Walsall Wood v Quorn

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Quorn come to play Walsall Wood on Saturday in great form, having collected 15 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and lost one of their last six fixtures, while Walsall Wood have two wins and a draw.

With 64 goals in 30 games so far this season, Quorn are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Walsall Wood, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The away team are sixth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn seven, earning 52 points.

The hosts are four places behind Quorn in 10th, with 13 wins and six draws putting them on 45 points.

Walsall Wood's last match was on March 23, a 3-0 loss against Corby Town.

Quorn beat AFC Rushden and Diamonds 7-1 last time out, also on March 23.
