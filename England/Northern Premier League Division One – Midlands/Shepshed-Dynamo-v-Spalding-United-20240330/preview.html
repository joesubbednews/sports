Shepshed Dynamo v Spalding United

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | The Dovecote
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Shepshed Dynamo host Spalding United on Saturday at the Dovecote in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

In their last league match, on March 23, Shepshed Dynamo beat Lye Town 3-1 away.

Spalding drew, 1-1 at home against Harborough Town.

With 70 goals in 31 games so far this season, Spalding are the league's highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Shepshed Dynamo, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

United are second in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn five, earning 68 points.

The hosts are 13 places behind the away team in 15th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

In the last 10 years, Shepshed Dynamo and Spalding have played each other on seven occasions. Spalding won six of them and they drew once.

On average, Shepshed Dynamo scored 0.7 goals and Spalding 2.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 23, when Spalding won 6-1 at home.

Shepshed Dynamo are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Spalding's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Shepshed Dynamo's seven.
