Coleshill Town v Harborough Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Harborough Town face Coleshill Town on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Harborough are unbeaten in 11, with nine wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Coleshill team who have won two and drawn three over the same number of games.

Harborough are top of the table after 30 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn five, earning 68 points.

Coleshill are 12 places behind Harborough Town in 13th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

With 64 goals in 30 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Coleshill Town, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Coleshill and Harborough have played each other on three occasions. Harborough won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when Harborough won 3-0 at home.

Coleshill's last match was on March 23, a 1-0 win against Boldmere St. Michaels.

Harborough drew 1-1 with Spalding United last time out, also on March 23.
