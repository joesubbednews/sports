Gresley v Sutton Coldfield Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | The Moat Ground
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Gresley are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Sutton Coldfield Town at the Moat Ground on Saturday, with just one point collected from their last six games.

Gresley have picked up just one draw in their last six Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands games, and face a Sutton Coldfield side whose last six games have brought two wins and four losses.

Gresley are 19th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won four and drawn seven, earning 19 points.

Sutton Coldfield are nine places ahead of the hosts in 10th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

With 30 goals in 30 games so far this season, Gresley are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 78 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Gresley and Sutton Coldfield have played each other on four occasions. Gresley won one of them and Sutton Coldfield the other.

On average, Gresley scored 1.5 goals and Sutton Coldfield 3.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Sutton Coldfield won 7-1 at home.

Gresley's last match was on March 16, a 1-1 draw against Coleshill Town.

Sutton Coldfield beat Quorn 5-3 last time out, also on March 16.
