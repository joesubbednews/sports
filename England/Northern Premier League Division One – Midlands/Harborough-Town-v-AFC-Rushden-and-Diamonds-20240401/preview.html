Harborough Town v AFC Rushden and Diamonds

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates AFC Rushden and Diamonds face a challenge away against high-flying Harborough Town on Monday.

AFC Rushden and Diamonds are 19th in the Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands table, and have picked up five wins and four draws in their 31 games to date.

Harborough, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 71 points, having won 22 and drawn five.

With 27 goals in 31 games so far this season, Rushden & Diamonds are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 67 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Harborough, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

Town are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the hosts' 14.

Harborough's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 win against Coleshill Town.

Rushden & Diamonds drew 1-1 with Loughborough Dynamo last time out, also on Saturday.
