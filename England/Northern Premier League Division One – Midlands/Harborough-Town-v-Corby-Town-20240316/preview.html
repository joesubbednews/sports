Harborough Town v Corby Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Harborough Town host Corby Town on Saturday on the back of nine consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

It means Harborough have picked up the maximum 27 points from their last nine games, and they face a Corby side who lost their last match, and have collected 11 points from the last possible 27.

Harborough are top of the table after 28 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn three, earning 66 points.

Corby are nine places behind Harborough Town in 10th, with 10 wins and nine draws putting them on 39 points.

With 62 goals in 28 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Corby Town are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last three years, Harborough and Corby have played each other on three occasions. Harborough won all of them.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when Harborough won 4-3 away.

Harborough's last match was on March 9, a 2-1 win against Coventry Sphinx.

Corby lost 2-0 against Bedworth United last time out, on March 2.
