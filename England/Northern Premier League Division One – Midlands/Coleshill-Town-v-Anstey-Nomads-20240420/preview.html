Coleshill Town v Anstey Nomads

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Anstey Nomads visit Coleshill Town on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Anstey Nomads have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Coleshill side who have lost their last four matches, and collected four points from the last possible 18.

With 76 goals in 31 games so far this season, Anstey Nomads are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Coleshill, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The away side are third in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn eight, earning 71 points.

Town are 11 places behind Anstey Nomads in 14th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

Coleshill's last match was on April 13, a 3-0 loss against Walsall Wood.

Anstey Nomads beat Coventry Sphinx 5-1 last time out, also on April 13.
