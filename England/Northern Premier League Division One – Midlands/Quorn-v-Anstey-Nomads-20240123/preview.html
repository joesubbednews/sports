Quorn v Anstey Nomads

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Sutton Park
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Anstey Nomads face Quorn on Tuesday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Anstey Nomads are unbeaten in eight, with six wins and two draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Quorn team who have won three and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 53 goals in 22 games so far this season, Anstey Nomads are the league's highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Quorn are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The visitors are third in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn seven, earning 49 points.

The home team are five places behind Anstey Nomads in eighth, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

Over the last two years, Quorn and Anstey Nomads have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on April 24, when Quorn won 1-0 away.

Quorn's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Corby Town.

Anstey Nomads beat Spalding United 5-0 last time out, on January 13.
