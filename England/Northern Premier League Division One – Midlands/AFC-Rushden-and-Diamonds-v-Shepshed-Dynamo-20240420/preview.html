AFC Rushden and Diamonds v Shepshed Dynamo

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Hayden Road
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Shepshed Dynamo come to Hayden Road to play AFC Rushden and Diamonds on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Rushden & Diamonds have a win and two draws.

Rushden & Diamonds are 19th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won six and drawn five, earning 23 points.

Shepshed Dynamo are four places ahead of the home team in 15th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

With 31 goals in 34 games so far this season, Rushden & Diamonds are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 72 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Rushden & Diamonds's last match was on April 13, a 2-2 draw against Boldmere St. Michaels.

Shepshed Dynamo drew 0-0 with Cambridge City last time out, also on April 13.
