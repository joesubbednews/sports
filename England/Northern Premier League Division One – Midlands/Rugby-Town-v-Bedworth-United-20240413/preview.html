Rugby Town v Bedworth United

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Butlin Road
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Rugby Town face Bedworth United at Butlin Road on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-1 victory over Shepshed Dynamo on April 6, Rugby sit 18th in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

They travel to play a Bedworth side 10th in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Loughborough Dynamo.

Rugby are in bad form in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, Bedworth's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Town's four.

With 37 goals in 34 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 75 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.
