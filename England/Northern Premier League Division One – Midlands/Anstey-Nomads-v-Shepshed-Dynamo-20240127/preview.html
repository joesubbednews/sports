Anstey Nomads v Shepshed Dynamo

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Anstey Nomads face Shepshed Dynamo on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Anstey Nomads are unbeaten in nine, with six wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Shepshed Dynamo team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 54 goals in 23 games so far this season, Anstey Nomads are the league's highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Shepshed Dynamo, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are third in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn eight, earning 50 points.

The away team are 12 places behind Anstey Nomads in 15th, with seven wins and four draws putting them on 25 points.

Anstey Nomads's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-1 draw against Quorn.

Shepshed Dynamo lost 2-1 against Walsall Wood last time out, also on Tuesday.
