AFC Rushden and Diamonds v Sutton Coldfield Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Hayden Road
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling AFC Rushden and Diamonds face Sutton Coldfield Town at Hayden Road on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Rugby Town on January 20, Rushden & Diamonds sit bottom of Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

They travel to play a Sutton Coldfield side ninth in the standings, who were held in their last match, 1-1 against Sporting Khalsa.

Rushden & Diamonds are in bad form in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Sutton Coldfield's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With 17 goals in 23 games so far this season, Rushden & Diamonds are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.
