Harborough Town v Bedworth United

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Harborough Town host Bedworth United on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Harborough have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Bedworth side who lost their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

With 55 goals in 24 games so far this season, Harborough are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Bedworth, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Town are second in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn three, earning 54 points.

United are 11 places behind the home side in 13th, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.

In the last five years, Harborough and Bedworth have played each other on four occasions. Harborough won two of them and they drew twice.

On average, Harborough scored 1.0 goal and Bedworth 0.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 17, when Harborough won 1-0 away.

Harborough's last match was on January 27, a 4-0 win against Gresley.

Bedworth lost 1-0 against Walsall Wood last time out, on Tuesday.
