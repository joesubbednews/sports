Sutton Coldfield Town v Lye Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Central Ground
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lye Town travel to Sutton Coldfield Town on Saturday in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Coleshill Town 3-1 at home.

Sutton Coldfield, meanwhile, lost their last match, 1-0 against AFC Rushden and Diamonds.

With 40 goals in 25 games so far this season, Sutton Coldfield are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Lye, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Lye Town are sixth in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

Sutton Coldfield Town are four places behind the visitors in 10th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

The home side are in mixed form in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Lye's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Sutton Coldfield's eight.
