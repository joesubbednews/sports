Loughborough Dynamo v Corby Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Nanpantan Sports Ground
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Corby Town visit Loughborough Dynamo at the Nanpantan Sports Ground on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

Corby have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Loughborough Dynamo side who have lost their last three matches, and collected four points from the last possible 18.

With 71 goals in 35 games so far this season, Corby are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Loughborough Dynamo are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Loughborough Dynamo and Corby have played each other on six occasions. Loughborough Dynamo won five of them and Corby one.

On average, Loughborough Dynamo scored 2.2 goals and Corby 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Loughborough Dynamo won 1-0 away.

The home team are fifth in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn six, earning 60 points.

Town are three places behind Loughborough Dynamo in eighth, with 14 wins and 10 draws putting them on 52 points.

Loughborough Dynamo's last match was on April 13, a 5-0 loss against Lye Town.

Corby beat Sporting Khalsa 7-1 last time out, also on April 13.
