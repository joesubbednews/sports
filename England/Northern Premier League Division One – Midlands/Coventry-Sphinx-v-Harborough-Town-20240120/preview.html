Coventry Sphinx v Harborough Town

Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands | Sphinx Drive
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Coventry Sphinx face a challenge against high-flying Harborough Town at Sphinx Drive on Saturday.

Coventry Sphinx are 17th in the Northern Premier League Division One – Midlands table, and have picked up four wins and eight draws in their 23 games to date.

Harborough, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 51 points, having won 16 and drawn three, and are one point behind table-toppers Spalding United.

With 51 goals in 23 games so far this season, Harborough are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Coventry Sphinx, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Coventry Sphinx's four.

Coventry Sphinx's last match was on January 13, a 3-0 loss against Sporting Khalsa.

Harborough beat Shepshed Dynamo 4-0 last time out, also on January 13.
