Newcastle Town v Runcorn Linnets

Northern Premier League – Division One West | Lyme Valley Stadium
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Newcastle Town host Runcorn Linnets in Saturday's match at the Lyme Valley Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Newcastle, who sit 17th in the league after 21 games, fell to a 1-2 away defeat to Mossley on January 6.

They face a Runcorn Linnets side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Kidsgrove Athletic, and who sit third in the table.

With 23 goals in 21 games so far this season, Newcastle are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Runcorn Linnets, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home side's seven.

Over the last year, Newcastle and Runcorn Linnets have played each other twice. Runcorn Linnets won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on August 22, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
