City of Liverpool v Avro

Northern Premier League – Division One West
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

City of Liverpool face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Avro defence on Saturday.

With 37 goals in 21 games, City of Liverpool are the joint-third-highest scorers in Northern Premier League Division One West ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face an Avro side who have scored 25 in 22 matches, but conceded only 20 goals, putting them joint-top of the league's tightest defences.

Avro are fifth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won eight and drawn 10, earning 34 points.

City of Liverpool are five places behind the away side in 10th, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

The hosts are in reasonable form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Avro's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to City of Liverpool's 12.

City of Liverpool's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-0 loss against Witton Albion.

Avro lost 1-0 against Witton Albion last time out, on January 6.
