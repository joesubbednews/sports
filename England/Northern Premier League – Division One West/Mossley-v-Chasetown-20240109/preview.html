Mossley v Chasetown

Northern Premier League – Division One West | Seel Park
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Mossley host Chasetown on Tuesday at Seel Park in Northern Premier League Division One West.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Mossley lost to Newcastle Town 2-1 at home.

Chasetown won, 1-0 away at Vauxhall Motors.

With 25 goals in 21 games so far this season, Chasetown are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Mossley, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Mossley's six.

Mossley are 13th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won eight and drawn four, earning 28 points.

Chasetown are two places ahead of the home side in 11th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.
