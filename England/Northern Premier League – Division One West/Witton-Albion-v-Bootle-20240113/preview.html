Witton Albion v Bootle

Northern Premier League – Division One West | Help For Heroes Stadium
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Witton Albion host Bootle at the Help For Heroes Stadium on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Witton have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Bootle side who have lost their last three matches, and collected nine points from the last possible 18.

With 27 goals in 21 games so far this season, Bootle are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Witton, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Albion are sixth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

The visitors are three places behind the home side in ninth, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

Witton's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-0 win against City of Liverpool.

Bootle lost 4-0 against Stalybridge Celtic last time out, on January 6.
