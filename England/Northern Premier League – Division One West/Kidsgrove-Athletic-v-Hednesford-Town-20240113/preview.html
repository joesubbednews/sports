Kidsgrove Athletic v Hednesford Town

Northern Premier League – Division One West | The Seddon Stadium
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Hednesford Town face Kidsgrove Athletic away at the Seddon Stadium on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-0 victory over Widnes on January 6, the Pitmen sit 19th in Northern Premier League Division One West.

They travel to play a Kidsgrove side eighth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 1-0 against Runcorn Linnets.

With 40 goals in 22 games so far this season, Kidsgrove are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Hednesford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Athletic are in disappointing form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's six.
