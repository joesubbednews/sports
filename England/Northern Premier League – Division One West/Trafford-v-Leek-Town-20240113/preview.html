Trafford v Leek Town

Northern Premier League – Division One West | Shawe View
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Trafford face a challenge against high-flying Leek Town at Shawe View on Saturday.

Trafford are 18th in the Northern Premier League – Division One West table, and have picked up four wins and 10 draws in their 22 games to date.

Leek, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 47 points, having won 15 and drawn two.

With 56 goals in 22 games so far this season, Leek are the league's highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Trafford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and four draws from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to Trafford's 10.

Over the last two years, Trafford and Leek have played each other on three occasions. Trafford won one of them and Leek the other.

Their last meeting was on August 22, when Leek won 3-0 at home.

Trafford's last match was on January 6, a 1-1 draw against Hanley Town.

Leek beat City of Liverpool 1-0 last time out, also on January 6.
