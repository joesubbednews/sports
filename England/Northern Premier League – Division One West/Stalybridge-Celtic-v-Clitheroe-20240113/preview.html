Stalybridge Celtic v Clitheroe

Northern Premier League – Division One West | Bower Fold
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Stalybridge Celtic and Clitheroe promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Northern Premier League Division One West's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Bower Fold, Stalybridge Celtic and Clitheroe sit joint-third in the goal-scoring charts, with 37 goals apiece.

Stalybridge Celtic are fourth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn seven, earning 37 points.

Clitheroe are three places behind the home side in seventh, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

Stalybridge Celtic are in mixed form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken eight points from 18.

Stalybridge Celtic's last match was on January 6, a 4-0 win against Bootle.

Clitheroe drew 0-0 with Nantwich Town last time out, also on January 6.
