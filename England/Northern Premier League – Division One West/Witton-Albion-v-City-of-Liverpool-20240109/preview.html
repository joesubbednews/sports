Witton Albion v City of Liverpool

Northern Premier League – Division One West
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Witton Albion host City of Liverpool on late Tuesday on the back of three consecutive wins in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Witton have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a City of Liverpool side who lost their last match, and have collected 12 points from the last possible 18.

With 37 goals in 20 games so far this season, City of Liverpool are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Witton are also above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Albion are ninth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn four, earning 31 points.

The visitors are one place behind the home side in 10th, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

Witton's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 win against Avro.

City of Liverpool lost 1-0 against Leek Town last time out, also on Saturday.
