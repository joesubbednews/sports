Stourbridge Women v Wolverhampton Wanderers Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Wolverhampton Wanderers Women travel looking to secure a first win in five Women's National League Premier Division – North games against Stourbridge Women on late Wednesday.

Wolverhampton Wanderers have lost one and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against West Brom WFC on October 22.

They face a Stourbridge side who have won just one and drawn one over that time.

With 23 goals in 15 games so far this season, Stourbridge are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 2.9 per game.

Wolverhampton Wanderers, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

The hosts are ninth in the table after 15 games, of which they have won four and drawn four, earning 16 points.

The away side are four places ahead of Stourbridge in fifth, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.

Over the last two years, Stourbridge and Wolverhampton Wanderers have played each other twice. Wolverhampton Wanderers won both of them.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when Wolverhampton Wanderers won 8-0 at home.

Stourbridge's last match was on February 11, a 4-1 loss against Stoke City Women.

Wolverhampton Wanderers drew 0-0 with Newcastle United Women last time out, on February 4.
