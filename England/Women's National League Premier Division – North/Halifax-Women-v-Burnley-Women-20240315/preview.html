Halifax Women v Burnley Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Friday, 15 March 2024 @ 0.00am

Relegation candidates Halifax Women face a challenge against high-flying Burnley Women on Friday.

Halifax Women are 10th in the Women's National League Premier Division – North table, and have picked up two wins and seven draws in their 12 games to date.

Burnley, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 28 points, having won eight and drawn four of their first 14 matches, and are 13 points behind table-toppers Newcastle United Women.

With 11 goals in 12 games so far this season, Halifax are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Burnley, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with no wins and five draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Halifax's five.

Halifax's last match was on March 3, a 3-0 loss against Nottingham Forest Women.

Burnley drew 1-1 with West Brom WFC last time out, on Sunday.
