Halifax Women v Nottingham Forest Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Monday, 25 March 2024 @ 0.00am

Relegation candidates Halifax Women face a challenge against high-flying Nottingham Forest Women on Monday.

Halifax Women are 10th in the Women's National League Premier Division – North table, and have picked up two wins and seven draws in their 13 games to date.

Nottingham Forest, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 31 points, having won nine and drawn four of their first 16 matches, and are 13 points behind table-toppers Newcastle United Women.

With 40 goals in 16 games so far this season, Nottingham Forest are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Halifax, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with no wins and four draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Halifax's four.

Halifax's last match was on March 17, a 1-0 loss against West Brom WFC.

Nottingham Forest drew 0-0 with Huddersfield Town Women last time out, on March 14.
