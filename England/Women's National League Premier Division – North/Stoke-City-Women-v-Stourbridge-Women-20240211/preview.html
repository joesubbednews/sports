Stoke City Women v Stourbridge Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Stoke City Women host Stourbridge Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Premier Division North.

In their last league match, on January 28, Stoke City drew with Wolverhampton Wanderers Women 0-0 away.

Stourbridge also drew, 0-0 at home against Halifax Women on February 4.

With 22 goals in 14 games so far this season, Stourbridge are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.8 per game.

Stoke City, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The hosts are ninth in the table after 11 games, of which they have won four and drawn two, earning 14 points.

The away team are two places ahead of Stoke City in seventh, with four wins and four draws putting them on 16 points.

Stoke City are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and two draws over that period, Stourbridge's form is identical – they have both taken eight points from 18.
