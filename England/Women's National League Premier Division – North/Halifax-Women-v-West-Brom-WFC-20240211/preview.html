Halifax Women v West Brom WFC

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Halifax Women welcome West Brom WFC on early Sunday hoping to break their five-match run of draws in Women's National League Premier Division North.

Halifax have earned five points from their last six games.

They face a West Brom WFC side who have collected eight points from the last possible 18, and lost their last game.

Halifax are 10th in the table after 10 games, of which they have won two and drawn six, earning 12 points.

West Brom WFC are two places ahead of the hosts in eighth, with four wins and three draws putting them on 15 points.

With 11 goals in 10 games so far this season, Halifax are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Halifax's last match was on February 4, a 0-0 draw against Stourbridge Women.

West Brom WFC beat Derby County Women 2-1 last time out, also on Sunday.
