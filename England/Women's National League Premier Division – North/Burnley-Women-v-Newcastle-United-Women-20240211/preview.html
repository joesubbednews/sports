Burnley Women v Newcastle United Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Burnley Women host Newcastle United Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Women's National League Premier Division North.

Burnley, who sit third in the league after 11 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to Nottingham Forest Women on January 28.

They face a Newcastle United side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Wolverhampton Wanderers Women, and who sit first in the table.

With 34 goals in 13 games so far this season, Newcastle United are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in four goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

Burnley are also above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in Women's National League Premier Division North, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Burnley's 11.
