Halifax Women v Derby County Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Halifax Women face Derby County Women on Sunday looking to secure a first win in 13 Women's National League Premier Division – North games.

Halifax have lost five and drawn seven matches since they last earned three points – against Liverpool Feds on September 17.

They face a Derby County side who have won four and drawn three over that time.

With 13 goals in 15 games so far this season, Halifax are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Derby County, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are 10th in the table after 15 games, of which they have won two and drawn eight, earning 14 points.

The away side are three places ahead of Halifax in seventh, with seven wins and three draws putting them on 24 points.

Halifax's last match was on April 7, a 5-1 loss against Wolverhampton Wanderers Women.

Derby County drew 1-1 with Nottingham Forest Women last time out, on March 31.
