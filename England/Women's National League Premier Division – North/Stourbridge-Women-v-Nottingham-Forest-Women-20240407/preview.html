Stourbridge Women v Nottingham Forest Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Stourbridge Women face a challenge against high-flying Nottingham Forest Women on Sunday.

Stourbridge Women are ninth in the Women's National League Premier Division – North table, and have picked up four wins and five draws in their 18 games to date.

Nottingham Forest, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 32 points, having won nine and drawn five of their first 17 matches, and are 13 points behind table-toppers Newcastle United Women.

With 41 goals in 17 games so far this season, Nottingham Forest are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 12 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Stourbridge, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.8 goals per game.

The home team are in terrible form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Stourbridge's two.

Stourbridge's last match was on March 31, a 4-0 loss against Burnley Women.

Nottingham Forest drew 1-1 with Derby County Women last time out, also on March 31.
