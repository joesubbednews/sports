Burnley Women v Huddersfield Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Huddersfield Town Women face a challenge away against high-flying Burnley Women on Sunday.

Huddersfield Town Women are 11th in the Women's National League Premier Division – North table, and have picked up two wins and two draws in their 15 games to date.

Burnley, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 32 points, having won nine and drawn five of their first 16 matches, and are 13 points behind table-toppers Newcastle United Women.

With 13 goals in 15 games so far this season, Huddersfield Town are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

Burnley, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Burnley and Huddersfield Town have played each other on six occasions. Burnley won four of them and they drew twice.

On average, Burnley scored 2.7 goals and Huddersfield Town 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when Burnley won 2-1 at home.

The home team are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Burnley's eight.

Burnley's last match was on March 31, a 4-0 win against Stourbridge Women.

Huddersfield Town drew 0-0 with Wolverhampton Wanderers Women last time out, also on March 31.
