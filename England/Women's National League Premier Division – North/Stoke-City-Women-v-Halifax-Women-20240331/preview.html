Stoke City Women v Halifax Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Halifax Women travel looking to secure a first win in 11 Women's National League Premier Division – North games against Stoke City Women on Sunday.

Halifax have lost four and drawn six matches since they last earned three points – against Liverpool Feds on September 17.

They face a Stoke City side who have won five and drawn two over that time.

With 11 goals in 13 games so far this season, Halifax are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Stoke City, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The visitors are 10th in the table after 13 games, of which they have won two and drawn seven, earning 13 points.

The home team are four places ahead of Halifax in sixth, with seven wins and three draws putting them on 24 points.

Stoke City's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against Burnley Women.

Halifax lost 1-0 against West Brom WFC last time out, on March 17.
