Burnley Women v Huddersfield Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Burnley Women face Huddersfield Town Women on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-3 victory over Newcastle United Women on February 11, Burnley sit third in the Women's National League Premier Division North.

They travel to play a Huddersfield Town side 11th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-1 against Derby County Women.

With 12 goals in 12 games so far this season, Huddersfield Town are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

Burnley, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to Burnley's eight.

In the last 10 years, Burnley and Huddersfield Town have played each other on six occasions. Burnley won four of them and they drew twice.

On average, Burnley scored 2.7 goals and Huddersfield Town 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when Burnley won 2-1 at home.
