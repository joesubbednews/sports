Newcastle United Women v Derby County Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Women's National League Premier Division North's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when table-topping Newcastle United Women host fourth-placed Derby County Women.

Newcastle United have picked up nine wins and three draws from 12 games so far this season, and sit 11 points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Derby County, meanwhile, have won six and drawn one, picking up 19 points.

With 34 goals in 12 games so far this season, Newcastle United are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in four goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

Derby County are also above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in good form in Women's National League Premier Division North, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Newcastle United's 14.

Newcastle United's last match was on January 21, a 0-0 draw against Nottingham Forest Women.

Derby County beat Stourbridge Women 2-0 last time out, also on January 21.
