Burnley Women v Stoke City Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match between Burnley Women and Stoke City Women promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Women's National League Premier Division North's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Burnley and Stoke City sit joint-fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 29 goals apiece.

Burnley are third in the table after 14 games, of which they have won eight and drawn four, earning 28 points.

Stoke City are two places behind the hosts in fifth, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

In the last five years, Burnley and Stoke City have played each other on four occasions. Burnley won all of them.

On average, Burnley scored 3.2 goals and Stoke City 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 9, when Burnley won 7-2 away.

Burnley are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Burnley's eight.

Burnley's last match was on March 10, a 1-1 draw against West Brom WFC.

Stoke City beat Derby County Women 2-0 last time out, on March 13.
