Stoke City Women v Fylde Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North | Norton Cricket Club & Miners Welfare Institute
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Fylde Women come to Norton Cricket Club & Miners Welfare Institute to play Stoke City Women on early Sunday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Stoke City have three wins and a draw.

Fylde are bottom of the table after 14 games, of which they have won none and drawn four, earning four points.

Stoke City are six places ahead of the visitors in sixth, with five wins and two draws putting them on 17 points.

With 13 goals in 14 games so far this season, Fylde are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 3.6 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last five years, Stoke City and Fylde have played each other on four occasions. Fylde won three of them and they drew once.

On average, Stoke City scored 1.5 goals and Fylde 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 19, when Fylde won 2-1 away.

Stoke City's last match was on February 11, a 4-1 win against Stourbridge Women.

Fylde drew 0-0 with Liverpool Feds last time out, also on February 11.
