Wolverhampton Wanderers Women v Halifax Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North | New Bucks Head Ground
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Halifax Women travel to the New Bucks Head Ground looking to secure a first win in 12 Women's National League Premier Division – North games against Wolverhampton Wanderers Women on Sunday.

Halifax have lost four and drawn seven matches since they last earned three points – against Liverpool Feds on September 17.

They face a Wolverhampton Wanderers side who have won five and drawn four over that time.

Halifax are 10th in the table after 14 games, of which they have won two and drawn eight, earning 14 points.

Wolverhampton Wanderers are five places ahead of the away team in fifth, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

With 12 goals in 14 games so far this season, Halifax are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

Wolverhampton Wanderers' last match was on March 31, a 0-0 draw against Huddersfield Town Women.

Halifax drew 1-1 with Stoke City Women last time out, also on March 31.
