Halifax Women v Wolverhampton Wanderers Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Halifax Women face a challenge against high-flying Wolverhampton Wanderers Women on Sunday.

Halifax Women are 10th in the Women's National League Premier Division – North table, and have picked up two wins and 10 draws in their 17 games to date.

Wolverhampton Wanderers, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 32 points, having won nine and drawn five, and are 17 points behind table-toppers Newcastle United Women.

With 13 goals in 17 games so far this season, Halifax are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Wolverhampton Wanderers, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Halifax's three.

Halifax's last match was on April 21, a 0-0 draw against Newcastle United Women.

Wolverhampton Wanderers beat Derby County Women 5-1 last time out, on Wednesday.
