Burnley Women v Derby County Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Burnley Women face Derby County Women on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Women's National League Premier Division North.

Burnley are unbeaten in six, with two wins and four draws, ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a Derby County team who have drawn three and lost three over the same number of games.

With 33 goals in 18 games so far this season, Burnley are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Derby County, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home team are third in the table after 18 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

The visitors are three places behind Burnley in sixth, with seven wins and four draws putting them on 25 points.

In the last five years, Burnley and Derby County have played each other on five occasions. Burnley won four of them and Derby County one.

On average, Burnley scored 2.2 goals and Derby County 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 3, when Burnley won 2-0 away.

Burnley's last match was on April 21, a 0-0 draw against Liverpool Feds.

Derby County lost 5-1 against Wolverhampton Wanderers Women last time out, on April 24.
