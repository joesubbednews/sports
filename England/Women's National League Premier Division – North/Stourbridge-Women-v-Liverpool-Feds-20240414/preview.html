Stourbridge Women v Liverpool Feds

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Stourbridge Women and Liverpool Feds both come into Sunday's early kick-off in poor form, having picked up two and four points respectively in their last six games.

Stourbridge have drawn two and lost four of the last six, while Liverpool Feds have taken four draws and two losses.

Stourbridge are ninth in the table after 19 games, of which they have won four and drawn five, earning 17 points.

Liverpool Feds are one place ahead of the hosts in eighth, with four wins and seven draws putting them on 19 points.

With 23 goals in 19 games so far this season, Stourbridge are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 2.8 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Stourbridge's last match was on April 7, a 2-0 loss against Nottingham Forest Women.

Liverpool Feds drew 0-0 with Newcastle United Women last time out, on March 31.
