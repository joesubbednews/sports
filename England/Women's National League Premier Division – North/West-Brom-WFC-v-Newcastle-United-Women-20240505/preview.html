West Brom WFC v Newcastle United Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Newcastle United Women face West Brom WFC on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Women's National League Premier Division North.

Newcastle United are unbeaten in 21, with 14 wins and seven draws, ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a West Brom WFC team who have won nine and drawn five over the same number of games.

Newcastle United are top of the table after 21 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn seven, earning 49 points.

West Brom WFC are five places behind the visitors in sixth, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

With 46 goals in 21 games so far this season, Newcastle United are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in seven goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

West Brom WFC's last match was on April 21, a 1-0 win against Stoke City Women.

Newcastle United drew 0-0 with Halifax Women last time out, also on April 21.
