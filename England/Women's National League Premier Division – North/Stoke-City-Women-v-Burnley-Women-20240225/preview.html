Stoke City Women v Burnley Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North | Norton Cricket Club & Miners Welfare Institute
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Burnley Women travel to Norton Cricket Club & Miners Welfare Institute for Sunday's early match against Stoke City Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Premier Division North.

Burnley, who sit third in the league after 12 games, fell to a 3-0 home defeat to Newcastle United Women on February 11.

They face a Stoke City side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-1 victory against Stourbridge Women, and who sit seventh in the table.

With 26 goals in 12 games so far this season, Burnley are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Stoke City are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last five years, Stoke City and Burnley have played each other on four occasions. Burnley won all of them.

On average, Stoke City scored 0.8 goals and Burnley 3.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 9, when Burnley won 7-2 away.

The home side are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Stoke City's 10.
