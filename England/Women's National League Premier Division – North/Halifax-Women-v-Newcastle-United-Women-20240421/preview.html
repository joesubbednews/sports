Halifax Women v Newcastle United Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Halifax Women face a challenge against high-flying Newcastle United Women on Sunday.

Halifax Women are 10th in the Women's National League Premier Division – North table, and have picked up two wins and nine draws in their 16 games to date.

Newcastle United, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 48 points, having won 14 and drawn six of their first 20 matches.

With 46 goals in 20 games so far this season, Newcastle United are the league's highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in seven goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

Halifax, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Halifax's three.

Halifax's last match was on April 14, a 0-0 draw against Derby County Women.

Newcastle United beat Huddersfield Town Women 1-0 last time out, also on April 14.
