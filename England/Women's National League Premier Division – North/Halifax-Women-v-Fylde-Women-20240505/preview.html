Halifax Women v Fylde Women

Women's National League Premier Division – North
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as ninth-ranked Halifax Women host bottom of the table Fylde Women.

Halifax have picked up 19 points from their first 19 Women's National League Premier Division – North games, with two wins and 13 draws.

That is 13 points more than Fylde have collected, having won none and drawn six.

Halifax are in bad form in the Women's National League Premier Division North, with no wins and four draws from their last six games.

But with no wins and two draws over that period, Fylde's form is even worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the home team's four.

With 14 goals in 21 games so far this season, Halifax are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 3.3 goals per game.

Halifax's last match was on Wednesday, a 0-0 draw against Wolverhampton Wanderers Women.

Fylde lost 5-0 against Stoke City Women last time out, on April 28.
