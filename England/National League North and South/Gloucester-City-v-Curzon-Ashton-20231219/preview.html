Gloucester City v Curzon Ashton

National League North and South | New Meadow Park
Tuesday, 19 December 2023 @ 7.45pm

Gloucester City host Curzon Ashton in Tuesday's late match at New Meadow Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in National League North and South.

Curzon Ashton are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

City are in disappointing form in National League North and South, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the Tigers' six.

In the last five years, Gloucester and Curzon Ashton have played each other on six occasions. Gloucester won two of them, Curzon Ashton three, and they drew once.

On average, the Tigers scored 1.5 goals and Curzon Ashton 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 11, when Curzon Ashton won 4-3 at home.
