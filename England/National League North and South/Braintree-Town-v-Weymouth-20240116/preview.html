Braintree Town v Weymouth

National League North and South | Cressing Road Stadium
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Braintree Town face a challenge against high-flying Weymouth at the Cressing Road Stadium on Tuesday.

Weymouth, meanwhile, are zero in the standings with 29 points, having won six and drawn 11, and are 30 points behind table-toppers Tamworth.

In the last five years, Braintree and Weymouth have played each other on four occasions. Weymouth won two of them and they drew twice.

On average, the Iron scored 0.2 goals and Weymouth 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 11, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Braintree, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in National League North and South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the Iron's seven.

Braintree's last match was on December 26, a 3-0 loss against Chelmsford City.

Weymouth beat Weston-super-Mare 3-2 last time out, also on December 26, with Brandon Goodship, Harry Parsons and Tom Bearwish on the scoresheet.
