Burton Albion v Cheltenham Town

EFL League One | Pirelli Stadium
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Cheltenham Town make the journey to the Pirelli Stadium to play Burton Albion on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Cheltenham's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six EFL League One matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

Cheltenham are 22nd in the table after 28 games, of which they have won six and drawn five, earning 23 points.

Burton are three places ahead of Town in 19th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

In the last five years, Burton and Cheltenham have played each other on four occasions. Burton won one of them and they drew the other.

On average, the Brewers scored 0.8 goals and the Robins 0.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 1, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

With 21 goals in 28 games so far this season, the Robins are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Albion are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Burton's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 loss against Lincoln City.

Cheltenham lost 3-1 against Wycombe Wanderers last time out, also on Saturday, with Matt Taylor on the scoresheet.
