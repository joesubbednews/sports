Barnsley v Leyton Orient

EFL League One | Oakwell
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leyton Orient come to Oakwell to play Barnsley on Saturday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while the Tykes have three wins and two draws.

With 53 goals in 29 games so far this season, Barnsley are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Orient, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have also conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In Devante Cole, the Tykes have the league's sharpest shooter so far this season. He has notched 17 goals in 29 appearances.

His goal rate of one every 141 minutes is quicker than that of Shaqai Forde, the visitors' top scorer with a goal every 157 minutes, and a total of eight goals in 25 games.

The home team are fifth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn eight, earning 53 points.

Orient are five places behind Barnsley in 10th, with 12 wins and nine draws putting them on 45 points.

Barnsley's last match was on February 3, a 1-1 draw against Bolton Wanderers, with Cole getting the goal for the Tykes.

Orient beat Port Vale 1-0 last time out, on Tuesday, with Idris El Mizouni on the scoresheet.
