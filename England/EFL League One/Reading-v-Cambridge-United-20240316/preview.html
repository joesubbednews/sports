Reading v Cambridge United

EFL League One | Select Car Leasing Stadium
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cambridge United come to the Select Car Leasing Stadium to play Reading on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just one point from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn just one of their last six fixtures, while the Royals have two wins and four losses.

Cambridge are 20th in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn nine, earning 39 points.

Reading are five places ahead of United in 15th, with 12 wins and nine draws putting them on 45 points.

With 32 goals in 37 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The Royals, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Reading's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-1 loss against Derby County, with Sam Smith getting the goal for the Royals.

Cambridge lost 6-0 against Lincoln City last time out, also on Tuesday.
