Burton Albion v Peterborough United

EFL League One | Pirelli Stadium
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Peterborough United visit Burton Albion at the Pirelli Stadium on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in EFL League One.

Peterborough have picked up nine points from their last six games, and they face a Brewers side who drew their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 68 goals in 35 games so far this season, Peterborough are the league's highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Burton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Burton and Peterborough have played each other on 13 occasions. Burton won three of them, Peterborough six, and they drew four times.

On average, the Brewers scored 1.2 goals and the Posh 2.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Peterborough won 4-0 at home.

United are fifth in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn eight, earning 65 points.

Albion are 13 places behind the Posh in 18th, with 10 wins and nine draws putting them on 39 points.

The away side's Harrison Burrows is among the league's most creative players, racking up 10 assists in 35 appearances so far this season, and holding second spot in EFL League One's assist charts.

For the Brewers, Mark Helm has set up the most goals, having laid on three assists in 26 games.

Burton's last match was on March 2, a 0-0 draw against Cheltenham Town.

Peterborough beat Northampton Town 5-1 last time out, on Tuesday, with Ephron Mason-Clark, Hector Kyprianou, Joel Randall, Josh Knight and Kwame Poku on the scoresheet.

Saturday's match will be refereed by Ross Joyce, who has taken charge of 13 EFL League One games so far this season, issuing four red cards and booking 57 players. He has awarded two penalties.

The last Burton game Joyce refereed was a 2-1 away loss to Carlisle United on October 24. His last Peterborough match was their 3-2 win away at Derby County on January 1.
