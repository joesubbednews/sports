Gresley v Walsall Wood

Northern Premier League – Division One Midlands | The Moat Ground
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Gresley face Walsall Wood at the Moat Ground on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-0 victory over AFC Rushden and Diamonds on January 6, Gresley sit 18th in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

They travel to play a Walsall Wood side 14th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 2-2 against Sporting Khalsa, on December 26.

Gresley are in disappointing form in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Walsall Wood's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

With 26 goals in 24 games so far this season, Gresley are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.
