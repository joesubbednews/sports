Spalding United v Anstey Nomads

Northern Premier League – Division One Midlands | Sir Halley Stewart Field
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Northern Premier League Division One Midlands's top two sides face each other at Sir Halley Stewart Field in Saturday's kick-off, when Spalding United host second-placed Anstey Nomads.

Spalding have picked up 16 wins and four draws from 23 games so far this season, and sit six points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Anstey Nomads, meanwhile, have won 13 and drawn seven of 21, picking up 46 points.

With 48 goals in 21 games so far this season, Anstey Nomads are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Spalding are also above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

United are in exceptional form in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to the home side's 18.

Spalding's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-0 win against Rugby Town.

Anstey Nomads drew 1-1 with Lye Town last time out, on January 6.
