Sutton Coldfield Town v Leicester Road

Northern Premier League – Division One Midlands | Central Ground
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leicester Road travel to Sutton Coldfield Town on Saturday in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Bedworth United 2-1 at home.

Sutton Coldfield, meanwhile, drew their last match, 2-2 against Coleshill Town.

Leicester Road are fourth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn seven, earning 43 points.

Sutton Coldfield are three places behind the visitors in seventh, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

With 38 goals in 22 games so far this season, Town are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Leicester Road are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are in reasonable form in Northern Premier League Division One Midlands, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with three wins and two draws over that period, Leicester Road's form is identical – they have both taken 11 points from 18.
