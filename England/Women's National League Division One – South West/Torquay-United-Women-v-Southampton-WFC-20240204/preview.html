Torquay United Women v Southampton WFC

Women's National League Division One – South West | Plainmoor Ground
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match at the Plainmoor Ground sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 11th-ranked Torquay United Women host 10th-placed Southampton WFC.

Torquay United have picked up four points from their first four Women's National League Division One – South West games, with one win and one draw.

That is one point less than Southampton WFC have collected, having won one and drawn two.

Torquay United are in bad form in Women's National League Division One South West, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Southampton WFC's form is slightly better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the hosts' three.

With 10 goals in nine games so far this season, the away side are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

Torquay United are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 3.3 goals per game.

Torquay United's last match was on January 28, a 3-1 loss against Keynsham Town LFC.

Southampton WFC lost 1-0 against Swindon Town last time out, also on January 28.
