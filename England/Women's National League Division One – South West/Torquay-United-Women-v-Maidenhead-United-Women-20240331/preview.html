Torquay United Women v Maidenhead United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Maidenhead United Women travel looking to secure a first win in nine Women's National League Division One – South West games against Torquay United Women on Sunday.

Maidenhead United have lost four and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Selsey Women on January 7.

They face a Torquay United side who have won two and drawn two over that time.

Torquay United are 11th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won three and drawn three, earning 12 points.

Maidenhead United are three places ahead of the home team in eighth, with three wins and six draws putting them on 15 points.

With 24 goals in 18 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Torquay United are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.4. They have conceded 2.7 goals per game.

Torquay United's last match was on Sunday, a 1-1 draw against Southampton WFC.

Maidenhead United drew 0-0 with Portishead LFC last time out, also on Sunday.
