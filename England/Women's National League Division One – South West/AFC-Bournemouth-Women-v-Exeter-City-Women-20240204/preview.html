AFC Bournemouth Women v Exeter City Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Women's National League Division One South West's top two sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when second-placed AFC Bournemouth Women host Exeter City Women.

Bournemouth Women have picked up 10 wins from 10 games so far this season, and sit four points below the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Exeter City, meanwhile, have won 11 and drawn one of 12, picking up 34 points.

With 67 goals in 12 games so far this season, Exeter City are the league's highest scorers with 5.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Bournemouth Women are also above average scorers, with 4.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

The home team are in exceptional form in Women's National League Division One South West, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Bournemouth Women's 18.

In the last five years, Bournemouth Women and Exeter City have played each other on five occasions. Bournemouth Women won one of them, Exeter City three, and they drew once.

On average, Bournemouth Women scored 1.2 goals and Exeter City 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 19, when Bournemouth Women won 2-1 at home.

Bournemouth Women's last match was on January 14, a 3-0 win against Swindon Town.

Exeter City beat Selsey Women 14-0 last time out, on January 21.
