Exeter City Women v Selsey Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Selsey Women face a challenge away against high-flying Exeter City Women on Sunday.

Selsey Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Division One – South West table, and have picked up no wins and no draws in their 11 games to date.

Exeter City, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 31 points, having won 10 and drawn one.

Exeter City are in fantastic form in Women's National League Division One South West, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and six losses over that period, Selsey's form is much worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to the hosts' 16.

With two goals in 11 games so far this season, the away side are the league's lowest scorers with 0.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 81 goals at a rate of 7.4 per game.

Exeter City, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 4.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Over the last two years, Exeter City and Selsey have played each other twice. Exeter City won both of them.

Their last meeting was on April 16, when Exeter City won 4-2 at home.

Exeter City's last match was on January 14, a 8-1 win against Southampton WFC.

Selsey lost 8-0 against Maidenhead United Women last time out, on January 7.
