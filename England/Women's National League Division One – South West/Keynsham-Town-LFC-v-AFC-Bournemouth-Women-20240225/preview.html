Keynsham Town LFC v AFC Bournemouth Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

AFC Bournemouth Women come to play Keynsham Town LFC on early Sunday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Keynsham Town have three wins and a draw.

With 50 goals in 11 games so far this season, Bournemouth Women are the league's third-highest scorers with 4.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in seven goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Keynsham Town are also above average scorers, with 3.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The visitors are second in the table after 11 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn one, earning 31 points.

The home side are three places behind Bournemouth Women in fifth, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

Over the last two years, Keynsham Town and Bournemouth Women have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on January 29 2023, when Keynsham Town won 2-0 away.

Keynsham Town's last match was on February 11, a 5-0 win against Selsey Women.

Bournemouth Women drew 1-1 with Exeter City Women last time out, on February 4.
