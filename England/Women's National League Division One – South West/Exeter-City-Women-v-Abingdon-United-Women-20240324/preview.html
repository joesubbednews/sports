Exeter City Women v Abingdon United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Exeter City Women face Abingdon United Women on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Women's National League Division One South West.

Exeter City are unbeaten in 16, with 13 wins and three draws, ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face an Abingdon United team who have won eight and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 77 goals in 16 games so far this season, Exeter City are the league's highest scorers with 4.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Abingdon United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home team are top of the table after 16 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn three, earning 42 points.

The visitors are four places behind Exeter City in fifth, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

Exeter City's last match was on March 10, a 5-0 win against Maidenhead United Women.

Abingdon United lost 6-0 against AFC Bournemouth Women last time out, also on March 10.
