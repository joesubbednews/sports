Swindon Town v Selsey Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Selsey Women face a challenge away against high-flying Swindon Town on Sunday.

Selsey Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Division One – South West table, and have picked up no wins and no draws in their 16 games to date.

Swindon, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 33 points, having won 10 and drawn three of their first 17 matches, and are nine points behind table-toppers Exeter City Women.

Swindon are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With no wins and six losses over that period, Selsey's form is much worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to Town's 12.

With two goals in 16 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 122 goals at a rate of 7.6 per game.

The home side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Swindon and Selsey have played each other on three occasions. Swindon won two of them and Selsey one.

Their last meeting was on February 4, when Swindon won 15-0 away.

Swindon's last match was on March 13, a 1-0 win against Maidenhead United Women.

Selsey lost 4-0 against Southampton WFC last time out, on March 14.
