Southampton WFC v Swindon Town

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Swindon Town travel for Sunday's early match against Southampton WFC looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Women's National League Division One South West.

Swindon, who sit third in the league after 12 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to AFC Bournemouth Women on January 14.

They face a Southampton WFC side who also lost their last match, a 8-1 defeat to Exeter City Women, and who sit 10th in the table.

With 10 goals in eight games so far this season, Southampton WFC are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 3.5 per game.

Swindon are also below average scorers, with 2.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Southampton WFC and Swindon have played each other on six occasions. Southampton WFC won four of them, Swindon one, and they drew once.

On average, Southampton WFC scored 2.5 goals and Swindon 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when Swindon won 6-2 at home.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Women's National League Division One South West, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Southampton WFC's five.
