Moneyfields Women v Maidenhead United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West | York Road Stadium
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Maidenhead United Women travel to the York Road Stadium looking to secure a first win in 10 Women's National League Division One – South West games against Moneyfields Women on Sunday.

Maidenhead United have lost four and drawn five matches since they last earned three points – against Selsey Women on January 7.

They face a Moneyfields side who have won three and drawn four over that time.

With 61 goals in 16 games so far this season, Moneyfields are scoring more than average in the league with 3.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Maidenhead United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are fifth in the table after 16 games, of which they have won eight and drawn four, earning 28 points.

The away side are three places behind Moneyfields in eighth, with three wins and seven draws putting them on 16 points.

In the last three years, Moneyfields and Maidenhead United have played each other on three occasions. Moneyfields won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 17, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Moneyfields's last match was on March 31, a 1-0 win against Swindon Town.

Maidenhead United drew 0-0 with Torquay United Women last time out, also on March 31.
