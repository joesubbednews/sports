Selsey Women v Swindon Town

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Selsey Women face a challenge against high-flying Swindon Town on Sunday.

Selsey Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Division One – South West table, and have picked up no wins and no draws in their 12 games to date.

Swindon, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 24 points, having won seven and drawn three of their first 13 matches, and are 10 points behind table-toppers Exeter City W.

Selsey are in awful form in Women's National League Division One South West, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Swindon's form is much better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the home side's zero.

With two goals in 12 games so far this season, Selsey are the league's lowest scorers with 0.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 95 goals at a rate of 7.9 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Selsey and Swindon have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on March 12, when Swindon won 2-1 at home.

Selsey's last match was on January 21, a 14-0 loss against Exeter City Women.

Swindon beat Southampton WFC 1-0 last time out, on January 28.
