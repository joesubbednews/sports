Swindon Town v Moneyfields Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match between Swindon Town and Moneyfields Women promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Women's National League Division One South West's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Swindon and Moneyfields sit second and fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 77 and 60 goals respectively.

Swindon are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Moneyfields's form is much worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Town's 15.

The hosts are third in the table after 18 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn three, earning 36 points.

The away team are four places behind Swindon in seventh, with seven wins and four draws putting them on 25 points.

In the last three years, Swindon and Moneyfields have played each other on three occasions. Moneyfields won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when they played out a 3-3 draw.

Swindon's last match was on Sunday, a 28-0 win against Selsey Women.

Moneyfields drew 0-0 with Bridgwater United Women last time out, also on Sunday.
