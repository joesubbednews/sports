Moneyfields Women v Exeter City Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sunday's match between Moneyfields Women and Exeter City Women promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Women's National League Division One South West's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Moneyfields and Exeter City sit second and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 54 and 68 goals respectively.

Exeter City are top of the table after 13 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn two, earning 35 points.

Moneyfields are four places behind the away side in fifth, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Exeter City's form is better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Moneyfields's nine.

In the last three years, Moneyfields and Exeter City have played each other on three occasions. Exeter City won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Exeter City won 5-3 at home.

Moneyfields's last match was on February 4, a 3-0 win against Abingdon United Women.

Exeter City drew 1-1 with AFC Bournemouth Women last time out, also on February 4.
