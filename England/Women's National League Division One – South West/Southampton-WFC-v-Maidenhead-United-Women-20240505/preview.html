Southampton WFC v Maidenhead United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Southampton WFC face Maidenhead United Women on Sunday looking to secure a first win in seven Women's National League Division One – South West games.

Southampton WFC have lost two and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Selsey Women on March 14.

They face a Maidenhead United side who have won one and drawn two over that time.

In the last 10 years, Southampton WFC and Maidenhead United have played each other on six occasions. Southampton WFC won three of them and they drew three times.

On average, Southampton WFC scored 1.8 goals and Maidenhead United 0.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 3, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Southampton WFC are 10th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won three and drawn seven, earning 16 points.

Maidenhead United are two places ahead of the home team in eighth, with four wins and seven draws putting them on 19 points.

With 21 goals in 21 games so far this season, Southampton WFC are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Southampton WFC's last match was on April 21, a 6-0 loss against Moneyfields Women.

Maidenhead United beat Keynsham Town LFC 2-1 last time out, also on April 21.
