Maidenhead United Women v Bridgwater United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West | York Road Stadium
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Maidenhead United Women are on a poor run ahead of hosting Bridgwater United Women at the York Road Stadium on Sunday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

The visitors are two places ahead of Maidenhead United in seventh, with five wins and one draw putting them on 16 points.

Bridgwater United drew 1-1 with Keynsham Town LFC last time out, also on February 4.
