Swindon Town v Portishead LFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Portishead LFC travel to Swindon Town on early Sunday in the Women's National League Division One South West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Maidenhead United Women 2-1 at home.

Swindon also won their last match, 15-0 against Selsey Women.

With 23 goals in 14 games so far this season, Portishead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

Swindon, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 3.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.5. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Town are third in the table after 14 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

The visitors are five places behind the home team in eighth, with four wins and two draws putting them on 14 points.

In the last five years, Swindon and Portishead have played each other on five occasions. Swindon won two of them, Portishead one, and they drew twice.

On average, Swindon scored 2.0 goals and Portishead 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Swindon won 3-2 away.

Swindon are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Portishead's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Swindon's 11.
