Bridgwater United Women v Keynsham Town LFC

Women's National League Division One – South West | Fairfax Park
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Keynsham Town LFC travel to Bridgwater United Women on early Sunday in Women's National League Division One South West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Torquay United Women 3-1 at home.

Bridgwater United, meanwhile, lost their last match, 1-0 against Abingdon United Women.

With 20 goals in 11 games so far this season, Bridgwater United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Keynsham Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 3.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in Women's National League Division One South West, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Bridgwater United's nine.

Keynsham Town are fifth in the table after 10 games, of which they have won six and drawn one, earning 19 points.

Bridgwater United are two places behind Keynsham Town in seventh, with five wins and six losses putting them on 15 points.
