Exeter City Women v Bridgwater United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South West's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when Exeter City Women host Bridgwater United Women.

Only one side – AFC Bournemouth Women – has conceded fewer goals than Exeter City to date: the home side have let in just 13 goals in 18 games.

Bridgwater United have conceded 28 goals in 18 games, giving them the joint-fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Exeter City are top of the table after 18 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn three, earning 48 points.

Bridgwater United are four places behind the home team in fifth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

Exeter City are in good form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with four wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 14 points from 18.

Exeter City's last match was on April 7, a 5-0 win against Keynsham Town LFC.

Bridgwater United beat Southampton WFC 3-0 last time out, on March 31.
