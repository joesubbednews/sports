Southampton WFC v Torquay United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 10th-ranked Southampton WFC host 11th-placed Torquay United Women.

Southampton WFC have picked up 13 points from their first 13 Women's National League Division One – South West games, with three wins and four draws.

That is two points more than Torquay United have collected, having won three and drawn two.

With 20 goals in 16 games so far this season, Southampton WFC are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Torquay United are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 2.8 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Southampton WFC's eight.

Southampton WFC's last match was on March 17, a 0-0 draw against Keynsham Town LFC.

Torquay United lost 2-0 against Bridgwater United Women last time out, on March 14.
