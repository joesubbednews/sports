Portishead LFC v Bridgwater United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Portishead LFC face Bridgwater United Women on Wednesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Maidenhead United Women on February 4, Portishead sit ninth in the Women's National League Division One South West.

They travel to play a Bridgwater United side seventh in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Maidenhead United Women, on February 11.

With 23 goals in 14 games so far this season, Portishead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

Bridgwater United are also below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Portishead's 10.
