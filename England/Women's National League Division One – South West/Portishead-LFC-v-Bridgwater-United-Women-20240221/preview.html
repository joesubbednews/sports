Portishead LFC v Bridgwater United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Portishead LFC host Bridgwater United Women on Wednesday in the Women's National League Division One South West.

In their last league match, on February 4, Portishead beat Maidenhead United Women 2-1 at home.

Bridgwater United drew, 0-0 away at Maidenhead United Women on February 11.

With 21 goals in 13 games so far this season, Bridgwater United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Portishead are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.5. They have conceded 3.2 goals per game.

The home side are eighth in the table after 14 games, of which they have won four and drawn two, earning 14 points.

The visitors are one place ahead of Portishead in seventh, with five wins and two draws putting them on 17 points.

Portishead are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Bridgwater United's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.
