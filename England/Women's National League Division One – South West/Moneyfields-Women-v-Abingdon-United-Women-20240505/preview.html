Moneyfields Women v Abingdon United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Moneyfields Women host Abingdon United Women on early Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Women's National League Division One South West.

Moneyfields have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face an Abingdon United side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 69 goals in 18 games so far this season, Moneyfields are scoring more than average in the league with 3.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Abingdon United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The hosts are fourth in the table after 18 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

The away team are one place behind Moneyfields in fifth, with 10 wins and two draws putting them on 32 points.

Moneyfields's last match was on April 21, a 6-0 win against Southampton WFC.

Abingdon United lost 2-0 against Swindon Town last time out, also on April 21.
