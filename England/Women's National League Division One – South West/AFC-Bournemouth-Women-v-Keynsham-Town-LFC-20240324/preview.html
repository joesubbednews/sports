AFC Bournemouth Women v Keynsham Town LFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South West's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when second-placed AFC Bournemouth Women host fourth-placed Keynsham Town LFC.

Bournemouth Women have picked up 13 wins and one draw from 14 games so far this season, and sit 13 points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Keynsham Town, meanwhile, have won eight and drawn three of 15, picking up 27 points.

With 60 goals in 14 games so far this season, Bournemouth Women are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 4.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in seven goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Keynsham Town are also above average scorers, with 2.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Bournemouth Women's 16.

In the last three years, Bournemouth Women and Keynsham Town have played each other on three occasions. Bournemouth Women won two of them and Keynsham Town one.

Their last meeting was on February 25, when Bournemouth Women won 1-0 away.

Bournemouth Women's last match was on March 17, a 3-0 win against Maidenhead United Women.

Keynsham Town drew 0-0 with Southampton WFC last time out, also on March 17.
