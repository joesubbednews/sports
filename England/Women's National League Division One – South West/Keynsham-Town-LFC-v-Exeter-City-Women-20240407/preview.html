Keynsham Town LFC v Exeter City Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South West's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when Exeter City Women visit Keynsham Town LFC.

Only one side – AFC Bournemouth Women – has conceded fewer goals than Exeter City to date: the away side have let in just 13 goals in 17 games.

Keynsham Town have conceded 26 goals in 16 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Exeter City are top of the table after 17 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn three, earning 45 points.

Keynsham Town are five places behind the away side in sixth, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Exeter City's form is better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Keynsham Town's nine.

In the last three years, Keynsham Town and Exeter City have played each other on three occasions. Exeter City won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Exeter City won 5-2 at home.

Keynsham Town's last match was on March 24, a 1-1 draw against AFC Bournemouth Women.

Exeter City beat Abingdon United Women 3-0 last time out, also on March 24.
