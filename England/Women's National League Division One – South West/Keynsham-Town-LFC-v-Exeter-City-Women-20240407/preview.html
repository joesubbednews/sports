Keynsham Town LFC v Exeter City Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South West's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when fourth-placed Keynsham Town LFC host table-topping Exeter City Women.

Keynsham Town have picked up eight wins and four draws from 16 games so far this season, and sit 17 points below the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Exeter City, meanwhile, have won 14 and drawn three of 17, picking up 45 points.

With 80 goals in 17 games so far this season, Exeter City are the league's highest scorers with 4.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Keynsham Town are also above average scorers, with 2.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.4. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Keynsham Town's nine.

In the last three years, Keynsham Town and Exeter City have played each other on three occasions. Exeter City won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Exeter City won 5-2 at home.

Keynsham Town's last match was on March 24, a 1-1 draw against AFC Bournemouth Women.

Exeter City beat Abingdon United Women 3-0 last time out, also on March 24.
