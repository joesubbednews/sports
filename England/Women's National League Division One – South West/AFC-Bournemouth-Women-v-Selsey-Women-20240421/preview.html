AFC Bournemouth Women v Selsey Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Selsey Women face a challenge away against high-flying AFC Bournemouth Women on Sunday.

Selsey Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Division One – South West table, and have picked up no wins and no draws in their 18 games to date.

Bournemouth Women, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 44 points, having won 14 and drawn two of their first 16 matches, and are four points behind table-toppers Exeter City Women.

Bournemouth Women are in good form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and six losses over that period, Selsey's form is much worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to the hosts' 14.

With two goals in 18 games so far this season, the away team are the league's lowest scorers with 0.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 154 goals at a rate of 8.6 per game.

Bournemouth Women, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 4.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 0.5 goals per game.

In the last three years, Bournemouth Women and Selsey have played each other on three occasions. Bournemouth Women won all of them.

Their last meeting was on August 20, when Bournemouth Women won 13-0 away.

Bournemouth Women's last match was on March 31, a 7-0 win against Portishead LFC.

Selsey lost 4-0 against Abingdon United Women last time out, on April 7.
