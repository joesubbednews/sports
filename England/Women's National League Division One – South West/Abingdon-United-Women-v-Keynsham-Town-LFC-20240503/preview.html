Abingdon United Women v Keynsham Town LFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Friday, 3 May 2024 @ 7.45pm

Keynsham Town LFC make the journey to play Abingdon United Women on late Friday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Keynsham Town's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Women's National League Division One – South West matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

With 38 goals in 20 games so far this season, Abingdon United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Keynsham Town are also below average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are fifth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn two, earning 32 points.

The away side are two places behind Abingdon United in seventh, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Abingdon United's last match was on April 21, a 2-0 loss against Swindon Town.

Keynsham Town lost 2-1 against Maidenhead United Women last time out, also on April 21.
