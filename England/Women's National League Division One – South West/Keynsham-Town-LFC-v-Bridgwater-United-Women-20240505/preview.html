Keynsham Town LFC v Bridgwater United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Keynsham Town LFC welcome Bridgwater United Women on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

The visitors are one place ahead of Keynsham Town in sixth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.
