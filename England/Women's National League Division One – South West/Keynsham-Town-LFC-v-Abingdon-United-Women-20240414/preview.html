Keynsham Town LFC v Abingdon United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Keynsham Town LFC host Abingdon United Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Division One South West.

In their last league match, on April 7, Keynsham Town lost to Exeter City Women 5-0 at home.

Abingdon United won, 4-0 away at Selsey Women.

With 35 goals in 18 games so far this season, Abingdon United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Keynsham Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Keynsham Town's eight.

Keynsham Town are seventh in the table after 17 games, of which they have won eight and drawn four, earning 28 points.

Abingdon United are one place ahead of the home team in sixth, with nine wins and two draws putting them on 29 points.
