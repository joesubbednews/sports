Moneyfields Women v Southampton WFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Southampton WFC face a challenge away against high-flying Moneyfields Women on Sunday.

Southampton WFC are 10th in the Women's National League Division One – South West table, and have picked up three wins and five draws in their 18 games to date.

Moneyfields, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 31 points, having won nine and drawn four of their first 17 matches, and are 17 points behind table-toppers Exeter City Women.

With 63 goals in 17 games so far this season, Moneyfields are scoring more than average in the league with 3.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Southampton WFC, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 2.4 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Moneyfields and Southampton WFC have played each other twice. Southampton WFC won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on March 5 2023, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Moneyfields's 10.

Moneyfields's last match was on April 7, a 2-1 win against Maidenhead United Women.

Southampton WFC lost 3-0 against Bridgwater United Women last time out, on March 31.
