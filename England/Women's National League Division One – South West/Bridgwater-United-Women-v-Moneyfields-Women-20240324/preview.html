Bridgwater United Women v Moneyfields Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Bridgwater United Women host Moneyfields Women on early Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Women's National League Division One South West.

Bridgwater United have picked up 11 points from their last six games, and they face a Moneyfields side who lost their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 60 goals in 14 games so far this season, Moneyfields are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 4.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Bridgwater United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are sixth in the table after 16 games, of which they have won eight and drawn two, earning 26 points.

The away side are one place behind Bridgwater United in seventh, with seven wins and three draws putting them on 24 points.

Bridgwater United's last match was on March 14, a 2-0 win against Torquay United Women.

Moneyfields lost 2-1 against Keynsham Town LFC last time out, on March 10.
