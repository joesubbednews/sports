Abingdon United Women v Moneyfields Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Women's National League Division One South West's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when Abingdon United Women host Moneyfields Women.

Only three sides – AFC Bournemouth Ladies, Exeter City Women and Swindon Town – have conceded fewer goals than Abingdon United to date: the home side have let in just 19 goals in 12 games.

Moneyfields have conceded the same number of goals in 10 games, giving them the joint-fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Abingdon United are fourth in the table after 12 games, of which they have won seven and drawn one, earning 22 points.

Moneyfields are two places behind the hosts in sixth, with five wins and three draws putting them on 18 points.

Abingdon United are in reasonable form in Women's National League Division One South West, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Abingdon United's 12.

Abingdon United's last match was on January 28, a 1-0 win against Bridgwater United Women.

Moneyfields drew 1-1 with Portishead LFC last time out, also on January 28.
