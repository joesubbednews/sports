Torquay United Women v Portishead LFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 11th-ranked Torquay United Women host ninth-placed Portishead LFC.

Torquay United have picked up 13 points from their first 13 Women's National League Division One – South West games, with three wins and four draws.

That is two points less than Portishead have collected, having won four and drawn three.

Torquay United are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Portishead's form is slightly worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the home team's six.

With 26 goals in 19 games so far this season, Torquay United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 3.1 goals per game.

Torquay United's last match was on March 31, a 0-0 draw against Maidenhead United Women.

Portishead lost 7-0 against AFC Bournemouth Women last time out, also on March 31.
