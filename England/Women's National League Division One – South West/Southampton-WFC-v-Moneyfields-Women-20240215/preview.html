Southampton WFC v Moneyfields Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Thursday, 15 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Southampton WFC welcome Moneyfields Women on late Thursday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Southampton WFC's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Women's National League Division One – South West matches, while their opponents have earned nine from a possible 18.

With 55 goals in 12 games so far this season, Moneyfields are the league's second-highest scorers with 4.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Southampton WFC, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 2.5. They have conceded 3.1 goals per game.

The home team are 11th in the table after 11 games, of which they have won one and drawn two, earning five points.

The visitors are five places ahead of Southampton WFC in sixth, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

Over the last two years, Southampton WFC and Moneyfields have played each other twice. Southampton WFC won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on March 5, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Southampton WFC's last match was on Sunday, a 3-0 loss against Abingdon United Women.

Moneyfields lost 4-1 against Exeter City Women last time out, also on Sunday.
