Southampton WFC v Bridgwater United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Southampton WFC welcome Bridgwater United Women on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Southampton WFC's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Women's National League Division One – South West matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

Southampton WFC are 11th in the table after 11 games, of which they have won one and drawn two, earning five points.

Bridgwater United are four places ahead of the home team in seventh, with five wins and two draws putting them on 17 points.

With 11 goals in 11 games so far this season, Southampton WFC are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 3.1 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Southampton WFC's last match was on February 11, a 3-0 loss against Abingdon United Women.

Bridgwater United drew 0-0 with Maidenhead United Women last time out, also on February 11.
