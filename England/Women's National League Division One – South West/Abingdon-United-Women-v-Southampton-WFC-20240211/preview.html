Abingdon United Women v Southampton WFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Abingdon United Women face Southampton WFC on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-3 victory over Moneyfields Women on February 4, Abingdon United sit fourth in the Women's National League Division One South West.

They travel to play a Southampton WFC side 11th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Torquay United Women.

Abingdon United are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Southampton WFC's form is worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the home side's nine.

With 11 goals in 10 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 3.1 per game.

Abingdon United are also below average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.
