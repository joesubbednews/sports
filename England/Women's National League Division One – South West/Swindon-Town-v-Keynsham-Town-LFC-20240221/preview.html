Swindon Town v Keynsham Town LFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Swindon Town host Keynsham Town LFC on Wednesday in the Women's National League Division One South West.

In their last league match, on February 4, Swindon beat Selsey Women 15-0 away.

Keynsham Town also won, 5-0 away at Selsey Women on February 11.

With 46 goals in 14 games so far this season, Swindon are scoring more than average in the league with 3.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Keynsham Town are also above average scorers, with 3.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Town are third in the table after 14 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

The visitors are two places behind the home team in fifth, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

In the last three years, Swindon and Keynsham Town have played each other on three occasions. Swindon won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on September 7, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Swindon are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Keynsham Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Swindon's 11.
