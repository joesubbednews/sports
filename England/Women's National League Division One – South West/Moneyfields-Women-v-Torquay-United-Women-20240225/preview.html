Moneyfields Women v Torquay United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Torquay United Women come to play Moneyfields Women on early Sunday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Moneyfields have two wins and three draws.

With 55 goals in 12 games so far this season, Moneyfields are the league's second-highest scorers with 4.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Torquay United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.5. They have conceded 2.9 goals per game.

The visitors are 10th in the table after 14 games, of which they have won two and drawn two, earning eight points.

The home side are four places ahead of Torquay United in sixth, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

Moneyfields's last match was on February 11, a 4-1 loss against Exeter City Women.

Torquay United drew 0-0 with Exeter City Women last time out, on Thursday.
