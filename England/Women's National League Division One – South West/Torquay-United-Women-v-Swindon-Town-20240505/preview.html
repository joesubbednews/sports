Torquay United Women v Swindon Town

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Torquay United Women face a challenge against high-flying Swindon Town on Sunday.

Torquay United Women are 11th in the Women's National League Division One – South West table, and have picked up three wins and five draws in their 21 games to date.

Swindon, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 40 points, having won 12 and drawn four, and are 17 points behind table-toppers Exeter City Women.

With 79 goals in 21 games so far this season, Swindon are scoring more than average in the league with 3.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Torquay United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Torquay United's six.

Torquay United's last match was on April 21, a 0-0 draw against Portishead LFC.

Swindon beat Abingdon United Women 2-0 last time out, also on April 21.
