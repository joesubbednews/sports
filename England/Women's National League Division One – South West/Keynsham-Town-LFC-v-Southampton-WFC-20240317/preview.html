Keynsham Town LFC v Southampton WFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Southampton WFC face Keynsham Town LFC away on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-0 victory over Selsey Women on Thursday, Southampton WFC sit 10th in the Women's National League Division One South West.

They travel to play a Keynsham Town side fourth in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Moneyfields Women, on March 10.

With 20 goals in 15 games so far this season, Southampton WFC are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

Keynsham Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are in good form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Keynsham Town's 13.

In the last three years, Keynsham Town and Southampton WFC have played each other on three occasions. Keynsham Town won one of them and Southampton WFC the other.

Their last meeting was on September 3, when Keynsham Town won 1-0 away.
