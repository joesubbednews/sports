Keynsham Town LFC v Torquay United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Torquay United Women make the journey to play Keynsham Town LFC on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Torquay United's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Women's National League Division One – South West matches, while their opponents have earned 12 from a possible 18.

Torquay United are 11th in the table after 11 games, of which they have won one and drawn one, earning four points.

Keynsham Town are five places ahead of the visitors in sixth, with five wins and one draw putting them on 16 points.

With 17 goals in 11 games so far this season, Torquay United are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 3.4 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 3.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Keynsham Town's last match was on January 14, a 3-2 win against Portishead LFC.

Torquay United lost 4-2 against Abingdon United Women last time out, also on January 14.
