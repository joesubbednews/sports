Portishead LFC v AFC Bournemouth Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Portishead LFC face a challenge against high-flying AFC Bournemouth Women on Sunday.

Portishead LFC are ninth in the Women's National League Division One – South West table, and have picked up four wins and three draws in their 17 games to date.

Bournemouth Women, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 41 points, having won 13 and drawn two of their first 15 matches, and are four points behind table-toppers Exeter City Women.

With 61 goals in 15 games so far this season, Bournemouth Women are the league's third-highest scorers with 4.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in eight goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Portishead, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.4. They have conceded 2.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Portishead's five.

In the last five years, Portishead and Bournemouth Women have played each other on five occasions. Bournemouth Women won all of them.

On average, Portishead scored 0.8 goals and Bournemouth Women 5.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Bournemouth Women won 4-0 at home.

Portishead's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against Maidenhead United Women.

Bournemouth Women drew 1-1 with Keynsham Town LFC last time out, also on Sunday.
