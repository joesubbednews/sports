Maidenhead United Women v Keynsham Town LFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Maidenhead United Women face Keynsham Town LFC on Sunday looking to secure a first win in 11 Women's National League Division One – South West games.

Maidenhead United have lost five and drawn five matches since they last earned three points – against Selsey Women on January 7.

They face a Keynsham Town side who have won four and drawn three over that time.

With 25 goals in 20 games so far this season, Maidenhead United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Keynsham Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 2.3 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The home team are eighth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won three and drawn seven, earning 16 points.

The visitors are one place ahead of Maidenhead United in seventh, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

In the last three years, Maidenhead United and Keynsham Town have played each other on three occasions. Maidenhead United won one of them and Keynsham Town the other.

Their last meeting was on August 20, when Maidenhead United won 4-3 away.

Maidenhead United's last match was on April 7, a 2-1 loss against Moneyfields Women.

Keynsham Town lost 3-1 against Abingdon United Women last time out, on April 14.
