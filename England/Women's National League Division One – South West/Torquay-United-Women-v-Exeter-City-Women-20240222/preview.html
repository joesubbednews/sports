Torquay United Women v Exeter City Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Thursday, 22 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Torquay United Women host Exeter City Women in Thursday's late match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Division One South West.

Torquay United, who sit 10th in the league after 13 games, fell to a 1-2 home defeat to Southampton WFC on February 4.

They face an Exeter City side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-1 victory against Moneyfields Women, and who sit first in the table.

With 72 goals in 14 games so far this season, Exeter City are the league's highest scorers with 5.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Torquay United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 3.2 goals per game.

The home side are in bad form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Torquay United's three.

Over the last two years, Torquay United and Exeter City have played each other twice. Exeter City won both of them.

Their last meeting was on October 22, when Exeter City won 7-0 at home.
