AFC Bournemouth Women v Torquay United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

AFC Bournemouth Women host Torquay United Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Women's National League Division One South West.

Bournemouth Women, who sit second in the league after 11 games, fell to a 1-1 home defeat to Exeter City Women on February 4.

They face a Torquay United side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against Southampton WFC, and who sit 10th in the table.

With 50 goals in 11 games so far this season, Bournemouth Women are the league's third-highest scorers with 4.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in seven goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Torquay United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 3.2 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in Women's National League Division One South West, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to Bournemouth Women's 16.
