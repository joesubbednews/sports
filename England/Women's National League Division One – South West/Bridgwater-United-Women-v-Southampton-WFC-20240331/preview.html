Bridgwater United Women v Southampton WFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Bridgwater United Women face Southampton WFC on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Women's National League Division One South West.

Bridgwater United are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a Southampton WFC team who have won two and drawn three over the same number of games.

Southampton WFC are 10th in the table after 17 games, of which they have won three and drawn five, earning 14 points.

Bridgwater United are five places ahead of the away side in fifth, with eight wins and three draws putting them on 27 points.

With 21 goals in 17 games so far this season, Southampton WFC are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.4. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Bridgwater United's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against Moneyfields Women.

Southampton WFC drew 1-1 with Torquay United Women last time out, also on Sunday.
