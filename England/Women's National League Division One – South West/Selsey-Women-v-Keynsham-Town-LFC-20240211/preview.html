Selsey Women v Keynsham Town LFC

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Selsey Women welcome Keynsham Town LFC on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their 13-game losing streak.

Selsey's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Women's National League Division One – South West matches, while their opponents have earned 10 from a possible 18.

Selsey are bottom of the table after 13 games, of which they have won none and lost 13, earning no points.

Keynsham Town are six places ahead of the home team in sixth, with six wins and two draws putting them on 20 points.

With two goals in 13 games so far this season, Selsey are the league's lowest scorers with 0.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 110 goals at a rate of 8.5 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

In the last three years, Selsey and Keynsham Town have played each other on three occasions. Selsey won two of them and Keynsham Town one.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Keynsham Town won 10-0 at home.

Selsey's last match was on February 4, a 15-0 loss against Swindon Town.

Keynsham Town drew 1-1 with Bridgwater United Women last time out, also on February 4.
