Exeter City Women v AFC Bournemouth Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

The Women's National League Division One South West's top two sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when Exeter City Women host second-placed AFC Bournemouth Women.

Exeter City have picked up 18 wins and three draws from 21 games so far this season, and sit on the same number of points as the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Bournemouth Women, meanwhile, have won 18 and drawn three, picking up 57 points.

With 106 goals in 21 games so far this season, Exeter City are the league's highest scorers with 5.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Bournemouth Women are also above average scorers, with 4.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 0.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in exceptional form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly worse – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Exeter City's 18.

In the last 10 years, Exeter City and Bournemouth Women have played each other on six occasions. Exeter City won three of them, Bournemouth Women one, and they drew twice.

On average, Exeter City scored 2.5 goals and Bournemouth Women 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 4, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Exeter City's last match was on April 28, a 5-0 win against Portishead LFC.

Bournemouth Women beat Moneyfields Women 2-1 last time out, on April 25.
