Exeter City Women v AFC Bournemouth Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

The Women's National League Division One South West's top two sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when Exeter City Women host second-placed AFC Bournemouth Women.

Exeter City have picked up 16 wins and three draws from 19 games so far this season, and sit four points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Bournemouth Women, meanwhile, have won 15 and drawn two of 17, picking up 47 points.

With 86 goals in 17 games so far this season, Bournemouth Women are the league's second-highest scorers with 5.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in eight goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Exeter City are also above average scorers, with 4.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.3. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with five wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 16 points from 18.

In the last 10 years, Exeter City and Bournemouth Women have played each other on six occasions. Exeter City won three of them, Bournemouth Women one, and they drew twice.

On average, Exeter City scored 2.5 goals and Bournemouth Women 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 4, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Exeter City's last match was on April 21, a 5-0 win against Bridgwater United Women.

Bournemouth Women beat Selsey Women 18-0 last time out, also on April 21.
