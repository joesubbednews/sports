Bridgwater United Women v Swindon Town

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Swindon Town visit Bridgwater United Women on early Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Women's National League Division One South West.

Swindon have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Bridgwater United side who also won their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 47 goals in 15 games so far this season, Swindon are scoring more than average in the league with 3.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Bridgwater United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.4. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Town are third in the table after 15 games, of which they have won nine and drawn three, earning 30 points.

The hosts are four places behind the away team in seventh, with six wins and two draws putting them on 20 points.

Bridgwater United's last match was on February 25, a 3-2 win against Southampton WFC.

Swindon beat Portishead LFC 1-0 last time out, also on February 25.
