AFC Bournemouth Women v Abingdon United Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South West's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when second-placed AFC Bournemouth Women host fourth-placed Abingdon United Women.

Bournemouth Women have picked up 11 wins and one draw from 12 games so far this season, and sit eight points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Abingdon United, meanwhile, have won eight and drawn two of 15, picking up 26 points.

With 51 goals in 12 games so far this season, Bournemouth Women are the league's third-highest scorers with 4.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in seven goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Abingdon United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.4. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Bournemouth Women's 16.

Bournemouth Women's last match was on February 25, a 1-0 win against Keynsham Town LFC.

Abingdon United drew 0-0 with Maidenhead United Women last time out, on February 28.
