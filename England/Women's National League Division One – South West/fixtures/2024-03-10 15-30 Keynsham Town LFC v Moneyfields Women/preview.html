Keynsham Town LFC v Moneyfields Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.30pm

Keynsham Town LFC host Moneyfields Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Division One South West.

In their last league match, on February 25, Keynsham Town lost to AFC Bournemouth Women 1-0 at home.

Moneyfields won, 4-2 at home against Torquay United Women.

With 59 goals in 13 games so far this season, Moneyfields are the league's second-highest scorers with 4.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Keynsham Town are also above average scorers, with 2.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.4. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 13 games, of which they have won seven and drawn three, earning 24 points.

The home side are one place behind Moneyfields in sixth, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

In the last three years, Keynsham Town and Moneyfields have played each other on three occasions. Moneyfields won all of them.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Moneyfields won 8-0 at home.

Keynsham Town are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Moneyfields's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Keynsham Town's 10.
