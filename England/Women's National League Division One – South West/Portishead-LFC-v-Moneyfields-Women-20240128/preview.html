Portishead LFC v Moneyfields Women

Women's National League Division One – South West
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Moneyfields Women travel to Portishead LFC on early Sunday in Women's National League Division One South West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Maidenhead United Women 0-0 away.

Portishead, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-2 against Keynsham Town LFC.

With 50 goals in nine games so far this season, Moneyfields are the league's second-highest scorers with 5.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Portishead, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 3.6 goals per game.

The home side are ninth in the table after 12 games, of which they have won three and drawn one, earning 10 points.

The visitors are four places ahead of Portishead in fifth, with five wins and two draws putting them on 17 points.

Portishead are in mixed form in Women's National League Division One South West, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Moneyfields's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

In the last three years, Portishead and Moneyfields have played each other on three occasions. Moneyfields won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when Moneyfields won 6-1 at home.
