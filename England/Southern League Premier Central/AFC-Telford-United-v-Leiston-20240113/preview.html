AFC Telford United v Leiston

Southern League Premier Central | New Bucks Head Ground
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

AFC Telford United face Leiston on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Southern League Premier Central.

The Bucks are unbeaten in 12, with eight wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Leiston team who have won six and drawn two over the same number of games.

Telford United are sixth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

Leiston are 10 places behind the Bucks in 16th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 32 goals in 22 games so far this season, the away team are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The hosts are also average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Telford United's last match was on January 6, a 1-0 win against Mickleover Sports.

Leiston lost 5-0 against St Ives Town last time out, also on January 6.
