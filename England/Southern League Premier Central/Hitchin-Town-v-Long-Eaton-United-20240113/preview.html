Hitchin Town v Long Eaton United

Southern League Premier Central | Top Field
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Long Eaton United travel to Top Field looking to secure a first win in six Southern League Premier Central games against Hitchin Town on Saturday.

Long Eaton have lost four and drawn one matches since they last earned three points – against Berkhamsted on December 9.

They face a Hitchin side who have won one and drawn two over that time.

Long Eaton are bottom of the table after 24 games, of which they have won two and drawn two, earning eight points.

Hitchin are 14 places ahead of United in eighth, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

With 17 goals in 24 games so far this season, the away team are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 69 goals at a rate of 2.9 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Hitchin's last match was on January 5, a 2-2 draw against Nuneaton Town.

Long Eaton lost 4-1 against Bromsgrove Sporting last time out, on January 6.
