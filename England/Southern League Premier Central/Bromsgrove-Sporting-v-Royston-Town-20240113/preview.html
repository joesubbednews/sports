Bromsgrove Sporting v Royston Town

Southern League Premier Central | The Victoria Ground
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Royston Town visit Bromsgrove Sporting at the Victoria Ground on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Southern League Premier Central.

Royston have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Bromsgrove Sporting side who also won their last match, and have collected nine points from the last possible 18.

Bromsgrove Sporting are 15th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

Royston are two places ahead of the hosts in 13th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

With 30 goals in 24 games so far this season, Bromsgrove Sporting are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Bromsgrove Sporting and Royston have played each other on three occasions. Bromsgrove Sporting won two of them and Royston one.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when Bromsgrove Sporting won 4-0 away.

Bromsgrove Sporting's last match was on January 6, a 4-1 win against Long Eaton United.

Royston beat AFC Sudbury 2-1 last time out, also on January 6.
