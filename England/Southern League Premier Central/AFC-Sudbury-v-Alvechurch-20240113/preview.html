AFC Sudbury v Alvechurch

Southern League Premier Central | The Wardale Williams Stadium
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Alvechurch travel to the Wardale Williams Stadium looking to secure a first win in 11 Southern League Premier Central games against AFC Sudbury on Saturday.

Alvechurch have lost seven and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Stamford on October 14.

They face a Sudbury side who have won five and drawn one over that time.

Alvechurch are 20th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn six, earning 21 points.

Sudbury are two places ahead of the visitors in 18th, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.

With 28 goals in 22 games so far this season, Alvechurch are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

The home team are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Sudbury's last match was on January 6, a 2-1 loss against Royston Town.

Alvechurch drew 2-2 with Barwell last time out, also on January 6.
