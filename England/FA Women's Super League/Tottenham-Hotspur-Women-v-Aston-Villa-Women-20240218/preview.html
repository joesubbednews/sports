Tottenham Hotspur Women v Aston Villa Women

FA Women's Super League | Brisbane Road
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Aston Villa Women travel to Tottenham Hotspur Women on early Sunday in the FA Women's Super League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Bristol City Women 2-2 at home, with goals from Adriana Leon and Jordan Nobbs.

Spurs also drew their last match, 1-1 against Liverpool Women, with their goal scored by Celin Ildhusøy.

In the last 10 years, Tottenham Hotspur and Aston Villa have played each other on 12 occasions. Tottenham Hotspur won five of them, Aston Villa three, and they drew four times.

On average, Spurs scored 1.6 goals and the Villa 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Tottenham Hotspur won 4-2 away.

With 16 goals in 13 games so far this season, Aston Villa are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Tottenham Hotspur are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In Martha Thomas, Spurs have one of the league's sharpest shooters so far this season. She has notched seven goals in 13 appearances, to sit fifth in the scoring charts.

Her goal rate of one every 156 minutes is quicker than that of Rachel Daly, the Villa's top scorer with a goal every 195 minutes, and a total of six goals in 13 games.

The home side are in mixed form in the FA Women's Super League, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.

Aston Villa are eighth in the table after 13 games, of which they have won four and drawn one, earning 13 points.

Tottenham Hotspur are two places ahead of Aston Villa in sixth, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.
