Leicester City WFC v Bristol City Women

FA Women's Super League | King Power Stadium
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bristol City Women come to the King Power Stadium to play Leicester City WFC on Sunday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Leicester City WFC have a win and two draws.

Bristol City are bottom of the table after 13 games, of which they have won one and drawn three, earning six points.

Leicester City WFC are five places ahead of the Robins in seventh, with three wins and four draws putting them on 13 points.

With 15 goals in 13 games so far this season, the away team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Leicester City WFC's last match was on February 4, a 2-0 loss against Manchester City Women.

Bristol City drew 2-2 with Aston Villa Women last time out, on February 3, with Amalie Grønbak Thestrup and Carrie Jones on the scoresheet.
