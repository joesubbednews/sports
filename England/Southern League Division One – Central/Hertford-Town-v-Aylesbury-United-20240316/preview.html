Hertford Town v Aylesbury United

Southern League Division One – Central | Hertingfordbury Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Aylesbury United face Hertford Town away at Hertingfordbury Park on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Hadley on February 24, Aylesbury sit 16th in the Southern League Division One Central.

They travel to play a Hertford side eighth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-2 against Leighton Town, on March 9.

With 43 goals in 26 games so far this season, Aylesbury are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Hertford are also average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Hertford and Aylesbury have played each other on five occasions. Hertford won two of them, Aylesbury one, and they drew twice.

On average, Hertford scored 1.6 goals and Aylesbury 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Hertford won 2-1 away.

Town are in reasonable form in the Southern League Division One Central, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, United's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home team's 12.
