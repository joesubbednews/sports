North Leigh v Biggleswade Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Eynsham Hall Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Leigh welcome Biggleswade Town to Eynsham Hall Park on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their six-game losing streak.

North Leigh's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Southern League Division One – Central matches, while their opponents have earned 15 from a possible 18.

Biggleswade are second in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn six, earning 57 points.

North Leigh are six places behind Town in eighth, with 14 wins and two draws putting them on 44 points.

With 58 goals in 29 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

North Leigh's last match was on April 13, a 4-0 loss against Waltham Abbey.

Biggleswade beat Thame United 1-0 last time out, also on April 13.
