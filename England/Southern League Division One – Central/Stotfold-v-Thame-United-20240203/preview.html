Stotfold v Thame United

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Thame United face Stotfold away on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over AFC Kempston Rovers on January 27, Thame sit 18th in Southern League Division One Central.

They travel to play a Stotfold side 10th in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Barton Rovers, on January 13.

With 27 goals in 22 games so far this season, Thame are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Stotfold, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, United's form is similar – they have both taken nine points from 18.
