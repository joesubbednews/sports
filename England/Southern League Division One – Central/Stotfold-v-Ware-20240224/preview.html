Stotfold v Ware

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stotfold host Ware on Saturday in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on February 17, Stotfold beat Kings Langley 2-1 at home.

Ware drew, 0-0 away at Hadley.

With 50 goals in 25 games so far this season, Ware are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Stotfold are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The visitors are seventh in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn three, earning 39 points.

The home team are four places behind Ware in 11th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

Stotfold are in reasonable form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Ware's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Stotfold's 11.
