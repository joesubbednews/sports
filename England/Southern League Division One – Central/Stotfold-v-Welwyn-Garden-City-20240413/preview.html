Stotfold v Welwyn Garden City

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stotfold host Welwyn Garden City on Saturday in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on April 6, Stotfold beat Hertford Town 3-1 away.

Welwyn Garden also won, 6-1 at home against Kings Langley.

With 63 goals in 27 games so far this season, Welwyn Garden are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Stotfold are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, City's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Stotfold's nine.

The visitors are ninth in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn two, earning 41 points.

Stotfold are one place behind Welwyn Garden in 10th, with 12 wins and five draws putting them on the same number of points.
