Hertford Town v AFC Kempston Rovers

Southern League Division One – Central | Hertingfordbury Park
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

With a win and two draws over that period, Kempston Rovers' form is slightly worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

On average, Hertford scored 2.2 goals and Kempston Rovers 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Hertford won 4-1 away.

Hertford are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.
