Cirencester Town v AFC Dunstable

Southern League Division One – Central | The Corinium Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

AFC Dunstable face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Cirencester Town defence on Saturday.

With 47 goals in 24 games, Dunstable are the joint-second-highest scorers in Southern League Division One Central ahead of the 3pm kick-off at the Corinium Stadium.

They face a Cirencester side who have scored 41 in 23 matches, but conceded only 26 goals, putting them second among the league's tightest defences – only Bedford Town have conceded fewer goals.

Dunstable are fifth in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn two, earning 38 points.

Cirencester are one place behind the away side in sixth, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last three years, Cirencester and Dunstable have played each other on three occasions. Cirencester won two of them and Dunstable one.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Cirencester won 3-2 away.

Town are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, Dunstable's form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.

Cirencester's last match was on February 3, a 1-0 loss against Hadley.

Dunstable lost 3-1 against Bedford Town last time out, also on February 3.
