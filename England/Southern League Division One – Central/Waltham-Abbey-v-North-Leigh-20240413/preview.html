Waltham Abbey v North Leigh

Southern League Division One – Central | Capershotts
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Leigh make the journey to Capershotts to play Waltham Abbey on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

With 60 goals in 33 games so far this season, Waltham Abbey are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

North Leigh, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are fifth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn eight, earning 53 points.

The visitors are two places behind Waltham Abbey in seventh, with 14 wins and two draws putting them on 44 points.

Waltham Abbey's last match was on April 6, a 0-0 draw against Barton Rovers.

North Leigh lost 4-2 against Kidlington last time out, on April 1.
