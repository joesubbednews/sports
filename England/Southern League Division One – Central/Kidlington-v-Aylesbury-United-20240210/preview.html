Kidlington v Aylesbury United

Southern League Division One – Central | Yarnton Road
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match at Yarnton Road sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 17th-ranked Kidlington host 16th-placed Aylesbury United.

Kidlington have picked up 24 points from their first 24 Southern League Division One – Central games, with six wins and six draws.

That is one point less than Aylesbury have collected, having won seven and drawn four.

With 25 goals in 21 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Aylesbury, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

In the last five years, Kidlington and Aylesbury have played each other on five occasions. Aylesbury won three of them and they drew twice.

On average, Kidlington scored 1.2 goals and Aylesbury 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, United's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Kidlington's seven.

Kidlington's last match was on February 3, a 2-0 loss against Waltham Abbey.

Aylesbury lost 3-1 against Biggleswade Town last time out, also on February 3.
