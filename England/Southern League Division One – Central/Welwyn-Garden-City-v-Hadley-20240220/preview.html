Welwyn Garden City v Hadley

Southern League Division One – Central
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Hadley travel to Welwyn Garden City on late Tuesday in the Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Ware 0-0 at home.

Welwyn Garden, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-2 against Barton Rovers.

With 46 goals in 21 games so far this season, Welwyn Garden are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Hadley, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The visitors are 15th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won eight and drawn two, earning 26 points.

City are two places ahead of Hadley in 13th, with nine wins and two draws putting them on 29 points.

The home team are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Hadley's form is slightly better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Welwyn Garden's nine.

Over the last two years, Welwyn Garden and Hadley have played each other twice. Hadley won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on April 10, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
