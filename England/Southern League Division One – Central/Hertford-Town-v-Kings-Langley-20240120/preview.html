Hertford Town v Kings Langley

Southern League Division One – Central | Hertingfordbury Park
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hertford Town are on a poor run ahead of hosting Kings Langley at Hertingfordbury Park on Saturday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Hertford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Kings are seventh in the table after 21 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Town are four places behind the away side in 11th, with eight wins and three draws putting them on 27 points.

Hertford's last match was on January 6, a 4-1 loss against AFC Dunstable.
