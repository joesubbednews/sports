AFC Kempston Rovers v Waltham Abbey

Southern League Division One – Central | Hillgrounds Leisure
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling AFC Kempston Rovers face Waltham Abbey at Hillgrounds Leisure on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over North Leigh on March 9, Kempston Rovers sit bottom of the Southern League Division One Central.

They travel to play a Waltham Abbey side fifth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 6-2 against AFC Dunstable, on February 24.

With 25 goals in 25 games so far this season, Kempston Rovers are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Waltham Abbey, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Kempston Rovers and Waltham Abbey have played each other on five occasions. Kempston Rovers won one of them and Waltham Abbey the other.

On average, Kempston Rovers scored 1.2 goals and Waltham Abbey 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Waltham Abbey won 3-1 at home.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Kempston Rovers' nine.
