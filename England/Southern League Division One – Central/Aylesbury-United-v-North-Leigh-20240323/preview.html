Aylesbury United v North Leigh

Southern League Division One – Central | ASM Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Leigh come to the ASM Stadium to play Aylesbury United on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just one point from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn just one of their last six fixtures, while Aylesbury have two wins and two draws.

Aylesbury are 16th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

North Leigh are 11 places ahead of United in fifth, with 14 wins and two draws putting them on 44 points.

With 49 goals in 27 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

In the last three years, Aylesbury and North Leigh have played each other on three occasions. Aylesbury won one of them and North Leigh the other.

Their last meeting was on September 2, when Aylesbury won 3-2 away.

Aylesbury's last match was on March 16, a 2-2 draw against Hertford Town.

North Leigh lost 3-1 against Ware last time out, also on March 16.
