AFC Dunstable v Kidlington

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

AFC Dunstable host Kidlington on Saturday in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on Tuesday, Dunstable beat Thame United 2-1 at home.

Kidlington drew, 1-1 at home against Bedford Town on Wednesday.

With 26 goals in 22 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Dunstable, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The home team are fifth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn three, earning 39 points.

The visitors are 10 places behind Dunstable in 15th, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.

In the last five years, Dunstable and Kidlington have played each other on five occasions. Dunstable won three of them, Kidlington one, and they drew once.

On average, Dunstable scored 1.6 goals and Kidlington 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Dunstable won 3-2 away.

Dunstable are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and two draws over that period, Kidlington's form is identical – they have both taken eight points from 18.
