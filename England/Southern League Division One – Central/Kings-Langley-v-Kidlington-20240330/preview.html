Kings Langley v Kidlington

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kidlington come to play Kings Langley on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Kings have two wins and four losses.

With 27 goals in 27 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Kings Langley, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The away team are 17th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won seven and drawn seven, earning 28 points.

Kings are eight places ahead of Kidlington in ninth, with 10 wins and nine draws putting them on 39 points.

Kings Langley's last match was on March 23, a 2-0 loss against Biggleswade Town.

Kidlington lost 1-0 against AFC Kempston Rovers last time out, also on March 23.
