Aylesbury United v Cirencester Town

Southern League Division One – Central | ASM Stadium
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Aylesbury United host Cirencester Town on Saturday at the ASM Stadium in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on February 3, Aylesbury lost to Biggleswade Town 3-1 at home.

Cirencester drew, 2-2 at home against AFC Dunstable on February 10.

Aylesbury are 17th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won seven and drawn four, earning 25 points.

Cirencester are 10 places ahead of United in seventh, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

With 40 goals in 24 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Aylesbury and Cirencester have played each other on three occasions. Cirencester won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Cirencester won 2-1 at home.

Aylesbury are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken eight points from 18.
