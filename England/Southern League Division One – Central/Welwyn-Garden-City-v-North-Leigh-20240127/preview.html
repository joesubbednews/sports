Welwyn Garden City v North Leigh

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Leigh travel to Welwyn Garden City on Saturday in Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Leighton Town 2-1 away.

Welwyn Garden, meanwhile, lost their last match, 4-3 against Thame United.

North Leigh are top of the table after 20 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn one, earning 40 points.

Welwyn Garden are 12 places behind the visitors in 13th, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

North Leigh are also above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are in reasonable form in Southern League Division One Central, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, North Leigh's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Welwyn Garden's 12.

In the last three years, Welwyn Garden and North Leigh have played each other on three occasions. Welwyn Garden won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Welwyn Garden won 2-1 away.
