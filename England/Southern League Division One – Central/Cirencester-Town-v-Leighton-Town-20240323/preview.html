Cirencester Town v Leighton Town

Southern League Division One – Central | The Corinium Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cirencester Town welcome Leighton Town to the Corinium Stadium on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Cirencester's struggles have left them with five points from their last six Southern League Division One – Central matches, while their opponents have earned nine from a possible 18.

With 49 goals in 29 games so far this season, Cirencester are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Leighton, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Cirencester Town are seventh in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

Leighton Town are five places behind the home team in 12th, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

Cirencester's last match was on March 16, a 2-1 loss against Stotfold.

Leighton lost 2-0 against Biggleswade last time out, also on March 16.
