Welwyn Garden City v Leighton Town

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leighton Town travel to Welwyn Garden City on Saturday in the Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Hertford Town 3-1 away.

Welwyn Garden also won their last match, 2-1 against AFC Kempston Rovers.

With 44 goals in 20 games so far this season, Welwyn Garden are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Leighton, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

City are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home side's nine.

Welwyn Garden are 12th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won nine and drawn two, earning 29 points.

The visitors are three places ahead of Welwyn Garden in ninth, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.
