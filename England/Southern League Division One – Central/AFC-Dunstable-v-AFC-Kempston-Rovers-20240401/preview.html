AFC Dunstable v AFC Kempston Rovers

Southern League Division One – Central
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates AFC Kempston Rovers face a challenge away against high-flying AFC Dunstable on Monday.

AFC Kempston Rovers are rooted to the bottom of the Southern League Division One – Central table, and have picked up seven wins and five draws in their 28 games to date.

Dunstable, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 52 points, having won 16 and drawn four of their first 31 matches.

With 27 goals in 28 games so far this season, Kempston Rovers are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Dunstable, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Southern League Division One Central, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Dunstable's 13.

In the last five years, Dunstable and Kempston Rovers have played each other on five occasions. Dunstable won four of them and they drew once.

On average, Dunstable scored 2.2 goals and Kempston Rovers 0.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 1, when Dunstable won 2-0 away.

Dunstable's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 win against Stotfold.

Kempston Rovers lost 3-1 against Cirencester Town last time out, also on Saturday.
