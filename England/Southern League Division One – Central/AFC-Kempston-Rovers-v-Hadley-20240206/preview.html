AFC Kempston Rovers v Hadley

Southern League Division One – Central | Hillgrounds Leisure
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Hadley travel to AFC Kempston Rovers on late Tuesday in Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Cirencester Town 1-0 at home.

Kempston Rovers, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Welwyn Garden City.

Kempston Rovers are bottom of the table after 20 games, of which they have won three and drawn four, earning 13 points.

Hadley are four places ahead of the home team in 15th, with eight wins and one draw putting them on 25 points.

Kempston Rovers are in disappointing form in Southern League Division One Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Kempston Rovers' five.

With 19 goals in 20 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Hadley are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Kempston Rovers and Hadley have played each other twice. Hadley won both of them.

Their last meeting was on March 11, when Hadley won 3-2 at home.
