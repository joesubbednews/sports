Leighton Town v Barton Rovers

Southern League Division One – Central | Bell Close
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Leighton Town host Barton Rovers on Tuesday at Bell Close in Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on January 27, Leighton beat Hertford Town 3-1 away.

Barton also won, 2-1 at home against Ware on Saturday.

Barton are 14th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won six and drawn eight, earning 26 points.

Leighton are five places ahead of Rovers in ninth, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

With 35 goals in 22 games so far this season, the away side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Barton's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Leighton's seven.
