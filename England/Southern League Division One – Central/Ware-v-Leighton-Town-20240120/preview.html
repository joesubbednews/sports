Ware v Leighton Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Wodson Park
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leighton Town travel to Ware on Saturday in Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to North Leigh 2-1 at home.

Ware also lost their last match, 4-2 against AFC Kempston Rovers.

With 42 goals in 20 games so far this season, Ware are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Leighton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Ware's six.

The away team are eighth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

Ware are two places behind Leighton in 10th, with nine wins and two draws putting them on the same number of points.
