Thame United v Waltham Abbey

Southern League Division One – Central | ASM Stadium
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Thame United face Waltham Abbey at the ASM Stadium on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Stotfold on Saturday, Thame sit 18th in Southern League Division One Central.

They travel to play a Waltham Abbey side fourth in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-0 against Kidlington.

With 29 goals in 23 games so far this season, Thame are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Waltham Abbey, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Thame and Waltham Abbey have played each other on four occasions. Thame won two of them, Waltham Abbey one, and they drew once.

On average, Thame scored 2.0 goals and Waltham Abbey 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 1, when Thame won 3-1 away.

United are in reasonable form in Southern League Division One Central, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home team's 12.
