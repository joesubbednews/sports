Biggleswade v Hertford Town

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Biggleswade host Hertford Town on Saturday in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on February 3, Biggleswade beat Kings Langley 2-0 at home.

Hertford also won, 3-1 at home against AFC Kempston Rovers on February 10.

Biggleswade are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Hertford's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

With 38 goals in 23 games so far this season, Town are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding at an average rate too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Biggleswade are also average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Biggleswade and Hertford have played each other on five occasions. Biggleswade won three of them, Hertford one, and they drew once.

On average, Biggleswade scored 2.6 goals and Hertford 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Hertford won 2-0 at home.

Biggleswade are 11th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn four, earning 31 points.

The away team are two places ahead of the home side in ninth, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.
