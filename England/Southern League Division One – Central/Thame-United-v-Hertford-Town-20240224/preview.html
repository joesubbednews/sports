Thame United v Hertford Town

Southern League Division One – Central | ASM Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Thame United welcome Hertford Town to the ASM Stadium on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Thame's struggles have left them with nine points from their last six Southern League Division One – Central matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

Thame are 18th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won seven and drawn three, earning 24 points.

Hertford are eight places ahead of United in 10th, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.

With 32 goals in 25 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Thame and Hertford have played each other on five occasions. Thame won one of them, Hertford two, and they drew twice.

On average, Thame scored 1.6 goals and Hertford 2.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Hertford won 5-1 at home.

Thame's last match was on February 17, a 3-1 loss against Leighton Town.

Hertford beat AFC Kempston Rovers 3-1 last time out, on February 10.
