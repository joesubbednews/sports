Leighton Town v Biggleswade Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Bell Close
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leighton Town host Biggleswade Town on Saturday at Bell Close in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on Monday, Leighton lost to Barton Rovers 2-0 away.

Biggleswade also lost, 1-0 at home against Bedford Town.

With 56 goals in 27 games so far this season, Biggleswade are scoring more than average in the league with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Leighton, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Biggleswade Town are fifth in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn six, earning 51 points.

Leighton Town are six places behind the away side in 11th, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Biggleswade's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Leighton's seven.
