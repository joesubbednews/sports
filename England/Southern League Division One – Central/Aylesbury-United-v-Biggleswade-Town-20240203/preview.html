Aylesbury United v Biggleswade Town

Southern League Division One – Central | ASM Stadium
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Biggleswade Town face Aylesbury United on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Southern League Division One Central.

Biggleswade are unbeaten in eight, with four wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face an Aylesbury team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

Biggleswade are third in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn five, earning 38 points.

Aylesbury are 11 places behind Town in 14th, with seven wins and four draws putting them on 25 points.

With 43 goals in 20 games so far this season, the away side are scoring more than average in the league with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

In the last three years, Aylesbury and Biggleswade have played each other on three occasions. Biggleswade won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Biggleswade won 2-1 at home.

Aylesbury's last match was on January 27, a 2-1 win against Bedford Town.

Biggleswade drew 2-2 with AFC Dunstable last time out, also on January 27.
