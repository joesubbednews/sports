Biggleswade Town v Thame United

Southern League Division One – Central | The Carlsberg Stadium
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Thame United face a challenge away against high-flying Biggleswade Town at the Carlsberg Stadium on Saturday.

Thame United are 18th in the Southern League Division One – Central table, and have picked up nine wins and four draws in their 31 games to date.

Biggleswade, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 54 points, having won 16 and drawn six of their first 28 matches, and are three points behind table-toppers Ware.

With 41 goals in 31 games so far this season, Thame are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Biggleswade, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Town are in reasonable form in the Southern League Division One Central, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, United's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' 12.

In the last three years, Biggleswade and Thame have played each other on three occasions. Biggleswade won two of them and Thame one.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Thame won 1-0 at home.

Biggleswade's last match was on April 6, a 1-0 win against Leighton Town.

Thame drew 3-3 with AFC Dunstable last time out, also on April 6.
