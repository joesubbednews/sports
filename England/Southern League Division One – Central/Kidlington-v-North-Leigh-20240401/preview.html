Kidlington v North Leigh

Southern League Division One – Central | Yarnton Road
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kidlington and North Leigh both come into Monday's kick-off at Yarnton Road in poor form, having picked up four and one points respectively in their last six games.

Kidlington have won one and drawn one of the last six, while North Leigh have taken just one draw.

Kidlington are 17th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won seven and drawn eight, earning 29 points.

North Leigh are 10 places ahead of the hosts in seventh, with 14 wins and two draws putting them on 44 points.

With 27 goals in 28 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Kidlington and North Leigh have played each other on five occasions. They won two each, and they drew once.

On average, Kidlington scored 1.0 goal and North Leigh 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 1, when North Leigh won 3-0 at home.

Kidlington's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Kings Langley.

North Leigh lost 3-0 against Biggleswade last time out, also on Saturday.
