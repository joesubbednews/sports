Barton Rovers v Leighton Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Sharpenhoe Road
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Barton Rovers face Leighton Town on Monday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Southern League Division One Central.

Barton are unbeaten in seven, with four wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Leighton team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

Barton are 15th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won eight and drawn 10, earning 34 points.

Leighton are five places ahead of Rovers in 10th, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

With 41 goals in 26 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Barton's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Hertford Town.

Leighton lost 2-1 against Hadley last time out, also on Saturday.
