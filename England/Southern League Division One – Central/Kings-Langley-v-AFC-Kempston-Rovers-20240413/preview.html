Kings Langley v AFC Kempston Rovers

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

AFC Kempston Rovers make the journey to play Kings Langley on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Kempston Rovers' struggles have left them with seven points from their last six Southern League Division One – Central matches, while their opponents have earned four from a possible 18.

Kempston Rovers are bottom of the table after 30 games, of which they have won seven and drawn five, earning 26 points.

Kings Langley are seven places ahead of the away team in 12th, with 10 wins and 10 draws putting them on 40 points.

With 28 goals in 30 games so far this season, Kempston Rovers are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 59 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Kings, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Kings Langley's last match was on April 6, a 6-1 loss against Welwyn Garden City.

Kempston Rovers lost 5-0 against Aylesbury United last time out, also on April 6.
