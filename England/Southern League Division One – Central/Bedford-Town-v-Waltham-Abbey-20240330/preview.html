Bedford Town v Waltham Abbey

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bedford Town host Waltham Abbey in Saturday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Southern League Division One Central.

Bedford, who sit second in the league after 28 games, fell to a 4-2 away defeat to Ware on March 23.

They face a Waltham Abbey side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against Stotfold, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 55 goals in 28 games so far this season, Bedford are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Waltham Abbey are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

In the last three years, Bedford and Waltham Abbey have played each other on three occasions. Bedford won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on October 17, when they played out a 0-0 draw.
