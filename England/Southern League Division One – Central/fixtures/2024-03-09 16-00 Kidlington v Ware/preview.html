Kidlington v Ware

Southern League Division One – Central | Yarnton Road
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kidlington face Ware at Yarnton Road on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Southern League Division One – Central games.

Kidlington have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Cirencester Town on January 13.

They face a Ware side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

With 26 goals in 24 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Ware, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The home team are 17th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won six and drawn seven, earning 25 points.

The visitors are 11 places ahead of Kidlington in sixth, with 13 wins and three draws putting them on 42 points.

In the last five years, Kidlington and Ware have played each other on four occasions. Kidlington won one of them, Ware two, and they drew once.

On average, Kidlington scored 1.8 goals and Ware 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 4 2023, when Kidlington won 4-1 at home.

Kidlington's last match was on February 24, a 4-0 loss against Biggleswade Town.

Ware beat Cirencester Town 1-0 last time out, on March 2.
