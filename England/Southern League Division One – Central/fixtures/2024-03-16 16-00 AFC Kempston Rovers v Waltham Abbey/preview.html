AFC Kempston Rovers v Waltham Abbey

Southern League Division One – Central | Hillgrounds Leisure
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

AFC Kempston Rovers host Waltham Abbey on Saturday at Hillgrounds Leisure in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on February 27, Kempston Rovers lost to Bedford Town 2-0 away.

Waltham Abbey also lost, 6-2 at home against AFC Dunstable on February 24.

Kempston Rovers are bottom of the table after 24 games, of which they have won five and drawn four, earning 19 points.

Waltham Abbey are 14 places ahead of the home side in fifth, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

With 23 goals in 24 games so far this season, Kempston Rovers are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Kempston Rovers are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Waltham Abbey's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

In the last five years, Kempston Rovers and Waltham Abbey have played each other on five occasions. Kempston Rovers won one of them and Waltham Abbey the other.

On average, Kempston Rovers scored 1.2 goals and Waltham Abbey 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Waltham Abbey won 3-1 at home.
