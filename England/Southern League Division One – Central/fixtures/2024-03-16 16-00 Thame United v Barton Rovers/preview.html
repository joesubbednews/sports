Thame United v Barton Rovers

Southern League Division One – Central | ASM Stadium
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Thame United welcome Barton Rovers to the ASM Stadium on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Thame's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Southern League Division One – Central matches, while their opponents have earned 11 from a possible 18.

Thame are 18th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won seven and drawn three, earning 24 points.

Barton are four places ahead of United in 14th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last five years, Thame and Barton have played each other on five occasions. Thame won two of them, Barton one, and they drew twice.

On average, Thame scored 1.2 goals and Barton 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

With 32 goals in 26 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Rovers, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Thame's last match was on February 24, a 1-0 loss against Hertford Town.

Barton beat Welwyn Garden City 3-2 last time out, on February 17.
