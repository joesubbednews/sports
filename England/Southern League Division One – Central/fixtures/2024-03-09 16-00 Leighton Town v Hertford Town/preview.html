Leighton Town v Hertford Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Bell Close
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leighton Town host Hertford Town on Saturday at Bell Close in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on February 17, Leighton beat Thame United 3-1 at home.

Hertford lost, 2-1 at home against Welwyn Garden City on March 2.

With 41 goals in 23 games so far this season, Leighton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Hertford, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Leighton Town are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Hertford Town's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

The away side are eighth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn three, earning 39 points.

Leighton are three places behind Hertford in 11th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.
