North Leigh v Hertford Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Eynsham Hall Park
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Leigh host Hertford Town in Saturday's match at Eynsham Hall Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Southern League Division One Central.

North Leigh, who sit top of the league after 22 games, fell to a 4-1 home defeat to Bedford Town on Tuesday.

They face a Hertford side who also lost their last match, a 3-1 defeat to Leighton Town, and who sit 12th in the table.

With 46 goals in 22 games so far this season, North Leigh are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Hertford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Town's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to North Leigh's 10.

In the last three years, North Leigh and Hertford have played each other on three occasions. North Leigh won one of them and Hertford the other.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Hertford won 2-1 at home.
