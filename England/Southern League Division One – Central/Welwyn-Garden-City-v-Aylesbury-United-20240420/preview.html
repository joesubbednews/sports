Welwyn Garden City v Aylesbury United

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Welwyn Garden City host Aylesbury United on Saturday in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on April 13, Welwyn Garden lost to Stotfold 3-1 away.

Aylesbury drew, 0-0 at home against Ware.

With 64 goals in 28 games so far this season, Welwyn Garden are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 59 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Aylesbury, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

United are 16th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn six, earning 36 points.

City are four places ahead of the visitors in 12th, with 13 wins and two draws putting them on 41 points.

In the last five years, Welwyn Garden and Aylesbury have played each other on five occasions. Welwyn Garden won four of them and Aylesbury one.

On average, Welwyn Garden scored 2.8 goals and Aylesbury 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when Aylesbury won 3-2 at home.

The home side are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Aylesbury's form is slightly better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Welwyn Garden's nine.
