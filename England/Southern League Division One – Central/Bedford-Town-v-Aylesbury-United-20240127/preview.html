Bedford Town v Aylesbury United

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Aylesbury United face a challenge away against high-flying Bedford Town on Saturday.

Aylesbury United are 16th in the Southern League Division One – Central table, and have picked up six wins and four draws in their 22 games to date.

Bedford, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 39 points, having won 11 and drawn six of their first 19 matches, and are one point behind table-toppers North Leigh.

Aylesbury, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.4 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, United's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the home team's nine.

In the last five years, Bedford and Aylesbury have played each other on five occasions. Bedford won two of them and they drew three times.

On average, Bedford scored 1.2 goals and Aylesbury 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Bedford's last match was on January 13, a 3-1 win against Kings Langley.

Aylesbury drew 0-0 with AFC Kempston Rovers last time out, on Tuesday.
