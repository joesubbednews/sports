Barton Rovers v Ware

Southern League Division One – Central | Sharpenhoe Road
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ware travel to Barton Rovers on Saturday in Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Biggleswade 1-0 at home.

Barton, meanwhile, drew their last match, 2-2 against Kings Langley.

With 48 goals in 22 games so far this season, Ware are the league's highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Barton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Rovers are 16th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

The away team are nine places ahead of the hosts in seventh, with 11 wins and two draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last five years, Barton and Ware have played each other on five occasions. Barton won one of them and Ware the other.

On average, Barton scored 0.8 goals and Ware 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Ware won 3-1 at home.

Barton are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Ware's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Barton's seven.
