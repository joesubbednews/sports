Kidlington v Hertford Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Yarnton Road
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hertford Town come to Yarnton Road to play Kidlington on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just one point from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn just one of their last six fixtures, while Kidlington have two wins and two draws.

Kidlington are 17th in the table after 32 games, of which they have won nine and drawn nine, earning 36 points.

Hertford are three places ahead of the home side in 14th, with 12 wins and five draws putting them on 41 points.

With 36 goals in 32 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last five years, Kidlington and Hertford have played each other on five occasions. Kidlington won one of them, Hertford three, and they drew once.

On average, Kidlington scored 1.6 goals and Hertford 2.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 19, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Kidlington's last match was on April 20, a 1-1 draw against Biggleswade.

Hertford lost 3-2 against Cirencester Town last time out, also on April 20.
