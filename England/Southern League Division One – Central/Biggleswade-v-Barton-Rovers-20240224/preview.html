Biggleswade v Barton Rovers

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Biggleswade host Barton Rovers on Saturday in the Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on February 3, Biggleswade beat Kings Langley 2-0 at home.

Barton also won, 3-2 at home against Welwyn Garden City on February 17.

Biggleswade are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Barton's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

Rovers are 14th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won seven and drawn eight, earning 29 points.

Biggleswade are two places ahead of the visitors in 12th, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

With 38 goals in 23 games so far this season, Barton are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Biggleswade are also average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Biggleswade and Barton have played each other on five occasions. Biggleswade won four of them and they drew once.

On average, Biggleswade scored 2.2 goals and Barton 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Biggleswade won 2-1 away.
