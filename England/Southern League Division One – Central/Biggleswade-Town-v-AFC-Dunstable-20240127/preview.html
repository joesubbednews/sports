Biggleswade Town v AFC Dunstable

Southern League Division One – Central | The Carlsberg Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of Southern League Division One Central's top sides face each other at the Carlsberg Stadium in Saturday's kick-off, when third-placed Biggleswade Town host fourth-placed AFC Dunstable.

Biggleswade have picked up 11 wins and four draws from 19 games so far this season, and sit on the same number of points as the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Dunstable, meanwhile, have won 12 and drawn one of 22, picking up 37 points.

With 41 goals in 19 games so far this season, Biggleswade are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Dunstable are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have also conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Town are in good form in Southern League Division One Central, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home team's 14.

In the last three years, Biggleswade and Dunstable have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Dunstable won 1-0 at home.

Biggleswade's last match was on January 13, a 2-1 win against Biggleswade.

Dunstable beat Hadley 2-1 last time out, on January 20.
