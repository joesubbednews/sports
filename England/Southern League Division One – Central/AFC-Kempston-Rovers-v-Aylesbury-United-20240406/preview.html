AFC Kempston Rovers v Aylesbury United

Southern League Division One – Central | Hillgrounds Leisure
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match at Hillgrounds Leisure sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table AFC Kempston Rovers host 17th-placed Aylesbury United.

Kempston Rovers have picked up 26 points from their first 26 Southern League Division One – Central games, with seven wins and five draws.

That is six points less than Aylesbury have collected, having won nine and drawn five.

With 28 goals in 29 games so far this season, Kempston Rovers are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Aylesbury, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

In the last five years, Kempston Rovers and Aylesbury have played each other on five occasions. Kempston Rovers won one of them, Aylesbury three, and they drew once.

On average, Kempston Rovers scored 1.4 goals and Aylesbury 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 23, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

The home team are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, United's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.

Kempston Rovers' last match was on Monday, a 2-1 loss against AFC Dunstable.

Aylesbury lost 2-1 against Thame United last time out, also on Monday.
