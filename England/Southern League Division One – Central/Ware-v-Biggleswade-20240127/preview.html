Ware v Biggleswade

Southern League Division One – Central | Wodson Park
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Biggleswade travel to Wodson Park looking to secure a first win in six Southern League Division One – Central games against Ware on Saturday.

Biggleswade have lost two and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against AFC Kempston Rovers on December 23.

They face a Ware side who have won just two and lost three over that time.

With 47 goals in 21 games so far this season, Ware are the league's highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Biggleswade, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Ware and Biggleswade have played each other on five occasions. Biggleswade won two of them and they drew three times.

On average, Ware scored 1.4 goals and Biggleswade 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Biggleswade won 4-0 at home.

The hosts are eighth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn two, earning 32 points.

The away side are three places behind Ware in 11th, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

Ware's last match was on January 20, a 5-1 win against Leighton Town, with getting the goals for Ware.

Biggleswade drew 0-0 with Kings Langley last time out, on Tuesday.
