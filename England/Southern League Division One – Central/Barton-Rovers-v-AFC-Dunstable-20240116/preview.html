Barton Rovers v AFC Dunstable

Southern League Division One – Central | Sharpenhoe Road
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

AFC Dunstable travel to Barton Rovers on late Tuesday in Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Aylesbury United 2-1 away.

Dunstable, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have also conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Rovers are 17th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won four and drawn seven, earning 19 points.

The visitors are 12 places ahead of the home side in fifth, with 11 wins and one draw putting them on 34 points.

In the last five years, Barton and Dunstable have played each other on four occasions. Dunstable won all of them.

Their last meeting was on December 3 2022, when Dunstable won 4-0 at home.

Barton are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Dunstable's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Barton's seven.
