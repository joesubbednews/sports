Barton Rovers v Hadley

Southern League Division One – Central | Sharpenhoe Road
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hadley visit Barton Rovers at Sharpenhoe Road on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Southern League Division One Central.

Hadley have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Barton side who lost their last match, and have collected nine points from the last possible 18.

Barton are 15th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won nine and drawn 11, earning 38 points.

Hadley are five places ahead of Rovers in 10th, with 13 wins and three draws putting them on 42 points.

With 43 goals in 29 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last three years, Barton and Hadley have played each other on three occasions. Barton won one of them and Hadley the other.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when Barton won 3-1 away.

Barton's last match was on April 13, a 3-0 loss against Cirencester Town.

Hadley beat Biggleswade 2-1 last time out, also on April 13.
