Biggleswade v Kings Langley

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Biggleswade face Kings Langley on Saturday looking to secure a first win in seven Southern League Division One – Central games.

Biggleswade have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against AFC Kempston Rovers on December 23.

They face a Kings Langley side who have won one and drawn four over that time.

With 45 goals in 23 games so far this season, Kings Langley are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Biggleswade, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Kings are eighth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won eight and drawn nine, earning 33 points.

The hosts are three places behind the away team in 11th, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

Biggleswade's last match was on January 27, a 1-0 loss against Ware.

Kings Langley drew 2-2 with Barton Rovers last time out, also on January 27, with on the scoresheet.
