Waltham Abbey v Stotfold

Southern League Division One – Central | Capershotts
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stotfold visit Waltham Abbey at Capershotts on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Southern League Division One Central.

Stotfold have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Waltham Abbey side who drew their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 48 goals in 26 games so far this season, Stotfold are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Waltham Abbey are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The home team are sixth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn seven, earning 43 points.

The visitors are four places behind Waltham Abbey in 10th, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

Waltham Abbey's last match was on March 16, a 0-0 draw against AFC Kempston Rovers.

Stotfold beat Cirencester Town 2-1 last time out, also on March 16.
