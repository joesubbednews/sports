AFC Kempston Rovers v Leighton Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Hillgrounds Leisure
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling AFC Kempston Rovers face Leighton Town at Hillgrounds Leisure on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Biggleswade Town on Tuesday, Kempston Rovers sit bottom of the Southern League Division One Central.

They travel to play a Leighton side eighth in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-1 against Thame United, on February 17.

With 23 goals in 23 games so far this season, Kempston Rovers are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Leighton, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.

Over the last two years, Kempston Rovers and Leighton have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Kempston Rovers won 3-1 away.
