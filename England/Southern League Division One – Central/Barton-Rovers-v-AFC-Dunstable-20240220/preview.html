Barton Rovers v AFC Dunstable

Southern League Division One – Central | Sharpenhoe Road
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

AFC Dunstable travel to Barton Rovers on late Tuesday in the Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Kidlington 2-0 at home.

Barton also won their last match, 3-2 against Welwyn Garden City.

With 51 goals in 26 games so far this season, Dunstable are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Barton, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn three, earning 42 points.

Rovers are nine places behind Dunstable in 14th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last five years, Barton and Dunstable have played each other on four occasions. Dunstable won all of them.

Their last meeting was on December 3 2022, when Dunstable won 4-0 at home.

The home side are in reasonable form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with three wins and two draws over that period, Dunstable's form is identical – they have both taken 11 points from 18.
