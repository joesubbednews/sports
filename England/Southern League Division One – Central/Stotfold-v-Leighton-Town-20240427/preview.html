Stotfold v Leighton Town

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leighton Town come to play Stotfold on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Stotfold have three wins and three losses.

With 61 goals in 32 games so far this season, Stotfold are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Leighton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Town are 15th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

The hosts are eight places ahead of the away team in seventh, with 14 wins and five draws putting them on 47 points.

Stotfold's last match was on April 20, a 4-1 win against AFC Kempston Rovers.

Leighton drew 1-1 with Bedford Town last time out, also on April 20.
