Kidlington v Stotfold

Southern League Division One – Central | Yarnton Road
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stotfold face Kidlington on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Southern League Division One Central.

Stotfold are unbeaten in five, with two wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Kidlington team who have won just two and lost three over the same number of games.

With 25 goals in 19 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Stotfold, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are 14th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won six and drawn five, earning 23 points.

The away team are five places ahead of Kidlington in ninth, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Kidlington's last match was on January 13, a 1-0 win against Cirencester Town.

Stotfold beat Barton Rovers 1-0 last time out, also on January 13.
