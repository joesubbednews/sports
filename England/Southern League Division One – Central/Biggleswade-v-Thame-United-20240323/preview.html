Biggleswade v Thame United

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Thame United make the journey to play Biggleswade on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Thame's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Southern League Division One – Central matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

Thame are 18th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won seven and drawn three, earning 24 points.

Biggleswade are five places ahead of United in 13th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

With 33 goals in 27 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Biggleswade and Thame have played each other on five occasions. Biggleswade won two of them, Thame one, and they drew twice.

On average, Biggleswade scored 1.6 goals and Thame 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 26, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Biggleswade's last match was on March 16, a 2-0 win against Leighton Town.

Thame lost 3-1 against Barton Rovers last time out, also on March 16.
