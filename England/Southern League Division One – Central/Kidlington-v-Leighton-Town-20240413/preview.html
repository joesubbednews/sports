Kidlington v Leighton Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Yarnton Road
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leighton Town make the journey to Yarnton Road to play Kidlington on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Leighton's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Southern League Division One – Central matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

Kidlington are 17th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won eight and drawn eight, earning 32 points.

Leighton are four places ahead of the home side in 13th, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

With 31 goals in 30 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Kidlington's last match was on April 6, a 2-0 loss against Ware.

Leighton lost 1-0 against Biggleswade Town last time out, also on April 6.
