Hertford Town v Cirencester Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Hertingfordbury Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hertford Town are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Cirencester Town at Hertingfordbury Park on Saturday, with just two points collected from their last six games.

Hertford have picked up two draws and four losses in their last six Southern League Division One – Central games, and face a Cirencester side whose last six games have brought two wins and two draws.

Cirencester, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Cirencester Town are sixth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn nine, earning 48 points.

Hertford Town are seven places behind the away team in 13th, with 12 wins and five draws putting them on 41 points.

In the last three years, Hertford and Cirencester have played each other on three occasions. Hertford won one of them and Cirencester the other.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Cirencester won 3-1 at home.

Hertford's last match was on April 13, a 7-0 loss against Bedford Town.

Cirencester beat Barton Rovers 3-0 last time out, also on April 13.
