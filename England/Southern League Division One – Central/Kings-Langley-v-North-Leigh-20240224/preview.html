Kings Langley v North Leigh

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Leigh travel for Saturday's match against Kings Langley looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Southern League Division One Central.

North Leigh, who sit second in the league after 23 games, fell to a 3-1 home defeat to Hertford Town on February 3.

They face a Kings Langley side who also lost their last match, a 2-1 defeat to Stotfold, and who sit ninth in the table.

With 47 goals in 23 games so far this season, North Leigh are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Kings Langley are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Kings are in bad form in the Southern League Division One Central, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's three.
