Leighton Town v Biggleswade

Southern League Division One – Central | Bell Close
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leighton Town host Biggleswade at Bell Close on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Southern League Division One Central.

Leighton have picked up nine points from their last six games, and they face a Biggleswade side who lost their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

With 44 goals in 24 games so far this season, Leighton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Biggleswade are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The away side are 13th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won nine and drawn four, earning 31 points.

Town are four places ahead of Biggleswade in ninth, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

Leighton's last match was on March 9, a 3-2 win against Hertford Town.

Biggleswade lost 5-4 against Welwyn Garden City last time out, on February 28.
