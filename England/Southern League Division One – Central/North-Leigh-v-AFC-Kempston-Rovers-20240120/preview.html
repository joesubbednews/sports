North Leigh v AFC Kempston Rovers

Southern League Division One – Central | Eynsham Hall Park
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Leigh host AFC Kempston Rovers in Saturday's match at Eynsham Hall Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Southern League Division One Central.

North Leigh, who sit top of the league after 20 games, fell to a 1-2 away defeat to Leighton Town on January 13.

They face a Kempston Rovers side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-2 victory against Ware, and who sit 19th in the table.

With 18 goals in 17 games so far this season, Kempston Rovers are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

North Leigh, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to North Leigh's 10.

In the last three years, North Leigh and Kempston Rovers have played each other on three occasions. North Leigh won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when North Leigh won 2-1 away.
