Hadley v Cirencester Town

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cirencester Town travel to Hadley on Saturday in Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Waltham Abbey 4-1 at home.

Hadley, meanwhile, drew their last match, 0-0 against Kidlington.

Hadley are 17th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won seven and drawn one, earning 22 points.

Cirencester are 12 places ahead of the home side in fifth, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

With 28 goals in 20 games so far this season, Hadley are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last three years, Hadley and Cirencester have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Cirencester won 3-0 at home.

Hadley are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
