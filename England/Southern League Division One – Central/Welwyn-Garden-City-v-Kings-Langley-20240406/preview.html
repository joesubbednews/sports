Welwyn Garden City v Kings Langley

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Welwyn Garden City and Kings Langley promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Southern League Division One Central's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Welwyn Garden and Kings sit joint-fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 57 goals apiece.

Welwyn Garden are 13th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn two, earning 38 points.

Kings Langley are three places ahead of City in 10th, with 10 wins and 10 draws putting them on 40 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Kings's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Welwyn Garden's nine.

Welwyn Garden's last match was on Monday, a 3-2 loss against Hadley.

Kings Langley lost 3-0 against Ware last time out, also on Monday.
