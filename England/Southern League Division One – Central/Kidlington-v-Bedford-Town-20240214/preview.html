Kidlington v Bedford Town

Southern League Division One – Central | Yarnton Road
Wednesday, 14 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Kidlington face a challenge against high-flying Bedford Town at Yarnton Road on Wednesday.

Kidlington are 17th in the Southern League Division One – Central table, and have picked up six wins and six draws in their 21 games to date.

Bedford, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 45 points, having won 13 and drawn six of their first 22 matches.

With 25 goals in 21 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Bedford, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Kidlington's seven.

In the last three years, Kidlington and Bedford have played each other on three occasions. Bedford won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when Bedford won 5-1 at home.

Kidlington's last match was on February 3, a 2-0 loss against Waltham Abbey.

Bedford beat AFC Dunstable 3-1 last time out, also on February 3.
