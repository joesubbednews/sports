Ware v Waltham Abbey

Southern League Division One – Central | Wodson Park
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Ware and Waltham Abbey promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Southern League Division One Central's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Wodson Park, Ware and Waltham Abbey sit second and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 49 and 50 goals respectively.

Waltham Abbey are fourth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

Ware are three places behind the away team in seventh, with 11 wins and two draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last five years, Ware and Waltham Abbey have played each other on five occasions. Ware won three of them and Waltham Abbey two.

On average, Ware scored 1.8 goals and Waltham Abbey 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Waltham Abbey won 4-1 at home.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Waltham Abbey's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Ware's nine.

Ware's last match was on February 3, a 2-1 loss against Barton Rovers.

Waltham Abbey beat Thame United 4-2 last time out, on Tuesday.
