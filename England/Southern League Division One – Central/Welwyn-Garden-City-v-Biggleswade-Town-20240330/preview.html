Welwyn Garden City v Biggleswade Town

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Biggleswade Town visit Welwyn Garden City on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Southern League Division One Central.

Biggleswade have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Welwyn Garden side who lost their last match, and have collected 12 points from the last possible 18.

Biggleswade are top of the table after 26 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn six, earning 51 points.

Welwyn Garden are 11 places behind Town in 12th, with 12 wins and two draws putting them on 38 points.

With 55 goals in 25 games so far this season, City are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The visitors are also above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Welwyn Garden and Biggleswade have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 24, when Biggleswade won 4-3 at home.

Welwyn Garden's last match was on March 23, a 1-0 loss against AFC Dunstable.

Biggleswade beat Kings Langley 2-0 last time out, also on March 23.
