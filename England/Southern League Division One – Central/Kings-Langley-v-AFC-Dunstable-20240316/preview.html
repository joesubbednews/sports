Kings Langley v AFC Dunstable

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Kings Langley and AFC Dunstable promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Southern League Division One Central's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Kings and Dunstable sit joint-third and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 53 and 57 goals respectively.

Dunstable are third in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn three, earning 45 points.

Kings Langley are seven places behind the visitors in 10th, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

Kings are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Dunstable's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

Kings Langley's last match was on February 27, a 4-2 loss against Hertford Town.

Dunstable beat Waltham Abbey 6-2 last time out, on February 24.
