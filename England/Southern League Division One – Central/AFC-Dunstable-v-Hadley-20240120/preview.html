AFC Dunstable v Hadley

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hadley visit AFC Dunstable on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Southern League Division One Central.

Dunstable, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have also conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The home team are fifth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn one, earning 34 points.

The visitors are 10 places behind Dunstable in 15th, with seven wins and 11 losses putting them on 21 points.

In the last three years, Dunstable and Hadley have played each other on three occasions. Hadley won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Hadley won 1-0 at home.

Dunstable's last match was on January 13, a 2-1 loss against Aylesbury United.

Hadley beat Waltham Abbey 4-3 last time out, also on January 13.
