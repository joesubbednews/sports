Aylesbury United v AFC Kempston Rovers

Southern League Division One – Central | ASM Stadium
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling AFC Kempston Rovers face Aylesbury United away at the ASM Stadium on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-2 victory over Ware on January 13, Kempston Rovers sit bottom of Southern League Division One Central.

They travel to play an Aylesbury side 17th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 4-2 against Barton Rovers, on Saturday.

Aylesbury are in bad form in Southern League Division One Central, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

And also with a win and a draw over that period, Kempston Rovers' form is identical – they have both taken four points from 18.

With 18 goals in 17 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

United, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

In the last five years, Aylesbury and Kempston Rovers have played each other on four occasions. Aylesbury won three of them and Kempston Rovers one.

On average, Aylesbury scored 2.2 goals and Kempston Rovers 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 18, when Aylesbury won 2-1 away.
