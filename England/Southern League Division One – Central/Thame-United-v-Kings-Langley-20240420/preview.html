Thame United v Kings Langley

Southern League Division One – Central | ASM Stadium
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kings Langley travel to Thame United on Saturday in the Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten AFC Kempston Rovers 6-2 at home.

Thame, meanwhile, lost their last match, 1-0 against Biggleswade Town.

With 41 goals in 32 games so far this season, Thame are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Kings Langley, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

United are 18th in the table after 32 games, of which they have won nine and drawn four, earning 31 points.

Kings are nine places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with 11 wins and 10 draws putting them on 43 points.

Thame are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.
