Thame United v Ware

Southern League Division One – Central | ASM Stadium
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Thame United face Ware at the ASM Stadium on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Biggleswade on March 23, Thame sit 18th in the Southern League Division One Central.

They travel to play a Ware side fourth in the standings, who also won their last match, 4-2 against Bedford Town.

With 35 goals in 28 games so far this season, Thame are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Ware, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.9 goals per game.

United are in bad form in the Southern League Division One Central, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' three.

In the last five years, Thame and Ware have played each other on five occasions. Ware won three of them and they drew twice.

On average, Thame scored 1.2 goals and Ware 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 23, when they played out a 4-4 draw.
