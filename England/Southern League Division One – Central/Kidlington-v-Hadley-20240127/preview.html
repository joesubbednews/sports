Kidlington v Hadley

Southern League Division One – Central | Yarnton Road
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hadley travel to Kidlington on Saturday in Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to AFC Dunstable 2-1 away.

Kidlington, meanwhile, won their last match, 1-0 against Cirencester Town.

Hadley are 17th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won seven and lost 12, earning 21 points.

Kidlington are three places ahead of the visitors in 14th, with six wins and five draws putting them on 23 points.

The home team are in disappointing form in Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Hadley's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Kidlington's six.

In the last three years, Kidlington and Hadley have played each other on three occasions. Kidlington won all of them.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Kidlington won 2-0 away.

With 25 goals in 19 games so far this season, Kidlington are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Hadley are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.
