Ware v AFC Dunstable

Southern League Division One – Central | Wodson Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Ware and AFC Dunstable promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Southern League Division One Central's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Wodson Park, Ware and Dunstable sit second and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 66 and 67 goals respectively.

Ware are top of the table after 34 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn four, earning 58 points.

Dunstable are four places behind the home side in fifth, with 17 wins and five draws putting them on 56 points.

Ware are in fantastic form in the Southern League Division One Central, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Ware's 16.

In the last 10 years, Ware and Dunstable have played each other on six occasions. Ware won five of them and Dunstable one.

On average, Ware scored 2.0 goals and Dunstable 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when Ware won 3-1 away.

Ware's last match was on April 13, a 0-0 draw against Aylesbury United.

Dunstable drew 3-3 with Thame United last time out, on April 6.
