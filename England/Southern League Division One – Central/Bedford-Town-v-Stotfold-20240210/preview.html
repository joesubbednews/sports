Bedford Town v Stotfold

Southern League Division One – Central
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stotfold travel to Bedford Town on Saturday in the Southern League Division One Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Thame United 2-0 at home.

Bedford, meanwhile, won their last match, 3-1 against AFC Dunstable.

Bedford are top of the table after 22 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn six, earning 45 points.

Stotfold are 12 places behind Town in 13th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

With 46 goals in 22 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring more than average in the league with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Bedford are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Stotfold's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Bedford's 10.
