Chipstead v Southall

Isthmian League – Division One South Central | High Road
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Southall visit Chipstead at High Road on late Tuesday on the back of five consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Southall have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Chipstead side who also won their last match, and have collected three points from the last possible 18.

With 51 goals in 21 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Chipstead, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The away team are second in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn three, earning 48 points.

The hosts are 14 places behind Southall in 16th, with six wins and two draws putting them on 20 points.

Chipstead's last match was on January 1, a 1-0 win against Corinthian-Casuals.

Southall beat Ashford Town Middlesex 2-1 last time out, on Wednesday.
