Leatherhead v Raynes Park Vale

Isthmian League – Division One South Central | Fetcham Grove
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Leatherhead host Raynes Park Vale in Tuesday's late match at Fetcham Grove looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Leatherhead, who sit fourth in the league after 20 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to Westfield Surrey on January 1.

They face a Vale side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with South Park, and who sit third in the table.

With 37 goals in 19 games so far this season, Vale are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Leatherhead, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four draws over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Leatherhead's 13.
