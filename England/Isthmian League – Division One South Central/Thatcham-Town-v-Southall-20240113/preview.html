Thatcham Town v Southall

Isthmian League – Division One South Central | Waterside Park
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Southall visit Thatcham Town at Waterside Park on Saturday on the back of six consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

It means Southall have picked up the maximum 18 points from their last six games, and they face a Thatcham side who have also won their last two matches, and collected 11 points from the last possible 18.

Southall are top of the table after 22 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn three, earning 51 points.

Thatcham are six places behind the visitors in seventh, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

With 52 goals in 22 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Town are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Thatcham's last match was on January 6, a 5-1 win against Ascot United.

Southall beat Chipstead 1-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
