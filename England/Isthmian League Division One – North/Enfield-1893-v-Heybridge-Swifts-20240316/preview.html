Enfield 1893 v Heybridge Swifts

Isthmian League Division One – North
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Heybridge Swifts travel to Enfield 1893 on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One North.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten New Salamis 2-1 at home.

Enfield 1893, meanwhile, lost their last match, 1-0 against Maldon and Tiptree.

With 24 goals in 22 games so far this season, Enfield 1893 are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Heybridge Swifts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home side are 16th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn four, earning 19 points.

The visitors are 10 places ahead of Enfield 1893 in sixth, with 13 wins and five draws putting them on 44 points.

Enfield 1893 are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Heybridge Swifts's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.
