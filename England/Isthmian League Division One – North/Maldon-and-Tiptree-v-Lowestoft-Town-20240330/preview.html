Maldon and Tiptree v Lowestoft Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Wallace Binder Stadium
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lowestoft Town travel to Maldon and Tiptree on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One North.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Brentwood Town 1-0 away.

The Jammers also won their last match, 1-0 against Enfield 1893.

With 63 goals in 23 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Maldon & Tiptree are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 23 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn two, earning 53 points.

The Jammers are seven places behind Trawler Boys in eighth, with 12 wins and three draws putting them on 39 points.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Maldon & Tiptree's 12.

In the last three years, Maldon & Tiptree and Lowestoft have played each other on three occasions. Maldon & Tiptree won one of them and Lowestoft the other.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Lowestoft won 2-0 at home.
