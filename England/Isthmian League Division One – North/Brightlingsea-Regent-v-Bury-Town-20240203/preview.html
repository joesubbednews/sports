Brightlingsea Regent v Bury Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | North Road
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brightlingsea Regent welcome Bury Town to North Road on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Brightlingsea Regent's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – North matches, while their opponents have earned 16 from a possible 18.

With 51 goals in 21 games so far this season, Bury are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Brightlingsea Regent are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are sixth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn five, earning 38 points.

The home team are three places behind the visitors in ninth, with 10 wins and one draw putting them on 31 points.

Brightlingsea Regent's last match was on January 27, a 2-0 loss against Maldon and Tiptree.

Bury beat Stowmarket Town 4-1 last time out, also on January 27.
