Witham Town v Gorleston

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Village Glass Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Witham Town host Gorleston on Saturday at the Village Glass Stadium in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on February 3, Witham lost to Basildon United 4-0 away.

Gorleston drew, 1-1 away at Bowers and Pitsea on February 11.

With 31 goals in 23 games so far this season, Witham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Gorleston are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's six.

Witham are 13th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won six and drawn six, earning 24 points.

Gorleston are two places ahead of Witham in 11th, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.

In the last three years, Witham and Gorleston have played each other on three occasions. Witham won one of them and Gorleston the other.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Gorleston won 4-0 at home.
