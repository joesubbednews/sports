Bowers and Pitsea v Lowestoft Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Len Salmon Stadium
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Isthmian League Division One North's top sides face each other at the Len Salmon Stadium in Saturday's kick-off, when third-placed Bowers and Pitsea host table-topping Lowestoft Town.

Bowers & Pitsea have picked up 14 wins and four draws from 24 games so far this season, and sit seven points below the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Trawler Boys, meanwhile, have won 17 and drawn two of 23, picking up 53 points.

With 63 goals in 23 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Bowers & Pitsea are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Bowers & Pitsea's 12.

Bowers & Pitsea's last match was on Sunday, a 1-1 draw against Gorleston.

Lowestoft beat Brentwood Town 1-0 last time out, on February 6.
