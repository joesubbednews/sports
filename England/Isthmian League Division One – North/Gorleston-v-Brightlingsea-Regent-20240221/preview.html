Gorleston v Brightlingsea Regent

Isthmian League Division One – North
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Brightlingsea Regent make the journey to play Gorleston on late Wednesday looking to pick up points to end their seven-game losing streak.

Brightlingsea Regent's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – North matches, while their opponents have earned 11 from a possible 18.

With 29 goals in 21 games so far this season, Gorleston are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Brightlingsea Regent, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are 11th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won seven and drawn six, earning 27 points.

The away side are one place ahead of Gorleston in 10th, with 10 wins and one draw putting them on 31 points.

Gorleston's last match was on February 11, a 1-1 draw against Bowers and Pitsea.

Brightlingsea Regent lost 2-0 against Felixstowe and Walton Utd. last time out, on February 13.
