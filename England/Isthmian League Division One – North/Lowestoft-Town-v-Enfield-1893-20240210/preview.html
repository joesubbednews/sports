Lowestoft Town v Enfield 1893

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Enfield 1893 travel to Lowestoft Town on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One North.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Maldon and Tiptree 1-0 at home.

Trawler Boys, meanwhile, won their last match, 1-0 against Brentwood Town.

With 63 goals in 23 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Enfield 1893, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 23 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn two, earning 53 points.

The away side are 15 places behind Trawler Boys in 16th, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.

The hosts are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Enfield 1893's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Lowestoft's 13.
