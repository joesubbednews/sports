Maldon and Tiptree v Redbridge

Isthmian League Division One – North | Wallace Binder Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Redbridge come to the Wallace Binder Stadium to play Maldon and Tiptree on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while the Jammers have four wins and two losses.

With 45 goals in 23 games so far this season, Maldon & Tiptree are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Redbridge, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The visitors are 15th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won five and drawn five, earning 20 points.

The Jammers are seven places ahead of Redbridge in eighth, with 12 wins and three draws putting them on 39 points.

Maldon & Tiptree's last match was on February 3, a 1-0 win against Enfield 1893.

Redbridge lost 1-0 against Felixstowe and Walton Utd. last time out, also on February 3.
