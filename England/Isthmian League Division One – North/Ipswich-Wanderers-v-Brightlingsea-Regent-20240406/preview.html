Ipswich Wanderers v Brightlingsea Regent

Isthmian League Division One – North
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brightlingsea Regent make the journey to play Ipswich Wanderers on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their seven-game losing streak.

Brightlingsea Regent's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – North matches, while their opponents have earned six from a possible 18.

The away side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.
