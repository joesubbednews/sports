Lowestoft Town v Witham Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Lowestoft Town host Witham Town in Tuesday's late match at Crown Meadow looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One North.

Trawler Boys, who sit top of the league after 19 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to Felixstowe and Walton Utd. on January 13.

They face a Witham side who also lost their last match, a 2-0 defeat to Bowers and Pitsea, and who sit 14th in the table.

With 55 goals in 19 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Witham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Lowestoft Town are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Witham Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Trawler Boys' nine.

Over the last two years, Lowestoft and Witham have played each other twice. Lowestoft won both of them.

Their last meeting was on February 25, when Lowestoft won 4-0 at home.
