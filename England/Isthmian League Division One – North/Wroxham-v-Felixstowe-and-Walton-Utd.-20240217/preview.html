Wroxham v Felixstowe and Walton Utd.

Isthmian League Division One – North | Trafford Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Felixstowe and Walton Utd. come to Trafford Park to play Wroxham on Saturday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Wroxham have a win and two draws.

With 56 goals in 24 games so far this season, Felixstowe & Walton Utd. are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Wroxham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The away side are second in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn five, earning 50 points.

The hosts are 12 places behind Felixstowe & Walton Utd. in 14th, with five wins and seven draws putting them on 22 points.

In the last three years, Wroxham and Felixstowe & Walton Utd. have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 21, when Wroxham won 2-0 away.

Wroxham's last match was on February 6, a 2-0 loss against Waltham Forest.

Felixstowe & Walton Utd. beat Brightlingsea Regent 2-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
