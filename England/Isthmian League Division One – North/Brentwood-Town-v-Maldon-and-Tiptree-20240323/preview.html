Brentwood Town v Maldon and Tiptree

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Brentwood Centre Arena
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brentwood Town host Maldon and Tiptree in Saturday's match at the Brentwood Centre Arena looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Brentwood, who sit fourth in the league after 24 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Lowestoft Town on February 6.

They face a Maldon & Tiptree side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Enfield 1893, and who sit eighth in the table.

With 45 goals in 23 games so far this season, Maldon & Tiptree are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Brentwood are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

In the last 10 years, Brentwood and Maldon & Tiptree have played each other on six occasions. Brentwood won three of them, Maldon & Tiptree one, and they drew twice.

On average, Brentwood scored 2.0 goals and the Jammers 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 13, when Brentwood won 3-1 away.

Town are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the Jammers's form is slightly worse – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.
