Redbridge v Brentwood Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Oakside Stadium
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brentwood Town face Redbridge on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian League Division One North.

Brentwood are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Redbridge team who have won just one and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 36 goals in 20 games so far this season, Brentwood are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Redbridge, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Town are fifth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn six, earning 36 points.

The hosts are 10 places behind the away team in 15th, with four wins and five draws putting them on 17 points.

Redbridge's last match was on January 13, a 1-1 draw against Wroxham.

Brentwood beat Maldon and Tiptree 3-1 last time out, also on January 13.
