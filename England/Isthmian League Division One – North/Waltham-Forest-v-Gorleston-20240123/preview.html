Waltham Forest v Gorleston

Isthmian League Division One – North | Wadham Lodge
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Gorleston face Waltham Forest on Tuesday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian League Division One North.

Gorleston are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Forest team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 26 goals in 19 games so far this season, Gorleston are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Forest are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The home team are 10th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

The visitors are one place behind Forest in 11th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

Forest's last match was on Saturday, a 2-2 draw against New Salamis.

Gorleston beat Enfield 1893 3-0 last time out, on January 14.
