New Salamis v Ipswich Wanderers

Isthmian League Division One – North | Coles Park
Friday, 23 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Ipswich Wanderers come to Coles Park to play New Salamis on late Friday in poor form, having collected just three points from their last six games.

The visitors have won just one of their last six fixtures, while New Salamis have three wins and a draw.

Ipswich are 18th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won five and drawn one, earning 16 points.

New Salamis are six places ahead of Wanderers in 12th, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.

With 18 goals in 21 games so far this season, the away team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

New Salamis's last match was on February 9, a 3-2 win against Grays Athletic.

Ipswich lost 1-0 against Bowers and Pitsea last time out, on January 27.
