Felixstowe and Walton Utd. v Ipswich Wanderers

Isthmian League Division One – North
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Felixstowe and Walton Utd. are on an excellent run ahead of hosting Ipswich Wanderers on Monday, with 16 points collected from their last six games.

Wanderers are 14 places behind Felixstowe & Walton Utd. in 16th, with six wins and one draw putting them on 19 points.
