Lowestoft Town v Grays Athletic

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lowestoft Town host Grays Athletic in Saturday's match at Crown Meadow looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One North.

Trawler Boys, who sit top of the league after 20 games, fell to a 1-3 home defeat to Witham Town on Tuesday.

They face a Grays side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-1 victory against Ipswich Wanderers, and who sit 18th in the table.

With 58 goals in 20 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Grays, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Lowestoft and Grays have played each other on six occasions. Lowestoft won four of them, Grays one, and they drew once.

On average, Trawler Boys scored 2.2 goals and Grays 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Lowestoft won 3-2 away.

Town are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Athletic's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Trawler Boys' nine.
