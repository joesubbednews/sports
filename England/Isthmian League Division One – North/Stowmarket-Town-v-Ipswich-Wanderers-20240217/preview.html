Stowmarket Town v Ipswich Wanderers

Isthmian League Division One – North
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table Stowmarket Town host 18th-placed Ipswich Wanderers.

Stowmarket have picked up four points from their first four Isthmian League Division One – North games, with no wins and four draws.

That is 12 points less than Ipswich have collected, having won five and drawn one.

Stowmarket are in terrible form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Ipswich's form is slightly better – they have taken three points from 18, compared to Town's one.

With 18 goals in 21 games so far this season, Wanderers are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 3.2 goals per game.

Stowmarket's last match was on February 3, a 3-1 loss against Bowers and Pitsea.

Ipswich lost 1-0 against Bowers and Pitsea last time out, on January 27.
