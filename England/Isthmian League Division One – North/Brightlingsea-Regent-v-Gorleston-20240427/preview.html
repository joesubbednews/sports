Brightlingsea Regent v Gorleston

Isthmian League Division One – North | North Road
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brightlingsea Regent welcome Gorleston to North Road on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their seven-game losing streak.

Brightlingsea Regent's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – North matches, while their opponents have earned 11 from a possible 18.

With 35 goals in 22 games so far this season, Gorleston are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Brightlingsea Regent, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are 10th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn one, earning 31 points.

The away side are one place behind Brightlingsea Regent in 11th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Brightlingsea Regent's last match was on February 13, a 2-0 loss against Felixstowe and Walton Utd..

Gorleston beat Stowmarket Town 6-0 last time out, on March 16.
