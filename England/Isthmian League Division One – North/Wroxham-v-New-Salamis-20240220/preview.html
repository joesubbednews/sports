Wroxham v New Salamis

Isthmian League Division One – North | Trafford Park
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

New Salamis travel to Wroxham on late Tuesday in the Isthmian League Division One North.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Grays Athletic 3-2 at home.

Wroxham, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-0 against Waltham Forest.

With 30 goals in 22 games so far this season, Wroxham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

New Salamis are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Wroxham's five.

Wroxham are 14th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

New Salamis are two places ahead of the home team in 12th, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.

Over the last two years, Wroxham and New Salamis have played each other twice. Wroxham won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on March 28, when Wroxham won 2-0 at home.
