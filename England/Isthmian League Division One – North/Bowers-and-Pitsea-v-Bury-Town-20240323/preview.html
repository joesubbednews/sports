Bowers and Pitsea v Bury Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Len Salmon Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Bowers and Pitsea and Bury Town promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Isthmian League Division One North's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at the Len Salmon Stadium, Bowers & Pitsea and Bury sit joint-fourth and second in the goal-scoring charts, with 45 and 59 goals respectively.

Bowers & Pitsea are third in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

Bury are two places behind the hosts in fifth, with 13 wins and five draws putting them on 44 points.

Bowers & Pitsea are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Bowers & Pitsea's 12.

Bowers & Pitsea's last match was on February 11, a 1-1 draw against Gorleston.

Bury beat Brightlingsea Regent 4-1 last time out, on February 3.
