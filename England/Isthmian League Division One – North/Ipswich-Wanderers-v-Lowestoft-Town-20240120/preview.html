Ipswich Wanderers v Lowestoft Town

Isthmian League Division One – North
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lowestoft Town travel for Saturday's match against Ipswich Wanderers looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One North.

Trawler Boys, who sit top of the league after 19 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to Felixstowe and Walton Utd. on January 13.

They face an Ipswich side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Basildon United, and who sit 16th in the table.

With 55 goals in 19 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Ipswich, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Wanderers are in bad form in Isthmian League Division One North, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' three.
