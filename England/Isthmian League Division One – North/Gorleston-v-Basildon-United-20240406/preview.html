Gorleston v Basildon United

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Basildon United travel to Gorleston on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One North.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Ipswich Wanderers 2-1 away.

Gorleston, meanwhile, won their last match, 6-0 against Stowmarket Town.

With 46 goals in 23 games so far this season, Basildon are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Gorleston, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

United are seventh in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn three, earning 39 points.

The hosts are four places behind the away side in 11th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

In the last three years, Gorleston and Basildon have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Basildon won 2-1 at home.

Gorleston are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Basildon's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Gorleston's 11.
