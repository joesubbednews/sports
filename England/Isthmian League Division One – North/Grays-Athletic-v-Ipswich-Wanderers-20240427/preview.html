Grays Athletic v Ipswich Wanderers

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Mill Field
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Grays Athletic host Ipswich Wanderers on Saturday at the Mill Field in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on February 9, Grays lost to New Salamis 3-2 away.

Ipswich also lost, 2-0 at home against Wroxham on March 30.

Grays are 18th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won four and drawn six, earning 18 points.

Ipswich are two places ahead of Athletic in 16th, with six wins and one draw putting them on 19 points.

The home side are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Wanderers' form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Grays's seven.

With 22 goals in 24 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Grays are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.
