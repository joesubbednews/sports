Felixstowe and Walton Utd. v Brentwood Town

Isthmian League Division One – North
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Felixstowe and Walton Utd. host Brentwood Town in Saturday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Felixstowe & Walton Utd., who sit second in the league after 24 games, fell to a 0-2 away defeat to Brightlingsea Regent on February 13.

They face a Brentwood side who also lost their last match, a 1-0 defeat to Lowestoft Town, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 56 goals in 24 games so far this season, Felixstowe & Walton Utd. are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Brentwood are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is worse – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Felixstowe & Walton Utd.'s 16.

In the last 10 years, Felixstowe & Walton Utd. and Brentwood have played each other on six occasions. Felixstowe & Walton Utd. won four of them and they drew twice.

On average, Felixstowe & Walton Utd. scored 1.5 goals and Brentwood 0.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 6, when they played out a 2-2 draw.
