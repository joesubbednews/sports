Redbridge v Bury Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Oakside Stadium
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Redbridge and Bury Town come into Saturday's kick-off at the Oakside Stadium in contrasting spells, with the away team hitting a rich vein of form while the hosts struggle.

Redbridge have won one and drawn one of the last six, while Bury have taken five wins and one draw.

With 59 goals in 23 games so far this season, Bury are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Redbridge, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Town are fifth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

The hosts are 10 places behind the away team in 15th, with five wins and five draws putting them on 20 points.

Redbridge's last match was on February 3, a 1-0 loss against Felixstowe and Walton Utd..

Bury beat Brightlingsea Regent 4-1 last time out, also on February 3.
