Lowestoft Town v Brentwood Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lowestoft Town host Brentwood Town in Saturday's match at Crown Meadow looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Trawler Boys, who sit top of the league after 23 games, fell to a 0-1 away defeat to Brentwood Town on February 6.

They face a Brentwood side who also lost their last match, a 1-0 defeat to Lowestoft Town, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 63 goals in 23 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Brentwood are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Lowestoft Town are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with four wins and a draw over that period, Brentwood Town's form is identical – they have both taken 13 points from 18.

In the last three years, Lowestoft and Brentwood have played each other on three occasions. Lowestoft won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on February 6, when Lowestoft won 1-0 away.
