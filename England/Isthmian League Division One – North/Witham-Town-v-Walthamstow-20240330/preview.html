Witham Town v Walthamstow

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Village Glass Stadium
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Witham Town host Walthamstow on Saturday at the Village Glass Stadium in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on February 3, Witham lost to Basildon United 4-0 away.

Walthamstow won, 2-0 at home against Wroxham on February 6.

With 31 goals in 23 games so far this season, Witham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Walthamstow are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home team's six.

Witham are 13th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won six and drawn six, earning 24 points.

Walthamstow are four places ahead of Witham in ninth, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.
