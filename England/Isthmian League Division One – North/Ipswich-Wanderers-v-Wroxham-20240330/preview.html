Ipswich Wanderers v Wroxham

Isthmian League Division One – North
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ipswich Wanderers host Wroxham on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on March 23, Ipswich beat Basildon United 2-1 at home.

Wroxham lost, 2-0 away at Walthamstow on February 6.

Ipswich are 16th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won six and drawn one, earning 19 points.

Wroxham are two places ahead of Wanderers in 14th, with five wins and seven draws putting them on 22 points.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Ipswich's six.

With 22 goals in 23 games so far this season, Ipswich are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Wroxham are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.
