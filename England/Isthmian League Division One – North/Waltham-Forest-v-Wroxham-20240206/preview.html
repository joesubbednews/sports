Waltham Forest v Wroxham

Isthmian League Division One – North | Wadham Lodge
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Waltham Forest host Wroxham on Tuesday at Wadham Lodge in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Forest drew with Lowestoft Town 2-2 at home.

Wroxham also drew, 1-1 away at Grays Athletic.

With 30 goals in 21 games so far this season, Wroxham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Forest are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

And also with a win and three draws over that period, the away team's form is identical – they have both taken six points from 18.

Wroxham are 13th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

Forest are three places ahead of Wroxham in 10th, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.
