Brightlingsea Regent v Lowestoft Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | North Road
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brightlingsea Regent welcome Lowestoft Town to North Road on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their seven-game losing streak.

Brightlingsea Regent's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – North matches, while their opponents have earned 13 from a possible 18.

With 63 goals in 23 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Brightlingsea Regent, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 23 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn two, earning 53 points.

The home side are nine places behind Trawler Boys in 10th, with 10 wins and one draw putting them on 31 points.

Brightlingsea Regent's last match was on February 13, a 2-0 loss against Felixstowe and Walton Utd..

Lowestoft beat Brentwood Town 1-0 last time out, on February 6.
