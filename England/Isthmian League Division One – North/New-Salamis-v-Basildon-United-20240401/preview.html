New Salamis v Basildon United

Isthmian League Division One – North | Coles Park
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

New Salamis host Basildon United on Monday at Coles Park in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on Saturday, New Salamis lost to Bowers and Pitsea 3-2 away.

Basildon also lost, 2-1 away at Ipswich Wanderers on March 23.

With 46 goals in 23 games so far this season, Basildon are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

New Salamis, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

United are seventh in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn three, earning 39 points.

The hosts are five places behind the away side in 12th, with seven wins and seven draws putting them on 28 points.

In the last three years, New Salamis and Basildon have played each other on three occasions. Basildon won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on December 27, when Basildon won 1-0 at home.

New Salamis are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Basildon's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to New Salamis's 10.
