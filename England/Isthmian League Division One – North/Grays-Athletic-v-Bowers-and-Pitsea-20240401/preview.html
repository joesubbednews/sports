Grays Athletic v Bowers and Pitsea

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Mill Field
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Grays Athletic face a challenge against high-flying Bowers and Pitsea at the Mill Field on Monday.

Grays Athletic are 18th in the Isthmian League Division One – North table, and have picked up four wins and six draws in their 23 games to date.

Bowers & Pitsea, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 49 points, having won 15 and drawn four of their first 25 matches, and are four points behind table-toppers Lowestoft Town.

With 30 goals in 23 games so far this season, Grays are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Bowers & Pitsea, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Athletic are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home side's seven.

Grays's last match was on February 9, a 3-2 loss against New Salamis.

Bowers & Pitsea beat New Salamis 3-2 last time out, on Saturday.
