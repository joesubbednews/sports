Gorleston v Brentwood Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of Isthmian League Division One North's meanest defences go head-to-head at Crown Meadow on Saturday, when Brentwood Town visit Gorleston.

Only one side – East Thurrock United – has conceded fewer goals than Brentwood to date: the away side have let in just 20 goals in 22 games.

Gorleston have conceded 24 goals in 19 games, giving them the third tightest back line so far this season.

Brentwood are third in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

Gorleston are eight places behind Town in 11th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

The hosts are in reasonable form in Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Gorleston's 12.

In the last three years, Gorleston and Brentwood have played each other on three occasions. Brentwood won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Gorleston's last match was on January 14, a 3-0 win against Enfield 1893.

Brentwood beat Waltham Forest 2-0 last time out, on January 27.
