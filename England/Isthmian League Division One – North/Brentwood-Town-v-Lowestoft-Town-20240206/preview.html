Brentwood Town v Lowestoft Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Brentwood Centre Arena
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Two of Isthmian League Division One North's top sides face each other at the Brentwood Centre Arena in Tuesday's late kick-off, when third-placed Brentwood Town host table-topping Lowestoft Town.

Brentwood have picked up 13 wins and six draws from 23 games so far this season, and sit five points below the visitors going into the 7.45pm match.

Trawler Boys, meanwhile, have won 16 and drawn two of 22, picking up 50 points.

With 62 goals in 22 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Brentwood are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Brentwood Town are in fantastic form in Isthmian League Division One North, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Lowestoft Town's form is much worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home side's 16.

Over the last two years, Brentwood and Lowestoft have played each other twice. they drew both of them.

Their last meeting was on April 18, when they played out a 3-3 draw.

Brentwood's last match was on Saturday, a 3-2 win against Gorleston.

Lowestoft drew 2-2 with Waltham Forest last time out, also on Saturday.
