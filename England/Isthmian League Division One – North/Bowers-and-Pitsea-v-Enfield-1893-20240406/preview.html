Bowers and Pitsea v Enfield 1893

Isthmian League Division One – North | Len Salmon Stadium
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Enfield 1893 face a challenge away against high-flying Bowers and Pitsea at the Len Salmon Stadium on Saturday.

Enfield 1893 are 17th in the Isthmian League Division One – North table, and have picked up five wins and four draws in their 22 games to date.

Bowers & Pitsea, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 49 points, having won 15 and drawn four of their first 25 matches, and are four points behind table-toppers Lowestoft Town.

With 24 goals in 22 games so far this season, Enfield 1893 are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Bowers & Pitsea, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Bowers & Pitsea's 12.

Bowers & Pitsea's last match was on Saturday, a 3-2 win against New Salamis.

Enfield 1893 lost 1-0 against Maldon and Tiptree last time out, on February 3.
