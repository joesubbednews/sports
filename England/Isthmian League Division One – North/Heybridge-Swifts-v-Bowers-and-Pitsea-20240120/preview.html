Heybridge Swifts v Bowers and Pitsea

Isthmian League Division One – North | Scraley Road Stadium
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of Isthmian League Division One North's top sides face each other at the Scraley Road Stadium in Saturday's kick-off, when third-placed Heybridge Swifts host fourth-placed Bowers and Pitsea.

Heybridge Swifts have picked up 12 wins and four draws from 20 games so far this season, and sit two points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Bowers & Pitsea, meanwhile, have won 12 and drawn two, picking up 38 points.

With 38 goals in 20 games so far this season, Heybridge Swifts are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Bowers & Pitsea are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly worse – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Heybridge Swifts's 14.

Heybridge Swifts's last match was on January 13, a 3-1 win against Grays Athletic.

Bowers & Pitsea drew 2-2 with Bury Town last time out, also on January 13.
