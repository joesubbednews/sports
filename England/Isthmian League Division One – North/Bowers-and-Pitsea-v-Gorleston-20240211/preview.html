Bowers and Pitsea v Gorleston

Isthmian League Division One – North | Len Salmon Stadium
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bowers and Pitsea face Gorleston on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian League Division One North.

Bowers & Pitsea are unbeaten in seven, with five wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Gorleston team who have won three and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 28 goals in 20 games so far this season, Gorleston are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Bowers & Pitsea, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home team are fourth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn three, earning 45 points.

The visitors are seven places behind Bowers & Pitsea in 11th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

Over the last two years, Bowers & Pitsea and Gorleston have played each other twice. Bowers & Pitsea won both of them.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when Bowers & Pitsea won 2-0 away.

Bowers & Pitsea's last match was on February 3, a 3-1 win against Stowmarket Town.

Gorleston lost 3-2 against Brentwood Town last time out, also on February 3.
