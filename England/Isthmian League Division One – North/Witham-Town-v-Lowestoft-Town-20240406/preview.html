Witham Town v Lowestoft Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Village Glass Stadium
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lowestoft Town travel to Witham Town on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One North.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Brentwood Town 1-0 away.

Witham, meanwhile, lost their last match, 4-0 against Basildon United.

With 63 goals in 23 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Witham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Lowestoft Town are top of the table after 23 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn two, earning 53 points.

Witham Town are 13 places behind Trawler Boys in 14th, with six wins and six draws putting them on 24 points.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Witham's six.

In the last three years, Witham and Lowestoft have played each other on three occasions. Lowestoft won all of them.

Their last meeting was on January 23, when Lowestoft won 3-1 at home.
