Maldon and Tiptree v Ipswich Wanderers

Isthmian League Division One – North | Wallace Binder Stadium
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ipswich Wanderers make the journey to the Wallace Binder Stadium to play Maldon and Tiptree on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Ipswich's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – North matches, while their opponents have earned 12 from a possible 18.

With 20 goals in 22 games so far this season, Ipswich are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Maldon & Tiptree, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Wanderers are 18th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn one, earning 16 points.

The Jammers are 10 places ahead of the away team in eighth, with 12 wins and three draws putting them on 39 points.

Maldon & Tiptree's last match was on February 3, a 1-0 win against Enfield 1893.

Ipswich lost 3-2 against New Salamis last time out, on February 23.
