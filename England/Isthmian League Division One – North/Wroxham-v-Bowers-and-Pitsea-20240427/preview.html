Wroxham v Bowers and Pitsea

Isthmian League Division One – North | Trafford Park
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bowers and Pitsea face Wroxham on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Bowers & Pitsea are unbeaten in nine, with six wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Wroxham team who have won two and drawn four over the same number of games.

With 32 goals in 23 games so far this season, Wroxham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Bowers & Pitsea, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The visitors are third in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn four, earning 49 points.

The home team are 10 places behind Bowers & Pitsea in 13th, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.

Wroxham's last match was on March 30, a 2-0 win against Ipswich Wanderers.

Bowers & Pitsea beat New Salamis 3-2 last time out, also on March 30.
