Brentwood Town v Lowestoft Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Brentwood Centre Arena
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Lowestoft Town travel to the Brentwood Centre Arena for Tuesday's late match against Brentwood Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One North.

Trawler Boys, who sit top of the league after 19 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to Felixstowe and Walton Utd. on Saturday.

They face a Brentwood side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-1 victory against Maldon and Tiptree, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 55 goals in 19 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Brentwood are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Brentwood Town are in reasonable form in Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Lowestoft Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home team's 11.

Over the last two years, Brentwood and Lowestoft have played each other twice. they drew both of them.

Their last meeting was on April 18, when they played out a 3-3 draw.
