Brentwood Town v Waltham Forest

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Brentwood Centre Arena
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brentwood Town face Waltham Forest on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian League Division One North.

Brentwood are unbeaten in six, with four wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Forest team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

Brentwood are fourth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn six, earning 39 points.

Forest are six places behind Town in 10th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

With 39 goals in 21 games so far this season, the home side are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Brentwood's last match was on January 20, a 3-1 win against Redbridge.

Forest drew 2-2 with New Salamis last time out, also on January 20.
