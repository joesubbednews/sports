Ipswich Wanderers v Grays Athletic

Isthmian League Division One – North
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Grays Athletic make the journey to play Ipswich Wanderers on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Ipswich's last match was on January 13, a 1-0 win against Basildon United.

Grays lost 3-1 against Heybridge Swifts last time out, also on January 13.
