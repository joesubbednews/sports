Redbridge v Bowers and Pitsea

Isthmian League Division One – North | Oakside Stadium
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Bowers and Pitsea face Redbridge on Tuesday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Bowers & Pitsea are unbeaten in eight, with five wins and three draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Redbridge team who have won two and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 28 goals in 19 games so far this season, Redbridge are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Bowers & Pitsea, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are third in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

The home side are 12 places behind Bowers & Pitsea in 15th, with five wins and five draws putting them on 20 points.

Redbridge's last match was on February 3, a 1-0 loss against Felixstowe and Walton Utd..

Bowers & Pitsea drew 1-1 with Gorleston last time out, on February 11.
