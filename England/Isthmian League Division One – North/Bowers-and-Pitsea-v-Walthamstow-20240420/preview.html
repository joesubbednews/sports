Bowers and Pitsea v Walthamstow

Isthmian League Division One – North | Len Salmon Stadium
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bowers and Pitsea face Walthamstow on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Bowers & Pitsea are unbeaten in nine, with six wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Walthamstow team who have won four and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 48 goals in 25 games so far this season, Bowers & Pitsea are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Walthamstow, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are third in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn four, earning 49 points.

The away side are six places behind Bowers & Pitsea in ninth, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

Bowers & Pitsea's last match was on March 30, a 3-2 win against New Salamis.

Walthamstow beat Wroxham 2-0 last time out, on February 6.
