Gorleston v Bury Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bury Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Gorleston defence on Saturday.

With 45 goals in 19 games, Bury are the third-highest scorers in Isthmian League Division One North ahead of the 3pm kick-off at Crown Meadow.

They face a Gorleston side who have scored 26 in 19 matches, but conceded only 24 goals, putting them joint-fourth among the league's tightest defences – only Heybridge Swifts, Brentwood Town and East Thurrock United have conceded fewer goals.

Gorleston are in reasonable form in Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Bury's form is slightly better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to the home side's 12.

Town are eighth in the table after 19 games, of which they have won nine and drawn five, earning 32 points.

Gorleston are three places behind the visitors in 11th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

In the last three years, Gorleston and Bury have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Gorleston's last match was on Sunday, a 3-0 win against Enfield 1893.

Bury drew 2-2 with Bowers and Pitsea last time out, on January 13.
