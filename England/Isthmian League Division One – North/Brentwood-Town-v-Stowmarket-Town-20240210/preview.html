Brentwood Town v Stowmarket Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Brentwood Centre Arena
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Brentwood Town face Stowmarket Town at the Brentwood Centre Arena on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-1 victory over Lowestoft Town on Tuesday, Brentwood sit third in the Isthmian League Division One North.

They travel to play a Stowmarket side 20th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-1 against Bowers and Pitsea, on February 3.

Brentwood are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Stowmarket's form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to Brentwood Town's 13.

With 25 goals in 24 games so far this season, Stowmarket Town are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 77 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

In the last five years, Brentwood and Stowmarket have played each other on five occasions. They won two each, and they drew once.

On average, Brentwood scored 1.2 goals and Stowmarket 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when Brentwood won 3-1 away.
