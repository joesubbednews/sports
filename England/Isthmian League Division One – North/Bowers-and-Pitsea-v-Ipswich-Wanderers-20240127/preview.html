Bowers and Pitsea v Ipswich Wanderers

Isthmian League Division One – North | Len Salmon Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bowers and Pitsea face Ipswich Wanderers on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian League Division One North.

Bowers & Pitsea are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face an Ipswich team who have won just one and lost four over the same number of games.

With 18 goals in 20 games so far this season, Ipswich are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Bowers & Pitsea, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home side are fifth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn three, earning 39 points.

Wanderers are 11 places behind Bowers & Pitsea in 16th, with five wins and one draw putting them on 16 points.

Bowers & Pitsea's last match was on January 20, a 2-2 draw against Heybridge Swifts.

Ipswich lost 4-1 against Grays Athletic last time out, on Tuesday.
