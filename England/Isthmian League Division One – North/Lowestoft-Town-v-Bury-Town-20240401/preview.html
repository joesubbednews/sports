Lowestoft Town v Bury Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Monday's match between Lowestoft Town and Bury Town promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Isthmian League Division One North's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Crown Meadow, Trawler Boys and Bury sit top and second in the goal-scoring charts, with 63 and 59 goals respectively.

Lowestoft are top of the table after 23 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn two, earning 53 points.

Bury are four places behind Lowestoft Town in fifth, with 13 wins and five draws putting them on 44 points.

Trawler Boys are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Bury Town's form is better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.

In the last three years, Lowestoft and Bury have played each other on three occasions. Bury won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on December 23, when Bury won 4-1 at home.

Lowestoft's last match was on February 6, a 1-0 win against Brentwood Town.

Bury beat Brightlingsea Regent 4-1 last time out, on February 3.
