Wroxham v Walthamstow

Isthmian League Division One – North | Trafford Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wroxham host Walthamstow on Saturday at Trafford Park in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on February 6, Wroxham lost to Walthamstow 2-0 away.

Walthamstow won, 2-0 at home against Wroxham.

Wroxham are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Walthamstow's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

With 30 goals in 22 games so far this season, Wroxham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Wroxham are 14th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

Walthamstow are five places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.
