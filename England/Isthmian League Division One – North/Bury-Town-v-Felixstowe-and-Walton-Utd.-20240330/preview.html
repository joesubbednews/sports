Bury Town v Felixstowe and Walton Utd.

Isthmian League Division One – North | Ram Meadow
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Bury Town and Felixstowe and Walton Utd. promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Isthmian League Division One North's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Ram Meadow, Bury and Felixstowe & Walton Utd. sit second and third in the goal-scoring charts, with 59 and 56 goals respectively.

Felixstowe & Walton Utd. are second in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn five, earning 50 points.

Bury are three places behind the visitors in fifth, with 13 wins and five draws putting them on 44 points.

Town are in fantastic form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with five wins and a draw over that period, Felixstowe & Walton Utd.'s form is identical – they have both taken 16 points from 18.

In the last five years, Bury and Felixstowe & Walton Utd. have played each other on five occasions. Bury won two of them and Felixstowe & Walton Utd. three.

On average, Bury scored 1.2 goals and Felixstowe & Walton Utd. 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Felixstowe & Walton Utd. won 3-0 at home.

Bury's last match was on February 3, a 4-1 win against Brightlingsea Regent.

Felixstowe & Walton Utd. beat Brightlingsea Regent 2-0 last time out, on February 13.
