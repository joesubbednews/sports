Basildon United v Brentwood Town

Isthmian League Division One – North
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brentwood Town travel for Saturday's match against Basildon United looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Brentwood, who sit fourth in the league after 24 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Lowestoft Town on February 6.

They face a Basildon side who also lost their last match, a 2-1 defeat to Ipswich Wanderers, and who sit seventh in the table.

With 46 goals in 23 games so far this season, Basildon are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Brentwood are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

In the last 10 years, Basildon and Brentwood have played each other on six occasions. Basildon won two of them and Brentwood four.

On average, Basildon scored 1.7 goals and Brentwood 2.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Basildon won 3-2 away.

United are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.
