Wroxham v New Salamis

Isthmian League Division One – North | Trafford Park
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Wroxham face New Salamis at Trafford Park on late Tuesday looking to secure a first win in seven Isthmian League Division One – North games.

Wroxham have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Felixstowe and Walton Utd. on November 21.

They face a New Salamis side who have won three and drawn one over that time.

With 26 goals in 18 games so far this season, Wroxham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

New Salamis are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The home side are 14th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won four and drawn six, earning 18 points.

The visitors are one place ahead of Wroxham in 13th, with five wins and six draws putting them on 21 points.

Over the last two years, Wroxham and New Salamis have played each other twice. Wroxham won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on March 28, when Wroxham won 2-0 at home.

Wroxham's last match was on Saturday, a 1-1 draw against Redbridge.

New Salamis beat Stowmarket Town 4-3 last time out, also on Saturday.
