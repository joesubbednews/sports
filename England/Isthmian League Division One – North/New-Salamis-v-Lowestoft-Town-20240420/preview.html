New Salamis v Lowestoft Town

Isthmian League Division One – North | Coles Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

New Salamis host Lowestoft Town on Saturday at Coles Park in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on March 30, New Salamis lost to Bowers and Pitsea 3-2 away.

Lowestoft won, 1-0 away at Brentwood Town on February 6.

With 63 goals in 23 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

New Salamis, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 23 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn two, earning 53 points.

The home team are 11 places behind Trawler Boys in 12th, with seven wins and seven draws putting them on 28 points.

New Salamis are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to New Salamis's 10.

In the last five years, New Salamis and Lowestoft have played each other on four occasions. New Salamis won one of them and Lowestoft the other.

On average, New Salamis scored 1.5 goals and Trawler Boys 3.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Lowestoft won 6-1 at home.
