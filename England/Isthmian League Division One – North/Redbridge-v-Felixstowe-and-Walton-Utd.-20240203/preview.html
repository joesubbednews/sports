Redbridge v Felixstowe and Walton Utd.

Isthmian League Division One – North | Oakside Stadium
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Redbridge face Felixstowe and Walton Utd. at the Oakside Stadium on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian League Division One – North games.

Redbridge have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Witham Town on December 16.

They face a Felixstowe & Walton Utd. side who have won three and drawn one over that time.

With 53 goals in 22 games so far this season, Felixstowe & Walton Utd. are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Redbridge, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The away team are second in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

The hosts are 13 places behind Felixstowe & Walton Utd. in 15th, with four wins and six draws putting them on 18 points.

Redbridge's last match was on Tuesday, a 0-0 draw against Heybridge Swifts.

Felixstowe & Walton Utd. beat Basildon United 1-0 last time out, on January 27, with on the scoresheet.
