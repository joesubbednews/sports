Waltham Forest v Basildon United

Isthmian League Division One – North | Wadham Lodge
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Basildon United travel to Waltham Forest on late Tuesday in Isthmian League Division One North.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Ipswich Wanderers 1-0 at home.

Forest, meanwhile, won their last match, 3-2 against Brightlingsea Regent.

With 41 goals in 20 games so far this season, Basildon are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Forest, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

United are sixth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn three, earning 36 points.

The hosts are four places behind the away team in 10th, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

Forest are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Basildon's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Forest's nine.
