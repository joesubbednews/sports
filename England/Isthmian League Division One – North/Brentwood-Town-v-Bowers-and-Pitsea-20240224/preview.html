Brentwood Town v Bowers and Pitsea

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Brentwood Centre Arena
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brentwood Town host Bowers and Pitsea in Saturday's match at the Brentwood Centre Arena looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Brentwood, who sit fourth in the league after 24 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Lowestoft Town on February 6.

They face a Bowers & Pitsea side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Gorleston, and who sit third in the table.

With 45 goals in 24 games so far this season, Bowers & Pitsea are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Brentwood are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Town are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly worse – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.
