Gorleston v Enfield 1893

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Gorleston host Enfield 1893 on Saturday at Crown Meadow in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on March 16, Gorleston beat Stowmarket Town 6-0 away.

Enfield 1893 lost, 1-0 at home against Maldon and Tiptree on February 3.

Enfield 1893 are 16th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn four, earning 19 points.

Gorleston are five places ahead of the visitors in 11th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 24 goals in 22 games so far this season, Enfield 1893 are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The home team are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Gorleston are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Enfield 1893's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Gorleston's 11.
