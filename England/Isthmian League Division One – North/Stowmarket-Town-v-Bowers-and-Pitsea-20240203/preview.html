Stowmarket Town v Bowers and Pitsea

Isthmian League Division One – North
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stowmarket Town and Bowers and Pitsea come into Saturday's kick-off in contrasting spells, with the away team hitting a rich vein of form while the hosts struggle.
