Redbridge v Ipswich Wanderers

Isthmian League Division One – North | Oakside Stadium
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Redbridge and Ipswich Wanderers both come into Tuesday's late kick-off at the Oakside Stadium in poor form, having picked up four and three points respectively in their last six games.

Redbridge have won one and drawn one of the last six, while Ipswich have taken just one win.

Ipswich are 16th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won five and drawn one, earning 16 points.

Redbridge are one place ahead of Wanderers in 15th, with four wins and five draws putting them on 17 points.

With 17 goals in 19 games so far this season, the away team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Redbridge's last match was on Saturday, a 1-1 draw against Wroxham.

Ipswich beat Basildon United 1-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
