Bowers and Pitsea v New Salamis

Isthmian League Division One – North | Len Salmon Stadium
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bowers and Pitsea face New Salamis on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Bowers & Pitsea are unbeaten in eight, with five wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a New Salamis team who have won four and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 45 goals in 24 games so far this season, Bowers & Pitsea are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

New Salamis, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The home team are third in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

The visitors are nine places behind Bowers & Pitsea in 12th, with seven wins and seven draws putting them on 28 points.

Bowers & Pitsea's last match was on February 11, a 1-1 draw against Gorleston.

New Salamis beat Ipswich Wanderers 3-2 last time out, on February 23.
