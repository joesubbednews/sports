Wroxham v Gorleston

Isthmian League Division One – North | Trafford Park
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wroxham host Gorleston on Monday at Trafford Park in the Isthmian League Division One North.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Wroxham beat Ipswich Wanderers 2-0 away.

Gorleston also won, 6-0 away at Stowmarket Town on March 16.

In the last 10 years, Wroxham and Gorleston have played each other on six occasions. Wroxham won two of them, Gorleston three, and they drew once.

On average, Wroxham scored 1.2 goals and Gorleston 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 32 goals in 23 games so far this season, Wroxham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Gorleston are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home team are 13th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won six and drawn seven, earning 25 points.

The visitors are two places ahead of Wroxham in 11th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Wroxham are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Gorleston's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.
