Maldon and Tiptree v Bowers and Pitsea

Isthmian League Division One – North | Wallace Binder Stadium
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Maldon and Tiptree and Bowers and Pitsea promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Isthmian League Division One North's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at the Wallace Binder Stadium, the Jammers and Bowers & Pitsea sit joint-fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 45 goals apiece.

Bowers & Pitsea are third in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

Maldon & Tiptree are five places behind the away side in eighth, with 12 wins and three draws putting them on 39 points.

The Jammers are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, Bowers & Pitsea's form is similar – they have both taken 12 points from 18.

Maldon & Tiptree's last match was on February 3, a 1-0 win against Enfield 1893.

Bowers & Pitsea drew 1-1 with Gorleston last time out, on February 11.
