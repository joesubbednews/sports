Brentwood Town v Enfield 1893

Isthmian League Division One – North | The Brentwood Centre Arena
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brentwood Town host Enfield 1893 in Saturday's match at the Brentwood Centre Arena looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Brentwood, who sit fourth in the league after 24 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Lowestoft Town on February 6.

They face an Enfield 1893 side who also lost their last match, a 1-0 defeat to Maldon and Tiptree, and who sit 16th in the table.

With 24 goals in 22 games so far this season, Enfield 1893 are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Brentwood, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Town are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.
