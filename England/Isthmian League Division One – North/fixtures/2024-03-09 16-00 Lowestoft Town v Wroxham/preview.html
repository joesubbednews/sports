Lowestoft Town v Wroxham

Isthmian League Division One – North | Crown Meadow
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wroxham travel to Lowestoft Town on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One North.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Walthamstow 2-0 away.

Trawler Boys, meanwhile, won their last match, 1-0 against Brentwood Town.

With 63 goals in 23 games so far this season, Lowestoft are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Wroxham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 23 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn two, earning 53 points.

The visitors are 13 places behind Trawler Boys in 14th, with five wins and seven draws putting them on 22 points.

The home team are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Wroxham's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Lowestoft's 13.

In the last five years, Lowestoft and Wroxham have played each other on four occasions. Lowestoft won two of them, Wroxham one, and they drew once.

On average, Trawler Boys scored 2.0 goals and Wroxham 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Lowestoft won 2-0 away.
