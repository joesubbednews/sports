Bury Town v Maldon and Tiptree

Isthmian League Division One – North | Ram Meadow
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bury Town are on an excellent run ahead of hosting Maldon and Tiptree at Ram Meadow on Saturday, with 16 points collected from their last six games.

Bury have picked up five wins and one draw in their last six Isthmian League Division One – North games, and face a Jammers side whose last six games have brought four wins and two losses.

With 59 goals in 23 games so far this season, Bury are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Maldon & Tiptree are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are fifth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

The Jammers are three places behind the home team in eighth, with 12 wins and three draws putting them on 39 points.

In the last five years, Bury and Maldon & Tiptree have played each other on five occasions. Bury won three of them and they drew twice.

On average, Bury scored 2.0 goals and the Jammers 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 30, when Bury won 4-2 away.

Bury's last match was on February 3, a 4-1 win against Brightlingsea Regent.

Maldon & Tiptree beat Enfield 1893 1-0 last time out, also on February 3.
