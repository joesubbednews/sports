Enfield 1893 v Witham Town

Isthmian League Division One – North
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Enfield 1893 host Witham Town on Tuesday in the Isthmian League Division One North.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Enfield 1893 are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One North, with one win and three draws from their last six games.
