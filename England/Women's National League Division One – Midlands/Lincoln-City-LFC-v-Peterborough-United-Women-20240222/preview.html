Lincoln City LFC v Peterborough United Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Thursday, 22 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Lincoln City LFC host Peterborough United Women on Thursday in the Women's National League Division One Midlands.

In their last league match, on February 11, Lincoln City lost to Boldmere St. Michaels Women 2-0 away.

Peterborough United won, 5-1 away at Leafield Athletic Women on February 4.

With 34 goals in 13 games so far this season, Peterborough United are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Lincoln City are also above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have also conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The away side are third in the table after 13 games, of which they have won nine and lost four, earning 27 points.

The hosts are five places behind Peterborough United in eighth, with three wins and five draws putting them on 14 points.

In the last five years, Lincoln City and Peterborough United have played each other on five occasions. Lincoln City won two of them and Peterborough United three.

On average, Lincoln City scored 2.6 goals and Peterborough United 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 6, when Peterborough United won 4-1 at home.

Lincoln City are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Peterborough United's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Lincoln City's seven.
