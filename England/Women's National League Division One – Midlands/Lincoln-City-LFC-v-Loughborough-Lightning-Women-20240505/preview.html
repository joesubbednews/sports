Lincoln City LFC v Loughborough Lightning Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Loughborough Lightning Women come to play Lincoln City LFC on early Sunday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Lincoln City have a win and two draws.

Loughborough Lightning are top of the table after 21 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn four, earning 52 points.

Lincoln City are seven places behind the away team in eighth, with four wins and eight draws putting them on 20 points.

With 56 goals in 21 games so far this season, Loughborough Lightning are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Lincoln City's last match was on April 21, a 0-0 draw against Notts County Women.

Loughborough Lightning drew 1-1 with Sheffield FC Women last time out, also on April 21.
