Northampton Town Women v Lincoln City LFC

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Northampton Town Women host Lincoln City LFC on Sunday in the Women's National League Division One Midlands.

In their last league match, on Tuesday, Northampton Town beat Boldmere St. Michaels Women 4-1 away.

Lincoln City also won, 2-1 at home against Sheffield FC Women on Thursday.

With 36 goals in 15 games so far this season, Northampton Town are scoring more than average in the league with 2.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Lincoln City are also above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have also conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Northampton Town's nine.

In the last five years, Northampton Town and Lincoln City have played each other on four occasions. Northampton Town won one of them and Lincoln City the other.

On average, Northampton Town scored 1.2 goals and Lincoln City 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when Lincoln City won 4-0 at home.

Northampton Town are fifth in the table after 15 games, of which they have won eight and drawn one, earning 25 points.

Lincoln City are three places behind the hosts in eighth, with four wins and six draws putting them on 18 points.
