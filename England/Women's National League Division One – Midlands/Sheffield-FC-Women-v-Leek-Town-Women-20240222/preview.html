Sheffield FC Women v Leek Town Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Thursday, 22 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Thursday's late match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 11th-ranked Sheffield FC Women host ninth-placed Leek Town Women.

Sheffield FC have picked up seven points from their first seven Women's National League Division One – Midlands games, with one win and four draws.

That is six points less than Leek Town have collected, having won three and drawn four.

Sheffield FC are in bad form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Leek Town's form is slightly better – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With six goals in 13 games so far this season, Sheffield FC are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Sheffield FC and Leek Town have played each other on three occasions. Leek Town won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on September 7, when Leek Town won 1-0 at home.

Sheffield FC's last match was on February 4, a 1-0 loss against Boldmere St. Michaels Women.

Leek Town drew 1-1 with Leafield Athletic Women last time out, on January 28.
