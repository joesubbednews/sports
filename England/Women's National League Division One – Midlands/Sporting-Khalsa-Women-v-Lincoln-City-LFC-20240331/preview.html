Sporting Khalsa Women v Lincoln City LFC

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sporting Khalsa Women host Lincoln City LFC on early Sunday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Women's National League Division One Midlands.

Sporting have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Lincoln City side who lost their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

With 69 goals in 19 games so far this season, Sporting are the league's highest scorers with 3.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Lincoln City are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The home side are top of the table after 19 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn two, earning 44 points.

The visitors are seven places behind Sporting in eighth, with four wins and seven draws putting them on 19 points.

In the last five years, Sporting and Lincoln City have played each other on five occasions. Sporting won two of them, Lincoln City one, and they drew twice.

On average, Sporting scored 2.6 goals and Lincoln City 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 3, when Sporting won 2-1 away.

Sporting's last match was on Sunday, a 5-0 win against Leek Town Women.

Lincoln City lost 2-0 against Solihull Moors Women last time out, also on Sunday.
