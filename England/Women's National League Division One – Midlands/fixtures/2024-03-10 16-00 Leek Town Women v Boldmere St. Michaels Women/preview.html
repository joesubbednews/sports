Leek Town Women v Boldmere St. Michaels Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands | Harrison Park
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Boldmere St. Michaels Women travel to Harrison Park for Sunday's match against Leek Town Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Division One Midlands.

Boldmere St. Michaels, who sit second in the league after 15 games, fell to a 3-0 home defeat to Sporting Khalsa Women on February 20.

They face a Leek Town side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Solihull Moors Women, and who sit ninth in the table.

Leek Town are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, Boldmere St. Michaels's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

With 19 goals in 14 games so far this season, Leek Town are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last three years, Leek Town and Boldmere St. Michaels have played each other on three occasions. Boldmere St. Michaels won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Boldmere St. Michaels won 4-1 at home.
