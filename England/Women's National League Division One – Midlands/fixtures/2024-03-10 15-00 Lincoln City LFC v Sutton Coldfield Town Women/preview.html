Lincoln City LFC v Sutton Coldfield Town Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Lincoln City LFC and Sutton Coldfield Town Women face each other on Sunday both looking for a first win in five games.

Lincoln City have lost two and drawn two of their last four matches, during which time Sutton Coldfield Town have suffered one loss and three draws.

With 23 goals in 16 games so far this season, Sutton Coldfield Town are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Lincoln City, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have also conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The hosts are eighth in the table after 14 games, of which they have won three and drawn five, earning 14 points.

The away team are one place ahead of Lincoln City in seventh, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.

Lincoln City's last match was on Sunday, a 2-1 loss against Sporting Khalsa Women.

Sutton Coldfield Town drew 2-2 with Leafield Athletic Women last time out, on February 20.
