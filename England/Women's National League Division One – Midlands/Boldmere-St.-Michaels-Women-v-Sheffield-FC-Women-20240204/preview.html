Boldmere St. Michaels Women v Sheffield FC Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Sheffield FC Women face a challenge away against high-flying Boldmere St. Michaels Women on Sunday.

Sheffield FC Women are 11th in the Women's National League Division One – Midlands table, and have picked up just one win and four draws in their 12 games to date.

Boldmere St. Michaels, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 27 points, having won nine and drawn none, and are four points behind table-toppers Loughborough Lightning W.

With six goals in 12 games so far this season, Sheffield FC are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Boldmere St. Michaels, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in fantastic form in Women's National League Division One Midlands, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Boldmere St. Michaels's 15.

Boldmere St. Michaels's last match was on January 14, a 3-0 win against Leafield Athletic Women.

Sheffield FC drew 0-0 with Solihull Moors Women last time out, on Sunday.
