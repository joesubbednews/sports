Sporting Khalsa Women v Peterborough United Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One Midlands's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when third-placed Sporting Khalsa Women host fourth-placed Peterborough United Women.

Sporting have picked up nine wins and one draw from 13 games so far this season, and sit one point above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Peterborough United, meanwhile, have won nine, picking up 27 points.

With 52 goals in 13 games so far this season, Sporting are the league's highest scorers with 4.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Peterborough United are also above average scorers, with 2.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Sporting and Peterborough United have played each other on six occasions. They won three each.

On average, Sporting scored 2.3 goals and Peterborough United 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 28, when Sporting won 5-0 away.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Sporting's 15.

Sporting's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-0 win against Boldmere St. Michaels Women.

Peterborough United beat Leafield Athletic Women 5-1 last time out, on February 4.
