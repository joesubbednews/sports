Peterborough United Women v Loughborough Lightning Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One Midlands's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when third-placed Peterborough United Women host second-placed Loughborough Lightning Women.

Peterborough United have picked up 11 wins and one draw from 16 games so far this season, and sit seven points below the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Loughborough Lightning, meanwhile, have won 13 and drawn two, picking up 41 points.

With 47 goals in 16 games so far this season, Peterborough United are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Loughborough Lightning are also above average scorers, with 2.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home team are in good form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Peterborough United's 13.

Peterborough United's last match was on March 13, a 6-1 win against Notts County Women.

Loughborough Lightning beat Leek Town Women 4-0 last time out, on March 14.
