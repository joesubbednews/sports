Solihull Moors Women v Peterborough United Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Peterborough United Women travel for Sunday's match against Solihull Moors Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Division One Midlands.

Peterborough United, who sit fourth in the league after 17 games, fell to a 2-1 home defeat to Loughborough Lightning Women on March 24.

They face a Solihull Moors side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-0 victory against Lincoln City LFC, and who sit sixth in the table.

With 48 goals in 17 games so far this season, Peterborough United are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Solihull Moors, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Solihull Moors's six.

In the last five years, Solihull Moors and Peterborough United have played each other on five occasions. Solihull Moors won two of them and Peterborough United three.

On average, Solihull Moors scored 2.0 goals and Peterborough United 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 29, when Peterborough United won 1-0 at home.
