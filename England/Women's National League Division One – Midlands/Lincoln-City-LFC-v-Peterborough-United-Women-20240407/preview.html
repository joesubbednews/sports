Lincoln City LFC v Peterborough United Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Lincoln City LFC host Peterborough United Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Division One Midlands.

In their last league match, on March 31, Lincoln City lost to Sporting Khalsa Women 7-1 away.

Peterborough United won, 4-3 away at Solihull Moors Women.

With 52 goals in 18 games so far this season, Peterborough United are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Lincoln City, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The away side are third in the table after 18 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn one, earning 37 points.

The hosts are five places behind Peterborough United in eighth, with four wins and seven draws putting them on 19 points.

Lincoln City are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Peterborough United's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Lincoln City's five.

In the last five years, Lincoln City and Peterborough United have played each other on five occasions. Lincoln City won two of them and Peterborough United three.

On average, Lincoln City scored 2.6 goals and Peterborough United 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 6, when Peterborough United won 4-1 at home.
