Solihull Moors Women v Leek Town Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Solihull Moors Women face Leek Town Women on Sunday looking to secure a first win in six Women's National League Division One – Midlands games.

Solihull Moors have lost two and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Leafield Athletic Women on November 16.

They face a Leek Town side who have won one and drawn three over that time.

Leek Town are ninth in the table after 13 games, of which they have won three and drawn four, earning 13 points.

Solihull Moors are three places ahead of the away side in sixth, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.

With 17 goals in 14 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Leek Town are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

In the last five years, Solihull Moors and Leek Town have played each other on five occasions. Solihull Moors won three of them and they drew twice.

On average, Solihull Moors scored 4.2 goals and Leek Town 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when Solihull Moors won 4-1 away.

Solihull Moors's last match was on February 11, a 0-0 draw against Sutton Coldfield Town Women.

Leek Town drew 1-1 with Leafield Athletic Women last time out, on January 28.
