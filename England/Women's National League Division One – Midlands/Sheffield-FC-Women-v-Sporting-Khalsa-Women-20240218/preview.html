Sheffield FC Women v Sporting Khalsa Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Sheffield FC Women face Sporting Khalsa Women on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-1 victory over Boldmere St. Michaels Women on February 4, Sheffield FC sit 11th in the Women's National League Division One Midlands.

They travel to play a Sporting side fourth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-1 against Loughborough Lightning Women, on February 11.

With 49 goals in 12 games so far this season, Sporting are the league's highest scorers with 4.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Sheffield FC, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The home side are in bad form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Sheffield FC's four.

Over the last two years, Sheffield FC and Sporting have played each other twice. Sporting won both of them.

Their last meeting was on March 19, when Sporting won 3-0 at home.
