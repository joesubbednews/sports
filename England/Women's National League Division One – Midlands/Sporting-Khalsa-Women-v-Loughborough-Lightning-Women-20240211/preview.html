Sporting Khalsa Women v Loughborough Lightning Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Women's National League Division One Midlands's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when fourth-placed Sporting Khalsa Women host table-topping Loughborough Lightning Women.

Sporting have picked up eight wins and one draw from 11 games so far this season, and sit seven points below the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Loughborough Lightning, meanwhile, have won 10 and drawn two of 13, picking up 32 points.

With 48 goals in 11 games so far this season, Sporting are the league's highest scorers with 4.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 12 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Loughborough Lightning are also above average scorers, with 2.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in Women's National League Division One Midlands, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Sporting's 16.

Sporting's last match was on January 28, a 5-0 win against Peterborough United Women.

Loughborough Lightning drew 3-3 with Lincoln City LFC last time out, on February 4.
