Sutton Coldfield Town Women v Loughborough Lightning Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sutton Coldfield Town Women and Loughborough Lightning Women come into Sunday's early kick-off in contrasting spells, with the away team hitting a rich vein of form while the hosts struggle.

Sutton Coldfield Town have drawn four and lost two of the last six, while Loughborough Lightning have taken five wins and one draw.

With 46 goals in 17 games so far this season, Loughborough Lightning are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Sutton Coldfield Town, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The visitors are second in the table after 17 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn two, earning 44 points.

The home team are five places behind Loughborough Lightning in seventh, with five wins and five draws putting them on 20 points.

Sutton Coldfield Town's last match was on Sunday, a 2-1 loss against Northampton Town Women.

Loughborough Lightning beat Peterborough United Women 2-1 last time out, also on Sunday.
