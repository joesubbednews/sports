Peterborough United Women v Leek Town Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Leek Town Women face a challenge away against high-flying Peterborough United Women on Sunday.

Leek Town Women are ninth in the Women's National League Division One – Midlands table, and have picked up three wins and four draws in their 13 games to date.

Peterborough United, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 27 points, having won nine and drawn none, and are five points behind table-toppers Loughborough Lightning Women.

With 34 goals in 13 games so far this season, Peterborough United are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Leek Town, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Division One Midlands, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Peterborough United's 12.

In the last five years, Peterborough United and Leek Town have played each other on five occasions. They won two each, and they drew once.

On average, Peterborough United scored 1.8 goals and Leek Town 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when Peterborough United won 3-1 away.

Peterborough United's last match was on Sunday, a 5-1 win against Leafield Athletic Women.

Leek Town drew 1-1 with Leafield Athletic Women last time out, on January 28.
