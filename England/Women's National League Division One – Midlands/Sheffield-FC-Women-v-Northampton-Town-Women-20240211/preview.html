Sheffield FC Women v Northampton Town Women

Women's National League Division One – Midlands
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sheffield FC Women are on a poor run ahead of hosting Northampton Town Women on Sunday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Sheffield FC have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Women's National League Division One – Midlands games, and face a Northampton Town side whose last six games have brought four wins and two losses.

Sheffield FC are 11th in the table after 13 games, of which they have won one and drawn four, earning seven points.

Northampton Town are six places ahead of the hosts in fifth, with seven wins and one draw putting them on 22 points.

With six goals in 13 games so far this season, Sheffield FC are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last three years, Sheffield FC and Northampton Town have played each other on three occasions. Sheffield FC won one of them and Northampton Town the other.

Their last meeting was on November 26, when Northampton Town won 3-0 at home.

Sheffield FC's last match was on February 4, a 1-0 loss against Boldmere St. Michaels Women.

Northampton Town beat Notts County Women 6-0 last time out, also on February 4.
