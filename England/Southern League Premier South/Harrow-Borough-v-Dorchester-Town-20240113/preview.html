Harrow Borough v Dorchester Town

Southern League Premier South | Earlsmead Stadium
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match at the Earlsmead Stadium sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 21st-ranked Harrow Borough host 20th-placed Dorchester Town.

Harrow have picked up 14 points from their first 14 Southern League Premier South games, with three wins and five draws.

That is five points less than Dorchester have collected, having won five and drawn four.

Harrow are in bad form in Southern League Premier South, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

And also with a win and five losses over that period, Dorchester's form is identical – they have both taken three points from 18.

With 30 goals in 24 games so far this season, Borough are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Harrow and Dorchester have played each other on five occasions. Harrow won one of them, Dorchester three, and they drew once.

On average, Harrow scored 1.6 goals and Dorchester 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Harrow's last match was on January 6, a 3-0 loss against Hanwell Town.

Dorchester lost 4-2 against Hungerford Town last time out, also on January 6.
