Sholing v Winchester City

Southern League Premier South | VT Group Sports Ground
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Winchester City face Sholing on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Southern League Premier South.

Winchester are unbeaten in six, with two wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Sholing team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 30 goals in 25 games so far this season, Sholing are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Winchester, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are 10th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn six, earning 36 points.

City are two places behind Sholing in 12th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

Sholing's last match was on January 6, a 3-1 win against AFC Totton.

Winchester drew 1-1 with Gosport Borough last time out, on January 1.
