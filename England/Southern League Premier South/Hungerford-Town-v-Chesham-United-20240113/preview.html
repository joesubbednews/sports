Hungerford Town v Chesham United

Southern League Premier South | Bulpit Lane
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Hungerford Town and Chesham United promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Southern League Premier South's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Bulpit Lane, Hungerford and Chesham sit second and joint-third in the goal-scoring charts, with 51 and 49 goals respectively.

Chesham are top of the table after 24 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn four, earning 52 points.

Hungerford are four places behind United in fifth, with 11 wins and nine draws putting them on 42 points.

Town are in reasonable form in Southern League Premier South, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home team's 12.

Hungerford's last match was on January 6, a 4-2 win against Dorchester Town.
