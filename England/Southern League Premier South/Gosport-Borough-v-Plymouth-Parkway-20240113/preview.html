Gosport Borough v Plymouth Parkway

Southern League Premier South | Privett Park
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of Southern League Premier South's meanest defences go head-to-head at Privett Park on Saturday, when Gosport Borough host Plymouth Parkway.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Gosport to date: the home side have let in just 20 goals in 22 games.

Plymouth Parkway have conceded 29 goals in 18 games, giving them the joint-third tightest back line so far this season.

Gosport are fourth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

Plymouth Parkway are 12 places behind Borough in 16th, with six wins and five draws putting them on 23 points.

The hosts are in reasonable form in Southern League Premier South, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Gosport's 11.

Over the last year, Gosport and Plymouth Parkway have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when Gosport won 2-0 away.

Gosport's last match was on January 1, a 1-1 draw against Winchester City.

Plymouth Parkway drew 1-1 with Tiverton Town last time out, on December 26.
