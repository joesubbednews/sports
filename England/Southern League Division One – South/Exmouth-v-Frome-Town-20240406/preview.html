Exmouth v Frome Town

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Exmouth host Frome Town in Saturday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Southern League Division One South.

Exmouth, who sit 17th in the league after 30 games, fell to a 1-3 home defeat to Willand Rovers on March 30.

They face a Frome side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against Melksham Town, and who sit second in the table.

With 67 goals in 31 games so far this season, Frome are the league's highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Exmouth, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Southern League Division One South, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Exmouth's 16.

In the last three years, Exmouth and Frome have played each other on three occasions. Exmouth won one of them and Frome the other.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Frome won 2-1 at home.
