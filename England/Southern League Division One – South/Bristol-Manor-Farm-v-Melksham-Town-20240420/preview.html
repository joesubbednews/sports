Bristol Manor Farm v Melksham Town

Southern League Division One – South | The Creek
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bristol Manor Farm host Melksham Town at the Creek on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Southern League Division One South.

Bristol Manor Farm have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Melksham side who also won their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 58 goals in 33 games so far this season, Bristol Manor Farm are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Melksham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The hosts are fourth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn eight, earning 50 points.

Town are 12 places behind Bristol Manor Farm in 16th, with eight wins and 11 draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last five years, Bristol Manor Farm and Melksham have played each other on five occasions. Bristol Manor Farm won one of them, Melksham two, and they drew twice.

On average, Bristol Manor Farm scored 2.2 goals and Melksham 2.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Bristol Manor Farm's last match was on April 13, a 4-0 win against Westbury United.

Melksham beat Bemerton Heath Harleq. 4-0 last time out, also on April 13.
