Bideford v Paulton Rovers

Southern League Division One – South | The Sports Ground
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bideford and Paulton Rovers both come into Saturday's kick-off at the Sports Ground in terrible form, having picked up two and one points respectively in their last six games.

Bideford have drawn two and lost four of the last six, while Paulton have taken just one draw.

Paulton are 19th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won seven and drawn three, earning 24 points.

Bideford are 11 places ahead of Rovers in eighth, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

In the last 10 years, Bideford and Paulton have played each other on 11 occasions. Bideford won three of them, Paulton seven, and they drew once.

On average, Bideford scored 0.8 goals and Paulton 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 26, when Bideford won 1-0 away.

With 32 goals in 27 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Bideford's last match was on March 16, a 0-0 draw against Westbury United.

Paulton drew 4-4 with Bemerton Heath Harleq. last time out, also on March 16.
