Willand Rovers v Bashley

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Willand Rovers face Bashley on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Southern League Division One South.

Willand are unbeaten in five, with one win and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Bashley team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

Bashley are 16th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won seven and drawn seven, earning 28 points.

Willand are nine places ahead of the visitors in seventh, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

With 31 goals in 25 games so far this season, Bashley are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Rovers, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last three years, Willand and Bashley have played each other on three occasions. Bashley won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 26, when Bashley won 3-2 at home.

Willand's last match was on March 16, a 0-0 draw against Bishop's Cleeve.

Bashley beat Mousehole 2-0 last time out, also on March 16.
