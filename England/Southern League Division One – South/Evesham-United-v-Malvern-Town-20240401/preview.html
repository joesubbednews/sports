Evesham United v Malvern Town

Southern League Division One – South | The Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Evesham United host Malvern Town on Monday at the Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium in the Southern League Division One South.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Evesham lost to Bristol Manor Farm 2-0 away.

Malvern drew, 1-1 at home against Melksham Town on Friday.

With 50 goals in 28 games so far this season, Malvern are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Evesham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

United are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

Evesham are eighth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn 11, earning 41 points.

The away side are five places behind Evesham in 13th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.
