Hamworthy United v Westbury United

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table Hamworthy United host 18th-placed Westbury United.

Hamworthy have picked up five points from their first five Southern League Division One – South games, with one win and two draws.

That is 17 points less than Westbury have collected, having won five and drawn seven.

Hamworthy are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

And also with a win and two draws over that period, Westbury's form is identical – they have both taken five points from 18.

With 35 goals in 25 games so far this season, Westbury United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Hamworthy United are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

In the last three years, Hamworthy and Westbury have played each other on three occasions. Hamworthy won two of them and Westbury one.

Their last meeting was on March 18, when Hamworthy won 3-2 at home.

Hamworthy's last match was on September 26, a 1-1 draw against Melksham Town.

Westbury lost 3-2 against Frome Town last time out, on February 16.
