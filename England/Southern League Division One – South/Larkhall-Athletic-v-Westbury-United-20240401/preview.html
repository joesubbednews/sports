Larkhall Athletic v Westbury United

Southern League Division One – South | The Plain Ham Ground
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Westbury United travel to Larkhall Athletic on Monday in the Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Yate Town 2-0 at home.

Larkhall, meanwhile, won their last match, 3-1 against Bashley.

With 38 goals in 29 games so far this season, Westbury are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Larkhall, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

United are 18th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won six and drawn eight, earning 26 points.

Athletic are 12 places ahead of the away team in sixth, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Westbury's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Larkhall's 10.

In the last three years, Larkhall and Westbury have played each other on three occasions. Larkhall won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
