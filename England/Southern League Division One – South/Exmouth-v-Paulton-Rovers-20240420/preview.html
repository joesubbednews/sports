Exmouth v Paulton Rovers

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 17th-ranked Exmouth host 19th-placed Paulton Rovers.

Exmouth have picked up 31 points from their first 31 Southern League Division One – South games, with eight wins and seven draws.

That is six points more than Paulton have collected, having won seven and drawn four.

Exmouth are in reasonable form in the Southern League Division One South, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Paulton's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the home side's 11.

With 33 goals in 32 games so far this season, Rovers are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 71 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Exmouth are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Exmouth and Paulton have played each other on three occasions. Exmouth won one of them and Paulton the other.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when Paulton won 2-1 at home.

Exmouth's last match was on April 13, a 1-0 loss against Cribbs.

Paulton lost 1-0 against Larkhall Athletic last time out, also on April 13.
