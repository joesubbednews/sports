Melksham Town v Frome Town

Southern League Division One – South
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Frome Town visit Melksham Town on Monday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Southern League Division One South.

Frome have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Melksham side who drew their last match, and have collected six points from the last possible 18.

With 65 goals in 30 games so far this season, Frome are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Melksham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Frome Town are second in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn six, earning 63 points.

Melksham Town are 13 places behind the visitors in 15th, with seven wins and 11 draws putting them on 32 points.

In the last five years, Melksham and Frome have played each other on five occasions. Melksham won one of them, Frome two, and they drew twice.

On average, Melksham scored 1.8 goals and Frome 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Melksham's last match was on Friday, a 1-1 draw against Malvern Town.

Frome beat Bideford 4-0 last time out, on Saturday.
