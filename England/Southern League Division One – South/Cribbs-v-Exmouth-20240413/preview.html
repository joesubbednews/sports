Cribbs v Exmouth

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Exmouth face a challenge away against high-flying Cribbs on Saturday.

Exmouth are 17th in the Southern League Division One – South table, and have picked up eight wins and seven draws in their 31 games to date.

Cribbs, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 58 points, having won 16 and drawn 10 of their first 33 matches, and are 11 points behind table-toppers Wimborne Town.

With 44 goals in 31 games so far this season, Exmouth are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Cribbs, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One South, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Cribbs's eight.

Cribbs's last match was on April 6, a 3-0 win against Bashley.

Exmouth drew 0-0 with Frome Town last time out, also on April 6.
