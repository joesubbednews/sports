Cribbs v Bishop's Cleeve

Southern League Division One – South
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 1.00pm

Bishop's Cleeve travel to Cribbs on Monday lunchtime in the Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Bemerton Heath Harleq. 2-0 at home.

Cribbs also won their last match, 1-0 against Paulton Rovers.

With 59 goals in 31 games so far this season, Bishop's Cleeve are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Cribbs, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home side are third in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn 10, earning 55 points.

The visitors are four places behind Cribbs in seventh, with 11 wins and nine draws putting them on 42 points.

Cribbs are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Bishop's Cleeve's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.
