Bemerton Heath Harleq. v Exmouth

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Exmouth face Bemerton Heath Harleq. away on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

They travel to play a Bemerton Heath Harleq. side fourth in the standings, who were held in their last match, 4-4 against Paulton Rovers.

With 38 goals in 28 games so far this season, Exmouth are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Bemerton Heath Harleq., meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One South, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Bemerton Heath Harleq.'s eight.
