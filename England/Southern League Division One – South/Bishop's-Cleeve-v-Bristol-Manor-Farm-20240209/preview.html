Bishop's Cleeve v Bristol Manor Farm

Southern League Division One – South | Kayte Lane
Friday, 9 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Bristol Manor Farm come to Kayte Lane to play Bishop's Cleeve on late Friday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

With 49 goals in 23 games so far this season, Bishop's Cleeve are the league's highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Bristol Manor Farm, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Bishop's Cleeve and Bristol Manor Farm have played each other on five occasions. They won two each, and they drew once.

On average, Bishop's Cleeve scored 2.2 goals and Bristol Manor Farm 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 3, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The home team are 10th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

The visitors are one place behind Bishop's Cleeve in 11th, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on the same number of points.

Bishop's Cleeve's last match was on Saturday, a 4-4 draw against Westbury United.

Bristol Manor Farm lost 2-1 against Wimborne Town last time out, also on Saturday.
