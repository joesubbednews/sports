Bideford v Bashley

Southern League Division One – South | The Sports Ground
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bideford host Bashley on Saturday at the Sports Ground in the Southern League Division One South.

In their last league match, on April 6, Bideford beat Bishop's Cleeve 1-0 away.

Bashley lost, 3-0 at home against Cribbs.

Bashley are 15th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

Bideford are five places ahead of the visitors in 10th, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

The home team are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Bashley's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Bideford's six.

In the last three years, Bideford and Bashley have played each other on three occasions. Bideford won two of them and Bashley one.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Bideford won 3-1 away.

With 37 goals in 29 games so far this season, Bideford are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Bashley are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.
