Frome Town v Bristol Manor Farm

Southern League Division One – South | AlderSmith Stadium
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Southern League Division One South's top sides face each other at the AlderSmith Stadium in Saturday's kick-off, when second-placed Frome Town host fourth-placed Bristol Manor Farm.

Frome have picked up 21 wins and eight draws from 34 games so far this season, and sit 18 points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Bristol Manor Farm, meanwhile, have won 15 and drawn eight, picking up 53 points.

With 69 goals in 34 games so far this season, Frome are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Bristol Manor Farm are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Town are in good form in the Southern League Division One South, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

But with five wins and one loss over that period, the away team's form is even better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 14.

In the last five years, Frome and Bristol Manor Farm have played each other on five occasions. Frome won two of them and they drew three times.

On average, Frome scored 1.2 goals and Bristol Manor Farm 0.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 9, when Frome won 2-1 away.

Frome's last match was on April 20, a 1-1 draw against Wimborne Town.

Bristol Manor Farm beat Melksham Town 3-0 last time out, also on April 20.
