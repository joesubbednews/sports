Bashley v Larkhall Athletic

Southern League Division One – South | The Recreation Ground
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bashley host Larkhall Athletic on Saturday at the Recreation Ground in the Southern League Division One South.

In their last league match, on March 23, Bashley beat Willand Rovers 2-0 away.

Larkhall also won, 2-0 at home against Malvern Town.

With 33 goals in 26 games so far this season, Bashley are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Larkhall, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are 15th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Athletic are six places ahead of Bashley in ninth, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

Bashley are in good form in the Southern League Division One South, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.

In the last three years, Bashley and Larkhall have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on January 31, when Larkhall won 2-0 at home.
