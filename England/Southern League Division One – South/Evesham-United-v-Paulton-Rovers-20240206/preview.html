Evesham United v Paulton Rovers

Southern League Division One – South | The Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Paulton Rovers make the journey to the Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium to play Evesham United on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Paulton's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Southern League Division One – South matches, while their opponents have earned 14 from a possible 18.

Evesham are fifth in the table after 24 games, of which they have won nine and drawn nine, earning 36 points.

Paulton are 11 places behind United in 16th, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

With 25 goals in 23 games so far this season, Rovers are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last five years, Evesham and Paulton have played each other on five occasions. Evesham won three of them and Paulton two.

On average, Evesham scored 1.6 goals and Paulton 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 28 2023, when Paulton won 3-2 at home.

Evesham's last match was on Saturday, a 1-1 draw against Bashley.

Paulton lost 2-1 against Tavistock last time out, also on Saturday.
