Larkhall Athletic v Willand Rovers

Southern League Division One – South | The Plain Ham Ground
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Larkhall Athletic host Willand Rovers at the Plain Ham Ground on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Southern League Division One South.

Larkhall have picked up 10 points from their last six games, and they face a Willand side who also won their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 39 goals in 21 games so far this season, Larkhall are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Willand, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Larkhall and Willand have played each other on seven occasions. Larkhall won three of them, Willand two, and they drew twice.

On average, Larkhall scored 1.0 goal and Willand 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Rovers are seventh in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn three, earning 33 points.

Athletic are four places behind the away side in 11th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Larkhall's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-0 win against Bashley.

Willand beat Mousehole 2-1 last time out, on January 27.
