Cribbs v Willand Rovers

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cribbs face Willand Rovers on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Southern League Division One South.

Cribbs are unbeaten in 14, with nine wins and five draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Willand team who have won four and drawn three over the same number of games.

Cribbs are third in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn six, earning 39 points.

Willand are five places behind the home team in eighth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

With 34 goals in 20 games so far this season, Cribbs are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Rovers are also average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Cribbs's last match was on January 13, a 1-1 draw against Evesham United.

Willand drew 0-0 with Westbury United last time out, also on January 13.
