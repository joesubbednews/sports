Paulton Rovers v Westbury United

Southern League Division One – South
Friday, 9 February 2024 @ 7.30pm

Paulton Rovers welcome Westbury United on late Friday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Paulton's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Southern League Division One – South matches, while their opponents have earned six from a possible 18.

Westbury are 17th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

Paulton are one place ahead of United in 16th, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

With 25 goals in 23 games so far this season, Rovers are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last three years, Paulton and Westbury have played each other on three occasions. Paulton won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Paulton won 4-3 away.

Paulton's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 loss against Tavistock.

Westbury drew 4-4 with Bishop's Cleeve last time out, also on Saturday.
