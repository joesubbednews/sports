Hamworthy United v Bideford

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Hamworthy United face Bideford on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-1 victory over Melksham Town on September 26, Hamworthy sit bottom of Southern League Division One South.

They travel to play a Bideford side fourth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-0 against Bemerton Heath Harleq., on Tuesday.

Hamworthy are in disappointing form in Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Bideford's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to United's five.

With nine goals in six games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Hamworthy and Bideford have played each other twice. Hamworthy won both of them.

Their last meeting was on December 3 2022, when Hamworthy won 5-1 at home.
