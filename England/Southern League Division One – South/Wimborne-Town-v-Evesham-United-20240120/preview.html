Wimborne Town v Evesham United

Southern League Division One – South | Cuthbury
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wimborne Town host Evesham United in Saturday's match at Cuthbury looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Southern League Division One South.

Wimborne, who sit second in the league after 20 games, fell to a 3-1 away defeat to Larkhall Athletic on January 13.

They face an Evesham side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Cribbs, and who sit sixth in the table.

With 43 goals in 20 games so far this season, Wimborne are the league's highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Evesham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Town are in good form in Southern League Division One South, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, United's form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.

In the last three years, Wimborne and Evesham have played each other on three occasions. Wimborne won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when they played out a 0-0 draw.
