Melksham Town v Bemerton Heath Harleq.

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bemerton Heath Harleq. travel looking to secure a first win in six Southern League Division One – South games against Melksham Town on Saturday.

Bemerton Heath Harleq. have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Larkhall Athletic on March 9.

They face a Melksham side who have won just one and drawn one over that time.

With 42 goals in 31 games so far this season, Melksham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Bemerton Heath Harleq., meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Town are 16th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 11, earning 32 points.

The away side are eight places ahead of the hosts in eighth, with 13 wins and six draws putting them on 45 points.

Melksham's last match was on April 6, a 4-3 loss against Evesham United.

Bemerton Heath Harleq. drew 1-1 with Westbury United last time out, also on April 6.
