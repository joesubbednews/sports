Bishop's Cleeve v Tavistock

Southern League Division One – South | Kayte Lane
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Tavistock come to Kayte Lane to play Bishop's Cleeve on Saturday in poor form, having collected just three points from their last six games.

The visitors have won just one of their last six fixtures, while Bishop's Cleeve have three wins and a draw.

With 42 goals in 21 games so far this season, Bishop's Cleeve are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Tavistock are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The visitors are 15th in the table after 17 games, of which they have won seven and drawn two, earning 23 points.

The home team are eight places ahead of Tavistock in seventh, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

In the last three years, Bishop's Cleeve and Tavistock have played each other on three occasions. Bishop's Cleeve won one of them and Tavistock the other.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Tavistock won 3-1 at home.

Bishop's Cleeve's last match was on January 13, a 6-1 win against Melksham Town.

Tavistock beat Frome Town 6-2 last time out, on January 17.
