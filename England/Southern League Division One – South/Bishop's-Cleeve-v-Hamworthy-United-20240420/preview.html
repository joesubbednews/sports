Bishop's Cleeve v Hamworthy United

Southern League Division One – South | Kayte Lane
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bishop's Cleeve face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Hamworthy United defence on Saturday.

With 63 goals in 34 games, Bishop's Cleeve are the third-highest scorers in the Southern League Division One South ahead of the 3pm kick-off at Kayte Lane.

They face a Hamworthy side who have scored nine in six matches, but conceded only 14 goals, putting them top of the league's tightest defences.

Hamworthy are bottom of the table after six games, of which they have won one and drawn two, earning five points.

Bishop's Cleeve are 12 places ahead of United in eighth, with 12 wins and nine draws putting them on 45 points.

The home side are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Bishop's Cleeve's 10.

Over the last two years, Bishop's Cleeve and Hamworthy have played each other twice. Hamworthy won both of them.

Their last meeting was on April 22, when Hamworthy won 2-1 at home.

Bishop's Cleeve's last match was on April 12, a 1-0 loss against Frome Town.

Hamworthy drew 1-1 with Melksham Town last time out, on September 26.
