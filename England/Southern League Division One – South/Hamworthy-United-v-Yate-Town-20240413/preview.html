Hamworthy United v Yate Town

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Hamworthy United face a challenge against high-flying Yate Town on Saturday.

Hamworthy United are rooted to the bottom of the Southern League Division One – South table, and have picked up one win and two draws in their six games to date.

Yate, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 47 points, having won 13 and drawn eight of their first 33 matches, and are 22 points behind table-toppers Wimborne Town.

With nine goals in six games so far this season, Hamworthy are the league's lowest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Yate are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

United are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

Hamworthy's last match was on September 26, a 1-1 draw against Melksham Town.

Yate beat Willand Rovers 3-0 last time out, on April 6.
