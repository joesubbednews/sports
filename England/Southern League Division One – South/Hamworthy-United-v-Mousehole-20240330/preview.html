Hamworthy United v Mousehole

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mousehole face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Hamworthy United defence on Saturday.

With 53 goals in 28 games, Mousehole are the fourth-highest scorers in the Southern League Division One South ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Hamworthy side who have scored nine in six matches, but conceded only 14 goals, putting them top of the league's tightest defences.

Hamworthy are bottom of the table after six games, of which they have won one and drawn two, earning five points.

Mousehole are 10 places ahead of United in 10th, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Hamworthy's five.

Hamworthy's last match was on September 26, a 1-1 draw against Melksham Town.

Mousehole beat Bristol Manor Farm 2-0 last time out, on March 23.
