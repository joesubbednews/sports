Wimborne Town v Paulton Rovers

Southern League Division One – South | Cuthbury
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Paulton Rovers face a challenge away against high-flying Wimborne Town at Cuthbury on Saturday.

Paulton Rovers are 19th in the Southern League Division One – South table, and have picked up seven wins and four draws in their 30 games to date.

Wimborne, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 66 points, having won 20 and drawn six.

With 65 goals in 30 games so far this season, Wimborne are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Paulton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Town are in fantastic form in the Southern League Division One South, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Rovers' form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the home side's 16.

In the last 10 years, Wimborne and Paulton have played each other on six occasions. Wimborne won three of them, Paulton one, and they drew twice.

On average, Wimborne scored 1.5 goals and Paulton 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 30, when Wimborne won 2-0 away.

Wimborne's last match was on March 23, a 3-2 win against Westbury United.

Paulton lost 3-0 against Yate Town last time out, on Monday.
