Westbury United v Bashley

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Westbury United face Bashley on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-0 victory over Malvern Town on April 20, Westbury sit 18th in the Southern League Division One South.

They travel to play a Bashley side 15th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 0-0 against Tavistock.

Westbury are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Bashley's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to United's five.

With 42 goals in 32 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Westbury and Bashley have played each other twice. Westbury won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on April 18 2023, when they played out a 0-0 draw.
