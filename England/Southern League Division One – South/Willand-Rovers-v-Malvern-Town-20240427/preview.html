Willand Rovers v Malvern Town

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Willand Rovers welcome Malvern Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their six-game losing streak.

Willand's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Southern League Division One – South matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

With 49 goals in 35 games so far this season, Willand are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Malvern, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Rovers are 13th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn eight, earning 41 points.

Town are one place ahead of the hosts in 12th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

Willand's last match was on April 20, a 3-2 loss against Evesham United.

Malvern lost 3-0 against Westbury United last time out, also on April 20.
