Tavistock v Frome Town

Southern League Division One – South
Wednesday, 17 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Tavistock face a challenge against high-flying Frome Town on Wednesday.

Tavistock are 17th in the Southern League Division One – South table, and have picked up six wins and two draws in their 16 games to date.

Frome, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 40 points, having won 12 and drawn four of their first 19 matches.

With 40 goals in 19 games so far this season, Frome are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Tavistock, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in Southern League Division One South, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Tavistock's three.

Over the last two years, Tavistock and Frome have played each other twice. Tavistock won both of them.

Their last meeting was on April 22, when Tavistock won 4-3 at home.

Tavistock's last match was on Saturday, a 2-0 loss against Exmouth.

Frome beat Paulton Rovers 1-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
