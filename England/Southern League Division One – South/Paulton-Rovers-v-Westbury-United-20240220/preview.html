Paulton Rovers v Westbury United

Southern League Division One – South
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.30pm

Tuesday's late match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 17th-ranked Paulton Rovers host 18th-placed Westbury United.

Paulton have picked up 23 points from their first 23 Southern League Division One – South games, with seven wins and two draws.

That is one point more than Westbury have collected, having won five and drawn seven.

Paulton are in bad form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Westbury's form is slightly better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Rovers' three.

With 26 goals in 24 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last three years, Paulton and Westbury have played each other on three occasions. Paulton won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Paulton won 4-3 away.

Paulton's last match was on Saturday, a 4-1 loss against Bashley.

Westbury lost 3-2 against Frome Town last time out, on Friday.
