Melksham Town v Hamworthy United

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hamworthy United travel to Melksham Town on Saturday in the Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Melksham Town 1-1 at home.

Melksham, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Exmouth.

Hamworthy are bottom of the table after six games, of which they have won one and drawn two, earning five points.

Melksham are five places ahead of United in 15th, with seven wins and 10 draws putting them on 31 points.

Town are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

And also with a win and two draws over that period, the away side's form is identical – they have both taken five points from 18.

In the last five years, Melksham and Hamworthy have played each other on four occasions. Hamworthy won two of them and they drew twice.

Their last meeting was on September 26, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 37 goals in 28 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Hamworthy are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.
