Tavistock v Yate Town

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Yate Town travel to Tavistock on Saturday in the Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Cribbs 3-3 at home.

Tavistock also drew their last match, 0-0 against Bashley.

Tavistock are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Yate's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

With 51 goals in 30 games so far this season, Tavistock are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Tavistock are 14th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn five, earning 38 points.

The visitors are seven places ahead of the hosts in seventh, with 13 wins and nine draws putting them on 48 points.

Over the last two years, Tavistock and Yate have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on January 30, when Yate won 5-1 at home.
