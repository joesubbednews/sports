Mousehole v Cribbs

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cribbs travel for Saturday's match against Mousehole looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Southern League Division One South.

Cribbs, who sit third in the league after 27 games, fell to a 5-1 away defeat to Larkhall Athletic on Wednesday.

They face a Mousehole side who also lost their last match, a 3-1 defeat to Wimborne Town, and who sit 11th in the table.

With 49 goals in 25 games so far this season, Mousehole are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Cribbs, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One South, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Mousehole's nine.
