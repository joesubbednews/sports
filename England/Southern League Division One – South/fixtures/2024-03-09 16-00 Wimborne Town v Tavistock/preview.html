Wimborne Town v Tavistock

Southern League Division One – South | Cuthbury
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wimborne Town face Tavistock on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Southern League Division One South.

Wimborne are unbeaten in seven, with five wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Tavistock team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 57 goals in 27 games so far this season, Wimborne are the league's highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Tavistock are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 27 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn six, earning 57 points.

The visitors are 13 places behind the home team in 14th, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.

Over the last two years, Wimborne and Tavistock have played each other twice. Wimborne won both of them.

Their last meeting was on January 28 2023, when Wimborne won 2-0 at home.

Wimborne's last match was on March 2, a 3-1 win against Mousehole.

Tavistock drew 1-1 with Melksham Town last time out, on Wednesday.
