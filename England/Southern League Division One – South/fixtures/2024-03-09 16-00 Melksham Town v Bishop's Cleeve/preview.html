Melksham Town v Bishop's Cleeve

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Melksham Town are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Bishop's Cleeve on Saturday, with just two points collected from their last six games.

Melksham have picked up two draws and four losses in their last six Southern League Division One – South games, and face a Bishop's Cleeve side whose last six games have brought one win and two draws.

With 56 goals in 27 games so far this season, Bishop's Cleeve are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Melksham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are 15th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won six and drawn 10, earning 28 points.

The away team are two places ahead of the hosts in 13th, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last three years, Melksham and Bishop's Cleeve have played each other on three occasions. Bishop's Cleeve won all of them.

Their last meeting was on January 13, when Bishop's Cleeve won 6-1 at home.

Melksham's last match was on Wednesday, a 1-1 draw against Tavistock.

Bishop's Cleeve drew 1-1 with Exmouth last time out, also on Wednesday.
