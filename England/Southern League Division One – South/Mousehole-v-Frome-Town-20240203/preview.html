Mousehole v Frome Town

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Mousehole and Frome Town promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Southern League Division One South's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Mousehole and Frome sit joint-fourth and third in the goal-scoring charts, with 41 and 44 goals respectively.

Frome are second in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn four, earning 43 points.

Mousehole are 10 places behind Town in 12th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Mousehole's five.

Mousehole's last match was on January 27, a 2-1 loss against Willand Rovers.

Frome beat Bashley 2-1 last time out, also on January 27.
