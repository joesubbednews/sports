Tavistock v Paulton Rovers

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Paulton Rovers make the journey to play Tavistock on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Paulton's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Southern League Division One – South matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

With 24 goals in 22 games so far this season, Paulton are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Tavistock, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

Rovers are 16th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won seven and drawn two, earning 23 points.

The home team are two places ahead of the visitors in 14th, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

In the last three years, Tavistock and Paulton have played each other on three occasions. Tavistock won all of them.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when Tavistock won 2-0 away.

Tavistock's last match was on Tuesday, a 5-1 loss against Yate Town.

Paulton lost 2-0 against Wimborne Town last time out, also on Tuesday.
