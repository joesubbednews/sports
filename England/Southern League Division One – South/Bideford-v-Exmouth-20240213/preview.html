Bideford v Exmouth

Southern League Division One – South | The Sports Ground
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Exmouth make the journey to the Sports Ground to play Bideford on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Exmouth's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Southern League Division One – South matches, while their opponents have earned 13 from a possible 18.

Exmouth are 19th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won three and drawn four, earning 13 points.

Bideford are 14 places ahead of the away side in fifth, with 11 wins and four draws putting them on 37 points.

With 26 goals in 23 games so far this season, Exmouth are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last three years, Bideford and Exmouth have played each other on three occasions. Bideford won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on August 28, when Bideford won 1-0 away.

Bideford's last match was on January 30, a 2-0 loss against Bemerton Heath Harleq..
