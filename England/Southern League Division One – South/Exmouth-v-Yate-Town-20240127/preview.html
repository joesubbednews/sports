Exmouth v Yate Town

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Exmouth face Yate Town on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Tavistock on January 13, Exmouth sit 19th in Southern League Division One South.

They travel to play a Yate side 13th in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Mousehole, on January 20.

Exmouth are in mixed form in Southern League Division One South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Yate's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

With 25 goals in 20 games so far this season, Exmouth are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.
