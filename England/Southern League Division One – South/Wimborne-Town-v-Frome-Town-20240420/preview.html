Wimborne Town v Frome Town

Southern League Division One – South | Cuthbury
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

The Southern League Division One South's top two sides face each other at Cuthbury in Saturday's kick-off, when Wimborne Town host second-placed Frome Town.

Wimborne have picked up 22 wins and six draws from 32 games so far this season, and sit two points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Frome, meanwhile, have won 21 and drawn seven of 33, picking up 70 points.

With 68 goals in 32 games so far this season, Wimborne are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Frome are also above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Wimborne Town are in exceptional form in the Southern League Division One South, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Frome Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to the home side's 18.

In the last 10 years, Wimborne and Frome have played each other on six occasions. Wimborne won one of them, Frome four, and they drew once.

On average, Wimborne scored 1.0 goal and Frome 2.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Wimborne's last match was on April 13, a 1-0 win against Willand Rovers.

Frome beat Bishop's Cleeve 1-0 last time out, on April 12.
