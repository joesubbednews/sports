Mousehole v Bristol Manor Farm

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bristol Manor Farm travel to Mousehole on Saturday in the Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Cribbs 3-1 at home.

Mousehole, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-0 against Bashley.

With 51 goals in 27 games so far this season, Mousehole are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Bristol Manor Farm are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The away side are sixth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn eight, earning 41 points.

The hosts are five places behind Bristol Manor Farm in 11th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

Mousehole are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Bristol Manor Farm's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Mousehole's 10.
