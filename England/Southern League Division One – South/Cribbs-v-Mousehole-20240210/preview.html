Cribbs v Mousehole

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mousehole face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Cribbs defence on Saturday.

With 44 goals in 22 games, Mousehole are the fourth-highest scorers in the Southern League Division One South ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Cribbs side who have scored 37 in 22 matches, but conceded only 24 goals, putting them second among the league's tightest defences – only Hamworthy United have conceded fewer goals.

Cribbs are second in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn six, earning 45 points.

Mousehole are 10 places behind the hosts in 12th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Cribbs are in reasonable form in the Southern League Division One South, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Cribbs's 12.

Cribbs's last match was on February 3, a 2-0 win against Bemerton Heath Harleq..

Mousehole beat Frome Town 3-2 last time out, also on February 3.
