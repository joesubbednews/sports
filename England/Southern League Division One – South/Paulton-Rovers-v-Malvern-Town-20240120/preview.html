Paulton Rovers v Malvern Town

Southern League Division One – South | Athletic Ground
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Malvern Town travel to Paulton Rovers on Saturday in Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Bristol Manor Farm 1-0 at home.

Paulton, meanwhile, lost their last match, 1-0 against Frome Town.

With 23 goals in 20 games so far this season, Paulton are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Malvern, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Rovers are 15th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won seven and drawn two, earning 23 points.

Town are four places ahead of the hosts in 11th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

Paulton are in mixed form in Southern League Division One South, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Paulton's nine.
