Westbury United v Bishop's Cleeve

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Bishop's Cleeve travel to Westbury United on early Saturday in Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Tavistock 4-3 at home.

Westbury also lost their last match, 1-0 against Cribbs.

With 45 goals in 22 games so far this season, Bishop's Cleeve are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Westbury, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

United are 17th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won five and drawn six, earning 21 points.

The away team are seven places ahead of the hosts in 10th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Westbury are in disappointing form in Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Bishop's Cleeve's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Westbury's five.

In the last five years, Westbury and Bishop's Cleeve have played each other on four occasions. Westbury won one of them and Bishop's Cleeve the other.

On average, Westbury scored 1.2 goals and Bishop's Cleeve 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when Bishop's Cleeve won 3-2 at home.
