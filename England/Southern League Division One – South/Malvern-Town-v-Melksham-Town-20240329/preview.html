Malvern Town v Melksham Town

Southern League Division One – South
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Malvern Town host Melksham Town on Friday in the Southern League Division One South.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Malvern lost to Larkhall Athletic 2-0 away.

Melksham also lost, 2-1 away at Exmouth on March 16.

With 37 goals in 28 games so far this season, Melksham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Malvern, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Melksham Town are 16th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 10, earning 31 points.

Malvern Town are three places ahead of the visitors in 13th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

The home side are in mixed form in the Southern League Division One South, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Melksham's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Malvern's nine.
