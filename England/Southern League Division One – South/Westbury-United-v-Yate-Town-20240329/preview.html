Westbury United v Yate Town

Southern League Division One – South
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Westbury United host Yate Town on Friday in the Southern League Division One South.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Westbury lost to Wimborne Town 3-2 away.

Yate also lost, 1-0 at home against Bishop's Cleeve.

Westbury are 18th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won six and drawn eight, earning 26 points.

Yate are six places ahead of United in 12th, with 10 wins and eight draws putting them on 38 points.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

And also with a win and two draws over that period, Town's form is identical – they have both taken five points from 18.

With 38 goals in 28 games so far this season, Westbury are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.
