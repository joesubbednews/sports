Paulton Rovers v Cribbs

Southern League Division One – South | Athletic Ground
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Paulton Rovers face a challenge against high-flying Cribbs at the Athletic Ground on Saturday.

Paulton Rovers are 19th in the Southern League Division One – South table, and have picked up seven wins and four draws in their 28 games to date.

Cribbs, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 52 points, having won 14 and drawn 10 of their first 30 matches, and are 14 points behind table-toppers Wimborne Town.

Paulton are in terrible form in the Southern League Division One South, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and four draws over that period, Cribbs's form is slightly better – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Rovers' two.

With 32 goals in 28 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Paulton's last match was on March 23, a 0-0 draw against Bideford.

Cribbs drew 2-2 with Tavistock last time out, also on March 23.
