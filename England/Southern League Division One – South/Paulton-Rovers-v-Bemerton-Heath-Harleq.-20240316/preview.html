Paulton Rovers v Bemerton Heath Harleq.

Southern League Division One – South | Athletic Ground
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Paulton Rovers face a challenge against high-flying Bemerton Heath Harleq. at the Athletic Ground on Saturday.

Paulton Rovers are 18th in the Southern League Division One – South table, and have picked up seven wins and two draws in their 26 games to date.

Bemerton Heath Harleq., meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 43 points, having won 13 and drawn four of their first 29 matches, and are 17 points behind table-toppers Wimborne Town.

Paulton are in awful form in the Southern League Division One South, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Bemerton Heath Harleq.'s form is much better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Rovers' zero.

With 28 goals in 26 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Paulton's last match was on February 24, a 6-2 loss against Bishop's Cleeve.

Bemerton Heath Harleq. beat Larkhall Athletic 2-1 last time out, on March 9.
