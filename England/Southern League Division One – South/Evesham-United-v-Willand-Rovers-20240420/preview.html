Evesham United v Willand Rovers

Southern League Division One – South | The Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Willand Rovers come to the Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium to play Evesham United on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Evesham have two wins and four losses.

With 47 goals in 33 games so far this season, Willand are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Evesham are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last five years, Evesham and Willand have played each other on five occasions. Evesham won two of them and Willand three.

On average, Evesham scored 1.0 goal and Willand 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 23, when Willand won 3-1 at home.

United are ninth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn 11, earning 44 points.

Rovers are three places behind the home side in 12th, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

Evesham's last match was on April 6, a 4-3 win against Melksham Town.

Willand lost 3-0 against Yate Town last time out, also on April 6.
