Hamworthy United v Larkhall Athletic

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Larkhall Athletic visit Hamworthy United on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in Southern League Division One South.

Larkhall have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Hamworthy side who drew their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

Hamworthy are bottom of the table after six games, of which they have won one and drawn two, earning five points.

Larkhall are 11 places ahead of United in ninth, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

With 41 goals in 22 games so far this season, Athletic are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

In the last three years, Hamworthy and Larkhall have played each other on three occasions. Hamworthy won two of them and Larkhall one.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Larkhall won 2-1 away.

Hamworthy's last match was on September 26, a 1-1 draw against Melksham Town.

Larkhall beat Willand Rovers 2-1 last time out, on February 3.
