Hamworthy United v Exmouth

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table Hamworthy United host 17th-placed Exmouth.

Hamworthy have picked up five points from their first five Southern League Division One – South games, with one win and two draws.

That is 26 points less than Exmouth have collected, having won eight and drawn seven.

Hamworthy are in disappointing form in the Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Exmouth's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to United's five.

With 45 goals in 33 games so far this season, the away team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Hamworthy and Exmouth have played each other twice. Hamworthy won both of them.

Their last meeting was on March 11 2023, when Hamworthy won 1-0 away.

Hamworthy's last match was on September 26, a 1-1 draw against Melksham Town.

Exmouth lost 2-1 against Paulton Rovers last time out, on April 20.
