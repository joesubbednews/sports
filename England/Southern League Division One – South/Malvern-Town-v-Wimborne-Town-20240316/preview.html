Malvern Town v Wimborne Town

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wimborne Town visit Malvern Town on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Southern League Division One South.

Wimborne have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Malvern side who have also won their last two matches, and collected 13 points from the last possible 18.

With 60 goals in 28 games so far this season, Wimborne are the league's highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Malvern are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Wimborne Town are top of the table after 28 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn six, earning 60 points.

Malvern Town are 11 places behind the visitors in 12th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

Malvern's last match was on March 2, a 4-0 win against Bemerton Heath Harleq..

Wimborne beat Tavistock 3-2 last time out, on March 9.
