Bristol Manor Farm v Hamworthy United

Southern League Division One – South | The Creek
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Hamworthy United face a challenge away against high-flying Bristol Manor Farm at the Creek on Saturday.

Hamworthy United are rooted to the bottom of the Southern League Division One – South table, and have picked up one win and two draws in their six games to date.

Bristol Manor Farm, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 47 points, having won 13 and drawn eight of their first 32 matches, and are 19 points behind table-toppers Wimborne Town.

With nine goals in six games so far this season, Hamworthy are the league's lowest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Bristol Manor Farm, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home team are in good form in the Southern League Division One South, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, United's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Bristol Manor Farm's 13.

Over the last two years, Bristol Manor Farm and Hamworthy have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on April 15, when Bristol Manor Farm won 2-1 at home.

Bristol Manor Farm's last match was on Monday, a 2-1 win against Willand Rovers.

Hamworthy drew 1-1 with Melksham Town last time out, on September 26.
