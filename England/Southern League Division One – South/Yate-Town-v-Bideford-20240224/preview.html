Yate Town v Bideford

Southern League Division One – South | Lodge Road
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bideford travel to Yate Town on Saturday in the Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Cribbs 2-1 at home.

Yate also lost their last match, 2-0 against Wimborne Town.

With 41 goals in 27 games so far this season, Yate are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Bideford, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The visitors are sixth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn four, earning 37 points.

Town are one place behind Bideford in seventh, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Southern League Division One South, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Bideford's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Yate's 11.
