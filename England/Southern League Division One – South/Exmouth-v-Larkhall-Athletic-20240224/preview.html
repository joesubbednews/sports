Exmouth v Larkhall Athletic

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Larkhall Athletic face Exmouth on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Southern League Division One South.

Larkhall are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face an Exmouth team who have won just one and drawn one over the same number of games.

Exmouth are 19th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won three and drawn five, earning 14 points.

Larkhall are nine places ahead of the home team in 10th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

With 28 goals in 24 games so far this season, Exmouth are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Athletic, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last three years, Exmouth and Larkhall have played each other on three occasions. Exmouth won one of them and Larkhall the other.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Larkhall won 5-0 at home.

Exmouth's last match was on February 17, a 2-2 draw against Bristol Manor Farm.

Larkhall drew 1-1 with Evesham United last time out, also on February 17.
