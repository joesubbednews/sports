Exmouth v Bashley

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 19th-ranked Exmouth host 18th-placed Bashley.

Exmouth have picked up 13 points from their first 13 Southern League Division One – South games, with three wins and four draws.

That is six points less than Bashley have collected, having won four and drawn seven.

Exmouth are in mixed form in Southern League Division One South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and three draws over that period, Bashley's form is worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the home team's seven.

With 23 goals in 21 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Exmouth are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Exmouth and Bashley have played each other twice. Exmouth won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on April 8, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Exmouth's last match was on February 3, a 3-0 loss against Malvern Town.

Bashley drew 1-1 with Evesham United last time out, also on February 3.
