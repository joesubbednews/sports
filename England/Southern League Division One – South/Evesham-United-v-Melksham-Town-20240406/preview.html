Evesham United v Melksham Town

Southern League Division One – South | The Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Evesham United welcome Melksham Town to the Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Evesham's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Southern League Division One – South matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

Melksham are 16th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 11, earning 32 points.

Evesham are seven places ahead of Town in ninth, with 10 wins and 11 draws putting them on 41 points.

With 39 goals in 30 games so far this season, the away side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last five years, Evesham and Melksham have played each other on five occasions. Evesham won two of them, Melksham one, and they drew twice.

On average, Evesham scored 1.6 goals and Melksham 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Evesham's last match was on Monday, a 2-0 loss against Malvern Town.

Melksham lost 2-1 against Frome Town last time out, also on Monday.
