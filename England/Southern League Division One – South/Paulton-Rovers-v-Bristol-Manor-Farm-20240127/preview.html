Paulton Rovers v Bristol Manor Farm

Southern League Division One – South | Athletic Ground
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bristol Manor Farm travel to Athletic Ground looking to secure a first win in six Southern League Division One – South games against Paulton Rovers on Saturday.

Bristol Manor Farm have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Willand Rovers on December 26.

They face a Paulton side who have won just two and lost three over that time.

In the last 10 years, Paulton and Bristol Manor Farm have played each other on eight occasions. Paulton won three of them, Bristol Manor Farm four, and they drew once.

On average, Paulton scored 1.8 goals and Bristol Manor Farm 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Paulton won 3-0 away.

With 23 goals in 20 games so far this season, Paulton are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Bristol Manor Farm are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Rovers are 16th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won seven and drawn two, earning 23 points.

The away team are seven places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with seven wins and seven draws putting them on 28 points.

Paulton's last match was on January 13, a 1-0 loss against Frome Town.

Bristol Manor Farm drew 0-0 with Bashley last time out, on January 20.
