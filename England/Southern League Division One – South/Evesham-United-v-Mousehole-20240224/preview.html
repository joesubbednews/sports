Evesham United v Mousehole

Southern League Division One – South | The Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Evesham United face Mousehole on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Southern League Division One South.

Evesham are unbeaten in eight, with four wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Mousehole team who have won three and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 46 goals in 23 games so far this season, Mousehole are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Evesham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

United are fifth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won nine and drawn 11, earning 38 points.

The away side are six places behind the hosts in 11th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

Evesham's last match was on February 17, a 1-1 draw against Larkhall Athletic.

Mousehole beat Cribbs 2-1 last time out, on February 10.
