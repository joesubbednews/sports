Frome Town v Malvern Town

Southern League Division One – South | AlderSmith Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Frome Town host Malvern Town in Saturday's match at the AlderSmith Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Southern League Division One South.

Frome, who sit third in the league after 22 games, fell to a 3-2 away defeat to Mousehole on February 3.

They face a Malvern side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-0 victory against Exmouth, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 46 goals in 22 games so far this season, Frome are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Malvern are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Frome Town are in reasonable form in Southern League Division One South, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Malvern Town's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home side's 12.
