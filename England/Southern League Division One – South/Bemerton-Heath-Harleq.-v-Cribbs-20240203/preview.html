Bemerton Heath Harleq. v Cribbs

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bemerton Heath Harleq. face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Cribbs defence on Saturday.

With 41 goals in 23 games, Bemerton Heath Harleq. are the joint-fourth-highest scorers in Southern League Division One South ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Cribbs side who have scored 35 in 21 matches, but conceded only 24 goals, putting them joint-second among the league's tightest defences – only Hamworthy United have conceded fewer goals.

Cribbs are third in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

Bemerton Heath Harleq. are two places behind the visitors in fifth, with 11 wins and three draws putting them on 36 points.

The home team are in mixed form in Southern League Division One South, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, Cribbs's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Bemerton Heath Harleq.'s nine.

Bemerton Heath Harleq.'s last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 win against Bideford.

Cribbs beat Westbury United 1-0 last time out, on January 27.
