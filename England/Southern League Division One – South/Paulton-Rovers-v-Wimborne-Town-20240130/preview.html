Paulton Rovers v Wimborne Town

Southern League Division One – South
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.30pm

Wimborne Town travel for Tuesday's late match against Paulton Rovers looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Southern League Division One South.

Wimborne, who sit third in the league after 20 games, fell to a 3-1 away defeat to Larkhall Athletic on January 13.

They face a Paulton side who also lost their last match, a 3-1 defeat to Bristol Manor Farm, and who sit 16th in the table.

With 24 goals in 21 games so far this season, Paulton are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Wimborne, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Rovers are in disappointing form in Southern League Division One South, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home team's six.

In the last five years, Paulton and Wimborne have played each other on five occasions. Paulton won one of them, Wimborne two, and they drew twice.

On average, Paulton scored 1.8 goals and Wimborne 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 22, when Wimborne won 2-0 away.
