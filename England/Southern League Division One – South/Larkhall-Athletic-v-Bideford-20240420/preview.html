Larkhall Athletic v Bideford

Southern League Division One – South | The Plain Ham Ground
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Larkhall Athletic host Bideford at the Plain Ham Ground on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Southern League Division One South.

Larkhall have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Bideford side who lost their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

With 38 goals in 30 games so far this season, Bideford are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Larkhall, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Larkhall and Bideford have played each other on six occasions. Bideford won four of them and they drew twice.

On average, Larkhall scored 0.7 goals and Bideford 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when Bideford won 2-1 at home.

Athletic are fifth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

The away side are five places behind the hosts in 10th, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

Larkhall's last match was on April 13, a 1-0 win against Paulton Rovers.

Bideford lost 3-1 against Bashley last time out, also on April 13.
