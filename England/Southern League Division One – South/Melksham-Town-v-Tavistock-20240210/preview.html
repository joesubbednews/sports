Melksham Town v Tavistock

Southern League Division One – South
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Melksham Town welcome Tavistock on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Melksham's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Southern League Division One – South matches, while their opponents have earned nine from a possible 18.

With 38 goals in 20 games so far this season, Tavistock are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Melksham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Town are 15th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won six and drawn eight, earning 26 points.

The away side are one place ahead of the hosts in 14th, with nine wins and two draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last three years, Melksham and Tavistock have played each other on three occasions. Melksham won one of them and Tavistock the other.

Their last meeting was on January 7 2023, when Tavistock won 2-1 away.

Melksham's last match was on February 3, a 4-1 loss against Yate Town.

Tavistock beat Paulton Rovers 2-1 last time out, also on February 3.
