Bedford Town v AFC Kempston Rovers

Southern League Division One Central
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Bedford Town face AFC Kempston Rovers on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-3 victory over Biggleswade Town on January 1, Bedford sit second in Southern League Division One Central.

They travel to play a Kempston Rovers side 19th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-0 against AFC Dunstable.

Bedford are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Kempston Rovers' form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to Town's nine.

With 14 goals in 16 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.
