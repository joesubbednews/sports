Ramsgate v East Grinstead Town

Isthmian League – Division One South East | Southwood Stadium
Wednesday, 10 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Ramsgate host East Grinstead Town in Wednesday's late match at the Southwood Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Ramsgate, who sit top of the league after 18 games, fell to a 4-2 away defeat to Lancing on Saturday.

They face an East Grinstead side who also lost their last match, a 3-0 defeat to Burgess Hill Town, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 52 goals in 18 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

East Grinstead, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The home team are in fantastic form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Town's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Ramsgate's 15.

Over the last two years, Ramsgate and East Grinstead have played each other on three occasions. Ramsgate won all of them.

Their last meeting was on March 11, when Ramsgate won 5-0 at home.
