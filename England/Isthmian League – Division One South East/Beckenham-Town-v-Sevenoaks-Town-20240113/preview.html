Beckenham Town v Sevenoaks Town

Isthmian League – Division One South East | Eden Park Avenue
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Beckenham Town and Sevenoaks Town both come into Saturday's kick-off at Eden Park Avenue in poor form, having picked up one and four points respectively in their last six games.

Beckenham have drawn just one of the last six, while Sevenoaks have taken one win and one draw.

Beckenham are bottom of the table after 17 games, of which they have won two and drawn two, earning eight points.

Sevenoaks are 12 places ahead of Beckenham Town in eighth, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

With 21 goals in 17 games so far this season, the home team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Sevenoaks Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Beckenham's last match was on January 6, a 4-0 loss against Sittingbourne.

Sevenoaks lost 2-1 against Herne Bay last time out, also on January 6.
