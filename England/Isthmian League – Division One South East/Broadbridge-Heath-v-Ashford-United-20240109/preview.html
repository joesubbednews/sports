Broadbridge Heath v Ashford United

Isthmian League – Division One South East
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Broadbridge Heath face Ashford United on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-0 victory over Phoenix Sports on Saturday, Broadbridge Heath sit 18th in Isthmian League Division One South East.

They travel to play an Ashford side 10th in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-2 against Three Bridges.

Broadbridge Heath are in disappointing form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Ashford's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home side's five.

With 24 goals in 18 games so far this season, Broadbridge Heath are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.
