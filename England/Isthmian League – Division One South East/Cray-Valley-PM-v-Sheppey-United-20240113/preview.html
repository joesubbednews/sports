Cray Valley PM v Sheppey United

Isthmian League – Division One South East
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Cray Valley PM and Sheppey United promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Isthmian League Division One South East's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Cray Valley PM and Sheppey sit fourth and third in the goal-scoring charts, with 43 and 44 goals respectively.

Cray Valley PM are second in the table after 16 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn five, earning 38 points.

Sheppey are four places behind the hosts in sixth, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

Cray Valley PM are in good form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, United's form is worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Cray Valley PM's 14.

Cray Valley PM's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-1 draw against Lancing.

Sheppey lost 3-1 against Chichester City last time out, on January 6.
