Erith and Belvedere v Lancing

Isthmian League – Division One South East
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Erith and Belvedere face a challenge against high-flying Lancing on Sunday.

Erith and Belvedere are 19th in the Isthmian League – Division One South East table, and have picked up three wins and three draws in their 20 games to date.

Lancing, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 35 points, having won 11 and drawn two of their first 19 matches, and are 11 points behind table-toppers Ramsgate.

With 10 goals in 20 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Lancing, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Erith & Belvedere's three.

Erith & Belvedere's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 loss against Merstham.

Lancing drew 1-1 with Cray Valley PM last time out, also on Tuesday.
