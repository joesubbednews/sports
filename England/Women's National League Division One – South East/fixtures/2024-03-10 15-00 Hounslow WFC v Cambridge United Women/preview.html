Hounslow WFC v Cambridge United Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Cambridge United Women travel to Hounslow WFC on early Sunday in the Women's National League Division One South East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with AFC Sudbury Women 0-0 at home.

Hounslow WFC also drew their last match, 0-0 against Worthing Women.

With 26 goals in 14 games so far this season, Hounslow WFC are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Cambridge United, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Hounslow WFC's 11.

Cambridge United are fifth in the table after 15 games, of which they have won six and drawn five, earning 23 points.

Hounslow WFC are one place behind Cambridge United in sixth, with seven wins and two draws putting them on the same number of points.
