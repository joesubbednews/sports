AFC Sudbury Women v Actonians

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

AFC Sudbury Women are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Actonians on Sunday, with just two points collected from their last six games.

Sudbury Women have picked up two draws and four losses in their last six Women's National League Division One – South East games, and face an Actonians side whose last six games have brought three wins and three losses.

Sudbury Women are 10th in the table after 16 games, of which they have won one and drawn five, earning eight points.

Actonians are three places ahead of the home team in seventh, with six wins and two draws putting them on 20 points.

With 23 goals in 16 games so far this season, Sudbury Women are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 3.1 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Sudbury Women's last match was on February 21, a 0-0 draw against Cambridge United Women.

Actonians beat Chesham United Women 6-2 last time out, on Tuesday.
