Norwich City v Worthing Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South East's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when second-placed Norwich City host third-placed Worthing Women.

Norwich have picked up 10 wins and three draws from 13 games so far this season, and sit three points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Worthing, meanwhile, have won nine and drawn three of 15, picking up 30 points.

With 40 goals in 13 games so far this season, Norwich are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Worthing, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

City are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to the home team's 16.

Norwich's last match was on February 4, a 2-1 win against London Seaward Women.

Worthing drew 0-0 with Hounslow WFC last time out, on February 11.
