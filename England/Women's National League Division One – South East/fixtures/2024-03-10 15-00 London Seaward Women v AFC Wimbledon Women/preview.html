London Seaward Women v AFC Wimbledon Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

AFC Wimbledon Women visit London Seaward Women on early Sunday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Women's National League Division One South East.

Wimbledon Women have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a London Seaward side who also won their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 66 goals in 15 games so far this season, Wimbledon Women are the league's highest scorers with 4.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

London Seaward, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The visitors are top of the table after 15 games, of which they have won 13 and lost two, earning 39 points.

The home team are seven places behind Wimbledon Women in eighth, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.

In the last five years, London Seaward and Wimbledon Women have played each other on five occasions. London Seaward won one of them, Wimbledon Women three, and they drew once.

On average, London Seaward scored 1.2 goals and Wimbledon Women 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when Wimbledon Women won 4-2 at home.

London Seaward's last match was on February 11, a 4-2 win against AFC Sudbury Women.

Wimbledon Women beat Chesham United Women 4-0 last time out, also on February 11.
