Chesham United Women v Ashford Town (Middx)

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Chesham United Women face a challenge against high-flying Ashford Town (Middx) on Sunday.

Chesham United Women are ninth in the Women's National League Division One – South East table, and have picked up four wins and two draws in their 15 games to date.

Ashford Town (Middx), meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 24 points, having won seven and drawn three of their first 14 matches, and are 15 points behind table-toppers AFC Wimbledon Women.

With 28 goals in 14 games so far this season, Ashford Town (Middx) are scoring at below the league average rate with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Chesham United, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game. They have conceded 3.2 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Chesham United's seven.

In the last three years, Chesham United and Ashford Town (Middx) have played each other on three occasions. Ashford Town (Middx) won all of them.

Their last meeting was on February 25, when Ashford Town (Middx) won 1-0 at home.

Chesham United's last match was on Tuesday, a 6-2 loss against Actonians.

Ashford Town (Middx) beat Chesham United Women 1-0 last time out, on February 25.
