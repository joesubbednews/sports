Worthing Women v Hounslow WFC

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South East's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when third-placed Worthing Women host fourth-placed Hounslow WFC.

Worthing have picked up nine wins and two draws from 14 games so far this season, and sit seven points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Hounslow WFC, meanwhile, have won seven and drawn one of 13, picking up 22 points.

With 26 goals in 13 games so far this season, Hounslow WFC are scoring at below the league average rate with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Worthing, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home team are in good form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 13 points from 18.

Worthing's last match was on January 28, a 2-0 win against AFC Sudbury Women.

Hounslow WFC beat Cambridge City Women 2-1 last time out, also on January 28.
