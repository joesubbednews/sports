Ashford Town (Middx) v London Seaward Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Ashford Town (Middx) host London Seaward Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Women's National League Division One South East.

Ashford Town (Middx), who sit fourth in the league after 12 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Actonians on January 14.

They face a London Seaward side who also lost their last match, a 1-0 defeat to Worthing Women, and who sit eighth in the table.

With 21 goals in 12 games so far this season, London Seaward are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Ashford Town (Middx), meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Women's National League Division One South East, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Ashford Town (Middx)'s six.

In the last three years, Ashford Town (Middx) and London Seaward have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
