Haywards Heath Town v London Seaward Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

London Seaward Women travel to play Haywards Heath Town on early Sunday hoping to break their six-match run of draws in the Women's National League Division One South East.

It means London Seaward have earned six points from their last six games.

They face a Haywards Heath side who have collected the same number of points from the last possible 18, and won their last game.

Haywards Heath are 11th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won two and drawn five, earning 11 points.

London Seaward are three places ahead of Town in eighth, with five wins and 10 draws putting them on 25 points.

With 16 goals in 20 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Haywards Heath's last match was on April 21, a 3-1 loss against Ashford Town (Middx).

London Seaward drew 3-3 with Hounslow WFC last time out, on April 24.
