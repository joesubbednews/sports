Haywards Heath Town v London Seaward Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

London Seaward Women travel looking to secure a first win in four Women's National League Division One – South East games against Haywards Heath Town on Sunday.

London Seaward have lost none and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against AFC Sudbury Women on February 11.

They face a Haywards Heath side who have won just one and lost two over that time.

Haywards Heath are 11th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won two and drawn four, earning 10 points.

London Seaward are three places ahead of Town in eighth, with five wins and seven draws putting them on 22 points.

With 16 goals in 19 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.0. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Haywards Heath's last match was on April 21, a 3-1 loss against Ashford Town (Middx).

London Seaward drew 3-3 with Actonians last time out, also on April 21.
