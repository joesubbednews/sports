Cambridge City Women v AFC Wimbledon Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Cambridge City Women face a challenge against high-flying AFC Wimbledon Women on Sunday.

Cambridge City Women are 11th in the Women's National League Division One – South East table, and have picked up just one win and four draws in their 16 games to date.

Wimbledon Women, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 42 points, having won 14 and drawn none.

With 72 goals in 16 games so far this season, Wimbledon Women are the league's highest scorers with 4.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Cambridge City, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in terrible form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Cambridge City's two.

In the last five years, Cambridge City and Wimbledon Women have played each other on five occasions. Wimbledon Women won all of them.

On average, Cambridge City scored 0.6 goals and Wimbledon Women 4.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Wimbledon Women won 8-1 at home.

Cambridge City's last match was on March 17, a 0-0 draw against Haywards Heath Town.

Wimbledon Women beat Haywards Heath Town 6-0 last time out, on March 13.
