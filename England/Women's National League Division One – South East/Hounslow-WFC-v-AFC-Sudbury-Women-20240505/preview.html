Hounslow WFC v AFC Sudbury Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Hounslow WFC face AFC Sudbury Women on Sunday looking to secure a first win in six Women's National League Division One – South East games.

Hounslow WFC have lost one and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Cambridge United Women on March 10.

They face a Sudbury Women side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

With 32 goals in 21 games so far this season, Sudbury Women are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 2.9 per game.

Hounslow WFC, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The away side are 10th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won three and drawn six, earning 15 points.

The hosts are four places ahead of Sudbury Women in sixth, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Hounslow WFC's last match was on April 24, a 3-3 draw against London Seaward Women.

Sudbury Women lost 4-1 against Norwich City last time out, on April 21.
