London Seaward Women v Chesham United Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Chesham United Women face London Seaward Women away on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 6-5 victory over Ashford Town (Middx) on March 10, Chesham United sit ninth in the Women's National League Division One South East.

They travel to play a London Seaward side eighth in the standings, who were held in their last match, 0-0 against Cambridge United Women, on March 14.

With 27 goals in 16 games so far this season, London Seaward are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Chesham United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.3 goals per game.

The home team are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to London Seaward's six.

In the last three years, London Seaward and Chesham United have played each other on three occasions. London Seaward won two of them and Chesham United one.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Chesham United won 4-1 at home.
