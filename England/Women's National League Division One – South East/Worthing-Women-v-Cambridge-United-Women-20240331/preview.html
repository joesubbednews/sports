Worthing Women v Cambridge United Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Worthing Women face Cambridge United Women on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Women's National League Division One South East.

Worthing are unbeaten in eight, with four wins and four draws, ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a Cambridge United team who have won one and drawn three over the same number of games.

Worthing are third in the table after 17 games, of which they have won nine and drawn five, earning 32 points.

Cambridge United are three places behind the home side in sixth, with six wins and six draws putting them on 24 points.

With 31 goals in 17 games so far this season, Worthing are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.0. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Worthing's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against Actonians.

Cambridge United drew 0-0 with London Seaward Women last time out, on March 14.
