Hounslow WFC v Cambridge City Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Cambridge City Women travel to Hounslow WFC on early Sunday in Women's National League Division One South East.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

Hounslow WFC are in good form in Women's National League Division One South East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Cambridge City's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Hounslow WFC's 13.
