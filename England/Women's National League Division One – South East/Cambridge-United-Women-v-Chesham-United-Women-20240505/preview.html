Cambridge United Women v Chesham United Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Cambridge United Women host Chesham United Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on April 28, Cambridge United drew with Ashford Town (Middx) 2-2 at home.

Chesham United lost, 6-1 at home against Norwich City.

With 42 goals in 20 games so far this season, Chesham United are scoring more than average in the league with 2.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 67 goals at a rate of 3.4 per game.

Cambridge United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Cambridge United's six.

Chesham United are ninth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won five and drawn four, earning 19 points.

Cambridge United are two places ahead of Chesham United in seventh, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last five years, Cambridge United and Chesham United have played each other on four occasions. Cambridge United won three of them and Chesham United one.

On average, Cambridge United scored 4.0 goals and Chesham United 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Cambridge United won 4-1 away.
