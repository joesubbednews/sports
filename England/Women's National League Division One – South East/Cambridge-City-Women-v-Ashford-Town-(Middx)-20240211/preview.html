Cambridge City Women v Ashford Town (Middx)

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Cambridge City Women welcome Ashford Town (Middx) on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Cambridge City's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Women's National League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

Cambridge City are 11th in the table after 14 games, of which they have won one and drawn three, earning six points.

Ashford Town (Middx) are six places ahead of the home side in fifth, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

With 17 goals in 14 games so far this season, Cambridge City are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last three years, Cambridge City and Ashford Town (Middx) have played each other on three occasions. Ashford Town (Middx) won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when Ashford Town (Middx) won 1-0 at home.

Cambridge City's last match was on January 28, a 2-1 loss against Hounslow WFC.

Ashford Town (Middx) drew 1-1 with London Seaward Women last time out, also on January 28.
