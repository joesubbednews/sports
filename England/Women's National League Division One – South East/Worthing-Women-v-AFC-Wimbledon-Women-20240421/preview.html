Worthing Women v AFC Wimbledon Women

Women's National League Division One – South East | The A2B Stadium
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Worthing Women host AFC Wimbledon Women in Sunday's match at the A2B Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Division One South East.

Worthing, who sit third in the league after 19 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Haywards Heath Town on April 3.

They face a Wimbledon Women side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Norwich City, and who sit first in the table.

With 78 goals in 18 games so far this season, Wimbledon Women are the league's highest scorers with 4.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Worthing, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.0. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Worthing's seven.

Over the last two years, Worthing and Wimbledon Women have played each other twice. Wimbledon Women won both of them.

Their last meeting was on October 29, when Wimbledon Women won 5-0 at home.
