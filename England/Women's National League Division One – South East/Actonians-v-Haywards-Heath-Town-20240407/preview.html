Actonians v Haywards Heath Town

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Haywards Heath Town face Actonians away on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Worthing Women on Wednesday, Haywards Heath sit 11th in the Women's National League Division One South East.

They travel to play an Actonians side fifth in the standings, who also won their last match, 4-2 against Hounslow WFC, on March 26.

With 13 goals in 17 games so far this season, Haywards Heath are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Actonians, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have also conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Town's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Actonians's 10.
