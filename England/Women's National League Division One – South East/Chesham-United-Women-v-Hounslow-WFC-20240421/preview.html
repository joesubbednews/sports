Chesham United Women v Hounslow WFC

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Chesham United Women are on a poor run ahead of hosting Hounslow WFC on Sunday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Chesham United have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Women's National League Division One – South East games, and face a Hounslow WFC side whose last six games have brought two wins and three draws.

With 38 goals in 17 games so far this season, Chesham United are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 3.4 per game.

Hounslow WFC, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.0. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are ninth in the table after 17 games, of which they have won five and drawn two, earning 17 points.

The away team are four places ahead of Chesham United in fifth, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

Chesham United's last match was on March 31, a 5-1 loss against AFC Sudbury Women.

Hounslow WFC drew 0-0 with Ashford Town (Middx) last time out, also on March 31.
