Chesham United Women v Haywards Heath Town

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Chesham United Women face Haywards Heath Town on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 6-2 victory over Cambridge City Women on January 14, Chesham United sit ninth in Women's National League Division One South East.

They travel to play a Haywards Heath side 12th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 0-0 against Hounslow WFC.

Chesham United are in mixed form in Women's National League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Haywards Heath's form is worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

With 11 goals in 12 games so far this season, Town are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 2.8 per game.

Chesham United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.3 goals per game.
