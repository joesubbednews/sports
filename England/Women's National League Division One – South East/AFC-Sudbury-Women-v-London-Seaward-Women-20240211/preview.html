AFC Sudbury Women v London Seaward Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

AFC Sudbury Women welcome London Seaward Women on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Sudbury Women's struggles have left them with just one point from their last six Women's National League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

Sudbury Women are 10th in the table after 13 games, of which they have won one and drawn three, earning six points.

London Seaward are two places ahead of the home team in eighth, with four wins and four draws putting them on 16 points.

With 18 goals in 13 games so far this season, Sudbury Women are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 3.3 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Sudbury Women's last match was on January 28, a 2-0 loss against Worthing Women.

London Seaward lost 2-1 against Norwich City last time out, on February 4.
