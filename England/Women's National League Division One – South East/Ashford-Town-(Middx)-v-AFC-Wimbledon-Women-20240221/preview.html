Ashford Town (Middx) v AFC Wimbledon Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South East's meanest defences go head-to-head on Wednesday, when Ashford Town (Middx) host AFC Wimbledon Women.

Only two sides – Norwich City and Worthing Women – have conceded fewer goals than Ashford Town (Middx) to date: the home side have let in just 20 goals in 13 games.

Wimbledon Women have conceded 21 goals in 15 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Wimbledon Women are top of the table after 15 games, of which they have won 13 and lost two, earning 39 points.

Ashford Town (Middx) are five places behind the away team in sixth, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, Wimbledon Women's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Ashford Town (Middx)'s seven.

In the last three years, Ashford Town (Middx) and Wimbledon Women have played each other on three occasions. Ashford Town (Middx) won one of them and Wimbledon Women the other.

Their last meeting was on September 6, when Ashford Town (Middx) won 2-0 away.

Ashford Town (Middx)'s last match was on January 28, a 1-1 draw against London Seaward Women.

Wimbledon Women beat Chesham United Women 4-0 last time out, on February 11.
