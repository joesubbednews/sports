Haywards Heath Town v AFC Sudbury Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Haywards Heath Town face AFC Sudbury Women on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-0 victory over Cambridge City Women on March 17, Haywards Heath sit bottom of the Women's National League Division One South East.

They travel to play a Sudbury Women side 10th in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Cambridge City Women, on March 12.

Haywards Heath are in bad form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Sudbury Women's form is slightly better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Town's three.

With 12 goals in 15 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.1 goals per game.
