Ashford Town (Middx) v Norwich City

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South East's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when Norwich City visit Ashford Town (Middx).

Only one side – Worthing Women – has conceded fewer goals than Norwich to date: the away side have let in just 20 goals in 21 games.

Ashford Town (Middx) have conceded 30 goals in 20 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Norwich are second in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn seven, earning 49 points.

Ashford Town (Middx) are three places behind City in fifth, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Ashford Town (Middx)'s six.

In the last three years, Ashford Town (Middx) and Norwich have played each other on three occasions. Norwich won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Norwich won 4-2 at home.

Ashford Town (Middx)'s last match was on April 28, a 2-2 draw against Cambridge United Women.

Norwich beat Chesham United Women 6-1 last time out, also on April 28.
