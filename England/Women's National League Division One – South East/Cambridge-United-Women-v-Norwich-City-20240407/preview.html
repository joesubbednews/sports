Cambridge United Women v Norwich City

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Norwich City face Cambridge United Women on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Women's National League Division One South East.

Norwich are unbeaten in 16, with 10 wins and six draws, ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a Cambridge United team who have won five and drawn six over the same number of games.

With 43 goals in 16 games so far this season, Norwich are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Cambridge United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

City are second in the table after 16 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn six, earning 36 points.

The hosts are five places behind the away team in seventh, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.

In the last five years, Cambridge United and Norwich have played each other on five occasions. They won two each, and they drew once.

On average, Cambridge United scored 1.2 goals and Norwich 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Cambridge United's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against Worthing Women.

Norwich drew 0-0 with AFC Wimbledon Women last time out, also on Sunday.
