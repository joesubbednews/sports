Actonians v London Seaward Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Actonians face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight London Seaward Women defence on Sunday.

With 42 goals in 19 games, Actonians are the third-highest scorers in the Women's National League Division One South East ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a London Seaward side who have scored 27 in 17 matches, but conceded only 24 goals, putting them fourth among the league's tightest defences – only AFC Wimbledon Women, Norwich City and Worthing Women have conceded fewer goals.

Actonians are fourth in the table after 19 games, of which they have won nine and drawn three, earning 30 points.

London Seaward are four places behind the home side in eighth, with five wins and six draws putting them on 21 points.

Actonians are in good form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Actonians's 13.

In the last five years, Actonians and London Seaward have played each other on five occasions. Actonians won two of them, London Seaward one, and they drew twice.

On average, Actonians scored 2.0 goals and London Seaward 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 20, when London Seaward won 3-1 at home.

Actonians's last match was on April 7, a 5-2 win against Haywards Heath Town.

London Seaward drew 0-0 with Cambridge City Women last time out, on March 31.
