Ashford Town (Middx) v Chesham United Women

Women's National League Division One – South East | The Robert Parker Stadium
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Ashford Town (Middx) host Chesham United Women on Sunday at the Robert Parker Stadium in the Women's National League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on January 28, Ashford Town (Middx) drew with London Seaward Women 1-1 at home.

Chesham United also drew, 3-3 away at AFC Sudbury Women on February 18.

With 29 goals in 13 games so far this season, Chesham United are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

Ashford Town (Middx), meanwhile, are average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The visitors are ninth in the table after 13 games, of which they have won four and drawn two, earning 14 points.

The home side are three places ahead of Chesham United in sixth, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

Ashford Town (Middx) are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Chesham United's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Ashford Town (Middx)'s seven.

Over the last two years, Ashford Town (Middx) and Chesham United have played each other twice. Ashford Town (Middx) won both of them.

Their last meeting was on February 26, when Ashford Town (Middx) won 2-0 at home.
