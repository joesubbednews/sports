Chesham United Women v AFC Sudbury Women

Women's National League Division One – South East | The Meadow
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match at the Meadow sees two relegation candidates play each other, as ninth-ranked Chesham United Women host 10th-placed AFC Sudbury Women.

Chesham United have picked up 13 points from their first 13 Women's National League Division One – South East games, with four wins and one draw.

That is seven points more than Sudbury Women have collected, having won one and drawn three.

Chesham United are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and six losses over that period, Sudbury Women's form is much worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to the home side's seven.

With 20 goals in 14 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 3.4 per game.

Chesham United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.2 goals per game.

Chesham United's last match was on February 11, a 4-0 loss against AFC Wimbledon Women.

Sudbury Women lost 4-2 against London Seaward Women last time out, also on February 11.
