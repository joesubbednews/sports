Cambridge United Women v Ashford Town (Middx)

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Cambridge United Women host Ashford Town (Middx) on Sunday in the Women's National League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on April 14, Cambridge United beat Cambridge City Women 3-0 at home.

Ashford Town (Middx) also won, 3-1 at home against Haywards Heath Town on April 21.

With 36 goals in 20 games so far this season, Cambridge United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Ashford Town (Middx), meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.0. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Cambridge United's six.

In the last three years, Cambridge United and Ashford Town (Middx) have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Cambridge United won 4-3 away.

Cambridge United are seventh in the table after 20 games, of which they have won seven and drawn seven, earning 28 points.

Ashford Town (Middx) are one place ahead of the home side in sixth, with eight wins and four draws putting them on the same number of points.
