Worthing Women v Actonians

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Actonians face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Worthing Women defence on Sunday.

With 33 goals in 16 games, Actonians are the joint-fourth-highest scorers in the Women's National League Division One South East ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a Worthing side who have scored 31 in 16 matches, but conceded only 15 goals, putting them second among the league's tightest defences – only Norwich City have conceded fewer goals.

Worthing are third in the table after 16 games, of which they have won nine and drawn four, earning 31 points.

Actonians are four places behind the hosts in seventh, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

Worthing are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away side's form is similar – they have both taken 12 points from 18.

Worthing's last match was on March 10, a 1-1 draw against Norwich City.

Actonians beat AFC Sudbury Women 4-1 last time out, also on March 10.
