AFC Wimbledon Women v Norwich City

Women's National League Division One – South East | War Memorial Sports Ground
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

The Women's National League Division One South East's top two sides face each other at the War Memorial Sports Ground in Sunday's kick-off, when AFC Wimbledon Women host second-placed Norwich City.

Wimbledon Women have picked up 15 wins from 17 games so far this season, and sit 10 points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Norwich, meanwhile, have won 10 and drawn five of 15, picking up 35 points.

With 78 goals in 17 games so far this season, Wimbledon Women are the league's highest scorers with 4.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Norwich are also above average scorers, with 2.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.0. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in exceptional form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, City's form is worse – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Wimbledon Women's 18.

In the last 10 years, Wimbledon Women and Norwich have played each other on six occasions. They won two each, and they drew twice.

On average, Wimbledon Women scored 1.2 goals and Norwich 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 3, when Norwich won 2-0 at home.

Wimbledon Women's last match was on March 24, a 6-0 win against Cambridge City Women.

Norwich drew 2-2 with Hounslow WFC last time out, also on March 24.
