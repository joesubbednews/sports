Cambridge United Women v AFC Sudbury Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

AFC Sudbury Women travel looking to secure a first win in 11 Women's National League Division One – South East games against Cambridge United Women on late Wednesday.

Sudbury Women have lost seven and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Haywards Heath Town on October 8.

They face a Cambridge United side who have won four and drawn two over that time.

With 23 goals in 15 games so far this season, Sudbury Women are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 3.3 per game.

Cambridge United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The away side are 10th in the table after 15 games, of which they have won one and drawn four, earning seven points.

The hosts are five places ahead of Sudbury Women in fifth, with six wins and four draws putting them on 22 points.

Over the last two years, Cambridge United and Sudbury Women have played each other twice. Cambridge United won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on October 22, when Cambridge United won 4-2 at home.

Cambridge United's last match was on February 11, a 5-1 win against Actonians.

Sudbury Women drew 3-3 with Chesham United Women last time out, on Sunday.
