Chesham United Women v AFC Wimbledon Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Chesham United Women host AFC Wimbledon Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Women's National League Division One South East.

Chesham United, who sit ninth in the league after 11 games, fell to a 1-2 home defeat to Haywards Heath Town on January 28.

They face a Wimbledon Women side who picked up a win in their last match, a 6-0 victory against Cambridge United Women, and who sit first in the table.

With 62 goals in 14 games so far this season, Wimbledon Women are the league's highest scorers with 4.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Chesham United are also above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.1 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Women's National League Division One South East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Chesham United's 10.

In the last three years, Chesham United and Wimbledon Women have played each other on three occasions. Wimbledon Women won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when Wimbledon Women won 5-2 at home.
