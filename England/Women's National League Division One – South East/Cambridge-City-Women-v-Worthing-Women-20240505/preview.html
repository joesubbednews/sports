Cambridge City Women v Worthing Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Cambridge City Women face a challenge against high-flying Worthing Women on Sunday.

Cambridge City Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Division One – South East table, and have picked up just one win and five draws in their 20 games to date.

Worthing, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 37 points, having won 10 and drawn seven of their first 21 matches, and are 12 points behind table-toppers AFC Wimbledon Women.

Cambridge City are in terrible form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, Worthing's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' two.

With 18 goals in 20 games so far this season, Cambridge City are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 3.1 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Cambridge City's last match was on April 14, a 3-0 loss against Cambridge United Women.

Worthing drew 2-2 with AFC Wimbledon Women last time out, on April 21.
