AFC Sudbury Women v Worthing Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates AFC Sudbury Women face a challenge against high-flying Worthing Women on Sunday.

AFC Sudbury Women are 10th in the Women's National League Division One – South East table, and have picked up just one win and three draws in their 12 games to date.

Worthing, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 26 points, having won eight and drawn two of their first 13 matches, and are seven points behind table-toppers AFC Wimbledon W.

Sudbury Women are in terrible form in Women's National League Division One South East, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Worthing's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home side's one.

With 18 goals in 12 games so far this season, Sudbury Women are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 3.4 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Sudbury Women's last match was on January 14, a 5-3 loss against AFC Wimbledon Women.

Worthing beat London Seaward Women 1-0 last time out, also on January 14.
