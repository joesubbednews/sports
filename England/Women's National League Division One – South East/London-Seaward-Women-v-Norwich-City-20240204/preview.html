London Seaward Women v Norwich City

Women's National League Division One – South East | Oakside Stadium
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Women's National League Division One South East's meanest defences go head-to-head at the Oakside Stadium on Sunday, when Norwich City visit London Seaward Women.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Norwich to date: the away side have let in just 12 goals in 12 games.

London Seaward have conceded 20 goals in 13 games, giving them the joint-third tightest back line so far this season.

Norwich are second in the table after 12 games, of which they have won nine and drawn three, earning 30 points.

London Seaward are six places behind City in eighth, with four wins and four draws putting them on 16 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in Women's National League Division One South East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to London Seaward's eight.

In the last 10 years, London Seaward and Norwich have played each other on six occasions. London Seaward won two of them, Norwich one, and they drew three times.

On average, London Seaward scored 2.2 goals and Norwich 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 22, when Norwich won 3-1 away.

London Seaward's last match was on January 28, a 1-1 draw against Ashford Town (Middx).

Norwich beat Actonians 5-1 last time out, also on January 28.
