Haywards Heath Town v Norwich City

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Haywards Heath Town face a challenge against high-flying Norwich City on Sunday.

Haywards Heath Town are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Division One – South East table, and have picked up just one win and two draws in their 13 games to date.

Norwich, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 33 points, having won 10 and drawn three, and are six points behind table-toppers AFC Wimbledon Women.

With 12 goals in 13 games so far this season, Haywards Heath are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

Norwich, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 3.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Town are in terrible form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, City's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to the home side's two.

Haywards Heath's last match was on January 28, a 2-1 loss against Chesham United Women.

Norwich beat London Seaward Women 2-1 last time out, on February 4.
