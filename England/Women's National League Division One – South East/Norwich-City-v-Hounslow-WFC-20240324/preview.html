Norwich City v Hounslow WFC

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One South East's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when second-placed Norwich City host fourth-placed Hounslow WFC.

Norwich have picked up 10 wins and four draws from 14 games so far this season, and sit eight points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Hounslow WFC, meanwhile, have won eight and drawn two of 15, picking up 26 points.

With 41 goals in 14 games so far this season, Norwich are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Hounslow WFC, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

City are in good form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 14.

Norwich's last match was on March 10, a 1-1 draw against Worthing Women.

Hounslow WFC beat Cambridge United Women 3-0 last time out, also on March 10.
