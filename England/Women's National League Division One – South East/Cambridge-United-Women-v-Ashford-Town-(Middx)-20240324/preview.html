Cambridge United Women v Ashford Town (Middx)

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Cambridge United Women host Ashford Town (Middx) on Sunday in the Women's National League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on March 14, Cambridge United drew with London Seaward Women 0-0 at home.

Ashford Town (Middx) lost, 6-5 away at Chesham United Women on March 10.

With 31 goals in 17 games so far this season, Cambridge United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Ashford Town (Middx), meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One South East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Cambridge United's six.

Ashford Town (Middx) are fifth in the table after 15 games, of which they have won seven and drawn three, earning 24 points.

Cambridge United are one place behind Ashford Town (Middx) in sixth, with six wins and six draws putting them on the same number of points.

In the last three years, Cambridge United and Ashford Town (Middx) have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Cambridge United won 4-3 away.
