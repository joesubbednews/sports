AFC Wimbledon Women v Cambridge United Women

Women's National League Division One – South East
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match between AFC Wimbledon Women and Cambridge United Women promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Women's National League Division One South East's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Wimbledon Women and Cambridge United sit top and joint-fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 56 and 26 goals respectively.

Wimbledon Women are top of the table after 13 games, of which they have won 11 and lost two, earning 33 points.

Cambridge United are four places behind the hosts in fifth, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.

Wimbledon Women are in fantastic form in Women's National League Division One South East, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Wimbledon Women's 15.

In the last five years, Wimbledon Women and Cambridge United have played each other on five occasions. Wimbledon Women won all of them.

On average, Wimbledon Women scored 5.4 goals and Cambridge United 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Wimbledon Women won 8-3 away.

Wimbledon Women's last match was on January 14, a 5-3 win against AFC Sudbury Women.

Cambridge United drew 2-2 with Haywards Heath Town last time out, on January 7.
