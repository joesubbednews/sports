Cheltenham Town LFC v Billericay Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Cheltenham Town LFC are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Billericay Town Women on Sunday, with just two points collected from their last six games.

Cheltenham Town have picked up two draws and four losses in their last six Women's National League Premier Division – South games, and face a Billericay Town side whose last six games have brought two wins and one draw.

With 20 goals in 18 games so far this season, Cheltenham Town are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Billericay Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The hosts are ninth in the table after 18 games, of which they have won four and drawn four, earning 16 points.

The away team are one place ahead of Cheltenham Town in eighth, with four wins and five draws putting them on 17 points.

In the last three years, Cheltenham Town and Billericay Town have played each other on three occasions. Cheltenham Town won all of them.

Their last meeting was on October 22, when Cheltenham Town won 1-0 away.

Cheltenham Town's last match was on March 14, a 2-1 loss against Oxford United Women.

Billericay Town lost 2-0 against C and K Basildon last time out, on March 13.
