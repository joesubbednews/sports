Cheltenham Town LFC v Portsmouth Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Portsmouth Women come to play Cheltenham Town LFC on early Sunday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

With 73 goals in 20 games so far this season, Portsmouth are the league's highest scorers with 3.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in seven goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

Cheltenham Town, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The away side are top of the table after 20 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn one, earning 55 points.

The hosts are seven places behind Portsmouth in eighth, with six wins and four draws putting them on 22 points.

In the last five years, Cheltenham Town and Portsmouth have played each other on four occasions. Cheltenham Town won one of them and Portsmouth the other.

On average, Cheltenham Town scored 0.8 goals and Portsmouth 2.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 20, when Portsmouth won 5-0 at home.

Cheltenham Town's last match was on March 31, a 3-0 win against Chatham Town Women.

Portsmouth beat Plymouth Argyle Women 3-0 last time out, on April 7.
