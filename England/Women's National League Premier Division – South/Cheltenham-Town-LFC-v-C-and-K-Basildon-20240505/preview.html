Cheltenham Town LFC v C and K Basildon

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Cheltenham Town LFC host C and K Basildon on Sunday in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

In their last league match, on April 21, Cheltenham Town lost to Portsmouth Women 3-2 at home.

C and K Basildon won, 1-0 at home against Chatham Town Women.

C and K Basildon are second in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn one, earning 49 points.

Cheltenham Town are six places behind the visitors in eighth, with six wins and four draws putting them on 22 points.

With 29 goals in 21 games so far this season, the home team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

C and K Basildon, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Cheltenham Town are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, C and K Basildon's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Cheltenham Town's seven.
