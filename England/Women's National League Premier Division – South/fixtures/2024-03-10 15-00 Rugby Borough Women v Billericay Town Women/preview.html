Rugby Borough Women v Billericay Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Rugby Borough Women host Billericay Town Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

Rugby Borough, who sit third in the league after 16 games, fell to a 4-1 away defeat to Ipswich Town Women on February 25.

They face a Billericay Town side who picked up a win in their last match, a 5-1 victory against Chatham Town Women, and who sit eighth in the table.

With 36 goals in 16 games so far this season, Rugby Borough are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Billericay Town are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Rugby Borough's 12.
