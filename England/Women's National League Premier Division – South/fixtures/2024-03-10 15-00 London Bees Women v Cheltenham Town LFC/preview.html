London Bees Women v Cheltenham Town LFC

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table London Bees Women host 10th-placed Cheltenham Town LFC.

London Bees have picked up two points from their first two Women's National League Premier Division – South games, with no wins and two draws.

That is 13 points less than Cheltenham Town have collected, having won four and drawn three.

London Bees are in terrible form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Cheltenham Town's form is better – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the hosts' one.

With 10 goals in 16 games so far this season, London Bees are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 3.6 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.4 goals per game.

In the last three years, London Bees and Cheltenham Town have played each other on three occasions. London Bees won one of them and Cheltenham Town the other.

Their last meeting was on September 3, when Cheltenham Town won 3-2 at home.

London Bees's last match was on February 21, a 2-0 loss against Milton Keynes Dons Women.

Cheltenham Town lost 2-1 against Cardiff City Women last time out, on February 18.
