Portsmouth Women v Ipswich Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Premier Division South's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when table-topping Portsmouth Women host fourth-placed Ipswich Town Women.

Portsmouth have picked up 15 wins from 16 games so far this season, and sit 16 points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Ipswich Town, meanwhile, have won nine and drawn two of 15, picking up 29 points.

With 62 goals in 16 games so far this season, Portsmouth are the league's highest scorers with 3.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in five goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

Ipswich Town are also above average scorers, with 3.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The hosts are in exceptional form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Portsmouth's 18.

In the last 10 years, Portsmouth and Ipswich Town have played each other on six occasions. Portsmouth won four of them, Ipswich Town one, and they drew once.

On average, Portsmouth scored 1.2 goals and Ipswich Town 0.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 4, when Portsmouth won 1-0 away.

Portsmouth's last match was on March 3, a 2-1 win against Cardiff City Women.

Ipswich Town beat Plymouth Argyle Women 3-1 last time out, also on March 3.
