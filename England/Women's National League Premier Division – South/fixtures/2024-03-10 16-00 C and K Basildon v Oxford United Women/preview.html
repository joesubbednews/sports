C and K Basildon v Oxford United Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Premier Division South's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when C and K Basildon host Oxford United Women.

Only one side – Portsmouth Women – has conceded fewer goals than C and K Basildon to date: the home side have let in just 13 goals in 15 games.

Oxford United have conceded 20 goals in 15 games, giving them the joint-fourth tightest back line so far this season.

C and K Basildon are second in the table after 15 games, of which they have won 12 and lost three, earning 36 points.

Oxford United are three places behind the home side in fifth, with nine wins and one draw putting them on 28 points.

C and K Basildon are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

And also with four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 12 points from 18.

C and K Basildon's last match was on February 11, a 3-0 win against London Bees Women.

Oxford United lost 2-0 against Rugby Borough Women last time out, on February 21.
