Oxford United Women v Cardiff City Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Oxford United Women host Cardiff City Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

In their last league match, on Thursday, Oxford United beat Cheltenham Town LFC 2-1 at home.

Cardiff City also won, 2-1 at home against Plymouth Argyle Women on March 10.

With 26 goals in 17 games so far this season, Cardiff City are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Oxford United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are fourth in the table after 17 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn one, earning 31 points.

The away team are three places behind Oxford United in seventh, with six wins and four draws putting them on 22 points.

Oxford United are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Cardiff City's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home side's nine.

In the last three years, Oxford United and Cardiff City have played each other on three occasions. Oxford United won all of them.

Their last meeting was on January 16 2022, when Oxford United won 2-0 at home.
