London Bees Women v Oxford United Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South | The Hive Stadium
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates London Bees Women face a challenge against high-flying Oxford United Women at the Hive Stadium on Sunday.

London Bees Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Premier Division – South table, and have picked up no wins and two draws in their 13 games to date.

Oxford United, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 25 points, having won eight and drawn one of their first 12 matches, and are eight points behind table-toppers Portsmouth Ladies.

London Bees are in terrible form in Women's National League Premier Division South, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Oxford United's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to the home side's two.

With nine goals in 13 games so far this season, London Bees are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 3.7 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

In the last 10 years, London Bees and Oxford United have played each other on 11 occasions. London Bees won four of them and Oxford United seven.

On average, London Bees scored 1.6 goals and Oxford United 2.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 22, when Oxford United won 5-1 away.

London Bees's last match was on January 21, a 6-1 loss against Ipswich Town Women.

Oxford United beat Plymouth Argyle Women 4-0 last time out, on January 28.
