Plymouth Argyle Women v Rugby Borough Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Plymouth Argyle Women host Rugby Borough Women on Sunday in Women's National League Premier Division South.

In their last league match, on February 4, Plymouth Argyle beat Milton Keynes Dons Women 4-3 away.

Rugby Borough also won, 3-0 at home against Cardiff City Women on January 14.

With 29 goals in 13 games so far this season, Rugby Borough are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Plymouth Argyle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 3.4 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 13 games, of which they have won nine and lost four, earning 27 points.

The home team are three places behind Rugby Borough in seventh, with five wins and one draw putting them on 16 points.

Plymouth Argyle are in mixed form in Women's National League Premier Division South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Rugby Borough's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Plymouth Argyle's 10.
