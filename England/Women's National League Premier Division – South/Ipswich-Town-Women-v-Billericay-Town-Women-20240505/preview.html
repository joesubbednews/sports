Ipswich Town Women v Billericay Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Billericay Town Women travel looking to secure a first win in six Women's National League Premier Division – South games against Ipswich Town Women on Sunday.

Billericay Town have lost four and drawn one matches since they last earned three points – against Chatham Town Women on February 21.

They face an Ipswich Town side who have won three and lost two over that time.

With 60 goals in 19 games so far this season, Ipswich Town are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Billericay Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The away side are 10th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won four and drawn six, earning 18 points.

The hosts are five places ahead of Billericay Town in fifth, with 11 wins and two draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last five years, Ipswich Town and Billericay Town have played each other on four occasions. Ipswich Town won two of them, Billericay Town one, and they drew once.

On average, Ipswich Town scored 1.2 goals and Billericay Town 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 3, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Ipswich Town's last match was on April 21, a 4-2 win against Cardiff City Women.

Billericay Town drew 1-1 with Plymouth Argyle Women last time out, also on April 21.
