Oxford United Women v Rugby Borough Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Oxford United Women host Rugby Borough Women in Wednesday's late match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

Oxford United, who sit fourth in the league after 14 games, fell to a 5-1 home defeat to Portsmouth Women on February 11.

They face a Rugby Borough side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-2 victory against Plymouth Argyle Women, and who sit third in the table.

With 36 goals in 14 games so far this season, Oxford United are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Rugby Borough are also above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Oxford United's 15.
