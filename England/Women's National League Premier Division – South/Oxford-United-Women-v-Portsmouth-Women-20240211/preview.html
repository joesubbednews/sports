Oxford United Women v Portsmouth Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Women's National League Premier Division South's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when third-placed Oxford United Women host table-topping Portsmouth Women.

Oxford United have picked up nine wins and one draw from 13 games so far this season, and sit eight points below the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Portsmouth, meanwhile, have won 12, picking up 36 points.

With 48 goals in 13 games so far this season, Portsmouth are the league's highest scorers with 3.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in three goals at a rate of 0.2 per game.

Oxford United are also above average scorers, with 2.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home team are in exceptional form in Women's National League Premier Division South, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

And also with six wins and no losses over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 18 points from 18.

In the last five years, Oxford United and Portsmouth have played each other on five occasions. Oxford United won three of them, Portsmouth one, and they drew once.

On average, Oxford United scored 1.6 goals and Portsmouth 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Portsmouth won 4-0 at home.

Oxford United's last match was on February 4, a 4-1 win against London Bees Women.

Portsmouth beat Ipswich Town Women 1-0 last time out, also on February 4.
