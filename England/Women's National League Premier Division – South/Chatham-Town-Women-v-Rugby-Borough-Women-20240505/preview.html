Chatham Town Women v Rugby Borough Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Chatham Town Women face a challenge against high-flying Rugby Borough Women on Sunday.

Chatham Town Women are 11th in the Women's National League Premier Division – South table, and have picked up no wins and six draws in their 21 games to date.

Rugby Borough, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 45 points, having won 15 and drawn none, and are 13 points behind table-toppers Portsmouth Women.

Chatham Town are in terrible form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Rugby Borough's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' one.

With 13 goals in 21 games so far this season, Chatham Town are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 2.9 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Chatham Town's last match was on April 21, a 1-0 loss against C and K Basildon.

Rugby Borough beat London Bees Women 5-0 last time out, also on April 21.
