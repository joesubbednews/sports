Plymouth Argyle Women v Oxford United Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Plymouth Argyle Women face Oxford United Women on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-2 victory over Cheltenham Town LFC on January 21, Plymouth Argyle sit ninth in Women's National League Premier Division South.

They travel to play an Oxford United side fifth in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-2 against Chatham Town Women, on January 14.

With 27 goals in 11 games so far this season, Oxford United are scoring more than average in the league with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 12 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Plymouth Argyle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 3.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Plymouth Argyle and Oxford United have played each other on eight occasions. Plymouth Argyle won one of them, Oxford United six, and they drew once.

On average, Plymouth Argyle scored 0.5 goals and Oxford United 2.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 10, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

The home side are in mixed form in Women's National League Premier Division South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Plymouth Argyle's seven.
