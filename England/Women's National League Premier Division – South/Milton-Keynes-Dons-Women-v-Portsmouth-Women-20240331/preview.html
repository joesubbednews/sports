Milton Keynes Dons Women v Portsmouth Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Premier Division South's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when Portsmouth Women visit Milton Keynes Dons Women.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Portsmouth to date: the away side have let in just seven goals in 18 games.

Milton Keynes Dons have conceded 23 goals in 18 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Portsmouth are top of the table after 18 games, of which they have won 17 and lost one, earning 51 points.

Milton Keynes Dons are five places behind the away side in sixth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Portsmouth's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to Milton Keynes Dons' 10.

In the last five years, Milton Keynes Dons and Portsmouth have played each other on five occasions. Milton Keynes Dons won one of them, Portsmouth three, and they drew once.

On average, Milton Keynes Dons scored 1.0 goal and Portsmouth 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Portsmouth won 3-0 at home.

Milton Keynes Dons' last match was on Sunday, a 3-0 win against Cardiff City Women.

Portsmouth beat Rugby Borough Women 4-0 last time out, also on Sunday.
