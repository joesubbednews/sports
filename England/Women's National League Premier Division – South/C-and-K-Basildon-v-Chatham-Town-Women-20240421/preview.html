C and K Basildon v Chatham Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Chatham Town Women face a challenge away against high-flying C and K Basildon on Sunday.

Chatham Town Women are 11th in the Women's National League Premier Division – South table, and have picked up no wins and six draws in their 20 games to date.

C and K Basildon, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 46 points, having won 15 and drawn one of their first 19 matches, and are nine points behind table-toppers Portsmouth Women.

C and K Basildon are in good form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Chatham Town's form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the home team's 13.

With 13 goals in 20 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 3.0 per game.

C and K Basildon, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

C and K Basildon's last match was on March 31, a 3-1 win against Cardiff City Women.

Chatham Town lost 3-0 against Cheltenham Town LFC last time out, also on March 31.
