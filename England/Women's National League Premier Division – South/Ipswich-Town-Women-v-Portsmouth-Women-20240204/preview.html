Ipswich Town Women v Portsmouth Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South | AEL Arena
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match between Ipswich Town Women and Portsmouth Women promises to be one for the neutrals, as Women's National League Premier Division South's two most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at the AEL Arena, Ipswich Town and Portsmouth sit second and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 40 and 47 goals respectively.

Portsmouth are top of the table after 12 games, of which they have won 11 and lost one, earning 33 points.

Ipswich Town are four places behind the visitors in fifth, with seven wins and one draw putting them on 22 points.

The home side are in mixed form in Women's National League Premier Division South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Portsmouth's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to Ipswich Town's 10.

In the last five years, Ipswich Town and Portsmouth have played each other on five occasions. Ipswich Town won one of them, Portsmouth three, and they drew once.

On average, Ipswich Town scored 0.4 goals and Portsmouth 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 15 2023, when Portsmouth won 1-0 at home.

Ipswich Town's last match was on January 21, a 6-1 win against London Bees Women.

Portsmouth beat C and K Basildon 2-1 last time out, on January 14.
