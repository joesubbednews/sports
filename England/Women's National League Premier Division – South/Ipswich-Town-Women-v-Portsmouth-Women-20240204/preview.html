Ipswich Town Women v Portsmouth Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South | AEL Arena
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Women's National League Premier Division South's top sides face each other at the AEL Arena in Sunday's early kick-off, when fourth-placed Ipswich Town Women host table-topping Portsmouth Women.

Portsmouth, meanwhile, have won 11 of 12, picking up 33 points.

Ipswich Town are also above average scorers, with 3.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in Women's National League Premier Division South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to Ipswich Town's 10.

In the last five years, Ipswich Town and Portsmouth have played each other on five occasions. Ipswich Town won one of them, Portsmouth three, and they drew once.

On average, Ipswich Town scored 0.4 goals and Portsmouth 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 15 2023, when Portsmouth won 1-0 at home.

Ipswich Town's last match was on January 21, a 6-1 win against London Bees Women.

Portsmouth beat C and K Basildon 2-1 last time out, on January 14.
