Ipswich Town Women v Rugby Borough Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South | AEL Arena
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match between Ipswich Town Women and Rugby Borough Women promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Women's National League Premier Division South's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at the AEL Arena, Ipswich Town and Rugby Borough sit second and fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 42 and 35 goals respectively.

Ipswich Town are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, Rugby Borough's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.

The away team are third in the table after 15 games, of which they have won 11 and lost four, earning 33 points.

Ipswich Town are three places behind Rugby Borough in sixth, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

Ipswich Town's last match was on February 11, a 2-2 draw against Cheltenham Town LFC.

Rugby Borough beat Oxford United Women 2-0 last time out, on Wednesday.
