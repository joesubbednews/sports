Portsmouth Women v Plymouth Argyle Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Portsmouth Women host Plymouth Argyle Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

Portsmouth, who sit top of the league after 19 games, fell to a 0-0 away defeat to Milton Keynes Dons Women on March 31.

They face a Plymouth Argyle side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against London Bees Women, and who sit ninth in the table.

With 70 goals in 19 games so far this season, Portsmouth are the league's highest scorers with 3.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in seven goals at a rate of 0.4 per game.

Plymouth Argyle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 3.1 goals per game.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Portsmouth's 16.

In the last five years, Portsmouth and Plymouth Argyle have played each other on five occasions. Portsmouth won four of them and Plymouth Argyle one.

On average, Portsmouth scored 2.4 goals and Plymouth Argyle 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 15, when Portsmouth won 3-1 away.
