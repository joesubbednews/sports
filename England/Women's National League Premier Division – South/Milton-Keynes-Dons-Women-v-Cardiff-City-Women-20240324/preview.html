Milton Keynes Dons Women v Cardiff City Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Milton Keynes Dons Women host Cardiff City Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

In their last league match, on March 13, Milton Keynes Dons lost to Rugby Borough Women 2-0 away.

Cardiff City also lost, 1-0 away at Oxford United Women on March 17.

With 26 goals in 18 games so far this season, Cardiff City are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Milton Keynes Dons are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Milton Keynes Dons' 10.

In the last three years, Milton Keynes Dons and Cardiff City have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 22, when Milton Keynes Dons won 2-0 away.

Milton Keynes Dons are sixth in the table after 17 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

Cardiff City are one place behind the home side in seventh, with six wins and four draws putting them on 22 points.
