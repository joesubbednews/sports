Oxford United Women v London Bees Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates London Bees Women face a challenge away against high-flying Oxford United Women on Sunday.

London Bees Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Premier Division – South table, and have picked up no wins and four draws in their 18 games to date.

Oxford United, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 34 points, having won 11 and drawn one, and are 14 points behind table-toppers Portsmouth Women.

Oxford United are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, London Bees's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.

With 11 goals in 18 games so far this season, the away team are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

Oxford United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Oxford United and London Bees have played each other on 12 occasions. Oxford United won eight of them and London Bees four.

On average, Oxford United scored 2.7 goals and London Bees 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 4, when Oxford United won 4-1 away.

Oxford United's last match was on March 17, a 1-0 win against Cardiff City Women.

London Bees drew 1-1 with Chatham Town Women last time out, on March 13.
