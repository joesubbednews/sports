Billericay Town Women v Chatham Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Wednesday's late match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 10th-ranked Billericay Town Women host 11th-placed Chatham Town Women.

Billericay Town have picked up 14 points from their first 14 Women's National League Premier Division – South games, with three wins and five draws.

That is nine points more than Chatham Town have collected, having won none and drawn five.

Billericay Town are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Chatham Town's form is worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

With 10 goals in 15 games so far this season, the away side are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 2.9 per game.

Billericay Town are also below average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Billericay Town's last match was on February 11, a 1-1 draw against Milton Keynes Dons Women.

Chatham Town lost 7-0 against Portsmouth Women last time out, on Sunday.
