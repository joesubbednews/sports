Ipswich Town Women v Milton Keynes Dons Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South | AEL Arena
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Ipswich Town Women host Milton Keynes Dons Women on Sunday at the AEL Arena in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

In their last league match, on February 11, Ipswich Town drew with Cheltenham Town LFC 2-2 away.

Milton Keynes Dons also drew, 1-1 away at Billericay Town Women.

With 42 goals in 13 games so far this season, Ipswich Town are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Milton Keynes Dons, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Ipswich Town and Milton Keynes Dons have played each other on six occasions. Ipswich Town won five of them and they drew once.

On average, Ipswich Town scored 2.2 goals and Milton Keynes Dons 0.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when Ipswich Town won 1-0 away.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Ipswich Town's eight.

Ipswich Town are fifth in the table after 13 games, of which they have won seven and drawn two, earning 23 points.

Milton Keynes Dons are one place behind the home side in sixth, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.
