Chatham Town Women v Cardiff City Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.30pm

Struggling Chatham Town Women face Cardiff City Women on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-3 victory over Oxford United Women on January 14, Chatham Town sit 11th in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

They travel to play a Cardiff City side 10th in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Billericay Town Women, on February 4.

Chatham Town are in bad form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Cardiff City's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' three.

With 10 goals in 13 games so far this season, Chatham Town are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.
