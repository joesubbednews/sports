Portsmouth Women v London Bees Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates London Bees Women face a challenge away against high-flying Portsmouth Women on Sunday.

London Bees Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Premier Division – South table, and have picked up no wins and four draws in their 21 games to date.

Portsmouth, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 58 points, having won 19 and drawn one.

With 76 goals in 21 games so far this season, Portsmouth are the league's highest scorers with 3.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in nine goals at a rate of 0.4 per game.

London Bees, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 3.2 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to Portsmouth's 16.

In the last five years, Portsmouth and London Bees have played each other on five occasions. Portsmouth won four of them and London Bees one.

On average, Portsmouth scored 2.8 goals and London Bees 0.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 3, when Portsmouth won 4-0 away.

Portsmouth's last match was on April 21, a 3-2 win against Cheltenham Town LFC.

London Bees lost 5-0 against Rugby Borough Women last time out, also on April 21.
