Ipswich Town Women v C and K Basildon

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Two of the Women's National League Premier Division South's meanest defences go head-to-head on Wednesday, when C and K Basildon visit Ipswich Town Women.

Only one side – Portsmouth Women – has conceded fewer goals than C and K Basildon to date: the away side have let in just 13 goals in 15 games.

Ipswich Town have conceded 15 goals in 13 games, giving them the third tightest back line so far this season.

C and K Basildon are second in the table after 15 games, of which they have won 12 and lost three, earning 36 points.

Ipswich Town are three places behind the away side in fifth, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, C and K Basildon's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Ipswich Town's eight.

Ipswich Town's last match was on February 11, a 2-2 draw against Cheltenham Town LFC.

C and K Basildon beat London Bees Women 3-0 last time out, also on February 11.
