Plymouth Argyle Women v C and K Basildon

Women's National League Premier Division – South | The Mill Field
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Plymouth Argyle Women face a challenge against high-flying C and K Basildon at the Mill Field on Sunday.

Plymouth Argyle Women are 10th in the Women's National League Premier Division – South table, and have picked up five wins and one draw in their 17 games to date.

C and K Basildon, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 42 points, having won 14 and drawn none, and are six points behind table-toppers Portsmouth Women.

With 29 goals in 17 games so far this season, Plymouth Argyle are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 3.3 per game.

C and K Basildon, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The home team are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Plymouth Argyle's six.

Plymouth Argyle's last match was on March 10, a 2-1 loss against Cardiff City Women.

C and K Basildon beat Billericay Town Women 2-0 last time out, on Wednesday.
