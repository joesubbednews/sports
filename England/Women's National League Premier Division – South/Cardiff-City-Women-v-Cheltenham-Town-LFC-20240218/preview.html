Cardiff City Women v Cheltenham Town LFC

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Cardiff City Women host Cheltenham Town LFC on Sunday in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

In their last league match, on February 11, Cardiff City beat Chatham Town Women 2-0 away.

Cheltenham Town drew, 2-2 at home against Ipswich Town Women.

Cardiff City are in mixed form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Cheltenham Town's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

The away team are ninth in the table after 15 games, of which they have won four and drawn three, earning 15 points.

Cardiff City are two places ahead of Cheltenham Town in seventh, with four wins and four draws putting them on 16 points.

With 18 goals in 15 games so far this season, Cheltenham Town are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Cardiff City are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Cardiff City and Cheltenham Town have played each other twice. Cheltenham Town won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when Cheltenham Town won 3-0 away.
