Plymouth Argyle Women v Milton Keynes Dons Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Milton Keynes Dons Women travel to Plymouth Argyle Women on early Sunday in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Oxford United Women 1-1 away.

Plymouth Argyle also drew their last match, 1-1 against Billericay Town Women.

With 32 goals in 21 games so far this season, Milton Keynes Dons are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Plymouth Argyle are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 3.0 goals per game.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Plymouth Argyle's five.

Plymouth Argyle are ninth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won six and drawn three, earning 21 points.

Milton Keynes Dons are three places ahead of the hosts in sixth, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

In the last five years, Plymouth Argyle and Milton Keynes Dons have played each other on five occasions. Plymouth Argyle won three of them and Milton Keynes Dons two.

On average, Plymouth Argyle scored 2.8 goals and Milton Keynes Dons 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 4, when Plymouth Argyle won 4-3 away.
