London Bees Women v Rugby Borough Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates London Bees Women face a challenge against high-flying Rugby Borough Women on Sunday.

London Bees Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Premier Division – South table, and have picked up no wins and four draws in their 20 games to date.

Rugby Borough, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 42 points, having won 14 and drawn none, and are 13 points behind table-toppers Portsmouth Women.

London Bees are in terrible form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Rugby Borough's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' two.

With 13 goals in 20 games so far this season, London Bees are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 3.1 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

London Bees's last match was on March 31, a 2-1 loss against Plymouth Argyle Women.

Rugby Borough beat Ipswich Town Women 1-0 last time out, also on March 31.
