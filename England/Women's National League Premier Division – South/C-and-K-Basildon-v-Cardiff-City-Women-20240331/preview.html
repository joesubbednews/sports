C and K Basildon v Cardiff City Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

C and K Basildon host Cardiff City Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

In their last league match, on March 17, C and K Basildon drew with Plymouth Argyle Women 2-2 away.

Cardiff City lost, 3-0 away at Milton Keynes Dons Women on March 24.

C and K Basildon are second in the table after 18 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn one, earning 43 points.

Cardiff City are five places behind the hosts in seventh, with six wins and four draws putting them on 22 points.

With 26 goals in 19 games so far this season, the away side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

C and K Basildon, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

C and K Basildon are in good form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Cardiff City's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.
