Rugby Borough Women v Ipswich Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Rugby Borough Women host Ipswich Town Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Premier Division South.

Rugby Borough, who sit third in the league after 19 games, fell to a 4-0 away defeat to Portsmouth Women on March 24.

They face an Ipswich Town side who picked up a win in their last match, a 5-0 victory against Chatham Town Women, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 56 goals in 17 games so far this season, Ipswich Town are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Rugby Borough are also above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Rugby Borough's 12.
