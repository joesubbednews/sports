Portsmouth Women v Chatham Town Women

Women's National League Premier Division – South
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Chatham Town Women face a challenge away against high-flying Portsmouth Women on Sunday.

Chatham Town Women are 11th in the Women's National League Premier Division – South table, and have picked up no wins and five draws in their 14 games to date.

Portsmouth, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 39 points, having won 13 and drawn none.

With 53 goals in 14 games so far this season, Portsmouth are the league's highest scorers with 3.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in four goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

Chatham Town, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in exceptional form in the Women's National League Premier Division South, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to Portsmouth's 18.

Portsmouth's last match was on February 11, a 5-1 win against Oxford United Women.

Chatham Town lost 2-0 against Cardiff City Women last time out, also on Sunday.
