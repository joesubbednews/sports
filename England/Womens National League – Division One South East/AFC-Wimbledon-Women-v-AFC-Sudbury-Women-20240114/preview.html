AFC Wimbledon Women v AFC Sudbury Women

Womens National League – Division One South East
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates AFC Sudbury Women face a challenge away against high-flying AFC Wimbledon Women on Sunday.

AFC Sudbury Women are 10th in the Womens National League – Division One South East table, and have picked up just one win and three draws in their 11 games to date.

Wimbledon Women, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 30 points, having won 10 and drawn none of their first 12 matches.

With 51 goals in 12 games so far this season, Wimbledon Women are the league's highest scorers with 4.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Sudbury Women, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in fantastic form in Womens National League Division One South East, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to Wimbledon Women's 15.

Wimbledon Women's last match was on January 7, a 7-2 win against Hounslow WFC.

Sudbury Women lost 4-3 against Ashford Town (Middx) last time out, also on January 7.
