London Seaward Women v Worthing Women

Womens National League – Division One South East
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Womens National League Division One South East's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when Worthing Women visit London Seaward Women.

Only one side – Norwich City – has conceded fewer goals than Worthing to date: the away side have let in just 14 goals in 12 games.

London Seaward have conceded 18 goals in 11 games, giving them the joint-third tightest back line so far this season.

Worthing are third in the table after 12 games, of which they have won seven and drawn two, earning 23 points.

London Seaward are four places behind the away side in seventh, with four wins and three draws putting them on 15 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in Womens National League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Worthing's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to London Seaward's seven.

London Seaward's last match was on December 17, a 2-2 draw against Cambridge City Women.

Worthing drew 0-0 with Chesham United Women last time out, on January 7.
