Cambridge United Women v Norwich City

Womens National League – Division One South East
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match between Cambridge United Women and Norwich City promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Womens National League Division One South East's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Cambridge United and Norwich sit joint-fourth and second in the goal-scoring charts, with 26 and 33 goals respectively.

Norwich are second in the table after 11 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

Cambridge United are three places behind City in fifth, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in Womens National League Division One South East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Cambridge United's eight.

Over the last year, Cambridge United and Norwich have played each other twice. Norwich won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Cambridge United's last match was on January 7, a 2-2 draw against Haywards Heath Town.

Norwich beat Chesham United Women 4-1 last time out, on December 17.
