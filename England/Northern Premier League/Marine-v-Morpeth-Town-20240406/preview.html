Marine v Morpeth Town

Northern Premier League | The Marine Travel Arena
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Marine host Morpeth Town in Saturday's match at the Marine Travel Arena looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League.

Marine, who sit fourth in the league after 37 games, fell to a 2-0 away defeat to Warrington Rylands on Monday.

They face a Morpeth side who also lost their last match, a 3-1 defeat to Warrington Rylands, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 75 goals in 37 games so far this season, Marine are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Morpeth are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Town's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Marine's 12.

In the last three years, Marine and Morpeth have played each other on three occasions. Marine won two of them and Morpeth one.

Their last meeting was on December 12, when Morpeth won 1-0 at home.
