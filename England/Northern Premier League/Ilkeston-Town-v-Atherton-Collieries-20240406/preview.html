Ilkeston Town v Atherton Collieries

Northern Premier League | New Manor Ground
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Atherton Collieries face Ilkeston Town away at the New Manor Ground on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-2 victory over Workington on Monday, Atherton Collieries sit 21st in the Northern Premier League.

They travel to play an Ilkeston side 11th in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Matlock Town.

Ilkeston are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Atherton Collieries's form is worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to Town's eight.

With 46 goals in 35 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 87 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.
