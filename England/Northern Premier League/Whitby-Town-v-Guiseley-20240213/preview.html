Whitby Town v Guiseley

Northern Premier League | Turnbull Ground
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Whitby Town host Guiseley on Tuesday at the Turnbull Ground in the Northern Premier League.

In their last league match, on February 3, Whitby beat Basford United 3-0 at home.

Guiseley drew, 1-1 away at Ashton United on Saturday.

With 51 goals in 28 games so far this season, Guiseley are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Whitby, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The Lions are seventh in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn seven, earning 46 points.

Town are two places behind the away team in ninth, with 13 wins and six draws putting them on 45 points.

In the last five years, Whitby and Guiseley have played each other on four occasions. Whitby won one of them and Guiseley the other.

On average, Whitby scored 1.2 goals and the Lions 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 15, when Whitby won 3-1 away.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, Guiseley's form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
