Matlock Town v Guiseley

Northern Premier League | DCJ Group Insurance Arena
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Matlock Town welcome Guiseley to the DCJ Group Insurance Arena on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Matlock's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Northern Premier League matches, while their opponents have earned six from a possible 18.

With 62 goals in 36 games so far this season, Guiseley are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Matlock, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last five years, Matlock and Guiseley have played each other on four occasions. Guiseley won all of them.

On average, Matlock scored 0.2 goals and the Lions 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 21, when Guiseley won 4-0 at home.

Town are 14th in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

The Lions are three places ahead of the hosts in 11th, with 16 wins and seven draws putting them on 55 points.

Matlock's last match was on April 6, a 4-0 loss against Radcliffe Borough.

Guiseley lost 2-0 against Warrington Rylands last time out, also on April 6.
