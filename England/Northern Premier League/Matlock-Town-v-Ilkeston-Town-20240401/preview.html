Matlock Town v Ilkeston Town

Northern Premier League | DCJ Group Insurance Arena
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Matlock Town welcome Ilkeston Town to the DCJ Group Insurance Arena on Monday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Matlock's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Northern Premier League matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

With 61 goals in 36 games so far this season, Ilkeston are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Matlock, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Matlock Town are 14th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

Ilkeston Town are three places ahead of the home team in 11th, with 15 wins and six draws putting them on 51 points.

Over the last two years, Matlock and Ilkeston have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Matlock won 1-0 away.

Matlock's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 loss against Marine.

Ilkeston drew 2-2 with Bradford Park Avenue last time out, also on Saturday.
