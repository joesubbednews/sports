Ilkeston Town v Hyde United

Northern Premier League | New Manor Ground
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hyde United are strong favourites to take all three points despite Ilkeston Town's home advantage in Saturday's match at the New Manor Ground.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.86 on Hyde sealing the win, with the visitors sitting second in the Northern Premier League table.

Ilkeston, who are 12th in the league and 16 points behind Hyde, are priced at 3.1 to win. A draw is set at 3.97.

With 64 goals in 34 games so far this season, Hyde are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Ilkeston are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, United's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home side's seven.

Ilkeston's last match was on February 24, a 2-1 loss against Radcliffe Borough.

Hyde beat Workington 1-0 last time out, on March 9.
