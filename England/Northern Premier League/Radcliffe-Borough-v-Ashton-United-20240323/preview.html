Radcliffe Borough v Ashton United

Northern Premier League | Stainton Park Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Radcliffe Borough are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Saturday's kick-off against Ashton United.

The Boro, who sit top of the Northern Premier League with 34 games played, are priced at 1.5 to seal victory at the Stainton Park Stadium.

Sitting eight places and 25 points behind them in the table, Ashton are 4.7 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 4.7.

With 90 goals in 34 games so far this season, Radcliffe are the league's highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Ashton are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Radcliffe and Ashton have played each other on seven occasions. Radcliffe won three of them, Ashton two, and they drew twice.

On average, the Boro scored 1.4 goals and Ashton 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Radcliffe won 2-0 away.

Borough are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with three wins and two draws over that period, United's form is identical – they have both taken 11 points from 18.

Radcliffe's last match was on March 16, a 3-0 win against Stafford Rangers.
