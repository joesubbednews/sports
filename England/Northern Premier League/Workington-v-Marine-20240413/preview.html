Workington v Marine

Northern Premier League | Borough Park
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Workington welcome Marine to Borough Park on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Workington's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Northern Premier League matches, while their opponents have earned 12 from a possible 18.

With 78 goals in 38 games so far this season, Marine are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Workington are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Workington and Marine have played each other on 15 occasions. Workington won eight of them, Marine three, and they drew four times.

On average, Workington scored 1.5 goals and Marine 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 28, when they played out a 4-4 draw.

The away side are fourth in the table after 38 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn eight, earning 68 points.

The hosts are 12 places behind Marine in 16th, with 10 wins and 11 draws putting them on 41 points.

Workington's last match was on April 6, a 2-1 loss against Worksop Town.

Marine beat Morpeth Town 3-1 last time out, also on April 6.
