Hyde United v Macclesfield

Northern Premier League | Ewen Fields
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hyde United host Macclesfield in Monday's match at Ewen Fields looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League.

Hyde, who sit fourth in the league after 37 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Worksop Town on Saturday.

They face a Macclesfield side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-1 victory against United of Manchester, and who sit second in the table.

With 74 goals in 34 games so far this season, Macclesfield are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Hyde are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

United are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.
