Whitby Town v Guiseley

Northern Premier League | Turnbull Ground
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Whitby Town host Guiseley on Tuesday at Turnbull Ground in Northern Premier League.

Town are one place behind the away team in eighth, with 12 wins and five draws putting them on 41 points.

In the last five years, Whitby and Guiseley have played each other on four occasions. Whitby won one of them and Guiseley the other.

On average, Whitby scored 1.2 goals and the Lions 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 15, when Whitby won 3-1 away.

The hosts are in mixed form in Northern Premier League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.
