Ilkeston Town v Marine

Northern Premier League | New Manor Ground
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Marine visit Ilkeston Town at the New Manor Ground on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in Northern Premier League.

Marine have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face an Ilkeston side who have also won their last four matches, and collected 12 points from the last possible 18.

With 64 goals in 29 games so far this season, Marine are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Ilkeston, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The away side are second in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn six, earning 54 points.

Town are five places behind Marine in seventh, with 14 wins and three draws putting them on 45 points.

Ilkeston's last match was on January 27, a 2-0 win against Stafford Rangers.

Marine beat United of Manchester 3-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
