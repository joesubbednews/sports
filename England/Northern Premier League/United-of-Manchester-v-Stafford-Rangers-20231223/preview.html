United of Manchester v Stafford Rangers

Northern Premier League | Broadhurst Park
Saturday, 23 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Stafford Rangers make the journey to Broadhurst Park to play United of Manchester on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

United of Manchester are five places ahead of Rangers in 16th, with seven wins and four draws putting them on 25 points.

In the last 10 years, United of Manchester and Stafford have played each other on nine occasions. They won four each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 19, when Stafford won 3-2 at home.

Stafford lost 1-0 against Whitby Town last time out, on Tuesday.
