Radcliffe Borough v Marske United

Northern Premier League | Stainton Park Stadium
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Radcliffe Borough host Marske United in Saturday's match at the Stainton Park Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League.

The Boro, who sit top of the league after 35 games, fell to a 5-2 home defeat to Ashton United on March 23.

They face a Marske side who also lost their last match, a 2-0 defeat to Morpeth Town, and who sit 21st in the table.

With 92 goals in 35 games so far this season, Radcliffe are the league's highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Marske, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.4 goals per game.

In the last three years, Radcliffe and Marske have played each other on three occasions. Radcliffe won two of them and Marske one.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Radcliffe won 5-2 away.

Borough are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, United's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the Boro's 10.
