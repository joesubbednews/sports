Morpeth Town v Atherton Collieries

Northern Premier League
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Morpeth Town and Atherton Collieries both come into Saturday's kick-off in poor form, having picked up only four points each in their last six games.

The two teams have each collected one win and one draw from their last six outings.

Atherton Collieries are bottom of the table after 31 games, of which they have won five and drawn four, earning 19 points.

Morpeth are 10 places ahead of the visitors in 12th, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on 44 points.

With 40 goals in 31 games so far this season, Atherton Collieries are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 79 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Morpeth and Atherton Collieries have played each other on eight occasions. Morpeth won four of them, Atherton Collieries two, and they drew twice.

On average, Morpeth scored 2.4 goals and Atherton Collieries 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 3, when Atherton Collieries won 4-3 at home.

Morpeth's last match was on February 24, a 3-0 win against United of Manchester.

Atherton Collieries lost 2-1 against Guiseley last time out, on Tuesday.
