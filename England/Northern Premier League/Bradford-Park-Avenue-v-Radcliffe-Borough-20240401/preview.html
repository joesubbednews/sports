Bradford Park Avenue v Radcliffe Borough

Northern Premier League | Horsfall Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Radcliffe Borough travel to the Horsfall Stadium for Monday's match against Bradford Park Avenue looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League.

The Boro, who sit top of the league after 35 games, fell to a 5-2 home defeat to Ashton United on March 23.

They face a Bradford Park Avenue side who secured a draw in their last match, a 2-2 tie with Ilkeston Town, and who sit 20th in the table.

With 92 goals in 35 games so far this season, Radcliffe are the league's highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Bradford Park Avenue, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Borough's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Bradford Park Avenue's five.
