Morpeth Town v Warrington Rylands

Northern Premier League
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Morpeth Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Warrington Rylands defence on Saturday.

With 65 goals in 35 games, Morpeth are the joint-fourth-highest scorers in the Northern Premier League ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Warrington Rylands side who have scored 52 in 33 matches, but conceded only 36 goals, putting them top of the league's tightest defences.

Warrington Rylands are fifth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn 12, earning 60 points.

Morpeth are seven places behind the away side in 12th, with 14 wins and eight draws putting them on 50 points.

In the last three years, Morpeth and Warrington Rylands have played each other on three occasions. Warrington Rylands won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Warrington Rylands won 2-0 at home.

Town are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Warrington Rylands's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.

Morpeth's last match was on March 23, a 3-0 loss against Macclesfield.

Warrington Rylands drew 0-0 with Hyde United last time out, also on March 23.
