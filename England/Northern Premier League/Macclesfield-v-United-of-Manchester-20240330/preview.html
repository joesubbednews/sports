Macclesfield v United of Manchester

Northern Premier League
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Macclesfield are on a fantastic run ahead of hosting United of Manchester on Saturday, with 15 points collected from their last six games.

Macclesfield have picked up five wins and one loss in their last six Northern Premier League games, and face a F.C. United side whose last six games have brought three wins and three losses.

With 71 goals in 33 games so far this season, Macclesfield are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

United of Manchester, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The hosts are second in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn five, earning 65 points.

F.C. United are 13 places behind Macclesfield in 15th, with 13 wins and four draws putting them on 43 points.

Macclesfield's last match was on March 23, a 3-0 win against Morpeth Town.

United of Manchester beat Basford United 2-0 last time out, also on March 23.
