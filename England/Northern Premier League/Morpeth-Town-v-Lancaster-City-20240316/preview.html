Morpeth Town v Lancaster City

Northern Premier League
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lancaster City travel to Morpeth Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Matlock Town 2-1 away.

Morpeth, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-1 against Atherton Collieries.

With 63 goals in 33 games so far this season, Morpeth are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Lancaster, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Morpeth and Lancaster have played each other on 11 occasions. Morpeth won three of them, Lancaster five, and they drew three times.

On average, Morpeth scored 2.4 goals and Lancaster 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 24, when Lancaster won 3-2 at home.

Town are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, City's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

The away team are ninth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn eight, earning 50 points.

Morpeth are two places behind Lancaster in 11th, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on 47 points.
