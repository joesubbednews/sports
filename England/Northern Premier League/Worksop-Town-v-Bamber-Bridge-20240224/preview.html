Worksop Town v Bamber Bridge

Northern Premier League | Windsor Food Service Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Worksop Town face Bamber Bridge on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League.

Worksop are unbeaten in five, with two wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Bamber Bridge team who have won just one and lost four over the same number of games.

With 58 goals in 30 games so far this season, Worksop are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Bamber Bridge, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Town are fourth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn eight, earning 53 points.

The visitors are 11 places behind the home side in 15th, with eight wins and 11 draws putting them on 35 points.

Worksop's last match was on February 17, a 0-0 draw against Matlock Town.

Bamber Bridge beat Morpeth Town 3-1 last time out, on February 10.
