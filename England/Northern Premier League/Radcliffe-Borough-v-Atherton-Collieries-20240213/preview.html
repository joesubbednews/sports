Radcliffe Borough v Atherton Collieries

Northern Premier League | Stainton Park Stadium
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Relegation candidates Atherton Collieries face a challenge away against high-flying Radcliffe Borough at the Stainton Park Stadium on Tuesday.

Atherton Collieries are 21st in the Northern Premier League table, and have picked up five wins and three draws in their 28 games to date.

The Boro, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 67 points, having won 22 and drawn one of their first 29 matches.

With 80 goals in 29 games so far this season, Radcliffe are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Atherton Collieries, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Radcliffe and Atherton Collieries have played each other on eight occasions. Radcliffe won three of them, Atherton Collieries two, and they drew three times.

On average, the Boro scored 1.4 goals and Atherton Collieries 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Radcliffe won 4-0 away.

Borough are in good form in the Northern Premier League, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the Boro's 13.

Radcliffe's last match was on Saturday, a 1-1 draw against Worksop Town.

Atherton Collieries lost 1-0 against Gainsborough Trinity last time out, also on Saturday.
