Matlock Town v Whitby Town

Northern Premier League | DCJ Group Insurance Arena
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Matlock Town are on a poor run ahead of hosting Whitby Town at the DCJ Group Insurance Arena on Saturday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Matlock have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Northern Premier League games, and face a Whitby side whose last six games have brought two wins and two draws.

In the last 10 years, Matlock and Whitby have played each other on 18 occasions. Matlock won seven of them, Whitby nine, and they drew twice.

On average, Matlock scored 1.3 goals and Whitby 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 5, when Whitby won 1-0 at home.

Matlock, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Whitby Town are eighth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn eight, earning 53 points.

Matlock Town are five places behind the visitors in 13th, with 13 wins and five draws putting them on 44 points.

Matlock's last match was on March 16, a 2-1 loss against Basford United.

Whitby drew 1-1 with Bradford Park Avenue last time out, also on March 16.
