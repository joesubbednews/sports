Ilkeston Town v Lancaster City

Northern Premier League | New Manor Ground
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ilkeston Town face Lancaster City on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Northern Premier League.

Ilkeston are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Lancaster team who have won three and lost two over the same number of games.

With 40 goals in 27 games so far this season, Lancaster are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Ilkeston, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Town are seventh in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

City are four places behind the hosts in 11th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

Ilkeston's last match was on February 3, a 1-1 draw against Marine.

Lancaster beat Matlock Town 1-0 last time out, also on February 3.
