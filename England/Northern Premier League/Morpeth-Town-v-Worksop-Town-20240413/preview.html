Morpeth Town v Worksop Town

Northern Premier League
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Morpeth Town welcome Worksop Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Morpeth's struggles have left them with nine points from their last six Northern Premier League matches, while their opponents have earned 12 from a possible 18.

With 67 goals in 37 games so far this season, Morpeth are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 73 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Worksop are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Worksop Town are sixth in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn eight, earning 65 points.

Morpeth Town are seven places behind the visitors in 13th, with 14 wins and eight draws putting them on 50 points.

Morpeth's last match was on April 6, a 3-1 loss against Marine.

Worksop beat Workington 2-1 last time out, also on April 6.
