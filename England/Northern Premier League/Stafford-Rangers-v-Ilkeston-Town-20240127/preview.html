Stafford Rangers v Ilkeston Town

Northern Premier League | Marston Road
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ilkeston Town visit Stafford Rangers at Marston Road on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Northern Premier League.

Ilkeston have picked up nine points from their last six games, and they face a Stafford side who have lost their last three matches, and collected six points from the last possible 18.

Stafford are 21st in the table after 27 games, of which they have won five and drawn two, earning 17 points.

Ilkeston are 13 places ahead of Rangers in eighth, with 13 wins and three draws putting them on 42 points.

With 27 goals in 27 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Stafford's last match was on January 20, a 2-0 loss against Worksop Town.

Ilkeston beat Basford United 4-1 last time out, also on January 20.
