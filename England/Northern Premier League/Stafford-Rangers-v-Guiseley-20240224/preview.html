Stafford Rangers v Guiseley

Northern Premier League | Marston Road
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stafford Rangers face Guiseley at Marston Road on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League games.

Stafford have lost four and drawn one matches since they last earned three points – against Matlock Town on December 30.

They face a Guiseley side who have won three and drawn one over that time.

Stafford are bottom of the table after 29 games, of which they have won five and drawn three, earning 18 points.

Guiseley are 15 places ahead of Rangers in seventh, with 14 wins and seven draws putting them on 49 points.

With 28 goals in 29 games so far this season, the home side are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The Lions, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, Stafford and Guiseley have played each other on three occasions. Guiseley won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when Guiseley won 2-0 at home.

Stafford's last match was on February 17, a 1-1 draw against Hyde United.

Guiseley beat Morpeth Town 4-1 last time out, also on February 17.
