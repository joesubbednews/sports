Ilkeston Town v Bradford Park Avenue

Northern Premier League | New Manor Ground
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bradford Park Avenue travel to Ilkeston Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Worksop Town 2-1 at home.

Ilkeston, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-1 against Guiseley.

Bradford Park Avenue are 19th in the table after 36 games, of which they have won six and drawn seven, earning 25 points.

Ilkeston are nine places ahead of the away team in 10th, with 15 wins and five draws putting them on 50 points.

With 39 goals in 36 games so far this season, Bradford Park Avenue are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 72 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

And also with a win and two draws over that period, Bradford Park Avenue's form is identical – they have both taken five points from 18.
