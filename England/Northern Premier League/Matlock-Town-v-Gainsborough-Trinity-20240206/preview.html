Matlock Town v Gainsborough Trinity

Northern Premier League
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Matlock Town host Gainsborough Trinity on Tuesday in Northern Premier League.

The Blues are 17th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

Town are four places ahead of the visitors in 13th, with 12 wins and four draws putting them on 40 points.

The home side are in mixed form in Northern Premier League, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Gainsborough Trinity's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Matlock's nine.
