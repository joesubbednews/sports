Workington v Bradford Park Avenue

Northern Premier League | Borough Park
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Bradford Park Avenue face Workington away at Borough Park on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-2 victory over Rylands on February 3, Bradford Park Avenue sit 20th in Northern Premier League.

They travel to play a Workington side 15th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-0 against Hyde United.

Workington are in bad form in Northern Premier League, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Bradford Park Avenue's form is similar – they have both taken three points from 18.

With 33 goals in 29 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.
