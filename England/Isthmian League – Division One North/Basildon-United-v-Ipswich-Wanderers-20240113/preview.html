Basildon United v Ipswich Wanderers

Isthmian League – Division One North
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Basildon United host Ipswich Wanderers on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One North.

Basildon have picked up 10 points from their last six games, and they face an Ipswich side who have lost their last eight matches, and collected no points from the last possible 18.

With 16 goals in 18 games so far this season, Ipswich are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Basildon, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Wanderers are 17th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won four and drawn one, earning 13 points.

United are 12 places ahead of the visitors in fifth, with 11 wins and three draws putting them on 36 points.

Basildon's last match was on Tuesday, a 4-2 win against Stowmarket Town.

Ipswich lost 2-1 against Maldon and Tiptree last time out, on January 6.
