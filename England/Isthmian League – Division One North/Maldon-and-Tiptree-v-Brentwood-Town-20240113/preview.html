Maldon and Tiptree v Brentwood Town

Isthmian League – Division One North | Wallace Binder Stadium
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Maldon and Tiptree host Brentwood Town at the Wallace Binder Stadium on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One North.

Maldon & Tiptree have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Brentwood side who drew their last match, and have collected nine points from the last possible 18.

Brentwood, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Town are sixth in the table after 19 games, of which they have won nine and drawn six, earning 33 points.

The Jammers are one place behind the visitors in seventh, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on the same number of points.

In the last five years, Maldon & Tiptree and Brentwood have played each other on five occasions. Maldon & Tiptree won one of them, Brentwood two, and they drew twice.

On average, the Jammers scored 1.2 goals and Brentwood 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 4, when Brentwood won 2-0 away.

Maldon & Tiptree's last match was on January 6, a 2-1 win against Ipswich Wanderers.

Brentwood drew 2-2 with Felixstowe and Walton Utd. last time out, also on January 6.
