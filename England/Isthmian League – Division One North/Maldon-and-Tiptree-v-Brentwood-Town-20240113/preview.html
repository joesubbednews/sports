Maldon and Tiptree v Brentwood Town

Isthmian League – Division One North | Wallace Binder Stadium
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Maldon and Tiptree face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Brentwood Town defence on Saturday.

With 39 goals in 19 games, Maldon & Tiptree are the fourth-highest scorers in Isthmian League Division One North ahead of the 3pm kick-off at the Wallace Binder Stadium.

They face a Brentwood side who have scored 33 in 19 matches, but conceded only 18 goals, putting them second among the league's tightest defences – only East Thurrock United have conceded fewer goals.

Brentwood are fifth in the table after 19 games, of which they have won nine and drawn six, earning 33 points.

Maldon & Tiptree are one place behind Town in sixth, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on the same number of points.

In the last five years, Maldon & Tiptree and Brentwood have played each other on five occasions. Maldon & Tiptree won one of them, Brentwood two, and they drew twice.

On average, the Jammers scored 1.2 goals and Brentwood 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 4, when Brentwood won 2-0 away.

The Jammers are in reasonable form in Isthmian League Division One North, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home side's 12.

Maldon & Tiptree's last match was on January 6, a 2-1 win against Ipswich Wanderers.

Brentwood drew 2-2 with Felixstowe and Walton Utd. last time out, also on January 6.
