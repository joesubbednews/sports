Bury Town v Bowers and Pitsea

Isthmian League – Division One North | Ram Meadow
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bury Town and Bowers and Pitsea both come into Saturday's kick-off at Ram Meadow in great form, having picked up 16 and 15 points respectively in their last six games.

Bury have won five and drawn one of the last six, while Bowers & Pitsea have taken five wins and one loss.

With 43 goals in 18 games so far this season, Bury are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Bowers & Pitsea are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 19 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn one, earning 37 points.

Town are four places behind Bowers & Pitsea in eighth, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

Bury's last match was on Sunday, a 5-0 win against New Salamis.

Bowers & Pitsea beat Witham Town 2-0 last time out, on January 6.
