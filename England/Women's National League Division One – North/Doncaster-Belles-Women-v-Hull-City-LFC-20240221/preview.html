Doncaster Belles Women v Hull City LFC

Women's National League Division One – North
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Wednesday's late match between Doncaster Belles Women and Hull City LFC promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Women's National League Division One North's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Doncaster Belles and Hull City sit third and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 27 and 34 goals respectively.

Hull City are third in the table after 12 games, of which they have won nine and drawn one, earning 28 points.

Doncaster Belles are four places behind the away team in seventh, with five wins and two draws putting them on 17 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One North, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Hull City's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Doncaster Belles's 10.

Doncaster Belles's last match was on February 11, a 3-0 win against Norton and Stockton.

Hull City beat Norton and Stockton 3-2 last time out, on February 4.
