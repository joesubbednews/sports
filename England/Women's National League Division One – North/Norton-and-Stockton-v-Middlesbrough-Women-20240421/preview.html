Norton and Stockton v Middlesbrough Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Middlesbrough Women visit Norton and Stockton on early Sunday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Women's National League Division One North.

Middlesbrough have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Norton & Stockton side who also won their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

Middlesbrough are top of the table after 20 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

Norton & Stockton are seven places behind the away side in eighth, with six wins and five draws putting them on 23 points.

With 39 goals in 20 games so far this season, Middlesbrough are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, Norton & Stockton and Middlesbrough have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 20, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Norton & Stockton's last match was on March 31, a 1-0 win against York City LFC.

Middlesbrough beat United of Manchester Women 2-1 last time out, also on March 31.
