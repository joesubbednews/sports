Chester-le-Street Town FC Women v Doncaster Belles Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Chester-le-Street Town FC Women are on a poor run ahead of hosting Doncaster Belles Women on Sunday, with just three points collected from their last six games.

Chester-le-Street Town FC have picked up just one win in their last six Women's National League Division One – North games, and face a Doncaster Belles side whose last six games have brought three wins and one draw.

With 16 goals in 13 games so far this season, Chester-le-Street Town FC are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Doncaster Belles, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The hosts are 11th in the table after 13 games, of which they have won two and drawn one, earning seven points.

The away side are four places ahead of Chester-le-Street Town FC in seventh, with five wins and two draws putting them on 17 points.

Chester-le-Street Town FC's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-0 loss against Norton and Stockton.

Doncaster Belles beat Norton and Stockton 3-0 last time out, on February 11.
