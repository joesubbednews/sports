Chester-le-Street Town FC Women v United of Manchester Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match sees the league's two bottom sides face each other, as rock-bottom Chester-le-Street Town FC Women host United of Manchester Women.

Chester-le-Street Town FC have picked up four points from their first four Women's National League Division One – North games, with one win and one draw.

That is one point less than United of Manchester have collected, having won one and drawn two.

Chester-le-Street Town FC are in terrible form in Women's National League Division One North, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with no wins and a draw over that period, United of Manchester's form is identical – they have both taken one point from 18.

With eight goals in 12 games so far this season, the away side are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 3.8 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Chester-le-Street Town FC and United of Manchester have played each other on three occasions. Chester-le-Street Town FC won two of them and United of Manchester one.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when United of Manchester won 5-4 at home.

Chester-le-Street Town FC's last match was on January 14, a 4-0 loss against Hull City LFC.

United of Manchester drew 0-0 with York City LFC last time out, also on January 14.
