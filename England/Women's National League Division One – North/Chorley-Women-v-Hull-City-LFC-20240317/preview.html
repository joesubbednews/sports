Chorley Women v Hull City LFC

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Chorley Women face a challenge against high-flying Hull City LFC on Sunday.

Chorley Women are ninth in the Women's National League Division One – North table, and have picked up three wins and three draws in their 13 games to date.

Hull City, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 31 points, having won 10 and drawn one, and are five points behind table-toppers Durham Cestria WFC.

With 38 goals in 13 games so far this season, Hull City are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Chorley, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in terrible form in the Women's National League Division One North, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Chorley's one.

Over the last two years, Chorley and Hull City have played each other twice. Hull City won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on April 23, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Chorley's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-1 loss against Stockport County Women.

Hull City beat Barnsley Women 4-1 last time out, also on Wednesday.
