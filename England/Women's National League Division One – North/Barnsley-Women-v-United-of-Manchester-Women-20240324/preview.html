Barnsley Women v United of Manchester Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Barnsley Women face United of Manchester Women on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-4 victory over Hull City LFC on March 13, Barnsley sit fourth in the Women's National League Division One North.

They travel to play an United of Manchester side 12th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-0 against Stockport County Women, on March 10.

Barnsley are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One North, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, United of Manchester's form is worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the home team's five.

With 11 goals in 16 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 3.6 per game.

Barnsley, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Barnsley and United of Manchester have played each other on three occasions. Barnsley won two of them and United of Manchester one.

Their last meeting was on December 17, when Barnsley won 6-0 away.
