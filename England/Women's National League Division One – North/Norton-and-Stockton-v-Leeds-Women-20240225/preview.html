Norton and Stockton v Leeds Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Norton and Stockton host Leeds Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Division One North.

In their last league match, on Wednesday, Norton & Stockton beat Chester-le-Street Town FC Women 2-0 away.

Leeds also won, 3-1 away at Chester-le-Street Town FC Women on February 4.

With 25 goals in 12 games so far this season, Leeds are scoring more than average in the league with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Norton & Stockton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Norton & Stockton and Leeds have played each other on five occasions. Norton & Stockton won three of them and Leeds two.

On average, Norton & Stockton scored 1.8 goals and Leeds 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 5, when Norton & Stockton won 2-0 away.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Division One North, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Norton & Stockton's 12.

Norton & Stockton are eighth in the table after 14 games, of which they have won four and drawn four, earning 16 points.

Leeds are two places ahead of the hosts in sixth, with seven wins and one draw putting them on 22 points.
