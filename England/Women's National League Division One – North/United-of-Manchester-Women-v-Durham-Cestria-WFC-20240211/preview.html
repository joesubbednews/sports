United of Manchester Women v Durham Cestria WFC

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates United of Manchester Women face a challenge against high-flying Durham Cestria WFC on Sunday.

United of Manchester Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Division One – North table, and have picked up just one win and two draws in their 13 games to date.

Durham Cestria WFC, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 23 points, having won seven and drawn two, and are five points behind table-toppers Hull City LFC.

United of Manchester are in terrible form in the Women's National League Division One North, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Durham Cestria WFC's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home team's one.

With eight goals in 13 games so far this season, United of Manchester are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 3.6 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

In the last three years, United of Manchester and Durham Cestria WFC have played each other on three occasions. Durham Cestria WFC won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Durham Cestria WFC won 3-0 at home.

United of Manchester's last match was on January 28, a 2-0 loss against Chester-le-Street Town FC Women.

Durham Cestria WFC beat Barnsley Women 3-2 last time out, on February 4.
