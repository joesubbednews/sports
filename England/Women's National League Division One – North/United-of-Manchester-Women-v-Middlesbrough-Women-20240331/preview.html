United of Manchester Women v Middlesbrough Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates United of Manchester Women face a challenge against high-flying Middlesbrough Women on Sunday.

United of Manchester Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Division One – North table, and have picked up just one win and two draws in their 17 games to date.

Middlesbrough, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 39 points, having won 11 and drawn six of their first 19 matches.

United of Manchester are in terrible form in the Women's National League Division One North, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Middlesbrough's form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to the hosts' one.

With 12 goals in 17 games so far this season, United of Manchester are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 3.5 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

United of Manchester's last match was on Sunday, a 3-1 loss against Barnsley Women.

Middlesbrough beat Chorley Women 3-0 last time out, also on Sunday.
