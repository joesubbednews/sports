Leeds Women v Chorley Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Chorley Women come to play Leeds Women on early Sunday in terrible form, having collected just one point from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn just one of their last six fixtures, while Leeds have three wins and three losses.

With 12 goals in 12 games so far this season, Chorley are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Leeds, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The away team are ninth in the table after 12 games, of which they have won three and drawn three, earning 12 points.

The hosts are three places ahead of Chorley in sixth, with seven wins and one draw putting them on 22 points.

In the last five years, Leeds and Chorley have played each other on five occasions. Leeds won one of them, Chorley two, and they drew twice.

On average, Leeds scored 1.8 goals and Chorley 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Leeds's last match was on February 4, a 3-1 win against Chester-le-Street Town FC Women.

Chorley lost 4-1 against Doncaster Belles Women last time out, on January 28.
