Chester-le-Street Town FC Women v Stockport County Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Stockport County Women face Chester-le-Street Town FC Women on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Women's National League Division One North.

Stockport County are unbeaten in seven, with four wins and three draws, ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a Chester-le-Street Town FC team who have won just two and lost five over the same number of games.

Chester-le-Street Town FC are 10th in the table after 16 games, of which they have won three and drawn one, earning 10 points.

Stockport County are five places ahead of the home side in fifth, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 21 goals in 16 games so far this season, Chester-le-Street Town FC are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Chester-le-Street Town FC and Stockport County have played each other on three occasions. Stockport County won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Stockport County won 2-0 at home.

Chester-le-Street Town FC's last match was on March 23, a 3-1 loss against Doncaster Belles Women.

Stockport County beat Chorley Women 2-1 last time out, on March 13.
