United of Manchester Women v Leeds Women

Women's National League Division One – North | Broadhurst Park
Friday, 15 March 2024 @ 0.00am

Leeds Women are strong favourites to take all three points despite United of Manchester Women's home advantage in Friday's early match at Broadhurst Park.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.17 on Leeds sealing the win, with the visitors sitting sixth in the Women's National League Division One North table.

United of Manchester, who are 12th in the league and 20 points behind Leeds, are priced at 12 to win. A draw is set at 7.

United of Manchester are in terrible form in the Women's National League Division One North, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Leeds's form is much better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' one.

With 11 goals in 16 games so far this season, United of Manchester are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 3.6 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, United of Manchester and Leeds have played each other on three occasions. Leeds won all of them.

Their last meeting was on October 11, when Leeds won 4-1 at home.

United of Manchester's last match was on Sunday, a 3-0 loss against Stockport County Women.

Leeds lost 3-2 against Durham Cestria WFC last time out, on March 3.
