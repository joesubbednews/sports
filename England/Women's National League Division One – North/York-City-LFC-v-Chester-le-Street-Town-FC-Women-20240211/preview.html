York City LFC v Chester-le-Street Town FC Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 10th-ranked York City LFC host 11th-placed Chester-le-Street Town FC Women.

York City have picked up eight points from their first eight Women's National League Division One – North games, with two wins and two draws.

That is one point more than Chester-le-Street Town FC have collected, having won two and drawn one.

York City are in bad form in Women's National League Division One North, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

But with a win and five losses over that period, Chester-le-Street Town FC's form is even worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the home side's four.

With 16 goals in 12 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

York City are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

York City's last match was on February 4, a 3-2 loss against Middlesbrough Women.

Chester-le-Street Town FC lost 3-1 against Leeds Women last time out, also on February 4.
