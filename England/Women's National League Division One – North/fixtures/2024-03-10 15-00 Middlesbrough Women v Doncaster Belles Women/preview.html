Middlesbrough Women v Doncaster Belles Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Doncaster Belles Women face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Middlesbrough Women defence on Sunday.

With 29 goals in 15 games, Doncaster Belles are the joint-third-highest scorers in the Women's National League Division One North ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a Middlesbrough side who have scored 26 in 16 matches, but conceded only 13 goals, putting them joint-second among the league's tightest defences – only Durham Cestria WFC have conceded fewer goals.

Middlesbrough are third in the table after 16 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

Doncaster Belles are four places behind the hosts in seventh, with six wins and two draws putting them on 20 points.

Middlesbrough are in reasonable form in the Women's National League Division One North, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Middlesbrough's 11.

Middlesbrough's last match was on February 21, a 0-0 draw against Durham Cestria WFC.

Doncaster Belles beat Barnsley Women 1-0 last time out, on March 3.
