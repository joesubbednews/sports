Chester-le-Street Town FC Women v Chorley Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Chester-le-Street Town FC Women face Chorley Women on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Doncaster Belles Women on February 25, Chester-le-Street Town FC sit 10th in the Women's National League Division One North.

They travel to play a Chorley side ninth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 4-1 against Doncaster Belles Women, on January 28.

Chester-le-Street Town FC are in disappointing form in the Women's National League Division One North, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Chorley's form is worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

With 12 goals in 12 games so far this season, the away team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Chester-le-Street Town FC are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.4 goals per game.

In the last three years, Chester-le-Street Town FC and Chorley have played each other on three occasions. Chorley won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Chorley won 3-2 at home.
