Hull City LFC v Durham Cestria WFC

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One North's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when third-placed Hull City LFC host table-topping Durham Cestria WFC.

Hull City have picked up 11 wins and one draw from 14 games so far this season, and sit two points below the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Durham Cestria WFC, meanwhile, have won 11 and drawn three of 18, picking up 36 points.

With 43 goals in 14 games so far this season, Hull City are the league's highest scorers with 3.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Durham Cestria WFC are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Women's National League Division One North, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

But with five wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is even better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Hull City's 15.

In the last three years, Hull City and Durham Cestria WFC have played each other on three occasions. Hull City won one of them and Durham Cestria WFC the other.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when Hull City won 2-0 away.

Hull City's last match was on March 17, a 5-0 win against Chorley Women.

Durham Cestria WFC beat Norton and Stockton 4-0 last time out, on March 14.
