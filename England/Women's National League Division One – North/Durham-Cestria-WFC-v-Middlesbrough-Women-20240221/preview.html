Durham Cestria WFC v Middlesbrough Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Two of the Women's National League Division One North's top sides face each other in Wednesday's late kick-off, when fourth-placed Durham Cestria WFC host second-placed Middlesbrough Women.

Durham Cestria WFC have picked up eight wins and two draws from 14 games so far this season, and sit three points below the visitors going into the 7.45pm match.

Middlesbrough, meanwhile, have won eight and drawn five of 15, picking up 29 points.

With 26 goals in 14 games so far this season, Durham Cestria WFC are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 10 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Middlesbrough, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home team are in good form in the Women's National League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Durham Cestria WFC's 13.

In the last three years, Durham Cestria WFC and Middlesbrough have played each other on three occasions. Durham Cestria WFC won two of them and Middlesbrough one.

Their last meeting was on September 6, when Middlesbrough won 1-0 at home.

Durham Cestria WFC's last match was on February 11, a 4-1 win against United of Manchester Women.

Middlesbrough drew 3-3 with Barnsley Women last time out, also on February 11.
