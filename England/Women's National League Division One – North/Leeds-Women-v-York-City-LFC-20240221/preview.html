Leeds Women v York City LFC

Women's National League Division One – North
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

York City LFC come to play Leeds Women on late Wednesday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Leeds have three wins and three losses.

York City are 10th in the table after 12 games, of which they have won two and drawn two, earning eight points.

Leeds are four places ahead of the visitors in sixth, with seven wins and one draw putting them on 22 points.

With 25 goals in 12 games so far this season, the home team are scoring more than average in the league with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

York City, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Leeds and York City have played each other on three occasions. Leeds won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 6, when Leeds won 3-1 away.

Leeds's last match was on February 4, a 3-1 win against Chester-le-Street Town FC Women.

York City lost 3-2 against Middlesbrough Women last time out, also on February 4.
