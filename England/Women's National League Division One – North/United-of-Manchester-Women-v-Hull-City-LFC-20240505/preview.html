United of Manchester Women v Hull City LFC

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates United of Manchester Women face a challenge against high-flying Hull City LFC on Sunday.

United of Manchester Women are rooted to the bottom of the Women's National League Division One – North table, and have picked up just two wins and two draws in their 21 games to date.

Hull City, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 45 points, having won 14 and drawn three of their first 20 matches.

With 52 goals in 20 games so far this season, Hull City are the league's highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

United of Manchester, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 3.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Women's National League Division One North, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to United of Manchester's three.

Over the last two years, United of Manchester and Hull City have played each other twice. Hull City won both of them.

Their last meeting was on December 3, when Hull City won 6-0 at home.

United of Manchester's last match was on April 20, a 3-0 loss against Doncaster Belles Women.

Hull City beat Leeds Women 5-1 last time out, on Tuesday.
