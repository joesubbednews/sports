Stockport County Women v Middlesbrough Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Women's National League Division One North's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when Middlesbrough Women visit Stockport County Women.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Middlesbrough to date: the away side have let in just seven goals in 12 games.

Stockport County have conceded 12 goals in as many games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Stockport County are in reasonable form in Women's National League Division One North, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Middlesbrough's form is better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to the home side's 11.

The visitors are third in the table after 12 games, of which they have won seven and drawn four, earning 25 points.

Stockport County are three places behind Middlesbrough in sixth, with four wins and five draws putting them on 17 points.

In the last three years, Stockport County and Middlesbrough have played each other on three occasions. Stockport County won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Stockport County's last match was on January 14, a 2-2 draw against Doncaster Belles Women.

Middlesbrough beat Leeds Women 3-1 last time out, also on January 14.
