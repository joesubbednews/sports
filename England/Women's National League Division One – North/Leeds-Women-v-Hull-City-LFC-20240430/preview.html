Leeds Women v Hull City LFC

Women's National League Division One – North
Tuesday, 30 April 2024 @ 7.45pm

Tuesday's late match between Leeds Women and Hull City LFC promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Women's National League Division One North's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Leeds and Hull City sit fourth and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 41 and 47 goals respectively.

Hull City are second in the table after 19 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn three, earning 42 points.

Leeds are four places behind the visitors in sixth, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

The home side are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One North, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Hull City's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Leeds's seven.

In the last three years, Leeds and Hull City have played each other on three occasions. Leeds won two of them and Hull City one.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Hull City won 4-3 at home.

Leeds's last match was on April 21, a 3-3 draw against Stockport County Women.

Hull City drew 1-1 with York City LFC last time out, also on April 21.
