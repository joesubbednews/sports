Middlesbrough Women v Barnsley Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Barnsley Women travel for Sunday's early match against Middlesbrough Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Women's National League Division One North.

Barnsley, who sit second in the league after 12 games, fell to a 3-2 away defeat to Durham Cestria WFC on February 4.

They face a Middlesbrough side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-2 victory against York City LFC, and who sit third in the table.

With 30 goals in 12 games so far this season, Barnsley are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 12 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Middlesbrough, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in Women's National League Division One North, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away team's form is slightly worse – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Middlesbrough's 13.

In the last three years, Middlesbrough and Barnsley have played each other on three occasions. Barnsley won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when they played out a 0-0 draw.
