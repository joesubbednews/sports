York City LFC v Barnsley Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates York City LFC face a challenge against high-flying Barnsley Women on Sunday.

York City LFC are 11th in the Women's National League Division One – North table, and have picked up two wins and three draws in their 15 games to date.

Barnsley, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 37 points, having won 11 and drawn four of their first 19 matches, and are five points behind table-toppers Middlesbrough Women.

With 42 goals in 19 games so far this season, Barnsley are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

York City, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The home side are in terrible form in the Women's National League Division One North, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to York City's two.

In the last three years, York City and Barnsley have played each other on three occasions. Barnsley won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when Barnsley won 2-1 at home.

York City's last match was on March 31, a 1-0 loss against Norton and Stockton.

Barnsley drew 1-1 with Leeds Women last time out, also on March 31.
