Chester-le-Street Town FC Women v Leeds Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Chester-le-Street Town FC Women face Leeds Women on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over United of Manchester Women on January 28, Chester-le-Street Town FC sit 11th in Women's National League Division One North.

They travel to play a Leeds side sixth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-1 against Middlesbrough Women, on January 14.

With 22 goals in 11 games so far this season, Leeds are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Chester-le-Street Town FC, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

The home team are in bad form in Women's National League Division One North, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Chester-le-Street Town FC's three.
