Leeds Women v Barnsley Women

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Leeds Women host Barnsley Women on Sunday in the Women's National League Division One North.

In their last league match, on March 24, Leeds drew with Norton and Stockton 0-0 at home.

Barnsley won, 3-1 at home against United of Manchester Women.

With 41 goals in 18 games so far this season, Barnsley are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Leeds are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The away team are fourth in the table after 18 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn three, earning 36 points.

The hosts are two places behind Barnsley in sixth, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

In the last five years, Leeds and Barnsley have played each other on five occasions. Leeds won three of them and Barnsley two.

On average, Leeds scored 2.2 goals and Barnsley 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Barnsley won 3-0 at home.

Leeds are in mixed form in the Women's National League Division One North, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Barnsley's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Leeds's 10.
