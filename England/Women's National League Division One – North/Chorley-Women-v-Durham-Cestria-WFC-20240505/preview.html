Chorley Women v Durham Cestria WFC

Women's National League Division One – North
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Chorley Women host Durham Cestria WFC in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's National League Division One North.

Chorley, who sit ninth in the league after 17 games, fell to a 1-2 home defeat to Barnsley Women on April 21.

They face a Durham Cestria WFC side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-0 victory against Chester-le-Street Town FC Women, and who sit second in the table.

With 15 goals in 17 games so far this season, Chorley are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Durham Cestria WFC, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The home side are in bad form in the Women's National League Division One North, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Chorley's four.

In the last five years, Chorley and Durham Cestria WFC have played each other on five occasions. Chorley won one of them and Durham Cestria WFC the other.

On average, Chorley scored 0.8 goals and Durham Cestria WFC 4.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 21, when Durham Cestria WFC won 2-0 at home.
