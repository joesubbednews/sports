Sholing v Swindon Supermarine

Southern League Premier – South | VT Group Sports Ground
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sholing host Swindon Supermarine on Saturday at the VT Group Sports Ground in the Southern League Premier South.

In their last league match, on April 13, Sholing lost to Walton and Hersham 1-0 away.

Swindon Supermarine won, 3-0 at home against Basingstoke Town.

With 44 goals in 36 games so far this season, Sholing are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Swindon Supermarine, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Southern League Premier South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Sholing's five.

Sholing are 16th in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn nine, earning 45 points.

Swindon Supermarine are two places ahead of the hosts in 14th, with 12 wins and 10 draws putting them on 46 points.
