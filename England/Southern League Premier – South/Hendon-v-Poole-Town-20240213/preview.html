Hendon v Poole Town

Southern League Premier – South | Earlsmead Stadium
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Poole Town make the journey to the Earlsmead Stadium to play Hendon on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Poole's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Southern League Premier – South matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

With 56 goals in 26 games so far this season, Poole are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Hendon are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Hendon and Poole have played each other on eight occasions. Hendon won three of them, Poole four, and they drew once.

On average, Hendon scored 1.5 goals and Poole 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

The home side are 10th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn nine, earning 39 points.

Town are two places behind Hendon in 12th, with 12 wins and two draws putting them on 38 points.

Hendon's last match was on February 3, a 3-2 loss against Tiverton Town.

Poole lost 2-0 against Salisbury last time out, on Saturday.
