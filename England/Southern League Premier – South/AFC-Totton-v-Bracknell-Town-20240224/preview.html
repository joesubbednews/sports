AFC Totton v Bracknell Town

Southern League Premier – South | Testwood Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

AFC Totton face Bracknell Town on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Southern League Premier South.

Totton are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Bracknell team who have won three and lost three over the same number of games.

With 56 goals in 28 games so far this season, Totton are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Bracknell are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The hosts are fifth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn 10, earning 49 points.

Town are three places behind Totton in eighth, with 14 wins and three draws putting them on 45 points.

Totton's last match was on February 17, a 1-0 win against Hayes and Yeading United.

Bracknell lost 3-1 against Dorchester Town last time out, also on February 17.
