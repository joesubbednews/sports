Poole Town v Didcot Town

Southern League Premier – South
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Didcot Town face Poole Town away on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-2 victory over Plymouth Parkway on April 13, Didcot sit bottom of the Southern League Premier South.

They travel to play a Poole side 15th in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-1 against Beaconsfield Town.

Poole are in bad form in the Southern League Premier South, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

And also with a win and a draw over that period, Didcot's form is identical – they have both taken four points from 18.

With 43 goals in 39 games so far this season, Didcot Town are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 88 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Poole Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.
