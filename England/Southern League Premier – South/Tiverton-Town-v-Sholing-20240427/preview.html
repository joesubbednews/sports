Tiverton Town v Sholing

Southern League Premier – South | Ladysmead
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sholing travel to Tiverton Town on Saturday in the Southern League Premier South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Swindon Supermarine 3-0 at home.

Tiverton also won their last match, 2-1 against Harrow Borough.

Tiverton are 18th in the table after 38 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn 10, earning 43 points.

Sholing are four places ahead of Town in 14th, with 13 wins and nine draws putting them on 48 points.

With 47 goals in 37 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Tiverton are in reasonable form in the Southern League Premier South, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Sholing's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Tiverton's 12.
