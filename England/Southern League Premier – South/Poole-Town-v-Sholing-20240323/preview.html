Poole Town v Sholing

Southern League Premier – South
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sholing make the journey to play Poole Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Sholing's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Southern League Premier – South matches, while their opponents have earned three from a possible 18.

With 37 goals in 31 games so far this season, Sholing are the league's lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Poole, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The away team are 14th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

Town are two places ahead of Sholing in 12th, with 13 wins and two draws putting them on 41 points.

Over the last two years, Poole and Sholing have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Sholing won 2-0 at home.

Poole's last match was on March 16, a 4-0 loss against Winchester City.

Sholing lost 3-1 against Harrow Borough last time out, on March 9.
