Sholing v Plymouth Parkway

Southern League Premier – South | VT Group Sports Ground
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Plymouth Parkway make the journey to the VT Group Sports Ground to play Sholing on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Plymouth Parkway's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Southern League Premier – South matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

Plymouth Parkway are 20th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Sholing are eight places ahead of the away team in 12th, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

In the last three years, Sholing and Plymouth Parkway have played each other on three occasions. Sholing won one of them and Plymouth Parkway the other.

Their last meeting was on August 15, when Sholing won 3-1 away.

With 40 goals in 32 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Plymouth Parkway are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Sholing's last match was on March 23, a 3-0 win against Poole Town.

Plymouth Parkway lost 2-0 against Bracknell Town last time out, also on March 23.
