Plymouth Parkway v Beaconsfield Town

Southern League Premier – South
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Plymouth Parkway face Beaconsfield Town on Tuesday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Southern League Premier South.

Plymouth Parkway are unbeaten in nine, with six wins and three draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Beaconsfield team who have won three and drawn four over the same number of games.

Plymouth Parkway, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are 15th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

The home side are one place ahead of the visitors in 14th, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on the same number of points.

Over the last two years, Plymouth Parkway and Beaconsfield have played each other twice. Beaconsfield won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on January 31, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Plymouth Parkway's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Dorchester Town.
