Didcot Town v Walton and Hersham

Southern League Premier – South | NPower Loop Meadow Stadium
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Walton and Hersham are strong favourites to take all three points despite Didcot Town's home advantage in Tuesday's late match at the NPower Loop Meadow Stadium.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.53 on Walton & Hersham sealing the win, with the visitors sitting seventh in the Southern League Premier South table.

Didcot, who are 22nd in the league and 23 points behind Walton & Hersham, are priced at 4.33 to win. A draw is set at 4.33.

With 33 goals in 30 games so far this season, Didcot are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 68 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Walton & Hersham, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

Didcot's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 loss against Beaconsfield Town.

Walton & Hersham beat Tiverton Town 2-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
