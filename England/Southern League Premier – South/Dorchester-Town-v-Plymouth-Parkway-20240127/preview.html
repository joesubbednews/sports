Dorchester Town v Plymouth Parkway

Southern League Premier – South | Jewson Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Plymouth Parkway face Dorchester Town on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Southern League Premier South.

Their last meeting was on September 26, when Plymouth Parkway won 5-0 at home.

Dorchester's last match was on January 20, a 3-2 win against Merthyr Town.

Plymouth Parkway beat Hayes and Yeading United 2-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
