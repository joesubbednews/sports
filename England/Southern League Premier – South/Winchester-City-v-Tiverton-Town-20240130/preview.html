Winchester City v Tiverton Town

Southern League Premier – South | The Denplan City Ground
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Tiverton Town travel to Winchester City on late Tuesday in Southern League Premier South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Poole Town 4-2 at home.

Winchester also won their last match, 2-0 against Basingstoke Town.

Winchester are in disappointing form in Southern League Premier South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Tiverton's form is slightly better – they have taken six points from 18, compared to City's five.

Town are 17th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won six and drawn nine, earning 27 points.

The home team are five places ahead of the visitors in 12th, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

With 42 goals in 26 games so far this season, Tiverton are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Winchester, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Winchester and Tiverton have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on April 22, when Winchester won 4-2 away.
