Hanwell Town v Didcot Town

Southern League Premier – South | Reynolds Field
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Didcot Town come to Reynolds Field to play Hanwell Town on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Hanwell have a win and two draws.

Didcot are bottom of the table after 35 games, of which they have won five and drawn nine, earning 24 points.

Hanwell are four places ahead of Didcot Town in 18th, with nine wins and 11 draws putting them on 38 points.

With 38 goals in 35 games so far this season, the away team are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 80 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Hanwell Town are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Hanwell's last match was on March 23, a 2-0 loss against Tiverton Town.

Didcot lost 4-0 against Gosport Borough last time out, also on March 23.
