Hungerford Town v AFC Totton

Southern League Premier – South | Bulpit Lane
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hungerford Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight AFC Totton defence on Saturday.

With 72 goals in 38 games, Hungerford are the fourth-highest scorers in the Southern League Premier South ahead of the 3pm kick-off at Bulpit Lane.

They face a Totton side who have scored 70 in 35 matches, but conceded only 38 goals, putting them joint-top of the league's tightest defences.

Totton are fourth in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn 11, earning 65 points.

Hungerford are four places behind the away team in eighth, with 15 wins and 10 draws putting them on 55 points.

Town are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Totton's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.

Hungerford's last match was on April 6, a 1-0 loss against Winchester City.

Totton beat Tiverton Town 5-0 last time out, also on April 6.
