AFC Totton v Harrow Borough

Southern League Premier – South | Testwood Stadium
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Harrow Borough make the journey to the Testwood Stadium to play AFC Totton on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Harrow's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Southern League Premier – South matches, while their opponents have earned nine from a possible 18.

Harrow are bottom of the table after 25 games, of which they have won three and drawn five, earning 14 points.

Totton are 16 places ahead of Borough in sixth, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

With 30 goals in 25 games so far this season, the away team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Totton's last match was on January 13, a 1-0 win against Salisbury.

Harrow lost 2-0 against Dorchester Town last time out, also on January 13.
