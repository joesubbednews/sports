Tiverton Town v Poole Town

Southern League Premier – South | Ladysmead
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Poole Town visit Tiverton Town at Ladysmead on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Southern League Premier South.

Poole have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Tiverton side who lost their last match, and have collected four points from the last possible 18.

With 50 goals in 21 games so far this season, Poole are scoring more than average in the league with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Tiverton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Tiverton and Poole have played each other on seven occasions. Tiverton won three of them and Poole four.

On average, Tiverton scored 1.1 goals and Poole 1.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 17, when Poole won 2-0 at home.

Tiverton Town are 19th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won five and drawn nine, earning 24 points.

Poole Town are nine places ahead of the home team in 10th, with 12 wins and two draws putting them on 38 points.

Tiverton's last match was on January 20, a 3-2 loss against Basingstoke Town.

Poole beat Swindon Supermarine 1-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
