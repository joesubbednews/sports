Tiverton Town v Poole Town

Southern League Premier – South | Ladysmead
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Tiverton Town face Poole Town at Ladysmead on Saturday looking to secure a first win in seven Southern League Premier – South games.

Tiverton have lost two and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Bracknell Town on December 9.

They face a Poole side who have won four and drawn one over that time.

With 49 goals in 20 games so far this season, Poole are scoring more than average in the league with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Tiverton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Tiverton and Poole have played each other on seven occasions. Tiverton won three of them and Poole four.

On average, Tiverton scored 1.1 goals and Poole 1.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 17, when Poole won 2-0 at home.

Tiverton Town are 19th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won five and drawn nine, earning 24 points.

Poole Town are eight places ahead of the home team in 11th, with 11 wins and two draws putting them on 35 points.

Tiverton's last match was on January 20, a 3-2 loss against Basingstoke Town.

Poole beat Dorchester Town 2-1 last time out, on December 26.
