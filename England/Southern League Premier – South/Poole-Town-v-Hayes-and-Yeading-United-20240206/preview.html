Poole Town v Hayes and Yeading United

Southern League Premier – South
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Poole Town welcome Hayes and Yeading United on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Poole's struggles have left them with nine points from their last six Southern League Premier – South matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

With 56 goals in 24 games so far this season, Poole are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Hayes & Yeading, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Poole and Hayes & Yeading have played each other on nine occasions. Poole won three of them, Hayes & Yeading four, and they drew twice.

On average, Poole scored 1.8 goals and Hayes & Yeading 2.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Hayes & Yeading won 5-3 at home.

United are 17th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won seven and drawn nine, earning 30 points.

Town are six places ahead of the visitors in 11th, with 12 wins and two draws putting them on 38 points.

Poole's last match was on Saturday, a 3-0 loss against Hanwell Town.

Hayes & Yeading beat Hungerford Town 3-2 last time out, also on Saturday.
