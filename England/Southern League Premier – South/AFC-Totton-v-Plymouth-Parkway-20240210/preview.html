AFC Totton v Plymouth Parkway

Southern League Premier – South | Testwood Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

AFC Totton face Plymouth Parkway on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Southern League Premier South.

Totton are unbeaten in five, with two wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Plymouth Parkway team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 55 goals in 27 games so far this season, Totton are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Plymouth Parkway, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are fifth in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn 10, earning 46 points.

The away team are 12 places behind Totton in 17th, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.

In the last three years, Totton and Plymouth Parkway have played each other on three occasions. Totton won two of them and Plymouth Parkway one.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Totton won 2-1 at home.

Totton's last match was on February 3, a 2-2 draw against Swindon Supermarine.

Plymouth Parkway lost 1-0 against Beaconsfield Town last time out, on January 30.
