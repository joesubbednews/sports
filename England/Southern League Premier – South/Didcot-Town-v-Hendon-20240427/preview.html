Didcot Town v Hendon

Southern League Premier – South | NPower Loop Meadow Stadium
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hendon are strong favourites to take all three points despite Didcot Town's home advantage in Saturday's match at the NPower Loop Meadow Stadium.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.62 on Hendon sealing the win, with the visitors sitting ninth in the Southern League Premier South table.

Didcot, who are 22nd in the league and 26 points behind Hendon, are priced at 4.5 to win. A draw is set at 3.6.

Didcot are in bad form in the Southern League Premier South, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, Hendon's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Town's four.

With 43 goals in 40 games so far this season, the home side are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 90 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Didcot's last match was on April 20, a 2-0 loss against Poole Town.

Hendon drew 0-0 with Gosport Borough last time out, also on April 20.
