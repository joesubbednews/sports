Merthyr Town v Hungerford Town

Southern League Premier – South
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Monday's match between Merthyr Town and Hungerford Town promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Southern League Premier South's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Merthyr and Hungerford sit fourth and second in the goal-scoring charts, with 68 and 72 goals respectively.

Merthyr are in bad form in the Southern League Premier South, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Hungerford's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Merthyr Town's three.

Hungerford Town are sixth in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn 10, earning 55 points.

The hosts are one place behind the away side in seventh, with 16 wins and six draws putting them on 54 points.

Merthyr's last match was on Saturday, a 2-0 loss against Tiverton Town.

Hungerford beat Bracknell Town 3-1 last time out, on Friday.
