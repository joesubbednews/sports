Hanwell Town v Hungerford Town

Southern League Premier – South | Reynolds Field
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hanwell Town are on a poor run ahead of hosting Hungerford Town at Reynolds Field on Saturday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Hanwell have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Southern League Premier – South games, and face a Hungerford side whose last six games have brought three wins and three losses.

With 73 goals in 39 games so far this season, Hungerford are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 69 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Hanwell, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Hanwell Town are 18th in the table after 38 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn 12, earning 42 points.

Hungerford Town are 10 places ahead of the hosts in eighth, with 16 wins and 10 draws putting them on 58 points.

Hanwell's last match was on April 13, a 2-1 loss against Dorchester Town.

Hungerford beat AFC Totton 1-0 last time out, also on April 13.
