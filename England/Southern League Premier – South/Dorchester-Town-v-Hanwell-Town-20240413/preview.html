Dorchester Town v Hanwell Town

Southern League Premier – South | Jewson Stadium
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Dorchester Town face Hanwell Town on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Southern League Premier South.

Dorchester are unbeaten in seven, with five wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Hanwell team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

Hanwell are 17th in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn 12, earning 42 points.

Dorchester are five places ahead of Hanwell Town in 12th, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on 47 points.

With 56 goals in 37 games so far this season, the away side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Dorchester Town are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Dorchester and Hanwell have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on February 6, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Dorchester's last match was on April 6, a 1-0 win against Hayes and Yeading United.

Hanwell drew 0-0 with Gosport Borough last time out, also on April 6.
