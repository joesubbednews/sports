Didcot Town v Dorchester Town

Southern League Premier – South | NPower Loop Meadow Stadium
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match at the NPower Loop Meadow Stadium sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 21st-ranked Didcot Town host 19th-placed Dorchester Town.

Didcot have picked up 18 points from their first 18 Southern League Premier – South games, with four wins and six draws.

That is eight points less than Dorchester have collected, having won seven and drawn five.

Didcot are in mixed form in Southern League Premier South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, Dorchester's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.

With 28 goals in 26 games so far this season, Didcot Town are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Dorchester Town are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Didcot's last match was on January 27, a 4-0 loss against Merthyr Town.

Dorchester drew 0-0 with Plymouth Parkway last time out, also on January 27.
