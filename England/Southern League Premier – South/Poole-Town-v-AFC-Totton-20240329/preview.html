Poole Town v AFC Totton

Southern League Premier – South
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Poole Town welcome AFC Totton on Friday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Poole's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Southern League Premier – South matches, while their opponents have earned 13 from a possible 18.

With 62 goals in 32 games so far this season, Totton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Poole are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn 10, earning 58 points.

Town are nine places behind Totton in 13th, with 13 wins and two draws putting them on 41 points.

Poole's last match was on Saturday, a 3-0 loss against Sholing.

Totton beat Beaconsfield Town 3-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
