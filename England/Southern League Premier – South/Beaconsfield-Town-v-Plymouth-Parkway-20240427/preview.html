Beaconsfield Town v Plymouth Parkway

Southern League Premier – South | Holloways Park
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Beaconsfield Town welcome Plymouth Parkway to Holloways Park on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Beaconsfield's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Southern League Premier – South matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

Plymouth Parkway are 20th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn nine, earning 39 points.

Beaconsfield are five places ahead of the visitors in 15th, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on 47 points.

With 62 goals in 40 games so far this season, Town are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 75 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Plymouth Parkway are also average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last three years, Beaconsfield and Plymouth Parkway have played each other on three occasions. Beaconsfield won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on January 30, when Beaconsfield won 1-0 away.

Beaconsfield's last match was on April 20, a 1-0 loss against Winchester City.

Plymouth Parkway drew 2-2 with Walton and Hersham last time out, also on April 20.
