Plymouth Parkway v Hungerford Town

Southern League Premier – South
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hungerford Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Plymouth Parkway defence on Saturday.

With 51 goals in 26 games, Hungerford are the second-highest scorers in Southern League Premier South ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Plymouth Parkway side who have scored 31 in 19 matches, but conceded only 30 goals, putting them joint-fourth among the league's tightest defences – only Salisbury, Gosport Borough and AFC Totton have conceded fewer goals.

Hungerford are fifth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn nine, earning 42 points.

Plymouth Parkway are 12 places behind Town in 17th, with six wins and six draws putting them on 24 points.

The home side are in good form in Southern League Premier South, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Plymouth Parkway's 14.

Plymouth Parkway's last match was on January 13, a 1-1 draw against Gosport Borough.

Hungerford lost 1-0 against Chesham United last time out, also on January 13.
