Winchester City v Didcot Town

Southern League Premier – South | The Denplan City Ground
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Didcot Town face Winchester City away at the Denplan City Ground on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Bracknell Town on January 13, Didcot sit 21st in Southern League Premier South.

They travel to play a Winchester side 13th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-1 against Walton and Hersham, on Saturday.

Winchester are in bad form in Southern League Premier South, with no wins and four draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Didcot's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to City's four.

With 26 goals in 24 games so far this season, Town are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.
