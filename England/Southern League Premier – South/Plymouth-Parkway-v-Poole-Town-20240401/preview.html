Plymouth Parkway v Poole Town

Southern League Premier – South
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Plymouth Parkway and Poole Town both come into Monday's kick-off in poor form, having picked up two and four points respectively in their last six games.

Plymouth Parkway have drawn two and lost four of the last six, while Poole have taken one win and one draw.

Plymouth Parkway are 20th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won eight and drawn eight, earning 32 points.

Poole are six places ahead of the hosts in 14th, with 13 wins and three draws putting them on 42 points.

With 40 goals in 27 games so far this season, Plymouth Parkway are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Plymouth Parkway and Poole have played each other twice. Plymouth Parkway won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on February 28 2023, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Plymouth Parkway's last match was on Saturday, a 1-1 draw against Sholing.

Poole drew 0-0 with AFC Totton last time out, on Friday.
