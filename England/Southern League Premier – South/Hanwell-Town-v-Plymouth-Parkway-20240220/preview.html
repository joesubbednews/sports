Hanwell Town v Plymouth Parkway

Southern League Premier – South
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Hanwell Town host Plymouth Parkway on Tuesday in the Southern League Premier South.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Hanwell drew with Winchester City 1-1 away.

Plymouth Parkway lost, 1-0 at home against Beaconsfield Town on January 30.

Plymouth Parkway are 18th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Hanwell are two places ahead of the away side in 16th, with eight wins and 10 draws putting them on 34 points.

With 49 goals in 29 games so far this season, Town are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Plymouth Parkway are also average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last three years, Hanwell and Plymouth Parkway have played each other on three occasions. Hanwell won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Hanwell won 2-1 away.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and three draws over that period, Plymouth Parkway's form is identical – they have both taken nine points from 18.
