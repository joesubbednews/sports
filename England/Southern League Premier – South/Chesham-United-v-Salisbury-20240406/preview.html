Chesham United v Salisbury

Southern League Premier – South | The Meadow
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Southern League Premier South's top sides face each other at the Meadow in Saturday's kick-off, when table-topping Chesham United host third-placed Salisbury.

Chesham have picked up 27 wins and six draws from 38 games so far this season, and sit 17 points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Salisbury, meanwhile, have won 20 and drawn 10 of 37, picking up 70 points.

With 77 goals in 38 games so far this season, Chesham are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Salisbury, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Chesham and Salisbury have played each other on seven occasions. Chesham won four of them, Salisbury two, and they drew once.

On average, Chesham scored 2.0 goals and Salisbury 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 9, when Salisbury won 3-1 at home.

United are in good form in the Southern League Premier South, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.

Chesham's last match was on Monday, a 4-1 win against Harrow Borough.

Salisbury beat Basingstoke Town 2-1 last time out, also on Monday.
