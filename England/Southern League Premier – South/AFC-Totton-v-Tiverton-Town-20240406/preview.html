AFC Totton v Tiverton Town

Southern League Premier – South | Testwood Stadium
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Tiverton Town face a challenge away against high-flying AFC Totton at the Testwood Stadium on Saturday.

Tiverton Town are 19th in the Southern League Premier – South table, and have picked up nine wins and 10 draws in their 35 games to date.

Totton, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 62 points, having won 17 and drawn 11 of their first 34 matches, and are 25 points behind table-toppers Chesham United.

With 65 goals in 34 games so far this season, Totton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Tiverton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Southern League Premier South, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Totton's 13.

Totton's last match was on Monday, a 3-0 win against Walton and Hersham.

Tiverton lost 2-1 against Dorchester Town last time out, also on Monday.
