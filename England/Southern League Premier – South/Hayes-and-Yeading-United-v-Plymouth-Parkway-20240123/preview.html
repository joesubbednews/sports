Hayes and Yeading United v Plymouth Parkway

Southern League Premier – South | The SKYex Community Stadium
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Plymouth Parkway face Hayes and Yeading United on Tuesday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Southern League Premier South.

Plymouth Parkway are unbeaten in seven, with five wins and two draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Hayes & Yeading team who have drawn four and lost three over the same number of games.

Hayes & Yeading are 20th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

Plymouth Parkway are four places ahead of United in 16th, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.

With 36 goals in 23 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Hayes & Yeading and Plymouth Parkway have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on April 15, when Hayes & Yeading won 2-1 at home.

Hayes & Yeading's last match was on January 13, a 1-1 draw against Tiverton Town.

Plymouth Parkway beat Hungerford Town 6-2 last time out, on Saturday.
