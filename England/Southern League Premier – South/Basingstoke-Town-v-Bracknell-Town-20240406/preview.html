Basingstoke Town v Bracknell Town

Southern League Premier – South
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Basingstoke Town host Bracknell Town on Saturday in the Southern League Premier South.

In their last league match, on Monday, Basingstoke lost to Salisbury 2-1 away.

Bracknell won, 5-1 at home against Sholing.

With 72 goals in 37 games so far this season, Bracknell are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Basingstoke are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Bracknell Town are fifth in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn four, earning 58 points.

Basingstoke Town are five places behind the visitors in 10th, with 14 wins and eight draws putting them on 50 points.

The home team are in good form in the Southern League Premier South, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with four wins and a draw over that period, Bracknell's form is identical – they have both taken 13 points from 18.

In the last three years, Basingstoke and Bracknell have played each other on three occasions. Bracknell won all of them.

Their last meeting was on October 17, when Bracknell won 4-0 at home.
