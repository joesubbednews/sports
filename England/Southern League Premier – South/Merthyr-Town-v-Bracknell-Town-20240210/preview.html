Merthyr Town v Bracknell Town

Southern League Premier – South
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Merthyr Town host Bracknell Town in Saturday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Southern League Premier South.

Merthyr, who sit fourth in the league after 29 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to Chesham United on Tuesday.

They face a Bracknell side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against Salisbury, and who sit seventh in the table.

With 62 goals in 29 games so far this season, Merthyr are the league's highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Bracknell are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Merthyr Town are in disappointing form in the Southern League Premier South, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Bracknell Town's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

In the last five years, Merthyr and Bracknell have played each other on four occasions. Bracknell won all of them.

On average, Merthyr scored 0.8 goals and Bracknell 3.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 14, when Bracknell won 1-0 at home.
