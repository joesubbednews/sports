Sholing v Hendon

Southern League Premier – South | VT Group Sports Ground
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hendon travel to Sholing on Saturday in Southern League Premier South.

With 35 goals in 26 games so far this season, Sholing are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Hendon, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are seventh in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn six, earning 39 points.

The away side are one place behind Sholing in eighth, with 10 wins and seven draws putting them on 37 points.

Sholing are in mixed form in Southern League Premier South, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Hendon's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home team's eight.
