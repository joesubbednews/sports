Tiverton Town v AFC Totton

Southern League Premier – South | Ladysmead
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Tiverton Town face a challenge against high-flying AFC Totton at Ladysmead on Saturday.

Tiverton Town are 20th in the Southern League Premier – South table, and have picked up six wins and 10 draws in their 30 games to date.

Totton, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 52 points, having won 14 and drawn 10 of their first 29 matches, and are 25 points behind table-toppers Chesham United.

With 57 goals in 29 games so far this season, Totton are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Tiverton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Town are in bad form in the Southern League Premier South, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

Tiverton's last match was on March 2, a 2-2 draw against Swindon Supermarine.

Totton beat Didcot Town 1-0 last time out, also on March 2.
