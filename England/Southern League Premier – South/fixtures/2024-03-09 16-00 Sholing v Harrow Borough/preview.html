Sholing v Harrow Borough

Southern League Premier – South | VT Group Sports Ground
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Harrow Borough travel to Sholing on Saturday in the Southern League Premier South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Bracknell Town 1-1 away.

Sholing, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Hungerford Town.

Harrow are bottom of the table after 32 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

Sholing are eight places ahead of Borough in 14th, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

The home team are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Sholing's seven.

With 36 goals in 30 games so far this season, Sholing are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Harrow are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.
