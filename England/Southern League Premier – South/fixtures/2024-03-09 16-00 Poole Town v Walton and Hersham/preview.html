Poole Town v Walton and Hersham

Southern League Premier – South
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Poole Town are on a poor run ahead of hosting Walton and Hersham on Saturday, with just three points collected from their last six games.

Poole have picked up just one win in their last six Southern League Premier – South games, and face a Walton & Hersham side whose last six games have brought two wins and one draw.

With 59 goals in 29 games so far this season, Poole are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Walton & Hersham are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The away team are seventh in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn seven, earning 46 points.

Town are five places behind Walton & Hersham in 12th, with 13 wins and two draws putting them on 41 points.

Poole's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-1 win against Gosport Borough.

Walton & Hersham lost 2-1 against Hanwell Town last time out, on March 2.
