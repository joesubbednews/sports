Bracknell Town v Merthyr Town

Southern League Premier – South
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Bracknell Town and Merthyr Town promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Southern League Premier South's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Bracknell and Merthyr sit joint-fourth and second in the goal-scoring charts, with 59 and 65 goals respectively.

Bracknell are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Merthyr's form is worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Bracknell Town's seven.

Merthyr Town are fifth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn four, earning 52 points.

The hosts are three places behind the away team in eighth, with 14 wins and four draws putting them on 46 points.

In the last five years, Bracknell and Merthyr have played each other on five occasions. Bracknell won all of them.

On average, Bracknell scored 3.2 goals and Merthyr 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 10, when Bracknell won 3-2 away.

Bracknell's last match was on March 2, a 1-1 draw against Harrow Borough.

Merthyr drew 1-1 with Hayes and Yeading United last time out, on February 24.
