Tiverton Town v Chesham United

Southern League Premier – South | Ladysmead
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Tiverton Town face a challenge against high-flying Chesham United at Ladysmead on Saturday.

Tiverton Town are 20th in the Southern League Premier – South table, and have picked up nine wins and 10 draws in their 36 games to date.

Chesham, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 90 points, having won 28 and drawn six of their first 39 matches.

With 79 goals in 39 games so far this season, Chesham are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Tiverton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Tiverton and Chesham have played each other on nine occasions. Tiverton won one of them, Chesham five, and they drew three times.

On average, Tiverton scored 0.7 goals and Chesham 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 5, when Chesham won 2-0 at home.

Town are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, United's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home side's nine.

Tiverton's last match was on April 6, a 5-0 loss against AFC Totton.

Chesham beat Salisbury 2-1 last time out, also on April 6.
