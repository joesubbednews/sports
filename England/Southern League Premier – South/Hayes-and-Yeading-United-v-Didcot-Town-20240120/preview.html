Hayes and Yeading United v Didcot Town

Southern League Premier – South | The SKYex Community Stadium
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Didcot Town face Hayes and Yeading United away at the SKYex Community Stadium on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Bracknell Town on January 13, Didcot sit 21st in Southern League Premier South.

They travel to play a Hayes & Yeading side 19th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 1-1 against Tiverton Town.

Hayes & Yeading are in bad form in Southern League Premier South, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Didcot's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to United's three.

With 26 goals in 24 games so far this season, Town are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.
