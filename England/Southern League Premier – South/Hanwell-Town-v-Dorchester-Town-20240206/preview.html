Hanwell Town v Dorchester Town

Southern League Premier – South
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Dorchester Town face Hanwell Town away on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Didcot Town on Saturday, Dorchester sit 19th in Southern League Premier South.

They travel to play a Hanwell side 15th in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-0 against Poole Town.

With 38 goals in 26 games so far this season, Dorchester are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Hanwell, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Hanwell Town are in good form in Southern League Premier South, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Dorchester Town's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.

Over the last two years, Hanwell and Dorchester have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on February 11, when Dorchester won 3-0 at home.
