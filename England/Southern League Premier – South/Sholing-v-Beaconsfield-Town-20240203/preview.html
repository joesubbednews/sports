Sholing v Beaconsfield Town

Southern League Premier – South | VT Group Sports Ground
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Beaconsfield Town face Sholing on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Southern League Premier South.

Beaconsfield are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Sholing team who have won two and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 35 goals in 27 games so far this season, Sholing are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Beaconsfield are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The hosts are 10th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn six, earning 39 points.

Town are three places behind Sholing in 13th, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

Sholing's last match was on January 27, a 3-0 loss against Salisbury.

Beaconsfield beat Plymouth Parkway 1-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
