Hendon v Hungerford Town

Southern League Premier – South | Silver Jubilee Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hendon host Hungerford Town on Saturday at Silver Jubilee Park in the Southern League Premier South.

In their last league match, on February 3, Hendon beat Tiverton Town 3-2 at home.

Hungerford lost, 1-0 at home against Walton and Hersham on February 10.

With 58 goals in 30 games so far this season, Hungerford are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Hendon are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are seventh in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn nine, earning 45 points.

The hosts are one place behind the away team in eighth, with 11 wins and nine draws putting them on 42 points.

Hendon are in reasonable form in the Southern League Premier South, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Hungerford's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Hendon's 11.
