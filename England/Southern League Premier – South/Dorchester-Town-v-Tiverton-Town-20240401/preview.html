Dorchester Town v Tiverton Town

Southern League Premier – South | Jewson Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Tiverton Town face Dorchester Town away at the Jewson Stadium on Monday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Merthyr Town on Saturday, Tiverton sit 19th in the Southern League Premier South.

They travel to play a Dorchester side 16th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 1-1 against Winchester City.

In the last 10 years, Dorchester and Tiverton have played each other on 10 occasions. Dorchester won five of them, Tiverton three, and they drew twice.

On average, Dorchester scored 1.8 goals and Tiverton 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 4 2023, when Tiverton won 3-0 at home.

With 50 goals in 33 games so far this season, Dorchester are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Tiverton are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Dorchester Town are in reasonable form in the Southern League Premier South, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Tiverton Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 11.
