Winchester City v Swindon Supermarine

Southern League Premier – South | The Denplan City Ground
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Winchester City host Swindon Supermarine on Tuesday at the Denplan City Ground in Southern League Premier South.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Winchester lost to Chesham United 3-0 at home.

Swindon Supermarine drew, 2-2 at home against AFC Totton.

Swindon Supermarine are 20th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won seven and drawn six, earning 27 points.

Winchester are eight places ahead of the visitors in 12th, with 10 wins and eight draws putting them on 38 points.

With 44 goals in 27 games so far this season, City are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Swindon Supermarine, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.4 goals per game.

The home team are in disappointing form in Southern League Premier South, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Swindon Supermarine's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Winchester's six.

In the last three years, Winchester and Swindon Supermarine have played each other on three occasions. Winchester won one of them and Swindon Supermarine the other.

Their last meeting was on March 4, when Swindon Supermarine won 6-1 away.
