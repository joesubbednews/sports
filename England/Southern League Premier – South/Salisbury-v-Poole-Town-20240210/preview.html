Salisbury v Poole Town

Southern League Premier – South | The Raymond McEnhill Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Salisbury host Poole Town in Saturday's match at the Raymond McEnhill Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Southern League Premier South.

Salisbury, who sit second in the league after 29 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to Bracknell Town on February 3.

They face a Poole side who also lost their last match, a 2-0 defeat to Hayes and Yeading United, and who sit 12th in the table.

With 56 goals in 25 games so far this season, Poole are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Salisbury are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Salisbury and Poole have played each other on 10 occasions. They won three each, and they drew four times.

On average, Salisbury scored 1.8 goals and Poole 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 30, when Salisbury won 5-4 away.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Town's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Salisbury's 10.
