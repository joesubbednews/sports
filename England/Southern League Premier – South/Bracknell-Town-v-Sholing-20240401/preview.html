Bracknell Town v Sholing

Southern League Premier – South
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bracknell Town are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Monday's kick-off against Sholing.

Bracknell, who sit fifth in the Southern League Premier South with 36 games played, are priced at 1.6 to seal victory.

Sitting seven places and 11 points behind them in the table, Sholing are 3.8 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 4.1.

With 41 goals in 33 games so far this season, Sholing are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Bracknell, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the home team's 10.
