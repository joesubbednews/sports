Hungerford Town v Bracknell Town

Southern League Premier – South | Bulpit Lane
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Friday's match between Hungerford Town and Bracknell Town promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Southern League Premier South's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Bulpit Lane, Hungerford and Bracknell sit second and joint-fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 69 and 66 goals respectively.

Bracknell are fifth in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn four, earning 55 points.

Hungerford are three places behind Bracknell Town in eighth, with 14 wins and 10 draws putting them on 52 points.

Hungerford Town are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home side's seven.

Hungerford's last match was on Saturday, a 6-1 win against Harrow Borough.

Bracknell beat Plymouth Parkway 2-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
