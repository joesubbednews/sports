Winchester City v Beaconsfield Town

Southern League Premier – South | The Denplan City Ground
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Beaconsfield Town travel to Winchester City on Saturday in the Southern League Premier South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Poole Town 3-1 away.

Winchester also lost their last match, 3-1 against Bracknell Town.

With 62 goals in 39 games so far this season, Beaconsfield are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 74 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Winchester are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

City are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the home side's seven.

In the last three years, Winchester and Beaconsfield have played each other on three occasions. Winchester won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Winchester won 2-1 away.

Winchester are 10th in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn 11, earning 50 points.

The visitors are three places behind Winchester in 13th, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on 47 points.
