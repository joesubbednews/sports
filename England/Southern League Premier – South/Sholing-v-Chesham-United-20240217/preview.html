Sholing v Chesham United

Southern League Premier – South | VT Group Sports Ground
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chesham United visit Sholing at the VT Group Sports Ground on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Southern League Premier South.

Chesham have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Sholing side who drew their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

Chesham are top of the table after 29 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn four, earning 67 points.

Sholing are eight places behind United in ninth, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

With 35 goals in 28 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Sholing's last match was on February 3, a 0-0 draw against Beaconsfield Town.

Chesham beat Swindon Supermarine 4-0 last time out, on February 10.
