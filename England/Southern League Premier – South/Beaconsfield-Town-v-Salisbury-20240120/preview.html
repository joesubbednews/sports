Beaconsfield Town v Salisbury

Southern League Premier – South | Holloways Park
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Salisbury travel to Holloways Park for Saturday's match against Beaconsfield Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Southern League Premier South.

Salisbury, who sit second in the league after 25 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to AFC Totton on January 13.

They face a Beaconsfield side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-2 victory against Merthyr Town, and who sit 15th in the table.

In the last 10 years, Beaconsfield and Salisbury have played each other on eight occasions. Beaconsfield won two of them, Salisbury three, and they drew three times.

On average, Beaconsfield scored 1.4 goals and Salisbury 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 5, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 41 goals in 25 games so far this season, Beaconsfield are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Salisbury, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in Southern League Premier South, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken eight points from 18.
