Didcot Town v Plymouth Parkway

Southern League Premier – South | NPower Loop Meadow Stadium
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Didcot Town face Plymouth Parkway at the NPower Loop Meadow Stadium on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-2 victory over Sholing on April 6, Didcot sit bottom of the Southern League Premier South.

They travel to play a Plymouth Parkway side 19th in the standings, who also won their last match, 4-1 against Swindon Supermarine, on April 7.

Didcot are in terrible form in the Southern League Premier South, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Plymouth Parkway's form is much better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Town's one.

Over the last two years, Didcot and Plymouth Parkway have played each other twice. Plymouth Parkway won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on September 6 2022, when Plymouth Parkway won 3-2 at home.

With 40 goals in 38 games so far this season, the home team are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 86 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.
