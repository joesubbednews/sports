Poole Town v Hanwell Town

Southern League Premier – South
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Poole Town are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Saturday's kick-off against Hanwell Town.

Poole, who sit 11th in Southern League Premier South with 23 games played, are priced at 1.5 to seal victory.

Sitting five places and nine points behind them in the table, Hanwell are 5.2 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 4.5.

With 56 goals in 23 games so far this season, Poole are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Hanwell, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last three years, Poole and Hanwell have played each other on three occasions. Poole won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 23, when Poole won 3-0 away.

Poole Town are in mixed form in Southern League Premier South, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Hanwell Town's form is slightly better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

Poole's last match was on Tuesday, a 5-4 loss against Salisbury.
