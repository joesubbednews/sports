Swindon Supermarine v Basingstoke Town

Southern League Premier – South | The Webbs Wood Stadium
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 1.30pm

Basingstoke Town travel to Swindon Supermarine on Saturday lunchtime in the Southern League Premier South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Bracknell Town 3-1 at home.

Swindon Supermarine also lost their last match, 4-1 against Plymouth Parkway.

With 66 goals in 38 games so far this season, Basingstoke are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 77 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Swindon Supermarine are also average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The hosts are 15th in the table after 39 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn 10, earning 43 points.

Town are four places ahead of Swindon Supermarine in 11th, with 14 wins and eight draws putting them on 50 points.

Swindon Supermarine are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier South, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's eight.

Over the last two years, Swindon Supermarine and Basingstoke have played each other twice. Basingstoke won both of them.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Basingstoke won 4-3 at home.
