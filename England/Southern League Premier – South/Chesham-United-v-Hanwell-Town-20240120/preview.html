Chesham United v Hanwell Town

Southern League Premier – South | The Meadow
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chesham United face Hanwell Town on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Southern League Premier South.

Chesham are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Hanwell team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

Chesham are top of the table after 25 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn four, earning 55 points.

Hanwell are 13 places behind United in 14th, with seven wins and seven draws putting them on 28 points.

With 50 goals in 25 games so far this season, the home side are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last three years, Chesham and Hanwell have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 5, when Chesham won 3-0 away.

Chesham's last match was on January 13, a 1-0 win against Hungerford Town.

Hanwell lost 2-1 against Basingstoke Town last time out, also on January 13.
