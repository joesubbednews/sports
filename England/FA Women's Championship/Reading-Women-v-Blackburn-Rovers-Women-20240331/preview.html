Reading Women v Blackburn Rovers Women

FA Women's Championship | Select Car Leasing Stadium
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 12.00pm

Reading Women welcome Blackburn Rovers Women to the Select Car Leasing Stadium on Sunday lunchtime looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Reading's struggles have left them with six points from their last six FA Women's Championship matches, while their opponents have earned 11 from a possible 18.

Reading are 10th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won four and drawn six, earning 18 points.

Blackburn Rovers are three places ahead of the Royals in seventh, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

With Emily Orman between the sticks, the home side can rely on one of the league's safest pair of hands. She has kept six clean sheets in her 17 appearances this season in the FA Women's Championship.

In the visitors' net, Alexandra Brooks also has six clean sheets in 18 games.

With 16 goals in 18 games so far this season, Reading are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Blackburn Rovers are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Reading's last match was on Sunday, a 5-1 loss against Sheffield United Women, with Madison Perry getting the goal for the Royals.

Blackburn Rovers beat Southampton Women 2-1 last time out, also on Sunday, with Megan Hornby on the scoresheet.
