Coventry City v Millwall

EFL Championship | The Coventry Building Society Arena
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 12.00pm

Coventry City host Millwall on Sunday at the Coventry Building Society Arena in EFL Championship.

In their last league match, on February 3, Coventry lost to Norwich City 2-1 away, with their goal scored by Callum O'Hare.

Millwall also lost, 1-0 away at Hull City.

With 30 goals in 30 games so far this season, Millwall are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Coventry, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Coventry and Millwall have played each other on 11 occasions. Coventry won six of them, Millwall three, and they drew twice.

On average, the Sky Blues scored 2.0 goals and the Lions 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Coventry won 3-0 away.

City are seventh in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn 11, earning 44 points.

The Lions are nine places behind the Sky Blues in 16th, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

The hosts are in reasonable form in EFL Championship, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Coventry's 11.
