Leeds United v Preston North End

EFL Championship | Elland Road
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 12.00pm

Leeds United are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday lunchtime's kick-off against Preston North End.

Sitting eight places and 13 points behind them in the table, Preston are 8.8 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 5.2.

The hosts are in mixed form in EFL Championship, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

Leeds's last match was on January 13, a 3-0 win against Cardiff City, with Daniel James, Georginio Rutter and Patrick Bamford getting the goals for Leeds.

Preston beat Bristol City 2-0 last time out, also on January 13, with Will Keane on the scoresheet.

The last Leeds game Webb refereed was a 1-0 home win against Queens Park Rangers on October 4. His last Preston match was their 4-2 loss away at Ipswich Town on October 7.
