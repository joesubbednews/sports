Stoke City v Birmingham City

EFL Championship | bet365 Stadium
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stoke City face Birmingham City on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in EFL Championship.

The Potters are unbeaten in seven, with two wins and five draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Birmingham team who have won one and drawn three over the same number of games.

In the last 10 years, Stoke and Birmingham have played each other on 11 occasions. Stoke won two of them, Birmingham five, and they drew four times.

On average, the Potters scored 0.9 goals and the Blues 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Stoke won 3-1 away.

Birmingham are 20th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won seven and drawn eight, earning 29 points.

Stoke are three places ahead of Birmingham City in 17th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

With 26 goals in 27 games so far this season, Stoke City are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The Blues are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Stoke's last match was on January 13, a 1-0 win against Rotherham United, with Lewis Baker getting the goal for the Potters.

Birmingham drew 2-2 with Swansea City last time out, also on January 13, with Jordan James and Siriki Dembele on the scoresheet.
