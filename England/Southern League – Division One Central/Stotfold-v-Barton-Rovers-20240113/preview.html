Stotfold v Barton Rovers

Southern League – Division One Central
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Barton Rovers face Stotfold on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Southern League Division One Central.

Barton are unbeaten in five, with one win and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Stotfold team who have won one and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 27 goals in 18 games so far this season, Barton are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Stotfold, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have also conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Rovers are 15th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won four and drawn seven, earning 19 points.

The hosts are two places ahead of the away team in 13th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

Stotfold's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-2 draw against Kings Langley.

Barton drew 1-1 with North Leigh last time out, on January 6.
