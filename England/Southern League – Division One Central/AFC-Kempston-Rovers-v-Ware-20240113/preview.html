AFC Kempston Rovers v Ware

Southern League – Division One Central | Hillgrounds Leisure
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

AFC Kempston Rovers are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Ware at Hillgrounds Leisure on Saturday, with just one point collected from their last six games.

Kempston Rovers have picked up just one draw in their last six Southern League – Division One Central games, and face a Ware side whose last six games have brought two wins and four losses.

Kempston Rovers are bottom of the table after 16 games, of which they have won two and drawn three, earning nine points.

Ware are 10 places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with nine wins and two draws putting them on 29 points.

With 14 goals in 16 games so far this season, Kempston Rovers are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Over the last year, Kempston Rovers and Ware have played each other twice. Ware won both of them.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Ware won 3-0 at home.

Kempston Rovers' last match was on January 1, a 2-0 loss against AFC Dunstable.

Ware lost 4-1 against Kings Langley last time out, also on January 1.
