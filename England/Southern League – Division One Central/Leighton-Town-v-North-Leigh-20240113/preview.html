Leighton Town v North Leigh

Southern League – Division One Central | Bell Close
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leighton Town host North Leigh on Saturday at Bell Close in Southern League Division One Central.

In their last league match, on January 6, Leighton lost to Waltham Abbey 2-1 away.

North Leigh drew, 1-1 away at Barton Rovers.

With 40 goals in 19 games so far this season, North Leigh are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Leighton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The visitors are top of the table after 19 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn one, earning 37 points.

Town are seven places behind North Leigh in eighth, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

The home side are in mixed form in Southern League Division One Central, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, North Leigh's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Leighton's nine.
