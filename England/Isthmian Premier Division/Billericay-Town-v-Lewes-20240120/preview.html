Billericay Town v Lewes

Isthmian Premier Division | AGP Arena
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of Isthmian Premier Division's meanest defences go head-to-head at the AGP Arena on Saturday, when Billericay Town host Lewes.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Billericay to date: the home side have let in just 18 goals in 23 games.

Lewes have conceded 32 goals in 22 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Billericay are third in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn three, earning 45 points.

Lewes are four places behind Town in seventh, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

In the last 10 years, Billericay and Lewes have played each other on seven occasions. Billericay won three of them and they drew four times.

On average, Billericay scored 1.7 goals and Lewes 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 25, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The hosts are in good form in Isthmian Premier Division, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Billericay's 13.

Billericay's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-1 draw against AFC Hornchurch.

Lewes beat Hashtag United 3-2 last time out, on Monday.
