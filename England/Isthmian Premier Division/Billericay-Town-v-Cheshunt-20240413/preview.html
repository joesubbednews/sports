Billericay Town v Cheshunt

Isthmian Premier Division | AGP Arena
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Billericay Town host Cheshunt at the AGP Arena on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Billericay have picked up 10 points from their last six games, and they face a Cheshunt side who also won their last match, and have collected 12 points from the last possible 18.

With 49 goals in 36 games so far this season, Cheshunt are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Billericay, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Town are fifth in the table after 39 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn four, earning 64 points.

The visitors are 13 places behind the home team in 18th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

Billericay's last match was on April 6, a 6-0 win against Haringey Borough.

Cheshunt beat Lewes 4-0 last time out, also on April 6.
