Lewes v Billericay Town

Isthmian Premier Division | The Dripping Pan
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Billericay Town travel to the Dripping Pan for Tuesday's late match against Lewes looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian Premier Division.

Billericay, who sit third in the league after 27 games, fell to a 2-1 home defeat to Folkestone Invicta on Saturday.

They face a Lewes side who also lost their last match, a 3-0 defeat to Wingate and Finchley, and who sit 10th in the table.

In the last 10 years, Lewes and Billericay have played each other on eight occasions. Billericay won four of them and they drew four times.

On average, Lewes scored 1.0 goal and Billericay 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 20, when Billericay won 4-0 at home.

With 44 goals in 26 games so far this season, Lewes are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Billericay are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Lewes's nine.
