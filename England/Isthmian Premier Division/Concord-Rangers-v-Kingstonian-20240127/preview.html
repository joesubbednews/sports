Concord Rangers v Kingstonian

Isthmian Premier Division | Thames Road
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Concord Rangers face Kingstonian at Thames Road on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Kingstonian on Wednesday, the Beach Boys sit 21st in Isthmian Premier Division.

They travel to play a Kingstonian side 22nd in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Concord Rangers.

Concord are in mixed form in Isthmian Premier Division, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and six losses over that period, Kingstonian's form is much worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to Rangers's eight.

With 18 goals in 19 games so far this season, the Beach Boys are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.
