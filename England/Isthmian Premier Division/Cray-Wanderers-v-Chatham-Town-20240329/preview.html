Cray Wanderers v Chatham Town

Isthmian Premier Division
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cray Wanderers host Chatham Town on Friday in the Isthmian Premier Division.

In their last league match, on Sunday, Cray lost to Folkestone Invicta 1-0 at home.

Chatham won, 2-1 at home against Haringey Borough on Saturday.

With 75 goals in 36 games so far this season, Chatham are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Cray, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Town are second in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 22 and drawn five, earning 71 points.

Wanderers are 12 places behind the visitors in 14th, with 12 wins and nine draws putting them on 45 points.

The home side are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Chatham's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Cray's nine.
