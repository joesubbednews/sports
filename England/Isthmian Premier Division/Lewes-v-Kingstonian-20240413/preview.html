Lewes v Kingstonian

Isthmian Premier Division | The Dripping Pan
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kingstonian make the journey to the Dripping Pan to play Lewes on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their seven-game losing streak.

Kingstonian's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Isthmian Premier Division matches, while their opponents have earned four from a possible 18.

Kingstonian are bottom of the table after 37 games, of which they have won four and drawn six, earning 18 points.

Lewes are 11 places ahead of the visitors in 11th, with 15 wins and seven draws putting them on 52 points.

In the last 10 years, Lewes and Kingstonian have played each other on 13 occasions. Lewes won five of them, Kingstonian six, and they drew twice.

On average, Lewes scored 1.8 goals and Kingstonian 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 16, when Lewes won 3-1 away.

With 51 goals in 37 games so far this season, Kingstonian are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 91 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Lewes's last match was on April 6, a 4-0 loss against Cheshunt.

Kingstonian lost 3-1 against Margate last time out, also on April 6.
