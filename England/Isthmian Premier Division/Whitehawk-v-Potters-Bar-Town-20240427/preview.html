Whitehawk v Potters Bar Town

Isthmian Premier Division | The Enclosed Ground
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Potters Bar Town make the journey to the Enclosed Ground to play Whitehawk on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Potters Bar's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Isthmian Premier Division matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

Potters Bar are 16th in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn nine, earning 48 points.

Whitehawk are three places ahead of Town in 13th, with 13 wins and 13 draws putting them on 52 points.

With 51 goals in 37 games so far this season, the Hawks are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Whitehawk's last match was on April 20, a 1-1 draw against Concord Rangers.

Potters Bar lost 1-0 against AFC Hornchurch last time out, also on April 20.
