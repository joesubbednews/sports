Hastings United v Horsham

Isthmian Premier Division | The Pilot Field
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Hastings United host Horsham at the Pilot Field on late Tuesday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Hastings have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Horsham side who have also won their last two matches, and collected nine points from the last possible 18.

With 53 goals in 28 games so far this season, Hastings are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Horsham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

United are fourth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

The away team are seven places behind the hosts in 11th, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

Over the last two years, Hastings and Horsham have played each other twice. they drew both of them.

Their last meeting was on April 10, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Hastings's last match was on Saturday, a 3-1 win against Kingstonian.

Horsham beat Hashtag United 2-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
