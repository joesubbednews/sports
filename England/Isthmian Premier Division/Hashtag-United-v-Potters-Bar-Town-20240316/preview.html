Hashtag United v Potters Bar Town

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Potters Bar Town face Hashtag United on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Potters Bar are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Hashtag team who have won two and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 59 goals in 34 games so far this season, Hashtag are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Potters Bar, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are 16th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn nine, earning 39 points.

United are six places ahead of the away side in 10th, with 12 wins and 12 draws putting them on 48 points.

Hashtag's last match was on March 9, a 4-2 win against Margate.

Potters Bar beat Cray Wanderers 2-1 last time out, on Wednesday.
