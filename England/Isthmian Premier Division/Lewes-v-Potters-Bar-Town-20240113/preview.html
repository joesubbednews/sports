Lewes v Potters Bar Town

Isthmian Premier Division | The Dripping Pan
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lewes face Potters Bar Town on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian Premier Division.

Lewes are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Potters Bar team who have won four and lost one over the same number of games.

With 33 goals in 20 games so far this season, Lewes are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Potters Bar, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are 15th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won seven and drawn six, earning 27 points.

The hosts are five places ahead of the away team in 10th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

In the last three years, Lewes and Potters Bar have played each other on five occasions. Lewes won three of them and Potters Bar two.

On average, Lewes scored 2.2 goals and Potters Bar 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 19, when Lewes won 2-1 away.

Lewes's last match was on January 6, a 1-1 draw against Cray Wanderers.

Potters Bar beat Hashtag United 5-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
