Enfield Town v Folkestone Invicta

Isthmian Premier Division | Queen Elizabeth Stadium
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Folkestone Invicta come to the Queen Elizabeth Stadium to play Enfield Town on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

Folkestone Invicta, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Town are third in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn four, earning 43 points.

The visitors are 14 places behind the home side in 17th, with six wins and five draws putting them on 23 points.

In the last five years, Enfield and Folkestone Invicta have played each other on eight occasions. Enfield won two of them, Folkestone Invicta three, and they drew three times.

On average, Enfield scored 1.1 goals and Folkestone Invicta 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 19, when Enfield won 2-1 away.

Enfield's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-0 win against Haringey Borough.

Folkestone Invicta drew 1-1 with Wingate and Finchley last time out, on January 6.
