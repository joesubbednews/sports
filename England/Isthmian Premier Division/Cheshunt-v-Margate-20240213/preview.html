Cheshunt v Margate

Isthmian Premier Division
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Margate make the journey to play Cheshunt on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Margate's struggles have left them with just one point from their last six Isthmian Premier Division matches, while their opponents have earned four from a possible 18.

Margate are 19th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

Cheshunt are one place ahead of the away side in 18th, with six wins and five draws putting them on 23 points.

In the last five years, Cheshunt and Margate have played each other on five occasions. Cheshunt won two of them, Margate one, and they drew twice.

On average, Cheshunt scored 1.6 goals and Margate 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 25, when Cheshunt won 2-1 away.

With 30 goals in 29 games so far this season, Margate are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Cheshunt's last match was on Saturday, a 1-1 draw against Bognor Regis Town.

Margate lost 2-1 against Chatham Town last time out, also on Saturday.
