Lewes v Concord Rangers

Isthmian Premier Division | The Dripping Pan
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Concord Rangers make the journey to the Dripping Pan to play Lewes on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Concord's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian Premier Division matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

Concord are 21st in the table after 30 games, of which they have won five and drawn six, earning 21 points.

Lewes are nine places ahead of Rangers in 12th, with 14 wins and six draws putting them on 48 points.

With 27 goals in 30 games so far this season, the Beach Boys are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Lewes's last match was on March 16, a 2-0 loss against Dulwich Hamlet.

Concord lost 2-0 against Potters Bar Town last time out, on Tuesday.
