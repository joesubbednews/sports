Canvey Island v Carshalton Athletic

Isthmian Premier Division | The Brockwell Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Carshalton Athletic travel to the Brockwell Stadium looking to secure a first win in five Isthmian Premier Division games against Canvey Island on Saturday.

Carshalton have lost one and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Lewes on January 30.

They face a Canvey Island side who have won just one and lost three over that time.

With 57 goals in 31 games so far this season, Carshalton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Canvey Island, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The Gulls are 14th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn two, earning 38 points.

Athletic are five places ahead of the home side in ninth, with 12 wins and nine draws putting them on 45 points.

In the last three years, Canvey Island and Carshalton have played each other on three occasions. Canvey Island won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 9, when Canvey Island won 3-0 away.

Canvey Island's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-1 loss against Cray Wanderers.

Carshalton drew 1-1 with Enfield Town last time out, on February 17.
