Hashtag United v Bognor Regis Town

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hashtag United welcome Bognor Regis Town to Coles Park on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Hashtag's struggles have left them with nine points from their last six Isthmian Premier Division matches, while their opponents have earned 11 from a possible 18.

With 35 goals in 23 games so far this season, Hashtag are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Bognor Regis, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

United are seventh in the table after 23 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

Town are two places behind the hosts in ninth, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

Hashtag's last match was on Tuesday, a 5-0 loss against Potters Bar Town.

Bognor Regis beat Cray Wanderers 2-1 last time out, also on Tuesday.
