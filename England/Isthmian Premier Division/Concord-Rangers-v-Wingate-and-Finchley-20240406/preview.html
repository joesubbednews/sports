Concord Rangers v Wingate and Finchley

Isthmian Premier Division | Thames Road
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Concord Rangers face a challenge against high-flying Wingate and Finchley at Thames Road on Saturday.

Concord Rangers are 21st in the Isthmian Premier Division table, and have picked up five wins and six draws in their 34 games to date.

The Blues, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 65 points, having won 20 and drawn five of their first 36 matches, and are 20 points behind table-toppers AFC Hornchurch.

Concord are in awful form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Wingate & Finchley's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Rangers's zero.

With 29 goals in 34 games so far this season, the Beach Boys are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 65 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The Blues, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Concord's last match was on Monday, a 2-0 loss against Billericay Town.

Wingate & Finchley beat Potters Bar Town 3-1 last time out, also on Monday.
