Canvey Island v Horsham

Isthmian Premier Division | The Brockwell Stadium
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Horsham travel to the Brockwell Stadium looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian Premier Division games against Canvey Island on Saturday.

Horsham have lost two and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Folkestone Invicta on December 16.

They face a Canvey Island side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

With 26 goals in 21 games so far this season, Horsham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Canvey Island, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last three years, Canvey Island and Horsham have played each other on three occasions. Horsham won all of them.

Their last meeting was on December 5, when Horsham won 2-1 at home.

The Gulls are 13th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn two, earning 32 points.

The visitors are two places ahead of the home team in 11th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

Canvey Island's last match was on January 13, a 3-2 win against Kingstonian.

Horsham drew 1-1 with Enfield Town last time out, on Tuesday.
