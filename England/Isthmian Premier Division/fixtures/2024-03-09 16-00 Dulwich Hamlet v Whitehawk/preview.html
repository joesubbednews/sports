Dulwich Hamlet v Whitehawk

Isthmian Premier Division | Imperial Fields
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Whitehawk face Dulwich Hamlet on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Isthmian Premier Division.

The Hawks are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Dulwich Hamlet team who have won four and lost one over the same number of games.

With 62 goals in 31 games so far this season, Dulwich Hamlet are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Whitehawk, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are ninth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn nine, earning 48 points.

The Hawks are five places behind Dulwich Hamlet in 14th, with 10 wins and 10 draws putting them on 40 points.

Dulwich Hamlet's last match was on February 24, a 4-2 win against Cheshunt.

Whitehawk beat Cray Wanderers 2-0 last time out, on February 27.
