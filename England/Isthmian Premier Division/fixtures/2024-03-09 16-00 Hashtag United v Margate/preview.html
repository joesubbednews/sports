Hashtag United v Margate

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Margate come to Coles Park to play Hashtag United on Saturday in poor form, having collected just three points from their last six games.

The visitors have won just one of their last six fixtures, while Hashtag have two wins and three draws.

With 34 goals in 32 games so far this season, Margate are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Hashtag, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The away side are 19th in the table after 32 games, of which they have won six and drawn seven, earning 25 points.

United are seven places ahead of Margate in 12th, with 11 wins and 12 draws putting them on 45 points.

Hashtag's last match was on March 2, a 4-2 win against Billericay Town.

Margate lost 3-1 against Horsham last time out, also on March 2.
