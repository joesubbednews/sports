Carshalton Athletic v Hastings United

Isthmian Premier Division | War Memorial Sports Ground
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hastings United face Carshalton Athletic on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian Premier Division.

Hastings are unbeaten in seven, with three wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Carshalton team who have won four and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 44 goals in 24 games so far this season, Carshalton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Hastings are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

United are sixth in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn six, earning 39 points.

Athletic are three places behind the away side in ninth, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last three years, Carshalton and Hastings have played each other on three occasions. Carshalton won one of them and Hastings the other.

Their last meeting was on November 21, when Hastings won 4-0 at home.

Carshalton's last match was on January 13, a 2-2 draw against AFC Hornchurch.

Hastings drew 2-2 with Dulwich Hamlet last time out, also on January 13.
