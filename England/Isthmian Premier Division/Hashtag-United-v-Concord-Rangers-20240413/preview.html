Hashtag United v Concord Rangers

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hashtag United are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Saturday's kick-off against Concord Rangers.

Hashtag, who sit ninth in the Isthmian Premier Division with 39 games played, are priced at 1.3 to seal victory at Coles Park.

Sitting 12 places and 37 points behind them in the table, Concord are 6.0 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 5.0.

Hashtag are in good form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and six losses over that period, Concord's form is much worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to United's 13.

With 29 goals in 35 games so far this season, Rangers are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 67 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The home side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Hashtag's last match was on April 5, a 3-1 win against Cray Wanderers.

Concord lost 2-0 against Wingate and Finchley last time out, on April 6.
