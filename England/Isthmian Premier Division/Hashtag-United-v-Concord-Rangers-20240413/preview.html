Hashtag United v Concord Rangers

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hashtag United host Concord Rangers at Coles Park on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Hashtag have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Beach Boys side who have lost their last 10 matches, and collected no points from the last possible 18.

With 29 goals in 35 games so far this season, Concord are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 67 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Hashtag, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Rangers are 21st in the table after 35 games, of which they have won five and drawn six, earning 21 points.

United are 12 places ahead of the Beach Boys in ninth, with 15 wins and 13 draws putting them on 58 points.

Hashtag's last match was on April 5, a 3-1 win against Cray Wanderers.

Concord lost 2-0 against Wingate and Finchley last time out, on April 6.
