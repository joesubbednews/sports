Horsham v Wingate and Finchley

Isthmian Premier Division
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wingate and Finchley face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Horsham defence on Saturday.

With 55 goals in 28 games, Wingate & Finchley are the third-highest scorers in the Isthmian Premier Division ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Horsham side who have scored 32 in 24 matches, but conceded only 26 goals, putting them third among the league's tightest defences – only AFC Hornchurch and Billericay Town have conceded fewer goals.

In the last 10 years, Horsham and Wingate & Finchley have played each other on seven occasions. Horsham won four of them, Wingate & Finchley two, and they drew once.

On average, Horsham scored 1.4 goals and the Blues 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 17, when Wingate & Finchley won 2-0 at home.

Wingate & Finchley are fourth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn two, earning 50 points.

Horsham are six places behind the Blues in 10th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Horsham's 11.

Horsham's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 win against Hastings United.

Wingate & Finchley beat Chatham Town 4-2 last time out, also on Tuesday.
