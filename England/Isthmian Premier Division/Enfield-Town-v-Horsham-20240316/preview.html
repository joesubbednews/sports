Enfield Town v Horsham

Isthmian Premier Division | Queen Elizabeth Stadium
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Enfield Town face Horsham on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Enfield are unbeaten in five, with two wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Horsham team who have won three and lost two over the same number of games.

With 58 goals in 32 games so far this season, Enfield are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Horsham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Enfield and Horsham have played each other on seven occasions. They won three each, and they drew once.

On average, Enfield scored 1.7 goals and Horsham 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 16, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn seven, earning 55 points.

Town are one place behind Horsham in sixth, with 15 wins and nine draws putting them on 54 points.

Enfield's last match was on March 9, a 1-0 win against Lewes.

Horsham lost 2-1 against Carshalton Athletic last time out, also on March 9.
