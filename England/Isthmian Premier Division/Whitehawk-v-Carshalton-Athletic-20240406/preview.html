Whitehawk v Carshalton Athletic

Isthmian Premier Division | The Enclosed Ground
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Carshalton Athletic come to the Enclosed Ground to play Whitehawk on Saturday in great form, having collected 15 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and lost one of their last six fixtures, while the Hawks have two wins and two draws.

With 69 goals in 38 games so far this season, Carshalton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Whitehawk, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Athletic are sixth in the table after 38 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn 10, earning 61 points.

The Hawks are nine places behind the visitors in 15th, with 11 wins and 12 draws putting them on 45 points.

Whitehawk's last match was on Monday, a 1-1 draw against Lewes.

Carshalton beat Dulwich Hamlet 3-0 last time out, also on Monday.
