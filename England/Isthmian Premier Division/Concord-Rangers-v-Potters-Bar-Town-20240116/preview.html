Concord Rangers v Potters Bar Town

Isthmian Premier Division | Thames Road
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Potters Bar Town travel to Concord Rangers on late Tuesday in Isthmian Premier Division.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Lewes 3-0 away.

The Beach Boys, meanwhile, drew their last match, 1-1 against Margate.

Concord are bottom of the table after 18 games, of which they have won three and drawn three, earning 12 points.

Potters Bar are seven places ahead of Rangers in 15th, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.

The Beach Boys are in disappointing form in Isthmian Premier Division, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home side's six.

With 16 goals in 18 games so far this season, Concord are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.
