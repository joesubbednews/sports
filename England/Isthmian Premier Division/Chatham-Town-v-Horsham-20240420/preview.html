Chatham Town v Horsham

Isthmian Premier Division
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chatham Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Horsham defence on Saturday.

With 84 goals in 40 games, Chatham are the second-highest scorers in the Isthmian Premier Division ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Horsham side who have scored 57 in 37 matches, but conceded only 46 goals, putting them second among the league's tightest defences – only AFC Hornchurch have conceded fewer goals.

Chatham are second in the table after 40 games, of which they have won 24 and drawn six, earning 78 points.

Horsham are four places behind Town in sixth, with 20 wins and seven draws putting them on 67 points.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Chatham's 11.

Chatham's last match was on April 13, a 2-0 win against Folkestone Invicta.

Horsham beat Cray Wanderers 2-0 last time out, also on April 13.
