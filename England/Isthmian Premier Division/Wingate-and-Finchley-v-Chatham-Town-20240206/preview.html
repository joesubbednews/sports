Wingate and Finchley v Chatham Town

Isthmian Premier Division
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Chatham Town are strong favourites to take all three points despite Wingate and Finchley's home advantage in Tuesday's late match.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.95 on Chatham sealing the win, with the visitors sitting second in the Isthmian Premier Division table.

Wingate & Finchley, who are sixth in the league and eight points behind Chatham, are priced at 3.1 to win. A draw is set at 3.6.

With 57 goals in 28 games so far this season, Chatham are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Wingate & Finchley are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The Blues are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Town's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

Wingate & Finchley's last match was on Saturday, a 3-0 win against Lewes.

Chatham beat Whitehawk 2-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
