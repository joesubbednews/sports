Chatham Town v Canvey Island

Isthmian Premier Division
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chatham Town host Canvey Island on Saturday in Isthmian Premier Division.

In their last league match, on January 13, Chatham beat Cheshunt 3-2 at home.

Canvey Island also won, 2-1 at home against Folkestone Invicta on Tuesday.

With 48 goals in 25 games so far this season, Chatham are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Canvey Island, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Town are second in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn four, earning 49 points.

The Gulls are nine places behind the hosts in 11th, with 11 wins and two draws putting them on 35 points.

Chatham are in good form in Isthmian Premier Division, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Chatham's 13.
