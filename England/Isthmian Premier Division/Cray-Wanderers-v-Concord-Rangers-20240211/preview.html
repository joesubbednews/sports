Cray Wanderers v Concord Rangers

Isthmian Premier Division
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cray Wanderers host Concord Rangers on Sunday in the Isthmian Premier Division.

In their last league match, on Tuesday, Cray drew with Folkestone Invicta 2-2 away.

Concord lost, 4-3 away at Potters Bar Town on February 3.

Concord are 20th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won four and drawn five, earning 17 points.

Cray are four places ahead of Rangers in 16th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

Wanderers are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the Beach Boys' form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.

With 21 goals in 22 games so far this season, the away side are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Cray are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.
