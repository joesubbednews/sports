Carshalton Athletic v Dulwich Hamlet

Isthmian Premier Division | War Memorial Sports Ground
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Dulwich Hamlet travel to Carshalton Athletic on Monday in the Isthmian Premier Division.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Billericay Town 2-1 at home.

Carshalton, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-1 against Concord Rangers.

With 67 goals in 35 games so far this season, Dulwich Hamlet are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Carshalton are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Athletic are sixth in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn 10, earning 58 points.

The visitors are two places behind the home team in eighth, with 15 wins and 10 draws putting them on 55 points.

Carshalton are in good form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with four wins and a draw over that period, Dulwich Hamlet's form is identical – they have both taken 13 points from 18.
