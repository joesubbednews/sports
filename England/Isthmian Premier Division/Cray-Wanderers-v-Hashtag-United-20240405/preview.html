Cray Wanderers v Hashtag United

Isthmian Premier Division
Friday, 5 April 2024 @ 7.45pm

Hashtag United travel to Cray Wanderers on late Friday in the Isthmian Premier Division.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Haringey Borough 5-0 at home.

Cray, meanwhile, drew their last match, 1-1 against Margate.

With 73 goals in 38 games so far this season, Hashtag are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 66 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Cray, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Wanderers are in disappointing form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, United's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home side's five.

Cray are 14th in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn 11, earning 47 points.

The visitors are four places ahead of Cray in 10th, with 14 wins and 13 draws putting them on 55 points.
