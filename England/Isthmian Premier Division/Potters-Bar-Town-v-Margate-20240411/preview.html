Potters Bar Town v Margate

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Thursday, 11 April 2024 @ 8.00pm

Struggling Margate face Potters Bar Town away at Coles Park on Thursday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-1 victory over Kingstonian on Saturday, Margate sit 19th in the Isthmian Premier Division.

They travel to play a Potters Bar side 13th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-1 against Wingate and Finchley, on April 1.

In the last 10 years, Potters Bar and Margate have played each other on 10 occasions. Potters Bar won five of them, Margate four, and they drew once.

On average, Potters Bar scored 1.5 goals and Margate 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Margate won 2-1 at home.

With 41 goals in 37 games so far this season, Margate are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 71 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Potters Bar, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are in good form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.
