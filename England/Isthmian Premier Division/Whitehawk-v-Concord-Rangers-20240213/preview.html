Whitehawk v Concord Rangers

Isthmian Premier Division | The Enclosed Ground
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Whitehawk host Concord Rangers on Tuesday at the Enclosed Ground in the Isthmian Premier Division.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Whitehawk drew with Haringey Borough 1-1 away.

Concord also drew, 1-1 away at Cray Wanderers on Sunday.

Concord are 20th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won four and drawn six, earning 18 points.

Whitehawk are five places ahead of Rangers in 15th, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last 10 years, Whitehawk and Concord have played each other on nine occasions. Whitehawk won two of them, Concord five, and they drew twice.

On average, the Hawks scored 0.6 goals and the Beach Boys 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 24 2018, when Whitehawk won 2-0 at home.

With 22 goals in 23 games so far this season, the Beach Boys are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The Hawks are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, the visitors' form is similar – they have both taken seven points from 18.
