Wingate and Finchley v Hashtag United

Isthmian Premier Division | Harry Abrahams Stadium
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hashtag United travel to Wingate and Finchley on Saturday in the Isthmian Premier Division.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Carshalton Athletic 2-2 at home.

The Blues also drew their last match, 1-1 against Horsham.

With 56 goals in 29 games so far this season, Wingate & Finchley are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Hashtag are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The Blues are fourth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn three, earning 51 points.

United are eight places behind the home team in 12th, with 10 wins and 10 draws putting them on 40 points.

Wingate & Finchley are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Wingate & Finchley's 10.
