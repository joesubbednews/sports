Bognor Regis Town v Hastings United

Isthmian Premier Division | Nyewood Lane
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bognor Regis Town welcome Hastings United to Nyewood Lane on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Bognor Regis's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Isthmian Premier Division matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

With 64 goals in 35 games so far this season, Hastings are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Bognor Regis are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

United are eighth in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn eight, earning 56 points.

Town are four places behind the visitors in 12th, with 13 wins and 11 draws putting them on 50 points.

In the last three years, Bognor Regis and Hastings have played each other on three occasions. Hastings won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Bognor Regis's last match was on Monday, a 5-4 loss against Horsham.

Hastings beat Folkestone Invicta 2-1 last time out, also on Monday.
