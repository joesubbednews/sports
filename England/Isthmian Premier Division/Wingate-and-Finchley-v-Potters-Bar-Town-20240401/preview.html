Wingate and Finchley v Potters Bar Town

Isthmian Premier Division | Harry Abrahams Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Potters Bar Town visit Wingate and Finchley at the Harry Abrahams Stadium on Monday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Potters Bar have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Blues side who have also won their last two matches, and collected 11 points from the last possible 18.

Wingate & Finchley are third in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn five, earning 62 points.

Potters Bar are 10 places behind the Blues in 13th, with 13 wins and nine draws putting them on 48 points.

With 64 goals in 35 games so far this season, the home team are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Town are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Wingate & Finchley and Potters Bar have played each other on eight occasions. Wingate & Finchley won three of them, Potters Bar four, and they drew once.

On average, the Blues scored 1.4 goals and Potters Bar 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Potters Bar won 3-2 at home.

Wingate & Finchley's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 win against AFC Hornchurch.

Potters Bar beat Kingstonian 6-1 last time out, on March 23.
