Potters Bar Town v Margate

Isthmian Premier Division | The Pakex Stadium
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Potters Bar Town host Margate at the Pakex Stadium on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Potters Bar have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Margate side who drew their last match, and have collected four points from the last possible 18.

In the last 10 years, Potters Bar and Margate have played each other on 10 occasions. Potters Bar won five of them, Margate four, and they drew once.

On average, Potters Bar scored 1.5 goals and Margate 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Margate won 2-1 at home.

Margate are 19th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won six and drawn eight, earning 26 points.

Potters Bar are six places ahead of the visitors in 13th, with 13 wins and nine draws putting them on 48 points.

With 37 goals in 35 games so far this season, Margate are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 69 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Potters Bar's last match was on March 23, a 6-1 win against Kingstonian.

Margate drew 1-1 with Enfield Town last time out, also on March 23.
