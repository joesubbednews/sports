Haringey Borough v Margate

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Haringey Borough and Margate both come into Saturday's kick-off at Coles Park in poor form, having picked up two and three points respectively in their last six games.

Margate are three places ahead of Borough in 18th, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.

Their last meeting was on November 7, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Haringey's last match was on February 17, a 3-0 loss against AFC Hornchurch.

Margate beat Canvey Island 3-2 last time out, also on February 17.
