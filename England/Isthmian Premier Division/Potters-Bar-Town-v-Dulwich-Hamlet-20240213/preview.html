Potters Bar Town v Dulwich Hamlet

Isthmian Premier Division | The Pakex Stadium
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Potters Bar Town host Dulwich Hamlet on Tuesday at the Pakex Stadium in the Isthmian Premier Division.

In their last league match, on February 6, Potters Bar drew with Carshalton Athletic 1-1 at home.

Dulwich Hamlet lost, 3-2 at home against AFC Hornchurch on Saturday.

With 56 goals in 29 games so far this season, Dulwich Hamlet are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Potters Bar, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home side's 10.

Potters Bar are 14th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

Dulwich Hamlet are three places ahead of Potters Bar in 11th, with 11 wins and nine draws putting them on 42 points.
