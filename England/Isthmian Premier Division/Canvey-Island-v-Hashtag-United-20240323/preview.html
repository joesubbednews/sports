Canvey Island v Hashtag United

Isthmian Premier Division | The Brockwell Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Canvey Island are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Hashtag United at the Brockwell Stadium on Saturday, with just two points collected from their last six games.

The Gulls have picked up two draws and four losses in their last six Isthmian Premier Division games, and face a Hashtag side whose last six games have brought two wins and three draws.

With 61 goals in 35 games so far this season, Hashtag are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Canvey Island, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The Gulls are 16th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn four, earning 40 points.

United are five places ahead of the hosts in 11th, with 12 wins and 12 draws putting them on 48 points.

In the last three years, Canvey Island and Hashtag have played each other on three occasions. Canvey Island won two of them and Hashtag one.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Hashtag won 3-1 at home.

Canvey Island's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-1 loss against Horsham.

Hashtag lost 3-2 against Potters Bar Town last time out, on March 16.
