Horsham v Concord Rangers

Isthmian Premier Division
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Horsham are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Saturday's kick-off against Concord Rangers.

Horsham, who sit sixth in the Isthmian Premier Division with 38 games played, are priced at 1.2 to seal victory.

Sitting 15 places and 45 points behind them in the table, Concord are 9.0 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 5.5.

Horsham are in fantastic form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Concord's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the home side's 15.

With 33 goals in 37 games so far this season, Rangers are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 70 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Horsham, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Horsham's last match was on April 20, a 3-1 win against Chatham Town.

Concord drew 1-1 with Whitehawk last time out, also on April 20.
