Hashtag United v Horsham

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hashtag United are on a poor run ahead of hosting Horsham at Coles Park on Saturday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Hashtag have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Isthmian Premier Division games, and face a Horsham side whose last six games have brought one win and three draws.

With 28 goals in 22 games so far this season, Horsham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Hashtag, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

United are 11th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn nine, earning 39 points.

The away side are one place behind the hosts in 12th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

Hashtag's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-2 win against Chatham Town.

Horsham beat Potters Bar Town 2-0 last time out, on January 27.
