Lewes v Carshalton Athletic

Isthmian Premier Division | The Dripping Pan
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Carshalton Athletic face Lewes on Tuesday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian Premier Division.

Carshalton are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Lewes team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 49 goals in 26 games so far this season, Carshalton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Lewes are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Lewes and Carshalton have played each other on 10 occasions. Lewes won three of them, Carshalton five, and they drew twice.

On average, Lewes scored 0.9 goals and Carshalton 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 23, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The hosts are eighth in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

Athletic are one place behind Lewes in ninth, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

Lewes's last match was on Saturday, a 4-2 win against Margate.

Carshalton drew 3-3 with Folkestone Invicta last time out, also on Saturday.
