Dulwich Hamlet v Enfield Town

Isthmian Premier Division | Imperial Fields
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Enfield Town face Dulwich Hamlet on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Enfield are unbeaten in nine, with five wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Dulwich Hamlet team who have won five and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 68 goals in 36 games so far this season, Enfield are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Dulwich Hamlet are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Dulwich Hamlet and Enfield have played each other on 10 occasions. Dulwich Hamlet won five of them, Enfield two, and they drew three times.

On average, Dulwich Hamlet scored 1.9 goals and Enfield 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when Enfield won 3-2 at home.

Town are fourth in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn 10, earning 64 points.

The home side are five places behind the visitors in ninth, with 15 wins and 10 draws putting them on 55 points.

Dulwich Hamlet's last match was on Monday, a 3-0 loss against Carshalton Athletic.

Enfield beat Cheshunt 3-0 last time out, also on Monday.
