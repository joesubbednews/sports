Canvey Island v Lewes

Isthmian Premier Division
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Lewes face Canvey Island on Tuesday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian Premier Division.

Lewes are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Canvey Island team who have won just one and drawn one over the same number of games.

In the last 10 years, Canvey Island and Lewes have played each other on seven occasions. Canvey Island won three of them, Lewes two, and they drew twice.

On average, the Gulls scored 2.1 goals and Lewes 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 22, when Lewes won 3-1 away.

With 33 goals in 20 games so far this season, Lewes are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Canvey Island, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The Gulls are 14th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn two, earning 29 points.

The away team are five places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

Canvey Island's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 loss against Haringey Borough.

Lewes drew 1-1 with Cray Wanderers last time out, also on Saturday.
