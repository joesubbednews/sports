Cheshunt v Carshalton Athletic

Isthmian Premier Division | The Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Carshalton Athletic visit Cheshunt at the Stadium on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Carshalton have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Cheshunt side who also won their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 64 goals in 35 games so far this season, Carshalton are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Cheshunt, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home team are 18th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

Athletic are 12 places ahead of Cheshunt in sixth, with 15 wins and 10 draws putting them on 55 points.

In the last five years, Cheshunt and Carshalton have played each other on five occasions. Cheshunt won one of them and Carshalton the other.

On average, Cheshunt scored 1.2 goals and Carshalton 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Cheshunt won 4-1 away.

Cheshunt's last match was on March 16, a 2-1 win against Concord Rangers.

Carshalton beat Margate 3-0 last time out, also on March 16.
