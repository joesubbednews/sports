Haringey Borough v Lewes

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Haringey Borough face Lewes at Coles Park on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-0 victory over Kingstonian on April 20, Haringey sit 20th in the Isthmian Premier Division.

They travel to play a Lewes side 11th in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Hashtag United.

Haringey are in bad form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Lewes's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Borough's three.

In the last 10 years, Haringey and Lewes have played each other on nine occasions. Haringey won two of them, Lewes six, and they drew once.

On average, Haringey scored 1.1 goals and Lewes 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Lewes won 4-1 at home.

With 31 goals in 40 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 82 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.
