Lewes v Horsham

Isthmian Premier Division | The Dripping Pan
Tuesday, 26 March 2024 @ 7.45pm

Lewes host Horsham on Tuesday at the Dripping Pan in the Isthmian Premier Division.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Lewes beat Concord Rangers 2-1 at home.

Horsham also won, 2-1 at home against Hastings United.

Horsham are third in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn seven, earning 61 points.

Lewes are six places behind the away side in ninth, with 15 wins and six draws putting them on 51 points.

In the last 10 years, Lewes and Horsham have played each other on seven occasions. Lewes won two of them, Horsham three, and they drew twice.

On average, Lewes scored 1.0 goal and Horsham 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 24, when Lewes won 3-1 away.

With 49 goals in 34 games so far this season, Horsham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Lewes are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Horsham's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Lewes's seven.
