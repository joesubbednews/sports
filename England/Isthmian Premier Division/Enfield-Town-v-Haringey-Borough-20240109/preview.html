Enfield Town v Haringey Borough

Isthmian Premier Division
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Haringey Borough face Enfield Town away on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Canvey Island on Saturday, Haringey sit 20th in Isthmian Premier Division.

They travel to play an Enfield side fifth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-0 against Cray Wanderers, on December 29.

With 23 goals in 23 games so far this season, Haringey are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Enfield, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Borough's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home side's 10.

In the last three years, Enfield and Haringey have played each other on five occasions. Enfield won two of them and they drew three times.

On average, Enfield scored 1.2 goals and Haringey 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 28, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
