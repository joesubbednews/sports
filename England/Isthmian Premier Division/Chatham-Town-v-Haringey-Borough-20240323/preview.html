Chatham Town v Haringey Borough

Isthmian Premier Division
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Haringey Borough face a challenge away against high-flying Chatham Town on Saturday.

Haringey Borough are 20th in the Isthmian Premier Division table, and have picked up five wins and nine draws in their 34 games to date.

Chatham, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 68 points, having won 21 and drawn five of their first 35 matches, and are 11 points behind table-toppers AFC Hornchurch.

With 27 goals in 34 games so far this season, Haringey are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Chatham, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Town are in good form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Borough's form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home side's 13.

Chatham's last match was on March 16, a 1-1 draw against Hastings United.

Haringey lost 4-0 against Cray Wanderers last time out, also on March 16.
