Hastings United v Chatham Town

Isthmian Premier Division | The Pilot Field
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chatham Town travel to Hastings United on Saturday in the Isthmian Premier Division.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Concord Rangers 2-1 at home.

Hastings, meanwhile, drew their last match, 1-1 against Billericay Town.

With 72 goals in 34 games so far this season, Chatham are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Hastings are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Town are second in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn four, earning 67 points.

United are six places behind the visitors in eighth, with 14 wins and seven draws putting them on 49 points.

The home team are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Chatham's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Hastings's 10.
