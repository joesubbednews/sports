Hastings United v Folkestone Invicta

Isthmian Premier Division | The Pilot Field
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Folkestone Invicta visit Hastings United at the Pilot Field on Monday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Folkestone Invicta have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Hastings side who also won their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

With 62 goals in 34 games so far this season, Hastings are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Folkestone Invicta, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The visitors are 15th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn nine, earning 45 points.

United are six places ahead of Folkestone Invicta in ninth, with 15 wins and eight draws putting them on 53 points.

In the last three years, Hastings and Folkestone Invicta have played each other on three occasions. Hastings won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Hastings won 5-0 away.

Hastings's last match was on Friday, a 5-0 win against Haringey Borough.

Folkestone Invicta beat Lewes 3-1 last time out, also on Friday.
