Whitehawk v Canvey Island

Isthmian Premier Division | The Enclosed Ground
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Canvey Island travel to the Enclosed Ground looking to secure a first win in nine Isthmian Premier Division games against Whitehawk on Saturday.

The Gulls have lost five and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Billericay Town on February 10.

They face a Whitehawk side who have won four and drawn three over that time.

Canvey Island are 17th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

Whitehawk are two places ahead of the Gulls in 15th, with 11 wins and 11 draws putting them on 44 points.

With 51 goals in 35 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 67 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The Hawks are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Whitehawk's last match was on March 23, a 4-0 loss against AFC Hornchurch.

Canvey Island drew 2-2 with Hashtag United last time out, also on March 23.
