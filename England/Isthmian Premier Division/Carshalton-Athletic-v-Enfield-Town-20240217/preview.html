Carshalton Athletic v Enfield Town

Isthmian Premier Division | War Memorial Sports Ground
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Carshalton Athletic host Enfield Town on Saturday at the War Memorial Sports Ground in the Isthmian Premier Division.

In their last league match, on February 10, Carshalton drew with Hashtag United 2-2 away.

Enfield won, 3-0 away at Canvey Island on February 3.

With 54 goals in 28 games so far this season, Enfield are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Carshalton are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Carshalton and Enfield have played each other on 11 occasions. Carshalton won five of them and Enfield six.

On average, Carshalton scored 1.6 goals and Enfield 1.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 23, when Carshalton won 3-0 away.

Athletic are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Town's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the home side's nine.

The visitors are sixth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

Carshalton are two places behind Enfield in eighth, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on 44 points.
