Horsham v Potters Bar Town

Isthmian Premier Division
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Horsham face Potters Bar Town on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian Premier Division games.

Horsham have lost two and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Folkestone Invicta on December 16.

They face a Potters Bar side who have won three and lost two over that time.

In the last 10 years, Horsham and Potters Bar have played each other on seven occasions. Horsham won five of them, Potters Bar one, and they drew once.

On average, Horsham scored 1.6 goals and Potters Bar 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 14, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Potters Bar are 15th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won seven and drawn six, earning 27 points.

Horsham are three places ahead of Town in 12th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

With 26 goals in 21 games so far this season, the home team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Horsham's last match was on January 16, a 1-1 draw against Enfield Town.

Potters Bar lost 3-0 against Lewes last time out, on January 13.
