Whitehawk v Cheshunt

Isthmian Premier Division | The Enclosed Ground
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Whitehawk welcome Cheshunt to the Enclosed Ground on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Whitehawk's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Isthmian Premier Division matches, while their opponents have earned three from a possible 18.

Cheshunt are 18th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won six and drawn four, earning 22 points.

Whitehawk are one place ahead of the visitors in 17th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

With 35 goals in 25 games so far this season, Cheshunt are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The Hawks are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Whitehawk's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 loss against Chatham Town.

Cheshunt lost 1-0 against AFC Hornchurch last time out, also on Saturday.
