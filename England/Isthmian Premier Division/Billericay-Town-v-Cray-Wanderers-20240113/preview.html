Billericay Town v Cray Wanderers

Isthmian Premier Division | AGP Arena
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Billericay Town host Cray Wanderers at the AGP Arena on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Isthmian Premier Division.

Billericay have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Cray side who lost their last match, and have collected six points from the last possible 18.

Billericay are fourth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn two, earning 41 points.

Cray are 12 places behind Town in 16th, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.

With 28 goals in 23 games so far this season, Wanderers are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Billericay and Cray have played each other on three occasions. Billericay won two of them and Cray one.

Their last meeting was on August 19, when Billericay won 1-0 away.

Billericay's last match was on January 6, a 3-1 win against Kingstonian.

Cray lost 2-1 against Bognor Regis Town last time out, on Tuesday.
