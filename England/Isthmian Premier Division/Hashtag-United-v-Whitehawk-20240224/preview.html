Hashtag United v Whitehawk

Isthmian Premier Division | Coles Park
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Whitehawk travel to Hashtag United on Saturday in the Isthmian Premier Division.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Folkestone Invicta 3-2 at home.

Hashtag, meanwhile, drew their last match, 0-0 against Wingate and Finchley.

With 40 goals in 27 games so far this season, Whitehawk are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Hashtag, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

United are in disappointing form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the Hawks's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home team's six.

The visitors are 15th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won nine and drawn nine, earning 36 points.

Hashtag are three places ahead of Whitehawk in 12th, with 10 wins and 11 draws putting them on 41 points.
