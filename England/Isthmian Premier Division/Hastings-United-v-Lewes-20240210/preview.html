Hastings United v Lewes

Isthmian Premier Division | The Pilot Field
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lewes make the journey to the Pilot Field to play Hastings United on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Lewes's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Isthmian Premier Division matches, while their opponents have earned 10 from a possible 18.

With 53 goals in 29 games so far this season, Hastings are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Lewes, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

United are fifth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

The visitors are six places behind the home team in 11th, with 12 wins and five draws putting them on 41 points.

In the last three years, Hastings and Lewes have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 26, when Lewes won 3-1 at home.

Hastings's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 loss against Horsham.

Lewes lost 3-0 against Billericay Town last time out, also on Tuesday.
