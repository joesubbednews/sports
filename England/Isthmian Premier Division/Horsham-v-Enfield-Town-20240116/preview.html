Horsham v Enfield Town

Isthmian Premier Division
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Enfield Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Horsham defence on Tuesday.

With 45 goals in 23 games, Enfield are the fourth-highest scorers in Isthmian Premier Division ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Horsham side who have scored 25 in 20 matches, but conceded only 24 goals, putting them third among the league's tightest defences – only AFC Hornchurch and Billericay Town have conceded fewer goals.

In the last 10 years, Horsham and Enfield have played each other on six occasions. They won three each.

On average, Horsham scored 2.7 goals and Enfield 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 8, when Horsham won 3-1 at home.

Enfield are fifth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn four, earning 43 points.

Horsham are seven places behind Town in 12th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Isthmian Premier Division, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Horsham's six.

Horsham's last match was on January 6, a 1-1 draw against AFC Hornchurch.

Enfield lost 2-0 against Folkestone Invicta last time out, on Saturday.
