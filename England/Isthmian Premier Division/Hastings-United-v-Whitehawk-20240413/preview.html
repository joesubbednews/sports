Hastings United v Whitehawk

Isthmian Premier Division | The Pilot Field
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hastings United host Whitehawk at the Pilot Field on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian Premier Division.

Hastings have picked up 11 points from their last six games, and they face a Hawks side who also won their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

In the last 10 years, Hastings and Whitehawk have played each other on six occasions. Hastings won four of them and they drew twice.

On average, Hastings scored 1.5 goals and the Hawks 0.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 15, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

With 65 goals in 36 games so far this season, Hastings are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Whitehawk, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.4 goals per game.

United are eighth in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn eight, earning 59 points.

The Hawks are six places behind the hosts in 14th, with 12 wins and 12 draws putting them on 48 points.

Hastings's last match was on April 6, a 1-0 win against Bognor Regis Town.

Whitehawk beat Carshalton Athletic 1-0 last time out, also on April 6.
