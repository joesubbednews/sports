Margate v Enfield Town

Isthmian Premier Division | Hartsdown Park
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Margate face a challenge against high-flying Enfield Town at Hartsdown Park on Saturday.

Margate are 19th in the Isthmian Premier Division table, and have picked up six wins and seven draws in their 34 games to date.

Enfield, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 57 points, having won 16 and drawn nine of their first 33 matches, and are 22 points behind table-toppers AFC Hornchurch.

With 36 goals in 34 games so far this season, Margate are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 68 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Enfield, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home team are in bad form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Margate's three.

In the last 10 years, Margate and Enfield have played each other on 13 occasions. Margate won two of them, Enfield nine, and they drew twice.

On average, Margate scored 0.7 goals and Enfield 2.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Enfield won 2-0 at home.

Margate's last match was on March 16, a 3-0 loss against Carshalton Athletic.

Enfield beat Horsham 2-1 last time out, also on March 16.
