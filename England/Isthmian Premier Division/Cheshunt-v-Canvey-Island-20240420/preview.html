Cheshunt v Canvey Island

Isthmian Premier Division | The Stadium
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cheshunt host Canvey Island on Saturday at the Stadium in the Isthmian Premier Division.

In their last league match, on April 13, Cheshunt lost to Billericay Town 2-1 away.

Canvey Island won, 4-1 at home against AFC Hornchurch on Tuesday.

Cheshunt are 18th in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn six, earning 36 points.

Canvey Island are one place ahead of the home team in 17th, with 14 wins and five draws putting them on 47 points.

With 50 goals in 37 games so far this season, Cheshunt are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The Gulls are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Cheshunt are in mixed form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.
