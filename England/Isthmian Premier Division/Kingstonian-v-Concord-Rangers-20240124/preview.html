Kingstonian v Concord Rangers

Isthmian Premier Division
Wednesday, 24 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Wednesday's late match sees the league's two bottom sides face each other, as Kingstonian host rock-bottom Concord Rangers.

Kingstonian have picked up 12 points from their first 12 Isthmian Premier Division games, with three wins and three draws.

That is the same number of points as the Beach Boys have collected, having also won three and drawn three.

Kingstonian are in terrible form in Isthmian Premier Division, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Concord's form is better – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' one.

With 16 goals in 18 games so far this season, Rangers are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Kingstonian are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Kingstonian's last match was on January 13, a 3-2 loss against Canvey Island.

Concord drew 1-1 with Margate last time out, also on January 13.
