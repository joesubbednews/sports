Folkestone Invicta v Concord Rangers

Isthmian Premier Division | The Fullicks Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Concord Rangers face Folkestone Invicta away at the Fullicks Stadium on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Billericay Town on Tuesday, the Beach Boys sit 20th in the Isthmian Premier Division.

They travel to play a Folkestone Invicta side 17th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-2 against Whitehawk, on February 17.

Folkestone Invicta are in disappointing form in the Isthmian Premier Division, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Concord's form is slightly better – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

With 25 goals in 25 games so far this season, Rangers are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Folkestone Invicta are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.
