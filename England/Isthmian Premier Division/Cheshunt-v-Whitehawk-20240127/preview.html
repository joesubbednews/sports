Cheshunt v Whitehawk

Isthmian Premier Division | The Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cheshunt welcome Whitehawk to the Stadium on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Cheshunt's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian Premier Division matches, while their opponents have earned six from a possible 18.

Cheshunt are 19th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn four, earning 19 points.

Whitehawk are five places ahead of the hosts in 14th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

With 31 goals in 22 games so far this season, Cheshunt are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The Hawks are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Cheshunt's last match was on January 13, a 3-2 loss against Chatham Town.

Whitehawk lost 5-1 against Wingate and Finchley last time out, also on January 13.
