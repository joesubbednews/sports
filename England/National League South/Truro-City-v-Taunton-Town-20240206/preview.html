Truro City v Taunton Town

National League South | Manadon Sports Hub
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Truro City host Taunton Town at Manadon Sports Hub on late Tuesday on the back of three consecutive wins in National League South.

Truro have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Taunton side who lost their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

In the last 10 years, Truro and Taunton have played each other on eight occasions. They won three each, and they drew twice.

On average, Truro scored 1.9 goals and Taunton 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 6 2022, when Truro won 1-0 at home.

Taunton are 20th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 10, earning 31 points.

Truro are two places ahead of Town in 18th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

With 31 goals in 26 games so far this season, the away side are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

City are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Truro's last match was on December 26, a 1-0 win against Torquay United.

Taunton lost 4-1 against Yeovil Town last time out, also on December 26.
