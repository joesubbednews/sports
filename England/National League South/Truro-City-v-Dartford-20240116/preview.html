Truro City v Dartford

National League South | Manadon Sports Hub
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Truro City host Dartford at Manadon Sports Hub on late Tuesday on the back of three consecutive wins in National League South.

Truro have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Darts side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

In the last 10 years, Truro and Dartford have played each other on eight occasions. Truro won four of them, Dartford three, and they drew once.

On average, Truro scored 1.9 goals and the Darts 2.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 30 2019, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 35 goals in 24 games so far this season, Truro are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.5 goals per game. But they are conceding at an average rate too, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Dartford are also average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The Darts are 16th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

City are one place ahead of the visitors in 15th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on the same number of points.

Truro's last match was on December 26, a 1-0 win against Torquay United.

Dartford lost 2-1 against Maidstone United last time out, on January 9.
