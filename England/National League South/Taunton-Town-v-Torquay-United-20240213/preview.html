Taunton Town v Torquay United

National League South | Cygnet Health Care Stadium
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Taunton Town host Torquay United on Tuesday at the Cygnet Health Care Stadium in the National League South.

In their last league match, on December 26, Taunton lost to Yeovil Town 4-1 away.

Torquay also lost, 1-0 at home against Truro City.

With 31 goals in 28 games so far this season, Taunton are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Torquay, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are 20th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 12, earning 33 points.

United are 11 places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with 14 wins and three draws putting them on 45 points.

Taunton are in disappointing form in the National League South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the Gulls' form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Taunton's five.
