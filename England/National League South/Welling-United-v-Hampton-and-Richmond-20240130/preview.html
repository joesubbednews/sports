Welling United v Hampton and Richmond

National League South | Park View Road
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Welling United face a challenge against high-flying Hampton and Richmond at Park View Road on Tuesday.

Welling United are 22nd in the National League South table, and have picked up four wins and nine draws in their 27 games to date.

Hampton & Richmond, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 46 points, having won 13 and drawn seven of their first 26 matches, and are 16 points behind table-toppers Yeovil Town.

In the last 10 years, Welling and Hampton & Richmond have played each other on 12 occasions. Welling won two of them, Hampton & Richmond seven, and they drew three times.

On average, the Wings scored 1.3 goals and Hampton & Richmond 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 25, when Welling won 3-0 at home.

With 30 goals in 27 games so far this season, Welling are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Hampton & Richmond, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

United are in bad form in National League South, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the Wings' four.

Welling's last match was on December 26, a 1-0 loss against Dartford.

Hampton & Richmond drew 3-3 with Slough Town last time out, also on December 26.
