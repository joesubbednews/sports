Truro City v Dover Athletic

National League South | Manadon Sports Hub
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Dover Athletic face Truro City away at Manadon Sports Hub on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Aveley on December 26, the Whites sit bottom of the National League South.

They travel to play a Truro side 18th in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Torquay United.

Truro are in fantastic form in the National League South, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Dover's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to City's 15.

With 29 goals in 31 games so far this season, Athletic are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.
