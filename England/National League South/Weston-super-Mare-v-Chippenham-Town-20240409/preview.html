Weston-super-Mare v Chippenham Town

National League South | Woodspring Stadium
Tuesday, 9 April 2024 @ 7.45pm

Weston-super-Mare welcome Chippenham Town to the Woodspring Stadium on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Weston-super-Mare's struggles have left them with nine points from their last six National League South matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

With 59 goals in 42 games so far this season, Chippenham are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Weston-super-Mare are also average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are 14th in the table after 41 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn seven, earning 52 points.

Town are three places ahead of Weston-super-Mare in 11th, with 16 wins and 13 draws putting them on 61 points.

Weston-super-Mare's last match was on December 26, a 3-2 loss against Weymouth.

Chippenham lost 2-1 against Bath City last time out, also on December 26.
