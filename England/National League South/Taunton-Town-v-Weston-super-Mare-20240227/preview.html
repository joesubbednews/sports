Taunton Town v Weston-super-Mare

National League South | Cygnet Health Care Stadium
Tuesday, 27 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Weston-super-Mare make the journey to the Cygnet Health Care Stadium to play Taunton Town on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Weston-super-Mare's struggles have left them with nine points from their last six National League South matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

With 31 goals in 29 games so far this season, Taunton are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Weston-super-Mare, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are 20th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 12, earning 33 points.

The visitors are four places ahead of the home team in 16th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

In the last five years, Taunton and Weston-super-Mare have played each other on five occasions. Taunton won two of them and Weston-super-Mare three.

On average, Taunton scored 1.6 goals and Weston-super-Mare 2.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 2 2022, when Taunton won 3-2 at home.

Taunton's last match was on December 26, a 4-1 loss against Yeovil Town.

Weston-super-Mare lost 3-2 against Weymouth last time out, also on December 26.
