Hartley Wintney v Marlow

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Memorial Playing Fields
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Marlow travel to Hartley Wintney on Monday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Corinthian-Casuals 4-1 at home.

Hartley Wintney, meanwhile, drew their last match, 2-2 against Metropolitan Police.

With 89 goals in 34 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Hartley Wintney are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The visitors are second in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 24 and drawn three, earning 75 points.

The home team are eight places behind Marlow in 10th, with 15 wins and six draws putting them on 51 points.

Hartley Wintney are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Marlow's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Hartley Wintney's five.
