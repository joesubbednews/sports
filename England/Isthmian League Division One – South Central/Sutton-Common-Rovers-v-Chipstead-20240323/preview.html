Sutton Common Rovers v Chipstead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

They travel to play a Sutton Common side 16th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Hanworth Villa.

Sutton Common are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Chipstead's form is slightly worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Rovers' six.

On average, Rovers scored 1.8 goals and Chipstead 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Sutton Common won 3-2 away.
