Hartley Wintney v Southall

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Memorial Playing Fields
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Southall visit Hartley Wintney at the Memorial Playing Fields on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Southall have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Hartley Wintney side who drew their last match, and have collected three points from the last possible 18.

With 73 goals in 37 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Hartley Wintney are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 23 and drawn five, earning 74 points.

The home side are six places behind Southall in 10th, with 15 wins and seven draws putting them on 52 points.

Hartley Wintney's last match was on April 13, a 1-1 draw against Raynes Park Vale.

Southall beat Uxbridge 3-2 last time out, also on April 13.
