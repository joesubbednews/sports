Marlow v Northwood

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alfred Davis Memorial Ground
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Marlow are on an excellent run ahead of hosting Northwood at the Alfred Davis Memorial Ground on Tuesday, with 16 points collected from their last six games.

Marlow have picked up five wins and one draw in their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central games, and face a Woods side whose last six games have brought three wins and three losses.

With 65 goals in 23 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Northwood, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The home side are fourth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn two, earning 50 points.

The Woods are 12 places behind Marlow in 16th, with seven wins and one draw putting them on 22 points.

In the last five years, Marlow and Northwood have played each other on five occasions. Marlow won three of them and Northwood two.

On average, Marlow scored 1.6 goals and the Woods 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when Marlow won 3-1 away.

Marlow's last match was on January 23, a 3-2 win against Hanworth Villa.

Northwood beat Uxbridge 3-0 last time out, on Saturday.
