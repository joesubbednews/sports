Ascot United v Corinthian-Casuals

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Corinthian-Casuals travel looking to secure a first win in 16 Isthmian League Division One – South Central games against Ascot United on Saturday.

Corinthian-Casuals have lost 11 and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Hanworth Villa on November 18.

They face an Ascot side who have won five and drawn four over that time.

Corinthian-Casuals are bottom of the table after 28 games, of which they have won three and drawn five, earning 14 points.

Ascot are eight places ahead of the away side in 13th, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

With 19 goals in 28 games so far this season, Corinthian-Casuals are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 67 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Ascot's last match was on February 10, a 1-0 win against Chipstead.

Corinthian-Casuals drew 1-1 with Southall last time out, on February 17.
