Hanworth Villa v Ascot United

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hanworth Villa welcome Ascot United on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Villa's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

With 35 goals in 27 games so far this season, Ascot are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Villa, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

United are 14th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

The home team are three places ahead of the visitors in 11th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last three years, Villa and Ascot have played each other on three occasions. Villa won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when Villa won 3-1 away.

Villa's last match was on Wednesday, a 4-1 loss against Southall.

Ascot drew 2-2 with Ashford Town Middlesex last time out, on Tuesday.
