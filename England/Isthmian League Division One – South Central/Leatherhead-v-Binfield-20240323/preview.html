Leatherhead v Binfield

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Fetcham Grove
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Binfield face a challenge away against high-flying Leatherhead at Fetcham Grove on Saturday.

Binfield are 20th in the Isthmian League Division One – South Central table, and have picked up just three wins and eight draws in their 30 games to date.

Leatherhead, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 68 points, having won 21 and drawn five of their first 32 matches, and are 10 points behind table-toppers Chertsey Town.

Leatherhead are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and four draws over that period, Binfield's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.

With 32 goals in 30 games so far this season, the away side are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 94 goals at a rate of 3.1 per game.

Leatherhead, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Leatherhead and Binfield have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Leatherhead won 2-0 away.

Leatherhead's last match was on March 16, a 4-1 win against Guernsey.

Binfield drew 0-0 with Marlow last time out, also on March 16.
