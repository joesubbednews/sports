Marlow v Chertsey Town

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alfred Davis Memorial Ground
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Two of Isthmian League Division One South Central's top sides face each other at Alfred Davis Memorial Ground in Tuesday's late kick-off, when fourth-placed Marlow host table-topping Chertsey Town.

Marlow have picked up 14 wins and two draws from 21 games so far this season, and sit eight points below the visitors going into the 7.45pm match.

The Curfews, meanwhile, have won 16 and drawn four, picking up 52 points.

With 58 goals in 21 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Chertsey are also above average scorers, with 2.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Marlow and Chertsey have played each other on six occasions. They won three each.

On average, Marlow scored 1.2 goals and the Curfews 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 18, when Marlow won 2-0 away.

The hosts are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Marlow's 10.

Marlow's last match was on Saturday, a 4-0 win against Metropolitan Police.

Chertsey beat Raynes Park Vale 4-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
