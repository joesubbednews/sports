Thatcham Town v South Park

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Waterside Park
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

South Park travel to Thatcham Town on Saturday in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

On average, Thatcham scored 2.2 goals and South Park 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when South Park won 5-1 at home.

Town are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

Thatcham are ninth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn four, earning 37 points.

South Park are one place behind Thatcham in 10th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.
