Uxbridge v Guernsey

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Honeycroft
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 1.30pm

Uxbridge host Guernsey on Saturday at Honeycroft in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

In their last league match, on March 16, Uxbridge lost to Chipstead 2-0 away.

Guernsey also lost, 4-1 at home against Leatherhead.

With 57 goals in 33 games so far this season, Uxbridge are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 59 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Guernsey are also average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The away team are 15th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won nine and drawn five, earning 32 points.

The Reds are three places ahead of Guernsey in 12th, with 13 wins and four draws putting them on 43 points.

In the last five years, Uxbridge and Guernsey have played each other on five occasions. Uxbridge won two of them, Guernsey one, and they drew twice.

On average, the Reds scored 1.4 goals and Guernsey 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Guernsey won 3-0 at home.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Guernsey's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Uxbridge's 10.
