Badshot Lea v Leatherhead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Badshot Lea host Leatherhead on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The home team are also average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Badshot Lea are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Leatherhead's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Badshot Lea's eight.
