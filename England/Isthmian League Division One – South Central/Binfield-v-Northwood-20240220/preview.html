Binfield v Northwood

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Binfield host Northwood on Tuesday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Binfield lost to South Park 2-1 away.

Northwood won, 4-1 at home against Thatcham Town.

Binfield are 20th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won three and drawn six, earning 15 points.

Northwood are four places ahead of the home side in 16th, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

In the last 10 years, Binfield and Northwood have played each other on seven occasions. Binfield won one of them, Northwood three, and they drew three times.

On average, Binfield scored 0.9 goals and the Woods 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 5, when Northwood won 1-0 at home.

With 29 goals in 27 games so far this season, Binfield are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 90 goals at a rate of 3.3 per game.

The Woods are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Binfield are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.
