Uxbridge v Ashford Town Middlesex

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Honeycroft
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Ashford Town Middlesex face Uxbridge away at Honeycroft on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Metropolitan Police on Tuesday, the Tangerines sit 18th in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

They travel to play an Uxbridge side 12th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 1-0 against Hanworth Villa, on February 17.

In the last 10 years, Uxbridge and Ashford Town Middlesex have played each other on seven occasions. Uxbridge won four of them, Ashford Town Middlesex two, and they drew once.

On average, the Reds scored 2.3 goals and the Tangerines 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Uxbridge won 2-1 away.

With 31 goals in 27 games so far this season, Ashford Town Middlesex are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Uxbridge, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The Reds are in bad form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

And also with a win and a draw over that period, the Tangerines's form is identical – they have both taken four points from 18.
