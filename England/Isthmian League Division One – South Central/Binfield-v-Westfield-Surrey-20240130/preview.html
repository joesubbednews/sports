Binfield v Westfield Surrey

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Binfield face Westfield Surrey on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Southall on Saturday, Binfield sit bottom of Isthmian League Division One South Central.

They travel to play a Westfield Surrey side sixth in the standings, who also won their last match, 5-4 against South Park.

Binfield are in disappointing form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Westfield Surrey's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

With 19 goals in 22 games so far this season, Binfield are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 81 goals at a rate of 3.7 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Binfield and Westfield Surrey have played each other on seven occasions. Binfield won two of them, Westfield Surrey three, and they drew twice.

On average, Binfield scored 1.4 goals and Westfield Surrey 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when Westfield Surrey won 7-2 at home.
