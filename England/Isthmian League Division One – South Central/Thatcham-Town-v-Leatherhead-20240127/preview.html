Thatcham Town v Leatherhead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Waterside Park
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Thatcham Town host Leatherhead on Saturday at Waterside Park in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

In their last league match, on January 20, Thatcham lost to Guernsey 3-1 away.

Leatherhead drew, 1-1 away at Hartley Wintney on Tuesday.

Leatherhead are fourth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn four, earning 49 points.

Thatcham are five places behind the visitors in ninth, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

With 41 goals in 23 games so far this season, Town are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Leatherhead, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Leatherhead's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Thatcham's eight.

In the last three years, Thatcham and Leatherhead have played each other on three occasions. Leatherhead won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Leatherhead won 5-0 at home.
