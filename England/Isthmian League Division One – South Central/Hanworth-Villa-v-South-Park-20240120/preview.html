Hanworth Villa v South Park

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

South Park face Hanworth Villa on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

South Park are unbeaten in seven, with three wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Villa team who have won four and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 43 goals in 21 games so far this season, Villa are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

South Park are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last three years, Villa and South Park have played each other on three occasions. Villa won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 14, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The visitors are 10th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won nine and drawn six, earning 33 points.

The home side are one place behind South Park in 11th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on the same number of points.

Villa's last match was on January 13, a 2-1 loss against Hartley Wintney.

South Park drew 1-1 with Uxbridge last time out, also on January 13.
