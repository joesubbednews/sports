Corinthian-Casuals v Thatcham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Corinthian-Casuals are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Thatcham Town on Saturday, with just two points collected from their last six games.

Corinthian-Casuals have picked up two draws and four losses in their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central games, and face a Thatcham side whose last six games have brought two wins and two draws.

Corinthian-Casuals are 20th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won three and drawn four, earning 13 points.

Thatcham are 11 places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

With 17 goals in 25 games so far this season, Corinthian-Casuals are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Corinthian-Casuals's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 loss against Raynes Park Vale.

Thatcham lost 2-0 against Leatherhead last time out, on January 27.
