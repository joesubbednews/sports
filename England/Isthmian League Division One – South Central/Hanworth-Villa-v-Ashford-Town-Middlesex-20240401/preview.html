Hanworth Villa v Ashford Town Middlesex

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ashford Town Middlesex make the journey to play Hanworth Villa on Monday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Ashford Town Middlesex's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central matches, while their opponents have earned 13 from a possible 18.

With 36 goals in 33 games so far this season, Ashford Town Middlesex are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 60 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Villa, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The Tangerines are 18th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won seven and drawn six, earning 27 points.

The home team are 11 places ahead of the visitors in seventh, with 14 wins and nine draws putting them on 51 points.

In the last three years, Villa and Ashford Town Middlesex have played each other on three occasions. Villa won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Villa won 5-3 away.

Villa's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Raynes Park Vale.

Ashford Town Middlesex lost 1-0 against Thatcham Town last time out, also on Saturday.
