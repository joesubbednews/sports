Southall v Metropolitan Police

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Robert Parker Stadium
Wednesday, 24 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Southall host Metropolitan Police at the Robert Parker Stadium on late Wednesday on the back of seven consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

It means Southall have picked up the maximum 21 points from their last seven games, and they face a Metropolitan Police side who have lost their last three matches, and collected seven points from the last possible 21.

With 53 goals in 23 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Metropolitan Police, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The hosts are second in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn three, earning 54 points.

The away side are 14 places behind Southall in 16th, with five wins and six draws putting them on 21 points.

Southall's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 win against Merthyr Town.

Metropolitan Police lost 2-1 against Binfield last time out, also on Saturday.
