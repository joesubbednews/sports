Uxbridge v Sutton Common Rovers

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Honeycroft
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sutton Common Rovers come to Honeycroft to play Uxbridge on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while the Reds have four wins and two losses.

With 45 goals in 33 games so far this season, Sutton Common are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Uxbridge, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Rovers are 15th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

The Reds are three places ahead of the away team in 12th, with 14 wins and four draws putting them on 46 points.

In the last five years, Uxbridge and Sutton Common have played each other on five occasions. Uxbridge won four of them and they drew once.

On average, the Reds scored 2.0 goals and Rovers 0.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Uxbridge won 2-1 away.

Uxbridge's last match was on March 23, a 2-0 win against Guernsey.

Sutton Common drew 2-0 with Chipstead last time out, also on March 23.
