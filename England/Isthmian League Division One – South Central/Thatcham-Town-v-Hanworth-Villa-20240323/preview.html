Thatcham Town v Hanworth Villa

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Waterside Park
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Thatcham Town host Hanworth Villa on Saturday at Waterside Park in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

In their last league match, on March 16, Thatcham drew with Hartley Wintney 2-2 away.

Villa won, 2-1 at home against Sutton Common Rovers.

With 57 goals in 30 games so far this season, Villa are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Thatcham are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home side's eight.

In the last three years, Thatcham and Villa have played each other on three occasions. Villa won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Thatcham are ninth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

Villa are one place behind Thatcham in 10th, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on 47 points.
