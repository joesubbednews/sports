Binfield v Southall

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Binfield face Southall on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Metropolitan Police on January 20, Binfield sit bottom of Isthmian League Division One South Central.

They travel to play a Southall side second in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Metropolitan Police, on Wednesday.

With 18 goals in 21 games so far this season, Binfield are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 81 goals at a rate of 3.9 per game.

Southall, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The hosts are in bad form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Binfield's three.

In the last three years, Binfield and Southall have played each other on three occasions. Southall won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Southall won 8-0 at home.
