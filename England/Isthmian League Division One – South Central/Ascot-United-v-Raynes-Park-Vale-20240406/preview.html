Ascot United v Raynes Park Vale

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Raynes Park Vale travel to Ascot United on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Sutton Common Rovers 2-1 away.

Ascot, meanwhile, drew their last match, 2-2 against Uxbridge.

Vale are fifth in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn six, earning 66 points.

Ascot are eight places behind the away team in 13th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

With 50 goals in 36 games so far this season, United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Vale, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, Vale's form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
