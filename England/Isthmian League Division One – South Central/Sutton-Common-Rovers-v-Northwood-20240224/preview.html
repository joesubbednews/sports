Sutton Common Rovers v Northwood

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Northwood travel to Sutton Common Rovers on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Thatcham Town 4-1 at home.

Rovers, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Hartley Wintney.

Sutton Common are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Northwood's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Rovers' five.

The Woods are 16th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won eight and drawn two, earning 26 points.

The hosts are two places ahead of the away side in 14th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 39 goals in 29 games so far this season, Sutton Common are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Northwood are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last five years, Sutton Common and Northwood have played each other on five occasions. Sutton Common won three of them and Northwood two.

On average, Rovers scored 2.4 goals and the Woods 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 28, when Northwood won 5-1 at home.
