Southall v Ascot United

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Robert Parker Stadium
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ascot United face Southall on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Ascot are unbeaten in six, with two wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Southall team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 65 goals in 30 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Ascot, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home side are third in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn five, earning 62 points.

United are 10 places behind Southall in 13th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last three years, Southall and Ascot have played each other on three occasions. Southall won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Southall won 3-1 away.

Southall's last match was on February 20, a 2-2 draw against Thatcham Town.

Ascot beat South Park 2-0 last time out, on March 2.
