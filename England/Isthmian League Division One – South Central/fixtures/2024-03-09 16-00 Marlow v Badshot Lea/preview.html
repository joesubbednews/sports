Marlow v Badshot Lea

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alfred Davis Memorial Ground
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Marlow host Badshot Lea in Saturday's match at the Alfred Davis Memorial Ground looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Marlow, who sit second in the league after 30 games, fell to a 4-2 home defeat to Guernsey on February 27.

They face a Badshot Lea side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Westfield Surrey, and who sit 11th in the table.

With 82 goals in 30 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Badshot Lea, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Marlow's 12.
