Westfield Surrey v Hanworth Villa

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hanworth Villa visit Westfield Surrey on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Villa have picked up 11 points from their last six games, and they face a Westfield Surrey side who also won their last match, and have collected 10 points from the last possible 18.

With 57 goals in 28 games so far this season, Westfield Surrey are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Villa are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The home team are sixth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn six, earning 54 points.

The visitors are two places behind Westfield Surrey in eighth, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on 44 points.

In the last three years, Westfield Surrey and Villa have played each other on three occasions. Villa won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Villa won 4-2 at home.

Westfield Surrey's last match was on February 24, a 1-0 win against Thatcham Town.

Villa beat Northwood 3-2 last time out, on Tuesday.
