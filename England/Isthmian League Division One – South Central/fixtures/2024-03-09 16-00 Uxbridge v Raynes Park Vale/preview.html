Uxbridge v Raynes Park Vale

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Honeycroft
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Raynes Park Vale travel to Uxbridge on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Leatherhead 1-0 at home.

The Reds, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-0 against Metropolitan Police.

With 50 goals in 29 games so far this season, Vale are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Uxbridge are also average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn five, earning 56 points.

The Reds are seven places behind Vale in 12th, with 12 wins and four draws putting them on 40 points.

The home team are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, Vale's form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
