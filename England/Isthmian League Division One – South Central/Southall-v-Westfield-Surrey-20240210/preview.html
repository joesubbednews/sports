Southall v Westfield Surrey

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Robert Parker Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Southall host Westfield Surrey in Saturday's match at the Robert Parker Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Southall, who sit second in the league after 27 games, fell to a 3-1 away defeat to South Park on February 3.

They face a Westfield Surrey side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against Northwood, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 59 goals in 27 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Westfield Surrey are also above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Southall's nine.

In the last three years, Southall and Westfield Surrey have played each other on three occasions. Southall won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Southall won 3-1 away.
