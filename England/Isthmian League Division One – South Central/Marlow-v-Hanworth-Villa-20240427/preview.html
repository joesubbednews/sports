Marlow v Hanworth Villa

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alfred Davis Memorial Ground
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hanworth Villa travel to Marlow on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Leatherhead 2-1 at home.

Marlow also won their last match, 2-1 against South Park.

With 97 goals in 37 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Villa are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home team are second in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 26 and drawn three, earning 81 points.

The visitors are seven places behind Marlow in ninth, with 15 wins and 10 draws putting them on 55 points.

Marlow are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Villa's form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.

In the last three years, Marlow and Villa have played each other on three occasions. Marlow won all of them.

Their last meeting was on January 23, when Marlow won 3-2 away.
