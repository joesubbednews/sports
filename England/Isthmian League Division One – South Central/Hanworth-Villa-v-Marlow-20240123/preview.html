Hanworth Villa v Marlow

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Marlow face Hanworth Villa on Tuesday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Marlow are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Villa team who have won two and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 62 goals in 22 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Villa are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn two, earning 47 points.

The home side are seven places behind Marlow in 11th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

Over the last two years, Villa and Marlow have played each other twice. Marlow won both of them.

Their last meeting was on March 11, when Marlow won 2-0 at home.

Villa's last match was on January 13, a 2-1 loss against Hartley Wintney.

Marlow beat Raynes Park Vale 4-1 last time out, on Saturday.
