Raynes Park Vale v Chipstead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Chipstead come to play Raynes Park Vale on late Tuesday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors are 18th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won six and drawn four, earning 22 points.

The home team are 12 places ahead of Chipstead in sixth, with 15 wins and five draws putting them on 50 points.

Vale's last match was on February 3, a 2-2 draw against Northwood.

Chipstead lost 1-0 against Ascot United last time out, on Saturday.
