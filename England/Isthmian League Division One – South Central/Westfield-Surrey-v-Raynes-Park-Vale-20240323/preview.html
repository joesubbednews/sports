Westfield Surrey v Raynes Park Vale

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Westfield Surrey host Raynes Park Vale on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Westfield Surrey have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Vale side who also won their last match, and have collected 10 points from the last possible 18.

With 61 goals in 30 games so far this season, Westfield Surrey are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Vale, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are fifth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn six, earning 60 points.

The away side are one place behind Westfield Surrey in sixth, with 18 wins and five draws putting them on 59 points.

Westfield Surrey's last match was on March 16, a 3-0 win against Ascot United.

Vale beat Southall 2-0 last time out, also on March 16.
