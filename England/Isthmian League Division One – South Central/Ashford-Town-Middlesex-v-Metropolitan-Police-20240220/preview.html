Ashford Town Middlesex v Metropolitan Police

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Robert Parker Stadium
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Ashford Town Middlesex are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Metropolitan Police at the Robert Parker Stadium on Tuesday, with just two points collected from their last six games.

The Tangerines have picked up two draws and four losses in their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central games, and face a Metropolitan Police side whose last six games have brought three wins and three losses.

Ashford Town Middlesex are 19th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won five and drawn six, earning 21 points.

Metropolitan Police are four places ahead of the Tangerines in 15th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 29 goals in 26 games so far this season, the home side are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Ashford Town Middlesex's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 loss against Raynes Park Vale.

Metropolitan Police beat Guernsey 2-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
