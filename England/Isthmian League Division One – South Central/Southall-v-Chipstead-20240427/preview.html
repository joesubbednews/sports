Southall v Chipstead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Robert Parker Stadium
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Southall face Chipstead on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Southall are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Chipstead team who have won one and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 44 goals in 38 games so far this season, Chipstead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 76 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Southall, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The away side are 19th in the table after 38 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

The hosts are 14 places ahead of Chipstead in fifth, with 23 wins and six draws putting them on 75 points.

In the last three years, Southall and Chipstead have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on January 9, when Southall won 1-0 away.

Southall's last match was on April 20, a 0-0 draw against Hartley Wintney.

Chipstead drew 0-0 with Thatcham Town last time out, also on April 20.
