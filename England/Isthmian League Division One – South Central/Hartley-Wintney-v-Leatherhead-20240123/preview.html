Hartley Wintney v Leatherhead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Memorial Playing Fields
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.30pm

Hartley Wintney face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Leatherhead defence on Tuesday.

With 46 goals in 23 games, Hartley Wintney are the fourth-highest scorers in Isthmian League Division One South Central ahead of the 7.30pm kick-off at the Memorial Playing Fields.

They face a Leatherhead side who have scored 36 in 22 matches, but conceded only 16 goals, putting them top of the league's tightest defences.

Leatherhead are third in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn three, earning 48 points.

Hartley Wintney are three places behind the visitors in sixth, with 13 wins and two draws putting them on 41 points.

The home team are in reasonable form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Leatherhead's form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Hartley Wintney's 12.

Hartley Wintney's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 win against Ascot United.

Leatherhead beat Northwood 2-1 last time out, on January 13.
