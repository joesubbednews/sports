Thatcham Town v Marlow

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Waterside Park
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Marlow travel to Thatcham Town on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Corinthian-Casuals 4-1 at home.

Thatcham, meanwhile, drew their last match, 1-1 against Chertsey Town.

With 89 goals in 34 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Thatcham are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The visitors are third in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 24 and drawn three, earning 75 points.

Town are five places behind Marlow in eighth, with 15 wins and seven draws putting them on 52 points.

The home team are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Marlow's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Thatcham's eight.

In the last five years, Thatcham and Marlow have played each other on five occasions. Thatcham won two of them, Marlow one, and they drew twice.

On average, Thatcham scored 0.8 goals and Marlow 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when Thatcham won 1-0 away.
