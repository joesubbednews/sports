Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

On average, Guernsey scored 1.2 goals and Thatcham 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Thatcham won 4-1 at home.

Guernsey are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.
