Corinthian-Casuals v Leatherhead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Corinthian-Casuals face a challenge against high-flying Leatherhead on Monday.

Corinthian-Casuals are rooted to the bottom of the Isthmian League Division One – South Central table, and have picked up just three wins and six draws in their 33 games to date.

Leatherhead, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 74 points, having won 23 and drawn five of their first 34 matches, and are eight points behind table-toppers Chertsey Town.

Corinthian-Casuals are in terrible form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Leatherhead's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to the hosts' two.

With 24 goals in 33 games so far this season, Corinthian-Casuals are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 84 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Corinthian-Casuals and Leatherhead have played each other on 10 occasions. Corinthian-Casuals won five of them, Leatherhead three, and they drew twice.

On average, Corinthian-Casuals scored 1.2 goals and Leatherhead 0.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Leatherhead won 1-0 at home.

Corinthian-Casuals's last match was on Saturday, a 4-1 loss against Marlow.

Leatherhead beat South Park 5-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
