Metropolitan Police v Chertsey Town

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Imber Court
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chertsey Town visit Metropolitan Police at Imber Court on Saturday on the back of six consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

It means Chertsey have picked up the maximum 18 points from their last six games, and they face a Metropolitan Police side who have also won their last two matches, and collected seven points from the last possible 18.

Chertsey are top of the table after 24 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn four, earning 61 points.

Metropolitan Police are 14 places behind Town in 15th, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.

With 68 goals in 24 games so far this season, the Curfews are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Metropolitan Police's last match was on January 27, a 2-0 win against Chipstead.

Chertsey beat Leatherhead 2-1 last time out, on Tuesday.
