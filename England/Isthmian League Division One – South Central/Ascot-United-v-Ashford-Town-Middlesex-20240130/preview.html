Ascot United v Ashford Town Middlesex

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Ashford Town Middlesex travel looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian League Division One – South Central games against Ascot United on late Tuesday.

The Tangerines have lost four and drawn one matches since they last earned three points – against Corinthian-Casuals on December 23.

They face an Ascot side who have won just one and drawn one over that time.

Ashford Town Middlesex are 18th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn five, earning 20 points.

Ascot are four places ahead of the Tangerines in 14th, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

With 26 goals in 22 games so far this season, the away team are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Ascot's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 draw against Sutton Common Rovers.

Ashford Town Middlesex lost 2-1 against Chertsey Town last time out, also on Saturday.
