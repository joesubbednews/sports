Guernsey v Leatherhead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Leatherhead travel to Guernsey on early Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Metropolitan Police 2-1 at home.

Guernsey also won their last match, 4-0 against Corinthian-Casuals.

Leatherhead are third in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn five, earning 65 points.

Guernsey are 11 places behind the away team in 14th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

With 49 goals in 31 games so far this season, Leatherhead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Guernsey are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Leatherhead's form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Guernsey's 12.

In the last three years, Guernsey and Leatherhead have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Leatherhead won 1-0 at home.
