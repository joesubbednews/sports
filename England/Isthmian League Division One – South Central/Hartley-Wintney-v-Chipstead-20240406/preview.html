Hartley Wintney v Chipstead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Memorial Playing Fields
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chipstead travel to Hartley Wintney on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to South Park 2-1 away.

Hartley Wintney, meanwhile, drew their last match, 2-2 against Metropolitan Police.

Chipstead are 19th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won seven and drawn five, earning 26 points.

Hartley Wintney are nine places ahead of the away team in 10th, with 15 wins and six draws putting them on 51 points.

With 42 goals in 35 games so far this season, Chipstead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 76 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Hartley Wintney are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

And also with a win and two draws over that period, Chipstead's form is identical – they have both taken five points from 18.
