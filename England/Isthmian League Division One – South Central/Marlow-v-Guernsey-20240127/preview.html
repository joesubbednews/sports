Marlow v Guernsey

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alfred Davis Memorial Ground
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Guernsey face a challenge away against high-flying Marlow at Alfred Davis Memorial Ground on Saturday.

Guernsey are 18th in the Isthmian League Division One – South Central table, and have picked up five wins and five draws in their 24 games to date.

Marlow, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 50 points, having won 16 and drawn two of their first 23 matches, and are five points behind table-toppers Chertsey Town.

With 65 goals in 23 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Guernsey, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Marlow's 16.

In the last five years, Marlow and Guernsey have played each other on five occasions. Marlow won three of them, Guernsey one, and they drew once.

On average, Marlow scored 2.2 goals and Guernsey 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Marlow won 1-0 away.

Marlow's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-2 win against Hanworth Villa.

Guernsey lost 3-2 against Raynes Park Vale last time out, on Wednesday.
