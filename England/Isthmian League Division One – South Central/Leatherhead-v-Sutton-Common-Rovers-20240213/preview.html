Leatherhead v Sutton Common Rovers

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Fetcham Grove
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Sutton Common Rovers travel to Leatherhead on late Tuesday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Westfield Surrey 1-1 away.

Leatherhead also won their last match, 4-0 against Uxbridge.

Leatherhead are fourth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn four, earning 55 points.

Sutton Common are 10 places behind the home team in 14th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 38 goals in 27 games so far this season, Rovers are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Leatherhead, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

Leatherhead are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.

In the last three years, Leatherhead and Sutton Common have played each other on three occasions. Leatherhead won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 4, when Leatherhead won 1-0 away.
