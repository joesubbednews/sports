Chertsey Town v Ascot United

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alwyns Lane Football Ground
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chertsey Town face Ascot United on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The Curfews are unbeaten in 19, with 14 wins and five draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face an Ascot team who have won six and drawn six over the same number of games.

Chertsey are top of the table after 35 games, of which they have won 26 and drawn eight, earning 86 points.

Ascot are 12 places behind Town in 13th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

With 89 goals in 35 games so far this season, the Curfews are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

United, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Chertsey's last match was on April 6, a 2-1 win against Hanworth Villa.

Ascot lost 3-2 against Raynes Park Vale last time out, also on April 6.
