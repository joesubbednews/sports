Badshot Lea v Leatherhead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Westfield Lane Stadium
Tuesday, 26 March 2024 @ 7.45pm

Leatherhead visit Badshot Lea at the Westfield Lane Stadium on late Tuesday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Leatherhead have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Badshot Lea side who have also won their last two matches, and collected 10 points from the last possible 18.

Leatherhead are third in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 22 and drawn five, earning 71 points.

Badshot Lea are seven places behind the away side in 10th, with 14 wins and seven draws putting them on 49 points.

With 54 goals in 31 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Leatherhead are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

Badshot Lea's last match was on Saturday, a 3-1 win against Corinthian-Casuals.

Leatherhead beat Binfield 2-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
