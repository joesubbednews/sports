Raynes Park Vale v Metropolitan Police

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Metropolitan Police travel to Raynes Park Vale on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Chertsey Town 1-0 at home.

Vale, meanwhile, drew their last match, 2-2 against Northwood.

With 30 goals in 27 games so far this season, Metropolitan Police are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Vale, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are sixth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn five, earning 50 points.

The visitors are nine places behind Vale in 15th, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.

Vale are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Metropolitan Police's form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.
