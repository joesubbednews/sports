South Park v Marlow

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Marlow travel for Saturday's match against South Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Marlow, who sit second in the league after 36 games, fell to a 2-1 home defeat to Westfield Surrey on April 13.

They face a South Park side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-0 victory against Northwood, and who sit seventh in the table.

With 95 goals in 36 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

South Park are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to South Park's 11.

In the last five years, South Park and Marlow have played each other on five occasions. South Park won three of them, Marlow one, and they drew once.

On average, South Park scored 2.4 goals and Marlow 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when they played out a 2-2 draw.
