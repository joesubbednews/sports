Raynes Park Vale v Corinthian-Casuals

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Raynes Park Vale and Corinthian-Casuals both come into Tuesday's late kick-off in poor form, having picked up four and two points respectively in their last six games.

Vale have won one and drawn one of the last six, while Corinthian-Casuals have taken two draws and four losses.

Corinthian-Casuals are 20th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won three and drawn four, earning 13 points.

Vale are 15 places ahead of the away side in fifth, with 14 wins and four draws putting them on 46 points.

With 17 goals in 24 games so far this season, Corinthian-Casuals are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 59 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Vale's last match was on Saturday, a 2-0 loss against Badshot Lea.

Corinthian-Casuals drew 1-1 with Hartley Wintney last time out, also on Saturday.
