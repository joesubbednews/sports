Raynes Park Vale v Marlow

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Marlow face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Raynes Park Vale defence on Saturday.

With 58 goals in 21 games, Marlow are the joint-top scorers in Isthmian League Division One South Central ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Vale side who have scored 39 in 21 matches, but conceded only 24 goals, putting them fourth among the league's tightest defences – only Chertsey Town, Southall and Leatherhead have conceded fewer goals.

Marlow are fourth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn two, earning 44 points.

Vale are one place behind the visitors in fifth, with 13 wins and four draws putting them on 43 points.

The home team are in disappointing form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Marlow's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Vale's six.

Over the last two years, Vale and Marlow have played each other twice. Marlow won both of them.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Marlow won 2-1 at home.

Vale's last match was on January 13, a 4-1 loss against Chertsey Town.

Marlow beat Metropolitan Police 4-0 last time out, also on January 13.
