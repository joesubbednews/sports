Binfield v Corinthian-Casuals

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match sees the league's two bottom sides face each other, as Binfield host rock-bottom Corinthian-Casuals.

Binfield have picked up 14 points from their first 14 Isthmian League Division One – South Central games, with three wins and five draws.

That is one point more than Corinthian-Casuals have collected, having won three and drawn four.

Binfield are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Corinthian-Casuals's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

With 18 goals in 26 games so far this season, the away side are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Binfield are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 3.4 goals per game.

Binfield's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-1 draw against Hanworth Villa.

Corinthian-Casuals lost 3-1 against Thatcham Town last time out, on February 3.
