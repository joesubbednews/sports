Chipstead v Hanworth Villa

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | High Road
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Chipstead face Hanworth Villa at High Road on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Hartley Wintney on April 6, Chipstead sit 19th in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

They travel to play a Villa side ninth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Chertsey Town.

With 44 goals in 36 games so far this season, Chipstead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 76 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Villa, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last three years, Chipstead and Villa have played each other on three occasions. Chipstead won one of them and Villa the other.

Their last meeting was on December 23, when Villa won 3-1 at home.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Chipstead's eight.
