Sutton Common Rovers v South Park

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Wednesday, 31 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Sutton Common Rovers face South Park on late Wednesday looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian League Division One – South Central games.

Rovers have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Raynes Park Vale on December 26.

They face a South Park side who have won one and drawn three over that time.

With 34 goals in 25 games so far this season, Sutton Common are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

South Park, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Rovers are 15th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won seven and drawn five, earning 26 points.

The visitors are five places ahead of the home side in 10th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last five years, Sutton Common and South Park have played each other on five occasions. Sutton Common won one of them and South Park the other.

On average, Rovers scored 1.6 goals and South Park 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when South Park won 2-1 at home.

Sutton Common's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 draw against Ascot United.

South Park lost 5-4 against Westfield Surrey last time out, also on Saturday.
