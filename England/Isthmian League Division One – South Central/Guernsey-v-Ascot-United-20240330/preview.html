Guernsey v Ascot United

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Guernsey host Ascot United on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

In their last league match, on March 23, Guernsey lost to Uxbridge 2-0 away.

Ascot also lost, 2-1 away at Marlow.

Guernsey are 16th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won nine and drawn five, earning 32 points.

Ascot are three places ahead of the hosts in 13th, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

With 44 goals in 34 games so far this season, United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Guernsey, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Guernsey are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home side's nine.
