South Park v Metropolitan Police

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Metropolitan Police make the journey to play South Park on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Metropolitan Police's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

With 33 goals in 31 games so far this season, Metropolitan Police are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

South Park, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The visitors are 17th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

The home team are 10 places ahead of Metropolitan Police in seventh, with 14 wins and eight draws putting them on 50 points.

South Park's last match was on March 16, a 1-0 win against Ashford Town Middlesex.

Metropolitan Police lost 2-1 against Leatherhead last time out, on March 9.
