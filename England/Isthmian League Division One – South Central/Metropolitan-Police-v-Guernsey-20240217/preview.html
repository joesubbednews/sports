Metropolitan Police v Guernsey

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Imber Court
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Metropolitan Police host Guernsey on Saturday at Imber Court in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

In their last league match, on February 3, Metropolitan Police lost to Chertsey Town 1-0 at home.

Guernsey won, 3-1 away at Ashford Town Middlesex.

Guernsey are 16th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won seven and drawn five, earning 26 points.

Metropolitan Police are one place ahead of the visitors in 15th, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.

With 30 goals in 27 games so far this season, the home team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Guernsey are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Metropolitan Police are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Guernsey's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Metropolitan Police's six.
