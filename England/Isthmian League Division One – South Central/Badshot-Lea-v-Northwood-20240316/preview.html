Badshot Lea v Northwood

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Badshot Lea face Northwood on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian League Division One – South Central games.

Badshot Lea have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Raynes Park Vale on January 27.

They face a Northwood side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

With 43 goals in 27 games so far this season, Badshot Lea are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Northwood, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The Woods are 15th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won nine and drawn four, earning 31 points.

The home team are three places ahead of the visitors in 12th, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

Badshot Lea's last match was on March 9, a 1-0 loss against Marlow.

Northwood drew 0-0 with Chertsey Town last time out, also on March 9.
