Marlow v Corinthian-Casuals

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alfred Davis Memorial Ground
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Corinthian-Casuals face a challenge away against high-flying Marlow at the Alfred Davis Memorial Ground on Saturday.

Corinthian-Casuals are rooted to the bottom of the Isthmian League Division One – South Central table, and have picked up just three wins and six draws in their 32 games to date.

Marlow, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 72 points, having won 23 and drawn three of their first 33 matches, and are nine points behind table-toppers Chertsey Town.

With 85 goals in 33 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Corinthian-Casuals, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to Marlow's 10.

Marlow's last match was on March 23, a 2-1 win against Ascot United.

Corinthian-Casuals lost 3-1 against Badshot Lea last time out, also on March 23.
