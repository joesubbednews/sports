Chertsey Town v South Park

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alwyns Lane Football Ground
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chertsey Town host South Park at the Alwyns Lane Football Ground on Saturday on the back of 10 consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

It means Chertsey have picked up the maximum 30 points from their last 10 games, and they face a South Park side who have also won their last two matches, and collected 16 points from the last possible 30.

Chertsey are top of the table after 28 games, of which they have won 23 and drawn four, earning 73 points.

South Park are eight places behind Town in ninth, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

With 74 goals in 28 games so far this season, the Curfews are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

The visitors are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Chertsey and South Park have played each other on six occasions. Chertsey won four of them and they drew twice.

On average, the Curfews scored 3.3 goals and South Park 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Chertsey won 4-0 away.

Chertsey's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-0 win against Marlow.

South Park beat Binfield 2-1 last time out, on February 17.
