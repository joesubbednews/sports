Chertsey Town v Badshot Lea

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alwyns Lane Football Ground
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chertsey Town host Badshot Lea at the Alwyns Lane Football Ground on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Chertsey have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Badshot Lea side who lost their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

Chertsey are top of the table after 37 games, of which they have won 28 and drawn eight, earning 92 points.

Badshot Lea are 11 places behind Town in 12th, with 14 wins and eight draws putting them on 50 points.

With 94 goals in 37 games so far this season, the Curfews are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Chertsey's last match was on April 20, a 2-0 win against Sutton Common Rovers.

Badshot Lea lost 4-0 against Westfield Surrey last time out, on April 16.
