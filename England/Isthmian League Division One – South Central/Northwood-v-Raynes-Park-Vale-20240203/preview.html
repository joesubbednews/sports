Northwood v Raynes Park Vale

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Northwood Park
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Northwood host Raynes Park Vale on Saturday at Northwood Park in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

In their last league match, on Tuesday, Northwood lost to Marlow 4-1 away.

Vale won, 2-0 at home against Corinthian-Casuals.

With 32 goals in 22 games so far this season, Northwood are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Vale, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The Woods are 17th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won seven and drawn one, earning 22 points.

The visitors are 12 places ahead of the home side in fifth, with 15 wins and four draws putting them on 49 points.

Northwood are in disappointing form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

And also with two wins and four losses over that period, Vale's form is identical – they have both taken six points from 18.
