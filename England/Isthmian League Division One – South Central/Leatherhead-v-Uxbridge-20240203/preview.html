Leatherhead v Uxbridge

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Fetcham Grove
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leatherhead host Uxbridge in Saturday's match at Fetcham Grove looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Leatherhead, who sit fourth in the league after 25 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to Chertsey Town on Tuesday.

They face an Uxbridge side who also lost their last match, a 3-0 defeat to Northwood, and who sit 12th in the table.

Leatherhead are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Uxbridge's form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

With 40 goals in 25 games so far this season, Leatherhead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

The Reds are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last three years, Leatherhead and Uxbridge have played each other on three occasions. Leatherhead won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when Leatherhead won 3-1 away.
