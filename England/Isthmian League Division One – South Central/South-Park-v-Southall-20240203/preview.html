South Park v Southall

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

South Park face Southall on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian League Division One – South Central games.

South Park have lost one and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Chipstead on December 26.

They face a Southall side who have won three and lost two over that time.

With 58 goals in 26 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

South Park are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The visitors are second in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn three, earning 57 points.

The home team are eight places behind Southall in 10th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last three years, South Park and Southall have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when Southall won 3-2 at home.

South Park's last match was on Wednesday, a 3-0 draw against Sutton Common Rovers.

Southall beat Hanworth Villa 4-1 last time out, also on Wednesday.
