Thatcham Town v Hartley Wintney

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Waterside Park
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Thatcham Town host Hartley Wintney on Tuesday at Waterside Park in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Thatcham beat South Park 3-0 at home.

Hartley Wintney lost, 5-0 away at Uxbridge.

With 52 goals in 28 games so far this season, Hartley Wintney are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Thatcham are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The visitors are seventh in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

Town are one place behind Hartley Wintney in eighth, with 12 wins and four draws putting them on 40 points.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Hartley Wintney's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Thatcham's 12.
