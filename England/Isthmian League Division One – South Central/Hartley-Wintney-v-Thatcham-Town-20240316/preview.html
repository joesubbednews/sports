Hartley Wintney v Thatcham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Memorial Playing Fields
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hartley Wintney host Thatcham Town on Saturday at the Memorial Playing Fields in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

In their last league match, on March 9, Hartley Wintney lost to South Park 6-2 away.

Thatcham won, 3-2 at home against Binfield.

With 56 goals in 31 games so far this season, Hartley Wintney are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Thatcham are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home team are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Hartley Wintney's six.

Hartley Wintney are seventh in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn four, earning 49 points.

The visitors are two places behind the hosts in ninth, with 14 wins and five draws putting them on 47 points.
