Ascot United v Westfield Surrey

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ascot United face Westfield Surrey on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Ascot are unbeaten in seven, with three wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Westfield Surrey team who have won four and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 58 goals in 29 games so far this season, Westfield Surrey are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Ascot, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The away side are fifth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn six, earning 57 points.

United are eight places behind Westfield Surrey in 13th, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

Over the last two years, Ascot and Westfield Surrey have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Westfield Surrey won 1-0 at home.

Ascot's last match was on March 9, a 1-0 win against Southall.

Westfield Surrey beat Hanworth Villa 1-0 last time out, also on March 9.
