Binfield v Corinthian-Casuals

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Tuesday, 16 April 2024 @ 7.45pm

Tuesday's late match sees the league's two bottom sides face each other, as Binfield host rock-bottom Corinthian-Casuals.

Binfield have picked up 24 points from their first 24 Isthmian League Division One – South Central games, with five wins and nine draws.

That is six points more than Corinthian-Casuals have collected, having won four and drawn six.

Binfield are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Corinthian-Casuals's form is worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.

With 28 goals in 36 games so far this season, the away side are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 90 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Binfield are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.9 goals per game.

Binfield's last match was on Saturday, a 3-3 draw against Guernsey.

Corinthian-Casuals lost 2-0 against Ashford Town Middlesex last time out, also on Saturday.
