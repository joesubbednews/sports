Binfield v Marlow

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Binfield face a challenge against high-flying Marlow on Saturday.

Binfield are 20th in the Isthmian League Division One – South Central table, and have picked up three wins and seven draws in their 29 games to date.

Marlow, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 68 points, having won 22 and drawn two of their first 31 matches, and are seven points behind table-toppers Chertsey Town.

With 83 goals in 31 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Binfield, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 3.2 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Binfield and Marlow have played each other on six occasions. Binfield won one of them, Marlow three, and they drew twice.

On average, Binfield scored 1.3 goals and Marlow 2.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Marlow won 8-1 at home.

The home team are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Binfield's six.

Binfield's last match was on March 9, a 3-2 loss against Thatcham Town.

Marlow beat Badshot Lea 1-0 last time out, also on March 9.
