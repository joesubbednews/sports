Badshot Lea v Westfield Surrey

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Westfield Lane Stadium
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Westfield Surrey visit Badshot Lea at the Westfield Lane Stadium on late Tuesday on the back of five consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Badshot Lea are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The visitors are seventh in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn four, earning 40 points.

The home team are one place behind Westfield Surrey in eighth, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

Badshot Lea's last match was on January 13, a 4-2 win against Guernsey.
