Northwood v South Park

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Northwood Park
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

South Park travel to Northwood on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Badshot Lea 2-2 at home.

The Woods, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Corinthian-Casuals.

With 60 goals in 34 games so far this season, Northwood are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

South Park are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

On average, the Woods scored 1.0 goal and South Park 2.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 23, when South Park won 2-0 at home.

The visitors are seventh in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn 10, earning 55 points.

The Woods are seven places behind South Park in 14th, with 11 wins and four draws putting them on 37 points.

The home side are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, South Park's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Northwood's seven.
