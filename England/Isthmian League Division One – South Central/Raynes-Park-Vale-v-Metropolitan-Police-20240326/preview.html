Raynes Park Vale v Metropolitan Police

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Tuesday, 26 March 2024 @ 7.45pm

Metropolitan Police come to play Raynes Park Vale on late Tuesday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Vale have four wins and two losses.

Vale are fifth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn five, earning 62 points.

Metropolitan Police are 12 places behind the home team in 17th, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.

With 33 goals in 32 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Vale, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Vale's last match was on Saturday, a 3-0 win against Westfield Surrey.

Metropolitan Police drew 0-0 with South Park last time out, also on Saturday.
