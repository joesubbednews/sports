Northwood v Thatcham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Northwood Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Thatcham Town visit Northwood at Northwood Park on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Thatcham have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Woods side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 35 goals in 24 games so far this season, Northwood are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Thatcham, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The Woods are 17th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won seven and drawn two, earning 23 points.

Town are nine places ahead of the hosts in eighth, with 13 wins and four draws putting them on 43 points.

In the last five years, Northwood and Thatcham have played each other on five occasions. Northwood won one of them, Thatcham three, and they drew once.

On average, the Woods scored 0.8 goals and Thatcham 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Thatcham won 2-0 at home.

Northwood's last match was on February 6, a 2-1 loss against Westfield Surrey.

Thatcham beat Hartley Wintney 2-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
