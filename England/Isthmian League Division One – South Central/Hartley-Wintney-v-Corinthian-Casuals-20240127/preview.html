Hartley Wintney v Corinthian-Casuals

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Memorial Playing Fields
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Corinthian-Casuals make the journey to the Memorial Playing Fields to play Hartley Wintney on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their six-game losing streak.

Corinthian-Casuals's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central matches, while their opponents have earned 10 from a possible 18.

Corinthian-Casuals are 20th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won three and drawn three, earning 12 points.

Hartley Wintney are 14 places ahead of the visitors in sixth, with 13 wins and three draws putting them on 42 points.

With 16 goals in 23 games so far this season, Corinthian-Casuals are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The home side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Hartley Wintney and Corinthian-Casuals have played each other twice. Hartley Wintney won both of them.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Hartley Wintney won 3-0 away.

Hartley Wintney's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-1 draw against Leatherhead.

Corinthian-Casuals lost 2-0 against Merthyr Town last time out, on January 13.
