Marlow v Chipstead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alfred Davis Memorial Ground
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Marlow host Chipstead in Saturday's match at the Alfred Davis Memorial Ground looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Marlow, who sit second in the league after 28 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Chertsey Town on Tuesday.

They face a Chipstead side who also lost their last match, a 2-1 defeat to Chertsey Town, and who sit 19th in the table.

With 76 goals in 28 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Chipstead, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The home team are in fantastic form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to Marlow's 15.

In the last five years, Marlow and Chipstead have played each other on five occasions. Marlow won three of them and they drew twice.

On average, Marlow scored 1.6 goals and Chipstead 0.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 6, when Marlow won 2-1 away.
