Hartley Wintney v Sutton Common Rovers

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Memorial Playing Fields
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hartley Wintney welcome Sutton Common Rovers to the Memorial Playing Fields on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Hartley Wintney's struggles have left them with five points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central matches, while their opponents have earned six from a possible 18.

With 38 goals in 28 games so far this season, Sutton Common are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Hartley Wintney, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Hartley Wintney and Sutton Common have played each other on eight occasions. Hartley Wintney won four of them, Sutton Common two, and they drew twice.

On average, Hartley Wintney scored 1.8 goals and Rovers 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Hartley Wintney won 5-1 away.

The hosts are seventh in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

Rovers are seven places behind Hartley Wintney in 14th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Hartley Wintney's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 loss against Thatcham Town.

Sutton Common lost 1-0 against Leatherhead last time out, also on Tuesday.
