Marlow v Westfield Surrey

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | Alfred Davis Memorial Ground
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Marlow and Westfield Surrey promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Isthmian League Division One South Central's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at the Alfred Davis Memorial Ground, Marlow and Westfield Surrey sit top and fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 94 and 66 goals respectively.

Marlow are second in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 25 and drawn three, earning 78 points.

Westfield Surrey are four places behind the hosts in sixth, with 20 wins and seven draws putting them on 67 points.

In the last 10 years, Marlow and Westfield Surrey have played each other on eight occasions. Marlow won four of them, Westfield Surrey three, and they drew once.

On average, Marlow scored 2.2 goals and Westfield Surrey 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 23, when Westfield Surrey won 3-2 at home.

Marlow are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with four wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is identical – they have both taken 13 points from 18.

Marlow's last match was on April 6, a 5-4 win against Thatcham Town.

Westfield Surrey beat Guernsey 2-1 last time out, also on April 6.
