Chipstead v Marlow

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | High Road
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Chipstead face a challenge against high-flying Marlow at High Road on Tuesday.

Chipstead are 18th in the Isthmian League Division One – South Central table, and have picked up six wins and four draws in their 25 games to date.

Marlow, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 53 points, having won 17 and drawn two of their first 24 matches, and are 11 points behind table-toppers Chertsey Town.

With 69 goals in 24 games so far this season, Marlow are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Chipstead, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Chipstead's five.

In the last five years, Chipstead and Marlow have played each other on four occasions. Marlow won two of them and they drew twice.

On average, Chipstead scored 0.5 goals and Marlow 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 4, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Chipstead's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Badshot Lea.

Marlow beat Northwood 4-1 last time out, on January 30.
