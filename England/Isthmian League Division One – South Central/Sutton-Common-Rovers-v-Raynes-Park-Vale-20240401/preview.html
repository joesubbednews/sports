Sutton Common Rovers v Raynes Park Vale

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sutton Common Rovers are on a poor run ahead of hosting Raynes Park Vale on Monday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Rovers have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central games, and face a Vale side whose last six games have brought three wins and one draw.

Vale are fifth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn six, earning 63 points.

Sutton Common are 10 places behind the visitors in 15th, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

With 45 goals in 34 games so far this season, Rovers are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Vale, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Sutton Common's last match was on Saturday, a 4-0 loss against Uxbridge.

Vale drew 0-0 with Hanworth Villa last time out, also on Saturday.
