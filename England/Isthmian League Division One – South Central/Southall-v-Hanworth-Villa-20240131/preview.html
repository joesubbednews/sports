Southall v Hanworth Villa

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Robert Parker Stadium
Wednesday, 31 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Southall host Hanworth Villa in Wednesday's late match at the Robert Parker Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One South Central.

Southall, who sit second in the league after 25 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Binfield on Saturday.

They face a Villa side who also lost their last match, a 3-2 defeat to Marlow, and who sit 11th in the table.

With 54 goals in 25 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Villa are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Southall and Villa have played each other on five occasions. Southall won one of them, Villa three, and they drew once.

On average, Southall scored 1.0 goal and Villa 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when they played out a 3-3 draw.

The hosts are in reasonable form in Isthmian League Division One South Central, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Southall's 12.
