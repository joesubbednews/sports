Southall v Northwood

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Robert Parker Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Northwood travel to Southall on Monday in the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Hartley Wintney 3-0 at home.

Southall also won their last match, 2-0 against Badshot Lea.

With 67 goals in 34 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Northwood are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The home side are fourth in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn five, earning 65 points.

The Woods are 10 places behind Southall in 14th, with 11 wins and four draws putting them on 37 points.

Southall are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

In the last three years, Southall and Northwood have played each other on three occasions. Southall won one of them and Northwood the other.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Southall won 2-1 away.
