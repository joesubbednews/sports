Guernsey v Northwood

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Northwood make the journey to play Guernsey on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Northwood's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South Central matches, while their opponents have earned four from a possible 18.

Guernsey are 16th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won nine and drawn six, earning 33 points.

Northwood are one place ahead of the hosts in 15th, with 11 wins and four draws putting them on 37 points.

With 60 goals in 35 games so far this season, the Woods are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Guernsey are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

In the last five years, Guernsey and Northwood have played each other on five occasions. Guernsey won one of them, Northwood three, and they drew once.

On average, Guernsey scored 1.2 goals and the Woods 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when Northwood won 2-1 at home.

Guernsey's last match was on April 13, a 3-3 draw against Binfield.

Northwood lost 3-0 against South Park last time out, also on April 13.
