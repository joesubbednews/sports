Southall v Leatherhead

Isthmian League Division One – South Central | The Robert Parker Stadium
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Isthmian League Division One South Central's top sides face each other at the Robert Parker Stadium in Saturday's kick-off, when fourth-placed Southall host second-placed Leatherhead.

Southall have picked up 21 wins and five draws from 35 games so far this season, and sit nine points below the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Leatherhead, meanwhile, have won 24 and drawn five, picking up 77 points.

With 69 goals in 35 games so far this season, Southall are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Leatherhead are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Southall's seven.

In the last five years, Southall and Leatherhead have played each other on four occasions. Southall won one of them, Leatherhead two, and they drew once.

On average, Southall scored 0.8 goals and Leatherhead 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 23, when Leatherhead won 3-1 away.

Southall's last match was on Monday, a 2-1 win against Northwood.

Leatherhead beat Corinthian-Casuals 3-2 last time out, also on Monday.
