Ascot United v Uxbridge

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Uxbridge come to play Ascot United on Monday in great form, having collected 15 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and lost one of their last six fixtures, while Ascot have three wins and a draw.

With 48 goals in 35 games so far this season, Ascot are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Uxbridge, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

United are 13th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

The Reds are two places ahead of the home side in 11th, with 15 wins and four draws putting them on 49 points.

Ascot's last match was on Saturday, a 4-2 win against Guernsey.

Uxbridge beat Sutton Common Rovers 4-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
