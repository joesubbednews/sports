Hanworth Villa v Binfield

Isthmian League Division One – South Central
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Binfield face Hanworth Villa away on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-1 victory over Hartley Wintney on Saturday, Binfield sit bottom of the Isthmian League Division One South Central.

They travel to play a Villa side 11th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 2-2 against Ascot United.

With 25 goals in 24 games so far this season, Binfield are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 85 goals at a rate of 3.5 per game.

Villa, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Isthmian League Division One South Central, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Villa's four.

In the last three years, Villa and Binfield have played each other on three occasions. Villa won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 23, when Villa won 3-0 away.
