Doncaster Belles Women v Stockport County Women

Womens National League – Division One North
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Doncaster Belles Women face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Stockport County Women defence on Sunday.

With 18 goals in 10 games, Doncaster Belles are the fourth-highest scorers in Womens National League Division One North ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a Stockport County side who have scored 14 in 11 matches, but conceded only 10 goals, putting them fourth among the league's tightest defences – only Barnsley Women, Middlesbrough Ladies and Durham Cestria WFC have conceded fewer goals.

Doncaster Belles are ninth in the table after 10 games, of which they have won three and drawn one, earning 10 points.

Stockport County are four places ahead of the home team in fifth, with four wins and four draws putting them on 16 points.

Doncaster Belles are in disappointing form in Womens National League Division One North, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Doncaster Belles's six.

Doncaster Belles's last match was on January 7, a 4-2 loss against Leeds Women.

Stockport County drew 0-0 with Durham Cestria WFC last time out, also on January 7.
