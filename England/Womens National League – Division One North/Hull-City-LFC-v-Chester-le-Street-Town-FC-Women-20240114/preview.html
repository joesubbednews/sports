Hull City LFC v Chester-le-Street Town FC Women

Womens National League – Division One North
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Hull City LFC host Chester-le-Street Town FC Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Womens National League Division One North.

Hull City, who sit second in the league after 10 games, fell to a 2-0 away defeat to Middlesbrough Women on January 7.

They face a Chester-le-Street Town FC side who also lost their last match, a 5-3 defeat to Barnsley Women, and who sit 11th in the table.

Hull City are in mixed form in Womens National League Division One North, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Chester-le-Street Town FC's form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

With 27 goals in 10 games so far this season, Hull City are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.7 goals per game.
