United of Manchester Women v York City LFC

Womens National League – Division One North
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table United of Manchester Women host 10th-placed York City LFC.

United of Manchester have picked up four points from their first four Womens National League – Division One North games, with one win and one draw.

That is three points less than York City have collected, having won two and drawn one.

United of Manchester are in awful form in Womens National League Division One North, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, York City's form is much better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' zero.

With eight goals in 11 games so far this season, United of Manchester are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 4.1 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

United of Manchester's last match was on December 17, a 6-0 loss against Barnsley Women.

York City lost 3-2 against Norton and Stockton last time out, on November 26.
