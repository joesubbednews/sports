Barnsley Women v Durham Cestria WFC

Womens National League – Division One North
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Womens National League Division One North's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when Barnsley Women host Durham Cestria WFC.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Barnsley to date: the home side have let in just six goals in 10 games.

Durham Cestria WFC have conceded seven goals in 10 games, giving them the third tightest back line so far this season.

Barnsley are top of the table after 10 games, of which they have won nine and drawn one, earning 28 points.

Durham Cestria WFC are five places behind the home side in sixth, with four wins and two draws putting them on 14 points.

Barnsley are in fantastic form in Womens National League Division One North, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Barnsley's 16.

Barnsley's last match was on January 7, a 5-3 win against Chester-le-Street Town FC Women.

Durham Cestria WFC drew 0-0 with Stockport County Women last time out, also on January 7.
