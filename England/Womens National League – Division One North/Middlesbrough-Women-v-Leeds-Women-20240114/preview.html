Middlesbrough Women v Leeds Women

Womens National League – Division One North
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Womens National League Division One North's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when third-placed Middlesbrough Women host fourth-placed Leeds Women.

Middlesbrough have picked up six wins and four draws from 11 games so far this season, and sit three points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Leeds, meanwhile, have won six and drawn one of 10, picking up 19 points.

With 21 goals in 10 games so far this season, Leeds are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Middlesbrough, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.5 goals per game.

The home side are in good form in Womens National League Division One North, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Middlesbrough's 14.

Middlesbrough's last match was on Sunday, a 2-0 win against Hull City LFC.

Leeds beat Doncaster Belles Women 4-2 last time out, also on January 7.
