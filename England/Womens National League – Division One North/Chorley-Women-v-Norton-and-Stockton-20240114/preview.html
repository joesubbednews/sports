Chorley Women v Norton and Stockton

Womens National League – Division One North
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Norton and Stockton travel to Chorley Women on early Sunday in Womens National League Division One North.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Stockport County Women 3-1 away.

Chorley, meanwhile, drew their last match, 1-1 against Middlesbrough Women.

Chorley are in disappointing form in Womens National League Division One North, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Norton & Stockton's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

The away team are eighth in the table after 10 games, of which they have won two and drawn four, earning 10 points.

Chorley are one place ahead of Norton & Stockton in seventh, with three wins and three draws putting them on 12 points.

With 10 goals in nine games so far this season, Chorley are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Norton & Stockton are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Chorley and Norton & Stockton have played each other on four occasions. Chorley won one of them and they drew the other.

On average, Chorley scored 1.2 goals and Norton & Stockton 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when they played out a 0-0 draw.
