Bedworth United v Walsall Wood

Northern Midlands Division One
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Walsall Wood travel to Bedworth United on late Tuesday in Northern Midlands Division One.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Sporting Khalsa 2-2 at home.

Bedworth, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Leicester Road.

Bedworth are in disappointing form in Northern Midlands Division One, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Walsall Wood's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to United's five.

With 26 goals in 24 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Walsall Wood are 14th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

Bedworth are two places ahead of Walsall Wood in 12th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.
