Spalding United v Rugby Town

Northern Midlands Division One | Sir Halley Stewart Field
Tuesday, 9 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Rugby Town face a challenge away against high-flying Spalding United at Sir Halley Stewart Field on Tuesday.

Rugby Town are 19th in the Northern Midlands Division One table, and have picked up five wins and one draw in their 22 games to date.

Spalding, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 49 points, having won 15 and drawn four.

With 49 goals in 22 games so far this season, Spalding are the league's highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Rugby, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.4 goals per game.

United are in fantastic form in Northern Midlands Division One, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Town's form is much worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the hosts' 16.

Spalding's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 win against Coventry Sphinx.

Rugby lost 3-1 against Sporting Khalsa last time out, also on Saturday.
