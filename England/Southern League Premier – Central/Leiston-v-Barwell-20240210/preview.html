Leiston v Barwell

Southern League Premier – Central | Victory Road
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leiston host Barwell at Victory Road on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Southern League Premier Central.

Leiston have picked up 10 points from their last six games, and they face a Barwell side who also won their last match, and have collected six points from the last possible 18.

In the last 10 years, Leiston and Barwell have played each other on nine occasions. They won four each, and they drew once.

On average, Leiston scored 1.2 goals and Barwell 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Leiston won 3-2 away.

With 47 goals in 30 games so far this season, Barwell are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Leiston, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home side are 10th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

The visitors are two places behind Leiston in 12th, with nine wins and 11 draws putting them on 38 points.

Leiston's last match was on February 3, a 2-1 win against Coalville Town.

Barwell beat Hitchin Town 2-0 last time out, also on February 3.
