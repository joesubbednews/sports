Coalville Town v Mickleover Sports

Southern League Premier – Central
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Tuesday's late match between Coalville Town and Mickleover Sports promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Southern League Premier Central's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Coalville and Mickleover Sports sit joint-third and second in the goal-scoring charts, with 56 and 57 goals respectively.

Mickleover Sports are top of the table after 28 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn five, earning 56 points.

Coalville are 13 places behind the visitors in 14th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last 10 years, Coalville and Mickleover Sports have played each other on six occasions. Coalville won five of them and they drew once.

On average, Coalville scored 2.8 goals and Mickleover Sports 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 22 2022, when Coalville won 4-1 at home.

Town are in disappointing form in Southern League Premier Central, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Mickleover Sports' form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home side's six.

Coalville's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 loss against Leiston.

Mickleover Sports beat AFC Sudbury 2-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
