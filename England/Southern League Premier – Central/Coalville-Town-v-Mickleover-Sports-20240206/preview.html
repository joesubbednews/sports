Coalville Town v Mickleover Sports

Southern League Premier – Central
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Mickleover Sports travel for Tuesday's late match against Coalville Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Southern League Premier Central.

Mickleover Sports, who sit third in the league after 26 games, fell to a 5-0 home defeat to St Ives Town on January 20.

They face a Coalville side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with AFC Telford United, and who sit 11th in the table.

With 54 goals in 24 games so far this season, Coalville are the league's highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Mickleover Sports are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Coalville and Mickleover Sports have played each other on six occasions. Coalville won five of them and they drew once.

On average, Coalville scored 2.8 goals and Mickleover Sports 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 22 2022, when Coalville won 4-1 at home.

Town are in mixed form in Southern League Premier Central, with two wins and four draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is similar – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
