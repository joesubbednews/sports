Leiston v Leamington

Southern League Premier – Central | Victory Road
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leamington are strong favourites to take all three points despite Leiston's home advantage in Saturday's match at Victory Road.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.91 on Leamington sealing the win, with the visitors sitting fifth in the Southern League Premier Central table.

Leiston, who are 11th in the league and 14 points behind the Brakes, are priced at 3.5 to win. A draw is set at 3.69.

With 58 goals in 38 games so far this season, Leamington are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Leiston, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Southern League Premier Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the Brakes's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Leiston's six.

Leiston's last match was on April 13, a 5-0 loss against Redditch United.

Leamington beat Stamford 3-0 last time out, also on April 13.
