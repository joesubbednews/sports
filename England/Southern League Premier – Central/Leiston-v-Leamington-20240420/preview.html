Leiston v Leamington

Southern League Premier – Central | Victory Road
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leiston host Leamington on Saturday at Victory Road in the Southern League Premier Central.

In their last league match, on April 6, Leiston beat Hitchin Town 2-0 at home.

Leamington lost, 1-0 away at AFC Telford United.

Leamington are fifth in the table after 37 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn 13, earning 64 points.

Leiston are five places behind the Brakes in 10th, with 15 wins and eight draws putting them on 53 points.

With 55 goals in 37 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The away team are also average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

Leiston are in disappointing form in the Southern League Premier Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Leamington's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Leiston's six.
