Nuneaton Town v Mickleover Sports

Southern League Premier – Central | The A1 Gasforce Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Nuneaton Town face a challenge against high-flying Mickleover Sports at the A1 Gasforce Stadium on Saturday.

Nuneaton Town are 20th in the Southern League Premier – Central table, and have picked up 10 wins and four draws in their 24 games to date.

Mickleover Sports, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 71 points, having won 22 and drawn five of their first 34 matches, and are five points behind table-toppers Needham Market.

With 69 goals in 34 games so far this season, Mickleover Sports are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Nuneaton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are in bad form in the Southern League Premier Central, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to the Boro's three.

In the last three years, Nuneaton and Mickleover Sports have played each other on three occasions. Nuneaton won one of them and Mickleover Sports the other.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Mickleover Sports won 2-0 at home.

Nuneaton's last match was on January 13, a 3-0 loss against St Ives Town.

Mickleover Sports beat Halesowen Town 1-0 last time out, on March 16.
