Long Eaton United v Nuneaton Town

Southern League Premier – Central
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table Long Eaton United host 20th-placed Nuneaton Town.

Long Eaton have picked up 19 points from their first 19 Southern League Premier – Central games, with four wins and seven draws.

That is 15 points less than the Boro have collected, having won 10 and drawn four.

Long Eaton are in disappointing form in the Southern League Premier Central, with no wins and five draws from their last six games.

With no wins and three draws over that period, Nuneaton's form is slightly worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to United's five.

With 37 goals in 36 games so far this season, the home side are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 95 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Long Eaton's last match was on March 23, a 3-2 loss against Stratford Town.

Nuneaton lost 3-0 against St Ives Town last time out, on January 13.
