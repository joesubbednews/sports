Leamington v Coalville Town

Southern League Premier – Central | The Phillips 66 Community Stadium
Tuesday, 19 March 2024 @ 7.45pm

Coalville Town travel to Leamington on late Tuesday in the Southern League Premier Central.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten St Ives Town 2-1 away.

The Brakes, meanwhile, drew their last match, 2-2 against Long Eaton United.

With 60 goals in 30 games so far this season, Coalville are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Leamington, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The Brakes are sixth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 12, earning 54 points.

Town are seven places behind the hosts in 13th, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

Leamington are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier Central, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Leamington's nine.
