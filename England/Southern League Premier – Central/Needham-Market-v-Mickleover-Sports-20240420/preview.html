Needham Market v Mickleover Sports

Southern League Premier – Central | Bloomfields
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

The Southern League Premier Central's top two sides face each other at Bloomfields in Saturday's kick-off, when Needham Market host second-placed Mickleover Sports.

Needham Market have picked up 26 wins and seven draws from 38 games so far this season, and sit seven points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Mickleover Sports, meanwhile, have won 24 and drawn six, picking up 78 points.

With 74 goals in 38 games so far this season, Needham Market are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Mickleover Sports are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in fantastic form in the Southern League Premier Central, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Needham Market's 15.

In the last three years, Needham Market and Mickleover Sports have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Needham Market's last match was on April 6, a 3-1 win against Royston Town.

Mickleover Sports drew 1-1 with Stratford Town last time out, on April 13.
