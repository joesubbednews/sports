Nuneaton Town v Leamington

Southern League Premier – Central | The A1 Gasforce Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leamington face Nuneaton Town on Monday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Southern League Premier Central.

The Brakes are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Nuneaton team who have drawn two and lost three over the same number of games.

Nuneaton are 20th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

Leamington are 15 places ahead of Town in fifth, with 17 wins and 13 draws putting them on 64 points.

In the last 10 years, Nuneaton and Leamington have played each other on six occasions. Nuneaton won two of them, Leamington three, and they drew once.

On average, the Boro scored 1.0 goal and the Brakes 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 1, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 33 goals in 24 games so far this season, the Boro are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The Brakes, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

Nuneaton's last match was on January 13, a 3-0 loss against St Ives Town.

Leamington beat Halesowen Town 1-0 last time out, on Saturday.
