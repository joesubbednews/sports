Berkhamsted v Coalville Town

Southern League Premier – Central
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Coalville Town are strong favourites to take all three points despite Berkhamsted's home advantage in Saturday's match.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.3 on Coalville sealing the win, with the visitors sitting 13th in the Southern League Premier Central table.

Berkhamsted, who are 21st in the league and 23 points behind Coalville, are priced at 7.85 to win. A draw is set at 5.

Berkhamsted are in terrible form in the Southern League Premier Central, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Coalville's form is much better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the home team's one.

With 22 goals in 30 games so far this season, Berkhamsted are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 66 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Berkhamsted's last match was on February 17, a 1-0 loss against Redditch United.

Coalville beat Hitchin Town 1-0 last time out, also on February 17.
