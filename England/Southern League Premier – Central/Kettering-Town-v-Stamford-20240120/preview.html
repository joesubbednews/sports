Kettering Town v Stamford

Southern League Premier – Central | Latimer Park
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kettering Town welcome Stamford to Latimer Park on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Kettering's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Southern League Premier – Central matches, while their opponents have earned nine from a possible 18.

With 52 goals in 25 games so far this season, Stamford are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Kettering, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Town are 20th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won six and drawn five, earning 23 points.

The away side are 13 places ahead of the Poppies in seventh, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

Kettering's last match was on January 13, a 2-1 loss against Stourbridge.

Stamford drew 2-2 with Redditch United last time out, also on January 13.
