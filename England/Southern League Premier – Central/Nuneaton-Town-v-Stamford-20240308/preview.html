Nuneaton Town v Stamford

Southern League Premier – Central
Friday, 8 March 2024 @ 7.45pm

Nuneaton Town face Stamford on late Friday looking to secure a first win in seven Southern League Premier – Central games.

The Boro have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Kettering Town on November 28.

They face a Stamford side who have won one and drawn three over that time.

With 61 goals in 30 games so far this season, Stamford are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Nuneaton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are 17th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

The away team are 10 places ahead of the Boro in seventh, with 12 wins and 10 draws putting them on 46 points.

Nuneaton's last match was on January 13, a 3-0 loss against St Ives Town.

Stamford lost 1-0 against AFC Telford United last time out, on February 20.
