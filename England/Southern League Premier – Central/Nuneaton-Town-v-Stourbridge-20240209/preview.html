Nuneaton Town v Stourbridge

Southern League Premier – Central | The A1 Gasforce Stadium
Friday, 9 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Nuneaton Town face Stourbridge at the A1 Gasforce Stadium on late Friday looking to secure a first win in seven Southern League Premier – Central games.

The Boro have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Kettering Town on November 28.

They face a Stourbridge side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

In the last 10 years, Nuneaton and Stourbridge have played each other on seven occasions. Nuneaton won two of them, Stourbridge one, and they drew four times.

On average, the Boro scored 1.9 goals and Stourbridge 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 28 2023, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

With 33 goals in 24 games so far this season, Nuneaton are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Stourbridge, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Town are 16th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

The away team are one place ahead of the Boro in 15th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on the same number of points.

Nuneaton's last match was on January 13, a 3-0 loss against St Ives Town.

Stourbridge drew 1-1 with Stamford last time out, on Saturday.
