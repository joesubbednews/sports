Alvechurch v Mickleover Sports

Southern League Premier – Central | Lye Meadow
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mickleover Sports visit Alvechurch at Lye Meadow on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Southern League Premier Central.

Mickleover Sports have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face an Alvechurch side who lost their last match, and have collected 10 points from the last possible 18.

With 69 goals in 34 games so far this season, Mickleover Sports are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Alvechurch, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The visitors are second in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 22 and drawn five, earning 71 points.

The home team are 12 places behind Mickleover Sports in 14th, with 11 wins and nine draws putting them on 42 points.

In the last five years, Alvechurch and Mickleover Sports have played each other on four occasions. They won two each.

On average, Alvechurch scored 1.8 goals and Mickleover Sports 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 15, when Mickleover Sports won 4-2 at home.

Alvechurch's last match was on March 23, a 2-0 loss against Kettering Town.

Mickleover Sports beat Halesowen Town 1-0 last time out, on March 16.
