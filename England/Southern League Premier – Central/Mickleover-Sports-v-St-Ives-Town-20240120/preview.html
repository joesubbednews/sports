Mickleover Sports v St Ives Town

Southern League Premier – Central | Don Amott Leisure Group Arena
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Mickleover Sports and St Ives Town promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Southern League Premier Central's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at the Don Amott Leisure Group Arena, Mickleover Sports and St Ives sit joint-top and joint-fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 53 and 48 goals respectively.

Mickleover Sports are second in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn five, earning 50 points.

St Ives are 12 places behind the home side in 14th, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last three years, Mickleover Sports and St Ives have played each other on three occasions. Mickleover Sports won two of them and St Ives one.

Their last meeting was on August 5, when Mickleover Sports won 2-0 away.

Mickleover Sports are in mixed form in Southern League Premier Central, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Mickleover Sports' 10.

Mickleover Sports' last match was on January 13, a 1-0 win against Berkhamsted.

St Ives beat Nuneaton Town 3-0 last time out, also on January 13.
