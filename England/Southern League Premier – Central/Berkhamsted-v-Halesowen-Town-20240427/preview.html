Berkhamsted v Halesowen Town

Southern League Premier – Central
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Halesowen Town are strong favourites to take all three points despite Berkhamsted's home advantage in Saturday's match.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.57 on Halesowen sealing the win, with the visitors sitting 10th in the Southern League Premier Central table.

Berkhamsted, who are 21st in the league and 32 points behind Halesowen, are priced at 3.8 to win. A draw is set at 4.5.

Berkhamsted are in bad form in the Southern League Premier Central, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Halesowen's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With 33 goals in 40 games so far this season, Berkhamsted are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 84 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Berkhamsted's last match was on April 20, a 6-2 loss against AFC Telford United.

Halesowen beat St Ives Town 4-3 last time out, also on April 20.
