Kettering Town v Barwell

Southern League Premier – Central | Latimer Park
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kettering Town host Barwell at Latimer Park on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Southern League Premier Central.

Kettering have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Barwell side who also won their last match, and have collected 12 points from the last possible 18.

With 42 goals in 34 games so far this season, Kettering are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Barwell, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are 17th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

The away team are seven places ahead of the Poppies in 10th, with 12 wins and 11 draws putting them on 47 points.

Over the last two years, Kettering and Barwell have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on August 14, when Barwell won 3-2 at home.

Kettering's last match was on March 23, a 2-0 win against Alvechurch.

Barwell beat Royston Town 2-1 last time out, also on March 23.
