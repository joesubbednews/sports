Alvechurch v Nuneaton Town

Southern League Premier – Central | Lye Meadow
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Nuneaton Town travel to Lye Meadow looking to secure a first win in seven Southern League Premier – Central games against Alvechurch on Saturday.

The Boro have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Kettering Town on November 28.

They face an Alvechurch side who have won just two and lost four over that time.

Nuneaton are 20th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

Alvechurch are five places ahead of Town in 15th, with 12 wins and nine draws putting them on 45 points.

In the last 10 years, Alvechurch and Nuneaton have played each other on 10 occasions. Alvechurch won two of them, Nuneaton seven, and they drew once.

On average, Alvechurch scored 1.4 goals and the Boro 2.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Nuneaton won 3-2 at home.

With 48 goals in 37 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The Boro are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Alvechurch's last match was on April 6, a 1-0 win against Bromsgrove Sporting.

Nuneaton lost 3-0 against St Ives Town last time out, on January 13.
