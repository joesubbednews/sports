AFC Telford United v Coalville Town

Southern League Premier – Central | New Bucks Head Ground
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Coalville Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight AFC Telford United defence on Tuesday.

With 53 goals in 23 games, Coalville are the joint-top scorers in Southern League Premier Central ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off at New Bucks Head Ground.

They face a Bucks side who have scored 35 in 24 matches, but conceded only 20 goals, putting them second among the league's tightest defences – only Needham Market have conceded fewer goals.

Telford United are sixth in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn eight, earning 41 points.

Coalville are seven places behind the Bucks in 13th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

The home team are in reasonable form in Southern League Premier Central, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Town's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Telford United's 12.

Telford United's last match was on January 13, a 0-0 draw against Leiston.

Coalville beat Redditch United 4-2 last time out, on January 6.
