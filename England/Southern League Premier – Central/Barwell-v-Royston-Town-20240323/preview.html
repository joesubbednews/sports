Barwell v Royston Town

Southern League Premier – Central | Kirkby Road
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Barwell host Royston Town on Saturday at Kirkby Road in the Southern League Premier Central.

Royston won, 1-0 at home against Redditch United.

In the last 10 years, Barwell and Royston have played each other on eight occasions. Barwell won five of them, Royston two, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Barwell won 3-0 away.

With 49 goals in 34 games so far this season, Royston are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Barwell, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Town are ninth in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

The home team are three places behind the visitors in 12th, with 11 wins and 11 draws putting them on 44 points.

Barwell are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

And also with three wins and three losses over that period, Royston's form is identical – they have both taken nine points from 18.
