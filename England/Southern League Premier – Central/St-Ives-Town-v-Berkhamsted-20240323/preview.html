St Ives Town v Berkhamsted

Southern League Premier – Central
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Berkhamsted travel looking to secure a first win in 10 Southern League Premier – Central games against St Ives Town on Saturday.

Berkhamsted have lost six and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Hitchin Town on December 30.

They face a St Ives side who have won six and lost three over that time.

With 24 goals in 33 games so far this season, Berkhamsted are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 69 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

St Ives, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The away team are bottom of the table after 33 games, of which they have won two and drawn 11, earning 17 points.

Town are 14 places ahead of Berkhamsted in eighth, with 15 wins and three draws putting them on 48 points.

St Ives's last match was on March 9, a 4-1 win against Alvechurch.

Berkhamsted drew 1-1 with Bromsgrove Sporting last time out, also on March 9.
