Hitchin Town v AFC Telford United

Southern League Premier – Central | Top Field
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

AFC Telford United travel to Top Field for Saturday's match against Hitchin Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Southern League Premier Central.

The Bucks, who sit fourth in the league after 33 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to AFC Sudbury on March 9.

They face a Hitchin side who also lost their last match, a 1-0 defeat to AFC Sudbury, and who sit 17th in the table.

Hitchin are in awful form in the Southern League Premier Central, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Telford United's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Town's zero.

With 48 goals in 34 games so far this season, the home team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The Bucks, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.
