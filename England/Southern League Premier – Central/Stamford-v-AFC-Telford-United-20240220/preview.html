Stamford v AFC Telford United

Southern League Premier – Central | The Zeeco Stadium
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Stamford face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight AFC Telford United defence on Tuesday.

With 58 goals in 28 games, Stamford are the joint-top scorers in the Southern League Premier Central ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off at the Zeeco Stadium.

They face a Bucks side who have scored 44 in 29 matches, but conceded only 26 goals, putting them joint-second among the league's tightest defences – only Needham Market have conceded fewer goals.

Telford United are sixth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn 10, earning 49 points.

Stamford are one place behind the Bucks in seventh, with 12 wins and 10 draws putting them on 46 points.

The home side are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier Central, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken nine points from 18.

Stamford's last match was on February 10, a 2-2 draw against AFC Sudbury.

Telford United beat Kettering Town 4-0 last time out, also on February 10.
