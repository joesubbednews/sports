Stamford v Hitchin Town

Southern League Premier – Central | The Zeeco Stadium
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stamford are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Saturday's kick-off against Hitchin Town.

Sitting 10 places and 16 points behind them in the table, Hitchin are 5.0 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 4.2.

Hitchin, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

With a win and a draw over that period, Town's form is worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Stamford's nine.
