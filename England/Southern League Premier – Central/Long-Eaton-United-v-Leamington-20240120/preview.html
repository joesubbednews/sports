Long Eaton United v Leamington

Southern League Premier – Central
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Long Eaton United face Leamington on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-2 victory over Hitchin Town on January 13, Long Eaton sit bottom of Southern League Premier Central.

They travel to play a Leamington side fifth in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-0 against Berkhamsted, on January 6.

Long Eaton are in bad form in Southern League Premier Central, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four draws over that period, Leamington's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to United's four.

With 20 goals in 25 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 71 goals at a rate of 2.8 per game.

The Brakes, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.
