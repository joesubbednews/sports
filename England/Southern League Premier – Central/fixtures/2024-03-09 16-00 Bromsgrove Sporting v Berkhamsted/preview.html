Bromsgrove Sporting v Berkhamsted

Southern League Premier – Central | The Victoria Ground
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Berkhamsted come to the Victoria Ground to play Bromsgrove Sporting on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Bromsgrove Sporting have two wins and a draw.

Berkhamsted are bottom of the table after 32 games, of which they have won two and drawn 10, earning 16 points.

Bromsgrove Sporting are six places ahead of the visitors in 16th, with 10 wins and seven draws putting them on 37 points.

With 23 goals in 32 games so far this season, Berkhamsted are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 68 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Bromsgrove Sporting's last match was on February 24, a 2-1 win against Hitchin Town.

Berkhamsted lost 2-1 against Stratford Town last time out, on March 2.
