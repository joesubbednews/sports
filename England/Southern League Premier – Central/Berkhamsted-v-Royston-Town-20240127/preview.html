Berkhamsted v Royston Town

Southern League Premier – Central
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Berkhamsted and Royston Town come into Saturday's kick-off in contrasting spells, with the away team hitting a rich vein of form while the hosts struggle.

Berkhamsted have won one and drawn one of the last six, while Royston have taken five wins and one draw.

Berkhamsted are 21st in the table after 26 games, of which they have won two and drawn eight, earning 14 points.

Royston are nine places ahead of the hosts in 12th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

With 19 goals in 26 games so far this season, Berkhamsted are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Berkhamsted's last match was on January 13, a 1-0 loss against Mickleover Sports.

Royston beat Bromsgrove Sporting 1-0 last time out, also on January 13.
