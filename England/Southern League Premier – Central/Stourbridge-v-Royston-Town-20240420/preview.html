Stourbridge v Royston Town

Southern League Premier – Central
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Royston Town travel to Stourbridge on Saturday in the Southern League Premier Central.

Stourbridge, meanwhile, lost their last match, 4-1 against St Ives Town.

In the last 10 years, Stourbridge and Royston have played each other on nine occasions. Stourbridge won four of them, Royston two, and they drew three times.

On average, Stourbridge scored 2.0 goals and Royston 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Royston, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier Central, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Stourbridge's seven.

Stourbridge are 14th in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn seven, earning 46 points.

The away side are five places ahead of the home team in ninth, with 17 wins and six draws putting them on 57 points.
