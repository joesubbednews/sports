Long Eaton United v Leamington

Southern League Premier – Central
Tuesday, 5 March 2024 @ 7.45pm

Leamington are strong favourites to take all three points despite Long Eaton United's home advantage in Tuesday's late match.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.22 on Leamington sealing the win, with the visitors sitting fifth in the Southern League Premier Central table.

Long Eaton, who are 22nd in the league and 39 points behind the Brakes, are priced at 9 to win. A draw is set at 5.5.

With 27 goals in 30 games so far this season, Long Eaton are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 84 goals at a rate of 2.8 per game.

Leamington, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

United are in disappointing form in the Southern League Premier Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the Brakes's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

Long Eaton's last match was on February 17, a 2-1 loss against Needham Market.

Leamington drew 0-0 with Kettering Town last time out, also on February 17.
