Nuneaton Town v Berkhamsted

Southern League Premier – Central
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Nuneaton Town face Berkhamsted on Sunday looking to secure a first win in seven Southern League Premier – Central games.

The Boro have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Kettering Town on November 28.

They face a Berkhamsted side who have won just one and drawn one over that time.

Berkhamsted are 21st in the table after 26 games, of which they have won two and drawn eight, earning 14 points.

Nuneaton are eight places ahead of the visitors in 13th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

With 19 goals in 26 games so far this season, Berkhamsted are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Nuneaton's last match was on January 13, a 3-0 loss against St Ives Town.

Berkhamsted lost 1-0 against Mickleover Sports last time out, also on January 13.
