Royston Town v Stratford Town

Southern League Premier – Central | Garden Walk Stadium
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stratford Town travel to the Garden Walk Stadium for Saturday's match against Royston Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Southern League Premier Central.

Stratford Town, who sit fourth in the league after 40 games, fell to a 2-1 home defeat to Bromsgrove Sporting on April 20.

They face a Royston side who also lost their last match, a 1-0 defeat to Stourbridge, and who sit ninth in the table.

With 67 goals in 40 games so far this season, Stratford are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Royston, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Royston and Stratford have played each other on 12 occasions. Royston won five of them, Stratford four, and they drew three times.

On average, Royston scored 1.7 goals and Stratford Town 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Stratford won 2-1 at home.

Royston Town are in mixed form in the Southern League Premier Central, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Stratford Town's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.
