Leamington v Coalville Town

Southern League Premier – Central | The Phillips 66 Community Stadium
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Coalville Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Leamington defence on Tuesday.

With 56 goals in 26 games, Coalville are the joint-fourth-highest scorers in the Southern League Premier Central ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off at the Phillips 66 Community Stadium.

They face a Brakes side who have scored 45 in 29 matches, but conceded only 26 goals, putting them joint-second among the league's tightest defences – only Needham Market have conceded fewer goals.

Leamington are fourth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 10, earning 52 points.

Coalville are 10 places behind the Brakes in 14th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

The home side are in reasonable form in the Southern League Premier Central, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Town's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Leamington's 12.

Leamington's last match was on February 10, a 3-1 win against Royston Town.

Coalville lost 2-1 against Leiston last time out, on February 3.
