Leamington v Coalville Town

Southern League Premier – Central | The Phillips 66 Community Stadium
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Leamington face Coalville Town on Tuesday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Southern League Premier Central.

The Brakes are unbeaten in 10, with four wins and six draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Coalville team who have won three and drawn four over the same number of games.

With 57 goals in 27 games so far this season, Coalville are scoring more than average in the league with 2.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Leamington, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The Brakes are fourth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 11, earning 53 points.

Town are nine places behind the hosts in 13th, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

Leamington's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Kettering Town.

Coalville beat Hitchin Town 1-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
