St Ives Town v Coalville Town

Southern League Premier – Central
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between St Ives Town and Coalville Town promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Southern League Premier Central's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, St Ives and Coalville sit top and joint-third in the goal-scoring charts, with 58 and 56 goals respectively.

In the last 10 years, St Ives and Coalville have played each other on 10 occasions. St Ives won four of them and Coalville six.

On average, St Ives scored 0.9 goals and Coalville 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 7, when St Ives won 1-0 away.

St Ives are in fantastic form in the Southern League Premier Central, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Coalville's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to St Ives Town's 15.

The hosts are eighth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn three, earning 42 points.

Coalville Town are six places behind St Ives in 14th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

St Ives's last match was on February 3, a 2-0 win against Redditch United.

Coalville lost 2-1 against Leiston last time out, also on February 3.
