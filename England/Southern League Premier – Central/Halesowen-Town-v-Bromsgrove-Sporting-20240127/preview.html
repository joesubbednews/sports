Halesowen Town v Bromsgrove Sporting

Southern League Premier – Central | The Grove
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Halesowen Town host Bromsgrove Sporting on Saturday at the Grove in Southern League Premier Central.

Halesowen, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Town are eight places ahead of Bromsgrove Sporting in ninth, with 10 wins and seven draws putting them on 37 points.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Southern League Premier Central, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Bromsgrove Sporting's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Halesowen's six.

Over the last two years, Halesowen and Bromsgrove Sporting have played each other twice. They won one each.
