Malvern Town v Bristol Manor Farm

Southern League – Division One South
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bristol Manor Farm travel to Malvern Town on Saturday in Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Frome Town 2-1 at home.

Malvern, meanwhile, drew their last match, 0-0 against Yate Town.

With 38 goals in 20 games so far this season, Malvern are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Bristol Manor Farm, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in Southern League Division One South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

Malvern are 12th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won six and drawn five, earning 23 points.

Bristol Manor Farm are three places ahead of Malvern in ninth, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.
