Evesham United v Cribbs

Southern League – Division One South | The Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Evesham United host Cribbs at the Spiers & Hartwell Jubilee Stadium on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Southern League Division One South.

Evesham have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Cribbs side who drew their last match, and have collected 14 points from the last possible 18.

Cribbs are second in the table after 19 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn five, earning 38 points.

Evesham are four places behind the away team in sixth, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.

With 33 goals in 21 games so far this season, United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Cribbs, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Evesham's last match was on January 6, a 1-0 win against Westbury United.

Cribbs drew 2-2 with Bashley last time out, on Tuesday.
