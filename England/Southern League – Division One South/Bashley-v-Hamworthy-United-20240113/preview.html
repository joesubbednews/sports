Bashley v Hamworthy United

Southern League – Division One South | The Recreation Ground
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match at the Recreation Ground sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 18th-ranked Bashley host bottom of the table Hamworthy United.

Bashley have picked up 17 points from their first 17 Southern League – Division One South games, with four wins and five draws.

That is 12 points more than Hamworthy have collected, having won one and drawn two.

Bashley are in disappointing form in Southern League Division One South, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

And also with a win and two draws over that period, Hamworthy's form is identical – they have both taken five points from 18.

With 21 goals in 17 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Bashley's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-2 draw against Cribbs.

Hamworthy drew 1-1 with Melksham Town last time out, on September 26.
