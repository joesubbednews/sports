Bideford v Mousehole

Southern League – Division One South | The Sports Ground
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bideford host Mousehole at the Sports Ground on Saturday on the back of six consecutive wins in Southern League Division One South.

It means Bideford have picked up the maximum 18 points from their last six games, and they face a Mousehole side who lost their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 39 goals in 18 games so far this season, Mousehole are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Bideford are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home team are fourth in the table after 18 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn three, earning 36 points.

The visitors are seven places behind Bideford in 11th, with seven wins and four draws putting them on 25 points.

Bideford's last match was on January 6, a 3-2 win against Bristol Manor Farm.

Mousehole lost 3-1 against Paulton Rovers last time out, also on January 6.
