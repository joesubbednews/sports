Bishop's Cleeve v Melksham Town

Southern League – Division One South | Kayte Lane
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Melksham Town travel to Bishop's Cleeve on Saturday in Southern League Division One South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Bashley 2-1 away.

Bishop's Cleeve also won their last match, 4-0 against Larkhall Athletic.

With 36 goals in 20 games so far this season, Bishop's Cleeve are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Melksham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in Southern League Division One South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Town's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Bishop's Cleeve's seven.

Bishop's Cleeve are ninth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won seven and drawn six, earning 27 points.

The visitors are one place behind the hosts in 10th, with six wins and eight draws putting them on 26 points.
