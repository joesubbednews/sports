Larkhall Athletic v Wimborne Town

Southern League – Division One South | The Plain Ham Ground
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Larkhall Athletic and Wimborne Town come into Saturday's kick-off at the Plain Ham Ground in contrasting spells, with the away team hitting a rich vein of form while the hosts struggle.

Larkhall have drawn two and lost four of the last six, while Wimborne have taken five wins and one draw.

With 42 goals in 19 games so far this season, Wimborne are the league's highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Larkhall, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 19 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn four, earning 40 points.

Athletic are 13 places behind the away side in 14th, with five wins and five draws putting them on 20 points.

Over the last year, Larkhall and Wimborne have played each other twice. Wimborne won both of them.

Their last meeting was on November 28, when Wimborne won 2-1 at home.

Larkhall's last match was on Tuesday, a 4-1 loss against Yate Town.

Wimborne beat Exmouth 4-1 last time out, on January 6.
