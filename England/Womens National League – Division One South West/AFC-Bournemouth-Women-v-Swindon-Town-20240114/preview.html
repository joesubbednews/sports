AFC Bournemouth Women v Swindon Town

Womens National League – Division One South West
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Two of Womens National League Division One South West's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when second-placed AFC Bournemouth Women host third-placed Swindon Town.

Bournemouth Women have picked up nine wins from nine games so far this season, and sit six points above the visitors going into the 2pm match.

Swindon, meanwhile, have won six and drawn three of 11, picking up 21 points.

Bournemouth Women are in exceptional form in Womens National League Division One South West, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Swindon's form is much worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 18.

With 46 goals in nine games so far this season, Bournemouth Women are the league's second-highest scorers with 5.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in six goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Town are also above average scorers, with 2.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.6. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Bournemouth Women and Swindon have played each other on four occasions. Bournemouth Women won two of them, Swindon one, and they drew once.

On average, Bournemouth Women scored 2.0 goals and Swindon 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 26, when Bournemouth Women won 2-1 away.

Bournemouth Women's last match was on November 5, a 4-0 win against Portishead LFC.

Swindon drew 0-0 with Exeter City Women last time out, on January 7.
