Chesterfield v Ebbsfleet United

National League | SMH Group Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Ebbsfleet United face a challenge away against high-flying Chesterfield at the SMH Group Stadium on Saturday.

Ebbsfleet United are 23rd in the National League table, and have picked up eight wins and seven draws in their 32 games to date.

The Spireites, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 80 points, having won 26 and drawn two of their first 31 matches.

With 77 goals in 31 games so far this season, Chesterfield are the league's highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Ebbsfleet, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The Spireites are in exceptional form in the National League, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, United's form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' 18.

Chesterfield's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-1 win against Dagenham and Redbridge, with Armando Dobra and James Berry-McNally getting the goals for the Spireites.

Ebbsfleet drew 0-0 with Oldham Athletic last time out, on Saturday.
