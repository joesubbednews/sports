Rochdale v Dagenham and Redbridge

National League | Crown Oil Arena
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Rochdale host Dagenham and Redbridge on Saturday at the Crown Oil Arena in National League.

In their last league match, on January 27, Rochdale lost to Aldershot Town 3-1 away, with their goal scored by Ian Henderson.

Dagenham & Redbridge drew, 1-1 away at Solihull Moors on Tuesday, with Dion Pereira scoring their goals.

With 37 goals in 30 games so far this season, Dagenham & Redbridge are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Rochdale, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The visitors are 16th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

The Dale are six places ahead of Dagenham & Redbridge in 10th, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

The home side are in mixed form in National League, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and two draws over that period, Dagenham & Redbridge's form is identical – they have both taken eight points from 18.
