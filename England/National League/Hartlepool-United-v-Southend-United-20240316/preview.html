Hartlepool United v Southend United

National League | The Suit Direct Stadium
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Southend United face Hartlepool United on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the National League.

The Shrimpers are unbeaten in eight, with four wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Hartlepool team who have won four and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 58 goals in 37 games so far this season, Hartlepool are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 66 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Southend, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Southend United are seventh in the table after 38 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn nine, earning 60 points.

Hartlepool United are five places behind the Shrimpers in 12th, with 14 wins and five draws putting them on 47 points.

Hartlepool's last match was on March 2, a 2-1 loss against Barnet, with Nicky Featherstone getting the goal for Pools.

Southend beat Dorking Wanderers 2-0 last time out, on Tuesday, with Gus Scott-Morriss and Harry Cardwell on the scoresheet.
