Rochdale v Boreham Wood

National League | Crown Oil Arena
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Rochdale welcome Boreham Wood to the Crown Oil Arena on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Rochdale's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six National League matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

With 43 goals in 34 games so far this season, Boreham Wood are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Rochdale, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The Wood are 14th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won nine and drawn 13, earning 40 points.

The Dale are one place ahead of the away team in 13th, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

Rochdale's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 loss against Solihull Moors, with Cian Hayes getting the goal for the Dale.

Boreham Wood lost 3-1 against Hartlepool United last time out, also on Saturday, with Kabongo Tshimanga on the scoresheet.
