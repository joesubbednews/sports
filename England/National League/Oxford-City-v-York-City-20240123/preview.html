Oxford City v York City

National League | RAW Charging Stadium
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

York City face Oxford City on Tuesday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in National League.

York are unbeaten in seven, with two wins and five draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face an Oxford team who have won just one and drawn one over the same number of games.

Oxford are 23rd in the table after 28 games, of which they have won six and drawn six, earning 24 points.

York are four places ahead of Oxford City in 19th, with six wins and 12 draws putting them on 30 points.

With 36 goals in 27 games so far this season, York City are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Oxford's last match was on January 6, a 2-1 loss against Hartlepool United, with Seydil Toure getting the goal for Oxford.

York drew 1-1 with Boreham Wood last time out, on January 7, with Adam Crookes on the scoresheet.
