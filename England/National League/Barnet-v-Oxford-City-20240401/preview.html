Barnet v Oxford City

National League | The Hive Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Oxford City face Barnet away at the Hive Stadium on Monday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Wealdstone on Friday, Oxford sit bottom of the National League.

They travel to play a Barnet side second in the standings, who were held in their last match, 2-2 against Solihull Moors.

With 79 goals in 41 games so far this season, Barnet are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Oxford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The Bees are in good form in the National League, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, City's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.
