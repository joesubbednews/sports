Oldham Athletic v Fylde

National League | Boundary Park
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Oldham Athletic face Fylde at Boundary Park on Friday looking to secure a first win in six National League games.

The Latics have lost one and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Eastleigh on February 27.

They face a Fylde side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

With 68 goals in 41 games so far this season, Fylde are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 74 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Oldham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Athletic are 10th in the table after 40 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn 15, earning 60 points.

The Coasters are three places behind the Latics in 13th, with 13 wins and 10 draws putting them on 49 points.

Oldham's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Dagenham and Redbridge.

Fylde beat Oxford City 2-1 last time out, also on Saturday, with Joe Westley and Nicholas George Haughton on the scoresheet.
