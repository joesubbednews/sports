Hartlepool United v York City

National League | The Suit Direct Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

York City face Hartlepool United on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in National League.

York are unbeaten in eight, with three wins and five draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Hartlepool team who have won two and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 38 goals in 28 games so far this season, York are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Hartlepool, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

City are 16th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 12, earning 33 points.

United are three places ahead of the visitors in 13th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

Hartlepool's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-0 loss against Kidderminster Harriers.

York beat Oxford City 2-1 last time out, also on Tuesday, with Dipo Akinyemi and Will Davies on the scoresheet.
