Oldham Athletic v Boreham Wood

National League | Boundary Park
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Oldham Athletic host Boreham Wood at Boundary Park on late Tuesday on the back of three consecutive wins in National League.

Oldham have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Wood side who drew their last match, and have collected 11 points from the last possible 18.

In James Norwood, Oldham have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. He has notched 14 goals in 26 appearances, to sit third in the scoring charts.

His goal rate of one every 150 minutes is much quicker than that of Lindokuhle Ndlovu, Boreham Wood's top scorer with a goal every 322 minutes, and a total of seven goals in 27 games.

With 33 goals in 27 games so far this season, the Wood are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Athletic, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The Latics are sixth in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn nine, earning 42 points.

The visitors are nine places behind the home side in 15th, with seven wins and 11 draws putting them on 32 points.

Oldham's last match was on January 6, a 1-0 win against Dorking Wanderers, with Karl Mike Fondop-Talum getting the goal for the Latics.

Boreham Wood drew 1-1 with York City last time out, on January 7, with Christopher Miles Bush on the scoresheet.
