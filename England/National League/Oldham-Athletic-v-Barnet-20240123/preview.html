Oldham Athletic v Barnet

National League | Boundary Park
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Oldham Athletic host Barnet at Boundary Park on late Tuesday on the back of three consecutive wins in National League.

Oldham have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Bees side who also won their last match, and have collected 13 points from the last possible 18.

With 57 goals in 28 games so far this season, Barnet are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Oldham are also above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The Bees are third in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn four, earning 55 points.

Athletic are four places behind the away side in seventh, with 11 wins and nine draws putting them on 42 points.

In the last three years, Oldham and Barnet have played each other on three occasions. Oldham won two of them and Barnet one.

Their last meeting was on November 22, when Oldham won 4-1 away.

Oldham's last match was on January 6, a 1-0 win against Dorking Wanderers, with Karl Mike Fondop-Talum getting the goal for the Latics.

Barnet beat Dagenham and Redbridge 2-1 last time out, on January 9, with Nicke Kabamba and Zak Brunt on the scoresheet.
