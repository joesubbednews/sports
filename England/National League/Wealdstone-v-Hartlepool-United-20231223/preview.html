Wealdstone v Hartlepool United

National League | Ruislip Manor Sports & Social Club
Saturday, 23 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Hartlepool United travel to Wealdstone on Saturday in National League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Chesterfield 1-0 at home.

Wealdstone, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-0 against Dagenham and Redbridge, with their goals scored by Sean Jordon Adarkwa and Tarryn Allarakhia.

In Emmanuel Dieseruvwe, Hartlepool have one of the league's sharpest shooters so far this season. He has notched 13 goals in 22 appearances, to sit second in the scoring charts.

Wealdstone's top scorers, with five goals each, are Olufela Olomola, Tahvon Campbell and Sam Bowen.

With 28 goals in 22 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Wealdstone are in reasonable form in National League, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Pools's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Wealdstone's 12.

The away team are 16th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

The home side are two places ahead of Hartlepool in 14th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.
