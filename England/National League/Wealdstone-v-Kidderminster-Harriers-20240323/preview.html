Wealdstone v Kidderminster Harriers

National League | Ruislip Manor Sports & Social Club
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kidderminster Harriers travel to Ruislip Manor Sports & Social Club looking to secure a first win in five National League games against Wealdstone on Saturday.

Kiddy have lost one and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Solihull Moors on February 27.

They face a Wealdstone side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

Kidderminster Harriers are 22nd in the table after 40 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn 12, earning 42 points.

Wealdstone are six places ahead of Kiddy in 16th, with 12 wins and 10 draws putting them on 46 points.

With 34 goals in 40 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The home team are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Wealdstone's last match was on Wednesday, a 1-0 loss against Bromley.

Kidderminster Harriers drew 0-0 with Bromley last time out, on March 16.
