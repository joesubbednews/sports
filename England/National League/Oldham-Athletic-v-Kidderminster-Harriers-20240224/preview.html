Oldham Athletic v Kidderminster Harriers

National League | Boundary Park
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Oldham Athletic host Kidderminster Harriers on Saturday at Boundary Park in the National League.

In their last league match, on Tuesday, Oldham drew with York City 1-1 away, with their goal scored by Karl Mike Fondop-Talum.

Kidderminster Harriers lost, 2-0 at home against Gateshead.

Kidderminster Harriers are 23rd in the table after 34 games, of which they have won eight and drawn nine, earning 33 points.

Oldham are 16 places ahead of Kiddy in seventh, with 14 wins and 11 draws putting them on 53 points.

With 29 goals in 34 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Athletic, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The Latics are in reasonable form in the National League, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Kidderminster Harriers's form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home team's 11.
