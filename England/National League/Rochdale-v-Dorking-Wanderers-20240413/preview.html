Rochdale v Dorking Wanderers

National League | Crown Oil Arena
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Dorking Wanderers travel to the Crown Oil Arena looking to secure a first win in 10 National League games against Rochdale on Saturday.

Wanderers have lost six and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Chesterfield on March 2.

They face a Rochdale side who have won three and drawn three over that time.

Dorking are 23rd in the table after 44 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn eight, earning 44 points.

Rochdale are 12 places ahead of Wanderers in 11th, with 15 wins and 12 draws putting them on 57 points.

With 50 goals in 44 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 80 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The Dale, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Rochdale's last match was on April 6, a 1-1 draw against Oldham Athletic, with Samuel Mather getting the goal for the Dale.

Dorking lost 4-2 against Wealdstone last time out, on Thursday, with Charlie Carter and Luke Robert Moore on the scoresheet.
