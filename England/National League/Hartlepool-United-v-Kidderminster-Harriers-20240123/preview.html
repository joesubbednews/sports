Hartlepool United v Kidderminster Harriers

National League | The Suit Direct Stadium
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Kidderminster Harriers face Hartlepool United away at the Suit Direct Stadium on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-2 victory over Aldershot Town on Saturday, Kiddy sit bottom of National League.

They travel to play a Hartlepool side 13th in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Ebbsfleet United.

With 24 goals in 29 games so far this season, Kidderminster Harriers are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Hartlepool, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

United are in mixed form in National League, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Kiddy's form is worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Pools' seven.
