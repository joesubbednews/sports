Oldham Athletic v Bromley

National League | Boundary Park
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bromley travel to Boundary Park for Saturday's match against Oldham Athletic looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in National League.

The Ravens, who sit second in the league after 32 games, fell to a 2-1 home defeat to Hartlepool United on February 3.

They face an Oldham side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Ebbsfleet United, and who sit eighth in the table.

With 53 goals in 32 games so far this season, Bromley are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Oldham, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Athletic are in good form in National League, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the Ravens's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the Latics' 13.

In the last three years, Oldham and Bromley have played each other on three occasions. Bromley won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 16, when Bromley won 3-0 at home.
