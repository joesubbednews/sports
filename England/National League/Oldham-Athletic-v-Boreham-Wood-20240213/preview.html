Oldham Athletic v Boreham Wood

National League | Boundary Park
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Boreham Wood travel to Oldham Athletic on late Tuesday in the National League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Maidenhead United 1-0 at home, with a goal from Érico Henrique Esteves de Sousa.

The Latics, meanwhile, drew their last match, 0-0 against Ebbsfleet United.

With 41 goals in 32 games so far this season, Boreham Wood are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Oldham, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Athletic are in good form in the National League, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the Wood's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the Latics' 13.

The hosts are eighth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn 10, earning 46 points.

The away team are six places behind Oldham in 14th, with nine wins and 13 draws putting them on 40 points.

In the last three years, Oldham and Boreham Wood have played each other on three occasions. Boreham Wood won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when they played out a 0-0 draw.
