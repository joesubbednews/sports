Oxford City v Eastleigh

National League | RAW Charging Stadium
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Eastleigh are strong favourites to take all three points despite Oxford City's home advantage in Tuesday's late match at the RAW Charging Stadium.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 2.0 on Eastleigh sealing the win, with the visitors sitting 16th in the National League table.

Oxford, who are 24th in the league and 11 points behind the Spitfires, are priced at 2.88 to win. A draw is set at 3.8.

Oxford are in bad form in the National League, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

But with no wins and two draws over that period, Eastleigh's form is even worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to City's four.

With 45 goals in 34 games so far this season, the home team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 68 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

The Spitfires, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Oxford's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Southend United.

Eastleigh lost 3-0 against Ebbsfleet United last time out, also on Saturday.
