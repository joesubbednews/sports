Dagenham and Redbridge v Kidderminster Harriers

National League | Chigwell Construction Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Kidderminster Harriers face Dagenham and Redbridge away at the Chigwell Construction Stadium on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Hartlepool United on Tuesday, Kiddy sit 23rd in National League.

They travel to play a Dagenham & Redbridge side 16th in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Dorking Wanderers.

With 25 goals in 30 games so far this season, Kidderminster Harriers are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Dagenham & Redbridge are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in National League, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Kiddy's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Dagenham & Redbridge's eight.
