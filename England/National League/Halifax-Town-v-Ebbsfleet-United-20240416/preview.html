Halifax Town v Ebbsfleet United

National League | The Shay Stadium
Tuesday, 16 April 2024 @ 7.45pm

Ebbsfleet United travel to Halifax Town on late Tuesday in the National League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Altrincham 2-0 at home.

The Shaymen also lost their last match, 2-0 against Barnet.

In the last 10 years, Halifax and Ebbsfleet have played each other on eight occasions. Halifax won two of them, Ebbsfleet five, and they drew once.

On average, the Shaymen scored 1.0 goal and Ebbsfleet 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Halifax won 2-0 away.

Ebbsfleet are 20th in the table after 44 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 10, earning 52 points.

Halifax are 12 places ahead of United in eighth, with 18 wins and 12 draws putting them on 66 points.

With 53 goals in 43 games so far this season, Town are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The Shaymen are in mixed form in the National League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Ebbsfleet's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the home team's 10.
