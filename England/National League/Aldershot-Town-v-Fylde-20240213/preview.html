Aldershot Town v Fylde

National League | Electrical Services Stadium
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Fylde come to the Electrical Services Stadium to play Aldershot Town on late Tuesday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while the Shots have three wins and two draws.

With 56 goals in 31 games so far this season, Aldershot are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Fylde, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Aldershot and Fylde have played each other on seven occasions. Aldershot won two of them, Fylde four, and they drew once.

On average, the Shots scored 0.9 goals and the Coasters 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Aldershot won 2-0 away.

Town are sixth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

The Coasters are 13 places behind the Shots in 19th, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

Aldershot's last match was on February 3, a 2-1 win against Halifax Town, with Kwame Thomas and Lorent Tolaj getting the goals for the Shots.

Fylde beat Eastleigh 2-1 last time out, on Saturday, with Josh Kay and Nicholas George Haughton on the scoresheet.
