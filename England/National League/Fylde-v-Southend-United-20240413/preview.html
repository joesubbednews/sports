Fylde v Southend United

National League | Mill Farm Stadium
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Southend United face Fylde on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the National League.

The Shrimpers are unbeaten in 14, with seven wins and seven draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Fylde team who have won seven and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 74 goals in 44 games so far this season, Fylde are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 77 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Southend, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

United are fifth in the table after 44 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn 12, earning 72 points.

The Coasters are eight places behind the Shrimpers in 13th, with 15 wins and 10 draws putting them on 55 points.

Fylde's last match was on Thursday, a 2-0 win against Gateshead, with Ethan Mitchell and Jonathan Ustabaşı getting the goals for the Coasters.

Southend beat Wealdstone 2-1 last time out, on Tuesday, with Cavaghn Miley and Nathan Ralph on the scoresheet.
