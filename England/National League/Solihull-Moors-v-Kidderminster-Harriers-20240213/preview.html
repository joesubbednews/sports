Solihull Moors v Kidderminster Harriers

National League | ARMCO Arena
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Kidderminster Harriers face Solihull Moors away at the ARMCO Arena on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-2 victory over Oxford City on February 3, Kiddy sit 22nd in the National League.

They travel to play a Solihull Moors side fourth in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-1 against Halifax Town, on February 6.

With 29 goals in 32 games so far this season, Kidderminster Harriers are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Solihull Moors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The Moors are in mixed form in the National League, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Kiddy's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.
