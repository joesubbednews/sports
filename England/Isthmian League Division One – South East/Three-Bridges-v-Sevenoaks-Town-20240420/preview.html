Three Bridges v Sevenoaks Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Jubilee Field
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges host Sevenoaks Town at Jubilee Field on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Three Bridges have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Sevenoaks side who also won their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 86 goals in 35 games so far this season, Three Bridges are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Sevenoaks, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Three Bridges and Sevenoaks have played each other on six occasions. Three Bridges won one of them, Sevenoaks three, and they drew twice.

On average, Three Bridges scored 1.2 goals and Sevenoaks 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

The home team are fifth in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn seven, earning 64 points.

Town are four places behind Three Bridges in ninth, with 13 wins and seven draws putting them on 46 points.

Three Bridges's last match was on Sunday, a 2-0 win against Erith and Belvedere.

Sevenoaks beat Littlehampton Town 2-1 last time out, on April 13.
