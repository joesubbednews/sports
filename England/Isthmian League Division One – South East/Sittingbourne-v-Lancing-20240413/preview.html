Sittingbourne v Lancing

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sittingbourne host Lancing in Saturday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Sittingbourne, who sit third in the league after 33 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to Three Bridges on April 6.

They face a Lancing side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-0 victory against East Grinstead Town, and who sit sixth in the table.

With 75 goals in 33 games so far this season, Sittingbourne are scoring more than average in the league with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Lancing are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Sittingbourne and Lancing have played each other on five occasions. Sittingbourne won two of them, Lancing one, and they drew twice.

On average, Sittingbourne scored 1.4 goals and Lancing 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

The home side are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Sittingbourne's 10.
