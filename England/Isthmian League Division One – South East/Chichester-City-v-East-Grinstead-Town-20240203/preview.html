Chichester City v East Grinstead Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chichester City host East Grinstead Town on Saturday in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Chichester, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

City are in disappointing form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

And also with a win and two draws over that period, Town's form is identical – they have both taken five points from 18.

On average, Chichester scored 1.2 goals and East Grinstead 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The home side are seventh in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn five, earning 35 points.

The visitors are six places behind Chichester in 13th, with seven wins and three draws putting them on 24 points.
