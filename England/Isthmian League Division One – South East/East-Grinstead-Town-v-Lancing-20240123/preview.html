East Grinstead Town v Lancing

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The SKYex Community Stadium
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.30pm

Lancing travel to the SKYex Community Stadium for Tuesday's late match against East Grinstead Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Lancing, who sit third in the league after 22 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Cray Valley PM on Saturday.

They face an East Grinstead side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Ashford United, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 28 goals in 20 games so far this season, East Grinstead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Lancing, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

In the last five years, East Grinstead and Lancing have played each other on five occasions. East Grinstead won three of them, Lancing one, and they drew once.

On average, East Grinstead scored 1.6 goals and Lancing 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 18, when East Grinstead won 1-0 away.
