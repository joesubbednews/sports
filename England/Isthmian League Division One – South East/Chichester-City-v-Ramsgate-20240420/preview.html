Chichester City v Ramsgate

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Isthmian League Division One South East's top sides face each other in Saturday's kick-off, when fourth-placed Chichester City host table-topping Ramsgate.

Chichester have picked up 20 wins and six draws from 35 games so far this season, and sit 17 points below the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Ramsgate, meanwhile, have won 26 and drawn five of 34, picking up 83 points.

With 95 goals in 34 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Chichester are also above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

City are in exceptional form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 18.

In the last 10 years, Chichester and Ramsgate have played each other on six occasions. Ramsgate won three of them and they drew three times.

On average, Chichester scored 0.8 goals and Ramsgate 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 13, when Ramsgate won 4-0 at home.

Chichester's last match was on April 13, a 3-1 win against Ashford United.

Ramsgate drew 2-2 with Cray Valley PM last time out, also on April 13.
