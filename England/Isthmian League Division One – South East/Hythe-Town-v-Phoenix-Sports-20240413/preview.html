Hythe Town v Phoenix Sports

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Reachfields Stadium
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hythe Town welcome Phoenix Sports to the Reachfields Stadium on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their six-game losing streak.

Hythe's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned 10 from a possible 18.

Phoenix Sports are 16th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Hythe are four places ahead of the away side in 12th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

With 44 goals in 34 games so far this season, Phoenix Sports are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 84 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Hythe and Phoenix Sports have played each other on four occasions. They won one each, and they drew twice.

On average, the Cannons scored 1.2 goals and Phoenix Sports 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Hythe won 4-0 away.

Hythe's last match was on April 6, a 5-2 loss against Beckenham Town.

Phoenix Sports lost 2-0 against Ramsgate last time out, also on April 6.
