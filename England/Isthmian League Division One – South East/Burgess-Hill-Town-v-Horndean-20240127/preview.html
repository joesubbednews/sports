Burgess Hill Town v Horndean

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Leylands Park
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Burgess Hill Town host Horndean on Saturday at Leylands Park in Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on Tuesday, Burgess Hill beat Beckenham Town 4-0 away.

Horndean also won, 3-1 away at Chichester City on January 13.

With 28 goals in 18 games so far this season, Horndean are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Burgess Hill, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The visitors are 14th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won six and drawn three, earning 21 points.

Town are five places ahead of Horndean in ninth, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

The home side are in reasonable form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

And also with four wins and two losses over that period, Horndean's form is identical – they have both taken 12 points from 18.
