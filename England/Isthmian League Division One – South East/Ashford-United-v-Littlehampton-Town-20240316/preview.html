Ashford United v Littlehampton Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Homelands
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Littlehampton Town make the journey to the Homelands to play Ashford United on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Littlehampton's struggles have left them with five points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

Littlehampton are 17th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won seven and drawn five, earning 26 points.

Ashford are six places ahead of Town in 11th, with 11 wins and two draws putting them on 35 points.

With 34 goals in 28 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last three years, Ashford and Littlehampton have played each other on three occasions. Ashford won two of them and Littlehampton one.

Their last meeting was on January 13, when Littlehampton won 1-0 at home.

Ashford's last match was on March 9, a 5-1 loss against Sittingbourne.

Littlehampton lost 5-1 against East Grinstead Town last time out, also on March 9.
