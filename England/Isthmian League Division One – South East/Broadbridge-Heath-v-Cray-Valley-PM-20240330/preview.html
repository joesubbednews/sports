Broadbridge Heath v Cray Valley PM

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cray Valley PM visit Broadbridge Heath on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Cray Valley PM have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Broadbridge Heath side who have also won their last two matches, and collected 13 points from the last possible 18.

With 75 goals in 30 games so far this season, Cray Valley PM are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Broadbridge Heath, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The away team are second in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 22 and drawn seven, earning 73 points.

The hosts are 10 places behind Cray Valley PM in 12th, with 10 wins and eight draws putting them on 38 points.

Over the last two years, Broadbridge Heath and Cray Valley PM have played each other twice. Cray Valley PM won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Cray Valley PM won 2-0 away.

Broadbridge Heath's last match was on March 23, a 3-1 win against Phoenix Sports.

Cray Valley PM beat Hythe Town 2-0 last time out, also on March 23.
