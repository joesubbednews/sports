Broadbridge Heath v Littlehampton Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Broadbridge Heath welcome Littlehampton Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Broadbridge Heath's struggles have left them with seven points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

Broadbridge Heath are 15th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won six and drawn seven, earning 25 points.

Littlehampton are one place ahead of the hosts in 14th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

In the last five years, Broadbridge Heath and Littlehampton have played each other on five occasions. Littlehampton won all of them.

On average, Broadbridge Heath scored 1.4 goals and Littlehampton 3.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 5, when Littlehampton won 2-1 at home.

With 31 goals in 26 games so far this season, Broadbridge Heath are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Broadbridge Heath's last match was on February 17, a 1-0 loss against Ramsgate.

Littlehampton drew 0-0 with Three Bridges last time out, also on February 17.
