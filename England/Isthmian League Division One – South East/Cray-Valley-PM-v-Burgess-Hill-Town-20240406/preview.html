Cray Valley PM v Burgess Hill Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cray Valley PM host Burgess Hill Town on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Cray Valley PM have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Burgess Hill side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 80 goals in 32 games so far this season, Cray Valley PM are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Burgess Hill, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are second in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 24 and drawn seven, earning 79 points.

Town are 11 places behind Cray Valley PM in 13th, with 10 wins and 10 draws putting them on 40 points.

In the last 10 years, Cray Valley PM and Burgess Hill have played each other on six occasions. Cray Valley PM won four of them and Burgess Hill two.

On average, Cray Valley PM scored 2.5 goals and Burgess Hill 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 20, when Cray Valley PM won 4-1 away.

Cray Valley PM's last match was on Monday, a 2-1 win against Sevenoaks Town.

Burgess Hill lost 3-2 against Three Bridges last time out, also on Monday.
