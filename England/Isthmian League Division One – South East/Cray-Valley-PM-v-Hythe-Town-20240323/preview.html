Cray Valley PM v Hythe Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hythe Town travel to Cray Valley PM on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Beckenham Town 2-1 at home.

Cray Valley PM, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-0 against Sheppey United.

With 73 goals in 29 games so far this season, Cray Valley PM are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Hythe, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are second in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn seven, earning 70 points.

Town are six places behind Cray Valley PM in eighth, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

Cray Valley PM are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the Cannons' form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.

In the last five years, Cray Valley PM and Hythe have played each other on four occasions. Cray Valley PM won three of them and they drew once.

On average, Cray Valley PM scored 2.8 goals and the Cannons 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 21 2023, when Cray Valley PM won 2-0 at home.
