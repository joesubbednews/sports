Beckenham Town v Sittingbourne

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Eden Park Avenue
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Beckenham Town face Sittingbourne at Eden Park Avenue on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Hythe Town on Thursday, Beckenham sit bottom of the Isthmian League Division One South East.

They travel to play a Sittingbourne side third in the standings, who lost in their last match, 1-0 against Broadbridge Heath, on March 16.

With 67 goals in 29 games so far this season, Sittingbourne are scoring more than average in the league with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Beckenham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home side's six.

In the last three years, Beckenham and Sittingbourne have played each other on three occasions. Beckenham won two of them and Sittingbourne one.

Their last meeting was on January 6, when Sittingbourne won 4-0 at home.
