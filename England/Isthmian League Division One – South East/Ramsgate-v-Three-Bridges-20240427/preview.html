Ramsgate v Three Bridges

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Southwood Stadium
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Isthmian League Division One South East's top sides face each other at the Southwood Stadium in Saturday's kick-off, when table-topping Ramsgate host fourth-placed Three Bridges.

Ramsgate have picked up 27 wins and five draws from 35 games so far this season, and sit 19 points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Three Bridges, meanwhile, have won 20 and drawn seven of 36, picking up 67 points.

With 99 goals in 35 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Three Bridges are also above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

But with six wins and no losses over that period, the visitors' form is even better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to Ramsgate's 14.

In the last 10 years, Ramsgate and Three Bridges have played each other on six occasions. Ramsgate won three of them, Three Bridges two, and they drew once.

On average, Ramsgate scored 1.8 goals and Three Bridges 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 23, when Ramsgate won 2-0 away.

Ramsgate's last match was on April 20, a 4-2 win against Chichester City.

Three Bridges beat Sevenoaks Town 2-1 last time out, also on April 20.
