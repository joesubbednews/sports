Herne Bay v Sheppey United

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Safety Net Stadium
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheppey United travel to Herne Bay on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Ashford United 5-1 at home.

Herne Bay also won their last match, 2-1 against Littlehampton Town.

With 68 goals in 35 games so far this season, Herne Bay are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Sheppey are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, United's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Herne Bay's 10.

Herne Bay are seventh in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn 11, earning 56 points.

The visitors are one place behind the hosts in eighth, with 13 wins and seven draws putting them on 46 points.
