Three Bridges v Lancing

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Jubilee Field
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges are on a poor run ahead of hosting Lancing at Jubilee Field on Saturday, with just four points collected from their last six games.
