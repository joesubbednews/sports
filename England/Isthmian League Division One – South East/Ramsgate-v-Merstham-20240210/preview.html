Ramsgate v Merstham

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Southwood Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Merstham face a challenge away against high-flying Ramsgate at the Southwood Stadium on Saturday.

Merstham are 17th in the Isthmian League Division One – South East table, and have picked up five wins and five draws in their 24 games to date.

Ramsgate, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 60 points, having won 19 and drawn three.

With 68 goals in 24 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Merstham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Ramsgate's 14.

Ramsgate's last match was on February 3, a 2-2 draw against Hythe Town.

Merstham lost 1-0 against Cray Valley PM last time out, also on February 3.
