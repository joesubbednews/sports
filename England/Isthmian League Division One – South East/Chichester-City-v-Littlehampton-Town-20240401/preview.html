Chichester City v Littlehampton Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chichester City host Littlehampton Town on Monday on the back of six consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

It means Chichester have picked up the maximum 18 points from their last six games, and they face a Littlehampton side who lost their last match, and have collected three points from the last possible 18.

With 37 goals in 31 games so far this season, Littlehampton are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 66 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Chichester, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

City are fourth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn six, earning 57 points.

Town are 12 places behind the home side in 16th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last three years, Chichester and Littlehampton have played each other on three occasions. Chichester won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on January 1, when Chichester won 3-0 away.

Chichester's last match was on Saturday, a 4-0 win against Lancing.

Littlehampton lost 2-1 against Ramsgate last time out, on Friday.
