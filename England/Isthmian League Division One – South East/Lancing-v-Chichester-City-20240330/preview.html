Lancing v Chichester City

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Culver Road
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lancing host Chichester City in Saturday's match at Culver Road looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Lancing, who sit fourth in the league after 32 games, fell to a 7-2 away defeat to Ramsgate on March 23.

They face a Chichester side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-0 victory against Sheppey United, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 62 goals in 31 games so far this season, Chichester are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Lancing are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, City's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Lancing's eight.

In the last five years, Lancing and Chichester have played each other on five occasions. Lancing won one of them, Chichester three, and they drew once.

On average, Lancing scored 0.2 goals and Chichester 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Chichester won 3-0 at home.
