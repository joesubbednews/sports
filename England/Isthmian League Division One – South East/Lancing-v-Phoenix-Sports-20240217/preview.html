Lancing v Phoenix Sports

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Culver Road
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Lancing face Phoenix Sports at Culver Road on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-4 victory over Sevenoaks Town on February 10, Lancing sit fourth in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

They travel to play a Phoenix Sports side 18th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 3-3 against Beckenham Town, on Wednesday.

Lancing are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Phoenix Sports' form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the home side's nine.

In the last 10 years, Lancing and Phoenix Sports have played each other on six occasions. Lancing won one of them, Phoenix Sports three, and they drew twice.

On average, Lancing scored 1.2 goals and Phoenix Sports 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Lancing won 2-1 away.

With 32 goals in 26 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 70 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

Lancing, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.
