Sevenoaks Town v Erith and Belvedere

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sevenoaks Town host Erith and Belvedere on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on February 10, Sevenoaks beat Lancing 4-2 at home.

Erith & Belvedere lost, 4-0 at home against Sittingbourne on Sunday.

Erith & Belvedere are 19th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won five and drawn four, earning 19 points.

Sevenoaks are 10 places ahead of the away side in ninth, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

With 15 goals in 26 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Erith & Belvedere's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Sevenoaks's five.
