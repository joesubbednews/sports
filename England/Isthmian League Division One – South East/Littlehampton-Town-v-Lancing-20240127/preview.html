Littlehampton Town v Lancing

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lancing travel for Saturday's match against Littlehampton Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Lancing, who sit third in the league after 22 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Cray Valley PM on January 20.

They face a Littlehampton side who also lost their last match, a 2-1 defeat to Sheppey United, and who sit 15th in the table.

With 27 goals in 21 games so far this season, Littlehampton are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Lancing, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home team's nine.

In the last three years, Littlehampton and Lancing have played each other on three occasions. Lancing won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Lancing won 3-2 at home.
