Littlehampton Town v Sittingbourne

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Sittingbourne travel for Tuesday's late match against Littlehampton Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Sittingbourne, who sit third in the league after 26 games, fell to a 3-0 home defeat to Chichester City on Saturday.

They face a Littlehampton side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Three Bridges, and who sit 14th in the table.

With 60 goals in 26 games so far this season, Sittingbourne are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Littlehampton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.

In the last five years, Littlehampton and Sittingbourne have played each other on four occasions. Littlehampton won two of them and they drew twice.

On average, Littlehampton scored 2.0 goals and Sittingbourne 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 18, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
