Sevenoaks Town v Beckenham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Beckenham Town travel looking to secure a first win in seven Isthmian League Division One – South East games against Sevenoaks Town on Saturday.

Beckenham have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Merstham on January 27.

They face a Sevenoaks side who have won three and lost three over that time.

Beckenham are bottom of the table after 27 games, of which they have won three and drawn six, earning 15 points.

Sevenoaks are 11 places ahead of Beckenham Town in ninth, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

With 36 goals in 27 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 70 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

Sevenoaks Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, Sevenoaks and Beckenham have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on January 13, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Sevenoaks's last match was on March 9, a 4-3 win against Sheppey United.

Beckenham lost 5-2 against Ramsgate last time out, also on March 9.
