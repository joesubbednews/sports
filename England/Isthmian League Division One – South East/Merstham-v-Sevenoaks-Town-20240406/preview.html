Merstham v Sevenoaks Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Moatside Stadium
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Merstham host Sevenoaks Town on Saturday at the Moatside Stadium in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on Monday, Merstham lost to Broadbridge Heath 1-0 at home.

Sevenoaks also lost, 2-1 away at Cray Valley PM.

With 42 goals in 33 games so far this season, Merstham are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Sevenoaks, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are 15th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

Town are five places ahead of Merstham in 10th, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

Merstham are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.

In the last three years, Merstham and Sevenoaks have played each other on three occasions. Sevenoaks won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when they played out a 2-2 draw.
