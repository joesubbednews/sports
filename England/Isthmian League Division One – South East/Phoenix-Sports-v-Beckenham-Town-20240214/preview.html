Phoenix Sports v Beckenham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Phoenix Sports FC Ground
Wednesday, 14 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Wednesday's late match at the Phoenix Sports FC Ground sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 18th-ranked Phoenix Sports host bottom of the table Beckenham Town.

Phoenix Sports have picked up 19 points from their first 19 Isthmian League Division One – South East games, with five wins and four draws.

That is six points more than Beckenham have collected, having won three and drawn four.

Phoenix Sports are in bad form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Beckenham's form is slightly better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the home side's four.

With 29 goals in 25 games so far this season, Phoenix Sports are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 67 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

Phoenix Sports' last match was on February 3, a 2-0 loss against Littlehampton Town.

Beckenham lost 3-2 against Ashford United last time out, also on February 3.
