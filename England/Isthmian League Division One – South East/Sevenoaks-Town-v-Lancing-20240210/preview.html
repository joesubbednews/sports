Sevenoaks Town v Lancing

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sevenoaks Town are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Lancing on Saturday, with just two points collected from their last six games.

Sevenoaks have picked up two draws and four losses in their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East games, and face a Lancing side whose last six games have brought four wins and two losses.

Lancing are fourth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn two, earning 47 points.

Sevenoaks are seven places behind the visitors in 11th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 45 goals in 25 games so far this season, Lancing are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Sevenoaks and Lancing have played each other on five occasions. Sevenoaks won two of them and Lancing three.

On average, Sevenoaks scored 1.8 goals and Lancing 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Sevenoaks won 2-1 away.

Sevenoaks's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-1 loss against Sittingbourne.

Lancing beat East Grinstead Town 2-0 last time out, also on Tuesday.
