Lancing v East Grinstead Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Culver Road
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

East Grinstead Town travel to Culver Road looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian League Division One – South East games against Lancing on Saturday.

East Grinstead have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Littlehampton Town on March 9.

They face a Lancing side who have won one and drawn two over that time.

In the last 10 years, Lancing and East Grinstead have played each other on six occasions. Lancing won two of them, East Grinstead three, and they drew once.

On average, Lancing scored 1.0 goal and East Grinstead 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 6, when Lancing won 2-0 away.

With 43 goals in 32 games so far this season, East Grinstead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 73 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Lancing, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Town are 17th in the table after 32 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

The hosts are 11 places ahead of the away side in sixth, with 17 wins and five draws putting them on 56 points.

Lancing's last match was on Monday, a 1-1 draw against Horndean.

East Grinstead drew 1-1 with Erith and Belvedere last time out, also on Monday.
