Herne Bay v Chichester City

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Safety Net Stadium
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chichester City come to the Safety Net Stadium to play Herne Bay on Saturday in great form, having collected 15 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and lost one of their last six fixtures, while Herne Bay have three wins and three losses.

City are fifth in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn six, earning 66 points.

The hosts are two places behind the away side in seventh, with 15 wins and 11 draws putting them on 56 points.

In the last five years, Herne Bay and Chichester have played each other on four occasions. Herne Bay won two of them, Chichester one, and they drew once.

On average, Herne Bay scored 1.5 goals and Chichester 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 16, when they played out a 3-3 draw.

Herne Bay's last match was on April 20, a 4-2 loss against Cray Valley PM.

Chichester lost 4-2 against Ramsgate last time out, also on April 20.
