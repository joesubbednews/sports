Chichester City v Sevenoaks Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chichester City host Sevenoaks Town on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on February 17, Chichester beat Sittingbourne 3-0 away.

Sevenoaks also won, 3-0 at home against Erith and Belvedere.

With 53 goals in 27 games so far this season, Chichester are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Sevenoaks are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

City are sixth in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

Town are three places behind the hosts in ninth, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

In the last five years, Chichester and Sevenoaks have played each other on five occasions. Chichester won two of them, Sevenoaks one, and they drew twice.

On average, Chichester scored 1.2 goals and Sevenoaks 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Sevenoaks won 1-0 at home.

Chichester are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is identical – they have both taken eight points from 18.
