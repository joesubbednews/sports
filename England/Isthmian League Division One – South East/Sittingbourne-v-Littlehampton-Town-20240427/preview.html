Sittingbourne v Littlehampton Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The WE Mannin Stadium
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sittingbourne host Littlehampton Town in Saturday's match at the WE Mannin Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Sittingbourne, who sit third in the league after 35 games, fell to a 1-2 away defeat to Phoenix Sports on April 20.

They face a Littlehampton side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-1 victory against Burgess Hill Town, and who sit 17th in the table.

With 79 goals in 35 games so far this season, Sittingbourne are scoring more than average in the league with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Littlehampton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Town's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Sittingbourne's 11.

In the last five years, Sittingbourne and Littlehampton have played each other on four occasions. Littlehampton won two of them and they drew twice.

On average, Sittingbourne scored 0.8 goals and Littlehampton 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 18 2023, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
