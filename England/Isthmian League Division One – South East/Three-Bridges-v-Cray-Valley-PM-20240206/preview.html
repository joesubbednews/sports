Three Bridges v Cray Valley PM

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Jubilee Field
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Tuesday's late match between Three Bridges and Cray Valley PM promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Isthmian League Division One South East's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Jubilee Field, Three Bridges and Cray Valley PM sit second and joint-third in the goal-scoring charts, with 58 and 54 goals respectively.

Cray Valley PM are second in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn six, earning 54 points.

Three Bridges are six places behind the visitors in eighth, with 11 wins and four draws putting them on 37 points.

The home side are in bad form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Cray Valley PM's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Three Bridges's three.

In the last five years, Three Bridges and Cray Valley PM have played each other on four occasions. Three Bridges won one of them, Cray Valley PM two, and they drew once.

On average, Three Bridges scored 1.8 goals and Cray Valley PM 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 11, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Three Bridges's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 loss against Sheppey United.

Cray Valley PM beat Merstham 1-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
