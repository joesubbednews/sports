Sevenoaks Town v Ashford United

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sevenoaks Town host Ashford United on Friday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Ashford lost, 3-2 away at Three Bridges.

In the last 10 years, Sevenoaks and Ashford have played each other on six occasions. Sevenoaks won two of them and Ashford four.

On average, Sevenoaks scored 1.0 goal and Ashford 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 22, when Ashford won 2-1 at home.

Sevenoaks, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, United's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the home side's 10.

Sevenoaks are eighth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn seven, earning 43 points.

The visitors are five places behind Sevenoaks in 13th, with 12 wins and two draws putting them on 38 points.
