Sittingbourne v Horndean

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The WE Mannin Stadium
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 1.00pm

Horndean travel to Sittingbourne on Friday lunchtime in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Erith and Belvedere 2-1 away.

Sittingbourne also won their last match, 3-2 against Beckenham Town.

With 70 goals in 30 games so far this season, Sittingbourne are scoring more than average in the league with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Horndean, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home team are third in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn six, earning 63 points.

The visitors are 11 places behind Sittingbourne in 14th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

Sittingbourne are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Horndean's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' 12.
