Sheppey United v Merstham

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Holm Park
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheppey United host Merstham on Saturday at Holm Park in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on February 3, Sheppey beat Three Bridges 1-0 at home.

Merstham drew, 2-2 at home against Ashford United on February 17.

With 33 goals in 26 games so far this season, Merstham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Sheppey, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The away side are 16th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won six and drawn six, earning 24 points.

United are nine places ahead of Merstham in seventh, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Merstham's form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Sheppey's eight.
