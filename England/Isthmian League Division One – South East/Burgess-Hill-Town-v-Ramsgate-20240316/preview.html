Burgess Hill Town v Ramsgate

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Leylands Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Burgess Hill Town are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Ramsgate at Leylands Park on Saturday, with just one point collected from their last six games.

Burgess Hill have picked up just one draw in their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East games, and face a Ramsgate side whose last six games have brought three wins and two draws.

With 78 goals in 28 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Burgess Hill, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The visitors are top of the table after 28 games, of which they have won 22 and drawn three, earning 69 points.

Town are 11 places behind Ramsgate in 12th, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last 10 years, Burgess Hill and Ramsgate have played each other on six occasions. Burgess Hill won one of them and Ramsgate the other.

On average, Burgess Hill scored 1.3 goals and Ramsgate 2.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 13, when Ramsgate won 4-2 at home.

Burgess Hill's last match was on March 9, a 3-0 loss against Chichester City.

Ramsgate beat Beckenham Town 5-2 last time out, also on March 9.
