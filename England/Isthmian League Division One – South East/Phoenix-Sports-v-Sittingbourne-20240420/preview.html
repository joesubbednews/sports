Phoenix Sports v Sittingbourne

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Phoenix Sports FC Ground
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sittingbourne travel to Phoenix Sports on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

The visitors are third in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn eight, earning 68 points.

The home side are 13 places behind Sittingbourne in 16th, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

In the last three years, Phoenix Sports and Sittingbourne have played each other on three occasions. Phoenix Sports won one of them and Sittingbourne the other.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when Sittingbourne won 2-1 at home.

Phoenix Sports are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Sittingbourne's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Phoenix Sports' 10.
