Chichester City v Beckenham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Beckenham Town come to play Chichester City on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Chichester have three wins and a draw.

Beckenham are bottom of the table after 18 games, of which they have won two and drawn three, earning nine points.

Chichester are 12 places ahead of Town in eighth, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

With 23 goals in 18 games so far this season, the away side are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

City, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last three years, Chichester and Beckenham have played each other on three occasions. Chichester won two of them and Beckenham one.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Chichester won 2-0 away.

Chichester's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-3 draw against Herne Bay.

Beckenham drew 2-2 with Sevenoaks Town last time out, on January 13.
