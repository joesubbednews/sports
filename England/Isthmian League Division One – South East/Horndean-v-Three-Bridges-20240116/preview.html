Horndean v Three Bridges

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Three Bridges make the journey to play Horndean on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Three Bridges's struggles have left them with seven points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned 12 from a possible 18.

With 55 goals in 19 games so far this season, Three Bridges are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Horndean, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

The away side are fifth in the table after 19 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

The hosts are eight places behind Three Bridges in 13th, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

Horndean's last match was on Saturday, a 3-1 win against Chichester City.

Three Bridges lost 3-2 against Ashford United last time out, on January 6.
