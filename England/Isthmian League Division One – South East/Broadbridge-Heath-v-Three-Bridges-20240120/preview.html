Broadbridge Heath v Three Bridges

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges make the journey to play Broadbridge Heath on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Three Bridges's struggles have left them with seven points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

With 55 goals in 19 games so far this season, Three Bridges are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Broadbridge Heath, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are 18th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won four and drawn six, earning 18 points.

The away side are 13 places ahead of Broadbridge Heath in fifth, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

Broadbridge Heath's last match was on January 13, a 2-0 loss against Sittingbourne.

Three Bridges lost 3-2 against Ashford United last time out, on January 6.
