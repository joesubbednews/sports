Ramsgate v Broadbridge Heath

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Southwood Stadium
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ramsgate host Broadbridge Heath in Saturday's match at the Southwood Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Ramsgate, who sit top of the league after 25 games, fell to a 2-0 home defeat to Merstham on February 10.

They face a Broadbridge Heath side who also lost their last match, a 3-1 defeat to Herne Bay, and who sit 14th in the table.

With 68 goals in 25 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Broadbridge Heath, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Ramsgate's 11.
