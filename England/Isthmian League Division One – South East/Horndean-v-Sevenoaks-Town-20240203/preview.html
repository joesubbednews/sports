Horndean v Sevenoaks Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sevenoaks Town travel looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian League Division One – South East games against Horndean on Saturday.

Sevenoaks have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against East Grinstead Town on December 26.

They face a Horndean side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

With 29 goals in 20 games so far this season, Horndean are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Sevenoaks, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home team are 15th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won six and drawn four, earning 22 points.

Town are five places ahead of Horndean in 10th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Horndean's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-1 draw against Cray Valley PM.

Sevenoaks drew 0-0 with Hythe Town last time out, on January 27.
