Beckenham Town v Hythe Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Eden Park Avenue
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hythe Town make the journey to Eden Park Avenue to play Beckenham Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Hythe's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned four from a possible 18.

Beckenham are bottom of the table after 32 games, of which they have won four and drawn seven, earning 19 points.

Hythe are nine places ahead of Beckenham Town in 11th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

With 44 goals in 32 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 80 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Hythe Town are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, Beckenham and Hythe have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on March 21, when Beckenham won 2-1 away.

Beckenham's last match was on Monday, a 2-1 loss against Phoenix Sports.

Hythe lost 3-0 against Sheppey United last time out, also on Monday.
