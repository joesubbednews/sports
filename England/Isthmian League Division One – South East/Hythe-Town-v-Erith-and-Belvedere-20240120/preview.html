Hythe Town v Erith and Belvedere

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Reachfields Stadium
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Erith and Belvedere make the journey to the Reachfields Stadium to play Hythe Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Erith & Belvedere's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned 10 from a possible 18.

Erith & Belvedere are 19th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won three and drawn three, earning 12 points.

Hythe are eight places ahead of the away side in 11th, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

With 10 goals in 21 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Hythe's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-1 loss against Lancing.

Erith & Belvedere lost 3-0 against Lancing last time out, on Sunday.
