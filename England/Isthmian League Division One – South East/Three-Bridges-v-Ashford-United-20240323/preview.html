Three Bridges v Ashford United

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Jubilee Field
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges face Ashford United on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Three Bridges are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face an Ashford team who have won two and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 72 goals in 30 games so far this season, Three Bridges are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Ashford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Three Bridges and Ashford have played each other on seven occasions. Three Bridges won one of them, Ashford five, and they drew once.

On average, Three Bridges scored 1.1 goals and Ashford 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 6, when Ashford won 3-2 at home.

The hosts are sixth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn seven, earning 49 points.

United are five places behind Three Bridges in 11th, with 12 wins and two draws putting them on 38 points.

Three Bridges's last match was on March 16, a 1-1 draw against Hythe Town.

Ashford lost 3-0 against Burgess Hill Town last time out, on Tuesday.
