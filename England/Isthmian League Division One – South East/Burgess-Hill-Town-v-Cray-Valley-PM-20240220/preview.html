Burgess Hill Town v Cray Valley PM

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Leylands Park
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.30pm

Cray Valley PM face Burgess Hill Town on Tuesday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Cray Valley PM are unbeaten in 25, with 18 wins and seven draws, ahead of the 7.30pm kick-off.

They face a Burgess Hill team who have won eight and drawn eight over the same number of games.

With 60 goals in 25 games so far this season, Cray Valley PM are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Burgess Hill, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The away side are second in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn seven, earning 61 points.

Town are nine places behind Cray Valley PM in 11th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

In the last five years, Burgess Hill and Cray Valley PM have played each other on five occasions. Burgess Hill won two of them and Cray Valley PM three.

On average, Burgess Hill scored 1.2 goals and Cray Valley PM 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 1, when Cray Valley PM won 3-1 at home.

Burgess Hill's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 loss against Hythe Town.

Cray Valley PM beat East Grinstead Town 4-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
