Merstham v Cray Valley PM

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Moatside Stadium
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Merstham face a challenge against high-flying Cray Valley PM at the Moatside Stadium on Saturday.

Merstham are 17th in the Isthmian League Division One – South East table, and have picked up five wins and five draws in their 23 games to date.

Cray Valley PM, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 51 points, having won 15 and drawn six of their first 21 matches, and are eight points behind table-toppers Ramsgate.

With 53 goals in 21 games so far this season, Cray Valley PM are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Merstham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Merstham's five.

Merstham's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-1 draw against Chichester City.

Cray Valley PM drew 1-1 with Horndean last time out, also on Tuesday.
