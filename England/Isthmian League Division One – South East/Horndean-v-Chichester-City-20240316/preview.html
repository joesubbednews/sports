Horndean v Chichester City

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chichester City visit Horndean on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Chichester have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Horndean side who also won their last match, and have collected 10 points from the last possible 18.

With 57 goals in 29 games so far this season, Chichester are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Horndean, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

City are fifth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

The hosts are nine places behind the away team in 14th, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

Horndean's last match was on March 9, a 4-0 win against Hythe Town.

Chichester beat Burgess Hill Town 3-0 last time out, also on March 9.
