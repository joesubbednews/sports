Beckenham Town v Horndean

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Eden Park Avenue
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Beckenham Town host Horndean on Saturday at Eden Park Avenue in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on April 13, Beckenham drew with East Grinstead Town 1-1 away.

Horndean lost, 3-1 at home against Merstham.

Beckenham are 19th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

Horndean are four places ahead of Town in 15th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

The home team are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Beckenham's seven.

With 50 goals in 34 games so far this season, Beckenham are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 83 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Horndean are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.
