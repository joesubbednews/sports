Phoenix Sports v East Grinstead Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Phoenix Sports FC Ground
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Phoenix Sports face East Grinstead Town at the Phoenix Sports FC Ground on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Merstham on March 9, Phoenix Sports sit 18th in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

They travel to play an East Grinstead side 16th in the standings, who also won their last match, 5-1 against Littlehampton Town.

Phoenix Sports are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, East Grinstead's form is slightly worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

In the last five years, Phoenix Sports and East Grinstead have played each other on five occasions. East Grinstead won four of them and they drew once.

On average, Phoenix Sports scored 1.0 goal and East Grinstead 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 13, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 38 goals in 29 games so far this season, Phoenix Sports are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 77 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.
