Herne Bay v Sevenoaks Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Safety Net Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sevenoaks Town travel to Herne Bay on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Beckenham Town 1-1 at home.

Herne Bay also drew their last match, 2-2 against Merstham.

With 58 goals in 30 games so far this season, Herne Bay are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Sevenoaks, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are seventh in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn 11, earning 47 points.

Town are three places behind Herne Bay in 10th, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

In the last three years, Herne Bay and Sevenoaks have played each other on three occasions. Herne Bay won two of them and Sevenoaks one.

Their last meeting was on January 6, when Herne Bay won 2-1 away.

Herne Bay are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's nine.
