Sevenoaks Town v Littlehampton Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sevenoaks Town and Littlehampton Town will both be looking for points in Saturday's kick-off to end their losing streaks.

The two teams have both gone three Isthmian League Division One – South East games without a win. Sevenoaks have picked up seven points from their last six games, while Littlehampton have earned just three.

Littlehampton are 17th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

Sevenoaks are seven places ahead of Littlehampton Town in 10th, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

With 38 goals in 33 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 70 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Sevenoaks Town are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, Sevenoaks and Littlehampton have played each other on three occasions. Sevenoaks won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Sevenoaks won 5-1 away.

Sevenoaks's last match was on April 6, a 5-0 loss against Merstham.

Littlehampton lost 2-1 against Herne Bay last time out, also on April 6.
