Broadbridge Heath v Erith and Belvedere

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Erith and Belvedere come to play Broadbridge Heath on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

Broadbridge Heath are five places ahead of the visitors in 14th, with five wins and seven draws putting them on 22 points.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Broadbridge Heath's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-0 win against Three Bridges.

Erith & Belvedere drew 1-1 with Beckenham Town last time out, also on Tuesday.
