Ashford United v Chichester City

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Homelands
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chichester City visit Ashford United at the Homelands on Saturday on the back of eight consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

It means Chichester have picked up the maximum 24 points from their last eight games, and they face an Ashford side who lost their last match, and have collected nine points from the last possible 24.

With 74 goals in 34 games so far this season, Chichester are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Ashford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

City are fourth in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn six, earning 63 points.

United are five places behind the away side in ninth, with 14 wins and two draws putting them on 44 points.

In the last five years, Ashford and Chichester have played each other on five occasions. Ashford won one of them, Chichester two, and they drew twice.

On average, Ashford scored 1.4 goals and Chichester 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Ashford won 2-1 away.

Ashford's last match was on April 6, a 5-1 loss against Sheppey United.

Chichester beat Erith and Belvedere 6-1 last time out, also on April 6.
