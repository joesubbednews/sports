Broadbridge Heath v Phoenix Sports

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Broadbridge Heath host Phoenix Sports on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on March 16, Broadbridge Heath beat Sittingbourne 1-0 away.

Phoenix Sports drew, 0-0 at home against East Grinstead Town.

Phoenix Sports are 18th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won six and drawn seven, earning 25 points.

Broadbridge Heath are five places ahead of the away team in 13th, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Phoenix Sports' form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Broadbridge Heath's 10.

With 37 goals in 30 games so far this season, Broadbridge Heath are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Phoenix Sports are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.
