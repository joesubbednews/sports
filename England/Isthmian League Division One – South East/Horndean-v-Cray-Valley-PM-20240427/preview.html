Horndean v Cray Valley PM

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cray Valley PM come to play Horndean on Saturday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The home side are 13 places behind Cray Valley PM in 15th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.
