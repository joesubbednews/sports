Lancing v Erith and Belvedere

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Culver Road
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Erith and Belvedere face a challenge away against high-flying Lancing at Culver Road on Saturday.

Erith and Belvedere are 19th in the Isthmian League Division One – South East table, and have picked up five wins and five draws in their 29 games to date.

Lancing, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 52 points, having won 16 and drawn four of their first 30 matches, and are 17 points behind table-toppers Ramsgate.

With 17 goals in 29 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 65 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Lancing, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Lancing's eight.

Lancing's last match was on March 9, a 2-2 draw against Broadbridge Heath.

Erith & Belvedere lost 3-1 against Cray Valley PM last time out, also on March 9.
