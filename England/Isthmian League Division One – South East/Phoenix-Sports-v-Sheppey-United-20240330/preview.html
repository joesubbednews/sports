Phoenix Sports v Sheppey United

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Phoenix Sports FC Ground
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheppey United make the journey to the Phoenix Sports FC Ground to play Phoenix Sports on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Sheppey's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned six from a possible 18.

Phoenix Sports are 18th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won six and drawn seven, earning 25 points.

Sheppey are eight places ahead of the home side in 10th, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

With 39 goals in 31 games so far this season, Phoenix Sports are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 80 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Phoenix Sports' last match was on March 23, a 3-1 loss against Broadbridge Heath.

Sheppey lost 2-0 against Chichester City last time out, also on March 23.
