Herne Bay v Beckenham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Safety Net Stadium
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Herne Bay host Beckenham Town on Saturday at the Safety Net Stadium in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on February 10, Herne Bay beat Broadbridge Heath 3-1 at home.

Beckenham drew, 3-3 away at Phoenix Sports on Wednesday.

Beckenham are bottom of the table after 24 games, of which they have won three and drawn five, earning 14 points.

Herne Bay are 15 places ahead of Town in fifth, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

With 31 goals in 24 games so far this season, the away side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Herne Bay are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Beckenham's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Herne Bay's 13.
