Erith and Belvedere v Horndean

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Erith and Belvedere face Horndean on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Isthmian League Division One – South East games.

Erith & Belvedere have lost four and drawn one matches since they last earned three points – against Hythe Town on February 6.

They face a Horndean side who have won just two and lost three over that time.

Erith & Belvedere are 19th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won five and drawn five, earning 20 points.

Horndean are five places ahead of the hosts in 14th, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

With 18 goals in 30 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 68 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Erith & Belvedere's last match was on March 16, a 3-1 loss against Lancing.

Horndean lost 3-0 against Chichester City last time out, also on March 16.
