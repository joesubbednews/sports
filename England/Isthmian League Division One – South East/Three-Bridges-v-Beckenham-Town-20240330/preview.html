Three Bridges v Beckenham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Jubilee Field
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges face Beckenham Town on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Three Bridges are unbeaten in seven, with four wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Beckenham team who have won one and drawn three over the same number of games.

Beckenham are bottom of the table after 30 games, of which they have won four and drawn seven, earning 19 points.

Three Bridges are 14 places ahead of Town in sixth, with 15 wins and seven draws putting them on 52 points.

With 75 goals in 31 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 59 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

In the last five years, Three Bridges and Beckenham have played each other on four occasions. They won two each.

On average, Three Bridges scored 2.0 goals and Beckenham 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Three Bridges won 5-1 away.

Three Bridges's last match was on March 23, a 3-2 win against Ashford United.

Beckenham lost 3-2 against Sittingbourne last time out, also on March 23.
