Beckenham Town v Erith and Belvedere

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Eden Park Avenue
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Beckenham Town face Erith and Belvedere at Eden Park Avenue on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Merstham on Saturday, Beckenham sit bottom of Isthmian League Division One South East.

They travel to play an Erith & Belvedere side 19th in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Phoenix Sports.

Beckenham are in bad form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

But with a win and five losses over that period, Erith & Belvedere's form is even worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to Town's four.

With 11 goals in 22 games so far this season, the away team are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.
