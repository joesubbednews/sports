Hythe Town v Horndean

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Reachfields Stadium
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hythe Town host Horndean at the Reachfields Stadium on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Hythe have picked up 11 points from their last six games, and they face a Horndean side who have lost their last two matches, and collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 40 goals in 22 games so far this season, Hythe are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Horndean, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The visitors are 15th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won eight and drawn four, earning 28 points.

Town are six places ahead of Horndean in ninth, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

Hythe's last match was on March 2, a 2-1 win against Herne Bay.

Horndean lost 3-1 against Three Bridges last time out, on February 27.
