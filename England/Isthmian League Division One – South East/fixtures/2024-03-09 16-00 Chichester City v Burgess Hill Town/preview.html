Chichester City v Burgess Hill Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chichester City face Burgess Hill Town on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Chichester are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Burgess Hill team who have drawn one and lost four over the same number of games.

With 54 goals in 28 games so far this season, Chichester are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Burgess Hill, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

City are sixth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn six, earning 45 points.

Town are six places behind the home side in 12th, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last five years, Chichester and Burgess Hill have played each other on five occasions. Chichester won three of them and they drew twice.

On average, Chichester scored 2.4 goals and Burgess Hill 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Chichester won 5-3 away.

Chichester's last match was on February 24, a 1-0 win against Sevenoaks Town.

Burgess Hill lost 3-1 against Merstham last time out, on February 27.
