Erith and Belvedere v Cray Valley PM

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cray Valley PM travel for Saturday's match against Erith and Belvedere looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Cray Valley PM, who sit second in the league after 27 games, fell to a 5-4 home defeat to Three Bridges on March 2.

They face an Erith & Belvedere side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Herne Bay, and who sit 19th in the table.

With 16 goals in 28 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Cray Valley PM, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Erith & Belvedere's five.
