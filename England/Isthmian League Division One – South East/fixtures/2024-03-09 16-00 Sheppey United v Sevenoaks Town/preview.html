Sheppey United v Sevenoaks Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Holm Park
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheppey United host Sevenoaks Town on Saturday at Holm Park in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on February 24, Sheppey lost to Merstham 1-0 at home.

Sevenoaks also lost, 1-0 at home against Broadbridge Heath on March 2.

With 48 goals in 26 games so far this season, Sheppey are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Sevenoaks, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

United are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

Sheppey are eighth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

The away team are two places behind Sheppey in 10th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

In the last three years, Sheppey and Sevenoaks have played each other on three occasions. Sheppey won two of them and Sevenoaks one.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Sheppey won 1-0 away.
