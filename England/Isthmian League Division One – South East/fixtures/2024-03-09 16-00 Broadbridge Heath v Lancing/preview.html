Broadbridge Heath v Lancing

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Broadbridge Heath host Lancing on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on March 2, Broadbridge Heath beat Sevenoaks Town 1-0 away.

Lancing drew, 1-1 at home against Beckenham Town.

With 34 goals in 28 games so far this season, Broadbridge Heath are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Lancing, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn three, earning 51 points.

The home team are nine places behind Lancing in 13th, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.

Broadbridge Heath are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Lancing's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Broadbridge Heath's nine.
