Beckenham Town v Ramsgate

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Eden Park Avenue
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Beckenham Town face a challenge against high-flying Ramsgate at Eden Park Avenue on Saturday.

Beckenham Town are rooted to the bottom of the Isthmian League Division One – South East table, and have picked up three wins and six draws in their 26 games to date.

Ramsgate, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 66 points, having won 21 and drawn three of their first 27 matches.

With 73 goals in 27 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Beckenham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

In the last three years, Beckenham and Ramsgate have played each other on three occasions. Ramsgate won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Ramsgate won 1-0 at home.

Beckenham's last match was on March 2, a 1-1 draw against Lancing.

Ramsgate beat Ashford United 4-0 last time out, also on March 2.
