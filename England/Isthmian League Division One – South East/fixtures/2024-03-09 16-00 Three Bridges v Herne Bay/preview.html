Three Bridges v Herne Bay

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Jubilee Field
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges host Herne Bay at Jubilee Field on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Three Bridges have picked up 10 points from their last six games, and they face a Herne Bay side who lost their last match, and have collected the same number of points from the last possible 18.

With 70 goals in 28 games so far this season, Three Bridges are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Herne Bay are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The home team are fifth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn five, earning 47 points.

The visitors are two places behind Three Bridges in seventh, with 12 wins and nine draws putting them on 45 points.

In the last three years, Three Bridges and Herne Bay have played each other on three occasions. Herne Bay won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when they played out a 3-3 draw.

Three Bridges's last match was on March 2, a 5-4 win against Cray Valley PM.

Herne Bay lost 2-1 against Hythe Town last time out, also on March 2.
