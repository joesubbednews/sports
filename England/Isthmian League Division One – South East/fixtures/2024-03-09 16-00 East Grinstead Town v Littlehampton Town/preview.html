East Grinstead Town v Littlehampton Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Estadio José Picasso Peratta
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

East Grinstead Town welcome Littlehampton Town to the Estadio José Picasso Peratta on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

East Grinstead's struggles have left them with just one point from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

East Grinstead are 17th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won seven and drawn three, earning 24 points.

Littlehampton are one place ahead of East Grinstead Town in 16th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

In the last three years, East Grinstead and Littlehampton have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when East Grinstead won 2-1 away.

With 33 goals in 27 games so far this season, Littlehampton Town are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

East Grinstead's last match was on March 2, a 2-0 loss against Sittingbourne.

Littlehampton lost 1-0 against Hythe Town last time out, on February 27.
