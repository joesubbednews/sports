Sheppey United v Ashford United

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Holm Park
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheppey United are on a poor run ahead of hosting Ashford United at Holm Park on Saturday, with just three points collected from their last six games.

Sheppey have picked up just one win in their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East games, and face an Ashford side whose last six games have brought three wins and three losses.

With 48 goals in 32 games so far this season, Ashford are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Sheppey, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Ashford United are eighth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn two, earning 44 points.

Sheppey United are one place behind the visitors in ninth, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

In the last three years, Sheppey and Ashford have played each other on three occasions. Sheppey won two of them and Ashford one.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Ashford won 3-2 at home.

Sheppey's last match was on Monday, a 3-0 win against Hythe Town.

Ashford beat Herne Bay 3-1 last time out, also on Monday.
