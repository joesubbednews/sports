Burgess Hill Town v Hythe Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Leylands Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hythe Town travel to Leylands Park looking to secure a first win in seven Isthmian League Division One – South East games against Burgess Hill Town on Saturday.

The Cannons have lost two and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Ashford United on December 26.

They face a Burgess Hill side who have won four and lost two over that time.

In the last 10 years, Burgess Hill and Hythe have played each other on seven occasions. Burgess Hill won two of them, Hythe four, and they drew once.

On average, Burgess Hill scored 2.0 goals and the Cannons 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

With 35 goals in 19 games so far this season, Hythe are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Burgess Hill, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Hythe Town are 12th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

Burgess Hill Town are two places ahead of the Cannons in 10th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

Burgess Hill's last match was on February 3, a 5-0 loss against Sittingbourne.

Hythe drew 4-4 with Littlehampton Town last time out, on February 10.
