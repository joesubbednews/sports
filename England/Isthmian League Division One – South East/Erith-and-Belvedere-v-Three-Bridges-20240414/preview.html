Erith and Belvedere v Three Bridges

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges visit Erith and Belvedere on Sunday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Three Bridges have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face an Erith & Belvedere side who lost their last match, and have collected two points from the last possible 18.

Erith & Belvedere are bottom of the table after 34 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

Three Bridges are 15 places ahead of the hosts in fifth, with 18 wins and seven draws putting them on 61 points.

With 23 goals in 34 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 79 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Erith & Belvedere's last match was on April 6, a 6-1 loss against Chichester City.

Three Bridges beat Sittingbourne 3-0 last time out, also on April 6.
