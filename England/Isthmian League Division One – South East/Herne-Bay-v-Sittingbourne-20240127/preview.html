Herne Bay v Sittingbourne

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Safety Net Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Herne Bay face Sittingbourne on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Herne Bay are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Sittingbourne team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 44 goals in 20 games so far this season, Sittingbourne are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Herne Bay are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn six, earning 39 points.

The home team are two places behind Sittingbourne in sixth, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last five years, Herne Bay and Sittingbourne have played each other on four occasions. Herne Bay won two of them, Sittingbourne one, and they drew once.

On average, Herne Bay scored 1.5 goals and Sittingbourne 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when Sittingbourne won 2-0 at home.

Herne Bay's last match was on January 20, a 2-0 win against Phoenix Sports.

Sittingbourne beat Broadbridge Heath 2-0 last time out, on January 13.
