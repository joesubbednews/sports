Erith and Belvedere v Burgess Hill Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Erith and Belvedere are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Burgess Hill Town on Saturday, with just one point collected from their last six games.
