East Grinstead Town v Lancing

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The SKYex Community Stadium
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.30pm

Lancing travel to the SKYex Community Stadium for Tuesday's late match against East Grinstead Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Lancing, who sit fourth in the league after 24 games, fell to a 3-0 home defeat to Herne Bay on Saturday.

They face an East Grinstead side who also lost their last match, a 7-1 defeat to Chichester City, and who sit 16th in the table.

With 31 goals in 23 games so far this season, East Grinstead are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Lancing, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

In the last five years, East Grinstead and Lancing have played each other on five occasions. East Grinstead won three of them, Lancing one, and they drew once.

On average, East Grinstead scored 1.6 goals and Lancing 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 18, when East Grinstead won 1-0 away.
