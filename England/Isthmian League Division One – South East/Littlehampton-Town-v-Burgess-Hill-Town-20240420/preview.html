Littlehampton Town v Burgess Hill Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Littlehampton Town welcome Burgess Hill Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Littlehampton's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

Littlehampton are 18th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

Burgess Hill are five places ahead of Littlehampton Town in 13th, with 10 wins and 11 draws putting them on 41 points.

With 39 goals in 34 games so far this season, the home side are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 72 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Burgess Hill Town are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Littlehampton and Burgess Hill have played each other on three occasions. Littlehampton won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on December 2, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Littlehampton's last match was on April 13, a 2-1 loss against Sevenoaks Town.

Burgess Hill drew 0-0 with Broadbridge Heath last time out, also on April 13.
