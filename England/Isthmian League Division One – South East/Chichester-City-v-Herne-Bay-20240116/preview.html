Chichester City v Herne Bay

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Herne Bay travel to Chichester City on late Tuesday in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Chichester, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-1 against Horndean.

With 39 goals in 20 games so far this season, Herne Bay are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Chichester, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away team are seventh in the table after 20 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

City are one place behind Herne Bay in eighth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

The hosts are in reasonable form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Herne Bay's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Chichester's 12.

Over the last two years, Chichester and Herne Bay have played each other twice. Herne Bay won both of them.

Their last meeting was on April 9 2022, when Herne Bay won 1-0 at home.
