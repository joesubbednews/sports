East Grinstead Town v Sittingbourne

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Estadio José Picasso Peratta
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.30pm

Sittingbourne visit East Grinstead Town at the Estadio José Picasso Peratta on late Tuesday on the back of three consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Sittingbourne have picked up 11 points from their last six games, and they face an East Grinstead side who drew their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 47 goals in 21 games so far this season, Sittingbourne are scoring more than average in the league with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

East Grinstead, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

Town are nine places behind Sittingbourne in 13th, with seven wins and three draws putting them on 24 points.

In the last five years, East Grinstead and Sittingbourne have played each other on five occasions. Sittingbourne won four of them and they drew once.

On average, East Grinstead scored 0.6 goals and Sittingbourne 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 8, when Sittingbourne won 3-1 away.

East Grinstead's last match was on Saturday, a 1-1 draw against Broadbridge Heath.

Sittingbourne beat Herne Bay 3-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
