Three Bridges v Sittingbourne

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Jubilee Field
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Three Bridges and Sittingbourne promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Isthmian League Division One South East's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Jubilee Field, Three Bridges and Sittingbourne sit second and fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 81 and 75 goals respectively.

Sittingbourne are third in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn seven, earning 67 points.

Three Bridges are two places behind the away side in fifth, with 17 wins and seven draws putting them on 58 points.

The hosts are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Sittingbourne's form is slightly worse – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Three Bridges's 14.

In the last five years, Three Bridges and Sittingbourne have played each other on five occasions. Three Bridges won two of them, Sittingbourne one, and they drew twice.

On average, Three Bridges scored 1.2 goals and Sittingbourne 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Sittingbourne won 4-1 at home.

Three Bridges's last match was on Monday, a 3-2 win against Burgess Hill Town.

Sittingbourne drew 2-2 with Ramsgate last time out, also on Monday.
