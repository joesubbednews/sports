Horndean v Sheppey United

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheppey United travel to Horndean on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Three Bridges 1-0 at home.

Horndean also won their last match, 4-0 against Broadbridge Heath.

With 48 goals in 25 games so far this season, Sheppey are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Horndean, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

United are sixth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

The home side are seven places behind the visitors in 13th, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

Horndean are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Sheppey's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Horndean's 13.
