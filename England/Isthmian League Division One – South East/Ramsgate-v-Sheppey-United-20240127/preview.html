Ramsgate v Sheppey United

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Southwood Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Ramsgate and Sheppey United promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Isthmian League Division One South East's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at the Southwood Stadium, Ramsgate and Sheppey sit top and fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 66 and 47 goals respectively.

Ramsgate are top of the table after 22 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn one, earning 58 points.

Sheppey are four places behind the home team in fifth, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

Ramsgate are in fantastic form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, United's form is much worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Ramsgate's 15.

In the last five years, Ramsgate and Sheppey have played each other on four occasions. Ramsgate won two of them, Sheppey one, and they drew once.

On average, Ramsgate scored 2.2 goals and Sheppey 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 14, when Ramsgate won 1-0 away.

Ramsgate's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 win against Three Bridges.

Sheppey beat Littlehampton Town 2-1 last time out, on January 20.
