Erith and Belvedere v Sittingbourne

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Erith and Belvedere face Sittingbourne on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-1 victory over Hythe Town on Tuesday, Erith & Belvedere sit 19th in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

They travel to play a Sittingbourne side third in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Sevenoaks Town.

Erith & Belvedere are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Sittingbourne's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to the home side's seven.

With 15 goals in 25 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.
