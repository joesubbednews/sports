Sheppey United v Hythe Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Holm Park
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheppey United welcome Hythe Town to Holm Park on Monday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Sheppey's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned four from a possible 18.

With 46 goals in 29 games so far this season, Hythe are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Sheppey, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, Sheppey and Hythe have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on January 1, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Town are ninth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

United are two places behind the Cannons in 11th, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

Sheppey's last match was on Saturday, a 3-1 loss against Phoenix Sports.

Hythe lost 1-0 against Merstham last time out, also on Saturday.
