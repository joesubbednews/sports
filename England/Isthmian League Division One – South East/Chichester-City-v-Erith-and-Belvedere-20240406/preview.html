Chichester City v Erith and Belvedere

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Erith and Belvedere face a challenge away against high-flying Chichester City on Saturday.

Erith and Belvedere are 19th in the Isthmian League Division One – South East table, and have picked up five wins and seven draws in their 33 games to date.

Chichester, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 60 points, having won 18 and drawn six, and are 19 points behind table-toppers Ramsgate.

Chichester are in exceptional form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With no wins and three draws over that period, Erith & Belvedere's form is much worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to City's 18.

With 22 goals in 33 games so far this season, the away team are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 73 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Chichester's last match was on Monday, a 2-0 win against Littlehampton Town.

Erith & Belvedere drew 1-1 with East Grinstead Town last time out, also on Monday.
