Ashford United v Herne Bay

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Homelands
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Herne Bay travel to Ashford United on Monday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten East Grinstead Town 3-2 at home.

Ashford also won their last match, 2-1 against Sevenoaks Town.

With 45 goals in 31 games so far this season, Ashford are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Herne Bay, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

United are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the home side's six.

In the last five years, Ashford and Herne Bay have played each other on four occasions. Ashford won two of them, Herne Bay one, and they drew once.

On average, Ashford scored 1.8 goals and Herne Bay 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 1, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Herne Bay are seventh in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 11, earning 53 points.

Ashford are three places behind Herne Bay in 10th, with 13 wins and two draws putting them on 41 points.
