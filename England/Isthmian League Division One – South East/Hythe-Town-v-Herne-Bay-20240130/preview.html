Hythe Town v Herne Bay

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Reachfields Stadium
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Hythe Town host Herne Bay on Tuesday at the Reachfields Stadium in Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Hythe drew with Sevenoaks Town 0-0 away.

Herne Bay lost, 3-0 at home against Sittingbourne.

With 44 goals in 23 games so far this season, Herne Bay are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Hythe are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The visitors are seventh in the table after 23 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

Town are five places behind Herne Bay in 12th, with eight wins and three draws putting them on 27 points.

The Cannons are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Herne Bay's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's eight.
