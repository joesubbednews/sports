Cray Valley PM v Horndean

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Cray Valley PM host Horndean on late Tuesday on the back of four consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Cray Valley PM have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Horndean side who lost their last match, and have collected nine points from the last possible 18.

With 52 goals in 20 games so far this season, Cray Valley PM are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Horndean, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The hosts are second in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn five, earning 50 points.

The away side are 12 places behind Cray Valley PM in 14th, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

Cray Valley PM's last match was on Saturday, a 2-0 win against Ashford United.

Horndean lost 1-0 against Burgess Hill Town last time out, also on Saturday.
