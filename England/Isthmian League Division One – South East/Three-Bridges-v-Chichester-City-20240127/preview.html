Three Bridges v Chichester City

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Jubilee Field
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges welcome Chichester City to Jubilee Field on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Three Bridges's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned 10 from a possible 18.

With 55 goals in 20 games so far this season, Three Bridges are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Chichester are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Three Bridges and Chichester have played each other on six occasions. Three Bridges won two of them, Chichester three, and they drew once.

On average, Three Bridges scored 1.0 goal and Chichester 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when Three Bridges won 1-0 away.

The hosts are seventh in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

City are one place behind Three Bridges in eighth, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on the same number of points.

Three Bridges's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 loss against Ramsgate.

Chichester lost 3-0 against Cray Valley PM last time out, also on Tuesday.
