Burgess Hill Town v Three Bridges

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Leylands Park
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges face Burgess Hill Town on Monday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Three Bridges are unbeaten in eight, with five wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Burgess Hill team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 78 goals in 32 games so far this season, Three Bridges are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Burgess Hill, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The away side are fifth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn seven, earning 55 points.

Town are seven places behind Three Bridges in 12th, with 10 wins and 10 draws putting them on 40 points.

In the last five years, Burgess Hill and Three Bridges have played each other on five occasions. Burgess Hill won three of them and they drew twice.

On average, Burgess Hill scored 1.4 goals and Three Bridges 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 1, when Burgess Hill won 3-2 away.

Burgess Hill's last match was on Saturday, a 2-2 draw against Erith and Belvedere.

Three Bridges beat Beckenham Town 3-2 last time out, also on Saturday.
