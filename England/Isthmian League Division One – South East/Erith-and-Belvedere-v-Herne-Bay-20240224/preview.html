Erith and Belvedere v Herne Bay

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Herne Bay visit Erith and Belvedere on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Herne Bay have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face an Erith & Belvedere side who have lost their last two matches, and collected seven points from the last possible 18.

Erith & Belvedere are 19th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won five and drawn four, earning 19 points.

Herne Bay are 14 places ahead of the hosts in fifth, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on 44 points.

With 15 goals in 27 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Erith & Belvedere's last match was on February 17, a 3-0 loss against Sevenoaks Town.

Herne Bay beat Beckenham Town 3-2 last time out, also on February 17.
