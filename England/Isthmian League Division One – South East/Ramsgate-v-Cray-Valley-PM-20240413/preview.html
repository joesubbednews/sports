Ramsgate v Cray Valley PM

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Southwood Stadium
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

The Isthmian League Division One South East's top two sides face each other at the Southwood Stadium in Saturday's kick-off, when Ramsgate host second-placed Cray Valley PM.

Ramsgate have picked up 26 wins and four draws from 33 games so far this season, and sit on the same number of points as the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Cray Valley PM, meanwhile, have won 25 and drawn seven, picking up 82 points.

With 93 goals in 33 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Cray Valley PM are also above average scorers, with 2.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The home team are in fantastic form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

But with six wins and no losses over that period, the visitors' form is even better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to Ramsgate's 16.

In the last five years, Ramsgate and Cray Valley PM have played each other on five occasions. Ramsgate won one of them, Cray Valley PM two, and they drew twice.

On average, Ramsgate scored 1.2 goals and Cray Valley PM 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Ramsgate's last match was on April 6, a 2-0 win against Phoenix Sports.

Cray Valley PM beat Burgess Hill Town 5-0 last time out, also on April 6.
