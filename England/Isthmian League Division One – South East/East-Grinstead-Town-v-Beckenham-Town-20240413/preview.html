East Grinstead Town v Beckenham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Estadio José Picasso Peratta
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Beckenham Town face East Grinstead Town away at the Estadio José Picasso Peratta on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 5-2 victory over Hythe Town on April 6, Beckenham sit 19th in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

They travel to play an East Grinstead side 18th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-0 against Lancing.

East Grinstead are in terrible form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Beckenham's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to East Grinstead Town's two.

With 43 goals in 33 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 76 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

Beckenham Town are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

In the last five years, East Grinstead and Beckenham have played each other on five occasions. Beckenham won three of them and they drew twice.

On average, East Grinstead scored 0.8 goals and Beckenham 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Beckenham won 3-0 at home.
