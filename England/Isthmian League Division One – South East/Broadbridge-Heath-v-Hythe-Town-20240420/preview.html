Broadbridge Heath v Hythe Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hythe Town make the journey to play Broadbridge Heath on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their seven-game losing streak.

Hythe's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned 11 from a possible 18.

With 44 goals in 35 games so far this season, Broadbridge Heath are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Hythe are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are 12th in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

The hosts are one place ahead of the Cannons in 11th, with 11 wins and 10 draws putting them on 43 points.

Broadbridge Heath's last match was on April 13, a 0-0 draw against Burgess Hill Town.

Hythe lost 1-0 against Phoenix Sports last time out, also on April 13.
