Sittingbourne v Sevenoaks Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Sittingbourne host Sevenoaks Town on late Tuesday on the back of five consecutive wins in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Sittingbourne have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Sevenoaks side who lost their last match, and have collected two points from the last possible 18.

With 54 goals in 23 games so far this season, Sittingbourne are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Sevenoaks, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The home side are third in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

Town are eight places behind Sittingbourne in 11th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

In the last five years, Sittingbourne and Sevenoaks have played each other on five occasions. They won one each, and they drew three times.

On average, Sittingbourne scored 0.8 goals and Sevenoaks 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Sittingbourne's last match was on Saturday, a 5-0 win against Burgess Hill Town.

Sevenoaks lost 3-0 against Horndean last time out, also on Saturday.
