Hythe Town v Ramsgate

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Reachfields Stadium
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of Isthmian League Division One South East's meanest defences go head-to-head at the Reachfields Stadium on Saturday, when Hythe Town host Ramsgate.

Only one side – Cray Valley PM – has conceded fewer goals than Hythe to date: the home side have let in just 18 goals in 16 games.

Ramsgate have conceded 20 goals in 23 games, giving them the joint-third tightest back line so far this season.

Ramsgate are top of the table after 23 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn two, earning 59 points.

Hythe are 11 places behind the away team in 12th, with eight wins and three draws putting them on 27 points.

In the last 10 years, Hythe and Ramsgate have played each other on six occasions. Hythe won one of them, Ramsgate four, and they drew once.

On average, the Cannons scored 1.3 goals and Ramsgate 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 22, when Ramsgate won 3-1 at home.

Town are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Ramsgate's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the Cannons' eight.

Hythe's last match was on January 27, a 0-0 draw against Sevenoaks Town.

Ramsgate drew 0-0 with Sheppey United last time out, also on January 27.
