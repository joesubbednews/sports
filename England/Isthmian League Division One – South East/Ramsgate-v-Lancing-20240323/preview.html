Ramsgate v Lancing

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Southwood Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Isthmian League Division One South East's top sides face each other at the Southwood Stadium in Saturday's kick-off, when table-topping Ramsgate host fourth-placed Lancing.

Ramsgate have picked up 23 wins and three draws from 29 games so far this season, and sit 17 points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Lancing, meanwhile, have won 17 and drawn four of 31, picking up 55 points.

With 80 goals in 29 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Lancing are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Ramsgate's 13.

In the last five years, Ramsgate and Lancing have played each other on five occasions. Ramsgate won three of them, Lancing one, and they drew once.

On average, Ramsgate scored 2.0 goals and Lancing 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 6, when Lancing won 4-2 at home.

Ramsgate's last match was on March 16, a 2-1 win against Burgess Hill Town.

Lancing beat Erith and Belvedere 3-1 last time out, also on March 16.
