Burgess Hill Town v Broadbridge Heath

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Leylands Park
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Burgess Hill Town host Broadbridge Heath on Saturday at Leylands Park in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on April 6, Burgess Hill lost to Cray Valley PM 5-0 away.

Broadbridge Heath drew, 1-1 at home against Horndean.

With 44 goals in 34 games so far this season, Broadbridge Heath are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Burgess Hill are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's seven.

Burgess Hill are 13th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn 10, earning 40 points.

Broadbridge Heath are two places ahead of Burgess Hill in 11th, with 11 wins and nine draws putting them on 42 points.
