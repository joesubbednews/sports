Hythe Town v Erith and Belvedere

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Reachfields Stadium
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Erith and Belvedere come to the Reachfields Stadium to play Hythe Town on late Tuesday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while the Cannons have a win and three draws.

Erith & Belvedere are 19th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won four and drawn four, earning 16 points.

Hythe are seven places ahead of the away team in 12th, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

With 12 goals in 24 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Hythe's last match was on Saturday, a 2-2 draw against Ramsgate.

Erith & Belvedere lost 1-0 against Broadbridge Heath last time out, also on Saturday.
