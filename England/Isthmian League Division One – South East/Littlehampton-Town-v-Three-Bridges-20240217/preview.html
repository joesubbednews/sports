Littlehampton Town v Three Bridges

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Three Bridges make the journey to play Littlehampton Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Three Bridges's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned 10 from a possible 18.

With 58 goals in 24 games so far this season, Three Bridges are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Littlehampton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Town are 15th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won seven and drawn four, earning 25 points.

The away side are seven places ahead of the hosts in eighth, with 11 wins and four draws putting them on 37 points.

In the last three years, Littlehampton and Three Bridges have played each other on three occasions. Littlehampton won one of them and Three Bridges the other.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Three Bridges won 3-1 at home.

Littlehampton's last match was on February 10, a 4-4 draw against Hythe Town.

Three Bridges lost 2-0 against Cray Valley PM last time out, on February 6.
