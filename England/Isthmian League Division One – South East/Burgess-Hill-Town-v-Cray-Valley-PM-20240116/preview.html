Burgess Hill Town v Cray Valley PM

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Leylands Park
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.30pm

Cray Valley PM face Burgess Hill Town on Tuesday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Isthmian League Division One South East.

Cray Valley PM are unbeaten in 17, with 12 wins and five draws, ahead of the 7.30pm kick-off.

They face a Burgess Hill team who have won five and drawn seven over the same number of games.

With 46 goals in 17 games so far this season, Cray Valley PM are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Burgess Hill, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The away team are second in the table after 17 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

Town are 10 places behind Cray Valley PM in 12th, with six wins and eight draws putting them on 26 points.

In the last five years, Burgess Hill and Cray Valley PM have played each other on five occasions. Burgess Hill won two of them and Cray Valley PM three.

On average, Burgess Hill scored 1.2 goals and Cray Valley PM 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 1, when Cray Valley PM won 3-1 at home.

Burgess Hill's last match was on Saturday, a 4-2 loss against Ramsgate.

Cray Valley PM beat Sheppey United 3-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
