Horndean v Lancing

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Horndean host Lancing on Monday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

In their last league match, on Friday, Horndean lost to Sittingbourne 3-0 away.

Lancing also lost, 4-0 at home against Chichester City on Saturday.

With 44 goals in 28 games so far this season, Horndean are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Lancing, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Horndean's six.

Lancing are sixth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn four, earning 55 points.

Horndean are eight places behind Lancing in 14th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.
