East Grinstead Town v Erith and Belvedere

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Estadio José Picasso Peratta
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Monday's match at the Estadio José Picasso Peratta sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 17th-ranked East Grinstead Town host 19th-placed Erith and Belvedere.

East Grinstead have picked up 28 points from their first 28 Isthmian League Division One – South East games, with eight wins and four draws.

That is seven points more than Erith & Belvedere have collected, having won five and drawn six.

East Grinstead are in bad form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

But with no wins and two draws over that period, Erith & Belvedere's form is even worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to Town's four.

With 21 goals in 32 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 72 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

East Grinstead's last match was on Saturday, a 3-2 loss against Herne Bay.

Erith & Belvedere drew 2-2 with Burgess Hill Town last time out, also on Saturday.
