Sheppey United v Erith and Belvedere

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Holm Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheppey United host Erith and Belvedere at Holm Park on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Sheppey have picked up nine points from their last six games, and they face an Erith & Belvedere side who have lost their last two matches, and collected two points from the last possible 18.

Erith & Belvedere are bottom of the table after 35 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

Sheppey are 12 places ahead of the visitors in eighth, with 14 wins and seven draws putting them on 49 points.

With 23 goals in 35 games so far this season, Erith & Belvedere are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 81 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Sheppey's last match was on April 13, a 2-1 win against Herne Bay.

Erith & Belvedere lost 2-0 against Three Bridges last time out, on Sunday.
