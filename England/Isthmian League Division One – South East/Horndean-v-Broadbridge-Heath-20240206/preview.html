Horndean v Broadbridge Heath

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Horndean host Broadbridge Heath on Tuesday in Isthmian League Division One South East.

The hosts are 14th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won seven and drawn four, earning 25 points.

The away side are one place ahead of Horndean in 13th, with six wins and seven draws putting them on the same number of points.

Horndean are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.
