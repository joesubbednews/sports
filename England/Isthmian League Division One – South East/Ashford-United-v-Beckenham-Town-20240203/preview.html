Ashford United v Beckenham Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Homelands
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ashford United welcome Beckenham Town to the Homelands on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Ashford's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

Beckenham are bottom of the table after 22 games, of which they have won three and drawn four, earning 13 points.

Ashford are nine places ahead of Town in 11th, with nine wins and one draw putting them on 28 points.

With 26 goals in 22 games so far this season, the away side are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last five years, Ashford and Beckenham have played each other on five occasions. Ashford won three of them, Beckenham one, and they drew once.

On average, Ashford scored 1.6 goals and Beckenham 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 14, when Ashford won 1-0 away.

Ashford's last match was on January 27, a 2-0 loss against Cray Valley PM.

Beckenham drew 1-1 with Erith and Belvedere last time out, on Tuesday.
