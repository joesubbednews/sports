Ashford United v Ramsgate

Isthmian League Division One – South East | The Homelands
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Ramsgate come to the Homelands to play Ashford United on late Tuesday in great form, having collected 15 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and lost one of their last six fixtures, while Ashford have two wins and a draw.

With 61 goals in 20 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 3.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Ashford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The visitors are top of the table after 20 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn one, earning 52 points.

United are nine places behind Ramsgate in 10th, with nine wins and one draw putting them on 28 points.

In the last five years, Ashford and Ramsgate have played each other on five occasions. Ashford won three of them and Ramsgate two.

On average, Ashford scored 2.4 goals and Ramsgate 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 22, when Ramsgate won 2-1 away.

Ashford's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 loss against Littlehampton Town.

Ramsgate beat Burgess Hill Town 4-2 last time out, also on Saturday.
