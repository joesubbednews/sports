Horndean v Ramsgate

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Ramsgate travel for Tuesday's late match against Horndean looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Ramsgate, who sit top of the league after 25 games, fell to a 2-0 home defeat to Merstham on Saturday.

They face a Horndean side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-0 victory against Broadbridge Heath, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 68 goals in 25 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Horndean, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are in good form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Horndean's 13.
