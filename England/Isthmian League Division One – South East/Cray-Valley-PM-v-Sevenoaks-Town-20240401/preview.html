Cray Valley PM v Sevenoaks Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cray Valley PM host Sevenoaks Town on Monday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

Cray Valley PM have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Sevenoaks side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 78 goals in 31 games so far this season, Cray Valley PM are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Sevenoaks, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are second in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 23 and drawn seven, earning 76 points.

Town are six places behind Cray Valley PM in eighth, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

In the last five years, Cray Valley PM and Sevenoaks have played each other on five occasions. Cray Valley PM won three of them and Sevenoaks two.

On average, Cray Valley PM scored 1.4 goals and Sevenoaks 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 1, when Cray Valley PM won 1-0 away.

Cray Valley PM's last match was on Saturday, a 3-2 win against Broadbridge Heath.

Sevenoaks lost 2-1 against Ashford United last time out, on Friday.
