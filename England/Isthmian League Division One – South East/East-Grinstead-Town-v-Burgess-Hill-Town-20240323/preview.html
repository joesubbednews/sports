East Grinstead Town v Burgess Hill Town

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Estadio José Picasso Peratta
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

East Grinstead Town and Burgess Hill Town both come into Saturday's kick-off at the Estadio José Picasso Peratta in poor form, having picked up only four points each in their last six games.

The two teams have each collected one win and one draw from their last six outings.

East Grinstead are 16th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won eight and drawn four, earning 28 points.

Burgess Hill are four places ahead of East Grinstead Town in 12th, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

With 39 goals in 29 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Burgess Hill Town are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last five years, East Grinstead and Burgess Hill have played each other on five occasions. East Grinstead won one of them and Burgess Hill the other.

On average, East Grinstead scored 0.6 goals and Burgess Hill 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 6, when Burgess Hill won 3-0 at home.

East Grinstead's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-2 loss against Hythe Town.

Burgess Hill beat Ashford United 3-0 last time out, also on Tuesday.
