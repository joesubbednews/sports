Phoenix Sports v Ramsgate

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Phoenix Sports FC Ground
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ramsgate come to the Phoenix Sports FC Ground to play Phoenix Sports on Saturday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Phoenix Sports have three wins and two draws.

With 91 goals in 32 games so far this season, Ramsgate are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Phoenix Sports, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

The away side are top of the table after 32 games, of which they have won 25 and drawn four, earning 79 points.

The hosts are 15 places behind Ramsgate in 16th, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.

In the last three years, Phoenix Sports and Ramsgate have played each other on three occasions. Ramsgate won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Ramsgate won 9-1 at home.

Phoenix Sports' last match was on Monday, a 2-1 win against Beckenham Town.

Ramsgate drew 2-2 with Sittingbourne last time out, also on Monday.
