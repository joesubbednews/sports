Beckenham Town v Sheppey United

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Eden Park Avenue
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheppey United travel to Beckenham Town on Saturday in the Isthmian League Division One South East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Three Bridges 1-0 at home.

Beckenham, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-2 against Ashford United.

Beckenham are bottom of the table after 23 games, of which they have won three and drawn four, earning 13 points.

Sheppey are 15 places ahead of Town in fifth, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

With 28 goals in 23 games so far this season, the home team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Beckenham are in disappointing form in the Isthmian League Division One South East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Beckenham's five.

In the last three years, Beckenham and Sheppey have played each other on three occasions. Beckenham won one of them and Sheppey the other.

Their last meeting was on November 7, when Sheppey won 2-0 at home.
