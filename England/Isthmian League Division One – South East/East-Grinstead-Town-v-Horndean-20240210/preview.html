East Grinstead Town v Horndean

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Estadio José Picasso Peratta
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

East Grinstead Town welcome Horndean to the Estadio José Picasso Peratta on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

East Grinstead's struggles have left them with five points from their last six Isthmian League Division One – South East matches, while their opponents have earned 13 from a possible 18.

East Grinstead are 16th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won seven and drawn three, earning 24 points.

Horndean are three places ahead of Town in 13th, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

With 31 goals in 24 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

East Grinstead's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-0 loss against Lancing.

Horndean beat Broadbridge Heath 4-0 last time out, also on Tuesday.
