Three Bridges v Ramsgate

Isthmian League Division One – South East | Jubilee Field
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Tuesday's late match between Three Bridges and Ramsgate promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Isthmian League Division One South East's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ramsgate are top of the table after 21 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn one, earning 55 points.

Three Bridges are six places behind the visitors in seventh, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

The home side are in mixed form in Isthmian League Division One South East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, Ramsgate's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Three Bridges's seven.

In the last five years, Three Bridges and Ramsgate have played each other on five occasions. They won two each, and they drew once.

On average, Three Bridges scored 1.4 goals and Ramsgate 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 28, when Three Bridges won 2-0 at home.

Three Bridges's last match was on January 6, a 3-2 loss against Ashford United.

Ramsgate beat Ashford United 3-2 last time out, on January 16.
