Hebburn Town v Belper Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Belper Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Hebburn Town defence on Saturday.

With 61 goals in 32 games, Belper are the fourth-highest scorers in the Northern Premier League Division One East ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Hebburn side who have scored 57 in 30 matches, but conceded only 34 goals, putting them third among the league's tightest defences – only Stockton Town and Dunston UTS have conceded fewer goals.

Hebburn are third in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn six, earning 60 points.

Belper are three places behind Hebburn Town in sixth, with 17 wins and six draws putting them on 57 points.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Belper Town's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Hebburn's 16.

Hebburn's last match was on April 13, a 3-0 win against Grimsby Borough.

Belper lost 5-0 against Cleethorpes Town last time out, also on April 13.
