Ashington AFC v North Ferriby

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ashington AFC welcome North Ferriby on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Ashington's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Northern Premier League Division One – East matches, while their opponents have earned nine from a possible 18.

With 59 goals in 35 games so far this season, Ashington are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

North Ferriby, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home team are 14th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

The visitors are six places ahead of Ashington in eighth, with 16 wins and three draws putting them on 51 points.

Ashington's last match was on April 20, a 2-0 loss against Liversedge.

North Ferriby beat Sheffield FC 1-0 last time out, also on April 20.
