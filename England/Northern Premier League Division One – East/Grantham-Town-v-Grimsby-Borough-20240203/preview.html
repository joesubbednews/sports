Grantham Town v Grimsby Borough

Northern Premier League Division One – East | South Kesteven Stadium
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Grantham Town face Grimsby Borough at the South Kesteven Stadium on Saturday looking to secure a first win in four Northern Premier League Division One – East games.

Grantham have lost none and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Brighouse Town on January 6.

They face a Grimsby side who have won just one and drawn one over that time.

Grantham are 19th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won four and drawn eight, earning 20 points.

Grimsby are seven places ahead of Town in 12th, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

With 30 goals in 24 games so far this season, Borough are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

In the last three years, Grantham and Grimsby have played each other on three occasions. Grantham won one of them and Grimsby the other.

Their last meeting was on September 16, when Grantham won 2-1 away.

Grantham's last match was on Tuesday, a 0-0 draw against Dunston UTS.

Grimsby lost 2-1 against Winterton Rangers last time out, on January 27.
