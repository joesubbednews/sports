Hebburn Town v Cleethorpes Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hebburn Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Cleethorpes Town defence on Saturday.

With 39 goals in 21 games, Hebburn are the joint-fourth-highest scorers in the Northern Premier League Division One East ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Cleethorpes side who have scored 29 in 20 matches, but conceded only 22 goals, putting them third among the league's tightest defences – only Stockton Town FC and Dunston UTS have conceded fewer goals.

Hebburn are second in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn four, earning 40 points.

Cleethorpes are nine places behind Hebburn Town in 11th, with seven wins and seven draws putting them on 28 points.

In the last five years, Hebburn and Cleethorpes have played each other on five occasions. Hebburn won two of them, Cleethorpes one, and they drew twice.

On average, Hebburn scored 1.4 goals and Cleethorpes 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Cleethorpes Town's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Hebburn's eight.

Hebburn's last match was on January 13, a 3-3 draw against Pontefract Collieries.

Cleethorpes beat Sheffield FC 2-1 last time out, also on January 13.
