Dunston UTS v Brighouse Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Dunston UTS host Brighouse Town in Saturday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Dunston UTS, who sit second in the league after 29 games, fell to a 3-2 home defeat to Carlton Town on March 23.

They face a Brighouse side who also lost their last match, a 2-0 defeat to Hebburn Town, and who sit 17th in the table.

With 54 goals in 29 games so far this season, Dunston UTS are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Brighouse, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The home side are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to Dunston UTS's 13.

In the last 10 years, Dunston UTS and Brighouse have played each other on six occasions. Dunston UTS won four of them, Brighouse one, and they drew once.

On average, Dunston UTS scored 2.3 goals and Brighouse 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 25, when Dunston UTS won 2-0 away.
