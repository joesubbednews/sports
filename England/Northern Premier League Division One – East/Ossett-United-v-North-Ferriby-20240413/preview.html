Ossett United v North Ferriby

Northern Premier League Division One – East | The Stade France Ingfield
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Ferriby travel to Ossett United on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Stocksbridge Park Steels 3-1 at home.

Ossett, meanwhile, lost their last match, 4-0 against Liversedge.

Ossett are 18th in the table after 32 games, of which they have won seven and drawn eight, earning 29 points.

North Ferriby are 10 places ahead of United in eighth, with 15 wins and three draws putting them on 48 points.

With 30 goals in 32 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Ossett are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, North Ferriby's form is similar – they have both taken six points from 18.

Over the last two years, Ossett and North Ferriby have played each other twice. North Ferriby won both of them.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when North Ferriby won 3-1 at home.
