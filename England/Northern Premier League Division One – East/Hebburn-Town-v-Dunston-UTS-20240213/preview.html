Hebburn Town v Dunston UTS

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Two of the Northern Premier League Division One East's top sides face each other in Tuesday's late kick-off, when fourth-placed Hebburn Town host third-placed Dunston UTS.

Hebburn have picked up 13 wins and five draws from 24 games so far this season, and sit on the same number of points as the visitors going into the 7.45pm match.

Dunston UTS, meanwhile, have won 13 and drawn five of 21, picking up 44 points.

With 40 goals in 21 games so far this season, Dunston UTS are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Hebburn are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Hebburn and Dunston UTS have played each other on six occasions. Hebburn won three of them, Dunston UTS two, and they drew once.

On average, Hebburn scored 1.2 goals and Dunston UTS 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 28, when Hebburn won 1-0 away.

Town are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to the home side's six.

Hebburn's last match was on February 3, a 3-1 win against Consett.

Dunston UTS beat Ashington AFC 4-1 last time out, also on February 3.
