Grimsby Borough v Consett

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Consett come to play Grimsby Borough on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just one point from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn just one of their last six fixtures, while Grimsby have two wins and a draw.

With 39 goals in 30 games so far this season, Grimsby are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Consett, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The away team are eighth in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn three, earning 42 points.

Borough are three places behind Consett in 11th, with 11 wins and four draws putting them on 37 points.

In the last three years, Grimsby and Consett have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when Consett won 2-0 at home.

Grimsby's last match was on March 5, a 3-2 loss against Sheffield FC.

Consett lost 3-2 against Stocksbridge Park Steels last time out, on Tuesday.
