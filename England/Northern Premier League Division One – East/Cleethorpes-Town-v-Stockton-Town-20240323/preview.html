Cleethorpes Town v Stockton Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bradley Community Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stockton Town face Cleethorpes Town on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Stockton are unbeaten in 20, with 14 wins and six draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Cleethorpes team who have won four and drawn seven over the same number of games.

With 63 goals in 31 games so far this season, Stockton are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Cleethorpes, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Stockton Town are top of the table after 31 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn eight, earning 68 points.

Cleethorpes Town are 12 places behind the visitors in 13th, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

In the last five years, Cleethorpes and Stockton have played each other on five occasions. Cleethorpes won one of them, Stockton three, and they drew once.

On average, Cleethorpes scored 1.2 goals and Stockton 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 10, when Stockton won 4-3 at home.

Cleethorpes's last match was on March 16, a 4-0 loss against Dunston UTS.

Stockton beat Grantham Town 2-0 last time out, also on March 16.
