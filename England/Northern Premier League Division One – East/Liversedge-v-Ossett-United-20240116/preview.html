Liversedge v Ossett United

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Ossett United make the journey to play Liversedge on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their six-game losing streak.

The home side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Liversedge's last match was on Saturday, a 2-0 loss against Newton Aycliffe.

Ossett lost 2-1 against Ashington AFC last time out, also on Saturday.
