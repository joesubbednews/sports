Stockton Town v Liversedge

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stockton Town face Liversedge on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Stockton are unbeaten in 10, with eight wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Liversedge team who have won three and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 50 goals in 21 games so far this season, Stockton are the league's highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Liversedge, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 21 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

The away side are 14 places behind the hosts in 15th, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

In the last five years, Stockton and Liversedge have played each other on four occasions. Stockton won one of them, Liversedge two, and they drew once.

On average, Stockton scored 1.5 goals and Liversedge 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Liversedge won 3-2 at home.

Stockton's last match was on January 13, a 2-2 draw against Bridlington Town.

Liversedge lost 2-0 against Newton Aycliffe last time out, also on January 13.
