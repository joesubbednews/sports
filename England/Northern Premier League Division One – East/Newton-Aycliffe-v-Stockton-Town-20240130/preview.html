Newton Aycliffe v Stockton Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Stockton Town face Newton Aycliffe on Tuesday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Stockton are unbeaten in 12, with eight wins and four draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Newton Aycliffe team who have won seven and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 50 goals in 23 games so far this season, Stockton are the league's highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Newton Aycliffe, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 23 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

The hosts are seven places behind the away team in eighth, with 11 wins and one draw putting them on 34 points.

Newton Aycliffe's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 loss against Belper Town.

Stockton drew 0-0 with Dunston UTS last time out, also on Saturday.
