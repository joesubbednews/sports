Grimsby Borough v Ashington AFC

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bradley Community Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Grimsby Borough host Ashington AFC on Saturday at the Bradley Community Stadium in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

In their last league match, on February 3, Grimsby beat Grantham Town 2-1 away.

Ashington lost, 4-1 away at Dunston UTS.

With 32 goals in 25 games so far this season, Grimsby are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Ashington, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Borough are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.

Ashington are 13th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won nine and drawn three, earning 30 points.

Grimsby are three places ahead of Ashington in 10th, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.
