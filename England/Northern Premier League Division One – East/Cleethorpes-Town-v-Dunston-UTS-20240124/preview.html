Cleethorpes Town v Dunston UTS

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bradley Community Stadium
Wednesday, 24 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Two of the Northern Premier League Division One East's meanest defences go head-to-head at the Bradley Community Stadium on Wednesday, when Dunston UTS visit Cleethorpes Town.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Dunston UTS to date: the away side have let in just 17 goals in 17 games.

Cleethorpes have conceded 22 goals in 21 games, giving them the third tightest back line so far this season.

Cleethorpes are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Dunston UTS's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to Town's nine.

The visitors are sixth in the table after 17 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn three, earning 36 points.

The home side are five places behind Dunston UTS in 11th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last five years, Cleethorpes and Dunston UTS have played each other on four occasions. Cleethorpes won one of them, Dunston UTS two, and they drew once.

On average, Cleethorpes scored 1.2 goals and Dunston UTS 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 28, when Dunston UTS won 1-0 away.

Cleethorpes's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Hebburn Town.

Dunston UTS beat Consett 2-0 last time out, on January 13.
