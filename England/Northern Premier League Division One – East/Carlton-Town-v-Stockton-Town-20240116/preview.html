Carlton Town v Stockton Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Two of the Northern Premier League Division One East's top sides face each other in Tuesday's late kick-off, when fourth-placed Carlton Town host table-topping Stockton Town.

Carlton have picked up 13 wins from 20 games so far this season, and sit seven points below the visitors going into the 7.45pm match.

Stockton, meanwhile, have won 14 and drawn four of 21, picking up 46 points.

With 50 goals in 21 games so far this season, Stockton are the league's highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Carlton, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Carlton Town are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Stockton Town's form is slightly better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to the home side's 12.

Over the last two years, Carlton and Stockton have played each other twice. Stockton won both of them.

Their last meeting was on March 25, when Stockton won 2-0 away.

Carlton's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 win against Winterton Rangers.

Stockton drew 2-2 with Bridlington Town last time out, also on Saturday.
