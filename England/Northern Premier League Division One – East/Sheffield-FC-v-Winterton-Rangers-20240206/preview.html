Sheffield FC v Winterton Rangers

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Winterton Rangers come to play Sheffield FC on late Tuesday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Sheffield FC have three wins and a draw.

Winterton are bottom of the table after 25 games, of which they have won six and drawn two, earning 20 points.

Sheffield FC are four places ahead of Rangers in 16th, with eight wins and three draws putting them on 27 points.

With 25 goals in 25 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Sheffield FC's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 win against Liversedge.

Winterton lost 3-2 against North Ferriby last time out, also on Saturday.
