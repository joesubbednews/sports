Pontefract Collieries v Winterton Rangers

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Winterton Rangers face Pontefract Collieries away on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 5-1 victory over Brighouse Town on Monday, Winterton sit bottom of the Northern Premier League Division One East.

They travel to play a Pontefract Collieries side seventh in the standings, who lost in their last match, 1-0 against Stocksbridge Park Steels.

With 32 goals in 33 games so far this season, Winterton are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 70 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Pontefract Collieries, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Rangers's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Pontefract Collieries's eight.
