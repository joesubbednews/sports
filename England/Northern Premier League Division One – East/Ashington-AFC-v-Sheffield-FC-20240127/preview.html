Ashington AFC v Sheffield FC

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ashington AFC host Sheffield FC on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Ashington have picked up 10 points from their last six games, and they face a Sheffield FC side who have lost their last two matches, and collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 39 goals in 21 games so far this season, Ashington are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Sheffield FC, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The away side are 16th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won six and drawn three, earning 21 points.

The hosts are seven places ahead of Sheffield FC in ninth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

Ashington's last match was on January 13, a 2-1 win against Ossett United.

Sheffield FC lost 2-1 against Cleethorpes Town last time out, also on January 13.
