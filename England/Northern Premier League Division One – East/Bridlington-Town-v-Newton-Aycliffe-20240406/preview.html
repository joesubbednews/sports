Bridlington Town v Newton Aycliffe

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bridlington Town host Newton Aycliffe on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

In their last league match, on Monday, Bridlington beat North Ferriby 2-1 at home.

Newton Aycliffe drew, 1-1 away at Sheffield FC on March 30.

With 42 goals in 34 games so far this season, Bridlington are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 64 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Newton Aycliffe, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are 16th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

The visitors are 11 places ahead of the home side in fifth, with 16 wins and six draws putting them on 54 points.

Bridlington are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Newton Aycliffe's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Bridlington's five.
