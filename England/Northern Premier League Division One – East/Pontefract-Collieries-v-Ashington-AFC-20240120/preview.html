Pontefract Collieries v Ashington AFC

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Pontefract Collieries and Ashington AFC promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Northern Premier League Division One East's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, Pontefract Collieries and Ashington sit third and joint-fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 40 and 39 goals respectively.

Pontefract Collieries are seventh in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn five, earning 35 points.

Ashington are two places behind the hosts in ninth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

Pontefract Collieries are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Pontefract Collieries's eight.

Pontefract Collieries's last match was on January 13, a 3-3 draw against Hebburn Town.

Ashington beat Ossett United 2-1 last time out, also on January 13.
