Hebburn Town v Brighouse Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Friday, 22 March 2024 @ 11.00pm

Hebburn Town host Brighouse Town on late Friday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Hebburn have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Brighouse side who lost their last match, and have collected three points from the last possible 18.

With 52 goals in 28 games so far this season, Hebburn are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Brighouse, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Hebburn Town are third in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn six, earning 54 points.

Brighouse Town are 13 places behind the home team in 16th, with seven wins and nine draws putting them on 30 points.

In the last five years, Hebburn and Brighouse have played each other on five occasions. They won two each, and they drew once.

On average, Hebburn scored 1.4 goals and Brighouse 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Hebburn's last match was on Saturday, a 2-0 win against Bridlington Town.

Brighouse lost 2-0 against Sheffield FC last time out, also on Saturday.
