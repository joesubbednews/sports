Ossett United v Belper Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East | The Stade France Ingfield
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Ossett United face a challenge against high-flying Belper Town at the Stade France Ingfield on Saturday.

Ossett United are 18th in the Northern Premier League Division One – East table, and have picked up seven wins and seven draws in their 29 games to date.

Belper, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 53 points, having won 16 and drawn five, and are 15 points behind table-toppers Stockton Town.

With 28 goals in 29 games so far this season, Ossett are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Belper, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

United are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

Ossett's last match was on March 16, a 0-0 draw against Winterton Rangers.

Belper drew 2-2 with Ashington AFC last time out, also on March 16.
