Hebburn Town v Bridlington Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Bridlington Town face a challenge away against high-flying Hebburn Town on Saturday.

Bridlington Town are 17th in the Northern Premier League Division One – East table, and have picked up eight wins and six draws in their 30 games to date.

Hebburn, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 51 points, having won 15 and drawn six of their first 27 matches, and are 14 points behind table-toppers Stockton Town.

Hebburn are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Bridlington's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to Hebburn Town's 11.

With 50 goals in 27 games so far this season, the hosts are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Bridlington Town, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last five years, Hebburn and Bridlington have played each other on five occasions. Hebburn won four of them and they drew once.

On average, Hebburn scored 1.6 goals and Bridlington 0.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when Hebburn won 1-0 away.

Hebburn's last match was on March 9, a 4-0 win against Sheffield FC.

Bridlington lost 3-0 against Pontefract Collieries last time out, also on March 9.
