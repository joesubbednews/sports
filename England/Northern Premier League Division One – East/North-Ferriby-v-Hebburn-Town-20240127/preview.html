North Ferriby v Hebburn Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Ferriby welcome Hebburn Town on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

North Ferriby's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Northern Premier League Division One – East matches, while their opponents have earned six from a possible 18.

Hebburn are second in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

North Ferriby are 10 places behind Town in 12th, with nine wins and two draws putting them on 29 points.

With 39 goals in 22 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The home side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

North Ferriby's last match was on January 20, a 3-0 loss against Grimsby Borough.

Hebburn drew 0-0 with Cleethorpes Town last time out, also on January 20.
