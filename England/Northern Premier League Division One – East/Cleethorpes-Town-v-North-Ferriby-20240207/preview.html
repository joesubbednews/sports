Cleethorpes Town v North Ferriby

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bradley Community Stadium
Wednesday, 7 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

North Ferriby travel to Cleethorpes Town on late Wednesday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Winterton Rangers 3-2 away.

Cleethorpes also won their last match, 3-0 against Bridlington Town.

With 34 goals in 24 games so far this season, Cleethorpes are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

North Ferriby, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Town are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

North Ferriby are ninth in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn two, earning 35 points.

Cleethorpes are two places behind North Ferriby in 11th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.
