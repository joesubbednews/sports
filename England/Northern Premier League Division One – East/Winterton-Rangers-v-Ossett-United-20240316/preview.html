Winterton Rangers v Ossett United

Northern Premier League Division One – East | West Street
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Winterton Rangers face Ossett United at West Street on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-0 victory over Liversedge on February 24, Winterton sit bottom of the Northern Premier League Division One East.

They travel to play an Ossett side 18th in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-1 against Consett.

Winterton are in bad form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Ossett's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Rangers's four.

With 27 goals in 28 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

United are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.
