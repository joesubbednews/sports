Winterton Rangers v Grantham Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East | West Street
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Winterton Rangers face Grantham Town at West Street on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Dunston UTS on April 20, Winterton sit 19th in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

They travel to play a Grantham side 18th in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Ashington AFC, on April 13.

With 35 goals in 36 games so far this season, Winterton are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 74 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Grantham are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Rangers are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's nine.
