Sheffield FC v Brighouse Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brighouse Town travel looking to secure a first win in seven Northern Premier League Division One – East games against Sheffield FC on Saturday.

Brighouse have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Liversedge on January 1.

They face a Sheffield FC side who have won four and lost two over that time.

Brighouse are 16th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won seven and drawn nine, earning 30 points.

Sheffield FC are two places ahead of Town in 14th, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.

With 41 goals in 25 games so far this season, the home side are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last five years, Sheffield FC and Brighouse have played each other on five occasions. Sheffield FC won three of them, Brighouse one, and they drew once.

On average, Sheffield FC scored 1.0 goal and Brighouse 0.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 8, when Sheffield FC won 1-0 away.

Sheffield FC's last match was on March 9, a 4-0 loss against Hebburn Town.

Brighouse drew 2-2 with Bridlington Town last time out, on February 24.
