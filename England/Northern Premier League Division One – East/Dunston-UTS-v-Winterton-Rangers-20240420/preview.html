Dunston UTS v Winterton Rangers

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Winterton Rangers face Dunston UTS away on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Stockton Town on April 13, Winterton sit bottom of the Northern Premier League Division One East.

They travel to play a Dunston UTS side second in the standings, who also won their last match, 5-2 against Newton Aycliffe, on Tuesday.

With 33 goals in 35 games so far this season, Winterton are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 73 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Dunston UTS, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Rangers's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Dunston UTS's 12.
