Hebburn Town v Consett

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hebburn Town face Consett on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – East games.

Hebburn have lost two and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Grantham Town on December 23.

They face a Consett side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

With 40 goals in 21 games so far this season, Consett are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Hebburn are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn two, earning 41 points.

Town are one place behind Consett in fifth, with 12 wins and five draws putting them on the same number of points.

In the last three years, Hebburn and Consett have played each other on three occasions. Hebburn won two of them and Consett one.

Their last meeting was on September 15, when Consett won 3-0 at home.

Hebburn's last match was on January 27, a 1-0 loss against North Ferriby.

Consett beat Cleethorpes Town 3-1 last time out, also on January 27.
