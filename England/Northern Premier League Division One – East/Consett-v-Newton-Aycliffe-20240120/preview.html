Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Consett are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Newton Aycliffe's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the home team's 10.
