Brighouse Town v Bridlington Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brighouse Town and Bridlington Town face each other on Saturday both looking for a first win in six games.

Both teams have lost three and drawn two of their last match, and will be looking to seal three points in the 3pm kick off.

Brighouse are 16th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won seven and drawn eight, earning 29 points.

Bridlington are one place ahead of Brighouse Town in 15th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on the same number of points.

With 36 goals in 27 games so far this season, Bridlington Town are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last five years, Brighouse and Bridlington have played each other on five occasions. Brighouse won two of them and Bridlington three.

On average, Brighouse scored 1.4 goals and Bridlington 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 24, when Bridlington won 3-0 at home.

Brighouse's last match was on February 17, a 2-1 loss against Newton Aycliffe.

Bridlington lost 2-1 against Sheffield FC last time out, also on February 17.
