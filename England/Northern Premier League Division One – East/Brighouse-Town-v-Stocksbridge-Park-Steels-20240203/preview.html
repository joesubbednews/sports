Brighouse Town v Stocksbridge Park Steels

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brighouse Town and Stocksbridge Park Steels face each other on Saturday both looking for a first win in four games.

Brighouse have lost two and drawn one of their last three matches, during which time the Steels have suffered no losses and three draws.

Stocksbridge are 18th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

Brighouse are three places ahead of the Steels in 15th, with seven wins and seven draws putting them on 28 points.

With 26 goals in 22 games so far this season, the away team are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last five years, Brighouse and Stocksbridge have played each other on five occasions. They won two each, and they drew once.

On average, Brighouse scored 1.6 goals and the Steels 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 16, when Stocksbridge won 3-0 at home.

Brighouse's last match was on January 27, a 1-0 loss against Ossett United.

Stocksbridge drew 0-0 with Ossett United last time out, on Tuesday.
