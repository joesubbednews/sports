Carlton Town v Consett

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bill Stokeld Stadium
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Carlton Town host Consett at the Bill Stokeld Stadium on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Carlton have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Consett side who drew their last match, and have collected six points from the last possible 18.

Carlton are fourth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn one, earning 58 points.

Consett are four places behind Town in eighth, with 14 wins and six draws putting them on 48 points.

With 51 goals in 30 games so far this season, the home side are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The visitors are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last three years, Carlton and Consett have played each other on three occasions. Carlton won one of them and Consett the other.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Consett won 2-1 at home.

Carlton's last match was on April 13, a 3-1 win against Newton Aycliffe.

Consett drew 2-2 with Liversedge last time out, also on April 13.
