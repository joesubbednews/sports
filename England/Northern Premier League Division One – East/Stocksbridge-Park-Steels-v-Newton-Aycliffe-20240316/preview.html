Stocksbridge Park Steels v Newton Aycliffe

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bracken Moor
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Newton Aycliffe face Stocksbridge Park Steels on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Newton Aycliffe are unbeaten in eight, with four wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Stocksbridge team who have won two and drawn four over the same number of games.

With 35 goals in 30 games so far this season, Stocksbridge are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Newton Aycliffe, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The away team are fifth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn five, earning 50 points.

The Steels are 10 places behind Newton Aycliffe in 15th, with seven wins and 11 draws putting them on 32 points.

Over the last two years, Stocksbridge and Newton Aycliffe have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on March 9, when Newton Aycliffe won 2-1 at home.

Stocksbridge's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-2 win against Consett.

Newton Aycliffe beat Stocksbridge Park Steels 2-1 last time out, on March 9.
