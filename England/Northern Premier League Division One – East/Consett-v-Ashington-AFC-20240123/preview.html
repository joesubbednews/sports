Consett v Ashington AFC

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 23 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Ashington AFC face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Consett defence on Tuesday.

With 39 goals in 21 games, Ashington are the joint-fourth-highest scorers in the Northern Premier League Division One East ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face a Consett side who have scored 37 in 20 matches, but conceded only 25 goals, putting them fourth among the league's tightest defences – only Stockton Town FC, Dunston UTS and Cleethorpes Town have conceded fewer goals.

Consett are fifth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn two, earning 38 points.

Ashington are four places behind the home team in ninth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

In the last three years, Consett and Ashington have played each other on three occasions. Consett won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on August 28, when Consett won 2-0 away.

Consett are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.

Consett's last match was on January 13, a 2-0 loss against Dunston UTS.

Ashington beat Ossett United 2-1 last time out, also on January 13.
