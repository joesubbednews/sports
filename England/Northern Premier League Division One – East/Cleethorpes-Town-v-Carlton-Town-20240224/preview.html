Cleethorpes Town v Carlton Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bradley Community Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cleethorpes Town host Carlton Town on Saturday at the Bradley Community Stadium in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

In their last league match, on February 17, Cleethorpes beat Ashington AFC 2-1 away.

Carlton lost, 4-0 away at Belper Town on February 3.

With 33 goals in 23 games so far this season, Carlton are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Cleethorpes, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Cleethorpes Town are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Carlton Town's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home side's six.

The visitors are eighth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn one, earning 40 points.

Cleethorpes are three places behind Carlton in 11th, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last three years, Cleethorpes and Carlton have played each other on three occasions. Carlton won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 5, when Carlton won 1-0 at home.
