North Ferriby v Stocksbridge Park Steels

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stocksbridge Park Steels travel to North Ferriby on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Pontefract Collieries 1-0 at home.

North Ferriby, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Bridlington Town.

With 39 goals in 34 games so far this season, Stocksbridge are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

North Ferriby, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The Steels are 15th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won nine and drawn 11, earning 38 points.

The home side are five places ahead of the visitors in 10th, with 14 wins and three draws putting them on 45 points.

North Ferriby are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Stocksbridge's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to North Ferriby's six.
