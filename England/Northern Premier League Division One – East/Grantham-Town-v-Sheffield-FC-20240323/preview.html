Grantham Town v Sheffield FC

Northern Premier League Division One – East | South Kesteven Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Grantham Town and Sheffield FC come into Saturday's kick-off at the South Kesteven Stadium in contrasting spells, with the away team hitting a rich vein of form while the hosts struggle.

Grantham have drawn two and lost four of the last six, while Sheffield FC have taken five wins and one loss.

Grantham are bottom of the table after 30 games, of which they have won four and drawn nine, earning 21 points.

Sheffield FC are 10 places ahead of Town in 10th, with 12 wins and three draws putting them on 39 points.

With 39 goals in 30 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 67 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Grantham and Sheffield FC have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on January 28 2023, when Grantham won 3-1 away.

Grantham's last match was on March 16, a 2-0 loss against Stockton Town.

Sheffield FC beat Winterton Rangers 4-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
