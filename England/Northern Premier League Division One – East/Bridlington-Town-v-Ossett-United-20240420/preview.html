Bridlington Town v Ossett United

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Ossett United face Bridlington Town away on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over North Ferriby on April 13, Ossett sit 17th in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

They travel to play a Bridlington side 16th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 0-0 against Stocksbridge Park Steels.

Bridlington are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Ossett's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Town's five.

In the last five years, Bridlington and Ossett have played each other on five occasions. They won one each, and they drew three times.

On average, Bridlington scored 1.2 goals and Ossett 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 31 goals in 33 games so far this season, United are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.
