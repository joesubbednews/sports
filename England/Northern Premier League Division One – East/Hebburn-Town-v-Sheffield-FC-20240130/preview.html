Hebburn Town v Sheffield FC

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 30 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Hebburn Town host Sheffield FC in Tuesday's late match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Hebburn, who sit fourth in the league after 23 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to North Ferriby on Saturday.

They face a Sheffield FC side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-1 victory against Ashington AFC, and who sit 16th in the table.

With 39 goals in 23 games so far this season, Hebburn are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Sheffield FC are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

In the last five years, Hebburn and Sheffield FC have played each other on four occasions. Hebburn won three of them and Sheffield FC one.

On average, Hebburn scored 2.0 goals and Sheffield FC 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 8, when Sheffield FC won 2-1 at home.
