Cleethorpes Town v Belper Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bradley Community Stadium
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Belper Town visit Cleethorpes Town at the Bradley Community Stadium on late Wednesday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Belper have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Cleethorpes side who also won their last match, and have collected six points from the last possible 18.

With 55 goals in 26 games so far this season, Belper are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Cleethorpes, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Belper Town are second in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn three, earning 51 points.

Cleethorpes Town are eight places behind the visitors in 10th, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

Cleethorpes's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 win against Ashington AFC.

Belper beat Consett 1-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
