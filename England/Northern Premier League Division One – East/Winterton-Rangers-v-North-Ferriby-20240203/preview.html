Winterton Rangers v North Ferriby

Northern Premier League Division One – East | West Street
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Winterton Rangers face North Ferriby at West Street on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Grimsby Borough on January 27, Winterton sit bottom of the Northern Premier League Division One East.

They travel to play a North Ferriby side ninth in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Hebburn Town.

With 23 goals in 24 games so far this season, Winterton are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

North Ferriby, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Rangers are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

In the last three years, Winterton and North Ferriby have played each other on three occasions. Winterton won one of them and North Ferriby the other.

Their last meeting was on September 16, when North Ferriby won 3-0 at home.
