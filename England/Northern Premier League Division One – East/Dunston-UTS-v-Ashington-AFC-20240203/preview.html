Dunston UTS v Ashington AFC

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ashington AFC face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Dunston UTS defence on Saturday.

With 40 goals in 22 games, Ashington are the joint-fourth-highest scorers in the Northern Premier League Division One East ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Dunston UTS side who have scored 36 in 20 matches, but conceded only 18 goals, putting them joint-top of the league's tightest defences.

Dunston UTS are third in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

Ashington are eight places behind the hosts in 11th, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

Dunston UTS are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Dunston UTS's 14.

Over the last two years, Dunston UTS and Ashington have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on September 16, when Ashington won 3-1 at home.

Dunston UTS's last match was on Tuesday, a 0-0 draw against Grantham Town.

Ashington lost 3-1 against Sheffield FC last time out, on January 27.
