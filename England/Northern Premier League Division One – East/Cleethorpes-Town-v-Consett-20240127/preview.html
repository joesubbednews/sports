Cleethorpes Town v Consett

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bradley Community Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Northern Premier League Division One East's meanest defences go head-to-head at the Bradley Community Stadium on Saturday, when Cleethorpes Town host Consett.

Only two sides – Dunston UTS and Stockton Town FC – have conceded fewer goals than Cleethorpes to date: the home side have let in just 25 goals in 22 games.

Consett have conceded the same number of goals in 20 games, giving them the joint-third tightest back line so far this season.

Consett are sixth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn two, earning 38 points.

Cleethorpes are five places behind the visitors in 11th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last three years, Cleethorpes and Consett have played each other on three occasions. Cleethorpes won one of them and Consett the other.

Their last meeting was on November 7, when Consett won 3-2 at home.

Town are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Consett's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's eight.

Cleethorpes's last match was on Wednesday, a 3-1 loss against Dunston UTS.

Consett lost 2-0 against Dunston UTS last time out, on January 13.
