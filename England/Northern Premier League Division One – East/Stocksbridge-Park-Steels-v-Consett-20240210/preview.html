Stocksbridge Park Steels v Consett

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bracken Moor
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stocksbridge Park Steels face Consett on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

The Steels are unbeaten in five, with one win and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Consett team who have won just two and lost three over the same number of games.

With 27 goals in 23 games so far this season, Stocksbridge are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Consett, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The Steels are 17th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

The away team are 12 places ahead of the hosts in fifth, with 13 wins and two draws putting them on 41 points.

Over the last two years, Stocksbridge and Consett have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on February 4 2023, when Consett won 1-0 away.

Stocksbridge's last match was on February 3, a 1-1 draw against Brighouse Town.

Consett lost 3-1 against Hebburn Town last time out, also on February 3.
