Newton Aycliffe v Stocksbridge Park Steels

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Newton Aycliffe face Stocksbridge Park Steels on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Newton Aycliffe are unbeaten in seven, with three wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Stocksbridge team who have won one and drawn five over the same number of games.

With 31 goals in 27 games so far this season, Stocksbridge are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Newton Aycliffe, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The Steels are 17th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won six and drawn 10, earning 28 points.

The hosts are 12 places ahead of the away team in fifth, with 14 wins and five draws putting them on 47 points.

Newton Aycliffe's last match was on Tuesday, a 0-0 draw against Stockton Town.

Stocksbridge lost 2-1 against Hebburn Town last time out, on March 2.
