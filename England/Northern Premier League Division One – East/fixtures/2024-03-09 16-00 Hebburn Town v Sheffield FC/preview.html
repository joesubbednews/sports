Hebburn Town v Sheffield FC

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sheffield FC visit Hebburn Town on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Sheffield FC have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Hebburn side who also won their last match, and have collected nine points from the last possible 18.

With 46 goals in 26 games so far this season, Hebburn are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Sheffield FC are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are fourth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn six, earning 48 points.

The visitors are 10 places behind the home side in 14th, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last five years, Hebburn and Sheffield FC have played each other on four occasions. Hebburn won three of them and Sheffield FC one.

On average, Hebburn scored 2.0 goals and Sheffield FC 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 8, when Sheffield FC won 2-1 at home.

Hebburn's last match was on March 2, a 2-1 win against Stocksbridge Park Steels.

Sheffield FC beat Grimsby Borough 3-2 last time out, on Tuesday.
