Dunston UTS v Belper Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Northern Premier League Division One East's top sides face each other in Saturday's kick-off, when third-placed Dunston UTS host second-placed Belper Town.

Dunston UTS have picked up 15 wins and six draws from 25 games so far this season, and sit one point below the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Belper, meanwhile, have won 16 and drawn four of 27, picking up 52 points.

With 55 goals in 27 games so far this season, Belper are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Dunston UTS are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Dunston UTS's 11.

Dunston UTS's last match was on March 2, a 1-0 win against North Ferriby.

Belper drew 0-0 with Stocksbridge Park Steels last time out, on February 24.
