Pontefract Collieries v Bridlington Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Windsor Food Service Stadium
Friday, 8 March 2024 @ 11.00pm

Bridlington Town travel to the Windsor Food Service Stadium looking to secure a first win in seven Northern Premier League Division One – East games against Pontefract Collieries on late Friday.

Bridlington have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Winterton Rangers on December 30.

They face a Pontefract Collieries side who have won two and drawn three over that time.

With 45 goals in 26 games so far this season, Pontefract Collieries are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Bridlington, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The hosts are sixth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn eight, earning 44 points.

Town are nine places behind Pontefract Collieries in 15th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

In the last five years, Pontefract Collieries and Bridlington have played each other on four occasions. Pontefract Collieries won two of them, Bridlington one, and they drew once.

On average, Pontefract Collieries scored 2.0 goals and Bridlington 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 18, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Pontefract Collieries's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-3 draw against Ashington AFC.

Bridlington drew 2-2 with Brighouse Town last time out, on February 24.
