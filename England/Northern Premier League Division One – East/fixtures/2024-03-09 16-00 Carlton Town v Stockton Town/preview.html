Carlton Town v Stockton Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Northern Premier League Division One East's meanest defences go head-to-head on Saturday, when Stockton Town visit Carlton Town.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Stockton to date: the away side have let in just 21 goals in 29 games.

Carlton have conceded 33 goals in 23 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Stockton are top of the table after 29 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn eight, earning 62 points.

Carlton are eight places behind Stockton Town in ninth, with 13 wins and one draw putting them on 40 points.

Carlton Town are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

Over the last two years, Carlton and Stockton have played each other twice. Stockton won both of them.

Their last meeting was on March 25, when Stockton won 2-0 away.

Carlton's last match was on February 3, a 4-0 loss against Belper Town.

Stockton drew 0-0 with Newton Aycliffe last time out, on Tuesday.
