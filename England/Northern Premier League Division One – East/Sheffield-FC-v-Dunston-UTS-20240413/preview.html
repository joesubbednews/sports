Sheffield FC v Dunston UTS

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Dunston UTS travel to Sheffield FC on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Grimsby Borough 3-0 at home.

Sheffield FC, meanwhile, lost their last match, 5-1 against Stockton Town.

With 60 goals in 31 games so far this season, Dunston UTS are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Sheffield FC are also above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The away side are second in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn seven, earning 64 points.

The hosts are 11 places behind Dunston UTS in 13th, with 12 wins and five draws putting them on 41 points.

Sheffield FC are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Dunston UTS's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Sheffield FC's eight.

In the last five years, Sheffield FC and Dunston UTS have played each other on four occasions. Sheffield FC won one of them and Dunston UTS the other.

On average, Sheffield FC scored 0.8 goals and Dunston UTS 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 11 2023, when Dunston UTS won 2-0 away.
