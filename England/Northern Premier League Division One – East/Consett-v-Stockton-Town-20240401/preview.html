Consett v Stockton Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stockton Town travel for Monday's match against Consett looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Stockton, who sit top of the league after 33 games, fell to a 2-0 home defeat to Carlton Town on Saturday.

They face a Consett side who also lost their last match, a 3-2 defeat to North Ferriby, and who sit eighth in the table.

With 63 goals in 33 games so far this season, Stockton are the league's highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Consett are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Consett's five.

In the last five years, Consett and Stockton have played each other on four occasions. They won two each.

On average, Consett scored 2.0 goals and Stockton 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Stockton won 3-2 at home.
