Newton Aycliffe v Belper Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Newton Aycliffe host Belper Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

In their last league match, on January 13, Newton Aycliffe beat Liversedge 2-0 at home.

Belper also won, 2-1 at home against North Ferriby.

With 46 goals in 22 games so far this season, Belper are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Newton Aycliffe, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are third in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn three, earning 39 points.

The home side are five places behind the visitors in eighth, with 11 wins and one draw putting them on 34 points.

Newton Aycliffe are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Belper's form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Newton Aycliffe's 12.
