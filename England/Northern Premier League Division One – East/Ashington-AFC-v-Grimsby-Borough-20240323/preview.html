Ashington AFC v Grimsby Borough

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Ashington AFC host Grimsby Borough on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

In their last league match, on March 16, Ashington drew with Belper Town 2-2 away.

Grimsby also drew, 2-2 at home against Consett.

With 53 goals in 30 games so far this season, Ashington are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Grimsby, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Borough's form is slightly worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Ashington's six.

The away team are 12th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn five, earning 38 points.

Ashington are one place ahead of Grimsby in 11th, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.
