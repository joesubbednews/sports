Brighouse Town v Winterton Rangers

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Monday's match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 17th-ranked Brighouse Town host bottom of the table Winterton Rangers.

Brighouse have picked up 30 points from their first 30 Northern Premier League Division One – East games, with seven wins and nine draws.

That is eight points more than Winterton have collected, having won six and drawn four.

Brighouse are in terrible form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with no wins and two draws over that period, Winterton's form is identical – they have both taken two points from 18.

With 27 goals in 32 games so far this season, Rangers are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 69 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Brighouse's last match was on Saturday, a 3-1 loss against Dunston UTS.

Winterton lost 2-0 against Cleethorpes Town last time out, also on Saturday.
