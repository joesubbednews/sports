Grimsby Borough v Hebburn Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hebburn Town come to play Grimsby Borough on Saturday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Grimsby have three wins and a draw.

Hebburn are third in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn six, earning 57 points.

Grimsby are seven places behind Town in 10th, with 14 wins and five draws putting them on 47 points.

With 54 goals in 29 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Borough, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last three years, Grimsby and Hebburn have played each other on three occasions. Grimsby won one of them and Hebburn the other.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Hebburn won 2-1 at home.

Grimsby's last match was on April 6, a 3-0 loss against Dunston UTS.

Hebburn beat Brighouse Town 2-0 last time out, on March 23.
