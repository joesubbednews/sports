Stockton Town v Grantham Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Grantham Town face a challenge away against high-flying Stockton Town on Saturday.

Grantham Town are 19th in the Northern Premier League Division One – East table, and have picked up four wins and nine draws in their 29 games to date.

Stockton, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 65 points, having won 19 and drawn eight of their first 30 matches.

With 61 goals in 30 games so far this season, Stockton are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Grantham, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Stockton Town are in fantastic form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and three draws over that period, Grantham Town's form is much worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the hosts' 16.

In the last three years, Stockton and Grantham have played each other on three occasions. Stockton won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when Stockton won 4-0 away.

Stockton's last match was on March 9, a 2-1 win against Carlton Town.

Grantham lost 4-2 against North Ferriby last time out, on February 24.
