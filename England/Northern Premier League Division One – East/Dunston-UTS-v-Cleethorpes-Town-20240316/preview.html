Dunston UTS v Cleethorpes Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Dunston UTS face Cleethorpes Town on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Dunston UTS are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Cleethorpes team who have won two and drawn one over the same number of games.

Dunston UTS are second in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn seven, earning 55 points.

Cleethorpes are 10 places behind the home side in 12th, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

With 48 goals in 27 games so far this season, Dunston UTS are scoring more than average in the league with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last five years, Dunston UTS and Cleethorpes have played each other on five occasions. Dunston UTS won three of them, Cleethorpes one, and they drew once.

On average, Dunston UTS scored 1.6 goals and Cleethorpes 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 24, when Dunston UTS won 3-1 away.

Dunston UTS's last match was on March 9, a 3-1 win against Belper Town.

Cleethorpes drew 3-3 with Newton Aycliffe last time out, on March 2.
