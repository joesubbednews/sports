Winterton Rangers v Newton Aycliffe

Northern Premier League Division One – East | West Street
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Winterton Rangers are on a poor run ahead of hosting Newton Aycliffe at West Street on Saturday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Winterton have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Northern Premier League Division One – East games, and face a Newton Aycliffe side whose last six games have brought three wins and three losses.

Winterton are bottom of the table after 25 games, of which they have won six and drawn two, earning 20 points.

Newton Aycliffe are 11 places ahead of Rangers in ninth, with 12 wins and one draw putting them on 37 points.

With 25 goals in 25 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 2.3 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Winterton's last match was on February 3, a 3-2 loss against North Ferriby.

Newton Aycliffe beat Ossett United 4-0 last time out, also on February 3.
