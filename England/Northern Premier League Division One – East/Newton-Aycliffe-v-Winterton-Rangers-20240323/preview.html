Newton Aycliffe v Winterton Rangers

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Winterton Rangers come to play Newton Aycliffe on Saturday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Newton Aycliffe have a win and four draws.

Winterton are 19th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won six and drawn four, earning 22 points.

Newton Aycliffe are 13 places ahead of Rangers in sixth, with 15 wins and five draws putting them on 50 points.

With 27 goals in 30 games so far this season, the away team are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 65 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Newton Aycliffe's last match was on March 16, a 1-0 loss against Stocksbridge Park Steels.

Winterton lost 4-0 against Sheffield FC last time out, on Tuesday.
