Ashington AFC v Stocksbridge Park Steels

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Stocksbridge Park Steels face Ashington AFC on Tuesday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

The Steels are unbeaten in five, with one win and four draws, ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.

They face an Ashington team who have won three and lost two over the same number of games.

With 27 goals in 23 games so far this season, Stocksbridge are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Ashington, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The Steels are 17th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

The hosts are seven places ahead of the away team in 10th, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last three years, Ashington and Stocksbridge have played each other on three occasions. Ashington won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 26, when Ashington won 2-1 away.

Ashington's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 win against Grimsby Borough.

Stocksbridge drew 1-1 with Brighouse Town last time out, on February 3.
