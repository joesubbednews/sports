North Ferriby v Liversedge

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

North Ferriby host Liversedge on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

In their last league match, on March 16, North Ferriby lost to Pontefract Collieries 4-1 away.

Liversedge drew, 0-0 away at Winterton Rangers on February 24.

With 37 goals in 27 games so far this season, Liversedge are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

North Ferriby, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The visitors are 15th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

The home team are six places ahead of Liversedge in ninth, with 13 wins and three draws putting them on 42 points.

North Ferriby are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, Liversedge's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.
