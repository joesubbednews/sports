Carlton Town v Bridlington Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 20 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Bridlington Town travel looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – East games against Carlton Town on late Tuesday.

Bridlington have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Winterton Rangers on December 30.

They face a Carlton side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

Bridlington are 15th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

Carlton are seven places ahead of Bridlington Town in eighth, with 13 wins and one draw putting them on 40 points.

With 36 goals in 27 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Carlton Town are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have also conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last three years, Carlton and Bridlington have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Carlton won 1-0 away.

Carlton's last match was on February 3, a 4-0 loss against Belper Town.

Bridlington lost 2-1 against Sheffield FC last time out, on Saturday.
