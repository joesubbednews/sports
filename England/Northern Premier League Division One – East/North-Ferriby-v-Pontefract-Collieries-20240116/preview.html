North Ferriby v Pontefract Collieries

Northern Premier League Division One – East
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

North Ferriby welcome Pontefract Collieries on late Tuesday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

North Ferriby's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Northern Premier League Division One – East matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

With 40 goals in 20 games so far this season, Pontefract Collieries are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

North Ferriby are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The visitors are seventh in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn five, earning 35 points.

The home side are three places behind Pontefract Collieries in 10th, with nine wins and two draws putting them on 29 points.

North Ferriby's last match was on Saturday, a 2-1 loss against Belper Town.

Pontefract Collieries drew 3-3 with Hebburn Town last time out, also on Saturday.
