Belper Town v Carlton Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Christchurch Meadow
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Carlton Town travel to Belper Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Liversedge 3-1 at home.

Belper, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-1 against Newton Aycliffe.

With 48 goals in 23 games so far this season, Belper are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Carlton, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Belper Town are second in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn three, earning 42 points.

Carlton Town are four places behind the hosts in sixth, with 13 wins and one draw putting them on 40 points.

In the last three years, Belper and Carlton have played each other on three occasions. Belper won one of them and Carlton the other.

Their last meeting was on October 17, when Carlton won 2-1 at home.

Belper are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Belper's 11.
