Stocksbridge Park Steels v Bridlington Town

Northern Premier League Division One – East | Bracken Moor
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bridlington Town come to Bracken Moor to play Stocksbridge Park Steels on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while the Steels have three wins and three losses.

Bridlington are 16th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

Stocksbridge are one place ahead of Town in 15th, with nine wins and 11 draws putting them on 38 points.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Bridlington won 4-0 at home.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.5. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Stocksbridge's last match was on April 6, a 3-1 loss against North Ferriby.

Bridlington lost 5-3 against Newton Aycliffe last time out, also on April 6.
