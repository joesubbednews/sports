Brighton and Hove Albion v Wolverhampton Wanderers

Premier League | The American Express Community Stadium
Monday, 22 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Wolverhampton Wanderers visit Brighton and Hove Albion at the American Express Community Stadium on late Monday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Premier League.

Wolves have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Brighton side who drew their last match, and have collected nine points from the last possible 18.

With 38 goals in 20 games so far this season, Brighton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Wolves, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Brighton and Wolves have played each other on 17 occasions. Brighton won seven of them, Wolves three, and they drew seven times.

On average, Brighton scored 1.6 goals and Wolves 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 19, when Brighton won 4-1 away.

In Hee-Chan Hwang, the away side have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. He has notched 10 goals in 20 appearances, to sit sixth in the scoring charts.

His goal rate of one every 145 minutes is quicker than that of João Pedro, Albion's top scorer with a goal every 163 minutes, and a total of seven goals in 20 games.

The hosts are seventh in the table after 20 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Wolves are four places behind Brighton in 11th, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

Brighton's last match was on January 2, a 0-0 draw against West Ham United.

Wolves beat Everton 3-0 last time out, on December 30, with Craig Dawson, Matheus Cunha and Max Kilman on the scoresheet.
