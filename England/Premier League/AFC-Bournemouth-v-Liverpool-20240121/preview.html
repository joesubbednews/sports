AFC Bournemouth v Liverpool

Premier League | Vitality Stadium
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 4.30pm

Liverpool are strong favourites to take all three points despite AFC Bournemouth's home advantage in Sunday's match at the Vitality Stadium.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.65 on Liverpool sealing the win, with the visitors sitting first in the Premier League table.

Bournemouth, who are 13th in the league and 20 points behind the Reds, are priced at 4.6 to win. A draw is set at 3.93.

With 43 goals in 20 games so far this season, Liverpool are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Bournemouth, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In Mohamed Salah, the Reds have the league's sharpest shooter so far this season. He has notched 14 goals in 20 appearances.

His goal rate of one every 125 minutes is quicker than that of Dominic Solanke, the Cherries's top scorer with a goal every 142 minutes, and a total of 12 goals in 19 games.

In the last 10 years, Bournemouth and Liverpool have played each other on 17 occasions. Bournemouth won two of them, Liverpool 14, and they drew once.

On average, the Cherries scored 0.7 goals and the Reds 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 1, when Liverpool won 2-1 away.

The hosts are in good form in the Premier League, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Bournemouth's 13.

Bournemouth's last match was on December 31, a 3-1 loss against Tottenham Hotspur, with Alex Scott getting the goal for the Cherries.

Liverpool beat Newcastle United 4-2 last time out, on January 1, with Salah (two), Cody Gakpo and Curtis Jones on the scoresheet.
