Chelsea v Burnley

Premier League | Stamford Bridge
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Burnley face Chelsea away at Stamford Bridge on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Brentford on March 16, the Clarets sit 19th in the Premier League.

They travel to play a Chelsea side 11th in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-2 against Newcastle United, on March 11.

In the last 10 years, Chelsea and Burnley have played each other on 15 occasions. Chelsea won 10 of them, Burnley one, and they drew four times.

On average, the Blues scored 2.6 goals and the Clarets 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Chelsea won 4-1 away.

With 29 goals in 29 games so far this season, Burnley are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Chelsea, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The Blues are in mixed form in the Premier League, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, the Clarets's form is worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.
