Crystal Palace v Luton Town

Premier League | Selhurst Park
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Luton Town make the journey to Selhurst Park to play Crystal Palace on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Luton's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Premier League matches, while their opponents have earned seven from a possible 18.

Luton are 18th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won five and drawn five, earning 20 points.

Palace are three places ahead of Town in 15th, with seven wins and seven draws putting them on 28 points.

With 32 goals in 27 games so far this season, the Eagles are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The Hatters are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

Palace's last match was on March 2, a 3-1 loss against Tottenham Hotspur, with Eberechi Eze getting the goal for the Eagles.

Luton lost 3-2 against Aston Villa last time out, also on Saturday, with Carlton Morris and Tahith Chong on the scoresheet.
