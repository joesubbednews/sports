Brighouse Town v Sheffield FC
---
Northern Premier League – Division One East
Friday, 8 December 2023 @ 7.45pm
---
Brighouse Town face Sheffield FC on late Friday looking to secure a first win in eight Northern Premier League – Division One East games.
Brighouse have lost four and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Consett on October 14.
They face a Sheffield FC side who have won just one and drawn one over that time.
Sheffield FC are bottom of the table after 13 games, of which they have won three and drawn two, earning 11 points.
Brighouse are six places ahead of the away side in 14th, with four wins and five draws putting them on 17 points.
With 20 goals in 13 games so far this season, Sheffield FC are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.
Town are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.
Brighouse's last match was on November 25, a 3-1 loss against Stockton Town.
Sheffield FC drew 4-4 with North Ferriby last time out, also on November 25.
