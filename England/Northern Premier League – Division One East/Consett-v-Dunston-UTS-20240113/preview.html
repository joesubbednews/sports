Consett v Dunston UTS

Northern Premier League – Division One East
Saturday, 13 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Northern Premier League Division One East's meanest defences go head-to-head on Saturday, when Dunston UTS visit Consett.

Only one side – Stockton Town FC – has conceded fewer goals than Dunston UTS to date: the away side have let in just 17 goals in 16 games.

Consett have conceded 23 goals in 19 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Consett are third in the table after 19 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn two, earning 38 points.

Dunston UTS are four places behind the hosts in seventh, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.

Consett are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Consett's 13.

In the last three years, Consett and Dunston UTS have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 22, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Consett's last match was on Tuesday, a 2-1 win against Liversedge.

Dunston UTS beat Newton Aycliffe 2-1 last time out, on December 26.
