Ossett United v Sheffield FC

Northern Premier League – Division One East | The Stade France Ingfield
Friday, 22 December 2023 @ 7.45pm

Ossett United host Sheffield FC on Friday at the Stade France Ingfield in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Ossett lost to Dunston UTS 2-0 away.

Sheffield FC won, 4-0 at home against Ashington AFC on Tuesday.

Sheffield FC are 16th in the table after 16 games, of which they have won five and drawn two, earning 17 points.

Ossett are one place ahead of the visitors in 15th, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.

With 21 goals in 17 games so far this season, United are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Sheffield FC, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Sheffield FC's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Ossett's six.

Over the last year, Ossett and Sheffield FC have played each other on three occasions. Ossett won one of them and Sheffield FC the other.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when Sheffield FC won 3-1 at home.
