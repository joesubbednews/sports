Dunston UTS v Stocksbridge Park Steels
---
Northern Premier League – Division One East
Tuesday, 12 December 2023 @ 7.45pm
---
Struggling Stocksbridge Park Steels face Dunston UTS away on Tuesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.
After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Cleethorpes Town on November 25, the Steels sit 17th in the Northern Premier League Division One East.
They travel to play a Dunston UTS side eighth in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-0 against Winterton Rangers.
With 19 goals in 15 games so far this season, Stocksbridge are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.
Dunston UTS, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.
The home team are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.
With two wins and a draw over that period, the Steels' form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Dunston UTS's 11.
