North Ferriby v Brighouse Town

Northern Premier League – Division One East
Saturday, 16 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Brighouse Town travel looking to secure a first win in nine Northern Premier League – Division One East games against North Ferriby on Saturday.

Brighouse have lost five and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Consett on October 14.

They face a North Ferriby side who have won four and drawn one over that time.

With 30 goals in 15 games so far this season, North Ferriby are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Brighouse, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

Town are 15th in the table after 17 games, of which they have won four and drawn five, earning 17 points.

The hosts are eight places ahead of the away side in seventh, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

North Ferriby's last match was on November 25, a 4-4 draw against Sheffield FC, with getting the goals for North Ferriby.

Brighouse lost 1-0 against Sheffield FC last time out, on December 8.
