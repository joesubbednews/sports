Dunston UTS v Belper Town

Northern Premier League – Division One East
Saturday, 6 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Belper Town face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Dunston UTS defence on Saturday.

With 44 goals in 20 games, Belper are the second-highest scorers in the Northern Premier League Division One East ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Dunston UTS side who have scored 31 in 16 matches, but conceded only 17 goals, putting them second among the league's tightest defences – only Stockton Town FC have conceded fewer goals.

Belper are third in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn three, earning 36 points.

Dunston UTS are three places behind Town in sixth, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Dunston UTS's 16.

Dunston UTS's last match was on December 26, a 2-1 win against Newton Aycliffe.

Belper beat Grantham Town 5-0 last time out, on December 30.
