Liversedge v Consett
---
Northern Premier League – Division One East
Tuesday, 5 December 2023 @ 7.45pm
---
Consett travel to Liversedge on late Tuesday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.
The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Carlton Town 2-1 at home.
Liversedge, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Pontefract Collieries.
With 29 goals in 14 games so far this season, Consett are scoring more than average in the league with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.
Liversedge, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.
The away side are third in the table after 14 games, of which they have won nine and drawn one, earning 28 points.
The hosts are six places behind Consett in ninth, with seven wins and seven losses putting them on 21 points.
Liversedge are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.
And also with four wins and two losses over that period, Consett's form is identical – they have both taken 12 points from 18.
