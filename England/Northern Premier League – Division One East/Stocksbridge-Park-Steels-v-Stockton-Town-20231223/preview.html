Stocksbridge Park Steels v Stockton Town

Northern Premier League – Division One East | Bracken Moor
Saturday, 23 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Stocksbridge Park Steels face a challenge against high-flying Stockton Town at Bracken Moor on Saturday.

Stocksbridge Park Steels are 17th in the Northern Premier League – Division One East table, and have picked up four wins and three draws in their 16 games to date.

Stockton, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 38 points, having won 12 and drawn two of their first 17 matches.

With 42 goals in 17 games so far this season, Stockton are the league's highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Stocksbridge, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The Steels are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

Over the last two years, Stocksbridge and Stockton have played each other on three occasions. Stockton won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Stocksbridge's last match was on December 15, a 2-2 draw against Liversedge.

Stockton beat Hebburn Town 3-1 last time out, on December 16.
