Ashington AFC v Stocksbridge Park Steels

Northern Premier League – Division One East
Saturday, 6 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Stocksbridge Park Steels face Ashington AFC away on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Sheffield FC on December 30, the Steels sit 17th in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

They travel to play an Ashington side 12th in the standings, who also won their last match, 4-0 against Hebburn Town, on December 26.

With 24 goals in 19 games so far this season, Stocksbridge are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Ashington, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the Steels' form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Ashington's seven.
