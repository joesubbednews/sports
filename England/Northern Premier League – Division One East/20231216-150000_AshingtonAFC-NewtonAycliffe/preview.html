Ashington AFC v Newton Aycliffe

Northern Premier League – Division One East
Saturday, 16 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Newton Aycliffe travel to Ashington AFC on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

The home team are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Ashington's 13.

Newton Aycliffe are 14th in the table after 15 games, of which they have won six and drawn one, earning 19 points.

Ashington are three places ahead of Newton Aycliffe in 11th, with six wins and two draws putting them on 20 points.

Over the last year, Ashington and Newton Aycliffe have played each other on three occasions. Newton Aycliffe won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 12, when Newton Aycliffe won 2-0 at home.
