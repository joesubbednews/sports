Consett v Winterton Rangers

Northern Premier League – Division One East
Saturday, 23 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Winterton Rangers face a challenge away against high-flying Consett on Saturday.

Winterton Rangers are 17th in the Northern Premier League – Division One East table, and have picked up five wins and no draws in their 18 games to date.

Consett, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 34 points, having won 11 and drawn one of their first 16 matches, and are four points behind table-toppers Stockton Town FC.

With 17 goals in 18 games so far this season, Winterton are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Consett, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Rangers' form is much worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to Consett's 15.

Consett's last match was on December 15, a 2-0 win against Pontefract Collieries, with getting the goals for Consett.

Winterton lost 2-0 against Belper Town last time out, on December 16.
