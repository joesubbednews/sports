Bridlington Town v Dunston UTS

Northern Premier League – Division One East
Saturday, 23 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Dunston UTS visit Bridlington Town on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Dunston UTS have picked up 11 points from their last six games, and they face a Bridlington side who also won their last match, and have collected the same number of points from the last possible 18.

With 26 goals in 19 games so far this season, Bridlington are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Dunston UTS, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The away team are sixth in the table after 14 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

Town are four places behind Dunston UTS in 10th, with seven wins and three draws putting them on 24 points.

Over the last year, Bridlington and Dunston UTS have played each other twice. Dunston UTS won both of them.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when Dunston UTS won 3-1 at home.

Bridlington's last match was on December 16, a 1-0 win against Grimsby Borough.

Dunston UTS beat Ossett United 2-0 last time out, also on December 16.
