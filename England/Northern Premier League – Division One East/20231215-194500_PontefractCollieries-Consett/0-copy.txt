Pontefract Collieries v Consett
---
Northern Premier League – Division One East
Friday, 15 December 2023 @ 7.45pm
---
Consett face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Pontefract Collieries defence on Friday.
With 31 goals in 15 games, Consett are the fourth-highest scorers in the Northern Premier League Division One East ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off.
They face a Pontefract Collieries side who have scored 29 in 14 matches, but conceded only 17 goals, putting them joint-fourth among the league's tightest defences – only Stockton Town FC, Dunston UTS and Cleethorpes Town have conceded fewer goals.
Consett are third in the table after 15 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn one, earning 31 points.
Pontefract Collieries are two places behind the visitors in fifth, with eight wins and three draws putting them on 27 points.
The home team are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.
With five wins and one loss over that period, Consett's form is slightly better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Pontefract Collieries' 13.
Pontefract Collieries' last match was on November 28, a 2-1 win against Liversedge.
Consett beat Grimsby Borough 2-0 last time out, on Saturday.
