Cleethorpes Town v Belper Town
---
Northern Premier League – Division One East | Bradley Community Stadium
Wednesday, 13 December 2023 @ 7.45pm
---
Belper Town travel to the Bradley Community Stadium for Wednesday's late match against Cleethorpes Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League Division One East.
Belper, who sit fourth in the league after 16 games, fell to a 3-0 home defeat to Hebburn Town on November 25.
They face a Cleethorpes side who also lost their last match, a 1-0 defeat to Carlton Town, and who sit 12th in the table.
With 35 goals in 16 games so far this season, Belper are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.
Cleethorpes, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.
Cleethorpes Town are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with one win and two draws from their last six games.
With two wins and a draw over that period, Belper Town's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home team's five.
