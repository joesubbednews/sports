Newton Aycliffe v North Ferriby

Northern Premier League – Division One East
Saturday, 23 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Newton Aycliffe host North Ferriby on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One East.

Newton Aycliffe have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a North Ferriby side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 30 goals in 16 games so far this season, North Ferriby are scoring more than average in the league with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

Newton Aycliffe are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The away team are eighth in the table after 16 games, of which they have won eight and drawn two, earning 26 points.

The hosts are one place behind North Ferriby in ninth, with eight wins and one draw putting them on 25 points.

Newton Aycliffe's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-0 win against Carlton Town.

North Ferriby lost 2-0 against Brighouse Town last time out, on December 16.
