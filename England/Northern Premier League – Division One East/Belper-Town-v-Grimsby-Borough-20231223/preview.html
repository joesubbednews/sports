Belper Town v Grimsby Borough

Northern Premier League – Division One East | Christchurch Meadow
Saturday, 23 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Grimsby Borough face a challenge away against high-flying Belper Town at Christchurch Meadow on Saturday.

Grimsby Borough are rooted to the bottom of the Northern Premier League – Division One East table, and have picked up four wins and one draw in their 16 games to date.

Belper, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 31 points, having won 10 and drawn one of their first 17 matches, and are seven points behind table-toppers Stockton Town FC.

Belper are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One East, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With no wins and six losses over that period, Grimsby's form is much worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to Town's nine.

With 37 goals in 17 games so far this season, the home team are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Borough, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Belper's last match was on December 16, a 2-0 win against Winterton Rangers.

Grimsby lost 1-0 against Bridlington Town last time out, also on December 16.
