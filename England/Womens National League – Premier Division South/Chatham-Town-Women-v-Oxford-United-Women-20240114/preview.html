Chatham Town Women v Oxford United Women

Womens National League – Premier Division South
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Oxford United Women visit Chatham Town Women on early Sunday on the back of four consecutive wins in Womens National League – Premier Division South.

Oxford United have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Chatham Town side who drew their last match, and have collected four points from the last possible 18.

Chatham Town are 11th in the table after 12 games, of which they have won none and drawn five, earning five points.

Oxford United are six places ahead of the home team in fifth, with six wins and one draw putting them on 19 points.

With eight goals in 12 games so far this season, Chatham Town are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Chatham Town's last match was on January 7, a 3-3 draw against Plymouth Argyle Women.

Oxford United beat Ipswich Town Women 1-0 last time out, also on January 7.
