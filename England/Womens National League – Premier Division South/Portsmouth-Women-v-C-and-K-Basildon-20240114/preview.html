Portsmouth Women v C and K Basildon

Womens National League – Premier Division South
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Womens National League – Premier Division South's top two sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when second-placed Portsmouth Women host C and K Basildon.

Portsmouth have picked up 10 wins from 11 games so far this season, and sit three points below the visitors going into the 2pm match.

C and K Basildon, meanwhile, have won 11 of 13, picking up 33 points.

With 45 goals in 11 games so far this season, Portsmouth are the league's highest scorers with 4.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in two goals at a rate of 0.2 per game.

C and K Basildon, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in exceptional form in Womens National League – Premier Division South, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Portsmouth's 18.

Portsmouth's last match was on January 7, a 1-0 win against Billericay Town Women.

C and K Basildon beat Milton Keynes Dons Women 3-2 last time out, also on Sunday.
