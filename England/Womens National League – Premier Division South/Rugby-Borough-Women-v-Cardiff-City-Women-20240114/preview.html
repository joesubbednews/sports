Rugby Borough Women v Cardiff City Women

Womens National League – Premier Division South
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Cardiff City Women face a challenge away against high-flying Rugby Borough Women on Sunday.

Cardiff City Women are ninth in the Womens National League – Premier Division South table, and have picked up two wins and four draws in their 11 games to date.

Rugby Borough, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 24 points, having won eight and drawn none of their first 12 matches, and are nine points behind table-toppers C&K Basildon.

With 17 goals in 11 games so far this season, Cardiff City are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Rugby Borough, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in Womens National League – Premier Division South, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Rugby Borough's nine.

Rugby Borough's last match was on January 7, a 4-0 win against Cheltenham Town LFC.

Cardiff City drew 2-2 with London Bees Women last time out, also on January 7.
