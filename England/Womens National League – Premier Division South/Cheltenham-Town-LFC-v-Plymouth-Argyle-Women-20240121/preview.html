Cheltenham Town LFC v Plymouth Argyle Women

Womens National League – Premier Division South | Kayte Lane
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Plymouth Argyle Women travel to Cheltenham Town LFC on early Sunday in Womens National League – Premier Division South.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Chatham Town Women 3-3 at home.

Cheltenham Town, meanwhile, lost their last match, 4-0 against Rugby Borough Women.

Plymouth Argyle are 10th in the table after 11 games, of which they have won three and drawn one, earning 10 points.

Cheltenham Town are three places ahead of the away side in seventh, with four wins and two draws putting them on 14 points.

With 14 goals in 12 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

Plymouth Argyle are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 3.5 goals per game.

Cheltenham Town are in mixed form in Womens National League – Premier Division South, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, Plymouth Argyle's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.

Over the last year, Cheltenham Town and Plymouth Argyle have played each other twice. Plymouth Argyle won both of them.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when Plymouth Argyle won 3-2 at home.
