1874 Northwich v Hednesford Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match sees the league's two bottom sides face each other, as rock-bottom 1874 Northwich host Hednesford Town.

1874 Northwich have picked up 17 points from their first 17 Northern Premier League Division One – West games, with four wins and five draws.

That is six points less than the Pitmen have collected, having won five and drawn eight.

1874 Northwich are in terrible form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Hednesford's form is better – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the home team's one.

With 20 goals in 29 games so far this season, 1874 Northwich are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 65 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

1874 Northwich's last match was on March 9, a 3-0 loss against Leek Town.

Hednesford lost 3-0 against Runcorn Linnets last time out, on February 24.
