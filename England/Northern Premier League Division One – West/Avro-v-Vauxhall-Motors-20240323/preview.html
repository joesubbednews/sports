Avro v Vauxhall Motors

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Vauxhall Motors face Avro away on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-1 victory over Stalybridge Celtic on March 16, Vauxhall Motors sit 17th in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

They travel to play an Avro side 14th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Runcorn Linnets.

Avro are in bad form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Vauxhall Motors's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With 30 goals in 30 games so far this season, Avro are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last five years, Avro and Vauxhall Motors have played each other on four occasions. Avro won two of them, Vauxhall Motors one, and they drew once.

On average, Avro scored 1.8 goals and Vauxhall Motors 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 15, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
