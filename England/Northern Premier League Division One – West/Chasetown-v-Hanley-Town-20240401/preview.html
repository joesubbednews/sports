Chasetown v Hanley Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Scholars Ground
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chasetown are on a poor run ahead of hosting Hanley Town at the Scholars Ground on Monday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Chasetown have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Northern Premier League Division One – West games, and face a Hanley side whose last six games have brought one win and two draws.

Hanley are 17th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

Chasetown are five places ahead of Town in 12th, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

With 28 goals in 31 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The home team are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Chasetown's last match was on Saturday, a 2-0 win against Avro.

Hanley lost 1-0 against Widnes last time out, also on Saturday.
