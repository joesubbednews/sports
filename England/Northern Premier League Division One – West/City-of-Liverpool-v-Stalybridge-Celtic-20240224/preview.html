City of Liverpool v Stalybridge Celtic

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stalybridge Celtic travel looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – West games against City of Liverpool on Saturday.

Stalybridge Celtic have lost two and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Bootle on January 6.

They face a City of Liverpool side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

With 46 goals in 25 games so far this season, City of Liverpool are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Stalybridge Celtic, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are seventh in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn 10, earning 40 points.

The home team are four places behind Stalybridge Celtic in 11th, with 11 wins and four draws putting them on 37 points.

City of Liverpool's last match was on February 17, a 2-0 win against 1874 Northwich.

Stalybridge Celtic lost 1-0 against Runcorn Linnets last time out, also on February 17.
