Witton Albion v 1874 Northwich

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Help For Heroes Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

1874 Northwich come to the Help For Heroes Stadium to play Witton Albion on Monday in terrible form, having collected just one point from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn just one of their last six fixtures, while Witton have three wins and three losses.

1874 Northwich are bottom of the table after 32 games, of which they have won four and drawn six, earning 18 points.

Witton are 15 places ahead of the visitors in fifth, with 15 wins and five draws putting them on 50 points.

With 22 goals in 32 games so far this season, 1874 Northwich are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 69 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Albion, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last three years, Witton and 1874 Northwich have played each other on three occasions. Witton won all of them.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Witton won 2-1 away.

Witton's last match was on Saturday, a 3-0 loss against Hednesford Town.

1874 Northwich lost 1-0 against Newcastle Town last time out, also on Saturday.
