Hednesford Town v Avro

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Keys Park
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Avro travel to Hednesford Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Leek Town 2-1 at home.

The Pitmen, meanwhile, drew their last match, 1-1 against Prescot Cables.

Hednesford are 19th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 10, earning 31 points.

Avro are nine places ahead of Town in 10th, with 12 wins and 12 draws putting them on 48 points.

With 37 goals in 34 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The Pitmen are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Avro's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Hednesford's eight.
