Stalybridge Celtic v Chasetown

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Bower Fold
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stalybridge Celtic host Chasetown on Saturday at Bower Fold in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

In their last league match, on April 6, Stalybridge Celtic lost to Nantwich Town 4-2 away.

Chasetown won, 6-4 at home against City of Liverpool.

With 47 goals in 34 games so far this season, Chasetown are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding at an average rate too, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Stalybridge Celtic are also average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Stalybridge Celtic's seven.

Stalybridge Celtic are 12th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn 11, earning 47 points.

Chasetown are one place ahead of the home team in 11th, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on the same number of points.
