Vauxhall Motors v Stalybridge Celtic

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Motassist Arena
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stalybridge Celtic travel to the Motassist Arena looking to secure a first win in seven Northern Premier League Division One – West games against Vauxhall Motors on Saturday.

Stalybridge Celtic have lost two and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Bootle on January 6.

They face a Vauxhall Motors side who have won one and drawn two over that time.

Vauxhall Motors are 18th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won nine and drawn four, earning 31 points.

Stalybridge Celtic are eight places ahead of the hosts in 10th, with 10 wins and 11 draws putting them on 41 points.

With 36 goals in 28 games so far this season, Vauxhall Motors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Vauxhall Motors's last match was on March 9, a 2-0 loss against Clitheroe.

Stalybridge Celtic drew 1-1 with City of Liverpool last time out, on February 24.
