Runcorn Linnets v Clitheroe

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Clitheroe face Runcorn Linnets on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

Clitheroe are unbeaten in six, with two wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Runcorn Linnets team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

Runcorn Linnets are third in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn four, earning 43 points.

Clitheroe are seven places behind the home team in 10th, with 10 wins and seven draws putting them on 37 points.

With 40 goals in 24 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Runcorn Linnets are also above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last five years, Runcorn Linnets and Clitheroe have played each other on five occasions. Runcorn Linnets won two of them, Clitheroe one, and they drew twice.

On average, Runcorn Linnets scored 1.0 goal and Clitheroe 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 25, when Clitheroe won 2-1 away.

Runcorn Linnets's last match was on February 3, a 0-0 draw against Trafford.

Clitheroe beat Avro 2-1 last time out, also on February 3.
