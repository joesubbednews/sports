Leek Town v Stalybridge Celtic

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Harrison Park
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leek Town face Stalybridge Celtic on Friday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

Leek are unbeaten in 18, with 14 wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Stalybridge Celtic team who have won five and drawn eight over the same number of games.

With 75 goals in 32 games so far this season, Leek are the league's highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Stalybridge Celtic are also above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Town are top of the table after 32 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn six, earning 69 points.

The visitors are nine places behind the home team in 10th, with 11 wins and 11 draws putting them on 44 points.

Leek's last match was on Saturday, a 1-1 draw against Hednesford Town.

Stalybridge Celtic beat Widnes 4-3 last time out, also on Saturday.
