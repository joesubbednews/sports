Clitheroe v Bootle

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Shawbridge
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Bootle travel to Clitheroe on late Tuesday in Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Witton Albion 2-2 away.

Clitheroe also drew their last match, 0-0 against Stalybridge Celtic.

With 37 goals in 22 games so far this season, Clitheroe are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Bootle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Clitheroe's six.

In the last five years, Clitheroe and Bootle have played each other on four occasions. Clitheroe won all of them.

On average, Clitheroe scored 4.2 goals and Bootle 0.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 7, when Clitheroe won 4-0 at home.

Clitheroe are seventh in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn six, earning 33 points.

Bootle are three places behind the home side in 10th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.
