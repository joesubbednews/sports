Hednesford Town v Bootle

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Keys Park
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Hednesford Town face a challenge against high-flying Bootle at Keys Park on Saturday.

Hednesford Town are 19th in the Northern Premier League Division One – West table, and have picked up seven wins and 10 draws in their 36 games to date.

Bootle, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 63 points, having won 18 and drawn nine, and are 13 points behind table-toppers Leek Town.

With 43 goals in 36 games so far this season, Hednesford are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 66 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Bootle, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the Pitmen's five.

Hednesford's last match was on April 20, a 3-1 loss against Clitheroe.

Bootle beat Hanley Town 3-0 last time out, also on April 20.
