Runcorn Linnets v Avro

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Runcorn Linnets host Avro in Saturday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

Runcorn Linnets, who sit third in the league after 30 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Bootle on March 9.

They face an Avro side who also lost their last match, a 2-0 defeat to Widnes, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 29 goals in 29 games so far this season, Avro are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Runcorn Linnets, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Runcorn Linnets's 10.

In the last three years, Runcorn Linnets and Avro have played each other on three occasions. Runcorn Linnets won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when Runcorn Linnets won 2-1 away.
