Nantwich Town v City of Liverpool

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Weaver Stadium
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

City of Liverpool travel to Nantwich Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Kidsgrove Athletic 5-0 at home.

Nantwich, meanwhile, lost their last match, 1-0 against Newcastle Town.

With 62 goals in 33 games so far this season, City of Liverpool are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Nantwich are also above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Town are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.

Nantwich are seventh in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn six, earning 54 points.

City of Liverpool are one place behind Nantwich in eighth, with 15 wins and seven draws putting them on 52 points.
