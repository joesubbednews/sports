Widnes v Nantwich Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Nantwich Town travel to Widnes on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to City of Liverpool 2-1 at home.

The Whites also lost their last match, 3-2 against Runcorn Linnets.

With 56 goals in 35 games so far this season, Widnes are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Nantwich are also above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The Whites are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's 12.

Widnes are eighth in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn six, earning 54 points.

The visitors are one place behind Widnes in ninth, with 16 wins and six draws putting them on the same number of points.
