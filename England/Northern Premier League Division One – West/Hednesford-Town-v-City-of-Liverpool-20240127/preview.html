Hednesford Town v City of Liverpool

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Keys Park
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

City of Liverpool travel to Hednesford Town on Saturday in Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Avro 1-1 at home.

The Pitmen also drew their last match, 1-1 against Kidsgrove Athletic.

With 38 goals in 22 games so far this season, City of Liverpool are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Hednesford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are 19th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

The away team are eight places ahead of the Pitmen in 11th, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

The hosts are in reasonable form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, City of Liverpool's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Hednesford's 11.
