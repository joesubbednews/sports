Witton Albion v Chasetown

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Help For Heroes Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chasetown come to the Help For Heroes Stadium to play Witton Albion on Saturday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Witton have four wins and two losses.

With 51 goals in 31 games so far this season, Witton are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Chasetown, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Albion are sixth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn five, earning 47 points.

The visitors are six places behind the home side in 12th, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

Witton's last match was on March 16, a 2-1 loss against Hanley Town.

Chasetown lost 3-0 against Nantwich Town last time out, also on March 16.
