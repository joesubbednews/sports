Mossley v Vauxhall Motors

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mossley welcome Vauxhall Motors on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Mossley's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Northern Premier League Division One – West matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

Vauxhall Motors are 17th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn four, earning 37 points.

Mossley are four places ahead of the visitors in 13th, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on 44 points.

With 42 goals in 35 games so far this season, the home team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Vauxhall Motors are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Mossley and Vauxhall Motors have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on November 28, when Mossley won 3-0 away.

Mossley's last match was on April 6, a 2-0 loss against Runcorn Linnets.

Vauxhall Motors lost 2-1 against Trafford last time out, also on April 6.
