Clitheroe v Runcorn Linnets

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Shawbridge
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Runcorn Linnets travel to Clitheroe on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors are second in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn four, earning 55 points.

The home side are three places behind Runcorn Linnets in fifth, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on 47 points.

In the last 10 years, Clitheroe and Runcorn Linnets have played each other on six occasions. Clitheroe won one of them, Runcorn Linnets three, and they drew twice.

On average, Clitheroe scored 0.8 goals and Runcorn Linnets 1.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 10, when Runcorn Linnets won 5-1 at home.

Clitheroe are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Runcorn Linnets's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Clitheroe's 10.
