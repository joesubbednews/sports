Prescot Cables v Hednesford Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Valerie Park
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Hednesford Town face a challenge away against high-flying Prescot Cables at Valerie Park on Saturday.

Hednesford Town are 19th in the Northern Premier League Division One – West table, and have picked up seven wins and nine draws in their 33 games to date.

Prescot Cables, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 58 points, having won 16 and drawn 10, and are 17 points behind table-toppers Leek Town.

With 52 goals in 33 games so far this season, Prescot Cables are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Hednesford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Prescot Cables's nine.

Prescot Cables's last match was on Monday, a 2-2 draw against Runcorn Linnets.

Hednesford lost 4-3 against Nantwich Town last time out, also on Monday.
