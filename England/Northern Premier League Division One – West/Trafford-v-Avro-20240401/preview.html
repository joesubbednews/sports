Trafford v Avro

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Shawe View
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Trafford face Avro at Shawe View on Monday looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – West games.

Trafford have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Newcastle Town on February 17.

They face an Avro side who have won just one and lost four over that time.

Trafford are 18th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won six and drawn 15, earning 33 points.

Avro are four places ahead of the home team in 14th, with 10 wins and 12 draws putting them on 42 points.

With 31 goals in 32 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Trafford are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Trafford's last match was on Friday, a 1-1 draw against Bootle.

Avro lost 2-0 against Chasetown last time out, on Saturday.
