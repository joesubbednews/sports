Bootle v Clitheroe

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bootle face Clitheroe on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

Bootle are unbeaten in nine, with six wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Clitheroe team who have won three and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 49 goals in 30 games so far this season, Clitheroe are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Bootle are also above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are fourth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn seven, earning 52 points.

The visitors are three places behind Bootle in seventh, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on 44 points.

In the last five years, Bootle and Clitheroe have played each other on five occasions. Bootle won one of them and Clitheroe the other.

On average, Bootle scored 0.8 goals and Clitheroe 3.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 27, when Bootle won 2-1 away.

Bootle's last match was on March 9, a 1-0 win against Runcorn Linnets.

Clitheroe beat Vauxhall Motors 2-0 last time out, also on March 9.
