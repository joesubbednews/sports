Runcorn Linnets v Bootle

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Runcorn Linnets host Bootle in Saturday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Runcorn Linnets, who sit third in the league after 23 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Newcastle Town on January 13.

They face a Bootle side who secured a draw in their last match, a 2-2 tie with Witton Albion, and who sit 10th in the table.

With 35 goals in 23 games so far this season, Runcorn Linnets are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Bootle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last five years, Runcorn Linnets and Bootle have played each other on five occasions. Runcorn Linnets won three of them, Bootle one, and they drew once.

On average, Runcorn Linnets scored 2.2 goals and Bootle 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 27, when Runcorn Linnets won 3-1 away.

The hosts are in mixed form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Runcorn Linnets's nine.
