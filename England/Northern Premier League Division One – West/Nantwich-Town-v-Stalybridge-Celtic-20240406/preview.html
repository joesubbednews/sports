Nantwich Town v Stalybridge Celtic

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Weaver Stadium
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Nantwich Town are on an excellent run ahead of hosting Stalybridge Celtic at the Weaver Stadium on Saturday, with 16 points collected from their last six games.

Nantwich have picked up five wins and one draw in their last six Northern Premier League Division One – West games, and face a Stalybridge Celtic side whose last six games have brought two wins and one draw.

With 54 goals in 34 games so far this season, Nantwich are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Stalybridge Celtic, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Nantwich and Stalybridge Celtic have played each other on 12 occasions. Nantwich won six of them, Stalybridge Celtic five, and they drew once.

On average, Nantwich scored 2.0 goals and Stalybridge Celtic 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Stalybridge Celtic won 3-2 at home.

Town are seventh in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn six, earning 51 points.

The visitors are three places behind the home side in 10th, with 12 wins and 11 draws putting them on 47 points.

Nantwich's last match was on Monday, a 4-3 win against Hednesford Town.

Stalybridge Celtic beat Mossley 1-0 last time out, also on Monday.
