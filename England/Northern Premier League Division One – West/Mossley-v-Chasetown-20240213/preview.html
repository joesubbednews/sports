Mossley v Chasetown

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Seel Park
Tuesday, 13 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Mossley face Chasetown at Seel Park on late Tuesday looking to secure a first win in seven Northern Premier League Division One – West games.

Mossley have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Stalybridge Celtic on December 26.

They face a Chasetown side who have won four and lost two over that time.

Mossley are 16th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

Chasetown are 10 places ahead of the hosts in sixth, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

With 31 goals in 25 games so far this season, Mossley are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Mossley's last match was on Saturday, a 0-0 draw against Nantwich Town.

Chasetown beat Kidsgrove Athletic 2-0 last time out, on February 3.
