Vauxhall Motors v Newcastle Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Motassist Arena
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Vauxhall Motors host Newcastle Town on Saturday at the Motassist Arena in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

In their last league match, on April 13, Vauxhall Motors beat Mossley 2-1 away.

Newcastle also won, 1-0 at home against Nantwich Town.

Vauxhall Motors are 16th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn four, earning 40 points.

Newcastle are three places ahead of the home side in 13th, with 13 wins and six draws putting them on 45 points.

With 41 goals in 35 games so far this season, Town are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Vauxhall Motors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Vauxhall Motors are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.
