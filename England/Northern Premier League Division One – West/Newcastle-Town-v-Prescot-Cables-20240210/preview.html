Newcastle Town v Prescot Cables

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Lyme Valley Stadium
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Prescot Cables face Newcastle Town on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Prescot Cables are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Newcastle team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 43 goals in 24 games so far this season, Prescot Cables are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Newcastle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The away side are second in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn five, earning 47 points.

Town are 11 places behind Prescot Cables in 13th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

In the last five years, Newcastle and Prescot Cables have played each other on four occasions. Newcastle won two of them, Prescot Cables one, and they drew once.

On average, Newcastle scored 1.5 goals and Prescot Cables 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 4 2023, when Newcastle won 3-2 at home.

Newcastle's last match was on February 3, a 3-1 loss against Widnes.

Prescot Cables beat City of Liverpool 4-1 last time out, also on February 3.
