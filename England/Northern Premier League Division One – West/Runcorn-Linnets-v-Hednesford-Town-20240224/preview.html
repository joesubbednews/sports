Runcorn Linnets v Hednesford Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Hednesford Town face a challenge away against high-flying Runcorn Linnets on Saturday.

Hednesford Town are 19th in the Northern Premier League Division One – West table, and have picked up five wins and eight draws in their 28 games to date.

Runcorn Linnets, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 49 points, having won 15 and drawn four of their first 27 matches, and are seven points behind table-toppers Leek Town.

With 45 goals in 27 games so far this season, Runcorn Linnets are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Hednesford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Town's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Runcorn Linnets's 13.

Runcorn Linnets's last match was on February 17, a 1-0 win against Stalybridge Celtic.

Hednesford lost 1-0 against Mossley last time out, also on February 17.
