Hanley Town v City of Liverpool

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

City of Liverpool travel to Hanley Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Prescot Cables 4-1 at home.

Hanley also lost their last match, 3-0 against Nantwich Town.

With 23 goals in 25 games so far this season, Hanley are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

City of Liverpool, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are 15th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

The visitors are four places ahead of the home side in 11th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

Hanley are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, City of Liverpool's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Hanley's six.

Over the last two years, Hanley and City of Liverpool have played each other twice. City of Liverpool won both of them.

Their last meeting was on February 11, when City of Liverpool won 1-0 at home.
