Nantwich Town v Mossley

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Weaver Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Nantwich Town host Mossley at the Weaver Stadium on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

Nantwich have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Mossley side who have also won their last two matches, and collected 14 points from the last possible 18.

With 48 goals in 31 games so far this season, Nantwich are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Mossley, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Town are ninth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

The visitors are one place behind the home side in 10th, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on the same number of points.

Nantwich's last match was on March 16, a 3-0 win against Chasetown.

Mossley beat Kidsgrove Athletic 3-2 last time out, also on March 16.
