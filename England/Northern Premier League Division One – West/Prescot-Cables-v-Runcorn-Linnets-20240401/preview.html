Prescot Cables v Runcorn Linnets

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Valerie Park
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Northern Premier League Division One West's top sides face each other at Valerie Park in Monday's kick-off, when second-placed Prescot Cables host third-placed Runcorn Linnets.

Prescot Cables have picked up 16 wins and nine draws from 32 games so far this season, and sit on the same number of points as the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Runcorn Linnets, meanwhile, have won 17 and drawn six of 33, picking up 57 points.

With 50 goals in 32 games so far this season, Prescot Cables are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Runcorn Linnets are also above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Prescot Cables and Runcorn Linnets have played each other on eight occasions. Prescot Cables won one of them, Runcorn Linnets six, and they drew once.

On average, Prescot Cables scored 0.6 goals and Runcorn Linnets 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Runcorn Linnets won 2-1 at home.

The home side are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Prescot Cables's nine.

Prescot Cables's last match was on Saturday, a 1-1 draw against Clitheroe.

Runcorn Linnets drew 0-0 with Nantwich Town last time out, on Friday.
