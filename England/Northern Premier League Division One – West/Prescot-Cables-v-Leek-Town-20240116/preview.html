Prescot Cables v Leek Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Valerie Park
Tuesday, 16 January 2024 @ 7.45pm

Northern Premier League Division One West's top two sides face each other at Valerie Park in Tuesday's late kick-off, when second-placed Prescot Cables host Leek Town.

Prescot Cables have picked up 12 wins and five draws from 22 games so far this season, and sit seven points below the visitors going into the 7.45pm match.

Leek, meanwhile, have won 15 and drawn three of 23, picking up 48 points.

With 56 goals in 23 games so far this season, Leek are the league's highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Prescot Cables are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Prescot Cables's 10.

In the last five years, Prescot Cables and Leek have played each other on four occasions. Leek won three of them and they drew once.

On average, Prescot Cables scored 0.8 goals and Leek 2.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 11, when Leek won 4-1 away.

Prescot Cables's last match was on Friday, a 1-1 draw against Vauxhall Motors.

Leek drew 0-0 with Trafford last time out, on Saturday.
