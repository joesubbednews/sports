Runcorn Linnets v Widnes

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

The Northern Premier League Division One West's two meanest defences go head-to-head on Saturday, when Widnes visit Runcorn Linnets.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Widnes to date: the away side have let in just 36 goals in 34 games.

Runcorn Linnets have conceded 37 goals in 36 games, giving them the second tightest back line so far this season.

Runcorn Linnets are second in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn eight, earning 62 points.

Widnes are four places behind the hosts in sixth, with 16 wins and six draws putting them on 54 points.

In the last 10 years, Runcorn Linnets and Widnes have played each other on six occasions. Runcorn Linnets won three of them, Widnes two, and they drew once.

On average, Runcorn Linnets scored 1.8 goals and the Whites 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Widnes won 2-0 at home.

Runcorn Linnets are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and four draws from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the Whites' form is better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Runcorn Linnets's 10.

Runcorn Linnets's last match was on April 13, a 0-0 draw against Hanley Town.

Widnes beat Prescot Cables 4-2 last time out, on April 12.
