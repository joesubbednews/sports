City of Liverpool v Hanley Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

City of Liverpool face Hanley Town on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

City of Liverpool are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Hanley team who have won just one and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 28 goals in 29 games so far this season, Hanley are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

City of Liverpool, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Town are 16th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

The hosts are eight places ahead of the away team in eighth, with 13 wins and six draws putting them on 45 points.

Over the last two years, City of Liverpool and Hanley have played each other twice. City of Liverpool won both of them.

Their last meeting was on February 11 2023, when City of Liverpool won 1-0 at home.

City of Liverpool's last match was on March 16, a 1-1 draw against Newcastle Town.

Hanley beat Witton Albion 2-1 last time out, also on March 16.
