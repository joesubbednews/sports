Trafford v Prescot Cables

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Shawe View
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Trafford face Prescot Cables at Shawe View on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Kidsgrove Athletic on April 20, Trafford sit 18th in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

They travel to play a Prescot Cables side fourth in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-0 against Stalybridge Celtic.

In the last 10 years, Trafford and Prescot Cables have played each other on seven occasions. Trafford won three of them and Prescot Cables four.

On average, Trafford scored 1.6 goals and Prescot Cables 2.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Prescot Cables won 5-1 at home.

With 40 goals in 37 games so far this season, Trafford are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Prescot Cables, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Trafford's eight.
