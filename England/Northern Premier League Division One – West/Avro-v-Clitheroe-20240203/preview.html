Avro v Clitheroe

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Clitheroe face Avro on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Clitheroe are unbeaten in five, with one win and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face an Avro team who have won one and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 27 goals in 25 games so far this season, Avro are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Clitheroe, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are fifth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won nine and drawn 11, earning 38 points.

The away side are six places behind Avro in 11th, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

Avro's last match was on January 27, a 1-0 win against Hanley Town.

Clitheroe drew 1-1 with Newcastle Town last time out, also on January 27.
