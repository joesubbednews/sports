Clitheroe v Hanley Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Shawbridge
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hanley Town travel to Clitheroe on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Chasetown 0-0 away.

Clitheroe, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Kidsgrove Athletic.

With 28 goals in 32 games so far this season, Hanley are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Clitheroe, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Town are 17th in the table after 32 games, of which they have won nine and drawn nine, earning 36 points.

The home team are eight places ahead of the visitors in ninth, with 13 wins and 10 draws putting them on 49 points.

Clitheroe are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Hanley's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Clitheroe's eight.

In the last three years, Clitheroe and Hanley have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Hanley won 2-0 at home.
