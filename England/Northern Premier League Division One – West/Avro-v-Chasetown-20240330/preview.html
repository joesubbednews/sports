Avro v Chasetown

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chasetown travel looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – West games against Avro on Saturday.

Chasetown have lost four and drawn one matches since they last earned three points – against Kidsgrove Athletic on February 3.

They face an Avro side who have won just one and drawn one over that time.

With 31 goals in 31 games so far this season, Avro are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Chasetown are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The visitors are 13th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

The home team are one place ahead of Chasetown in 12th, with 10 wins and 12 draws putting them on 42 points.

Avro's last match was on March 23, a 1-0 win against Vauxhall Motors.

Chasetown lost 2-0 against Witton Albion last time out, also on March 23.
