Nantwich Town v Leek Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Weaver Stadium
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leek Town face Nantwich Town on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Leek are unbeaten in 10, with nine wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Nantwich team who have won four and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 57 goals in 24 games so far this season, Leek are the league's highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Nantwich, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Leek Town are top of the table after 24 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn three, earning 51 points.

Nantwich Town are 13 places behind the away side in 14th, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

Nantwich's last match was on January 27, a 1-0 loss against Prescot Cables.

Leek beat Mossley 1-0 last time out, also on January 27.
