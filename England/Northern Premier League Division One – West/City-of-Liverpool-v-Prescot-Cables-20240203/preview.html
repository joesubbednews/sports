City of Liverpool v Prescot Cables

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between City of Liverpool and Prescot Cables promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Northern Premier League Division One West's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game, City of Liverpool and Prescot Cables sit second and joint-fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 43 and 39 goals respectively.

Prescot Cables are second in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

City of Liverpool are eight places behind the visitors in 10th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

In the last 10 years, City of Liverpool and Prescot Cables have played each other on eight occasions. City of Liverpool won three of them, Prescot Cables two, and they drew three times.

On average, City of Liverpool scored 2.6 goals and Prescot Cables 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 3, when Prescot Cables won 2-1 at home.

The home side are in mixed form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Prescot Cables's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to City of Liverpool's 10.

City of Liverpool's last match was on January 27, a 5-1 win against Hednesford Town.

Prescot Cables beat Nantwich Town 1-0 last time out, also on January 27.
