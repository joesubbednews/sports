Prescot Cables v Stalybridge Celtic

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Valerie Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Prescot Cables host Stalybridge Celtic in Saturday's match at Valerie Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

Prescot Cables, who sit fourth in the league after 35 games, fell to a 4-2 away defeat to Widnes on April 12.

They face a Stalybridge Celtic side who also lost their last match, a 1-0 defeat to Chasetown, and who sit 12th in the table.

With 55 goals in 35 games so far this season, Prescot Cables are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Stalybridge Celtic, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, the away team's form is similar – they have both taken six points from 18.
