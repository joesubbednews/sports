Chasetown v Nantwich Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Scholars Ground
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Nantwich Town travel to Chasetown on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Kidsgrove Athletic 4-1 away.

Chasetown, meanwhile, drew their last match, 1-1 against Widnes.

With 45 goals in 30 games so far this season, Nantwich are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Chasetown, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Chasetown's seven.

Chasetown are 12th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

The visitors are three places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with 12 wins and five draws putting them on 41 points.
