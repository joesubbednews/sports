Stalybridge Celtic v Widnes

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Bower Fold
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stalybridge Celtic face Widnes at Bower Fold on Saturday looking to secure a first win in eight Northern Premier League Division One – West games.

Stalybridge Celtic have lost three and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Bootle on January 6.

They face a Widnes side who have won four and drawn two over that time.

With 44 goals in 30 games so far this season, Widnes are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Stalybridge Celtic, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The Whites are seventh in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn six, earning 45 points.

The hosts are four places behind the away team in 11th, with 10 wins and 11 draws putting them on 41 points.

Stalybridge Celtic's last match was on March 16, a 3-1 loss against Vauxhall Motors.

Widnes beat Trafford 2-0 last time out, also on March 16.
