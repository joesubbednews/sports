Witton Albion v Newcastle Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Help For Heroes Stadium
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Newcastle Town travel to Witton Albion on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Leek Town 2-1 at home.

Witton, meanwhile, won their last match, 3-0 against 1874 Northwich.

With 56 goals in 34 games so far this season, Witton are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 57 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Newcastle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Albion are fifth in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn five, earning 53 points.

Town are nine places behind the home team in 14th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

Witton are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Witton's nine.

In the last three years, Witton and Newcastle have played each other on three occasions. Witton won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Witton won 2-1 away.
