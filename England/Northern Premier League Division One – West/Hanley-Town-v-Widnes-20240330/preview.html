Hanley Town v Widnes

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Widnes travel to Hanley Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Stalybridge Celtic 4-3 away.

Hanley, meanwhile, drew their last match, 0-0 against City of Liverpool.

Hanley are 16th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

Widnes are seven places ahead of Town in ninth, with 13 wins and six draws putting them on 45 points.

With 28 goals in 30 games so far this season, the home side are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The Whites, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Hanley are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Hanley's five.

In the last three years, Hanley and Widnes have played each other on three occasions. Hanley won one of them and Widnes the other.

Their last meeting was on September 13, when Hanley won 1-0 away.
