Kidsgrove Athletic v Clitheroe

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Seddon Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kidsgrove Athletic are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Clitheroe at the Seddon Stadium on Monday, with just one point collected from their last six games.

Kidsgrove have picked up just one draw in their last six Northern Premier League Division One – West games, and face a Clitheroe side whose last six games have brought two wins and two draws.

With 53 goals in 33 games so far this season, Clitheroe are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Kidsgrove are also above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Kidsgrove and Clitheroe have played each other on six occasions. Kidsgrove won five of them and they drew once.

On average, Kidsgrove scored 2.2 goals and Clitheroe 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Athletic are 16th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won nine and drawn nine, earning 36 points.

The away side are nine places ahead of the hosts in seventh, with 13 wins and 10 draws putting them on 49 points.

Kidsgrove's last match was on Friday, a 3-2 loss against Vauxhall Motors.

Clitheroe drew 1-1 with Prescot Cables last time out, on Saturday.
