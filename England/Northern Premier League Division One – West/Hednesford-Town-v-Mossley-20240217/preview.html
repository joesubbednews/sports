Hednesford Town v Mossley

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Keys Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mossley travel to Hednesford Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Chasetown 2-1 at home.

The Pitmen, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-1 against Hanley Town.

Hednesford are 19th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

Mossley are four places ahead of Town in 15th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

The Pitmen are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the home team's eight.

With 33 goals in 27 games so far this season, Hednesford are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Mossley are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.
