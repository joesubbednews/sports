Widnes v Witton Albion

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Widnes host Witton Albion on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

In their last league match, on February 10, Widnes drew with Stalybridge Celtic 1-1 at home.

Witton won, 3-0 at home against Stalybridge Celtic on February 3.

With 42 goals in 27 games so far this season, Witton are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Widnes are also above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Albion are fourth in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

The Whites are four places behind the visitors in eighth, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

The home team are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Witton's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Widnes's 10.

In the last three years, Widnes and Witton have played each other on three occasions. Widnes won one of them and Witton the other.

Their last meeting was on September 30, when Witton won 2-1 at home.
