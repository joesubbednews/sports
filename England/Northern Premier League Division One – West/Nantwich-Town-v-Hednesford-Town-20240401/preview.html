Nantwich Town v Hednesford Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Weaver Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Hednesford Town face Nantwich Town away at the Weaver Stadium on Monday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-0 victory over Witton Albion on Saturday, the Pitmen sit 19th in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

They travel to play a Nantwich side ninth in the standings, who were held in their last match, 0-0 against Runcorn Linnets, on Friday.

With 38 goals in 32 games so far this season, Hednesford are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Nantwich, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Nantwich and Hednesford have played each other on seven occasions. Nantwich won two of them, Hednesford three, and they drew twice.

On average, Nantwich scored 1.7 goals and the Pitmen 2.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Hednesford won 3-2 at home.

Nantwich Town are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Hednesford Town's form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home team's 13.
