Chasetown v Trafford

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Scholars Ground
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Trafford face Chasetown on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Trafford are unbeaten in seven, with two wins and five draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Chasetown team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

Trafford are 18th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won four and drawn 11, earning 23 points.

Chasetown are nine places ahead of the away side in ninth, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

With 24 goals in 23 games so far this season, Trafford are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Chasetown's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-0 loss against Nantwich Town.

Trafford drew 0-0 with Leek Town last time out, on January 13.
