Bootle v Nantwich Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bootle host Nantwich Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

In their last league match, on February 10, Bootle drew with Kidsgrove Athletic 1-1 at home.

Nantwich also drew, 0-0 away at Mossley.

With 36 goals in 27 games so far this season, Nantwich are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Bootle, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are seventh in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn six, earning 39 points.

Town are four places behind Bootle in 11th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

Bootle are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's eight.
