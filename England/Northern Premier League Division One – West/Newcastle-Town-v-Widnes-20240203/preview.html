Newcastle Town v Widnes

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Lyme Valley Stadium
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Newcastle Town face Widnes on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Newcastle are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Widnes team who have won three and lost two over the same number of games.

With 28 goals in 24 games so far this season, Newcastle are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Widnes, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

In the last five years, Newcastle and Widnes have played each other on five occasions. Newcastle won three of them, Widnes one, and they drew once.

On average, Newcastle scored 2.0 goals and the Whites 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 16, when Newcastle won 2-0 away.

Town are 13th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won nine and drawn five, earning 32 points.

The Whites are four places ahead of the home team in ninth, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.
