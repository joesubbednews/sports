Vauxhall Motors v Runcorn Linnets

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Motassist Arena
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Runcorn Linnets travel to the Motassist Arena for Saturday's match against Vauxhall Motors looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Northern Premier League Division One West.

Runcorn Linnets, who sit third in the league after 23 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Newcastle Town on January 13.

They face a Vauxhall Motors side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Prescot Cables, and who sit 17th in the table.

With 27 goals in 23 games so far this season, Vauxhall Motors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Runcorn Linnets, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Vauxhall Motors's seven.
