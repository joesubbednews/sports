Hanley Town v Witton Albion

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hanley Town are on a poor run ahead of hosting Witton Albion on Saturday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Hanley have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Northern Premier League Division One – West games, and face a Witton side whose last six games have brought four wins and two losses.

With 26 goals in 28 games so far this season, Hanley are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Witton, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Town are 17th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Albion are 12 places ahead of the home team in fifth, with 14 wins and five draws putting them on 47 points.

In the last three years, Hanley and Witton have played each other on three occasions. Witton won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when Witton won 2-0 at home.

Hanley's last match was on March 9, a 2-1 loss against Mossley.

Witton beat Clitheroe 3-0 last time out, on March 2.
