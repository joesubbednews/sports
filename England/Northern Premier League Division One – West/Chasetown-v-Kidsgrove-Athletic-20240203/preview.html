Chasetown v Kidsgrove Athletic

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Scholars Ground
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kidsgrove Athletic travel to the Scholars Ground looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – West games against Chasetown on Saturday.

Kidsgrove have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against 1874 Northwich on December 23.

They face a Chasetown side who have won three and lost two over that time.

With 42 goals in 24 games so far this season, Kidsgrove are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Chasetown, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are seventh in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn six, earning 36 points.

Athletic are five places behind Chasetown in 12th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

Chasetown's last match was on January 27, a 3-1 loss against Bootle.

Kidsgrove lost 3-1 against Witton Albion last time out, also on January 27.
