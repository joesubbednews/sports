Nantwich Town v Trafford

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Weaver Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Nantwich Town host Trafford on Saturday at the Weaver Stadium in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

In their last league match, on February 17, Nantwich lost to Bootle 3-1 away.

Trafford won, 2-1 at home against Newcastle Town.

Trafford are 16th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won six and drawn 13, earning 31 points.

Nantwich are three places ahead of the away side in 13th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

With 31 goals in 28 games so far this season, Trafford are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Town are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Trafford's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Nantwich's seven.
