Hanley Town v Hednesford Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Friday, 2 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Hanley Town face Hednesford Town on late Friday looking to secure a first win in seven Northern Premier League Division One – West games.

Hanley have lost two and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Vauxhall Motors on December 12.

They face a Hednesford side who have won two and drawn two over that time.

Hednesford are 19th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

Hanley are three places ahead of Hednesford Town in 16th, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.

With 20 goals in 23 games so far this season, Hanley Town are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The Pitmen are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Hanley's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 loss against Avro.

Hednesford lost 5-1 against City of Liverpool last time out, also on Saturday.
