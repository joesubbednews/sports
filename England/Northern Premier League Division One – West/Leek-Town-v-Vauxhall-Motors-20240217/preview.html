Leek Town v Vauxhall Motors

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Harrison Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Vauxhall Motors face a challenge away against high-flying Leek Town at Harrison Park on Saturday.

Vauxhall Motors are 18th in the Northern Premier League Division One – West table, and have picked up eight wins and three draws in their 25 games to date.

Leek, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 55 points, having won 17 and drawn four of their first 26 matches.

With 60 goals in 26 games so far this season, Leek are the league's highest scorers with 2.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Vauxhall Motors, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Town are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the home team's 14.

Leek's last match was on Tuesday, a 0-0 draw against Prescot Cables.

Vauxhall Motors lost 3-2 against Bootle last time out, on February 3.
