Witton Albion v Leek Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Help For Heroes Stadium
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Witton Albion and Leek Town promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Northern Premier League Division One West's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at the Help For Heroes Stadium, Witton and Leek sit joint-third and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 61 and 82 goals respectively.

Leek are top of the table after 36 games, of which they have won 23 and drawn seven, earning 76 points.

Witton are four places behind Town in fifth, with 18 wins and five draws putting them on 59 points.

Albion are in reasonable form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's 12.

In the last three years, Witton and Leek have played each other on three occasions. Witton won one of them and Leek the other.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Leek won 4-1 at home.

Witton's last match was on April 13, a 4-0 win against Trafford.

Leek drew 1-1 with Bootle last time out, also on April 13.
