Mossley v Kidsgrove Athletic

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mossley face Kidsgrove Athletic on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

Mossley are unbeaten in seven, with three wins and four draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Kidsgrove team who have drawn three and lost four over the same number of games.

In the last 10 years, Mossley and Kidsgrove have played each other on six occasions. Mossley won one of them, Kidsgrove two, and they drew three times.

On average, Mossley scored 1.8 goals and Kidsgrove 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 38 goals in 30 games so far this season, Mossley are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Kidsgrove, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Athletic are 15th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won nine and drawn nine, earning 36 points.

The hosts are four places ahead of the away side in 11th, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

Mossley's last match was on March 9, a 2-1 win against Hanley Town.

Kidsgrove drew 1-1 with Prescot Cables last time out, also on March 9.
