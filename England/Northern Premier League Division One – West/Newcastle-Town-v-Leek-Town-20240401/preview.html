Newcastle Town v Leek Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Lyme Valley Stadium
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Leek Town come to the Lyme Valley Stadium to play Newcastle Town on Monday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Newcastle have two wins and a draw.

With 78 goals in 33 games so far this season, Leek are the league's highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Newcastle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Leek Town are top of the table after 33 games, of which they have won 22 and drawn six, earning 72 points.

Newcastle Town are 12 places behind the away team in 13th, with 12 wins and six draws putting them on 42 points.

In the last 10 years, Newcastle and Leek have played each other on seven occasions. Newcastle won one of them, Leek five, and they drew once.

On average, Newcastle scored 1.3 goals and Leek 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 26, when Leek won 3-0 at home.

Newcastle's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 win against 1874 Northwich.

Leek beat Stalybridge Celtic 3-0 last time out, on Friday.
