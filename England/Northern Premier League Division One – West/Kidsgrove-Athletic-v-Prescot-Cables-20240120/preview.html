Kidsgrove Athletic v Prescot Cables

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Seddon Stadium
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match between Kidsgrove Athletic and Prescot Cables promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Northern Premier League Division One West's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at the Seddon Stadium, Kidsgrove and Prescot Cables sit second and joint-third in the goal-scoring charts, with 41 and 38 goals respectively.

Prescot Cables are second in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

Kidsgrove are six places behind the away team in eighth, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

Athletic are in disappointing form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Prescot Cables's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

In the last five years, Kidsgrove and Prescot Cables have played each other on five occasions. Kidsgrove won one of them, Prescot Cables three, and they drew once.

On average, Kidsgrove scored 0.8 goals and Prescot Cables 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Prescot Cables won 2-1 at home.

Kidsgrove's last match was on January 13, a 1-1 draw against Hednesford Town.

Prescot Cables drew 1-1 with Vauxhall Motors last time out, on January 12.
