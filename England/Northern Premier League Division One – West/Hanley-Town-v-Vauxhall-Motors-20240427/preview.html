Hanley Town v Vauxhall Motors

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hanley Town host Vauxhall Motors on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

In their last league match, on April 20, Hanley lost to Bootle 3-0 away.

Vauxhall Motors won, 3-1 at home against Newcastle Town.

Hanley are 16th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn 10, earning 40 points.

Vauxhall Motors are one place ahead of Town in 15th, with 13 wins and four draws putting them on 43 points.

With 32 goals in 35 games so far this season, the home team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Hanley are in disappointing form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Vauxhall Motors's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Hanley's six.
