Mossley v Nantwich Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Seel Park
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mossley face Nantwich Town at Seel Park on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – West games.

Mossley have lost three and drawn two matches since they last earned three points – against Stalybridge Celtic on December 26.

They face a Nantwich side who have won three and lost two over that time.

Mossley are 16th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

Nantwich are four places ahead of the hosts in 12th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

With 31 goals in 24 games so far this season, Mossley are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Town, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Mossley's last match was on February 3, a 1-1 draw against 1874 Northwich.

Nantwich beat Hanley Town 3-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
