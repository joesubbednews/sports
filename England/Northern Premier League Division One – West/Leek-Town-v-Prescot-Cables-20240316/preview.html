Leek Town v Prescot Cables

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Harrison Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

The Northern Premier League Division One West's top two sides face each other at Harrison Park in Saturday's kick-off, when Leek Town host second-placed Prescot Cables.

Leek have picked up 20 wins and five draws from 30 games so far this season, and sit 12 points above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

Prescot Cables, meanwhile, have won 15 and drawn eight of 29, picking up 53 points.

With 71 goals in 30 games so far this season, Leek are the league's highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Prescot Cables are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Town are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' 14.

In the last five years, Leek and Prescot Cables have played each other on five occasions. Leek won three of them and they drew twice.

On average, Leek scored 2.0 goals and Prescot Cables 0.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 13, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Leek's last match was on March 9, a 3-0 win against 1874 Northwich.

Prescot Cables drew 1-1 with Kidsgrove Athletic last time out, also on March 9.
