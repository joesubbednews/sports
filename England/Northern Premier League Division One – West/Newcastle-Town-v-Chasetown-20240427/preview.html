Newcastle Town v Chasetown

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Lyme Valley Stadium
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Chasetown visit Newcastle Town at the Lyme Valley Stadium on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

Chasetown have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Newcastle side who lost their last match, and have collected six points from the last possible 18.

With 42 goals in 36 games so far this season, Newcastle are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Chasetown, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Town are 13th in the table after 36 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn six, earning 45 points.

The away side are two places ahead of the hosts in 11th, with 15 wins and eight draws putting them on 53 points.

Newcastle's last match was on April 20, a 3-1 loss against Vauxhall Motors.

Chasetown beat Mossley 2-0 last time out, also on April 20.
