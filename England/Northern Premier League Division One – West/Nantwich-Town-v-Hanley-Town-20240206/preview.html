Nantwich Town v Hanley Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Weaver Stadium
Tuesday, 6 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Nantwich Town host Hanley Town on Tuesday at the Weaver Stadium in Northern Premier League Division One West.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Nantwich lost to Leek Town 3-1 at home.

Hanley won, 3-1 at home against Hednesford Town on Friday.

Hanley are 15th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

Nantwich are one place ahead of Hanley Town in 14th, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

With 23 goals in 24 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Nantwich Town are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Hanley's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Nantwich's 10.
