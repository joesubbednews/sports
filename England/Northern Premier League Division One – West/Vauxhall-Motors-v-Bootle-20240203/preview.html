Vauxhall Motors v Bootle

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Motassist Arena
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Vauxhall Motors face Bootle at the Motassist Arena on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – West games.

Vauxhall Motors have lost four and drawn one matches since they last earned three points – against Witton Albion on December 23.

They face a Bootle side who have won just one and drawn one over that time.

Vauxhall Motors are 17th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

Bootle are nine places ahead of the hosts in eighth, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

With 29 goals in 24 games so far this season, Vauxhall Motors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Vauxhall Motors's last match was on January 27, a 4-2 loss against Runcorn Linnets.

Bootle beat Chasetown 3-1 last time out, also on January 27.
