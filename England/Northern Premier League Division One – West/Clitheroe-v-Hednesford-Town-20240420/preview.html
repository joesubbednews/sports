Clitheroe v Hednesford Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Shawbridge
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hednesford Town travel to Clitheroe on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Avro 3-0 at home.

Clitheroe, meanwhile, won their last match, 4-1 against 1874 Northwich.

With 61 goals in 36 games so far this season, Clitheroe are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Hednesford, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Town are 19th in the table after 35 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 10, earning 31 points.

The hosts are 10 places ahead of the Pitmen in ninth, with 14 wins and 10 draws putting them on 52 points.

Clitheroe are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is identical – they have both taken eight points from 18.
