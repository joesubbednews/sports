Runcorn Linnets v Bootle

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Runcorn Linnets host Bootle in Saturday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

Runcorn Linnets, who sit third in the league after 29 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to Leek Town on March 2.

They face a Bootle side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Mossley, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 48 goals in 29 games so far this season, Runcorn Linnets are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Bootle are also above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Runcorn Linnets's 13.

In the last five years, Runcorn Linnets and Bootle have played each other on five occasions. Runcorn Linnets won three of them, Bootle one, and they drew once.

On average, Runcorn Linnets scored 2.2 goals and Bootle 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 27, when Runcorn Linnets won 3-1 away.
