Clitheroe v Vauxhall Motors

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Shawbridge
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Vauxhall Motors face Clitheroe away at Shawbridge on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-1 victory over 1874 Northwich on February 24, Vauxhall Motors sit 18th in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

They travel to play a Clitheroe side ninth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-0 against Witton Albion, on March 2.

With 47 goals in 29 games so far this season, Clitheroe are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Vauxhall Motors, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Clitheroe's seven.
