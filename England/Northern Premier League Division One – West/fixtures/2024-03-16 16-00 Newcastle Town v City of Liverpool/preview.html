Newcastle Town v City of Liverpool

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Lyme Valley Stadium
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

City of Liverpool travel to Newcastle Town on Saturday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Trafford 1-0 away.

Newcastle, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-1 against City of Liverpool.

With 51 goals in 28 games so far this season, City of Liverpool are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Newcastle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The away team are sixth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

Town are seven places behind City of Liverpool in 13th, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, City of Liverpool's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Newcastle's seven.

In the last five years, Newcastle and City of Liverpool have played each other on five occasions. Newcastle won two of them and City of Liverpool three.

On average, Newcastle scored 1.2 goals and City of Liverpool 2.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 27, when City of Liverpool won 3-1 at home.
