Kidsgrove Athletic v Prescot Cables

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Seddon Stadium
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kidsgrove Athletic face Prescot Cables at the Seddon Stadium on Saturday looking to secure a first win in 11 Northern Premier League Division One – West games.

Kidsgrove have lost six and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against 1874 Northwich on December 23.

They face a Prescot Cables side who have won five and drawn three over that time.

Prescot Cables are second in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn seven, earning 52 points.

Kidsgrove are 13 places behind the away team in 15th, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

With 47 goals in 28 games so far this season, Prescot Cables are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Athletic are also above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last five years, Kidsgrove and Prescot Cables have played each other on five occasions. Kidsgrove won one of them, Prescot Cables three, and they drew once.

On average, Kidsgrove scored 0.8 goals and Prescot Cables 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Prescot Cables won 2-1 at home.

Kidsgrove's last match was on March 2, a 4-1 loss against Nantwich Town.

Prescot Cables beat Witton Albion 3-2 last time out, on February 24.
