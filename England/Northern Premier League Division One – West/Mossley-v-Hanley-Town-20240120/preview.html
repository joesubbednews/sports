Mossley v Hanley Town

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hanley Town travel looking to secure a first win in six Northern Premier League Division One – West games against Mossley on Saturday.

Hanley have lost one and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Vauxhall Motors on December 12.

They face a Mossley side who have won just one and drawn one over that time.

Hanley are 16th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won seven and drawn six, earning 27 points.

Mossley are one place ahead of Town in 15th, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

With 20 goals in 22 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last three years, Mossley and Hanley have played each other on three occasions. Mossley won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Mossley won 2-0 away.

Mossley's last match was on January 13, a 4-0 loss against Widnes.

Hanley drew 1-1 with Trafford last time out, on January 6.
