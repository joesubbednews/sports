Mossley v City of Liverpool

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

City of Liverpool face Mossley on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

City of Liverpool are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Mossley team who have won three and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 52 goals in 30 games so far this season, City of Liverpool are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Mossley, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last five years, Mossley and City of Liverpool have played each other on five occasions. Mossley won one of them, City of Liverpool three, and they drew once.

On average, Mossley scored 0.8 goals and City of Liverpool 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 10, when City of Liverpool won 4-1 at home.

The away side are eighth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn seven, earning 46 points.

The hosts are three places behind City of Liverpool in 11th, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on 44 points.

Mossley's last match was on March 23, a 2-1 loss against Nantwich Town.

City of Liverpool drew 0-0 with Hanley Town last time out, also on March 23.
