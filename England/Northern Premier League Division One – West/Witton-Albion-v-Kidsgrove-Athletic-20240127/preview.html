Witton Albion v Kidsgrove Athletic

Northern Premier League Division One – West | Help For Heroes Stadium
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Witton Albion host Kidsgrove Athletic on Saturday at the Help For Heroes Stadium in Northern Premier League Division One West.

In their last league match, on January 20, Witton lost to Nantwich Town 1-0 away.

Kidsgrove drew, 1-1 at home against Hednesford Town on January 13.

With 41 goals in 23 games so far this season, Kidsgrove are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Witton, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Albion are in good form in Northern Premier League Division One West, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Athletic's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.

Witton are seventh in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn five, earning 35 points.

The away team are two places behind Witton in ninth, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last three years, Witton and Kidsgrove have played each other on three occasions. Witton won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Witton won 3-1 away.
