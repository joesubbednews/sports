Kidsgrove Athletic v Trafford

Northern Premier League Division One – West | The Seddon Stadium
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Saturday's match at the Seddon Stadium sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 17th-ranked Kidsgrove Athletic host 18th-placed Trafford.

Kidsgrove have picked up 39 points from their first 39 Northern Premier League Division One – West games, with 10 wins and nine draws.

That is three points more than Trafford have collected, having won seven and drawn 15.

In the last 10 years, Kidsgrove and Trafford have played each other on seven occasions. Kidsgrove won three of them, Trafford two, and they drew twice.

On average, Kidsgrove scored 1.1 goals and Trafford 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Kidsgrove won 2-1 away.

Kidsgrove are in bad form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Trafford's form is slightly better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Athletic's four.

With 38 goals in 36 games so far this season, the away side are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Kidsgrove's last match was on April 13, a 5-0 loss against City of Liverpool.

Trafford lost 4-0 against Witton Albion last time out, also on April 13.
