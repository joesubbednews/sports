Widnes v Vauxhall Motors

Northern Premier League Division One – West
Monday, 1 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Vauxhall Motors travel to Widnes on Monday in the Northern Premier League Division One West.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Kidsgrove Athletic 3-2 at home.

The Whites also won their last match, 1-0 against Hanley Town.

With 48 goals in 32 games so far this season, Widnes are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Vauxhall Motors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The away team are 15th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn four, earning 37 points.

The Whites are seven places ahead of Vauxhall Motors in eighth, with 14 wins and six draws putting them on 48 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Northern Premier League Division One West, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, Vauxhall Motors's form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
