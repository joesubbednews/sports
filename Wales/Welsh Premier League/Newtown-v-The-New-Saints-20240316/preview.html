Newtown v The New Saints

Welsh Premier League | G.F. Grigg Latham Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 2.30pm

Newtown host The New Saints in Saturday's early match at G.F. Grigg Latham Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Welsh Premier League.

The White Stars, who sit sixth in the league after 27 games, fell to a 0-2 home defeat to Cardiff Met on March 9.

They face a The New Saints side who picked up a win in their last match, a 5-1 victory against Connah's Quay, and who sit first in the table.

With 98 goals in 27 games so far this season, The New Saints are the league's highest scorers with 3.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Newtown, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The White Stars are in bad form in the Welsh Premier League, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, the Saints' form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to the hosts' three.

In the last 10 years, Newtown and The New Saints have played each other on 36 occasions. Newtown won four of them, The New Saints 26, and they drew six times.

On average, the White Stars scored 0.8 goals and the Saints 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 6, when The New Saints won 3-0 at home.
