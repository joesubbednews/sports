Wrexham Women v Swansea City

Welsh Premier Women's League
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Wrexham Women host Swansea City in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Welsh Premier Women's League.

Wrexham, who sit third in the league after 16 games, fell to a 6-1 home defeat to Cardiff City on March 17.

They face a Swansea side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-2 victory against Wrexham Women, and who sit second in the table.

With 38 goals in 15 games so far this season, Swansea are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Wrexham are also above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Welsh Premier Women's League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, City's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Wrexham's 10.

In the last three years, Wrexham and Swansea have played each other on three occasions. Swansea won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on March 10, when Swansea won 3-2 at home.
