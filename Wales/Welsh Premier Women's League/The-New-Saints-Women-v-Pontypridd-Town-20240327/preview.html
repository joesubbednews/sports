The New Saints Women v Pontypridd Town

Welsh Premier Women's League
Wednesday, 27 March 2024 @ 7.45pm

Struggling Pontypridd Town face The New Saints Women away on Wednesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Barry Town United Women on Sunday, Pontypridd sit bottom of the Welsh Premier Women's League.

They travel to play a The New Saints side fourth in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-1 against Cardiff Metropolitan.

With 14 goals in 17 games so far this season, Pontypridd are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The New Saints, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.8 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, The New Saints and Pontypridd have played each other on eight occasions. The New Saints won four of them, Pontypridd two, and they drew twice.

On average, the New Saints scored 2.2 goals and Pontypridd 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 26, when The New Saints won 3-1 at home.

The New Saints are in bad form in the Welsh Premier Women's League, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's four.
