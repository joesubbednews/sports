Cardiff Metropolitan v Pontypridd Town

Welsh Premier Women's League
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Cardiff Metropolitan face Pontypridd Town on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Barry Town United Women on Wednesday, Cardiff Metropolitan sit sixth in the Welsh Premier Women's League.

They travel to play a Pontypridd side eighth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 4-0 against The New Saints Women.

In the last 10 years, Cardiff Metropolitan and Pontypridd have played each other on seven occasions. Cardiff Metropolitan won six of them and Pontypridd one.

On average, Cardiff Metropolitan scored 2.1 goals and Pontypridd 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 17, when Pontypridd won 2-1 at home.

With 14 goals in 18 games so far this season, Pontypridd are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Cardiff Metropolitan are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Welsh Premier Women's League, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.
