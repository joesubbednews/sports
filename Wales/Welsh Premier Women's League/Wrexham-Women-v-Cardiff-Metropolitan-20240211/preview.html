Wrexham Women v Cardiff Metropolitan

Welsh Premier Women's League
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 5.010pm

Relegation candidates Cardiff Metropolitan face a challenge away against high-flying Wrexham Women on Sunday.

Cardiff Metropolitan are seventh in the Welsh Premier Women's League table, and have picked up three wins and two draws in their 13 games to date.

Wrexham, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 28 points, having won nine and drawn one, and are six points behind table-toppers Cardiff City.

Wrexham are in reasonable form in Welsh Premier Women's League, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Cardiff Metropolitan's form is much worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the hosts' 12.

With 14 goals in 13 games so far this season, the away side are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Wrexham, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Wrexham's last match was on February 4, a 3-2 win against Pontypridd Town.

Cardiff Metropolitan lost 4-0 against Aberystwyth Town last time out, on February 1.
