Cardiff City v Swansea City

Welsh Premier Women's League
Wednesday, 27 March 2024 @ 7.45pm

The Welsh Premier Women's League's top two sides face each other in Wednesday's late kick-off, when Cardiff City host second-placed Swansea City.

Cardiff have picked up 14 wins and one draw from 17 games so far this season, and sit five points above the visitors going into the 7.45pm match.

The Swans, meanwhile, have won 12 and drawn two of 16, picking up 38 points.

With 52 goals in 17 games so far this season, Cardiff are the league's highest scorers with 3.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in nine goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Swansea are also above average scorers, with 2.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Cardiff City are in reasonable form in the Welsh Premier Women's League, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Swansea City's form is better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to the home team's 12.

In the last 10 years, Cardiff and Swansea have played each other on 12 occasions. Cardiff won four of them, Swansea six, and they drew twice.

On average, Cardiff scored 1.1 goals and the Swans 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 11, when Swansea won 2-0 away.

Cardiff's last match was on Sunday, a 2-0 win against Aberystwyth Town.

Swansea beat Wrexham Women 3-2 last time out, also on Sunday.
