Cardiff City v Wrexham Women

Welsh Premier Women's League
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 5.010pm

Cardiff City host Wrexham Women in Sunday's late match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Welsh Premier Women's League.

Cardiff, who sit top of the league after 19 games, fell to a 2-0 away defeat to Swansea City on March 31.

They face a Wrexham side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-0 victory against Aberystwyth Town, and who sit third in the table.

With 56 goals in 19 games so far this season, Cardiff are the league's highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Wrexham are also above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

City are in reasonable form in the Welsh Premier Women's League, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home side's 12.

In the last three years, Cardiff and Wrexham have played each other on three occasions. Cardiff won all of them.

Their last meeting was on March 17, when Cardiff won 6-1 away.
