Swansea City v Aberystwyth Town

Welsh Premier Women's League
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Swansea City host Aberystwyth Town in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Welsh Premier Women's League.

The Swans, who sit second in the league after 12 games, fell to a 0-5 home defeat to The New Saints Women on January 28.

They face an Aberystwyth side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-0 victory against Cardiff Metropolitan, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 32 goals in 12 games so far this season, Swansea are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Aberystwyth, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Swansea and Aberystwyth have played each other on nine occasions. Swansea won five of them and they drew four times.

On average, the Swans scored 3.0 goals and Aberystwyth 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 29, when Swansea won 4-0 away.

City are in fantastic form in Welsh Premier Women's League, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Town's form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the Swans' 15.
