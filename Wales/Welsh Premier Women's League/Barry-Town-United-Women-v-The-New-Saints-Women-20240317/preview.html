Barry Town United Women v The New Saints Women

Welsh Premier Women's League
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as sixth-ranked Barry Town United Women host fifth-placed The New Saints Women.

Barry Town United have picked up 16 points from their first 16 Welsh Premier Women's League games, with five wins and one draw.

That is one point less than the New Saints have collected, having won five and drawn two.

Barry Town United are in mixed form in the Welsh Premier Women's League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, The New Saints' form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

With 18 goals in 15 games so far this season, Barry Town United are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

The New Saints, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.9 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Barry Town United and The New Saints have played each other on eight occasions. Barry Town United won five of them and The New Saints three.

On average, Barry Town United scored 2.6 goals and the New Saints 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 21, when Barry Town United won 4-1 at home.

Barry Town United's last match was on March 10, a 1-1 draw against Pontypridd Town.

The New Saints lost 4-1 against Cardiff Metropolitan last time out, also on March 10.
