Aberystwyth Town v Wrexham Women

Welsh Premier Women's League
Wednesday, 27 March 2024 @ 7.45pm

Wrexham Women travel for Wednesday's late match against Aberystwyth Town looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Welsh Premier Women's League.

Wrexham, who sit third in the league after 17 games, fell to a 3-2 home defeat to Swansea City on Sunday.

They face an Aberystwyth side who also lost their last match, a 2-0 defeat to Cardiff City, and who sit sixth in the table.

With 21 goals in 16 games so far this season, Aberystwyth are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Wrexham, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Town are in disappointing form in the Welsh Premier Women's League, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

Over the last two years, Aberystwyth and Wrexham have played each other twice. Wrexham won both of them.

Their last meeting was on January 28, when Wrexham won 1-0 at home.
