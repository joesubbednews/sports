Glasgow City Women v Hamilton Academical

SWPL 1 | Petershill Park
Wednesday, 7 February 2024 @ 8.00pm

Struggling Hamilton Academical face Glasgow City Women away at Petershill Park on Wednesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-1 victory over Dundee United Women on January 28, the Accies sit bottom of SWPL 1.

They travel to play a Glasgow City side third in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Celtic Women.

Glasgow City are in exceptional form in SWPL 1, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Hamilton Academical's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the hosts' 18.

With 14 goals in 18 games so far this season, the Accies are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 73 goals at a rate of 4.1 per game.

Glasgow City, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.2. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Glasgow City and Hamilton Academical have played each other on seven occasions. Glasgow City won all of them.

On average, Glasgow City scored 4.7 goals and the Accies 0.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 13, when Glasgow City won 5-0 away.
