Spartans Women v Motherwell Women

SWPL 1 | Ainslie Park Stadium
Wednesday, 17 April 2024 @ 7.45pm

Motherwell Women visit Spartans Women at the Ainslie Park Stadium on late Wednesday on the back of four consecutive wins in SWPL 1.

Motherwell have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Spartans side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

In the last 10 years, Spartans and Motherwell have played each other on 12 occasions. They won four each, and they drew four times.

On average, the Spartans scored 1.3 goals and Motherwell 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 3, when Motherwell won 4-1 away.

Spartans are 10th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won four and drawn four, earning 16 points.

Motherwell are two places ahead of the Spartans in eighth, with nine wins and one draw putting them on 28 points.

With 31 goals in 25 games so far this season, the home team are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

Spartans's last match was on Sunday, a 2-1 loss against Montrose Women, with Rebecca Galbraith getting the goal for the Spartans.

Motherwell beat Dundee United Women 2-1 last time out, also on Sunday, with Laura Berry and Morgan Cross on the scoresheet.
