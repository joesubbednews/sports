Motherwell Women v Celtic Women

SWPL 1 | K Park Training Academy
Wednesday, 7 February 2024 @ 8.00pm

Celtic Women travel to K Park Training Academy for Wednesday's late match against Motherwell Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in SWPL 1.

Celtic, who sit second in the league after 18 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Glasgow City Women on January 28.

They face a Motherwell side who also lost their last match, a 5-0 defeat to Hibernian Women, and who sit eighth in the table.

With 90 goals in 18 games so far this season, Celtic are the league's highest scorers with 5.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 10 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Motherwell, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.2. They have conceded 2.7 goals per game.

In Amy Gallagher, the away side have the league's most on-form striker so far this season. She has notched 18 goals in 17 appearances.

Her goal rate of one every 79 minutes is quicker than that of Carla Boyce, the hosts' top scorer with a goal every 149 minutes, and a total of seven goals in 18 games.

In the last 10 years, Motherwell and Celtic have played each other on nine occasions. Celtic won eight of them and they drew once.

On average, Motherwell scored 0.1 goals and Celtic 5.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Celtic won 6-0 at home.

Motherwell are in bad form in SWPL 1, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Celtic's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Motherwell's four.
