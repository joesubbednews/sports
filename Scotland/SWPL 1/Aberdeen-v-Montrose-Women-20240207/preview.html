Aberdeen v Montrose Women

SWPL 1 | Balmoral Stadium
Wednesday, 7 February 2024 @ 7.30pm

Aberdeen welcome Montrose Women to the Balmoral Stadium on late Wednesday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Aberdeen's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six SWPL 1 matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

Montrose are 10th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won two and drawn four, earning 10 points.

Aberdeen are three places ahead of the away team in seventh, with six wins and one draw putting them on 19 points.

With 16 goals in 18 games so far this season, Montrose are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 74 goals at a rate of 4.1 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.2. They have conceded 3.1 goals per game.

Aberdeen's last match was on January 28, a 2-1 loss against Rangers Women, with Bayley Hutchison getting the goal for Aberdeen.

Montrose lost 2-1 against Partick Thistle Women last time out, also on January 28, with Jade McLaren on the scoresheet.
