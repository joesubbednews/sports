Montrose Women v Hamilton Academical

SWPL 1 | Links Park
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 1.00pm

Struggling Montrose Women face Hamilton Academical at Links Park on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Dundee United Women on March 17, Montrose sit ninth in SWPL 1.

They travel to play a Hamilton Academical side 12th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 4-0 against Motherwell Women.

Montrose are in disappointing form in SWPL 1, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Hamilton Academical's form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

With 17 goals in 23 games so far this season, the Accies are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 92 goals at a rate of 4.0 per game.

Montrose are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.7 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Montrose and Hamilton Academical have played each other twice. Montrose won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on March 3, when Montrose won 7-0 at home.
