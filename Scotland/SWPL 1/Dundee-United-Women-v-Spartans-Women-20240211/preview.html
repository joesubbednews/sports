Dundee United Women v Spartans Women

SWPL 1 | Gussie Park
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Sunday's match at Gussie Park sees two relegation candidates play each other, as ninth-ranked Dundee United Women host 11th-placed Spartans Women.

Dundee United have picked up 10 points from their first 10 SWPL 1 games, with three wins and one draw.

That is one point more than the Spartans have collected, having won two and drawn three.

Dundee United are in awful form in SWPL 1, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Spartans's form is slightly better – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the home side's zero.

In the last 10 years, Dundee United and Spartans have played each other on six occasions. Dundee United won two of them and Spartans four.

On average, Dundee United scored 1.0 goal and the Spartans 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 8, when Dundee United won 2-1 away.

With 16 goals in 19 games so far this season, Dundee United are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 73 goals at a rate of 3.8 per game.

The Spartans are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.2. They have conceded 2.7 goals per game.

Dundee United's last match was on January 31, a 4-0 loss against Hibernian Women.

Spartans lost 1-0 against Partick Thistle Women last time out, on Wednesday.
