Dundee United Women v Hamilton Academical

SWPL 1 | Foundation Park
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Saturday's early match at Foundation Park sees the league's two bottom sides face each other, as rock-bottom Dundee United Women host Hamilton Academical.

Dundee United have picked up 10 points from their first 10 SWPL 1 games, with three wins and one draw.

That is three points less than the Accies have collected, having won three and drawn four.

Dundee United are in awful form in SWPL 1, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Hamilton Academical's form is better – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the home team's zero.

In the last 10 years, Dundee United and Hamilton Academical have played each other on nine occasions. They won three each, and they drew three times.

On average, Dundee United scored 2.0 goals and the Accies 2.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 17, when Hamilton Academical won 5-2 at home.

With 25 goals in 27 games so far this season, Dundee United are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 103 goals at a rate of 3.8 per game.

The Accies are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.8 goals per game.

Dundee United's last match was on Sunday, a 5-1 loss against Aberdeen, with Morgan Steedman getting the goal for Dundee United.

Hamilton Academical lost 3-0 against Spartans Women last time out, also on Sunday.
