Hamilton Academical v Spartans Women

SWPL 1 | ZLX Stadium
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Struggling Hamilton Academical face Spartans Women at the ZLX Stadium on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Motherwell Women on February 11, the Accies sit bottom of SWPL 1.

They travel to play a Spartans side ninth in the standings, who also won their last match, 6-0 against Dundee United Women.

In the last 10 years, Hamilton Academical and Spartans have played each other on nine occasions. Hamilton Academical won one of them, Spartans five, and they drew three times.

On average, the Accies scored 1.4 goals and the Spartans 2.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Hamilton Academical are in mixed form in SWPL 1, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Spartans's form is worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the Accies's seven.

With 16 goals in 20 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 80 goals at a rate of 4.0 per game.

The Spartans are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.
