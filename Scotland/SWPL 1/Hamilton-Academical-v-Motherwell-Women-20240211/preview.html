Hamilton Academical v Motherwell Women

SWPL 1 | ZLX Stadium
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Motherwell Women make the journey to the ZLX Stadium to play Hamilton Academical on Sunday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Motherwell's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six SWPL 1 matches, while their opponents have earned four from a possible 18.

Hamilton Academical are bottom of the table after 19 games, of which they have won one and drawn three, earning six points.

Motherwell are four places ahead of the Accies in eighth, with five wins and one draw putting them on 16 points.

In the last 10 years, Hamilton Academical and Motherwell have played each other on eight occasions. Hamilton Academical won two of them and Motherwell six.

On average, the Accies scored 1.0 goal and Motherwell 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 27, when Motherwell won 4-0 at home.

With 14 goals in 19 games so far this season, the home team are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 79 goals at a rate of 4.2 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.2. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.

Hamilton Academical's last match was on Wednesday, a 6-0 loss against Glasgow City Women.

Motherwell lost 2-0 against Celtic Women last time out, also on Wednesday.
