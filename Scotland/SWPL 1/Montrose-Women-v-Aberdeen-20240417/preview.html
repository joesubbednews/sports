Montrose Women v Aberdeen

SWPL 1 | Links Park
Wednesday, 17 April 2024 @ 7.30pm

Struggling Montrose Women face Aberdeen at Links Park on Wednesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Spartans Women on Sunday, Montrose sit ninth in SWPL 1.

They travel to play an Aberdeen side seventh in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-2 against Hamilton Academical.

With 30 goals in 25 games so far this season, Montrose are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 88 goals at a rate of 3.5 per game.

Aberdeen are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.1 goals per game.

In Bayley Hutchison, the visitors have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. She has notched 19 goals in 25 appearances, to sit fourth in the scoring charts.

Her goal rate of one every 113 minutes is much quicker than that of Jade McLaren, the home side's top scorer with a goal every 279 minutes, and a total of eight goals in 25 games.

Montrose are in reasonable form in SWPL 1, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Aberdeen's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Montrose's 12.

Over the last two years, Montrose and Aberdeen have played each other twice. Aberdeen won both of them.

Their last meeting was on February 7, when Aberdeen won 2-1 at home.
