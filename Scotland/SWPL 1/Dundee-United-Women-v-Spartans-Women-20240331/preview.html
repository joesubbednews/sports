Dundee United Women v Spartans Women

SWPL 1 | Foundation Park
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Sunday's match at Foundation Park sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 11th-ranked Dundee United Women host 10th-placed Spartans Women.

Dundee United have picked up 10 points from their first 10 SWPL 1 games, with three wins and one draw.

That is three points less than the Spartans have collected, having won three and drawn four.

Dundee United are in awful form in SWPL 1, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Spartans's form is better – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the home team's zero.

In the last 10 years, Dundee United and Spartans have played each other on seven occasions. Dundee United won two of them and Spartans five.

On average, Dundee United scored 0.9 goals and the Spartans 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 11, when Spartans won 6-0 away.

With 19 goals in 23 games so far this season, Dundee United are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 87 goals at a rate of 3.8 per game.

The Spartans are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.

Dundee United's last match was on March 17, a 1-0 loss against Montrose Women.

Spartans lost 2-0 against Aberdeen last time out, also on March 17.
