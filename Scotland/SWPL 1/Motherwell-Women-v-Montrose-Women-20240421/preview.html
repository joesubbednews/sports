Motherwell Women v Montrose Women

SWPL 1 | K Park Training Academy
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

Motherwell Women face Montrose Women on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in SWPL 1.

Motherwell are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 4pm kick-off.

They face a Montrose team who have won four and lost one over the same number of games.

With 32 goals in 26 games so far this season, Montrose are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 91 goals at a rate of 3.5 per game.

Motherwell are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The away team are ninth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won six and drawn four, earning 22 points.

The hosts are one place ahead of Montrose in eighth, with nine wins and two draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last three years, Motherwell and Montrose have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Motherwell's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-2 draw against Spartans Women, with Laura Berry getting the goals for Motherwell.

Montrose lost 3-2 against Aberdeen last time out, also on Wednesday, with Jade McLaren and Louise Anne Brown on the scoresheet.
