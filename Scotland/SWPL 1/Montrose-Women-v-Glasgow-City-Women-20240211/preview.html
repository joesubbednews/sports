Montrose Women v Glasgow City Women

SWPL 1 | Links Park
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Relegation candidates Montrose Women face a challenge against high-flying Glasgow City Women at Links Park on Sunday.

Montrose Women are 10th in the SWPL 1 table, and have picked up two wins and four draws in their 19 games to date.

Glasgow City, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 47 points, having won 15 and drawn two, and are eight points behind table-toppers Rangers Women.

Montrose are in bad form in SWPL 1, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Glasgow City's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With 17 goals in 19 games so far this season, Montrose are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 76 goals at a rate of 4.0 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.2. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

Montrose's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-1 loss against Aberdeen, with Holly Daniel getting the goal for Montrose.

Glasgow City beat Hamilton Academical 6-0 last time out, also on Wednesday, with Emily Whelan (two), Aleigh Gambone, Cori Michelle Sullivan, Lauren Davidson and Wilma Forsblom on the scoresheet.
