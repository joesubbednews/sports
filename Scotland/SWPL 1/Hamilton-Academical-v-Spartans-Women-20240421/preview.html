Hamilton Academical v Spartans Women

SWPL 1 | ZLX Stadium
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

Struggling Hamilton Academical face Spartans Women at the ZLX Stadium on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 5-2 victory over Dundee United Women on Wednesday, the Accies sit 11th in SWPL 1.

They travel to play a Spartans side 10th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 2-2 against Motherwell Women.

In the last 10 years, Hamilton Academical and Spartans have played each other on 10 occasions. Hamilton Academical won one of them, Spartans five, and they drew four times.

On average, the Accies scored 1.4 goals and the Spartans 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 18, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Hamilton Academical are in bad form in SWPL 1, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Spartans's form is slightly better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the Accies's four.

With 25 goals in 26 games so far this season, the home team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 99 goals at a rate of 3.8 per game.

The Spartans are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.
