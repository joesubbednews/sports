Aberdeen v Dundee United Women

SWPL 1 | Balmoral Stadium
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Dundee United Women make the journey to the Balmoral Stadium to play Aberdeen on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their eight-game losing streak.

Dundee United's struggles have left them with no points from their last six SWPL 1 matches, while their opponents have earned four from a possible 18.

In the last 10 years, Aberdeen and Dundee United have played each other on seven occasions. Aberdeen won five of them and Dundee United two.

On average, Aberdeen scored 2.7 goals and Dundee United 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Dundee United won 3-2 at home.

Dundee United are 10th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won three and drawn one, earning 10 points.

Aberdeen are three places ahead of the away team in seventh, with seven wins and one draw putting them on 22 points.

In Bayley Hutchison, the hosts have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. She has notched 14 goals in 20 appearances, to sit fourth in the scoring charts.

Her goal rate of one every 122 minutes is much quicker than that of Rachel Todd, Dundee United's top scorer with a goal every 288 minutes, and a total of six goals in 20 games.

With 16 goals in 20 games so far this season, Dundee United are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 79 goals at a rate of 4.0 per game.

Aberdeen are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.0 goals per game.

Aberdeen's last match was on Sunday, a 4-0 loss against Hearts Women.

Dundee United lost 6-0 against Spartans Women last time out, also on Sunday.
