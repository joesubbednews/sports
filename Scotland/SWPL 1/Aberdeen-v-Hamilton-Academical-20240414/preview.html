Aberdeen v Hamilton Academical

SWPL 1 | Balmoral Stadium
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Hamilton Academical make the journey to the Balmoral Stadium to play Aberdeen on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Hamilton Academical's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six SWPL 1 matches, while their opponents have earned nine from a possible 18.

Hamilton Academical are bottom of the table after 24 games, of which they have won two and drawn four, earning 10 points.

Aberdeen are five places ahead of the Accies in seventh, with nine wins and one draw putting them on 28 points.

In the last 10 years, Aberdeen and Hamilton Academical have played each other on 11 occasions. Aberdeen won seven of them, Hamilton Academical three, and they drew once.

On average, Aberdeen scored 1.6 goals and the Accies 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 10, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 18 goals in 24 games so far this season, the away team are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 94 goals at a rate of 3.9 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 3.1 goals per game.

Aberdeen's last match was on March 31, a 3-0 loss against Motherwell Women.

Hamilton Academical lost 2-1 against Montrose Women last time out, also on March 31, with Chloe Muir on the scoresheet.
