Celtic Women v Montrose Women

SWPL 1 | Excelsior Stadium
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Montrose Women face a challenge away against high-flying Celtic Women at the Excelsior Stadium on Sunday.

Montrose Women are 10th in the SWPL 1 table, and have picked up two wins and four draws in their 16 games to date.

Celtic, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 43 points, having won 14 and drawn one, and are three points behind table-toppers Rangers W.

With 81 goals in 16 games so far this season, Celtic are the league's highest scorers with 5.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in nine goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Montrose, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.2. They have conceded 3.9 goals per game.

In Amy Gallagher, the home side have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. She has notched 16 goals in 15 appearances, to sit second in the scoring charts.

The visitors' top scorers, with three goals in 16 games each, are Demi Taylor and Jade McLaren.

Celtic are in good form in SWPL 1, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Montrose's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Celtic's 13.

Celtic's last match was on December 17, a 1-1 draw against Hearts Women, with Caitlin Hayes getting the goal for Celtic.

Montrose drew 1-1 with Spartans Women last time out, also on December 17, with Taylor on the scoresheet.
