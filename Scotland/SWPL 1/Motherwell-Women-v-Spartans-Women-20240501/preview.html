Motherwell Women v Spartans Women

SWPL 1 | K Park Training Academy
Wednesday, 1 May 2024 @ 8.00pm

Struggling Spartans Women face Motherwell Women away at K Park Training Academy on Wednesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-0 victory over Hamilton Academical on April 21, the Spartans sit 10th in SWPL 1.

They travel to play a Motherwell side eighth in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Montrose Women.

Motherwell are in fantastic form in SWPL 1, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Spartans's form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' 16.

In the last 10 years, Motherwell and Spartans have played each other on 13 occasions. They won four each, and they drew five times.

On average, Motherwell scored 1.5 goals and the Spartans 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 17, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

With 36 goals in 27 games so far this season, the Spartans are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 65 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Motherwell are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.
