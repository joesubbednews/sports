Montrose Women v Motherwell Women

SWPL 1 | Links Park
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 2.00pm

Motherwell Women come to Links Park to play Montrose Women on early Sunday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

On average, Montrose scored 1.5 goals and Motherwell 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 21, when Motherwell won 2-1 at home.

Montrose's last match was on Tuesday, a 3-3 draw against Aberdeen, with Tammy-Lee Harkin (two) and Cassie Cowper getting the goals for Montrose.

Motherwell beat Spartans Women 4-2 last time out, on Wednesday, with Laura Berry (two), Kayla Jardine and Louisa Boyes on the scoresheet.
