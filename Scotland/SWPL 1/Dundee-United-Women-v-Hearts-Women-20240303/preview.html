Dundee United Women v Hearts Women

SWPL 1 | Gussie Park
Sunday, 3 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Relegation candidates Dundee United Women face a challenge against high-flying Hearts Women at Gussie Park on Sunday.

Dundee United Women are 11th in the SWPL 1 table, and have picked up three wins and one draw in their 21 games to date.

Hearts, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 39 points, having won 12 and drawn three, and are 18 points behind table-toppers Rangers Women.

Dundee United are in awful form in SWPL 1, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Hearts's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the hosts' zero.

With 19 goals in 21 games so far this season, Dundee United are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 83 goals at a rate of 4.0 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

With Charlotte Parker-Smith between the sticks, Hearts can rely on one of the league's safest pair of hands. She has kept six clean sheets in her 18 appearances this season in SWPL 1.

In Dundee United's net, Megan Sidey has one clean sheet in 10 games. She has conceded a goal every 21 minutes, four times as often as the 85 minutes between goals for Parker-Smith.

In the last three years, Dundee United and Hearts have played each other on three occasions. Hearts won all of them.

Their last meeting was on August 20, when Hearts won 3-0 at home.

Dundee United's last match was on February 18, a 4-3 loss against Aberdeen, with Morgan Steedman (two) and Rachel Todd getting the goals for Dundee United.

Hearts beat Montrose Women 5-0 last time out, also on February 18, with Olufolasade Adamolekun (two), Dannielle Collean McGinley, Emma Brownlie and Megan Bell on the scoresheet.
