Dundee United Women v Hamilton Academical

SWPL 1 | Gussie Park
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 4.00pm

Sunday's match at Gussie Park sees two relegation candidates play each other, as ninth-ranked Dundee United Women host bottom of the table Hamilton Academical.

Dundee United have picked up 10 points from their first 10 SWPL 1 games, with three wins and one draw.

That is seven points more than the Accies have collected, having won none and drawn three.

Dundee United are in bad form in SWPL 1, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

But with no wins and a draw over that period, Hamilton Academical's form is even worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the home team's three.

In the last 10 years, Dundee United and Hamilton Academical have played each other on seven occasions. Dundee United won three of them, Hamilton Academical one, and they drew three times.

On average, Dundee United scored 2.1 goals and the Accies 2.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 3, when Dundee United won 5-3 away.

With 10 goals in 17 games so far this season, the Accies are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 72 goals at a rate of 4.2 per game.

Dundee United are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.2. They have conceded 3.8 goals per game.

Dundee United's last match was on January 14, a 10-0 loss against Rangers Women.

Hamilton Academical lost 4-0 against Hibernian Women last time out, also on January 14.
