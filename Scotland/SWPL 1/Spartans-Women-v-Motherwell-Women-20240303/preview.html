Spartans Women v Motherwell Women

SWPL 1 | Ainslie Park Stadium
Sunday, 3 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Motherwell Women make the journey to the Ainslie Park Stadium to play Spartans Women on Sunday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Motherwell's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six SWPL 1 matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

In the last 10 years, Spartans and Motherwell have played each other on 11 occasions. Spartans won four of them, Motherwell three, and they drew four times.

On average, the Spartans scored 1.4 goals and Motherwell 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 20, when Motherwell won 1-0 at home.

Spartans are ninth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won three and drawn four, earning 13 points.

Motherwell are one place ahead of the Spartans in eighth, with five wins and one draw putting them on 16 points.

With 25 goals in 21 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.

Spartans's last match was on February 18, a 1-1 draw against Hamilton Academical, with Hannah Jordan getting the goal for the Spartans.

Motherwell lost 3-0 against Glasgow City Women last time out, also on February 18.
