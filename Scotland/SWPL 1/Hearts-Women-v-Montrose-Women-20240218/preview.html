Hearts Women v Montrose Women

SWPL 1 | Oriam National Performance Centre
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Montrose Women make the journey to Oriam National Performance Centre to play Hearts Women on Sunday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Montrose's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six SWPL 1 matches, while their opponents have earned 13 from a possible 18.

Montrose are 11th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won two and drawn four, earning 10 points.

Hearts are five places ahead of the away team in sixth, with 11 wins and three draws putting them on 36 points.

With 18 goals in 20 games so far this season, Montrose are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 81 goals at a rate of 4.0 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.1. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Hearts's last match was on February 11, a 4-0 win against Aberdeen, with Dannielle Collean McGinley (two), Jenna Penman and Lizzie Waldie getting the goals for Hearts.

Montrose lost 5-1 against Glasgow City Women last time out, also on Sunday, with Tammy-Lee Harkin on the scoresheet.
