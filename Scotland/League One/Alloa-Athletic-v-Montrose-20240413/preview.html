Alloa Athletic v Montrose

League One | Indodrill Stadium
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Alloa Athletic host Montrose in Saturday's match at the Indodrill Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in League One.

Alloa, who sit third in the league after 32 games, fell to a 1-5 away defeat to Stirling Albion on April 6.

They face a Montrose side who also lost their last match, a 7-1 defeat to Falkirk, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 55 goals in 32 games so far this season, Alloa are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Montrose are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have also conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Alloa and Montrose have played each other on 13 occasions. Alloa won six of them, Montrose three, and they drew four times.

On average, Alloa scored 1.9 goals and the Gable Endies 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 2, when Montrose won 4-3 at home.

In Bobby Wales, Athletic have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. He has notched 10 goals in 22 appearances, to sit fifth in the scoring charts.

The Gable Endies's top scorer, with 10 goals in 30 games, is Kane Hester.

The home side are in mixed form in League One, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
