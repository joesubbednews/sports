Stranraer v Spartans

League Two | Stair Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Stranraer host Spartans in Saturday's match at Stair Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in League Two.

Stranraer, who sit eighth in the league after 23 games, fell to a 0-2 home defeat to Clyde on February 10.

They face a Spartans side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Dumbarton, and who sit third in the table.

In Blair Henderson, Spartans have the league's most on-form striker so far this season. He has notched 13 goals in 23 appearances.

Stranraer's top scorers, with four goals each, are Thomas Orr and James Dolan.

With 36 goals in 23 games so far this season, the Spartans are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Stranraer are in mixed form in League Two, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Stranraer's seven.

Over the last two years, Stranraer and Spartans have played each other twice. Spartans won both of them.

Their last meeting was on December 23, when Spartans won 3-0 at home.
