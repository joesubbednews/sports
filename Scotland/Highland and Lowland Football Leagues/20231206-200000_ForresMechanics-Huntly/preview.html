Forres Mechanics v Huntly

Highland and Lowland Football Leagues | Mosset Park
Wednesday, 6 December 2023 @ 8.00pm

Forres Mechanics host Huntly in Wednesday's late match at Mosset Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Highland and Lowland Football Leagues.

Forres Mechanics, who sit zero in the league after 14 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Rothes on November 25.

They face a Huntly side who also lost their last match, a 3-1 defeat to Banks O' Dee, and who sit 12th in the table.

Forres Mechanics are in terrible form in Highland and Lowland Football Leagues, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Huntly's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' two.

With 40 goals in 14 games so far this season, the away side are scoring more than average in the league with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Forres Mechanics, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Forres Mechanics and Huntly have played each other on four occasions. Forres Mechanics won three of them and Huntly one.

On average, Forres Mechanics scored 3.5 goals and Huntly 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 4, when Huntly won 2-1 at home.
