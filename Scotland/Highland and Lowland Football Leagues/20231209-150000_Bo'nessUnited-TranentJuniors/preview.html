Bo'ness United v Tranent Juniors

Highland and Lowland Football Leagues | Newtown Park
Saturday, 9 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Tranent Juniors face Bo'ness United on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Highland and Lowland Football Leagues.

The Belters are unbeaten in eight, with six wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Bo'ness team who have won five and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 35 goals in 14 games so far this season, Tranent Juniors are scoring more than average in the league with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Bo'ness are also above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

United are sixth in the table after 16 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn one, earning 31 points.

The Belters are one place behind the hosts in seventh, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

Bo'ness's last match was on November 18, a 4-3 win against East Stirlingshire, with Finlay Malcolm, Lewis McArthur, Lucas Stenhouse and Thomas Grant getting the goals for Bo'ness.

Tranent Juniors beat Gala Fairydean Rovers 2-1 last time out, on November 11, with Alassan Jones and Cameron Ross on the scoresheet.
