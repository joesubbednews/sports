Strathspey Thistle v Wick Academy

Highland and Lowland Football Leagues | Seafield Park
Wednesday, 6 December 2023 @ 8.00pm

Wick Academy travel to Seafield Park for Wednesday's late match against Strathspey Thistle looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Highland and Lowland Football Leagues.

Wick Academy, who sit zero in the league after 13 games, fell to a 3-2 home defeat to Nairn County on November 25.

They face a Strathspey Thistle side who also lost their last match, a 4-1 defeat to Banks O' Dee, and who sit zero in the table.

Strathspey Thistle are in disappointing form in Highland and Lowland Football Leagues, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Wick Academy's form is slightly worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the home team's six.

With 18 goals in 15 games so far this season, Strathspey Thistle are the league's joint--6th-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 59 goals at a rate of 3.9 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 2.5 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Strathspey Thistle and Wick Academy have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on March 22, when Strathspey Thistle won 2-1 away.
