Formartine United v Brechin City

Highland and Lowland Football Leagues | North Lodge Park
Saturday, 9 December 2023 @ 3.00pm

Brechin City come to North Lodge Park to play Formartine United on Saturday in great form, having collected 15 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and lost one of their last six fixtures, while Formartine have three wins and a draw.

Brechin are fifth in the table after 13 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn one, earning 34 points.

Formartine are four places behind City in ninth, with nine wins and two draws putting them on 29 points.

With 32 goals in 13 games so far this season, the away team are scoring more than average in the league with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in six goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

United are also above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Formartine's last match was on November 18, a 2-1 loss against Banks O' Dee, with Mark Lawrence getting the goal for Formartine.

Brechin lost 1-0 against Fraserburgh last time out, on November 25.
