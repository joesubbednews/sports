Strathspey Thistle v Formartine United

Highland and Lowland Football Leagues | Seafield Park
Saturday, 16 December 2023 @ 2.00pm

Strathspey Thistle host Formartine United in Saturday's early match at Seafield Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Highland and Lowland Football Leagues.

Strathspey Thistle, who sit zero in the league after 16 games, fell to a 2-0 home defeat to Forres Mechanics on December 9.

They face a Formartine side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-3 victory against Brechin City, and who sit seventh in the table.

Strathspey Thistle are in bad form in Highland and Lowland Football Leagues, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Formartine's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the hosts' three.

With 37 goals in 15 games so far this season, United are scoring more than average in the league with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Strathspey Thistle, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 3.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Strathspey Thistle and Formartine have played each other on three occasions. Formartine won all of them.

Their last meeting was on August 5, when Formartine won 4-0 at home.
