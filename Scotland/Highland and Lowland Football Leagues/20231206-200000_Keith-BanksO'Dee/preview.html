Keith v Banks O' Dee

Highland and Lowland Football Leagues | Kynoch Park
Wednesday, 6 December 2023 @ 8.00pm

Keith host Banks O' Dee in Wednesday's late match at Kynoch Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Highland and Lowland Football Leagues.

Keith, who sit zero in the league after 12 games, fell to a 3-1 away defeat to Brechin City on November 22.

They face a Banks O' Dee side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-1 victory against Strathspey Thistle, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 42 goals in 16 games so far this season, Banks O' Dee are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Keith, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in Highland and Lowland Football Leagues, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Keith's four.
