Livingston Women v Kilmarnock Women

SWPL 2 | Tony Macaroni Arena
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 4.00pm

Two of SWPL 2's top sides face each other at the Tony Macaroni Arena in Sunday's kick-off, when third-placed Livingston Women host second-placed Kilmarnock Women.

Livingston have picked up eight wins and two draws from 14 games so far this season, and sit one point below the visitors going into the 4pm match.

Kilmarnock, meanwhile, have won eight and drawn three, picking up 27 points.

With 36 goals in 14 games so far this season, Kilmarnock are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Livingston are also above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in SWPL 2, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Livingston's 13.

Over the last two years, Livingston and Kilmarnock have played each other twice. Kilmarnock won both of them.

Their last meeting was on October 22, when Kilmarnock won 4-2 at home.
