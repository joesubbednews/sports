Gartcairn Women v Queen's Park Women

SWPL 2 | MTC Park
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Gartcairn Women host Queen's Park Women in Sunday's early match at MTC Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in SWPL 2.

Gartcairn, who sit eighth in the league after 14 games, fell to a 1-3 away defeat to Glasgow Women on January 14.

They face a Queen's Park side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Boroughmuir Thistle, and who sit first in the table.

With 47 goals in 14 games so far this season, Queen's Park are the league's highest scorers with 3.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Gartcairn, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 3.6 goals per game.

The home side are in bad form in SWPL 2, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Gartcairn's four.

In the last 10 years, Gartcairn and Queen's Park have played each other on six occasions. Gartcairn won two of them and Queen's Park four.

On average, Gartcairn scored 1.3 goals and Queen's Park 2.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 22, when Queen's Park won 4-0 at home.
