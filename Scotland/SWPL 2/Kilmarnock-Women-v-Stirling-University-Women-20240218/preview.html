Kilmarnock Women v Stirling University Women

SWPL 2 | The BBSP Stadium Rugby Park
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Stirling University Women face a challenge away against high-flying Kilmarnock Women at the BBSP Stadium Rugby Park on Sunday.

Stirling University Women are seventh in the SWPL 2 table, and have picked up three wins and three draws in their 16 games to date.

Kilmarnock, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 33 points, having won 10 and drawn three of their first 17 matches, and are 10 points behind table-toppers Queen's Park Women.

Kilmarnock are in mixed form in SWPL 2, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Stirling University's form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the home side's 10.

With 13 goals in 16 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

Kilmarnock, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Kilmarnock and Stirling University have played each other on 12 occasions. Kilmarnock won nine of them, Stirling University one, and they drew twice.

On average, Kilmarnock scored 2.9 goals and Stirling University 0.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when Kilmarnock won 4-0 away.

Kilmarnock's last match was on February 11, a 3-2 win against Glasgow Women, with getting the goals for Kilmarnock.

Stirling University lost 12-0 against Queen's Park Women last time out, also on February 11.
