Stirling University Women v Glasgow Women

SWPL 2 | Gannochy Sports Centre Stirling University
Sunday, 3 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Sunday's early match at Gannochy Sports Centre Stirling University sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table Stirling University Women host sixth-placed Glasgow Women.

Stirling University have picked up 12 points from their first 12 SWPL 2 games, with three wins and three draws.

That is six points less than Glasgow have collected, having won four and drawn six.

Stirling University are in terrible form in SWPL 2, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Glasgow's form is better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the hosts' one.

In the last 10 years, Stirling University and Glasgow have played each other on nine occasions. Stirling University won two of them, Glasgow five, and they drew twice.

On average, Stirling University scored 0.8 goals and Glasgow 2.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 17, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

With 13 goals in 17 games so far this season, Stirling University are the league's lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 3.6 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 2.0. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Stirling University's last match was on February 18, a 10-0 loss against Kilmarnock Women.

Glasgow drew 0-0 with Livingston Women last time out, also on February 18.
