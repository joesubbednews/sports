Livingston Women v Gartcairn Women

SWPL 2 | Tony Macaroni Arena
Sunday, 3 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Gartcairn Women face Livingston Women away at the Tony Macaroni Arena on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-1 victory over Boroughmuir Thistle on February 18, Gartcairn sit seventh in SWPL 2.

They travel to play a Livingston side third in the standings, who were held in their last match, 0-0 against Glasgow Women.

With 24 goals in 18 games so far this season, Gartcairn are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 3.2 per game.

Livingston are also below average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.0. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in SWPL 2, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Livingston's seven.

In the last five years, Livingston and Gartcairn have played each other on four occasions. They won two each.

On average, Livingston scored 2.5 goals and Gartcairn 2.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 10, when Livingston won 2-1 away.
