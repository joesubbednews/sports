St. Johnstone Women v Boroughmuir Thistle

SWPL 2 | Riverside Stadium
Sunday, 3 March 2024 @ 1.00pm

Boroughmuir Thistle travel to the Riverside Stadium for Sunday lunchtime's match against St. Johnstone Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in SWPL 2.

Boroughmuir Thistle, who sit fourth in the league after 18 games, fell to a 3-1 home defeat to Gartcairn Women on February 18.

They face a St. Johnstone side who also lost their last match, a 5-0 defeat to Queen's Park Women, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 32 goals in 17 games so far this season, St. Johnstone are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Boroughmuir Thistle, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, St. Johnstone and Boroughmuir Thistle have played each other on 12 occasions. St. Johnstone won two of them, Boroughmuir Thistle six, and they drew four times.

On average, St. Johnstone scored 1.4 goals and Boroughmuir Thistle 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 17, when Boroughmuir Thistle won 2-0 at home.

The hosts are in mixed form in SWPL 2, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to St. Johnstone's nine.
