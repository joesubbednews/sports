Gartcairn Women v St. Johnstone Women

SWPL 2 | MTC Park
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Gartcairn Women face St. Johnstone Women at MTC Park on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Stirling University Women on January 28, Gartcairn sit bottom of SWPL 2.

They travel to play a St. Johnstone side sixth in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-0 against Livingston Women.

In the last 10 years, Gartcairn and St. Johnstone have played each other on six occasions. Gartcairn won two of them and St. Johnstone four.

On average, Gartcairn scored 1.8 goals and St. Johnstone 2.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 13, when St. Johnstone won 4-1 at home.

With 19 goals in 16 games so far this season, Gartcairn are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 3.4 per game.

St. Johnstone, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in SWPL 2, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Gartcairn's 10.
