Gartcairn Women v Boroughmuir Thistle

SWPL 2 | MTC Park
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Gartcairn Women face Boroughmuir Thistle at MTC Park on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-2 victory over St. Johnstone Women on April 14, Gartcairn sit sixth in SWPL 2.

They travel to play a Boroughmuir Thistle side fourth in the standings, who were held in their last match, 3-3 against Livingston Women.

Gartcairn are in fantastic form in SWPL 2, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With no wins and three draws over that period, Boroughmuir Thistle's form is much worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the home side's 15.

In the last 10 years, Gartcairn and Boroughmuir Thistle have played each other on eight occasions. Gartcairn won two of them, Boroughmuir Thistle four, and they drew twice.

On average, Gartcairn scored 1.2 goals and Boroughmuir Thistle 3.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 18, when Gartcairn won 3-1 away.

With 35 goals in 23 games so far this season, Gartcairn are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 63 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.
