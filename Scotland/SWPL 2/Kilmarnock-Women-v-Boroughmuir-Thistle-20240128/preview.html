Kilmarnock Women v Boroughmuir Thistle

SWPL 2 | The BBSP Stadium Rugby Park
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Kilmarnock Women host Boroughmuir Thistle in Sunday's match at the BBSP Stadium Rugby Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in SWPL 2.

Kilmarnock, who sit third in the league after 15 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Livingston Women on January 21.

They face a Boroughmuir Thistle side who also lost their last match, a 2-0 defeat to Glasgow Women, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 36 goals in 15 games so far this season, Kilmarnock are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Boroughmuir Thistle are also above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Kilmarnock and Boroughmuir Thistle have played each other on 12 occasions. Kilmarnock won eight of them, Boroughmuir Thistle three, and they drew once.

On average, Kilmarnock scored 1.9 goals and Boroughmuir Thistle 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Boroughmuir Thistle won 2-1 at home.

The hosts are in mixed form in SWPL 2, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Kilmarnock's eight.
