Kilmarnock Women v St. Johnstone Women

SWPL 2 | The BBSP Stadium Rugby Park
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates St. Johnstone Women face a challenge away against high-flying Kilmarnock Women at the BBSP Stadium Rugby Park on Sunday.

St. Johnstone Women are sixth in the SWPL 2 table, and have picked up four wins and no draws in their 13 games to date.

Kilmarnock, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 24 points, having won seven and drawn three, and are 12 points behind table-toppers Queen's Park W.

With 33 goals in 13 games so far this season, Kilmarnock are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

St. Johnstone are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.4 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in SWPL 2, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to Kilmarnock's eight.

Over the last two years, Kilmarnock and St. Johnstone have played each other on nine occasions. Kilmarnock won two of them, St. Johnstone three, and they drew four times.

On average, Kilmarnock scored 1.7 goals and St. Johnstone 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 15, when St. Johnstone won 4-2 at home.

Kilmarnock's last match was on December 17, a 2-2 draw against Gartcairn Women, with getting the goals for Kilmarnock.

St. Johnstone lost 2-0 against Boroughmuir Thistle last time out, also on December 17.
