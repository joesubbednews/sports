Stirling University Women v Gartcairn Women

SWPL 2 | Gannochy Sports Centre Stirling University
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Gartcairn Women face Stirling University Women away at Gannochy Sports Centre Stirling University on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-2 victory over Queen's Park Women on January 21, Gartcairn sit bottom of SWPL 2.

They travel to play a Stirling University side seventh in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-1 against Livingston Women, on January 14.

In the last 10 years, Stirling University and Gartcairn have played each other on six occasions. Stirling University won three of them, Gartcairn two, and they drew once.

On average, Stirling University scored 1.2 goals and Gartcairn 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Stirling University won 1-0 away.

Stirling University are in bad form in SWPL 2, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Gartcairn's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With 12 goals in 14 games so far this season, Stirling University are the league's lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 3.5 goals per game.
