Stirling University Women v Livingston Women

SWPL 2 | Gannochy Sports Centre Stirling University
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Struggling Stirling University Women face Livingston Women at Gannochy Sports Centre Stirling University on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-0 victory over Glasgow Women on December 17, Stirling University sit seventh in SWPL 2.

They travel to play a Livingston side fourth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 1-0 against Queen's Park Women.

With 11 goals in 13 games so far this season, Stirling University are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

Livingston, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in SWPL 2, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Stirling University's four.
