St. Johnstone v Hibernian

Premiership | McDiarmid Park
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hibernian are strong favourites to take all three points despite St. Johnstone's home advantage in Saturday's match at McDiarmid Park.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.85 on Hibernian sealing the win, with the visitors sitting seventh in the Premiership table.

St. Johnstone, who are 10th in the league and eight points behind Hibs, are priced at 3.6 to win. A draw is set at 3.15.

In the last 10 years, St. Johnstone and Hibernian have played each other on 24 occasions. St. Johnstone won nine of them, Hibernian eight, and they drew seven times.

On average, the Saints scored 1.0 goals and Hibs 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 6, when St. Johnstone won 2-1 away.

Hibernian, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The Saints are in mixed form in the Premiership, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Hibs's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the home side's seven.

St. Johnstone's last match was on April 13, a 2-0 loss against Kilmarnock.

Hibernian drew 1-1 with Motherwell last time out, also on April 13, with Myziane Maolida on the scoresheet.
