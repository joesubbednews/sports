Premiership | McDiarmid Park

Aberdeen are strong favourites to take all three points despite St. Johnstone's home advantage in Wednesday's late match at McDiarmid Park.

Aberdeen beat Ross County 3-0 last time out, on January 2, with Jamie McGrath (two) and Miovski on the scoresheet.
