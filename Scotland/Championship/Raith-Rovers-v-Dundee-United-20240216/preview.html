Raith Rovers v Dundee United

Championship | Stark's Park
Friday, 16 February 2024 @ 7.45pm

Raith Rovers host Dundee United in Friday's late match at Stark's Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Championship.

Raith, who sit second in the league after 22 games, fell to a 3-2 home defeat to Inverness CT on January 27.

They face a Dundee side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against Ayr United, and who sit first in the table.

With 45 goals in 22 games so far this season, Dundee are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Raith are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.4. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In Lewis Vaughan, Rovers have the league's sharpest shooter so far this season. He has notched 11 goals in 22 appearances.

His goal rate of one every 138 minutes is quicker than that of Louis Moult, United's top scorer with a goal every 158 minutes, and a total of nine goals in 19 games.

In the last 10 years, Raith and Dundee have played each other on seven occasions. They won two each, and they drew three times.

On average, Raith scored 0.9 goals and the Terrors 1.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 16, when Raith won 1-0 away.

The home team are in disappointing form in the Championship, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the Terrors' form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Raith's five.
