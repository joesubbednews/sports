Edusport Academy v Hearts U21

Lowland Football League | Alliance Park
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Hearts U21 travel to Alliance Park for Saturday's match against Edusport Academy looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Lowland Football League.

Hearts, who sit third in the league after 24 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to Stirling University on February 4.

They face an Edusport Academy side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Tranent Juniors, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 68 goals in 24 games so far this season, Hearts U21 are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Edusport Academy, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The Braves are in mixed form in the Lowland Football League, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Hearts's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

In the last three years, Edusport Academy and Hearts U21 have played each other on three occasions. Edusport Academy won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 25, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
