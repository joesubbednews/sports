Cowdenbeath v Gretna 2008

Lowland Football League | Central Park
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Gretna 2008 travel to Central Park looking to secure a first win in 16 Lowland Football League games against Cowdenbeath on Saturday.

Gretna 2008 have lost 11 and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against BSC Glasgow on November 4.

They face a Cowdenbeath side who have won seven and drawn two over that time.

Gretna 2008 are 17th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won one and drawn five, earning eight points.

Cowdenbeath are six places ahead of the away team in 11th, with 11 wins and 10 draws putting them on 43 points.

With 20 goals in 31 games so far this season, Gretna 2008 are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 102 goals at a rate of 3.3 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, Cowdenbeath and Gretna 2008 have played each other on three occasions. Cowdenbeath won two of them and Gretna 2008 one.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Cowdenbeath won 3-2 away.

Cowdenbeath's last match was on April 6, a 4-2 loss against Cumbernauld Colts.

Gretna 2008 drew 0-0 with Gala Fairydean Rovers last time out, also on April 6.
