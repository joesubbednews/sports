Albion Rovers v East Kilbride

Lowland Football League | The Reigart Stadium
Tuesday, 2 April 2024 @ 7.45pm

East Kilbride face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Albion Rovers defence on Tuesday.

With 89 goals in 30 games, East Kilbride are the top scorers in the Lowland Football League ahead of the 7.45pm kick-off at the Reigart Stadium.

They face a Wee Rovers side who have scored 40 in 30 matches, but conceded only 27 goals, putting them top of the league's tightest defences.

East Kilbride are top of the table after 30 games, of which they have won 23 and drawn three, earning 72 points.

Albion are six places behind the Kilby in seventh, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on 47 points.

Rovers are in mixed form in the Lowland Football League, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the Wee Rovers' seven.

Albion's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 loss against Civil Service Strollers.

East Kilbride beat BSC Glasgow 6-1 last time out, on March 24.
