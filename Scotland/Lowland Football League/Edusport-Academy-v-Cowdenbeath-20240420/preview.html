Edusport Academy v Cowdenbeath

Lowland Football League | Alliance Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Edusport Academy host Cowdenbeath at Alliance Park on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Lowland Football League.

Edusport Academy have picked up 10 points from their last six games, and they face a Cowdenbeath side who also won their last match, and have collected nine points from the last possible 18.

With 44 goals in 33 games so far this season, Edusport Academy are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Cowdenbeath are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The Braves are 12th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn nine, earning 42 points.

The away side are two places ahead of the hosts in 10th, with 12 wins and 10 draws putting them on 46 points.

In the last three years, Edusport Academy and Cowdenbeath have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Edusport Academy's last match was on April 13, a 3-0 win against Gala Fairydean Rovers.

Cowdenbeath beat Gretna 2008 2-1 last time out, also on April 13.
