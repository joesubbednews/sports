Berwick Rangers v Edusport Academy

Lowland Football League | Shielfield Park
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Edusport Academy travel to Berwick Rangers on Saturday in the Lowland Football League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to East Stirlingshire 1-0 at home.

Berwick, meanwhile, won their last match, 4-1 against Cowdenbeath.

In the last 10 years, Berwick and Edusport Academy have played each other on nine occasions. Berwick won three of them, Edusport Academy four, and they drew twice.

On average, Berwick scored 1.1 goals and the Braves 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 6, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Edusport Academy are 14th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won eight and drawn nine, earning 33 points.

Berwick are three places ahead of the Braves in 11th, with 12 wins and five draws putting them on 41 points.

Rangers are in reasonable form in the Lowland Football League, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the home team's 12.

With 35 goals in 30 games so far this season, Edusport Academy are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Berwick are also below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.
