Berwick Rangers v Gretna 2008

Lowland Football League | Shielfield Park
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Gretna 2008 make the journey to Shielfield Park to play Berwick Rangers on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Gretna 2008's struggles have left them with just one point from their last six Lowland Football League matches, while their opponents have earned 12 from a possible 18.

Gretna 2008 are 17th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won one and drawn four, earning seven points.

Berwick are five places ahead of the away team in 12th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last 10 years, Berwick and Gretna 2008 have played each other on nine occasions. Berwick won six of them, Gretna 2008 one, and they drew twice.

On average, Berwick scored 1.4 goals and Gretna 2008 0.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Berwick won 1-0 away.

With 20 goals in 28 games so far this season, Gretna 2008 are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 96 goals at a rate of 3.4 per game.

Rangers are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Berwick's last match was on February 24, a 1-0 loss against Celtic B.

Gretna 2008 lost 4-1 against Cumbernauld Colts last time out, also on February 24.
