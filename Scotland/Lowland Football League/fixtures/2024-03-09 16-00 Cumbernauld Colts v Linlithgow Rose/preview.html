Cumbernauld Colts v Linlithgow Rose

Lowland Football League | Broadwood Stadium
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Lowland Football League's meanest defences go head-to-head at the Broadwood Stadium on Saturday, when Linlithgow Rose visit Cumbernauld Colts.

Only two sides – Albion Rovers and Tranent Juniors – have conceded fewer goals than Linlithgow Rose to date: the away side have let in just 31 goals in 27 games.

Colts have conceded 32 goals in 27 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Cumbernauld Colts are second in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn six, earning 54 points.

Linlithgow Rose are five places behind Colts in seventh, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on 44 points.

The hosts are in good form in the Lowland Football League, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Cumbernauld Colts's 13.

Cumbernauld Colts's last match was on February 24, a 4-1 win against Gretna 2008.

Linlithgow Rose lost 2-1 against Tranent Juniors last time out, on Tuesday.
