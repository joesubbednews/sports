Edinburgh University v Cowdenbeath

Lowland Football League | East Peffermill
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Edinburgh University welcome Cowdenbeath to East Peffermill on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their 13-game losing streak.

Edinburgh University's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Lowland Football League matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

Edinburgh University are bottom of the table after 27 games, of which they have won one and drawn one, earning four points.

Cowdenbeath are seven places ahead of the home side in 11th, with nine wins and 10 draws putting them on 37 points.

With 14 goals in 27 games so far this season, Edinburgh University are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 112 goals at a rate of 4.1 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have also conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, Edinburgh University and Cowdenbeath have played each other on three occasions. Cowdenbeath won all of them.

Their last meeting was on August 19, when Cowdenbeath won 7-1 at home.

Edinburgh University's last match was on February 24, a 3-1 loss against East Stirlingshire.

Cowdenbeath drew 2-2 with Stirling University last time out, on March 2.
