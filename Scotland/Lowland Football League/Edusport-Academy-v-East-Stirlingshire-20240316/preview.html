Edusport Academy v East Stirlingshire

Lowland Football League | Alliance Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Edusport Academy host East Stirlingshire on Saturday at Alliance Park in the Lowland Football League.

In their last league match, on March 9, Edusport Academy drew with Stirling University 0-0 at home.

East Stirlingshire lost, 2-0 at home against East Kilbride.

East Stirlingshire are 16th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won seven and drawn five, earning 26 points.

Edusport Academy are three places ahead of the Shire in 13th, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last 10 years, Edusport Academy and East Stirlingshire have played each other on 10 occasions. Edusport Academy won one of them, East Stirlingshire four, and they drew five times.

On average, the Braves scored 1.1 goals and the Shire 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 2, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The Braves are in disappointing form in the Lowland Football League, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.

With 35 goals in 29 games so far this season, Edusport Academy are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

East Stirlingshire are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.
