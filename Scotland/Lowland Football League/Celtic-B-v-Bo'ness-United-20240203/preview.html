Celtic B v Bo'ness United

Lowland Football League | Excelsior Stadium
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bo'ness United visit Celtic B at the Excelsior Stadium on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in Lowland Football League.

Bo'ness have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Celtic B side who drew their last match, and have collected 11 points from the last possible 18.

With 51 goals in 23 games so far this season, Celtic B are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Bo'ness are also above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

United are fourth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn three, earning 45 points.

The hosts are four places behind the away side in eighth, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

In the last five years, Celtic B and Bo'ness have played each other on five occasions. Celtic B won four of them and Bo'ness one.

On average, Celtic B scored 2.2 goals and Bo'ness 0.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 17, when Bo'ness won 1-0 at home.

Celtic B's last match was on January 27, a 2-2 draw against Linlithgow Rose.

Bo'ness beat Edinburgh University 3-1 last time out, also on January 27.
