Linlithgow Rose v Bo'ness United

Lowland Football League | Prestonfield
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Bo'ness United visit Linlithgow Rose at Prestonfield on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Lowland Football League.

Bo'ness have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Linlithgow Rose side who lost their last match, and have collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 60 goals in 30 games so far this season, Bo'ness are scoring more than average in the league with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Linlithgow Rose are also above average scorers, with 1.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

United are third in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn four, earning 58 points.

The hosts are five places behind the away side in eighth, with 12 wins and nine draws putting them on 45 points.

Linlithgow Rose's last match was on March 16, a 1-0 loss against Hearts U21.

Bo'ness beat East Kilbride 2-0 last time out, also on March 16.
