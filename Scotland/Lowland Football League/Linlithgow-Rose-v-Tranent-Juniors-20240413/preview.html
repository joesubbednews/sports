Linlithgow Rose v Tranent Juniors

Lowland Football League | Prestonfield
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

The Lowland Football League's two meanest defences go head-to-head at Prestonfield on Saturday, when Linlithgow Rose host Tranent Juniors.

Only one side – Albion Rovers – has conceded fewer goals than Linlithgow Rose to date: the home side have let in just 37 goals in 32 games.

The Belters have conceded the same number of goals in 31 games, giving them the joint-second tightest back line so far this season.

Linlithgow Rose are in bad form in the Lowland Football League, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Tranent Juniors's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

The Belters are fifth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn six, earning 57 points.

Linlithgow Rose are two places behind the away team in seventh, with 13 wins and nine draws putting them on 48 points.

In the last three years, Linlithgow Rose and Tranent Juniors have played each other on three occasions. Tranent Juniors won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on March 5, when Tranent Juniors won 2-1 at home.

Linlithgow Rose's last match was on April 6, a 2-1 win against Berwick Rangers.

Tranent Juniors drew 1-1 with Albion Rovers last time out, on Wednesday.
