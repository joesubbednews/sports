Albion Rovers v Edinburgh University

Lowland Football League | The Reigart Stadium
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Edinburgh University make the journey to the Reigart Stadium to play Albion Rovers on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their 15-game losing streak.

Edinburgh University's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Lowland Football League matches, while their opponents have earned 11 from a possible 18.

Edinburgh University are bottom of the table after 29 games, of which they have won one and drawn one, earning four points.

Albion are 11 places ahead of the visitors in seventh, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on 47 points.

With 14 goals in 29 games so far this season, Edinburgh University are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 115 goals at a rate of 4.0 per game.

Rovers are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Albion's last match was on Tuesday, a 1-0 win against Cumbernauld Colts.

Edinburgh University lost 1-0 against BSC Glasgow last time out, on March 16.
