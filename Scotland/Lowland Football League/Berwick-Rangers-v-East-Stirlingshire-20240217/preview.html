Berwick Rangers v East Stirlingshire

Lowland Football League | Shielfield Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Berwick Rangers host East Stirlingshire at Shielfield Park on Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in the Lowland Football League.

Berwick have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Shire side who lost their last match, and have collected four points from the last possible 18.

East Stirlingshire are 16th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won five and drawn five, earning 20 points.

Berwick are five places ahead of the Shire in 11th, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

In the last 10 years, Berwick and East Stirlingshire have played each other on 18 occasions. Berwick won 11 of them, East Stirlingshire four, and they drew three times.

On average, Berwick scored 2.2 goals and the Shire 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when Berwick won 2-1 away.

With 31 goals in 26 games so far this season, Rangers are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Berwick's last match was on February 10, a 2-0 win against Stirling University.

East Stirlingshire lost 1-0 against Gala Fairydean Rovers last time out, also on February 10.
