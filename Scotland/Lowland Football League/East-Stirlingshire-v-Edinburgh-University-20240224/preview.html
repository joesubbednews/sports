East Stirlingshire v Edinburgh University

Lowland Football League | Falkirk Community Stadium
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling East Stirlingshire face Edinburgh University at the Falkirk Community Stadium on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Berwick Rangers on February 17, the Shire sit 16th in the Lowland Football League.

They travel to play an Edinburgh University side 18th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 7-0 against East Kilbride.

East Stirlingshire are in mixed form in the Lowland Football League, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and six losses over that period, Edinburgh University's form is much worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to the Shire's seven.

In the last 10 years, East Stirlingshire and Edinburgh University have played each other on 13 occasions. East Stirlingshire won 10 of them, Edinburgh University one, and they drew twice.

On average, the Shire scored 2.3 goals and Edinburgh University 0.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when East Stirlingshire won 3-0 away.

With 13 goals in 26 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 109 goals at a rate of 4.2 per game.

The home team are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.
