Bo'ness United v Edusport Academy

Lowland Football League | Newtown Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Edusport Academy travel to Bo'ness United on Saturday in the Lowland Football League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Hearts U21 2-1 at home.

Bo'ness, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Cumbernauld Colts.

With 34 goals in 26 games so far this season, Edusport Academy are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Bo'ness, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Bo'ness and Edusport Academy have played each other on seven occasions. Bo'ness won five of them, Edusport Academy one, and they drew once.

On average, Bo'ness scored 1.9 goals and the Braves 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Bo'ness won 2-1 away.

United are fifth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn three, earning 45 points.

The Braves are seven places behind the home team in 12th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

Bo'ness are in mixed form in the Lowland Football League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Bo'ness's 10.
