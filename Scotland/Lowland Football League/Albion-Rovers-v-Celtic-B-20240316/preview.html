Albion Rovers v Celtic B

Lowland Football League | The Reigart Stadium
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Celtic B face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Albion Rovers defence on Saturday.

With 68 goals in 28 games, Celtic B are the third-highest scorers in the Lowland Football League ahead of the 3pm kick-off at the Reigart Stadium.

They face a Wee Rovers side who have scored 37 in 26 matches, but conceded only 23 goals, putting them top of the league's tightest defences.

Albion are in good form in the Lowland Football League, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Celtic B's form is better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Rovers' 13.

The visitors are sixth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn four, earning 49 points.

The Wee Rovers are two places behind Celtic B in eighth, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

Over the last two years, Albion and Celtic B have played each other twice. Celtic B won both of them.

Their last meeting was on September 2, when Celtic B won 2-1 at home.

Albion's last match was on March 8, a 2-1 loss against Hearts U21.

Celtic B beat Tranent Juniors 2-1 last time out, on March 9.
