Clachnacuddin v Inverurie Loco Works

Highland Football League | Grant Street Park
Wednesday, 10 April 2024 @ 8.00pm

Struggling Clachnacuddin face Inverurie Loco Works at Grant Street Park on Wednesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Banks O' Dee on March 30, Clachnacuddin sit 17th in the Highland Football League.

They travel to play an Inverurie Loco Works side 10th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Deveronvale, on Saturday.

In the last 10 years, Clachnacuddin and Inverurie Loco Works have played each other on 13 occasions. Clachnacuddin won six of them and Inverurie Loco Works seven.

On average, Clachnacuddin scored 1.4 goals and Inverurie Loco Works 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 23, when Clachnacuddin won 1-0 at home.

Clachnacuddin are in bad form in the Highland Football League, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Inverurie Loco Works's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With 41 goals in 31 games so far this season, Clachnacuddin are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 84 goals at a rate of 2.7 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.
