Wick Academy v Banks O' Dee

Highland Football League | Harmsworth Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Banks O' Dee travel to Wick Academy on Saturday in the Highland Football League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Brora Rangers 2-1 away.

Wick Academy, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-1 against Forres Mechanics.

With 85 goals in 33 games so far this season, Banks O' Dee are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Wick Academy, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn eight, earning 71 points.

The home side are eight places behind Banks O' Dee in 12th, with nine wins and five draws putting them on 32 points.

Wick Academy are in disappointing form in the Highland Football League, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Banks O' Dee's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Wick Academy's six.

In the last three years, Wick Academy and Banks O' Dee have played each other on three occasions. Banks O' Dee won all of them.

Their last meeting was on July 29, when Banks O' Dee won 4-0 at home.
