Deveronvale v Brora Rangers

Highland Football League | Princess Royal Park
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Brora Rangers face Deveronvale on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Highland Football League.

Brora are unbeaten in 11, with seven wins and four draws, ahead of the 2pm kick-off.

They face a Deveronvale team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

In the last 10 years, Deveronvale and Brora have played each other on 13 occasions. Brora won all of them.

On average, Deveronvale scored 0.6 goals and Brora 4.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 28 2023, when Brora won 6-0 away.

Deveronvale are 14th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won three and drawn six, earning 15 points.

Brora are five places ahead of the home team in ninth, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

With 25 goals in 21 games so far this season, Deveronvale are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 2.5 per game.

Rangers, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Deveronvale's last match was on Saturday, a 5-1 loss against Huntly.

Brora beat Keith 4-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
