Buckie Thistle v Keith

Highland Football League | Victoria Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Buckie Thistle host Keith at Victoria Park on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Highland Football League.

Buckie Thistle have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Keith side who have lost their last three matches, and collected seven points from the last possible 18.

With 97 goals in 33 games so far this season, Buckie Thistle are the league's highest scorers with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Keith, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home team are top of the table after 33 games, of which they have won 25 and drawn three, earning 78 points.

The visitors are 12 places behind Buckie Thistle in 13th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

In the last 10 years, Buckie Thistle and Keith have played each other on 12 occasions. Buckie Thistle won 10 of them and Keith two.

On average, Buckie Thistle scored 4.0 goals and Keith 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 8, when Buckie Thistle won 3-0 away.

Buckie Thistle's last match was on Wednesday, a 7-0 win against Strathspey Thistle.

Keith lost 1-0 against Brechin City last time out, on Tuesday.
