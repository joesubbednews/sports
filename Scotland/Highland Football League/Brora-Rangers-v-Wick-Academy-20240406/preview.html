Brora Rangers v Wick Academy

Highland Football League | Dudgeon Park
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wick Academy travel to Brora Rangers on Saturday in the Highland Football League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Brechin City 2-1 at home.

Brora, meanwhile, drew their last match, 3-3 against Deveronvale.

In the last 10 years, Brora and Wick Academy have played each other on 14 occasions. Brora won 11 of them and they drew three times.

On average, Brora scored 2.9 goals and Wick Academy 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 28, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

With 40 goals in 28 games so far this season, Wick Academy are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 62 goals at a rate of 2.2 per game.

Brora, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Rangers are sixth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn eight, earning 53 points.

The away team are seven places behind the hosts in 13th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Brora are in mixed form in the Highland Football League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Wick Academy's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Brora's 10.
