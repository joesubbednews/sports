Banks O' Dee v Brora Rangers

Highland Football League | Spain Park
Saturday, 24 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Highland Football League's meanest defences go head-to-head at Spain Park on Saturday, when Banks O' Dee host Brora Rangers.

Only one side – Brechin City – has conceded fewer goals than Banks O' Dee to date: the home side have let in just 20 goals in 23 games.

Brora have conceded 22 goals in 18 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Banks O' Dee are in exceptional form in the Highland Football League, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Brora's form is much worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 18.

Banks O' Dee are second in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn six, earning 54 points.

Rangers are seven places behind Banks O' Dee in ninth, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

Over the last two years, Banks O' Dee and Brora have played each other twice. They won one each.

Their last meeting was on March 25, when Banks O' Dee won 4-3 at home.

Banks O' Dee's last match was on February 14, a 4-0 win against Rothes.

Brora beat Lossiemouth 3-2 last time out, on Wednesday.
