Banks O' Dee v Rothes

Highland Football League | Spain Park
Wednesday, 14 February 2024 @ 8.00pm

Banks O' Dee host Rothes at Spain Park on late Wednesday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Highland Football League.

Banks O' Dee have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Rothes side who have lost their last two matches, and collected two points from the last possible 18.

With 62 goals in 22 games so far this season, Banks O' Dee are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Rothes, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are second in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn six, earning 51 points.

The visitors are eight places behind Banks O' Dee in 10th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last three years, Banks O' Dee and Rothes have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Banks O' Dee's last match was on Saturday, a 4-1 win against Deveronvale.

Rothes lost 3-1 against Buckie Thistle last time out, also on Saturday.
