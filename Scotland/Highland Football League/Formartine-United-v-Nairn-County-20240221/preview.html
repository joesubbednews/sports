Formartine United v Nairn County

Highland Football League | North Lodge Park
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 8.00pm

Formartine United face Nairn County on Wednesday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Highland Football League.

Formartine are unbeaten in seven, with five wins and two draws, ahead of the 8pm kick-off.

They face a Nairn team who have won four and lost three over the same number of games.

In the last 10 years, Formartine and Nairn have played each other on 12 occasions. Formartine won 11 of them and they drew once.

On average, Formartine scored 2.8 goals and Nairn 0.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 10 2022, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 53 goals in 21 games so far this season, Formartine are scoring more than average in the league with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Nairn are also above average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

United are fifth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

County are one place behind the hosts in sixth, with 14 wins and one draw putting them on 43 points.

Formartine's last match was on February 10, a 2-2 draw against Fraserburgh.

Nairn beat Deveronvale 4-2 last time out, on Saturday.
