Wick Academy v Keith

Highland Football League | Harmsworth Park
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wick Academy host Keith on Saturday at Harmsworth Park in the Highland Football League.

In their last league match, on Wednesday, Wick Academy lost to Formartine United 3-1 away.

Keith also lost, 3-0 at home against Buckie Thistle on Monday.

In the last 10 years, Wick Academy and Keith have played each other on 11 occasions. Wick Academy won seven of them, Keith two, and they drew twice.

On average, Wick Academy scored 1.9 goals and Keith 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 5 2023, when Keith won 2-0 at home.

Wick Academy are 13th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

Keith are one place ahead of the hosts in 12th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Wick Academy are in disappointing form in the Highland Football League, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Wick Academy's six.

With 33 goals in 30 games so far this season, Keith are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 53 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.
