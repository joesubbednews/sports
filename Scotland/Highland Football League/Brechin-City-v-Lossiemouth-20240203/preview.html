Brechin City v Lossiemouth

Highland Football League | Glebe Park
Saturday, 3 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brechin City host Lossiemouth at Glebe Park on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in Highland Football League.

Brechin have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Lossiemouth side who lost their last match, and have collected four points from the last possible 18.

Brechin are top of the table after 19 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn one, earning 49 points.

Lossiemouth are 11 places behind City in 12th, with five wins and three draws putting them on 18 points.

With 53 goals in 19 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 12 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last three years, Brechin and Lossiemouth have played each other on three occasions. Brechin won all of them.

Their last meeting was on January 24, when Brechin won 3-0 away.

Brechin's last match was on January 27, a 6-1 win against Strathspey Thistle.

Lossiemouth lost 5-1 against Nairn County last time out, on Wednesday.
