Rothes v Forres Mechanics

Highland Football League | MacKessack Park
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Forres Mechanics travel to MacKessack Park looking to secure a first win in 11 Highland Football League games against Rothes on Saturday.

Forres Mechanics have lost six and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Clachnacuddin on February 7.

They face a Rothes side who have won one and drawn three over that time.

In the last 10 years, Rothes and Forres Mechanics have played each other on 13 occasions. Rothes won four of them, Forres Mechanics six, and they drew three times.

On average, Rothes scored 1.7 goals and Forres Mechanics 2.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Rothes won 1-0 away.

Forres Mechanics are 15th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

Rothes are four places ahead of the visitors in 11th, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

With 31 goals in 30 games so far this season, Forres Mechanics are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The home team are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

Rothes's last match was on March 23, a 5-0 loss against Fraserburgh.

Forres Mechanics lost 4-0 against Formartine United last time out, on Saturday.
