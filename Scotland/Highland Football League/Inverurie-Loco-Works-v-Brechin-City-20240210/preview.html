Inverurie Loco Works v Brechin City

Highland Football League | Harlaw Park
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brechin City visit Inverurie Loco Works at Harlaw Park on Saturday on the back of six consecutive wins in the Highland Football League.

It means Brechin have picked up the maximum 18 points from their last six games, and they face an Inverurie Loco Works side who have also won their last two matches, and collected 10 points from the last possible 18.

Brechin are top of the table after 20 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn one, earning 52 points.

Inverurie Loco Works are 10 places behind City in 11th, with seven wins and two draws putting them on 23 points.

With 55 goals in 20 games so far this season, the away side are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 12 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.

In the last three years, Inverurie Loco Works and Brechin have played each other on three occasions. Brechin won all of them.

Their last meeting was on October 4, when Brechin won 2-1 at home.

Inverurie Loco Works's last match was on February 3, a 4-1 win against Rothes.

Brechin beat Lossiemouth 2-0 last time out, also on February 3.
