Huntly v Wick Academy

Highland Football League | Christie Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Wick Academy travel to Huntly on Saturday in the Highland Football League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Strathspey Thistle 6-1 away.

Huntly, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Formartine United.

With 76 goals in 28 games so far this season, Huntly are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Wick Academy, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Huntly and Wick Academy have played each other on 12 occasions. They won four each, and they drew four times.

On average, Huntly scored 1.8 goals and Wick Academy 2.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

The away side are 12th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won seven and drawn five, earning 26 points.

The hosts are four places ahead of Wick Academy in eighth, with 14 wins and four draws putting them on 46 points.

Huntly are in mixed form in the Highland Football League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, Wick Academy's form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
