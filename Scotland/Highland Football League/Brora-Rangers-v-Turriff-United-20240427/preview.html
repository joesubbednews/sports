Brora Rangers v Turriff United

Highland Football League | Dudgeon Park
Saturday, 27 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brora Rangers are on a poor run ahead of hosting Turriff United at Dudgeon Park on Saturday, with just four points collected from their last six games.
