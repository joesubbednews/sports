Brora Rangers v Buckie Thistle

Highland Football League | Dudgeon Park
Wednesday, 14 February 2024 @ 8.00pm

Two of the Highland Football League's meanest defences go head-to-head at Dudgeon Park on Wednesday, when Brora Rangers host Buckie Thistle.

Only one side – Brechin City – has conceded fewer goals than Brora to date: the home side have let in just 13 goals in 15 games.

Buckie Thistle have conceded 18 goals in 16 games, giving them the third tightest back line so far this season.

In the last 10 years, Brora and Buckie Thistle have played each other on 12 occasions. Brora won seven of them, Buckie Thistle three, and they drew twice.

On average, Brora scored 3.2 goals and Buckie Thistle 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 15, when Buckie Thistle won 2-0 away.

Brora are in good form in the Highland Football League, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

But with five wins and one loss over that period, Buckie Thistle's form is even better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Rangers's 14.

The away side are fifth in the table after 16 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn one, earning 40 points.

The hosts are four places behind Buckie Thistle in ninth, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

Brora's last match was on February 3, a 3-0 win against Deveronvale.

Buckie Thistle beat Rothes 3-1 last time out, on Saturday.
