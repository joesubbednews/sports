Brora Rangers v Lossiemouth

Highland Football League | Dudgeon Park
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 8.00pm

Brora Rangers host Lossiemouth on Wednesday at Dudgeon Park in the Highland Football League.

In their last league match, on Saturday, Brora drew with Turriff United 2-2 away.

Lossiemouth lost, 3-0 away at Wick Academy.

In the last 10 years, Brora and Lossiemouth have played each other on 13 occasions. Brora won 11 of them, Lossiemouth one, and they drew once.

On average, Brora scored 4.2 goals and Lossiemouth 0.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 19, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 26 goals in 24 games so far this season, Lossiemouth are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 48 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Brora, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Rangers are in reasonable form in the Highland Football League, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the home team's 11.

Lossiemouth are 12th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won six and drawn three, earning 21 points.

Brora are three places ahead of Lossiemouth in ninth, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.
