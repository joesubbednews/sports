Forres Mechanics v Wick Academy

Highland Football League | Mosset Park
Wednesday, 3 April 2024 @ 8.00pm

Forres Mechanics face Wick Academy at Mosset Park on late Wednesday looking to secure a first win in 11 Highland Football League games.

Forres Mechanics have lost six and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Clachnacuddin on February 7.

They face a Wick Academy side who have won five and drawn one over that time.

In the last 10 years, Forres Mechanics and Wick Academy have played each other on 13 occasions. Forres Mechanics won three of them, Wick Academy five, and they drew five times.

On average, Forres Mechanics scored 1.9 goals and Wick Academy 2.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 4, when Wick Academy won 3-2 at home.

Forres Mechanics are 15th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

Wick Academy are two places ahead of the hosts in 13th, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

With 31 goals in 30 games so far this season, Forres Mechanics are the league's lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Forres Mechanics's last match was on Saturday, a 4-0 loss against Formartine United.

Wick Academy lost 2-1 against Brechin City last time out, also on Saturday.
