Keith v Brechin City

Highland Football League | Kynoch Park
Wednesday, 3 April 2024 @ 8.00pm

Brechin City travel to Keith on late Wednesday in the Highland Football League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Wick Academy 2-1 away.

Keith also won their last match, 1-0 against Huntly.

Brechin are top of the table after 30 games, of which they have won 23 and drawn three, earning 72 points.

Keith are 11 places behind City in 12th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 78 goals in 30 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring more than average in the league with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Keith are in reasonable form in the Highland Football League, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Brechin's form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Keith's 11.

In the last three years, Keith and Brechin have played each other on three occasions. Brechin won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 22, when Brechin won 3-1 at home.
