Formartine United v Fraserburgh

Highland Football League | North Lodge Park
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Highland Football League's top sides face each other at North Lodge Park in Saturday's kick-off, when third-placed Formartine United host fourth-placed Fraserburgh.

Formartine have picked up 14 wins and three draws from 20 games so far this season, and sit one point above the visitors going into the 3pm match.

The Broch, meanwhile, have won 14 and drawn two of 21, picking up 44 points.

With 55 goals in 21 games so far this season, Fraserburgh are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Formartine are also above average scorers, with 2.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

United are in fantastic form in the Highland Football League, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the Broch's form is worse – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 16.

In the last 10 years, Formartine and Fraserburgh have played each other on 13 occasions. Formartine won six of them, Fraserburgh three, and they drew four times.

On average, Formartine scored 1.2 goals and the Broch 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 4, when Formartine won 2-1 away.

Formartine's last match was on February 3, a 2-1 win against Turriff United.

Fraserburgh beat Forres Mechanics 2-1 last time out, also on February 3.
