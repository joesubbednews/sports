Deveronvale v Inverurie Loco Works

Highland Football League | Princess Royal Park
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Deveronvale face Inverurie Loco Works at Princess Royal Park on Saturday looking to secure a first win in six Highland Football League games.

Deveronvale have lost two and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Turriff United on March 6.

They face an Inverurie Loco Works side who have won two and drawn two over that time.

Deveronvale are 16th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won four and drawn 10, earning 22 points.

Inverurie Loco Works are six places ahead of the hosts in 10th, with 11 wins and four draws putting them on 37 points.

In the last 10 years, Deveronvale and Inverurie Loco Works have played each other on 13 occasions. Deveronvale won one of them, Inverurie Loco Works 11, and they drew once.

On average, Deveronvale scored 0.8 goals and Inverurie Loco Works 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 25, when Inverurie Loco Works won 3-0 at home.

With 40 goals in 31 games so far this season, Deveronvale are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 80 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Deveronvale's last match was on Wednesday, a 3-3 draw against Brora Rangers.

Inverurie Loco Works lost 2-1 against Keith last time out, on March 23.
