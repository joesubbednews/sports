Brechin City v Buckie Thistle

Highland Football League | Glebe Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Highland Football League's meanest defences go head-to-head at Glebe Park on Saturday, when Brechin City host Buckie Thistle.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Brechin to date: the home side have let in just 12 goals in 21 games.

Buckie Thistle have conceded 19 goals in 17 games, giving them the third tightest back line so far this season.

Brechin are top of the table after 21 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn one, earning 55 points.

Buckie Thistle are four places behind City in fifth, with 14 wins and one draw putting them on 43 points.

The hosts are in exceptional form in the Highland Football League, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Brechin's 18.

In the last three years, Brechin and Buckie Thistle have played each other on three occasions. Brechin won two of them and Buckie Thistle one.

Their last meeting was on April 22, when Brechin won 2-0 away.

Brechin's last match was on February 10, a 2-0 win against Inverurie Loco Works.

Buckie Thistle beat Brora Rangers 5-1 last time out, on Wednesday.
