Forres Mechanics v Formartine United

Highland Football League | Mosset Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Formartine United face Forres Mechanics on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Highland Football League.

Formartine are unbeaten in seven, with five wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Forres Mechanics team who have won two and drawn one over the same number of games.

Formartine are third in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

Forres Mechanics are 10 places behind United in 13th, with five wins and four draws putting them on 19 points.

With 53 goals in 21 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring more than average in the league with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The home side, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Forres Mechanics and Formartine have played each other on 12 occasions. Forres Mechanics won one of them, Formartine seven, and they drew four times.

On average, Forres Mechanics scored 0.7 goals and Formartine 2.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Formartine won 2-0 at home.

Forres Mechanics's last match was on February 7, a 4-1 win against Clachnacuddin.

Formartine drew 2-2 with Fraserburgh last time out, on February 10.
