Brora Rangers v Brechin City

Highland Football League | Dudgeon Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brora Rangers welcome Brechin City to Dudgeon Park on Saturday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Brora's struggles have left them with just four points from their last six Highland Football League matches, while their opponents have earned 12 from a possible 18.

With 85 goals in 33 games so far this season, Brechin are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Brora are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

City are second in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 25 and drawn three, earning 78 points.

Rangers are five places behind the away side in seventh, with 15 wins and eight draws putting them on 53 points.

In the last three years, Brora and Brechin have played each other on three occasions. Brechin won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when Brechin won 2-1 at home.

Brora's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-0 loss against Fraserburgh.

Brechin beat Keith 1-0 last time out, on Tuesday.
