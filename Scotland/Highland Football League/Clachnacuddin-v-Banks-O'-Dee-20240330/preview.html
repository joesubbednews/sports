Clachnacuddin v Banks O' Dee

Highland Football League | Grant Street Park
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Relegation candidates Clachnacuddin face a challenge against high-flying Banks O' Dee at Grant Street Park on Saturday.

Clachnacuddin are 17th in the Highland Football League table, and have picked up just three wins and seven draws in their 30 games to date.

Banks O' Dee, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 65 points, having won 19 and drawn eight, and are four points behind table-toppers Brechin City.

With 81 goals in 30 games so far this season, Banks O' Dee are the league's highest scorers with 2.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Clachnacuddin, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Highland Football League, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Clachnacuddin's four.

In the last three years, Clachnacuddin and Banks O' Dee have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Clachnacuddin's last match was on March 23, a 2-1 loss against Strathspey Thistle.

Banks O' Dee beat Formartine United 4-1 last time out, also on March 23.
