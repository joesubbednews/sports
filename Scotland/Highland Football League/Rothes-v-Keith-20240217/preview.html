Rothes v Keith

Highland Football League | MacKessack Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Keith face Rothes away at MacKessack Park on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Turriff United on Wednesday, Keith sit 16th in the Highland Football League.

They travel to play a Rothes side 10th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 4-0 against Banks O' Dee.

Rothes are in terrible form in the Highland Football League, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Keith's form is slightly better – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the home side's two.

In the last 10 years, Rothes and Keith have played each other on 12 occasions. Rothes won seven of them and Keith five.

On average, Rothes scored 2.3 goals and Keith 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 22 2022, when Rothes won 3-0 at home.

With 31 goals in 26 games so far this season, Rothes are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.
