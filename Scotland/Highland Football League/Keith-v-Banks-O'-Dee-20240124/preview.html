Keith v Banks O' Dee

Highland Football League | Kynoch Park
Wednesday, 24 January 2024 @ 8.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Keith face a challenge against high-flying Banks O' Dee at Kynoch Park on Wednesday.

Keith are 17th in the Highland Football League table, and have picked up three wins and two draws in their 16 games to date.

Banks O' Dee, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 42 points, having won 12 and drawn six of their first 19 matches, and are one point behind table-toppers Brechin City.

With 53 goals in 19 games so far this season, Banks O' Dee are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Keith, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in Highland Football League, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Keith's three.

Over the last two years, Keith and Banks O' Dee have played each other twice. Banks O' Dee won both of them.

Their last meeting was on April 22, when Banks O' Dee won 3-2 away.

Keith's last match was on January 13, a 6-2 loss against Formartine United.

Banks O' Dee beat Inverurie Loco Works 3-0 last time out, on Saturday.
