Fraserburgh v Brora Rangers

Highland Football League | Bellslea Park
Wednesday, 17 April 2024 @ 8.00pm

Fraserburgh host Brora Rangers at Bellslea Park on late Wednesday on the back of eight consecutive wins in the Highland Football League.

It means Fraserburgh have picked up the maximum 24 points from their last eight games, and they face a Brora side who have lost their last two matches, and collected 10 points from the last possible 24.

With 89 goals in 32 games so far this season, Fraserburgh are the league's highest scorers with 2.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Brora are also above average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The Broch are second in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 23 and drawn three, earning 72 points.

Rangers are five places behind the hosts in seventh, with 15 wins and eight draws putting them on 53 points.

In the last 10 years, Fraserburgh and Brora have played each other on 13 occasions. They won five each, and they drew three times.

On average, the Broch scored 1.3 goals and Brora 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 2, when Fraserburgh won 2-1 away.

Fraserburgh's last match was on Saturday, a 5-1 win against Strathspey Thistle.

Brora lost 2-1 against Banks O' Dee last time out, also on Saturday.
