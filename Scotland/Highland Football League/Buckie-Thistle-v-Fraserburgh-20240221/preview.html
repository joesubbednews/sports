Buckie Thistle v Fraserburgh

Highland Football League | Victoria Park
Wednesday, 21 February 2024 @ 8.00pm

Two of the Highland Football League's top sides face each other at Victoria Park in Wednesday's late kick-off, when fourth-placed Buckie Thistle host third-placed Fraserburgh.

Buckie Thistle have picked up 15 wins and one draw from 18 games so far this season, and sit two points below the visitors going into the 8pm match.

The Broch, meanwhile, have won 15 and drawn three of 23, picking up 48 points.

With 58 goals in 18 games so far this season, Buckie Thistle are scoring more than average in the league with 3.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Fraserburgh are also above average scorers, with 2.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Buckie Thistle and Fraserburgh have played each other on 13 occasions. Buckie Thistle won five of them, Fraserburgh three, and they drew five times.

On average, Buckie Thistle scored 2.2 goals and the Broch 1.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 12, when Buckie Thistle won 3-1 away.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Highland Football League, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the Broch's form is worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Buckie Thistle's 15.

Buckie Thistle's last match was on Saturday, a 3-2 win against Brechin City.

Fraserburgh beat Huntly 6-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
