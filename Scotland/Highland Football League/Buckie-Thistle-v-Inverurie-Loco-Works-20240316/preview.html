Buckie Thistle v Inverurie Loco Works

Highland Football League | Victoria Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Inverurie Loco Works face Buckie Thistle on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Highland Football League.

Inverurie Loco Works are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Buckie Thistle team who have won three and lost two over the same number of games.

With 67 goals in 23 games so far this season, Buckie Thistle are scoring more than average in the league with 2.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Inverurie Loco Works, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Buckie Thistle and Inverurie Loco Works have played each other on 13 occasions. Buckie Thistle won six of them, Inverurie Loco Works five, and they drew twice.

On average, Buckie Thistle scored 1.4 goals and Inverurie Loco Works 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Buckie Thistle won 2-0 away.

The home side are fourth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 18 and drawn one, earning 55 points.

The visitors are six places behind Buckie Thistle in 10th, with 11 wins and three draws putting them on 36 points.

Buckie Thistle's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-1 win against Banks O' Dee.

Inverurie Loco Works beat Wick Academy 3-1 last time out, on March 9.
