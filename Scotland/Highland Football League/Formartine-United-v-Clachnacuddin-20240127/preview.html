Formartine United v Clachnacuddin

Highland Football League | North Lodge Park
Saturday, 27 January 2024 @ 2.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Clachnacuddin face a challenge away against high-flying Formartine United at North Lodge Park on Saturday.

Clachnacuddin are 16th in the Highland Football League table, and have picked up just two wins and six draws in their 21 games to date.

Formartine, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 39 points, having won 12 and drawn three of their first 18 matches, and are seven points behind table-toppers Brechin City.

In the last 10 years, Formartine and Clachnacuddin have played each other on 13 occasions. Formartine won 11 of them, Clachnacuddin one, and they drew once.

On average, Formartine scored 3.3 goals and Clachnacuddin 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 16, when Formartine won 5-0 away.

With 46 goals in 18 games so far this season, Formartine are scoring more than average in the league with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Clachnacuddin, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 3.0 goals per game.

United are in reasonable form in Highland Football League, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' 11.

Formartine's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-2 draw against Rothes.

Clachnacuddin drew 2-2 with Fraserburgh last time out, on January 13.
