Fraserburgh v Rothes

Highland Football League | Bellslea Park
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Fraserburgh host Rothes at Bellslea Park on Saturday on the back of five consecutive wins in the Highland Football League.

Fraserburgh have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Rothes side who lost their last match, and have collected five points from the last possible 18.

With 75 goals in 29 games so far this season, Fraserburgh are the league's third-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Rothes, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The Broch are second in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn three, earning 63 points.

The visitors are nine places behind the home team in 11th, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

In the last 10 years, Fraserburgh and Rothes have played each other on 12 occasions. Fraserburgh won all of them.

On average, the Broch scored 4.0 goals and Rothes 0.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Fraserburgh won 3-0 away.

Fraserburgh's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-1 win against Forres Mechanics.

Rothes lost 3-1 against Turriff United last time out, on March 16.
