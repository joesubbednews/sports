Deveronvale v Formartine United

Highland Football League | Princess Royal Park
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Formartine United face Deveronvale on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Highland Football League.

Formartine are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Deveronvale team who have won one and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 84 goals in 33 games so far this season, Formartine are scoring more than average in the league with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 43 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Deveronvale, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.

The home team are 16th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won five and drawn 10, earning 25 points.

United are 11 places ahead of Deveronvale in fifth, with 21 wins and six draws putting them on 69 points.

In the last 10 years, Deveronvale and Formartine have played each other on 13 occasions. Deveronvale won one of them, Formartine nine, and they drew three times.

On average, Deveronvale scored 1.3 goals and Formartine 3.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 9, when Formartine won 5-0 at home.

Deveronvale's last match was on April 13, a 6-1 loss against Buckie Thistle.

Formartine drew 1-1 with Inverurie Loco Works last time out, on Wednesday.
