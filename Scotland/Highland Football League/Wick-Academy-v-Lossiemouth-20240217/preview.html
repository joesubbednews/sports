Wick Academy v Lossiemouth

Highland Football League | Harmsworth Park
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Lossiemouth travel to Wick Academy on Saturday in the Highland Football League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Inverurie Loco Works 1-0 at home.

Wick Academy, meanwhile, won their last match, 3-2 against Strathspey Thistle.

In the last 10 years, Wick Academy and Lossiemouth have played each other on 13 occasions. Wick Academy won nine of them, Lossiemouth three, and they drew once.

On average, Wick Academy scored 2.4 goals and Lossiemouth 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Lossiemouth won 1-0 at home.

Wick Academy are 14th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won four and drawn four, earning 16 points.

Lossiemouth are two places ahead of the hosts in 12th, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

Wick Academy are in disappointing form in the Highland Football League, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Wick Academy's five.

With 21 goals in 19 games so far this season, the home side are the league's lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 49 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

Lossiemouth are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.
