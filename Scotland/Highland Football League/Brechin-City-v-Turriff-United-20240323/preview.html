Brechin City v Turriff United

Highland Football League | Glebe Park
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brechin City face Turriff United on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Highland Football League.

Brechin are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Turriff team who have won just one and drawn one over the same number of games.

Brechin are top of the table after 27 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn three, earning 66 points.

Turriff are eight places behind City in ninth, with 14 wins and one draw putting them on 43 points.

With 70 goals in 27 games so far this season, the home side are scoring more than average in the league with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

United are also above average scorers, with 2.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last three years, Brechin and Turriff have played each other on three occasions. Brechin won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Brechin won 3-0 away.

Brechin's last match was on March 16, a 2-0 win against Clachnacuddin.

Turriff beat Rothes 3-1 last time out, also on March 16.
