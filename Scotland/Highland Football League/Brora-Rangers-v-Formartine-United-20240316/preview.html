Brora Rangers v Formartine United

Highland Football League | Dudgeon Park
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brora Rangers host Formartine United at Dudgeon Park on Saturday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Highland Football League.

Brora have picked up 11 points from their last six games, and they face a Formartine side who also won their last match, and have collected the same number of points from the last possible 18.

With 63 goals in 25 games so far this season, Formartine are scoring more than average in the league with 2.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Brora are also above average scorers, with 2.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Brora and Formartine have played each other on 11 occasions. They won four each, and they drew three times.

On average, Brora scored 2.4 goals and Formartine 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when they played out a 3-3 draw.

United are fifth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn five, earning 53 points.

Rangers are one place behind the visitors in sixth, with 14 wins and seven draws putting them on 49 points.

Brora's last match was on Wednesday, a 2-1 win against Nairn County.

Formartine beat Huntly 2-1 last time out, on March 9.
