Turriff United v Brora Rangers

Highland Football League | The Haughs
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Turriff United host Brora Rangers on Saturday at the Haughs in the Highland Football League.

In their last league match, on Wednesday, Turriff lost to Keith 1-0 away.

Brora also lost, 5-1 at home against Buckie Thistle.

With 56 goals in 23 games so far this season, Turriff are scoring more than average in the league with 2.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Brora, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.9 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Turriff and Brora have played each other on 12 occasions. Brora won 11 of them and they drew once.

On average, Turriff scored 0.6 goals and Brora 3.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 4, when Brora won 4-0 away.

United are in reasonable form in the Highland Football League, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Rangers's form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the hosts' 12.

Turriff are eighth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 13 and lost 10, earning 39 points.

The away side are one place behind Turriff in ninth, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.
