Strathspey Thistle v Brora Rangers

Highland Football League | Seafield Park
Wednesday, 7 February 2024 @ 8.00pm

Strathspey Thistle and Brora Rangers come into Wednesday's late kick-off at Seafield Park in contrasting spells, with the away team hitting a rich vein of form while the hosts struggle.

Strathspey Thistle have drawn just one of the last six, while Brora have taken four wins and two draws.

Strathspey Thistle are bottom of the table after 20 games, of which they have won two and drawn one, earning seven points.

Brora are nine places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

In the last 10 years, Strathspey Thistle and Brora have played each other on 12 occasions. Brora won all of them.

On average, Strathspey Thistle scored 0.5 goals and Brora 5.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 16, when Brora won 3-1 at home.

With 23 goals in 20 games so far this season, Strathspey Thistle are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 73 goals at a rate of 3.6 per game.

Rangers, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Strathspey Thistle's last match was on Saturday, a 3-2 loss against Wick Academy.

Brora beat Deveronvale 3-0 last time out, also on Saturday.
