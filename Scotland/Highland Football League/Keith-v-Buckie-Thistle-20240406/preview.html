Keith v Buckie Thistle

Highland Football League | Kynoch Park
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Buckie Thistle travel to Keith on Saturday in the Highland Football League.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Deveronvale 0-0 away.

Keith, meanwhile, won their last match, 1-0 against Huntly.

With 73 goals in 28 games so far this season, Buckie Thistle are scoring more than average in the league with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Keith, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Keith and Buckie Thistle have played each other on 11 occasions. Keith won two of them and Buckie Thistle nine.

On average, Keith scored 1.2 goals and Buckie Thistle 4.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 11 2023, when Buckie Thistle won 3-0 at home.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 20 and drawn three, earning 63 points.

The home side are eight places behind Buckie Thistle in 12th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Keith are in reasonable form in the Highland Football League, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with three wins and two draws over that period, Buckie Thistle's form is identical – they have both taken 11 points from 18.
