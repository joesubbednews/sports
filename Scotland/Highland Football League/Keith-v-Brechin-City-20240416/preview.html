Keith v Brechin City

Highland Football League | Kynoch Park
Tuesday, 16 April 2024 @ 8.00pm

Brechin City are strong favourites to take all three points despite Keith's home advantage in Tuesday's late match at Kynoch Park.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.35 on Brechin sealing the win, with the visitors sitting first in the Highland Football League table.

Keith, who are 13th in the league and 45 points behind Brechin, are priced at 6.5 to win. A draw is set at 4.33.

With 84 goals in 32 games so far this season, Brechin are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Keith, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Highland Football League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, City's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Keith's 10.

In the last three years, Keith and Brechin have played each other on three occasions. Brechin won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 22, when Brechin won 3-1 at home.

Keith's last match was on Saturday, a 2-0 loss against Wick Academy.

Brechin beat Forres Mechanics 4-1 last time out, also on Saturday.
