Wick Academy v Brechin City

Highland Football League | Harmsworth Park
Saturday, 20 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Wick Academy face a challenge against high-flying Brechin City at Harmsworth Park on Saturday.

Wick Academy are 15th in the Highland Football League table, and have picked up three wins and four draws in their 17 games to date.

Brechin, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 43 points, having won 14 and drawn one.

Wick Academy are in terrible form in Highland Football League, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Brechin's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to the hosts' two.

With 18 goals in 17 games so far this season, Wick Academy are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 2.6 per game.

City, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.9. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Wick Academy and Brechin have played each other twice. Brechin won both of them.

Their last meeting was on March 25, when Brechin won 10-0 at home.

Wick Academy's last match was on January 13, a 3-1 loss against Turriff United.

Brechin beat Rothes 3-1 last time out, also on January 13.
