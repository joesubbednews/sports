Fraserburgh v Banks O' Dee

Highland Football League | Bellslea Park
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Highland Football League's top sides face each other at Bellslea Park in Saturday's kick-off, when third-placed Fraserburgh host table-topping Banks O' Dee.

Banks O' Dee, meanwhile, have won 17 and drawn eight, picking up 59 points.
