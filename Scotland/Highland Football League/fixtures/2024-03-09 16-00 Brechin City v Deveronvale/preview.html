Brechin City v Deveronvale

Highland Football League | Glebe Park
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Brechin City host Deveronvale in Saturday's match at Glebe Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Highland Football League.

They face a Deveronvale side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-1 victory against Turriff United, and who sit 16th in the table.
