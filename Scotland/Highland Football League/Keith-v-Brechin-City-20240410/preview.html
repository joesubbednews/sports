Keith v Brechin City

Highland Football League | Kynoch Park
Wednesday, 10 April 2024 @ 8.00pm

Brechin City travel to Kynoch Park for Wednesday's late match against Keith looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Highland Football League.

Brechin, who sit top of the league after 31 games, fell to a 4-2 away defeat to Fraserburgh on Saturday.

They face a Keith side who also lost their last match, a 3-0 defeat to Buckie Thistle, and who sit 12th in the table.

With 80 goals in 31 games so far this season, Brechin are scoring more than average in the league with 2.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Keith, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Keith and Brechin have played each other on three occasions. Brechin won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 22, when Brechin won 3-1 at home.

The home side are in mixed form in the Highland Football League, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, City's form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
