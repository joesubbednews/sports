Rossvale Women v East Fife Women

Women's Championship | Petershill Park
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Two of the Women's Championship's top sides face each other at Petershill Park in Sunday lunchtime's kick-off, when table-topping Rossvale Women host third-placed East Fife Women.

Rossvale have picked up 15 wins from 16 games so far this season, and sit 12 points above the visitors going into the 12pm match.

East Fife, meanwhile, have won 11, picking up 33 points.

With 78 goals in 16 games so far this season, Rossvale are the league's highest scorers with 4.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

East Fife are also above average scorers, with 3.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in exceptional form in the Women's Championship, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Rossvale's 18.

In the last three years, Rossvale and East Fife have played each other on three occasions. Rossvale won one of them and East Fife the other.

Their last meeting was on January 21, when East Fife won 3-1 away.
