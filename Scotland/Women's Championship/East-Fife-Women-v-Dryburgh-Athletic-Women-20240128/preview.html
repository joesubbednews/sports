East Fife Women v Dryburgh Athletic Women

Women's Championship | MGM Timber Bayview Stadium
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

East Fife Women host Dryburgh Athletic Women in Sunday's match at the MGM Timber Bayview Stadium looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Women's Championship.

East Fife, who sit fourth in the league after 10 games, fell to a 1-3 away defeat to Rossvale Women on January 21.

They face a Dryburgh Athletic side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-2 victory against Renfrew Women, and who sit seventh in the table.

With 20 goals in nine games so far this season, Dryburgh Athletic are scoring at below the league average rate with 2.2 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

East Fife are also below average scorers, with 2.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in Women's Championship, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to East Fife's 15.

In the last five years, East Fife and Dryburgh Athletic have played each other on four occasions. East Fife won two of them, Dryburgh Athletic one, and they drew once.

On average, East Fife scored 1.5 goals and Dryburgh Athletic 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 3, when East Fife won 1-0 away.
