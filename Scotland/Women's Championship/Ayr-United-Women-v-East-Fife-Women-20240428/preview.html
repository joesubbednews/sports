Ayr United Women v East Fife Women

Women's Championship | Queen Margaret Academy
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Ayr United Women host East Fife Women in Sunday lunchtime's match at Queen Margaret Academy looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Ayr United, who sit second in the league after 18 games, fell to a 1-2 home defeat to Rossvale Women on April 14.

They face an East Fife side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against Inverness CT Women, and who sit third in the table.

With 65 goals in 18 games so far this season, East Fife are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Ayr United are also above average scorers, with 3.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Women's Championship, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

But with five wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is even better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Ayr United's 15.

Over the last two years, Ayr United and East Fife have played each other twice. Ayr United won both of them.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when Ayr United won 2-1 away.
