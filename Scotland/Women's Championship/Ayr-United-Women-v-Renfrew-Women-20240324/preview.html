Ayr United Women v Renfrew Women

Women's Championship | Queen Margaret Academy
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.30pm

Two of the Women's Championship's top sides face each other at Queen Margaret Academy in Sunday's early kick-off, when second-placed Ayr United Women host fourth-placed Renfrew Women.

Ayr United have picked up 11 wins and one draw from 15 games so far this season, and sit 10 points above the visitors going into the 2.30pm match.

Renfrew, meanwhile, have won seven and drawn three, picking up 24 points.

With 54 goals in 15 games so far this season, Ayr United are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Renfrew are also above average scorers, with 3.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Ayr United and Renfrew have played each other on eight occasions. They won three each, and they drew twice.

On average, Ayr United scored 1.6 goals and Renfrew 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The home team are in fantastic form in the Women's Championship, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Ayr United's 15.
