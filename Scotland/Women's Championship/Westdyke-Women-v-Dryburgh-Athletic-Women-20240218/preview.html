Westdyke Women v Dryburgh Athletic Women

Women's Championship | Lawsondale Playing Fields
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Dryburgh Athletic Women face Westdyke Women away at Lawsondale Playing Fields on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Inverness CT Women on February 11, Dryburgh Athletic sit seventh in the Women's Championship.

They travel to play a Westdyke side fifth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 9-1 against Rossvale Women.

With 23 goals in 11 games so far this season, Dryburgh Athletic are scoring at below the league average rate with 2.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Westdyke, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 3.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 3.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Women's Championship, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Westdyke's six.

In the last five years, Westdyke and Dryburgh Athletic have played each other on four occasions. Westdyke won one of them and Dryburgh Athletic the other.

On average, Westdyke scored 0.8 goals and Dryburgh Athletic 4.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 1, when Westdyke won 2-1 away.
