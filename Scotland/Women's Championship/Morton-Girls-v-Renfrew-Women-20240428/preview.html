Morton Girls v Renfrew Women

Women's Championship | Inverclyde Academy
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Renfrew Women travel to Inverclyde Academy for Sunday lunchtime's match against Morton Girls looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Renfrew, who sit zero in the league after 17 games, fell to a 3-0 home defeat to Dryburgh Athletic Women on April 7.

They face a Morton Girls side who also lost their last match, a 3-0 defeat to Westdyke Women, and who sit zero in the table.

Morton Girls are in terrible form in the Women's Championship, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Renfrew's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' one.

With 20 goals in 17 games so far this season, Morton Girls are the league's-3th-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 69 goals at a rate of 4.1 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 2.8 goals per game. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Morton Girls and Renfrew have played each other on eight occasions. Morton Girls won one of them and Renfrew the other.

On average, Morton Girls scored 0.9 goals and Renfrew 3.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 17, when Renfrew won 2-1 at home.
