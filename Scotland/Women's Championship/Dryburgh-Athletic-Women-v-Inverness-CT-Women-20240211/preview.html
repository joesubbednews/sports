Dryburgh Athletic Women v Inverness CT Women

Women's Championship | Thomson Park
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Inverness CT Women travel to Thomson Park for Sunday's match against Dryburgh Athletic Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Inverness CT, who sit third in the league after 10 games, fell to a 3-1 home defeat to Rossvale Women on January 28.

They face a Dryburgh Athletic side who also lost their last match, a 2-1 defeat to East Fife Women, and who sit seventh in the table.

With 39 goals in 10 games so far this season, Inverness CT are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Dryburgh Athletic, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Dryburgh Athletic and Inverness CT have played each other on nine occasions. Dryburgh Athletic won two of them, Inverness CT six, and they drew once.

On average, Dryburgh Athletic scored 2.0 goals and Inverness CT 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when Inverness CT won 2-1 at home.

The home side are in mixed form in the Women's Championship, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Dryburgh Athletic's nine.
