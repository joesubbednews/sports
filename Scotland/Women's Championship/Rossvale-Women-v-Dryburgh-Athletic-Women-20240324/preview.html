Rossvale Women v Dryburgh Athletic Women

Women's Championship | Petershill Park
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 1.00pm

Two of the Women's Championship's meanest defences go head-to-head at Petershill Park on Sunday, when Rossvale Women host Dryburgh Athletic Women.

Only one side – Edinburgh City Women – has conceded fewer goals than Rossvale to date: the home side have let in just 15 goals in 15 games.

Dryburgh Athletic have conceded 24 goals in 15 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Rossvale are top of the table after 15 games, of which they have won 14 and lost one, earning 42 points.

Dryburgh Athletic are five places behind the hosts in sixth, with six wins and two draws putting them on 20 points.

Rossvale are in fantastic form in the Women's Championship, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Rossvale's 15.

In the last five years, Rossvale and Dryburgh Athletic have played each other on five occasions. Rossvale won all of them.

On average, Rossvale scored 3.2 goals and Dryburgh Athletic 0.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when Rossvale won 4-0 away.
