Westdyke Women v Morton Girls

Women's Championship | Lawsondale Playing Fields
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Morton Girls travel to Lawsondale Playing Fields for Sunday lunchtime's match against Westdyke Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Morton Girls, who sit zero in the league after 16 games, fell to a 5-0 home defeat to Westdyke Women on March 31.

They face a Westdyke side who picked up a win in their last match, a 11-1 victory against Hutchison Vale, and who sit zero in the table.

Westdyke are in mixed form in the Women's Championship, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Morton Girls's form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.

With 20 goals in 16 games so far this season, the away side are the league's-3th-lowest scorers with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 66 goals at a rate of 4.1 per game.

Westdyke, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 3.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 3.2 goals per game.

Over the last two years, Westdyke and Morton Girls have played each other twice. Westdyke won both of them.

Their last meeting was on March 31, when Westdyke won 5-0 away.
