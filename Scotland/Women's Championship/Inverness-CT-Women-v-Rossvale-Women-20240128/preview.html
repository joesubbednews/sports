Inverness CT Women v Rossvale Women

Women's Championship | Ferry Brae Park
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Rossvale Women travel to Ferry Brae Park for Sunday's match against Inverness CT Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Women's Championship.

Rossvale, who sit top of the league after 10 games, fell to a 3-1 home defeat to East Fife Women on January 21.

They face an Inverness CT side who picked up a win in their last match, a 9-0 victory against Morton Girls, and who sit second in the table.

With 46 goals in 10 games so far this season, Rossvale are the league's highest scorers with 4.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Inverness CT are also above average scorers, with 4.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home team are in fantastic form in Women's Championship, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

And also with five wins and one loss over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 15 points from 18.

In the last five years, Inverness CT and Rossvale have played each other on five occasions. Inverness CT won two of them and Rossvale three.

On average, Inverness CT scored 2.6 goals and Rossvale 3.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 3, when Rossvale won 4-2 at home.
