East Fife Women v Inverness CT Women

Women's Championship | MGM Timber Bayview Stadium
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Inverness CT Women travel to the MGM Timber Bayview Stadium for Sunday's match against East Fife Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Inverness CT, who sit third in the league after 12 games, fell to a 4-2 home defeat to Renfrew Women on February 18.

They face an East Fife side who also lost their last match, a 2-1 defeat to Renfrew Women, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 41 goals in 12 games so far this season, Inverness CT are the league's third-highest scorers with 3.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

East Fife, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 2.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

In the last five years, East Fife and Inverness CT have played each other on four occasions. East Fife won all of them.

On average, East Fife scored 2.2 goals and Inverness CT 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when East Fife won 3-1 away.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Women's Championship, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to East Fife's 12.
