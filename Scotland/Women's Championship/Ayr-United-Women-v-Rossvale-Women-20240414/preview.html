Ayr United Women v Rossvale Women

Women's Championship | Queen Margaret Academy
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Ayr United Women host Rossvale Women in Sunday lunchtime's match at Queen Margaret Academy looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Ayr United, who sit second in the league after 17 games, fell to a 2-6 away defeat to Inverness CT Women on April 7.

They face a Rossvale side who secured a draw in their last match, a 4-4 tie with East Fife Women, and who sit first in the table.

With 82 goals in 17 games so far this season, Rossvale are the league's highest scorers with 4.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Ayr United are also above average scorers, with 3.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Women's Championship, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

But with five wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is even better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Ayr United's 15.

In the last 10 years, Ayr United and Rossvale have played each other on nine occasions. Rossvale won eight of them and they drew once.

On average, Ayr United scored 0.8 goals and Rossvale 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 10, when Rossvale won 2-1 away.
