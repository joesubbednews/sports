Dryburgh Athletic Women v Morton Girls

Women's Championship | Dundee International Sports Centre
Friday, 3 May 2024 @ 7.00pm

Dryburgh Athletic Women host Morton Girls in Friday's late match at Dundee International Sports Centre looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Dryburgh Athletic, who sit fourth in the league after 19 games, fell to a 0-4 home defeat to Westdyke Women on Sunday.

They face a Morton Girls side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-2 victory against Renfrew Women, and who sit zero in the table.

In the last 10 years, Dryburgh Athletic and Morton Girls have played each other on seven occasions. Dryburgh Athletic won four of them and they drew three times.

On average, Dryburgh Athletic scored 2.6 goals and Morton Girls 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 10, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Dryburgh Athletic are in mixed form in the Women's Championship, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Morton Girls's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the home team's 10.

With 24 goals in 18 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's-2th-lowest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 71 goals at a rate of 3.9 per game.

Dryburgh Athletic are also below average scorers, with 2.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.
