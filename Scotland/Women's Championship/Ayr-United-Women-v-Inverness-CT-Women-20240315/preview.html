Ayr United Women v Inverness CT Women

Women's Championship | Queen Margaret Academy
Friday, 15 March 2024 @ 7.30pm

Ayr United Women host Inverness CT Women in Friday's late match at Queen Margaret Academy looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Ayr United, who sit second in the league after 13 games, fell to a 2-1 home defeat to Rossvale Women on Sunday.

They face an Inverness CT side who also lost their last match, a 3-2 defeat to East Fife Women, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 47 goals in 13 games so far this season, Ayr United are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Inverness CT are also above average scorers, with 3.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The home side are in good form in the Women's Championship, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Ayr United's 13.

In the last five years, Ayr United and Inverness CT have played each other on five occasions. Ayr United won three of them, Inverness CT one, and they drew once.

On average, Ayr United scored 2.8 goals and Inverness CT 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on August 27, when Inverness CT won 1-0 at home.
