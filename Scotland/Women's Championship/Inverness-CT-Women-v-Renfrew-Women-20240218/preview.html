Inverness CT Women v Renfrew Women

Women's Championship | Ferry Brae Park
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 12.30pm

Inverness CT Women host Renfrew Women in Sunday lunchtime's match at Ferry Brae Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Inverness CT, who sit third in the league after 11 games, fell to a 2-0 away defeat to Dryburgh Athletic Women on February 11.

They face a Renfrew side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against East Fife Women, and who sit sixth in the table.

With 39 goals in 11 games so far this season, Inverness CT are the league's third-highest scorers with 3.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Renfrew, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 2.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

In the last five years, Inverness CT and Renfrew have played each other on five occasions. Inverness CT won one of them, Renfrew three, and they drew once.

On average, Inverness CT scored 1.8 goals and Renfrew 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 1, when Inverness CT won 3-0 away.

The home side are in mixed form in the Women's Championship, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Inverness CT's nine.
