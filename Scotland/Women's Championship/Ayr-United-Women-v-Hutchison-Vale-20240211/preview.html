Ayr United Women v Hutchison Vale

Women's Championship | Queen Margaret Academy
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Hutchison Vale face a challenge away against high-flying Ayr United Women at Queen Margaret Academy on Sunday.

Hutchison Vale are rooted to the bottom of the Women's Championship table, and have picked up no wins and no draws in their 10 games to date.

Ayr United, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 22 points, having won seven and drawn one, and are eight points behind table-toppers Rossvale Women.

Ayr United are in good form in Women's Championship, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and six losses over that period, Vale's form is much worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.

With three goals in 10 games so far this season, the away team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.3 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 76 goals at a rate of 7.6 per game.

Ayr United, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 3.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.7. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Ayr United and Vale have played each other on seven occasions. Ayr United won six of them and Vale one.

On average, Ayr United scored 3.9 goals and Vale 0.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when Ayr United won 9-0 away.
