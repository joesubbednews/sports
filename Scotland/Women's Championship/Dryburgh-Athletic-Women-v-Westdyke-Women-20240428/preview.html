Dryburgh Athletic Women v Westdyke Women

Women's Championship | Dundee International Sports Centre
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Dryburgh Athletic Women host Westdyke Women in Sunday lunchtime's match at Dundee International Sports Centre looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Dryburgh Athletic, who sit zero in the league after 18 games, fell to a 0-3 away defeat to Hutchison Vale on April 14.

They face a Westdyke side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-0 victory against Morton Girls, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 35 goals in 18 games so far this season, Dryburgh Athletic are the league's-2th-lowest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Westdyke, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 3.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 3.0 goals per game.

In the last five years, Dryburgh Athletic and Westdyke have played each other on five occasions. Dryburgh Athletic won four of them and Westdyke one.

On average, Dryburgh Athletic scored 4.2 goals and Westdyke 0.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 18, when Dryburgh Athletic won 2-1 away.

The home team are in mixed form in the Women's Championship, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Dryburgh Athletic's 10.
