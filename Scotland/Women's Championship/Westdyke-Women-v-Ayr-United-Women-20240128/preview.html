Westdyke Women v Ayr United Women

Women's Championship | Lawsondale Playing Fields
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sunday's match between Westdyke Women and Ayr United Women promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Women's Championship's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Lawsondale Playing Fields, Westdyke and Ayr United sit third and fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 31 and 27 goals respectively.

Ayr United are third in the table after nine games, of which they have won six and drawn one, earning 19 points.

Westdyke are two places behind the away team in fifth, with five wins and four losses putting them on 15 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in Women's Championship, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Ayr United's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Westdyke's nine.
