Hutchison Vale v Westdyke Women

Women's Championship | Saughton Sports Complex
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Hutchison Vale host Westdyke Women in Sunday lunchtime's match at Saughton Sports Complex looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Vale, who sit zero in the league after 16 games, fell to a 12-1 home defeat to Inverness CT Women on March 24.

They face a Westdyke side who picked up a win in their last match, a 5-0 victory against Morton Girls, and who sit zero in the table.

With seven goals in 16 games so far this season, Vale are the league's-4th-lowest scorers with 0.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 130 goals at a rate of 8.1 per game.

Westdyke, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 3.3 goals per game.

The home side are in awful form in the Women's Championship, with no wins and six losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Vale's zero.

Over the last two years, Vale and Westdyke have played each other twice. Westdyke won both of them.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when Westdyke won 6-0 at home.
