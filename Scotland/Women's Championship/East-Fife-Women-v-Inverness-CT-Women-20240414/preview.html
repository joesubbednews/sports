East Fife Women v Inverness CT Women

Women's Championship | MGM Timber Bayview Stadium
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Struggling East Fife Women face Inverness CT Women at the MGM Timber Bayview Stadium on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-4 victory over Rossvale Women on April 7, East Fife sit third in the Women's Championship.

They travel to play an Inverness CT side fourth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 6-2 against Ayr United Women.

With 63 goals in 17 games so far this season, East Fife are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Inverness CT are also above average scorers, with 3.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Women's Championship, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to East Fife's 13.

In the last five years, East Fife and Inverness CT have played each other on five occasions. East Fife won all of them.

On average, East Fife scored 2.4 goals and Inverness CT 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on February 25, when East Fife won 3-2 at home.
