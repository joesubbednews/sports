Morton Girls v East Fife Women

Women's Championship | Inverclyde Academy
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 2.00pm

Relegation candidates Morton Girls face a challenge against high-flying East Fife Women at Inverclyde Academy on Sunday.

Morton Girls are eighth in the Women's Championship table, and have picked up two wins and one draw in their 14 games to date.

East Fife, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 30 points, having won 10 and drawn none of their first 15 matches, and are 12 points behind table-toppers Rossvale Women.

With 20 goals in 14 games so far this season, Morton Girls are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 55 goals at a rate of 3.9 per game.

East Fife, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 3.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The home side are in bad form in the Women's Championship, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Morton Girls's four.
