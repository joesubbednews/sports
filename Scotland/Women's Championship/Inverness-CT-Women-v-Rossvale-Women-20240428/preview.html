Inverness CT Women v Rossvale Women

Women's Championship | Ferry Brae Park
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Inverness CT Women host Rossvale Women in Sunday lunchtime's match at Ferry Brae Park looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Women's Championship.

Inverness CT, who sit zero in the league after 18 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to East Fife Women on April 14.

They face a Rossvale side who also lost their last match, a 2-1 defeat to Ayr United Women, and who sit first in the table.

With 83 goals in 18 games so far this season, Rossvale are the league's highest scorers with 4.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Inverness CT are also above average scorers, with 3.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Inverness CT and Rossvale have played each other on six occasions. Inverness CT won two of them and Rossvale four.

On average, Inverness CT scored 2.3 goals and Rossvale 3.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 28, when Rossvale won 3-1 away.

The hosts are in bad form in the Women's Championship, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Inverness CT's four.
