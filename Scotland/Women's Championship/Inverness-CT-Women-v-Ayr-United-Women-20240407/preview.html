Inverness CT Women v Ayr United Women

Women's Championship | Ferry Brae Park
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 12.00pm

Struggling Inverness CT Women face Ayr United Women at Ferry Brae Park on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 12-1 victory over Hutchison Vale on March 24, Inverness CT sit bottom of the Women's Championship.

They travel to play an Ayr United side second in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Renfrew Women.

With 60 goals in 16 games so far this season, Inverness CT are the league's second-highest scorers with 3.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

Ayr United are also above average scorers, with 3.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 2.8. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Inverness CT and Ayr United have played each other on six occasions. Inverness CT won one of them, Ayr United four, and they drew once.

On average, Inverness CT scored 1.3 goals and Ayr United 2.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on March 15, when Ayr United won 3-1 at home.

The hosts are in bad form in the Women's Championship, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Inverness CT's four.
