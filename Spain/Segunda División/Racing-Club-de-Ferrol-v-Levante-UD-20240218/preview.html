Racing Club de Ferrol v Levante UD

Segunda División
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.15pm

Racing Club de Ferrol host Levante UD in Sunday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División.

Racing, who sit fourth in the league after 26 games, fell to a 2-0 away defeat to Burgos CF on February 11.

They face a Levante side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Leganés, and who sit 11th in the table.

With 33 goals in 26 games so far this season, Racing are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Levante are also above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away team's Sergio Lozano Lluch is among the league's most creative players, racking up six assists in 22 appearances so far this season, and holding second spot in the Segunda División's assist charts.

For Ferrol, Iker Losada Aragunde and Heber Pena Picos have set up the most goals, having laid on five assists apiece to date.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, Levante's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Racing's eight.
