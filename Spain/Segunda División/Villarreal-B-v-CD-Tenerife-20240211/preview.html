Villarreal B v CD Tenerife

Segunda División | Estadio de la Cerámica
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 1.00pm

CD Tenerife travel to Estadio de la Cerámica looking to secure a first win in seven Segunda División games against Villarreal B on Sunday lunchtime.

Tenerife have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against AD Alcorcón on December 11.

They face a Villarreal B side who have won one and drawn two over that time.

In Juan Soriano Oropesa, Tenerife can rely on one of the league's safest pair of hands. He has kept 11 clean sheets in his 25 appearances this season, and no 'keeper has prevented the opposition scoring more often in the Segunda División.

In Villarreal B's net, Iker Álvarez has three clean sheets in 16 games. He has conceded a goal every 65 minutes, 70% more often than the 113 minutes between goals for Soriano Oropesa.

The hosts are 20th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won six and drawn seven, earning 25 points.

The away team are five places ahead of Villarreal B in 15th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

With 22 goals in 25 games so far this season, Tenerife are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Villarreal B are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last five years, Villarreal B and Tenerife have played each other on four occasions. They won one each, and they drew twice.

On average, Villarreal B scored 1.0 goal and Tenerife 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when Villarreal B won 1-0 away.

Villarreal B's last match was on February 3, a 3-0 loss against Mirandés.

Tenerife drew 0-0 with FC Andorra last time out, also on February 3.
