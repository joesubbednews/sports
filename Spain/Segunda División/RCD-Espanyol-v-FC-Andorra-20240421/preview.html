RCD Espanyol v FC Andorra

Segunda División | RCDE Stadium
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 1.00pm

Relegation candidates FC Andorra face a challenge away against high-flying RCD Espanyol at the RCDE Stadium on Sunday.

FC Andorra are 21st in the Segunda División table, and have picked up nine wins and eight draws in their 35 games to date.

Espanyol, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 58 points, having won 15 and drawn 13, and are four points behind table-toppers Leganés.

With 27 goals in 35 games so far this season, Andorra are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Espanyol, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

In Martin Braithwaite, the home team have the league's sharpest shooter so far this season. He has notched 18 goals in 32 appearances.

The visitors' top scorers, with four goals each, are Manuel Nieto Sánchez and Aurélien Scheidler.

Espanyol are in mixed form in the Segunda División, with two wins and four draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Andorra's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Espanyol's 10.

Espanyol's last match was on April 12, a 0-0 draw against Leganés.

Andorra lost 2-0 against SD Eibar last time out, on Sunday.
