SD Amorebieta v Real Valladolid

Segunda División | Instalaciones de Lezama Campo 2
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 1.00pm

Struggling SD Amorebieta face Real Valladolid at Instalaciones de Lezama Campo 2 on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-1 victory over Racing Club de Ferrol on April 5, Amorebieta sit 20th in the Segunda División.

They travel to play a Valladolid side fourth in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-0 against FC Cartagena, on April 7.

With 30 goals in 34 games so far this season, Amorebieta are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Valladolid, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Segunda División, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Amorebieta's 13.

In the last three years, Amorebieta and Valladolid have played each other on three occasions. Amorebieta won one of them and Valladolid the other.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when Valladolid won 2-1 at home.
