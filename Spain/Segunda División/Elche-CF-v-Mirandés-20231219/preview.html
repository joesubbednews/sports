Elche CF v Mirandés

Segunda División | Estadio Manuel Martínez Valero
Tuesday, 19 December 2023 @ 8.30pm

Elche CF are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Tuesday's late kick-off against Mirandés.

Elche CF, who sit 12th in the Segunda División with 20 games played, are priced at 1.6 to seal victory at the Estadio Manuel Martínez Valero.

Sitting one place and two points behind them in the table, Mirandés are 4.8 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 3.6.

With 29 goals in 20 games so far this season, Mirandés are scoring more than average in the league with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Elche, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Elche and Mirandés have played each other on seven occasions. Elche won two of them, Mirandés four, and they drew once.

On average, Elche CF scored 1.3 goals and Mirandés 1.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on July 7 2020, when Mirandés won 1-0 at home.

In Carlos Martín, the away team have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. He has notched eight goals in 18 appearances, to sit third in the scoring charts.

His goal rate of one every 149 minutes is slightly quicker than that of Óscar Plano, Elche CF's top scorer with a goal every 148 minutes, and a total of seven goals in 20 games.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Mirandés' form is similar – they have both taken nine points from 18.

Elche's last match was on Friday, a 3-2 loss against Real Oviedo, with Nicolás Fernández and Plano Pedreño getting the goals for Elche CF.

Mirandés beat Albacete Balompié 2-0 last time out, on Saturday, with Martín Domínguez on the scoresheet.
