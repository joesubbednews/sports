Real Zaragoza v FC Cartagena

Segunda División | Estadio de la Romareda
Friday, 16 February 2024 @ 7.30pm

FC Cartagena face Real Zaragoza on Friday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División.

Cartagena are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 7.30pm kick-off.

They face a Zaragoza team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

In the last 10 years, Zaragoza and Cartagena have played each other on eight occasions. They won three each, and they drew twice.

On average, Zaragoza scored 1.0 goal and Cartagena 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when Zaragoza won 3-1 away.

Cartagena are 17th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won seven and drawn seven, earning 28 points.

Zaragoza are five places ahead of the visitors in 12th, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

With 26 goals in 26 games so far this season, Cartagena are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Zaragoza's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 loss against SD Eibar.

Cartagena beat Mirandés 1-0 last time out, also on Sunday, with Darío Poveda Romera on the scoresheet.
