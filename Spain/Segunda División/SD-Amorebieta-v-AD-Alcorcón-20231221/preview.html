SD Amorebieta v AD Alcorcón

Segunda División | Instalaciones de Lezama Campo 2
Thursday, 21 December 2023 @ 8.30pm

Struggling SD Amorebieta face AD Alcorcón at Instalaciones de Lezama Campo 2 on Thursday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-1 victory over Real Zaragoza on Sunday, SD Amorebieta sit 21st in the Segunda División.

They travel to play an Alcorcón side 20th in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against SD Eibar.

Amorebieta are in disappointing form in the Segunda División, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Alcorcón's form is slightly worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to SD Amorebieta's five.

With 17 goals in 20 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

AD Alcorcón are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Amorebieta's Josué Dorrio Ortega is among the league's most creative players, racking up five assists in 20 appearances so far this season, and holding fourth spot in the Segunda División's assist charts.

For the visitors, Emmanuel Addai has set up the most goals, having laid on three assists in 18 games.
