CD Eldense v RCD Espanyol

Segunda División | Estadio Municipal Nuevo Pepico Amat
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 1.00pm

Both CD Eldense and RCD Espanyol will be looking to extend their unbeaten runs at the Estadio Municipal Nuevo Pepico Amat on Sunday lunchtime.

The two sides have gone five Segunda División matches since they last lost – during which they have each won one and drawn four.

With 36 goals in 23 games so far this season, Espanyol are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Eldense are also above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In Martin Braithwaite, the visitors have the league's sharpest shooter so far this season. He has notched 11 goals in 21 appearances.

His goal rate of one every 166 minutes is slightly quicker than that of Mario Soberón Gutiérrez, the home side's top scorer with a goal every 181 minutes, and a total of eight goals in 21 games.

Espanyol are third in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn eight, earning 38 points.

Eldense are 12 places behind Espanyol in 15th, with seven wins and nine draws putting them on 30 points.

Eldense's last match was on January 20, a 2-0 win against SD Amorebieta, with Juan Tomás Ortuño Martínez and Soberón Gutiérrez getting the goals for Eldense.

Espanyol beat Villarreal B 2-1 last time out, also on January 20, with Braithwaite on the scoresheet.
