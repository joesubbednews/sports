Racing Club de Ferrol v SD Huesca

Segunda División
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 8.00pm

SD Huesca face Racing Club de Ferrol on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Segunda División.

Huesca are unbeaten in 11, with four wins and seven draws, ahead of the 8pm kick-off.

They face a Racing team who have won three and drawn four over the same number of games.

In Álvaro Fernández, Huesca can rely on one of the league's safest pair of hands. He has kept 17 clean sheets in his 34 appearances this season, and no 'keeper has prevented the opposition scoring more often in the Segunda División.

In Racing's net, Ander Cantero Armendáriz has 10 clean sheets in 34 games. He has conceded a goal every 87 minutes, 70% more often than the 146 minutes between goals for Fernández Llorente.

With 29 goals in 34 games so far this season, the away team are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Ferrol, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are 10th in the table after 34 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn 12, earning 51 points.

Huesca are three places behind Racing in 13th, with nine wins and 15 draws putting them on 42 points.

Racing's last match was on April 5, a 3-1 loss against SD Amorebieta, with Jon García Herrero getting the goal for Racing.

Huesca drew 0-0 with Leganés last time out, on April 7.

Sunday's match will be refereed by Álvaro Moreno Aragón, who has taken charge of 17 Segunda División games so far this season, issuing two red cards and booking 71 players. He has awarded 10 penalties.

The last Racing game Moreno Aragón refereed was a 1-1 home draw with CD Eldense on October 22. He is yet to oversee a match featuring Huesca this season.
