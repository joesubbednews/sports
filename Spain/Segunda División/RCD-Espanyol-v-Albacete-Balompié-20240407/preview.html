RCD Espanyol v Albacete Balompié

Segunda División | RCDE Stadium
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 5.30pm

RCD Espanyol face Albacete Balompié on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División.

Espanyol are unbeaten in seven, with three wins and four draws, ahead of the 5.30pm kick-off.

They face an Albacete team who have won one and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 49 goals in 33 games so far this season, Espanyol are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Albacete are also above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In Martin Braithwaite, the home side have the league's sharpest shooter so far this season. He has notched 18 goals in 30 appearances.

His goal rate of one every 141 minutes is much quicker than that of Manuel Fuster Lázaro, the visitors' top scorer with a goal every 348 minutes, and a total of eight goals in 33 games.

Espanyol are third in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 12, earning 54 points.

Albacete are 15 places behind Espanyol in 18th, with seven wins and 12 draws putting them on 33 points.

Espanyol's last match was on March 29, a 0-0 draw against Burgos CF.

Albacete drew 1-1 with SD Huesca last time out, on March 30, with Alberto Quiles Piosa on the scoresheet.
