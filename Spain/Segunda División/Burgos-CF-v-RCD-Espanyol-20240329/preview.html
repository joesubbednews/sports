Burgos CF v RCD Espanyol

Segunda División | Estadio Municipal de El Plantío
Friday, 29 March 2024 @ 7.30pm

RCD Espanyol face Burgos CF on Friday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División.

Espanyol are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 7.30pm kick-off.

They face a Burgos team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 49 goals in 32 games so far this season, Espanyol are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Burgos are also above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are second in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 11, earning 53 points.

The home team are six places behind Espanyol in eighth, with 14 wins and seven draws putting them on 49 points.

In Martin Braithwaite, Espanyol have the league's sharpest shooter so far this season. He has notched 18 goals in 29 appearances.

His goal rate of one every 139 minutes is quicker than that of Curro Sánchez, Burgos's top scorer with a goal every 221 minutes, and a total of 12 goals in 31 games.

Burgos's last match was on Sunday, a 3-0 loss against SD Huesca.

Espanyol drew 1-1 with CD Tenerife last time out, on Saturday, with José Gragera on the scoresheet.
