Elche CF v FC Cartagena
---
Segunda División | Estadio Manuel Martínez Valero
Sunday, 10 December 2023 @ 1.00pm
---
Struggling FC Cartagena face Elche CF away at the Estadio Manuel Martínez Valero on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.
After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Sporting Gijón on December 2, FC Cartagena sit bottom of the Segunda División.
They travel to play an Elche side seventh in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-0 against AD Alcorcón, on December 3.
With 15 goals in 18 games so far this season, Cartagena are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.
Elche are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.
Elche CF are in good form in the Segunda División, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.
With a win and three draws over that period, FC Cartagena's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.
Over the last two years, Elche and Cartagena have played each other twice. Cartagena won both of them.
Their last meeting was on July 22, when Cartagena won 1-0 at home.
