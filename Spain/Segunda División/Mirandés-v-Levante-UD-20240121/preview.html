Mirandés v Levante UD

Segunda División | Estadio Municipal de Anduva
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 3.15pm

Mirandés face Levante UD on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División.

Mirandés are unbeaten in seven, with two wins and five draws, ahead of the 3.15pm kick-off.

They face a Levante team who have won three and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 30 goals in 22 games so far this season, Levante are scoring more than average in the league with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Mirandés are also above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are sixth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

The home team are seven places behind Levante in 13th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

Levante's Sergio Lozano Lluch is among the league's most creative players, racking up six assists in 18 appearances so far this season, and holding second spot in the Segunda División's assist charts.

For Mirandés, Gabriel Martínez Aguilera has set up the most goals, having laid on four assists in 22 games.

In the last three years, Mirandés and Levante have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Mirandés's last match was on January 13, a 0-0 draw against AD Alcorcón.

Levante beat Albacete Balompié 3-2 last time out, also on January 13, with Mohamed Bouldini (two) and Pablo Martínez Andrés on the scoresheet.

Sunday's match will be refereed by José Luis Guzmán Mansilla, who has taken charge of 10 Segunda División games so far this season, issuing one red card and booking 51 players. He has awarded five penalties.

The last Levante game Guzmán Mansilla refereed was the 2-1 win at home against Real Valladolid on December 2. He is yet to oversee a match featuring Mirandés this season.
