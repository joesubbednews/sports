Real Oviedo v Villarreal B

Segunda División | Estadio Nuevo Carlos Tartiere
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 5.30pm

Villarreal B make the journey to the Estadio Nuevo Carlos Tartiere to play Real Oviedo on late Sunday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Villarreal B's struggles have left them with five points from their last six Segunda División matches, while their opponents have earned 10 from a possible 18.

With 28 goals in 31 games so far this season, Villarreal B are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 46 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Oviedo, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The visitors are 19th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won seven and drawn nine, earning 30 points.

The home side are 12 places ahead of Villarreal B in seventh, with 12 wins and 12 draws putting them on 48 points.

In Álex Forés Mendoza, Villarreal B have one of the league's sharpest shooters so far this season. He has notched 11 goals in 31 appearances, to sit fifth in the scoring charts.

Oviedo's top scorers, with eight goals in 31 games each, are Francisco Sebastián Moyano Jiménez and Borja Bastón.

In the last three years, Oviedo and Villarreal B have played each other on three occasions. Villarreal B won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 21, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Oviedo's last match was on March 16, a 1-1 draw against Racing Santander, with Alemão getting the goal for Oviedo.

Villarreal B lost 2-0 against SD Eibar last time out, also on March 16.
