FC Andorra v SD Eibar

Segunda División | Estadi Nacional
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 3.15pm

SD Eibar travel to Estadi Nacional for Sunday's match against FC Andorra looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División.

Eibar, who sit third in the league after 34 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to CD Tenerife on April 7.

They face an Andorra side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-0 victory against CD Eldense, and who sit 19th in the table.

With 55 goals in 34 games so far this season, Eibar are the league's highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Andorra, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In Jon Bautista Orgilles, the visitors have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. He has notched 12 goals in 29 appearances, to sit fifth in the scoring charts.

The home side's top scorers, with four goals each, are Manuel Nieto Sánchez and Aurélien Scheidler.

Andorra are in mixed form in the Segunda División, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Eibar's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Andorra's seven.

In the last three years, Andorra and Eibar have played each other on three occasions. Andorra won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on December 10, when they played out a 2-2 draw.
