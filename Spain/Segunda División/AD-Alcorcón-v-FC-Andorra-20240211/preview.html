AD Alcorcón v FC Andorra

Segunda División | Estadio Santo Domingo
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 3.15pm

Sunday's match at the Estadio Santo Domingo sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 19th-ranked AD Alcorcón host 21st-placed FC Andorra.

Alcorcón have picked up 25 points from their first 25 Segunda División games, with six wins and seven draws.

That is one point more than Andorra have collected, having won six and drawn six.

Alcorcón are in reasonable form in the Segunda División, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Andorra's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the hosts' 11.

With 20 goals in 25 games so far this season, Alcorcón are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Alcorcón's last match was on February 3, a 1-0 loss against SD Huesca.

Andorra drew 0-0 with CD Tenerife last time out, also on February 3.
