Sporting Gijón v Villarreal B

Segunda División | Estadio Municipal El Molinón
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 1.00pm

Villarreal B come to the Estadio Municipal El Molinón to play Sporting Gijón on Sunday lunchtime in poor form, having collected just three points from their last six games.

The visitors have won just one of their last six fixtures, while Sporting have three wins and three losses.

Villarreal B are bottom of the table after 35 games, of which they have won eight and drawn nine, earning 33 points.

Sporting are 16 places ahead of the away team in sixth, with 15 wins and 10 draws putting them on 55 points.

With 32 goals in 35 games so far this season, Villarreal B are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

In Álex Forés Mendoza, Villarreal B have one of the league's sharpest shooters so far this season. He has notched 13 goals in 35 appearances, to sit fourth in the scoring charts.

His goal rate of one every 197 minutes is quicker than that of Gaspar Campos Ansó Fernández, Sporting's top scorer with a goal every 234 minutes, and a total of 10 goals in 33 games.

In the last three years, Sporting and Villarreal B have played each other on three occasions. Sporting won two of them and Villarreal B one.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Sporting won 3-0 away.

Sporting's last match was on April 13, a 1-0 win against FC Cartagena, with Mario González Gutiérrez getting the goal for Sporting.

Villarreal B lost 1-0 against AD Alcorcón last time out, on April 15.
