Elche CF v Real Valladolid

Segunda División | Estadio Manuel Martínez Valero
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 8.00pm

Real Valladolid travel to the Estadio Manuel Martínez Valero for Sunday's late match against Elche CF looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División.

Valladolid, who sit fourth in the league after 21 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Racing Club de Ferrol on December 21.

They face an Elche side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Mirandés, and who sit 12th in the table.

In the last 10 years, Elche and Valladolid have played each other on 12 occasions. They won two each, and they drew eight times.

On average, Elche scored 1.2 goals and Valladolid 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

With 21 goals in 21 games so far this season, Elche are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Valladolid, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Segunda División, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.
