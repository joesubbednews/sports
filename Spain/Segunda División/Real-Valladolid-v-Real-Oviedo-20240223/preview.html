Real Valladolid v Real Oviedo

Segunda División | Estadio Municipal José Zorrilla
Friday, 23 February 2024 @ 7.30pm

Real Valladolid host Real Oviedo on Friday at the Estadio Municipal José Zorrilla in the Segunda División.

In their last league match, on February 12, Valladolid drew with Albacete Balompié 0-0 at home.

Oviedo lost, 1-0 away at Sporting Gijón on February 10.

In the last 10 years, Valladolid and Oviedo have played each other on 10 occasions. Valladolid won six of them and Oviedo four.

On average, Valladolid scored 1.6 goals and Oviedo 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Valladolid won 1-0 away.

With 28 goals in 26 games so far this season, Valladolid are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Oviedo are also average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The home side are sixth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn five, earning 41 points.

The visitors are four places behind Valladolid in 10th, with nine wins and 11 draws putting them on 38 points.

Valladolid are in disappointing form in the Segunda División, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Oviedo's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.
