Villarreal B v Real Valladolid

Segunda División | Estadio de la Cerámica
Monday, 18 December 2023 @ 7.30pm

Villarreal B host Real Valladolid on Monday at Estadio de la Cerámica in the Segunda División.

In their last league match, on December 8, Villarreal B lost to Albacete Balompié 2-0 away.

Valladolid won, 2-1 at home against SD Amorebieta on December 9, with Israel Salazar Piriz and Stanko Jurić on the scoresheet.

Valladolid are second in the table after 19 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn two, earning 35 points.

Villarreal B are 16 places behind Real Valladolid in 18th, with five wins and five draws putting them on 20 points.

With 25 goals in 19 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The home side, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Villarreal B are in mixed form in the Segunda División, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Valladolid's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Villarreal B's seven.

In Álex Forés Mendoza, the hosts have one of the league's sharpest shooters so far this season. He has notched seven goals in 19 appearances, to sit seventh in the scoring charts.

His goal rate of one every 171 minutes is slightly quicker than that of Mamadou Sylla Diallo, Valladolid's top scorer with a goal every 168 minutes, and a total of six goals in 14 games.
