Valencia B v SD Formentera

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Antonio Puchades
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 11.00am

SD Formentera travel to Valencia B on early Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Espanyol B 1-1 at home, with a goal from Adrián Montalban Ribas.

Valencia B, meanwhile, won their last match, 6-0 against La Nucía, with their goals scored by Hugo González Sotos and Marco Camús Muñoz.

With 38 goals in 27 games so far this season, Valencia B are scoring more than average in the league with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Formentera, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The visitors are 13th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

The home side are five places ahead of Formentera in eighth, with 10 wins and seven draws putting them on 37 points.

In the last three years, Valencia B and Formentera have played each other on three occasions. Valencia B won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when Valencia B won 3-1 away.

Valencia B are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Formentera's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Valencia B's 10.
