Peña Deportiva v La Nucía

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Campo Municipal del Santa Eulalia del Río
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling La Nucía face Peña Deportiva away at Campo Municipal del Santa Eulalia del Río on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Manresa on April 20, la Nucía sit bottom of the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

They travel to play a Peña Deportiva side 12th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 5-1 against Hércules CF, on April 21.

With 22 goals in 32 games so far this season, La Nucía are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Peña Deportiva, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, la Nucía's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Peña Deportiva's eight.
