SD Formentera v Penya Independent

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Municipal
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling Penya Independent face SD Formentera away at the Estadio Municipal on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Espanyol B on April 21, Penya Independent sit 16th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

They travel to play a Formentera side 14th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 0-0 against Andratx, on April 20.

Formentera are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Penya Independent's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

With 31 goals in 32 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Formentera are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.
