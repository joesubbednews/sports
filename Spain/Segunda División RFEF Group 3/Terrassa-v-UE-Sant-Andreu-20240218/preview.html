Terrassa v UE Sant Andreu

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

UE Sant Andreu travel to Terrassa on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten La Nucía 4-1 at home, with goals from Gerard Oliva Gorgori, Sergi Serrano Bernat and Alexis García Gallego.

Terrassa, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-1 against Torrent, with their goal scored by Gil Muntadas Cumbrubí.

With 37 goals in 22 games so far this season, UE Sant Andreu are the league's joint-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Terrassa, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The away side are fifth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn five, earning 35 points.

The hosts are seven places behind UE Sant Andreu in 12th, with six wins and nine draws putting them on 27 points.

Terrassa are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, UE Sant Andreu's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Terrassa's 11.
