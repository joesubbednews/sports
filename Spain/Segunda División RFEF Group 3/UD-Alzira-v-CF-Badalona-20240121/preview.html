UD Alzira v CF Badalona

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 4.00pm

CF Badalona face UD Alzira on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Badalona are unbeaten in six, with one win and five draws, ahead of the 4pm kick-off.

They face an Alzira team who have won four and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 22 goals in 18 games so far this season, Badalona are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 12 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Alzira are also average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 18 games, of which they have won eight and drawn eight, earning 32 points.

The home side are two places behind Badalona in seventh, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.

In the last three years, Alzira and Badalona have played each other on three occasions. Alzira won one of them and Badalona the other.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when Badalona won 1-0 at home.

Alzira's last match was on January 14, a 3-1 loss against Peña Deportiva.

Badalona drew 1-1 with Terrassa last time out, also on January 14.
