Lleida Esportiu v CE Europa

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Camp d'Esports de Lleida
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Two of the Segunda División RFEF Group 3's top sides face each other at Camp d'Esports de Lleida in Sunday's early kick-off, when second-placed Lleida Esportiu host third-placed CE Europa.

Lleida Esportiu have picked up 18 wins and four draws from 32 games so far this season, and sit two points above the visitors going into the 11am match.

CE Europa, meanwhile, have won 15 and drawn 11 of 31, picking up 56 points.

With 53 goals in 31 games so far this season, CE Europa are the league's highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Lleida Esportiu are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

In the last three years, Lleida Esportiu and CE Europa have played each other on three occasions. Lleida Esportiu won one of them and CE Europa the other.

Their last meeting was on December 17, when CE Europa won 3-0 at home.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Lleida Esportiu's 10.

Lleida Esportiu's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against Cerdanyola del Vallès.

CE Europa beat CF Badalona 2-1 last time out, also on April 21.
