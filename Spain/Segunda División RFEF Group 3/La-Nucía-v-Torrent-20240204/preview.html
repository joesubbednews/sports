La Nucía v Torrent

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Camilo Cano
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 5.00pm

La Nucía face Torrent at the Estadio Camilo Cano on late Sunday looking to secure a first win in nine Segunda División RFEF Group 3 games.

La Nucía have lost four and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Terrassa on November 19.

They face a Torrent side who have won three and drawn two over that time.

La Nucía are bottom of the table after 20 games, of which they have won two and drawn seven, earning 13 points.

Torrent are 11 places ahead of la Nucía in seventh, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

With 12 goals in 20 games so far this season, the home team are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have also conceded 1.0 goal per game.

La Nucía's last match was on January 28, a 2-2 draw against SD Formentera.

Torrent beat Hércules CF 2-1 last time out, also on January 28.
