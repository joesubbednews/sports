SD Formentera v UE Sant Andreu

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Municipal
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 10.00am

UE Sant Andreu face SD Formentera on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

UE Sant Andreu are unbeaten in nine, with six wins and three draws, ahead of the 10am kick-off.

They face a Formentera team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 49 goals in 30 games so far this season, UE Sant Andreu are the league's joint-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Formentera, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The away side are third in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn eight, earning 53 points.

The hosts are 11 places behind UE Sant Andreu in 14th, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

Formentera's last match was on Sunday, a 3-0 loss against Torrent.

UE Sant Andreu drew 1-1 with Espanyol B last time out, also on Sunday, with Alberto Garcia Nieto on the scoresheet.
