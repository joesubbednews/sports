SD Formentera v La Nucía

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Municipal
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 10.00am

La Nucía travel to the Estadio Municipal looking to secure a first win in eight Segunda División RFEF Group 3 games against SD Formentera on Sunday.

La Nucía have lost four and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Terrassa on November 19.

They face a Formentera side who have won four and drawn one over that time.

La Nucía are bottom of the table after 19 games, of which they have won two and drawn six, earning 12 points.

Formentera are nine places ahead of la Nucía in ninth, with seven wins and four draws putting them on 25 points.

With 10 goals in 19 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The home side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Formentera's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 win against Hércules CF.

La Nucía drew 0-0 with Espanyol B last time out, also on Sunday.
