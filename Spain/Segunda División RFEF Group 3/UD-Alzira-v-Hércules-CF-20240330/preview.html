UD Alzira v Hércules CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Hércules CF travel to play UD Alzira on Saturday hoping to break their five-match run of draws in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Hércules have earned five points from their last six games.

They face an Alzira side who have collected six points from the last possible 18, and who also drew their last game.

With 39 goals in 28 games so far this season, Hércules are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Alzira, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away side are fifth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn eight, earning 47 points.

The hosts are five places behind Hércules in 10th, with eight wins and 12 draws putting them on 36 points.

In the last five years, Alzira and Hércules have played each other on five occasions. Alzira won two of them, Hércules one, and they drew twice.

On average, Alzira scored 1.2 goals and Hércules 0.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Alzira's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against CE Europa.

Hércules drew 1-1 with Terrassa last time out, also on Sunday, with Carlos De la Nava García on the scoresheet.
