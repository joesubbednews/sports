Lleida Esportiu v UD Alzira

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Camp d'Esports de Lleida
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 4.00pm

Lleida Esportiu host UD Alzira in Sunday's match at Camp d'Esports de Lleida looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Lleida Esportiu, who sit second in the league after 19 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Terrassa on January 21.

They face an Alzira side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with CF Badalona, and who sit seventh in the table.

With 26 goals in 19 games so far this season, Lleida Esportiu are scoring more than average in the league with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Alzira, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Lleida Esportiu's seven.

In the last three years, Lleida Esportiu and Alzira have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when Lleida Esportiu won 2-0 away.
