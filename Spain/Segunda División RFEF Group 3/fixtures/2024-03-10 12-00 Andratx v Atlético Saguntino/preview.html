Andratx v Atlético Saguntino

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Sa Plana
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Andratx host Atlético Saguntino on Sunday at the Estadio Sa Plana in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

In their last league match, on March 3, Andratx drew with UD Alzira 2-2 away, with goals from Lado Mokhevishvili (own goal) and Pere Amer Rodríguez.

Atlético Saguntino lost, 2-0 at home against UE Sant Andreu.

With 24 goals in 24 games so far this season, Atlético Saguntino are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Andratx, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The visitors are 14th in the table after 24 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

The home side are three places ahead of Atlético Saguntino in 11th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

Andratx are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Atlético Saguntino's form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Andratx's eight.
