Penya Independent v CE Europa

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Mid-season relegation candidates Penya Independent face a challenge against high-flying CE Europa on Sunday.

Penya Independent are rooted to the bottom of the Segunda División RFEF Group 3 table, and have picked up three wins and six draws in their 21 games to date.

CE Europa, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 40 points, having won 11 and drawn seven.

With 36 goals in 21 games so far this season, CE Europa are the league's highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Penya Independent, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Penya Independent's four.

Penya Independent's last match was on February 4, a 1-1 draw against Cerdanyola del Vallès.

CE Europa drew 2-2 with Andratx last time out, also on February 4.
