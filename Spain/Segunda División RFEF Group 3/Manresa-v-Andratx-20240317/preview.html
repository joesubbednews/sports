Manresa v Andratx

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Manresa are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Andratx on Sunday, with just one point collected from their last six games.

Manresa have picked up just one draw in their last six Segunda División RFEF Group 3 games, and face an Andratx side whose last six games have brought one win and three draws.

Manresa are 17th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won five and drawn eight, earning 23 points.

Andratx are seven places ahead of the hosts in 10th, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

With 20 goals in 26 games so far this season, Manresa are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Manresa's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against UE Sant Andreu.

Andratx drew 1-1 with Atlético Saguntino last time out, also on March 10, with Adrián Garrido Martínez on the scoresheet.
