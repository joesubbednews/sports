UE Sant Andreu v Torrent

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Camp Municipal Narcís Sala
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

Torrent travel to UE Sant Andreu on late Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Valencia B 2-2 away, with a goal from Christian Albert Simo.

UE Sant Andreu, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-1 against SD Formentera, with their goal scored by Antoni Paredes García.

With 50 goals in 31 games so far this season, UE Sant Andreu are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Torrent, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home side are fifth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn eight, earning 53 points.

The visitors are two places behind UE Sant Andreu in seventh, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

UE Sant Andreu are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Torrent's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.
