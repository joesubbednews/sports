Penya Independent v Torrent

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Torrent travel looking to secure a first win in four Segunda División RFEF Group 3 games against Penya Independent on Sunday.

Torrent have lost none and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against SD Formentera on April 7.

They face a Penya Independent side who have won just one and lost two over that time.

Penya Independent are 16th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

Torrent are nine places ahead of the hosts in seventh, with 12 wins and 10 draws putting them on 46 points.

With 31 goals in 33 games so far this season, Penya Independent are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 50 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Penya Independent's last match was on April 28, a 3-0 loss against SD Formentera.

Torrent drew 1-1 with Andratx last time out, also on April 28, with Christian Albert Simo on the scoresheet.
