Terrassa v La Nucía

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 6.00pm

Terrassa are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday's late kick-off against La Nucía.

Terrassa, who sit 10th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3 with 28 games played, are priced at 1.3 to seal victory.

Sitting eight places and 20 points behind them in the table, La Nucía are 9.5 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 4.5.

Terrassa are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, La Nucía's form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.

With 18 goals in 28 games so far this season, la Nucía are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Terrassa, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Terrassa's last match was on Sunday, a 1-1 draw against Hércules CF, with Sergio Buenacasa Alba getting the goal for Terrassa.

La Nucía lost 6-0 against Valencia B last time out, also on March 24.
