Terrassa v La Nucía

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 6.00pm

La Nucía come to play Terrassa on late Sunday in terrible form, having collected just one point from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn just one of their last six fixtures, while Terrassa have three wins and a draw.

La Nucía are bottom of the table after 28 games, of which they have won three and drawn eight, earning 17 points.

Terrassa are nine places ahead of la Nucía in ninth, with nine wins and 10 draws putting them on 37 points.

With 18 goals in 28 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The home side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Terrassa's last match was on Sunday, a 1-1 draw against Hércules CF, with Sergio Buenacasa Alba getting the goal for Terrassa.

La Nucía lost 6-0 against Valencia B last time out, also on March 24.
