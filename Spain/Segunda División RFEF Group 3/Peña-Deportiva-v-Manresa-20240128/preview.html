Peña Deportiva v Manresa

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Campo Municipal del Santa Eulalia del Río
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 11.00am

Manresa travel to Peña Deportiva on early Sunday in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Valencia B 3-0 away.

Peña Deportiva, meanwhile, won their last match, 1-0 against Atlético Saguntino.

With 16 goals in 19 games so far this season, Manresa are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Peña Deportiva, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The visitors are 14th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

The home team are eight places ahead of Manresa in sixth, with seven wins and six draws putting them on 27 points.

Peña Deportiva are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Manresa's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Peña Deportiva's nine.

In the last three years, Peña Deportiva and Manresa have played each other on three occasions. Peña Deportiva won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on September 19, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
