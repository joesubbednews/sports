UD Alzira v Torrent

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Torrent travel to UD Alzira on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Terrassa 2-1 at home, with goals from Joaquim Araújo Delgado and Matías Nicolás Chavarría.

Alzira also won their last match, 2-0 against SD Formentera, with their goal scored by Pablo Palacín Serra.

With 23 goals in 22 games so far this season, Torrent are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Alzira, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The visitors are sixth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn five, earning 32 points.

The home side are two places behind Torrent in eighth, with seven wins and nine draws putting them on 30 points.

Alzira are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Torrent's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Alzira's eight.
