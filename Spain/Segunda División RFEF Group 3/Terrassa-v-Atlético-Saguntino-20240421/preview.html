Terrassa v Atlético Saguntino

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

Terrassa host Atlético Saguntino on late Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Terrassa have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face an Atlético Saguntino side who drew their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 29 goals in 30 games so far this season, Atlético Saguntino are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Terrassa, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away side are 14th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn seven, earning 37 points.

The hosts are eight places ahead of Atlético Saguntino in sixth, with 12 wins and 10 draws putting them on 46 points.

In the last three years, Terrassa and Atlético Saguntino have played each other on three occasions. Terrassa won two of them and Atlético Saguntino one.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when Atlético Saguntino won 1-0 at home.

Terrassa's last match was on April 14, a 3-1 win against UD Alzira, with Aythami Perera García, Gil Muntadas Cumbrubí and Jofre Cherta Clara getting the goals for Terrassa.

Atlético Saguntino drew 0-0 with La Nucía last time out, also on April 14.
