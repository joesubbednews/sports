Cerdanyola del Vallès v Atlético Saguntino

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Cerdanyola del Vallès host Atlético Saguntino on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

In their last league match, on March 16, Cerdanyola del Vallès lost to UD Alzira 1-0 away.

Atlético Saguntino won, 2-1 at home against Penya Independent on March 17, with Luis Navarro Genis (two) scoring their goals.

Cerdanyola del Vallès are 15th in the table after 27 games, of which they have won seven and drawn eight, earning 29 points.

Atlético Saguntino are two places ahead of the home side in 13th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

With 27 goals in 26 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Cerdanyola del Vallès are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Cerdanyola del Vallès are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Atlético Saguntino's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.
