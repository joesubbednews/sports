Espanyol B v Torrent

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Ciutat Esportiva Dani Jarque
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Torrent travel to Espanyol B on early Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Lleida Esportiu 2-0 at home, with goals from Matías Nicolás Chavarría and Adrián Pérez Torrecilla.

Espanyol B, meanwhile, drew their last match, 1-1 against SD Formentera, with their goal scored by Francisco Javier Hernandez Coarasa.

With 31 goals in 27 games so far this season, Torrent are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Espanyol B, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are sixth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn nine, earning 39 points.

The away side are one place behind Espanyol B in seventh, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on the same number of points.

Espanyol B are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, Torrent's form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
