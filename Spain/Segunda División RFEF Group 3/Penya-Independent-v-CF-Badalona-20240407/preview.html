Penya Independent v CF Badalona

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 11.00am

CF Badalona travel for Sunday's early match against Penya Independent looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Badalona, who sit top of the league after 29 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Andratx on March 30.

They face a Penya Independent side who also lost their last match, a 4-1 defeat to Peña Deportiva, and who sit 16th in the table.

With 28 goals in 29 games so far this season, Penya Independent are the league's third-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Badalona, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Penya Independent's nine.
