Penya Independent v Valencia B

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling Penya Independent face Valencia B on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Andratx on January 20, Penya Independent sit 17th in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

They travel to play a Valencia B side 15th in the standings, who also won their last match, 3-0 against Manresa, on January 21.

Penya Independent are in bad form in Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Valencia B's form is slightly better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the hosts' three.

With 15 goals in 19 games so far this season, Penya Independent are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.
