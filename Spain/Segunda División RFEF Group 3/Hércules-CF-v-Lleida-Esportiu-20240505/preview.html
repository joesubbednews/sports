Hércules CF v Lleida Esportiu

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio José Rico Pérez
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Hércules CF host Lleida Esportiu in Sunday's early match at the Estadio José Rico Pérez looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Hércules, who sit top of the league after 33 games, fell to a 1-2 away defeat to CF Badalona on April 28.

They face a Lleida Esportiu side who also lost their last match, a 1-0 defeat to CE Europa, and who sit third in the table.

With 51 goals in 33 games so far this season, Hércules are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Lleida Esportiu are also above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Hércules's 16.

In the last 10 years, Hércules and Lleida Esportiu have played each other on 17 occasions. Hércules won eight of them, Lleida Esportiu four, and they drew five times.

On average, Hércules scored 0.9 goals and Lleida Esportiu 0.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 7, when Hércules won 1-0 away.
