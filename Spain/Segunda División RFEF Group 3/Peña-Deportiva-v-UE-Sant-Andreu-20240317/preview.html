Peña Deportiva v UE Sant Andreu

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Campo Municipal del Santa Eulalia del Río
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 11.00am

UE Sant Andreu face Peña Deportiva on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

UE Sant Andreu are unbeaten in five, with four wins and one draw, ahead of the 11am kick-off.

They face a Peña Deportiva team who have won just one and lost four over the same number of games.

With 43 goals in 26 games so far this season, UE Sant Andreu are the league's joint-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Peña Deportiva are also above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn six, earning 45 points.

The home team are seven places behind UE Sant Andreu in 11th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

Peña Deportiva's last match was on March 10, a 3-2 win against Torrent, with Sergio Chica Villares (two) and Marc Fraile Rosello getting the goals for Peña Deportiva.

UE Sant Andreu drew 0-0 with Manresa last time out, also on Sunday.
