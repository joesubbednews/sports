Manresa v Espanyol B

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Manresa are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Espanyol B on Sunday, with just one point collected from their last six games.

Manresa have picked up just one draw in their last six Segunda División RFEF Group 3 games, and face an Espanyol B side whose last six games have brought two wins and four draws.

Manresa are 15th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn seven, earning 22 points.

Espanyol B are five places ahead of the hosts in 10th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

With 18 goals in 22 games so far this season, Manresa are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Manresa and Espanyol B have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Espanyol B won 2-0 at home.

Manresa's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 loss against Lleida Esportiu.

Espanyol B beat Atlético Saguntino 2-1 last time out, also on Sunday.
