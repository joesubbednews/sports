UE Sant Andreu v Espanyol B

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Camp Municipal Narcís Sala
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

UE Sant Andreu face Espanyol B on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

UE Sant Andreu are unbeaten in eight, with six wins and two draws, ahead of the 5pm kick-off.

They face an Espanyol B team who have won five and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 48 goals in 29 games so far this season, UE Sant Andreu are the league's highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Espanyol B, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home side are second in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn seven, earning 52 points.

The visitors are four places behind UE Sant Andreu in sixth, with 11 wins and nine draws putting them on 42 points.

UE Sant Andreu's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 win against Lleida Esportiu, with Antoni Paredes García getting the goal for UE Sant Andreu.

Espanyol B beat Torrent 1-0 last time out, also on March 31, with Marc Jurado on the scoresheet.
