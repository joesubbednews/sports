Manresa v Hércules CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 11.30am

Relegation candidates Manresa face a challenge against high-flying Hércules CF on Sunday.

Manresa are 17th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3 table, and have picked up six wins and eight draws in their 30 games to date.

Hércules, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 53 points, having won 15 and drawn eight, and are three points behind table-toppers CF Badalona.

With 22 goals in 30 games so far this season, Manresa are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Hércules, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four draws over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Manresa's four.

In the last three years, Manresa and Hércules have played each other on three occasions. Hércules won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on December 3, when Hércules won 1-0 at home.

Manresa's last match was on April 7, a 4-0 loss against CE Europa.

Hércules beat Atlético Saguntino 3-0 last time out, also on Sunday.
