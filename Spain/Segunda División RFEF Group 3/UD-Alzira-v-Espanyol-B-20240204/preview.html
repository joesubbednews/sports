UD Alzira v Espanyol B

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Espanyol B face UD Alzira on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Espanyol B are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 4pm kick-off.

They face an Alzira team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 22 goals in 20 games so far this season, Espanyol B are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Alzira are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Alzira and Espanyol B have played each other on three occasions. Alzira won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on September 23, when Alzira won 2-1 away.

The visitors are 11th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won six and drawn seven, earning 25 points.

The home side are one place ahead of Espanyol B in 10th, with six wins and eight draws putting them on 26 points.

Alzira's last match was on January 28, a 3-1 loss against Lleida Esportiu.

Espanyol B drew 2-2 with Terrassa last time out, also on Sunday.
