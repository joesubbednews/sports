Lleida Esportiu v UE Sant Andreu

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Camp d'Esports de Lleida
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 6.00pm

Lleida Esportiu host UE Sant Andreu in Sunday's late match at Camp d'Esports de Lleida looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Lleida Esportiu, who sit second in the league after 28 games, fell to a 2-0 away defeat to Torrent on March 24.

They face an UE Sant Andreu side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with CF Badalona, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 47 goals in 28 games so far this season, UE Sant Andreu are the league's highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Lleida Esportiu are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Lleida Esportiu's nine.

Over the last two years, Lleida Esportiu and UE Sant Andreu have played each other twice. UE Sant Andreu won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when they played out a 1-1 draw.
