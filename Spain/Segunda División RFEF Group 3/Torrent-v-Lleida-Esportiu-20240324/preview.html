Torrent v Lleida Esportiu

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Torrent host Lleida Esportiu on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

In their last league match, on March 16, Torrent lost to CF Badalona 3-1 away, with their goal scored by Christian Albert Simo.

Lleida Esportiu won, 3-0 at home against SD Formentera on March 17, with Jon Bakero, Juan Camilo Becerra Maya and Manuel Jesús Vázquez Florido on the scoresheet.

With 38 goals in 27 games so far this season, Lleida Esportiu are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Torrent, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The visitors are second in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn three, earning 51 points.

The home team are six places behind Lleida Esportiu in eighth, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

Torrent are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, Lleida Esportiu's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Torrent's seven.
