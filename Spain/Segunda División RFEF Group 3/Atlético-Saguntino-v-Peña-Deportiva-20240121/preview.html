Atlético Saguntino v Peña Deportiva

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Nou Camp de Morvedre
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 11.00am

Peña Deportiva travel to Atlético Saguntino on early Sunday in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten UD Alzira 3-1 at home.

Atlético Saguntino, meanwhile, drew their last match, 0-0 against Manresa.

With 24 goals in 18 games so far this season, Peña Deportiva are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Atlético Saguntino, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Atlético Saguntino's 11.

In the last three years, Atlético Saguntino and Peña Deportiva have played each other on three occasions. Peña Deportiva won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Atlético Saguntino are 11th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won six and drawn five, earning 23 points.

Peña Deportiva are two places ahead of the home team in ninth, with six wins and six draws putting them on 24 points.
