CE Europa v Espanyol B

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

CE Europa host Espanyol B on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

In their last league match, on April 28, CE Europa beat Lleida Esportiu 1-0 away, with their goal scored by Adrià Gené Sans.

Espanyol B also won, 3-1 at home against Cerdanyola del Vallès, with Roger Martínez Santamaría (two) and Francisco Javier Hernandez Coarasa on the scoresheet.

With 57 goals in 33 games so far this season, CE Europa are the league's highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Espanyol B, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home side are second in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn 12, earning 60 points.

The visitors are six places behind CE Europa in eighth, with 12 wins and 10 draws putting them on 46 points.

In the last three years, CE Europa and Espanyol B have played each other on three occasions. Espanyol B won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on January 7, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

CE Europa are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Espanyol B's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' 11.
