SD Formentera v UD Alzira

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Municipal
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 10.00am

SD Formentera host UD Alzira on Sunday at the Estadio Municipal in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

In their last league match, on February 4, Formentera lost to Terrassa 2-1 away.

Alzira drew, 2-2 at home against Espanyol B.

With 25 goals in 21 games so far this season, Formentera are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding at an average rate too, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Alzira are also average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are 13th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won seven and drawn five, earning 26 points.

The away team are two places ahead of Formentera in 11th, with six wins and nine draws putting them on 27 points.

In the last five years, Formentera and Alzira have played each other on four occasions. Formentera won two of them, Alzira one, and they drew once.

On average, Formentera scored 1.8 goals and Alzira 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 1, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Formentera are in reasonable form in Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Alzira's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the home side's 11.
