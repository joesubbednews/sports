UE Sant Andreu v UD Alzira

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Camp Municipal Narcís Sala
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

UE Sant Andreu host UD Alzira on Sunday at Camp Municipal Narcís Sala in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

In their last league match, on February 18, UE Sant Andreu beat Terrassa 1-0 away, with their goal scored by Alexis García Gallego.

Alzira drew, 1-1 at home against Torrent.

With 38 goals in 23 games so far this season, UE Sant Andreu are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Alzira, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are fifth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn five, earning 38 points.

The away side are three places behind UE Sant Andreu in eighth, with seven wins and 10 draws putting them on 31 points.

UE Sant Andreu are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Alzira's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the home team's nine.
