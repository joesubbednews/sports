CF Badalona v Peña Deportiva

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.00am

CF Badalona face Peña Deportiva on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Badalona are unbeaten in nine, with three wins and six draws, ahead of the 11am kick-off.

They face a Peña Deportiva team who have won four and drawn one over the same number of games.

Badalona are second in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn nine, earning 39 points.

Peña Deportiva are four places behind the home team in sixth, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 27 goals in 21 games so far this season, Badalona are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

The visitors are also above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Badalona and Peña Deportiva have played each other on seven occasions. Badalona won four of them and Peña Deportiva three.

On average, Badalona scored 1.6 goals and Peña Deportiva 1.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 1, when Badalona won 3-1 away.

Badalona's last match was on February 4, a 2-1 win against Manresa.

Peña Deportiva lost 1-0 against Valencia B last time out, also on February 4.
