Lleida Esportiu v SD Formentera

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Camp d'Esports de Lleida
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Lleida Esportiu host SD Formentera in Sunday's early match at Camp d'Esports de Lleida looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Lleida Esportiu, who sit third in the league after 26 games, fell to a 3-1 away defeat to Espanyol B on March 9.

They face a Formentera side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with CF Badalona, and who sit eighth in the table.

With 35 goals in 26 games so far this season, Lleida Esportiu are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Formentera, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Lleida Esportiu and Formentera have played each other on seven occasions. Lleida Esportiu won three of them and Formentera four.

On average, Lleida Esportiu scored 0.9 goals and Formentera 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Formentera won 1-0 at home.

The home side are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Lleida Esportiu's nine.
