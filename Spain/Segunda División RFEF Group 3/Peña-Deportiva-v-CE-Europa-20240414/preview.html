Peña Deportiva v CE Europa

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Campo Municipal del Santa Eulalia del Río
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 11.00am

CE Europa travel to Peña Deportiva on early Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Manresa 4-0 at home.

Peña Deportiva, meanwhile, drew their last match, 1-1 against Cerdanyola del Vallès.

With 49 goals in 29 games so far this season, CE Europa are the league's joint-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Peña Deportiva are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The away team are fifth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 10, earning 52 points.

The hosts are five places behind CE Europa in 10th, with 11 wins and seven draws putting them on 40 points.

In the last three years, Peña Deportiva and CE Europa have played each other on three occasions. CE Europa won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 3, when CE Europa won 6-0 at home.

Peña Deportiva are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, CE Europa's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Peña Deportiva's 10.
