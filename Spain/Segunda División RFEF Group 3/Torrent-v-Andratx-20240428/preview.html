Torrent v Andratx

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Andratx travel to Torrent on early Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with SD Formentera 0-0 at home.

Torrent also drew their last match, 0-0 against UE Sant Andreu.

With 36 goals in 31 games so far this season, Torrent are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Andratx are also average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are seventh in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn eight, earning 44 points.

The away side are two places behind Torrent in ninth, with 11 wins and 10 draws putting them on 43 points.

Torrent are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Andratx's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's eight.
