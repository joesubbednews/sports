CE Europa v Valencia B

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 11.00am

CE Europa are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday's early kick-off against Valencia B.

CE Europa, who sit third in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3 with 23 games played, are priced at 1.4 to seal victory.

Sitting seven places and 14 points behind them in the table, Valencia B are 6.9 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 3.6.

With 39 goals in 23 games so far this season, CE Europa are the league's highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Valencia B are also above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home side are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to CE Europa's 12.

CE Europa's last match was on February 18, a 2-1 win against Cerdanyola del Vallès, with Marc Vales González getting the goals for CE Europa.

Valencia B lost 2-0 against CF Badalona last time out, also on Sunday.
