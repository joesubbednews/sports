Torrent v Terrassa

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Terrassa face Torrent on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Terrassa are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 11am kick-off.

They face a Torrent team who have won three and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 21 goals in 21 games so far this season, Torrent are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Terrassa, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are seventh in the table after 21 games, of which they have won eight and drawn five, earning 29 points.

The away side are three places behind Torrent in 10th, with six wins and nine draws putting them on 27 points.

Torrent's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 loss against La Nucía.

Terrassa beat SD Formentera 2-1 last time out, also on February 4.
