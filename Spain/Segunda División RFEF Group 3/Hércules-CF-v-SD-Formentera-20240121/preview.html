Hércules CF v SD Formentera

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio José Rico Pérez
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 11.00am

Hércules CF host SD Formentera in Sunday's early match at the Estadio José Rico Pérez looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Hércules, who sit second in the league after 18 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to Espanyol B on January 14.

They face a Formentera side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with CE Europa, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 29 goals in 18 games so far this season, Hércules are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Formentera, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Hércules and Formentera have played each other on three occasions. Hércules won two of them and Formentera one.

Their last meeting was on September 10, when Hércules won 2-1 away.

The home team are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
