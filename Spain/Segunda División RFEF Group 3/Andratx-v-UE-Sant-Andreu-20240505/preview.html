Andratx v UE Sant Andreu

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Sa Plana
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Andratx host UE Sant Andreu on Sunday at the Estadio Sa Plana in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

In their last league match, on April 28, Andratx drew with Torrent 1-1 away, with their goal scored by Gerardo Bonet Fernández.

UE Sant Andreu also drew, 0-0 at home against Valencia B.

With 50 goals in 33 games so far this season, UE Sant Andreu are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Andratx, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn 10, earning 55 points.

The home team are five places behind UE Sant Andreu in 10th, with 11 wins and 11 draws putting them on 44 points.

Andratx are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, UE Sant Andreu's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Andratx's eight.
