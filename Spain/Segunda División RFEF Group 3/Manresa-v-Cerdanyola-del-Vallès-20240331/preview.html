Manresa v Cerdanyola del Vallès

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling Manresa face Cerdanyola del Vallès on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-1 victory over Penya Independent on March 24, Manresa sit 17th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

They travel to play a Cerdanyola del Vallès side 15th in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Atlético Saguntino.

Manresa are in terrible form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Cerdanyola del Vallès's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' one.

With 21 goals in 28 games so far this season, Manresa are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.
