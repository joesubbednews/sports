Lleida Esportiu v Manresa

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Camp d'Esports de Lleida
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Lleida Esportiu host Manresa in Sunday's match at Camp d'Esports de Lleida looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Lleida Esportiu, who sit third in the league after 21 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Atlético Saguntino on February 4.

They face a Manresa side who also lost their last match, a 2-1 defeat to CF Badalona, and who sit 15th in the table.

Lleida Esportiu are in bad form in Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

But with no wins and two draws over that period, Manresa's form is even worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the home side's four.

With 18 goals in 21 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Lleida Esportiu, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

In the last three years, Lleida Esportiu and Manresa have played each other on three occasions. Manresa won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on October 1, when Manresa won 1-0 at home.
