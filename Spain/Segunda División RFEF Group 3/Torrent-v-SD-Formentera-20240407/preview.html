Torrent v SD Formentera

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 11.00am

SD Formentera travel to Torrent on early Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Valencia B 4-2 away.

Torrent also lost their last match, 1-0 against Espanyol B.

Formentera are 14th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

Torrent are five places ahead of the away team in ninth, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

With 32 goals in 29 games so far this season, Formentera are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have also conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Torrent are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Formentera's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Torrent's seven.
