Atlético Saguntino v CE Europa

Segunda División RFEF Group 3 | Estadio Nou Camp de Morvedre
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 4.30pm

Atlético Saguntino host CE Europa on Sunday at the Estadio Nou Camp de Morvedre in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

In their last league match, on March 24, Atlético Saguntino lost to Cerdanyola del Vallès 1-0 away.

CE Europa drew, 0-0 at home against UD Alzira.

With 44 goals in 27 games so far this season, CE Europa are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Atlético Saguntino, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are third in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn 10, earning 49 points.

The home side are 11 places behind CE Europa in 14th, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

Atlético Saguntino are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, CE Europa's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Atlético Saguntino's seven.
