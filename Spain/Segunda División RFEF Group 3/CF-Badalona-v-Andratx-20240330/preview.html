CF Badalona v Andratx

Segunda División RFEF Group 3
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

CF Badalona face Andratx on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 3.

Badalona are unbeaten in 16, with eight wins and eight draws, ahead of the 4pm kick-off.

They face an Andratx team who have won five and drawn five over the same number of games.

Badalona are top of the table after 28 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn 11, earning 56 points.

Andratx are 10 places behind the hosts in 11th, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

With 37 goals in 28 games so far this season, Badalona are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last three years, Badalona and Andratx have played each other on three occasions. Andratx won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Andratx won 2-1 at home.

Badalona's last match was on Sunday, a 1-1 draw against UE Sant Andreu, with Isaiah Jeronimo Navarro Diaz getting the goal for Badalona.

Andratx lost 2-0 against Peña Deportiva last time out, on March 23.
