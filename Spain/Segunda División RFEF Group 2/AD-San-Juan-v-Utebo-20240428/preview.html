AD San Juan v Utebo

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio San Juan
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 11.00am

AD San Juan host Utebo in Sunday's early match at the Estadio San Juan looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

San Juan, who sit 15th in the league after 32 games, fell to a 0-3 home defeat to UD Mutilvera on April 20.

They face an Utebo side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-0 victory against Brea, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 28 goals in 32 games so far this season, San Juan are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Utebo, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home side are in good form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to San Juan's 13.

In the last three years, San Juan and Utebo have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on December 17, when Utebo won 3-2 at home.
