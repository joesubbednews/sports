CD Valle Egüés v Real Zaragoza B

Segunda División RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Real Zaragoza B are strong favourites to take all three points despite CD Valle Egüés's home advantage in Sunday's match.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

Valle Egüés's last match was on Sunday, a 6-2 loss against Deportivo Alaves B, with Pablo Manuel Palacios Lezana getting the goals for Valle Egüés.

Zaragoza B beat CD Izarra 3-1 last time out, also on March 10, with Adrian Liso Lahoz and Marcos Cuenca Aranda on the scoresheet.
