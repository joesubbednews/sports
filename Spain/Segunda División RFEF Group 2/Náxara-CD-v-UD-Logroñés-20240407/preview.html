Náxara CD v UD Logroñés

Segunda División RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

Relegation candidates Náxara CD face a challenge against high-flying UD Logroñés on Sunday.

Náxara CD are 17th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2 table, and have picked up five wins and six draws in their 29 games to date.

Logroñés, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 58 points, having won 16 and drawn 10, and are 11 points behind table-toppers Athletic Club B.

With 58 goals in 29 games so far this season, Logroñés are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Náxara CD, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Náxara CD's 10.

Náxara CD's last match was on March 30, a 1-0 loss against Brea.

Logroñés beat CD Tudelano 3-1 last time out, on Sunday, with Aitor Seguín Cid, Julen Monreal Perales and Unai García Sebastián on the scoresheet.
