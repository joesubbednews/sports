UD Logroñés v Real Zaragoza B

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Nuevo Municipal Las Gaunas
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 5.00pm

UD Logroñés host Real Zaragoza B in Sunday's late match at the Estadio Nuevo Municipal Las Gaunas looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

Logroñés, who sit third in the league after 22 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Deportivo Alaves B on February 11.

They face a Zaragoza B side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-0 victory against Brea, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 46 goals in 22 games so far this season, Logroñés are the league's highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 12 goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Zaragoza B are also above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

The home side are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Logroñés's 11.
