Deportivo Alaves B v CD Valle Egüés

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Ciudad Deportiva José Luis Compañón
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 5.00pm

Deportivo Alaves B and CD Valle Egüés both come into Sunday's late kick-off at Ciudad Deportiva José Luis Compañón in poor form, having picked up four and two points respectively in their last six games.

Deportivo Alaves B have won one and drawn one of the last six, while Valle Egüés have taken two draws and four losses.

With 41 goals in 25 games so far this season, Deportivo Alaves B are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Valle Egüés, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away team are 15th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won five and drawn five, earning 20 points.

The hosts are 10 places ahead of Valle Egüés in fifth, with 13 wins and three draws putting them on 42 points.

Deportivo Alaves B's last match was on March 3, a 1-0 loss against CD Izarra.

Valle Egüés lost 2-0 against Utebo last time out, on March 2.
