Barakaldo CF v Brea

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Nuevo Lasesarre
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 5.00pm

Relegation candidates Brea face a challenge away against high-flying Barakaldo CF at the Estadio Nuevo Lasesarre on Sunday.

Brea are 16th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2 table, and have picked up four wins and six draws in their 25 games to date.

Barakaldo, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 54 points, having won 15 and drawn nine, and are six points behind table-toppers Athletic Club B.

With 11 goals in 25 games so far this season, Brea are the league's lowest scorers with 0.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Barakaldo, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Barakaldo's 12.

Barakaldo's last match was on March 2, a 1-0 win against Gernika Club, with Markel Artetxe Artetxe getting the goal for Barakaldo.

Brea lost 1-0 against Arenas de Getxo last time out, on March 3.
