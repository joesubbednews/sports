Brea v Athletic Club B

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Relegation candidates Brea face a challenge against high-flying Athletic Club B at Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena on Sunday.

Brea are 17th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2 table, and have picked up five wins and six draws in their 33 games to date.

Athletic Club B, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 79 points, having won 24 and drawn seven.

With 63 goals in 33 games so far this season, Athletic Club B are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Brea, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Brea's three.

Brea's last match was on April 28, a 4-1 loss against UD Logroñés.

Athletic Club B beat Gernika Club 3-1 last time out, also on April 28, with Aingeru Olabarrieta Capelo, Alejandro Rego Mora and Urko Iruretagoiena Lertxundi on the scoresheet.
