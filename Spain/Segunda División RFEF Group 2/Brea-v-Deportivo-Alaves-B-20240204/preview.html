Brea v Deportivo Alaves B

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Brea face Deportivo Alaves B at Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-0 victory over CD Valle Egüés on January 27, Brea sit 16th in Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

They travel to play a Deportivo Alaves B side fourth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Gernika Club, on January 28.

With 38 goals in 20 games so far this season, Deportivo Alaves B are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Brea, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The home side are in bad form in Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Brea's three.

In the last three years, Brea and Deportivo Alaves B have played each other on three occasions. Deportivo Alaves B won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Deportivo Alaves B won 5-0 at home.
