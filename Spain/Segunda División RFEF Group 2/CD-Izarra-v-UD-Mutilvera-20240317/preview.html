CD Izarra v UD Mutilvera

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Merkatondoa
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 3.30pm

CD Izarra host UD Mutilvera on Sunday at the Estadio Merkatondoa in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

In their last league match, on March 10, Izarra lost to Real Zaragoza B 3-1 away.

Mutilvera drew, 0-0 at home against UD Barbastro on March 9.

In the last 10 years, Izarra and Mutilvera have played each other on nine occasions. They won three each, and they drew three times.

On average, Izarra scored 0.8 goals and Mutilvera 0.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Mutilvera won 1-0 at home.

Izarra are 14th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won six and drawn six, earning 24 points.

Mutilvera are two places ahead of the home side in 12th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 17 goals in 26 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Izarra are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Izarra are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, Mutilvera's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.
