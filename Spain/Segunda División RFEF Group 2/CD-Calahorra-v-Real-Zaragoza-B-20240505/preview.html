CD Calahorra v Real Zaragoza B

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio La Planilla
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Real Zaragoza B travel to CD Calahorra on early Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Barakaldo CF 4-0 at home.

Calahorra also lost their last match, 3-2 against Deportivo Alaves B, with their goal scored by Ignacio Ruiz Lluch.

With 48 goals in 33 games so far this season, Zaragoza B are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Calahorra, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn 11, earning 56 points.

The home team are five places behind Zaragoza B in 10th, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

Calahorra are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Zaragoza B's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Calahorra's six.
