Brea v Náxara CD

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Struggling Brea face Náxara CD at Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-2 victory over CD Tudelano on March 24, Brea sit bottom of the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

They travel to play a Náxara CD side 17th in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Gernika Club.

Brea are in terrible form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Náxara CD's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' one.

With 11 goals in 28 games so far this season, Brea are the league's lowest scorers with 0.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.
