CD Calahorra v Utebo

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio La Planilla
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 3.30pm

CD Calahorra face Utebo on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

Calahorra are unbeaten in five, with two wins and three draws, ahead of the 3.30pm kick-off.

They face an Utebo team who have won one and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 16 goals in 21 games so far this season, Calahorra are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Utebo, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The away side are fourth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

The hosts are seven places behind Utebo in 11th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

Calahorra's last match was on February 4, a 1-1 draw against Barakaldo CF.

Utebo beat UD Barbastro 1-0 last time out, also on February 4.
