Real Zaragoza B v Real Sociedad III

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Ciudad Deportiva del Real Zaragoza
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 11.30am

Real Sociedad III travel to Ciudad Deportiva del Real Zaragoza looking to secure a first win in six Segunda División RFEF Group 2 games against Real Zaragoza B on Sunday.

Sociedad III have lost two and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against UD Logroñés on December 3.

They face a Zaragoza B side who have won one and drawn three over that time.

With 20 goals in 19 games so far this season, Sociedad III are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Zaragoza B are also average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The home side are sixth in the table after 19 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

The visitors are one place behind Zaragoza B in seventh, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Zaragoza B's last match was on Sunday, a 2-2 draw against Náxara CD.

Sociedad III drew 2-2 with Deportivo Alaves B last time out, on January 20.
