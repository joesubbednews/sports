CD Calahorra v AD San Juan

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio La Planilla
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 0.00am

AD San Juan travel to the Estadio La Planilla looking to secure a first win in six Segunda División RFEF Group 2 games against CD Calahorra on Sunday.

San Juan have lost four and drawn one matches since they last earned three points – against CD Valle Egüés on November 18.

They face a Calahorra side who have won one and drawn two over that time.

San Juan are 17th in the table after 17 games, of which they have won three and drawn two, earning 11 points.

Calahorra are six places ahead of the visitors in 11th, with five wins and three draws putting them on 18 points.

With 11 goals in 17 games so far this season, the home team are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

San Juan are also below average scorers, with 0.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Calahorra's last match was on January 7, a 0-0 draw against Real Zaragoza B.

San Juan drew 1-1 with Arenas de Getxo last time out, also on January 7.
