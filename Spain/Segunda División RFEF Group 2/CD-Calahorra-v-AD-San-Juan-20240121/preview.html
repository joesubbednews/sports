CD Calahorra v AD San Juan

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio La Planilla
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 3.30pm

AD San Juan come to the Estadio La Planilla to play CD Calahorra on Sunday in terrible form, having collected just one point from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn just one of their last six fixtures, while Calahorra have two wins and two draws.

San Juan are 17th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won three and drawn two, earning 11 points.

Calahorra are seven places ahead of the visitors in 10th, with six wins and three draws putting them on 21 points.

With 11 goals in 18 games so far this season, San Juan are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Calahorra's last match was on January 13, a 2-0 win against UD Mutilvera.

San Juan lost 2-0 against Barakaldo CF last time out, also on January 13.
