Real Sociedad III v Náxara CD

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Zubieta XXI
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 5.00pm

Struggling Náxara CD face Real Sociedad III away at the Estadio Zubieta XXI on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Utebo on March 10, Náxara CD sit 17th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

They travel to play a Sociedad III side seventh in the standings, who were held in their last match, 0-0 against CD Tudelano.

With 18 goals in 26 games so far this season, Náxara CD are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Sociedad III are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Sociedad III's six.

In the last three years, Sociedad III and Náxara CD have played each other on three occasions. Sociedad III won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on November 5, when Sociedad III won 1-0 away.
