UD Barbastro v CD Tudelano

Segunda División RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

UD Barbastro face CD Tudelano on Sunday looking to secure a first win in eight Segunda División RFEF Group 2 games.

Barbastro have lost two and drawn five matches since they last earned three points – against CD Valle Egüés on February 18.

They face a Tudelano side who have won two and drawn two over that time.

With 21 goals in 30 games so far this season, Barbastro are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.7 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Tudelano, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are 11th in the table after 30 games, of which they have won eight and drawn 13, earning 37 points.

The visitors are one place ahead of Barbastro in 10th, with nine wins and 11 draws putting them on 38 points.

Barbastro's last match was on April 7, a 1-0 loss against CD Calahorra.

Tudelano lost 2-1 against Athletic Club B last time out, on April 5, with Gianluca Simeone on the scoresheet.
