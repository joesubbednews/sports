CD Izarra v UD Barbastro

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Merkatondoa
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 3.30pm

CD Izarra face UD Barbastro at the Estadio Merkatondoa on Sunday looking to secure a first win in seven Segunda División RFEF Group 2 games.

Izarra have lost three and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against CD Tudelano on December 9.

They face a Barbastro side who have won three and drawn two over that time.

Izarra are 15th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won four and drawn five, earning 17 points.

Barbastro are six places ahead of the home team in ninth, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

With 17 goals in 21 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Izarra are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Izarra's last match was on February 3, a 2-1 loss against Athletic Club B.

Barbastro lost 1-0 against Utebo last time out, on February 4.
