CD Tudelano v CD Izarra

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal Ciudad de Tudela
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

CD Tudelano host CD Izarra on Sunday at the Estadio Municipal Ciudad de Tudela in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

In their last league match, on April 14, Tudelano drew with UD Barbastro 0-0 away.

Izarra won, 2-1 at home against CD Calahorra, with Martín Bengoa Díez scoring their goals.

In the last 10 years, Tudelano and Izarra have played each other on 15 occasions. Tudelano won five of them, Izarra three, and they drew seven times.

On average, Tudelano scored 1.0 goal and Izarra 0.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 9, when Izarra won 1-0 at home.

With 28 goals in 31 games so far this season, Izarra are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Tudelano, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The visitors are 13th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won nine and drawn six, earning 33 points.

The home team are three places ahead of Izarra in 10th, with nine wins and 12 draws putting them on 39 points.

Tudelano are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Izarra's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Tudelano's eight.
