Náxara CD v Barakaldo CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Mid-season relegation candidates Náxara CD face a challenge against high-flying Barakaldo CF on Sunday.

Náxara CD are rooted to the bottom of the Segunda División RFEF Group 2 table, and have picked up just two wins and four draws in their 22 games to date.

Barakaldo, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 47 points, having won 13 and drawn eight, and are six points behind table-toppers Athletic Club B.

Náxara CD are in terrible form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Barakaldo's form is much better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to the hosts' two.

With 35 goals in 22 games so far this season, the away side are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Náxara CD, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Náxara CD's last match was on February 11, a 4-0 loss against Arenas de Getxo.

Barakaldo beat CD Tudelano 3-1 last time out, also on February 11, with Ander Fernández Pacheco, Endika Bujan de Rueda and Íñigo Orozco Andonegi on the scoresheet.
