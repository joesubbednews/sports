CD Calahorra v UD Barbastro

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio La Planilla
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

UD Barbastro travel to the Estadio La Planilla looking to secure a first win in seven Segunda División RFEF Group 2 games against CD Calahorra on Sunday.

Barbastro have lost one and drawn five matches since they last earned three points – against CD Valle Egüés on February 18.

They face a Calahorra side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

With 21 goals in 29 games so far this season, Barbastro are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.7 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Calahorra are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home team are 10th in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn seven, earning 37 points.

The visitors are one place ahead of Calahorra in ninth, with eight wins and 13 draws putting them on the same number of points.

Calahorra's last match was on March 30, a 2-1 loss against Athletic Club B, with Iker Hernández Ezquerro getting the goal for Calahorra.

Barbastro lost 2-0 against Barakaldo CF last time out, also on March 30.
