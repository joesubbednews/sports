Barakaldo CF v Real Sociedad III

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Nuevo Lasesarre
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 5.00pm

Barakaldo CF face Real Sociedad III on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

Barakaldo are unbeaten in 12, with eight wins and four draws, ahead of the 5pm kick-off.

They face a Sociedad III team who have won four and drawn five over the same number of games.

Barakaldo are second in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn nine, earning 48 points.

Sociedad III are five places behind the home side in seventh, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

With 35 goals in 23 games so far this season, Barakaldo are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Barakaldo's last match was on February 18, a 0-0 draw against Náxara CD.

Sociedad III drew 2-2 with Arenas de Getxo last time out, also on February 18, with Eñaut Lete Larrañaga and Manex Gibelalde Mujika on the scoresheet.
