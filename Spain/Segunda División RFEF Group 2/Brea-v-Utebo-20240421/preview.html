Brea v Utebo

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Brea are on a poor run ahead of hosting Utebo at Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena on Sunday, with just three points collected from their last six games.

Brea have picked up just one win in their last six Segunda División RFEF Group 2 games, and face an Utebo side whose last six games have brought two wins and two draws.

Brea are 17th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won five and drawn six, earning 21 points.

Utebo are 12 places ahead of the home team in fifth, with 15 wins and 10 draws putting them on 55 points.

With 14 goals in 31 games so far this season, Brea are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 45 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Brea and Utebo have played each other on nine occasions. Brea won two of them, Utebo five, and they drew twice.

On average, Brea scored 0.6 goals and Utebo 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 10, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

Brea's last match was on April 14, a 2-0 loss against Gernika Club.

Utebo beat UD Mutilvera 2-0 last time out, on April 13, with Angel Pérez Hidalgo and Luis Ángel Forcén Sacramento on the scoresheet.
