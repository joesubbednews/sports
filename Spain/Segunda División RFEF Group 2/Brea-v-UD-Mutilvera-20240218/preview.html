Brea v UD Mutilvera

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.30pm

UD Mutilvera travel to Brea on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Gernika Club 3-0 at home.

Brea, meanwhile, lost their last match, 2-0 against Real Zaragoza B.

Brea are 16th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won four and drawn five, earning 17 points.

Mutilvera are four places ahead of the home team in 12th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

Brea are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Brea's five.

In the last three years, Brea and Mutilvera have played each other on three occasions. Brea won two of them and Mutilvera one.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Brea won 2-1 away.

With 11 goals in 22 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Mutilvera are also below average scorers, with 0.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.
