Deportivo Alaves B v UD Logroñés

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Ciudad Deportiva José Luis Compañón
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Sunday's early match between Deportivo Alaves B and UD Logroñés promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of Segunda División RFEF Group 2's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Ciudad Deportiva José Luis Compañón, Deportivo Alaves B and Logroñés sit third and top in the goal-scoring charts, with 38 and 46 goals respectively.

Logroñés are third in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn nine, earning 42 points.

Deportivo Alaves B are two places behind the away side in fifth, with 12 wins and two draws putting them on 38 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Logroñés's form is better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Deportivo Alaves B's 10.

Deportivo Alaves B's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 loss against Brea.

Logroñés beat CD Valle Egüés 3-2 last time out, also on Sunday.
