CD Valle Egüés v AD San Juan

Segunda División RFEF Group 2
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling CD Valle Egüés face AD San Juan on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-1 victory over UD Mutilvera on March 24, Valle Egüés sit 16th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

They travel to play a San Juan side 15th in the standings, who also won their last match, 4-0 against CD Izarra, on March 23.

Valle Egüés are in terrible form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, San Juan's form is much better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' two.

In the last 10 years, Valle Egüés and San Juan have played each other on six occasions. Valle Egüés won one of them, San Juan three, and they drew twice.

On average, Valle Egüés scored 0.8 goals and San Juan 1.7 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when San Juan won 2-0 at home.

With 21 goals in 28 games so far this season, Valle Egüés are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.
