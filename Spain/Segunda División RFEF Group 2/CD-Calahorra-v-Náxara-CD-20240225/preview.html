CD Calahorra v Náxara CD

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio La Planilla
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 3.30pm

CD Calahorra are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday's kick-off against Náxara CD.

Calahorra, who sit 10th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2 with 23 games played, are priced at 1.2 to seal victory at the Estadio La Planilla.

Sitting eight places and 19 points behind them in the table, Náxara CD are 17.0 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 4.6.

Calahorra are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With no wins and three draws over that period, Náxara CD's form is much worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the hosts' 12.

With 14 goals in 23 games so far this season, the away team are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Calahorra are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Calahorra's last match was on Sunday, a 1-1 draw against CD Tudelano, with Iker Hernández Ezquerro getting the goal for Calahorra.

Náxara CD drew 0-0 with Barakaldo CF last time out, also on Sunday.
