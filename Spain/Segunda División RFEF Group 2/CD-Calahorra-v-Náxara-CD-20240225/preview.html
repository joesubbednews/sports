CD Calahorra v Náxara CD

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio La Planilla
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 3.30pm

CD Calahorra face Náxara CD on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

Calahorra are unbeaten in seven, with three wins and four draws, ahead of the 3.30pm kick-off.

They face a Náxara CD team who have drawn three and lost four over the same number of games.

Náxara CD are bottom of the table after 23 games, of which they have won two and drawn five, earning 11 points.

Calahorra are eight places ahead of the visitors in 10th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 14 goals in 23 games so far this season, Náxara CD are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Calahorra's last match was on Sunday, a 1-1 draw against CD Tudelano, with Iker Hernández Ezquerro getting the goal for Calahorra.

Náxara CD drew 0-0 with Barakaldo CF last time out, also on Sunday.
