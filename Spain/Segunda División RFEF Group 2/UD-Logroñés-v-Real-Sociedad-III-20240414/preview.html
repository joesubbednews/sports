UD Logroñés v Real Sociedad III

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Nuevo Municipal Las Gaunas
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

UD Logroñés host Real Sociedad III at the Estadio Nuevo Municipal Las Gaunas on late Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

Logroñés have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face a Sociedad III side who also won their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 61 goals in 30 games so far this season, Logroñés are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Sociedad III, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home side are third in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn 10, earning 61 points.

The visitors are four places behind Logroñés in seventh, with 11 wins and nine draws putting them on 42 points.

Logroñés's last match was on April 7, a 3-0 win against Náxara CD, with Andoni Ugarte Mendizábal (two) and Carlos Bravo Expósito getting the goals for Logroñés.

Sociedad III beat Brea 3-2 last time out, on April 6, with Ekain Orobengoa Arbelaiz, Jakes Gorosabel Añorga and Manex Gibelalde Mujika on the scoresheet.
