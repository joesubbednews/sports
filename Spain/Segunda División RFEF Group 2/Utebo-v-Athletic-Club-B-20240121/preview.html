Utebo v Athletic Club B

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Santa Ana
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 4.30pm

Athletic Club B face Utebo on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

Athletic Club B are unbeaten in 16, with 14 wins and two draws, ahead of the 4.30pm kick-off.

They face an Utebo team who have won nine and drawn five over the same number of games.

Athletic Club B are top of the table after 18 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn three, earning 45 points.

Utebo are four places behind the visitors in fifth, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

With 37 goals in 18 games so far this season, Athletic Club B are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in six goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

The home team are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Utebo's last match was on January 14, a 1-1 draw against Arenas de Getxo.

Athletic Club B drew 0-0 with UD Logroñés last time out, also on January 14.
