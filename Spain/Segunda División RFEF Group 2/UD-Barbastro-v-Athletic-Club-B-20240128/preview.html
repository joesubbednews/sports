UD Barbastro v Athletic Club B

Segunda División RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 3.30pm

Athletic Club B face UD Barbastro on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 2.

Athletic Club B are unbeaten in 17, with 14 wins and three draws, ahead of the 3.30pm kick-off.

They face a Barbastro team who have won seven and drawn five over the same number of games.

Athletic Club B are top of the table after 19 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn four, earning 46 points.

Barbastro are seven places behind the away team in eighth, with seven wins and seven draws putting them on 28 points.

With 37 goals in 19 games so far this season, Athletic Club B are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in six goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have also conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Barbastro's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against UD Logroñés.

Athletic Club B drew 0-0 with Utebo last time out, also on Sunday.
