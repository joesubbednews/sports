Gernika Club v AD San Juan

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Urbieta
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 0.00am

AD San Juan travel to the Estadio Urbieta looking to secure a first win in 10 Segunda División RFEF Group 2 games against Gernika Club on Sunday.

San Juan have lost five and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against CD Valle Egüés on November 18.

They face a Gernika Club side who have won three and drawn two over that time.

San Juan are 17th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won three and drawn five, earning 14 points.

Gernika Club are seven places ahead of the visitors in 10th, with six wins and nine draws putting them on 27 points.

With 13 goals in 21 games so far this season, San Juan are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last five years, Gernika Club and San Juan have played each other on five occasions. They won one each, and they drew three times.

On average, Gernika Club scored 0.6 goals and San Juan 0.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Gernika Club's last match was on February 4, a 1-0 loss against Real Zaragoza B.

San Juan drew 0-0 with Náxara CD last time out, also on February 4.
