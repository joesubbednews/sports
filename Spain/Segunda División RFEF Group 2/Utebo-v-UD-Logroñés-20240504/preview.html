Utebo v UD Logroñés

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Santa Ana
Saturday, 4 May 2024 @ 5.30pm

Two of the Segunda División RFEF Group 2's top sides face each other at the Estadio Santa Ana in Saturday's late kick-off, when fourth-placed Utebo host third-placed UD Logroñés.

Utebo have picked up 17 wins and 10 draws from 33 games so far this season, and sit seven points below the visitors going into the 5.30pm match.

Logroñés, meanwhile, have won 19 and drawn 11, picking up 68 points.

With 67 goals in 33 games so far this season, Logroñés are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Utebo are also above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to Utebo's 13.

Utebo's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 win against AD San Juan.

Logroñés beat Brea 4-1 last time out, also on Sunday, with Unai García Sebastián (two), Alejandro Miguel Arias De Haro and Cristian Fernández Linde on the scoresheet.
