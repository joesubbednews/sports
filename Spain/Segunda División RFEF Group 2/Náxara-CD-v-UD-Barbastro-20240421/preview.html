Náxara CD v UD Barbastro

Segunda División RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

Náxara CD welcome UD Barbastro on Sunday looking to pick up points to end their three-game losing streak.

Náxara CD's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Segunda División RFEF Group 2 matches, while their opponents have earned four from a possible 18.

Náxara CD are bottom of the table after 31 games, of which they have won five and drawn six, earning 21 points.

Barbastro are seven places ahead of the home team in 11th, with eight wins and 14 draws putting them on 38 points.

With 20 goals in 31 games so far this season, Náxara CD are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 52 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Náxara CD's last match was on April 13, a 4-0 loss against Athletic Club B.

Barbastro drew 0-0 with CD Tudelano last time out, on April 14.
