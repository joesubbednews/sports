Brea v CD Calahorra

Segunda División RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 11.00am

CD Calahorra are strong favourites to take all three points despite Brea's home advantage in Sunday's early match at Estadio Municipal de Piedrabuena.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.77 on Calahorra sealing the win, with the visitors sitting ninth in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2 table.

Brea, who are 18th in the league and 16 points behind Calahorra, are priced at 4.33 to win. A draw is set at 3.1.

Brea are in bad form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Calahorra's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With 11 goals in 26 games so far this season, Brea are the league's lowest scorers with 0.4 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Brea's last match was on Sunday, a 2-0 loss against Barakaldo CF.

Calahorra lost 1-0 against Gernika Club last time out, also on Sunday.
