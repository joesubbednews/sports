Náxara CD v CD Valle Egüés

Segunda División RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as bottom of the table Náxara CD host 16th-placed CD Valle Egüés.

Náxara CD have picked up 21 points from their first 21 Segunda División RFEF Group 2 games, with five wins and six draws.

That is the same number of points as Valle Egüés have collected, having also won five and drawn six.

Náxara CD are in bad form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 2, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

But with no wins and six losses over that period, Valle Egüés's form is even worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to the home team's three.

With 20 goals in 33 games so far this season, Náxara CD are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 58 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

Náxara CD's last match was on April 28, a 3-0 loss against CD Izarra.

Valle Egüés lost 3-0 against CD Tudelano last time out, also on April 28.
