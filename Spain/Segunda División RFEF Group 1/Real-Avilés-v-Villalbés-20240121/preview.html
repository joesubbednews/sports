Real Avilés v Villalbés

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Román Suárez Puerta
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 4.00pm

Real Avilés host Villalbés on Sunday at the Estadio Román Suárez Puerta in Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

In their last league match, on January 14, Avilés lost to Deportivo La Coruña B 1-0 away.

Villalbés drew, 0-0 at home against Real Oviedo B.

With 16 goals in 18 games so far this season, Villalbés are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Avilés are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have also conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Avilés's 10.

Avilés are 12th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won four and drawn nine, earning 21 points.

Villalbés are three places ahead of the hosts in ninth, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.
