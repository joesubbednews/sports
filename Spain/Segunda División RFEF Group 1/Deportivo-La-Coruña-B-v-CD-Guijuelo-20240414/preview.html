Deportivo La Coruña B v CD Guijuelo

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Ciudad Deportiva de Abegondo - Campo 2
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

CD Guijuelo travel to Deportivo La Coruña B on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Ourense CF 1-1 at home, with a goal from Asiel Mateo del Cerro.

Deportivo La Coruña B, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-2 against Cayón, with their goals scored by Martín Ochoa Ruiz and Alejandro Alfaro Cascales.

With 40 goals in 30 games so far this season, Deportivo La Coruña B are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Guijuelo, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The away side are fourth in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn eight, earning 47 points.

The hosts are eight places behind Guijuelo in 12th, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

Deportivo La Coruña B are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Guijuelo's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Deportivo La Coruña B's nine.
