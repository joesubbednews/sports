Real Oviedo B v Arandina CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio El Requexón
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Sunday's early match at the Estadio El Requexón sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 15th-ranked Real Oviedo B host bottom of the table Arandina CF.

Oviedo B have picked up 30 points from their first 30 Segunda División RFEF Group 1 games, with six wins and 12 draws.

That is eight points more than Arandina have collected, having won four and drawn 10.

Oviedo B are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Arandina's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' five.

With 26 goals in 29 games so far this season, Oviedo B are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Oviedo B's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 loss against Club Marino de Luanco.

Arandina drew 1-1 with Pontevedra CF last time out, on March 30, with Gonzalo Serrano Iranzo on the scoresheet.
