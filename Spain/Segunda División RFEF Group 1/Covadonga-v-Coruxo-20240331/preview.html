Covadonga v Coruxo

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Juan Antonio A. Rabanal
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Struggling Covadonga face Coruxo at the Estadio Juan Antonio A. Rabanal on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-0 victory over Cayón on March 17, Covadonga sit 16th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

They travel to play a Coruxo side ninth in the standings, who also won their last match, 1-0 against Club Marino de Luanco.

With 32 goals in 27 games so far this season, Covadonga are scoring more than average in the league with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Coruxo, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Covadonga's 10.

In the last three years, Covadonga and Coruxo have played each other on three occasions. Coruxo won all of them.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when Coruxo won 3-1 at home.
