Langreo v CD Guijuelo

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Nuevo Ganzábal
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 11.00am

CD Guijuelo travel to Langreo on early Sunday in Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Real Valladolid B 2-0 away.

Langreo, meanwhile, drew their last match, 0-0 against Cayón.

With 18 goals in 17 games so far this season, Guijuelo are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Langreo are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away side are sixth in the table after 17 games, of which they have won eight and drawn three, earning 27 points.

The hosts are one place behind Guijuelo in seventh, with seven wins and six draws putting them on the same number of points.

In the last three years, Langreo and Guijuelo have played each other on three occasions. Guijuelo won all of them.

Their last meeting was on September 2, when Guijuelo won 2-0 at home.

Langreo are in reasonable form in Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Guijuelo's form is slightly worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Langreo's 11.
