Zamora CF v Arandina CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Ruta de la Plata
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Zamora CF host Arandina CF in Sunday's early match at the Estadio Ruta de la Plata looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

Zamora, who sit third in the league after 33 games, fell to a 1-1 away defeat to Langreo on April 28.

They face an Arandina side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against Club Marino de Luanco, and who sit 15th in the table.

With 36 goals in 33 games so far this season, Arandina are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Zamora, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Zamora's nine.
