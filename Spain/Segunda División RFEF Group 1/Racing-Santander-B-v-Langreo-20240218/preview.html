Racing Santander B v Langreo

Segunda División RFEF Group 1
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.30am

Langreo travel looking to secure a first win in nine Segunda División RFEF Group 1 games against Racing Santander B on Sunday.

Langreo have lost two and drawn six matches since they last earned three points – against Gimnástica Torrelavega on December 3.

They face a Racing Santander B side who have won two and drawn four over that time.

With 32 goals in 22 games so far this season, Racing Santander B are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Langreo, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are fourth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

The away team are three places behind Racing Santander B in seventh, with seven wins and 10 draws putting them on 31 points.

In the last three years, Racing Santander B and Langreo have played each other on three occasions. They won one each, and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Langreo won 4-1 at home.

Racing Santander B's last match was on February 10, a 1-1 draw against Real Valladolid B.

Langreo lost 2-0 against Real Avilés last time out, on Sunday.
