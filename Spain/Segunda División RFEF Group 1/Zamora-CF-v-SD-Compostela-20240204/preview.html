Zamora CF v SD Compostela

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Ruta de la Plata
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 4.30pm

Zamora CF face SD Compostela on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

Zamora are unbeaten in five, with two wins and three draws, ahead of the 4.30pm kick-off.

They face a Compostela team who have won two and drawn one over the same number of games.

Zamora are third in the table after 19 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn five, earning 38 points.

Compostela are four places behind the hosts in seventh, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

In the last 10 years, Zamora and Compostela have played each other on eight occasions. Zamora won one of them, Compostela three, and they drew four times.

On average, Zamora scored 0.6 goals and Compostela 1.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 24, when Zamora won 1-0 away.

With 21 goals in 19 games so far this season, Zamora are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in nine goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

Zamora's last match was on January 21, a 2-0 win against Covadonga.

Compostela drew 0-0 with Arandina CF last time out, on January 20.
