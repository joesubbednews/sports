CD Guijuelo v Villalbés

Segunda División RFEF Group 1
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

Villalbés travel looking to secure a first win in six Segunda División RFEF Group 1 games against CD Guijuelo on Sunday.

Villalbés have lost one and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against Deportivo La Coruña B on March 9.

They face a Guijuelo side who have won two and drawn two over that time.

Guijuelo are fourth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn nine, earning 48 points.

Villalbés are nine places behind the home team in 13th, with seven wins and 13 draws putting them on 34 points.

With 21 goals in 31 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Guijuelo, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

Guijuelo's last match was on April 14, a 2-2 draw against Deportivo La Coruña B.

Villalbés drew 2-2 with Cayón last time out, on April 13, with José Luis Varela González and Nathaniel Nicholas on the scoresheet.
