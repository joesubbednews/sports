Langreo v Villalbés

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Nuevo Ganzábal
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

Langreo host Villalbés on Sunday at the Estadio Nuevo Ganzábal in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

In their last league match, on March 31, Langreo beat Deportivo La Coruña B 1-0 away, with their goal scored by Iago Novo Marta.

Villalbés drew, 0-0 at home against Real Valladolid B on March 30.

Langreo are fifth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn 12, earning 42 points.

Villalbés are nine places behind the home side in 14th, with seven wins and 11 draws putting them on 32 points.

Langreo are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Langreo's eight.

With 19 goals in 29 games so far this season, Villalbés are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.
