Zamora CF v Gimnástica Torrelavega

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Ruta de la Plata
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 5.00pm

Gimnástica Torrelavega travel to Zamora CF on late Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Villalbés 2-1 at home, with goals from Unai Hernández Lestón and Javier Delgado Quintana.

Zamora also won their last match, 1-0 against Real Avilés, with their goal scored by Luis Miguel Luengo Herrera.

With 34 goals in 28 games so far this season, Gimnástica Torrelavega are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Zamora, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

The hosts are third in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn nine, earning 54 points.

The away side are eight places behind Zamora in 11th, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

Zamora are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Gimnástica Torrelavega's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home team's nine.

In the last three years, Zamora and Gimnástica Torrelavega have played each other on three occasions. Gimnástica Torrelavega won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on November 19, when Gimnástica Torrelavega won 2-1 at home.
