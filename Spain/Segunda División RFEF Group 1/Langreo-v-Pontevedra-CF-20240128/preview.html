Langreo v Pontevedra CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Nuevo Ganzábal
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 4.00pm

Pontevedra CF face Langreo on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

Pontevedra are unbeaten in six, with four wins and two draws, ahead of the 4pm kick-off.

They face a Langreo team who have won one and drawn four over the same number of games.

With 42 goals in 19 games so far this season, Pontevedra are the league's highest scorers with 2.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Langreo, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away team are top of the table after 19 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn six, earning 39 points.

The hosts are four places behind Pontevedra in fifth, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

In the last 10 years, Langreo and Pontevedra have played each other on six occasions. Pontevedra won four of them and they drew twice.

On average, Langreo scored 0.8 goals and Pontevedra 2.3 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when Pontevedra won 6-0 at home.

Langreo's last match was on January 20, a 1-1 draw against Coruxo.

Pontevedra beat Real Valladolid B 3-1 last time out, on January 21.
