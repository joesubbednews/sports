Deportivo La Coruña B v Langreo

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Ciudad Deportiva de Abegondo - Campo 2
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 12.00pm

Deportivo La Coruña B host Langreo on Sunday at Ciudad Deportiva de Abegondo-Campo 2 in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

In their last league match, on March 17, Deportivo La Coruña B beat Gimnástica Torrelavega 1-0 at home, with their goal scored by Kevin Sánchez Rey.

Langreo also won, 1-0 away at SD Compostela, with Luis Sánchez Marcos scoring their goals.

With 26 goals in 27 games so far this season, Langreo are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Deportivo La Coruña B, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 27 games, of which they have won nine and drawn 11, earning 38 points.

The home side are five places behind Langreo in 10th, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

Deportivo La Coruña B are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Langreo's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Deportivo La Coruña B's nine.
