Deportivo La Coruña B v Langreo

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Ciudad Deportiva de Abegondo - Campo 2
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 12.00pm

Deportivo La Coruña B host Langreo on Sunday at Ciudad Deportiva de Abegondo-Campo 2 in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

In their last league match, on March 23, Deportivo La Coruña B beat Real Valladolid B 4-3 away, with goals from Diego Gómez Pérez and Mario Najera Leon.

Langreo drew, 1-1 at home against Ourense CF, with Juan López Pérez scoring their goals.

With 38 goals in 28 games so far this season, Deportivo La Coruña B are scoring more than average in the league with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Langreo, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 28 games, of which they have won nine and drawn 12, earning 39 points.

The home team are four places behind Langreo in ninth, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

Deportivo La Coruña B are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Langreo's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Deportivo La Coruña B's nine.
