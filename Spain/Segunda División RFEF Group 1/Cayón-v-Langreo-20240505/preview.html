Cayón v Langreo

Segunda División RFEF Group 1
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Langreo travel to Cayón on early Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Zamora CF 1-1 at home.

Cayón, meanwhile, lost their last match, 5-2 against Real Valladolid B, with their goal scored by Marcos Sáez Humara.

Cayón are 16th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won six and drawn 12, earning 30 points.

Langreo are nine places ahead of the home side in seventh, with 11 wins and 14 draws putting them on 47 points.

With 30 goals in 33 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Cayón are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Cayón are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Langreo's form is slightly better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.
