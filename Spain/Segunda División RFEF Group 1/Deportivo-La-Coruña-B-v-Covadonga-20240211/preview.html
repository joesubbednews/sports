Deportivo La Coruña B v Covadonga

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Ciudad Deportiva de Abegondo - Campo 2
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Deportivo La Coruña B face Covadonga on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

Deportivo La Coruña B are unbeaten in five, with one win and four draws, ahead of the 4pm kick-off.

They face a Covadonga team who have won one and drawn two over the same number of games.

Covadonga are 17th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won three and drawn seven, earning 16 points.

Deportivo La Coruña B are four places ahead of the away team in 13th, with five wins and nine draws putting them on 24 points.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.
