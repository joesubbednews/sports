CD Guijuelo v Club Marino de Luanco

Segunda División RFEF Group 1
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Club Marino de Luanco face CD Guijuelo on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

Club Marino de Luanco are unbeaten in 11, with four wins and seven draws, ahead of the 4pm kick-off.

They face a Guijuelo team who have won four and drawn four over the same number of games.

In the last 10 years, Guijuelo and Club Marino de Luanco have played each other on six occasions. Guijuelo won four of them, Club Marino de Luanco one, and they drew once.

On average, Guijuelo scored 1.0 goal and Club Marino de Luanco 0.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 29, when Club Marino de Luanco won 1-0 at home.

Guijuelo are fourth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn seven, earning 37 points.

Club Marino de Luanco are five places behind the home side in ninth, with six wins and 14 draws putting them on 32 points.

With 20 goals in 25 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Guijuelo are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have also conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Guijuelo's last match was on March 3, a 2-1 win against Racing Santander B, with Jesús Cambil Fajardo getting the goals for Guijuelo.

Club Marino de Luanco beat Cayón 1-0 last time out, also on March 3, with Juan Aspra Fernández on the scoresheet.
