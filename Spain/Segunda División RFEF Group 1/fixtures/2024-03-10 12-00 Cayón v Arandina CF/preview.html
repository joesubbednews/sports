Cayón v Arandina CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 1
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling Arandina CF face Cayón away on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Langreo on March 2, Arandina sit 17th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

They travel to play a Cayón side 16th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 1-0 against Club Marino de Luanco, on March 3.

Cayón are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Arandina's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the home team's five.

With 23 goals in 25 games so far this season, Cayón are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.
