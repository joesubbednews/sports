Real Oviedo B v Zamora CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio El Requexón
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 3.15pm

Zamora CF travel to the Estadio El Requexón for Sunday's match against Real Oviedo B looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

Zamora, who sit third in the league after 25 games, fell to a 2-1 home defeat to Deportivo La Coruña B on March 3.

They face an Oviedo B side who also lost their last match, a 3-2 defeat to Pontevedra CF, and who sit 14th in the table.

With 23 goals in 25 games so far this season, Oviedo B are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Zamora, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

In the last three years, Oviedo B and Zamora have played each other on three occasions. Oviedo B won one of them and Zamora the other.

Their last meeting was on October 28, when Zamora won 3-0 at home.

The home team are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Oviedo B's 10.
