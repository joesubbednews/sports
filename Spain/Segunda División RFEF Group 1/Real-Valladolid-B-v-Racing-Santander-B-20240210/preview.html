Real Valladolid B v Racing Santander B

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Campo 1 Anexos del José Zorrilla
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Real Valladolid B host Racing Santander B on Saturday at Campo 1 Anexos del José Zorrilla in Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

In their last league match, on January 27, Valladolid B beat Real Oviedo B 1-0 at home.

Racing Santander B also won, 1-0 away at Villalbés on January 28.

With 29 goals in 20 games so far this season, Racing Santander B are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Valladolid B are also above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The away side are fifth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

The hosts are four places behind Racing Santander B in ninth, with eight wins and two draws putting them on 26 points.

Valladolid B are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 1, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Racing Santander B's form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Valladolid B's seven.

In the last three years, Valladolid B and Racing Santander B have played each other on three occasions. Valladolid B won two of them and Racing Santander B one.

Their last meeting was on October 1, when Racing Santander B won 4-0 at home.
