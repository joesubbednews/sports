Langreo v Real Avilés

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Nuevo Ganzábal
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Langreo welcome Real Avilés to the Estadio Nuevo Ganzábal on Sunday hoping to break their five-match run of draws in Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

Langreo have earned five points from their last six games.

They face an Avilés side who have collected 10 points from the last possible 18, and who also drew their last game.

Their last meeting was on October 1, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Avilés, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home team are seventh in the table after 20 games, of which they have won seven and drawn nine, earning 30 points.

The visitors are three places behind Langreo in 10th, with five wins and 10 draws putting them on 25 points.

Langreo's last match was on January 28, a 0-0 draw against Pontevedra CF.

Avilés drew 1-1 with Gimnástica Torrelavega last time out, also on January 28.
