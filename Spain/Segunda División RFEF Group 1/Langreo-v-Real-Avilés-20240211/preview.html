Langreo v Real Avilés

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Nuevo Ganzábal
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Langreo welcome Real Avilés to the Estadio Nuevo Ganzábal on Sunday hoping to break their six-match run of draws in Segunda División RFEF Group 1.

It means Langreo have earned six points from their last six games.

Their last meeting was on October 1, when they played out a 2-2 draw.

Avilés, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The visitors are four places behind Langreo in 11th, with five wins and 11 draws putting them on 26 points.

Langreo's last match was on February 3, a 1-1 draw against Real Oviedo B.

Avilés drew 1-1 with Real Valladolid B last time out, also on February 3.
