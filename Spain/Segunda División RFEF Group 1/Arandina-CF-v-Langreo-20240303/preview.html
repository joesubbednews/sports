Arandina CF v Langreo

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio El Montecillo
Sunday, 3 March 2024 @ 0.00am

Arandina CF face Langreo at the Estadio El Montecillo on Sunday looking to secure a first win in 18 Segunda División RFEF Group 1 games.

Arandina have lost 10 and drawn seven matches since they last earned three points – against Gimnástica Torrelavega on October 8.

They face a Langreo side who have won six and drawn eight over that time.

Arandina are bottom of the table after 23 games, of which they have won two and drawn eight, earning 14 points.

Langreo are 12 places ahead of the hosts in sixth, with eight wins and 10 draws putting them on 34 points.

With 19 goals in 23 games so far this season, Arandina are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Arandina's last match was on February 17, a 3-2 loss against Gimnástica Torrelavega, with Gonzalo Serrano Iranzo and Kevin Manzano Cortés getting the goals for Arandina.

Langreo beat Racing Santander B 3-2 last time out, on February 18.
