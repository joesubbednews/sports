Real Oviedo B v Deportivo La Coruña B

Segunda División RFEF Group 1 | Estadio El Requexón
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Real Oviedo B welcome Deportivo La Coruña B to the Estadio El Requexón on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Oviedo B's struggles have left them with just one point from their last six Segunda División RFEF Group 1 matches, while their opponents have earned eight from a possible 18.

Oviedo B are 17th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won six and drawn 12, earning 30 points.

Deportivo La Coruña B are six places ahead of the hosts in 11th, with 10 wins and 11 draws putting them on 41 points.

With 26 goals in 33 games so far this season, Oviedo B are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have also conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Oviedo B's last match was on April 28, a 2-0 loss against Ourense CF.

Deportivo La Coruña B drew 2-2 with Pontevedra CF last time out, also on April 28, with Diego Gómez Pérez on the scoresheet.
