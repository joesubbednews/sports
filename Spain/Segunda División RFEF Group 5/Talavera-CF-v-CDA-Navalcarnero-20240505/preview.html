Talavera CF v CDA Navalcarnero

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio El Prado
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

CDA Navalcarnero visit Talavera CF at the Estadio El Prado on early Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

CDA Navalcarnero have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Talavera side who lost their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 31 goals in 33 games so far this season, Talavera are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

CDA Navalcarnero are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home side are seventh in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn 10, earning 46 points.

The visitors are four places behind Talavera in 11th, with 10 wins and 13 draws putting them on 43 points.

Talavera's last match was on April 28, a 1-0 loss against CD Numancia.

CDA Navalcarnero beat Ursaria 2-1 last time out, also on April 28, with Alberto Gutiérrez Borondo and Mario Rivas Viondi on the scoresheet.
