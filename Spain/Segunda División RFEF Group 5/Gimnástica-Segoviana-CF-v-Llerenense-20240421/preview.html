Gimnástica Segoviana CF v Llerenense

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Municipal de La Albuera
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

Gimnástica Segoviana CF host Llerenense at Estadio Municipal de La Albuera on late Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

Gimnástica Segoviana have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Llerenense side who have lost their last two matches, and collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 41 goals in 30 games so far this season, Gimnástica Segoviana are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Llerenense, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The hosts are third in the table after 30 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 10, earning 52 points.

The away team are 10 places behind Gimnástica Segoviana in 13th, with 10 wins and eight draws putting them on 38 points.

Gimnástica Segoviana's last match was on April 14, a 2-0 win against Montijo, with Daniel Plomer Gordillo getting the goals for Gimnástica Segoviana.

Llerenense drew 0-0 with CP Cacereño last time out, also on April 14.
