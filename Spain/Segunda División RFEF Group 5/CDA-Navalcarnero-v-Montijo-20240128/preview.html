CDA Navalcarnero v Montijo

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling Montijo face CDA Navalcarnero away on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over CD Numancia on January 21, Montijo sit 17th in Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

They travel to play a CDA Navalcarnero side 16th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 0-0 against Gimnástica Segoviana CF.

CDA Navalcarnero are in terrible form in Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Montijo's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' two.

With 15 goals in 19 games so far this season, CDA Navalcarnero are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last three years, CDA Navalcarnero and Montijo have played each other on three occasions. CDA Navalcarnero won two of them and Montijo one.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when CDA Navalcarnero won 1-0 away.
