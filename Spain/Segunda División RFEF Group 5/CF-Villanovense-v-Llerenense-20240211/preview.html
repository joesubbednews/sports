CF Villanovense v Llerenense

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Municipal Villanovense
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Llerenense make the journey to the Estadio Municipal Villanovense to play CF Villanovense on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Llerenense's struggles have left them with six points from their last six Segunda División RFEF Group 5 matches, while their opponents have earned the same number from a possible 18.

With 17 goals in 21 games so far this season, Llerenense are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Villanovense are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have also conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The visitors are 12th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won seven and drawn five, earning 26 points.

The home team are two places ahead of Llerenense in 10th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on the same number of points.

Villanovense's last match was on February 4, a 1-0 loss against Montijo.

Llerenense lost 1-0 against CDA Navalcarnero last time out, on February 3.
