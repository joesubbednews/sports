Llerenense v Getafe B

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Llerenense welcome Getafe B on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their five-game losing streak.

Llerenense's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Segunda División RFEF Group 5 matches, while their opponents have earned 11 from a possible 18.

With 17 goals in 22 games so far this season, Llerenense are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Getafe B, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The away team are sixth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

The hosts are six places behind Getafe B in 12th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

Llerenense's last match was on February 11, a 1-0 loss against CF Villanovense.

Getafe B beat Montijo 1-0 last time out, also on Sunday, with Marcos Denia on the scoresheet.
