Montijo v CD Numancia

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Municipal Emilio Macarro Rodriguez
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 11.00am

Montijo host CD Numancia in Sunday's early match at the Estadio Municipal Emilio Macarro Rodriguez looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

Montijo, who sit 18th in the league after 18 games, fell to a 1-2 away defeat to Illescas on January 13.

They face a Numancia side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-1 victory against Gimnástica Segoviana CF, and who sit first in the table.

With 32 goals in 18 games so far this season, Numancia are the league's highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Montijo, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home team are in bad form in Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Montijo's four.
