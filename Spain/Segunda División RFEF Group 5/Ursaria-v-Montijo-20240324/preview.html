Ursaria v Montijo

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Polideportivo La Juventud
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Montijo come to the Estadio Polideportivo La Juventud to play Ursaria on early Sunday in poor form, having collected just three points from their last six games.

The visitors have won just one of their last six fixtures, while Ursaria have two wins and a draw.

Montijo are bottom of the table after 27 games, of which they have won six and drawn four, earning 22 points.

Ursaria are four places ahead of the visitors in 14th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

With 23 goals in 27 games so far this season, Montijo are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The home side are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Ursaria's last match was on Sunday, a 2-0 loss against Gimnástica Segoviana CF.

Montijo lost 2-1 against UD San Fernando last time out, also on March 17.
