Llerenense v Guadalajara

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Llerenense are on a poor run ahead of hosting Guadalajara on Sunday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Llerenense have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Segunda División RFEF Group 5 games, and face a Guadalajara side whose last six games have brought two wins and two draws.

With 21 goals in 26 games so far this season, Llerenense are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Guadalajara, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are 14th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

The away side are three places ahead of Llerenense in 11th, with 10 wins and four draws putting them on 34 points.

Llerenense's last match was on March 10, a 2-1 loss against AD Unión Adarve, with Laerte Polydoro do Nascimento getting the goal for Llerenense.

Guadalajara beat Montijo 2-1 last time out, on March 9, with Diego Morcillo Muñoz and Sergio Marcos González on the scoresheet.
