Gimnástica Segoviana CF v Getafe B

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Municipal de La Albuera
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Gimnástica Segoviana CF host Getafe B on Sunday at Estadio Municipal de La Albuera in Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

In their last league match, on January 28, Gimnástica Segoviana beat CF Villanovense 2-1 away.

Getafe B drew, 3-3 at home against CP Cacereño.

With 24 goals in 20 games so far this season, Gimnástica Segoviana are scoring more than average in the league with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Getafe B, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are sixth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won eight and drawn eight, earning 32 points.

The visitors are one place behind Gimnástica Segoviana in seventh, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Gimnástica Segoviana are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Getafe B's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.
