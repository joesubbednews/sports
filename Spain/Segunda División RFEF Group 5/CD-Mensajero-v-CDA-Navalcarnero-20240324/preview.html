CD Mensajero v CDA Navalcarnero

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Silvestre Carrillo
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 11.30am

Sunday's early match at the Estadio Silvestre Carrillo sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 17th-ranked CD Mensajero host 16th-placed CDA Navalcarnero.

Mensajero have picked up 25 points from their first 25 Segunda División RFEF Group 5 games, with five wins and 10 draws.

That is four points less than CDA Navalcarnero have collected, having won six and drawn 11.

With 23 goals in 27 games so far this season, CDA Navalcarnero are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Mensajero are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Mensajero's six.

Mensajero's last match was on Sunday, a 3-0 loss against CD Numancia.

CDA Navalcarnero drew 0-0 with Getafe B last time out, also on Sunday.
