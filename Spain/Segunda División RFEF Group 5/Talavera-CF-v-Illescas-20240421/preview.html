Talavera CF v Illescas

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio El Prado
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

Illescas come to the Estadio El Prado to play Talavera CF on Sunday in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Talavera have a win and two draws.

With 30 goals in 31 games so far this season, Talavera are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Illescas, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home team are seventh in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn 10, earning 43 points.

The visitors are one place behind Talavera in eighth, with 11 wins and 10 draws putting them on the same number of points.

Talavera's last match was on April 14, a 1-0 loss against Atlético Paso.

Illescas lost 2-1 against Ursaria last time out, also on April 14, with Víctor García Mingo on the scoresheet.
