Talavera CF v Getafe B

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio El Prado
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 4.00pm

Two of Segunda División RFEF Group 5's meanest defences go head-to-head at the Estadio El Prado on Sunday, when Talavera CF host Getafe B.

Only one side – Atlético Paso – has conceded fewer goals than Talavera to date: the home side have let in just 10 goals in 18 games.

Getafe B have conceded 17 goals in 18 games, giving them the joint-fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Talavera are fourth in the table after 18 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

Getafe B are two places behind the home team in sixth, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Talavera are in disappointing form in Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Talavera's six.

Talavera's last match was on January 14, a 1-0 win against CF Villanovense.

Getafe B beat Ursaria 1-0 last time out, also on January 14.
