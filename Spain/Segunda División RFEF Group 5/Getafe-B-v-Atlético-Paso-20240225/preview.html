Getafe B v Atlético Paso

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 10.00am

Getafe B host Atlético Paso on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

In their last league match, on February 18, Getafe B beat Llerenense 1-0 away.

Atlético Paso lost, 1-0 at home against CF Villanovense.

With 17 goals in 23 games so far this season, Atlético Paso are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Getafe B, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home side are fifth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn seven, earning 37 points.

The visitors are one place behind Getafe B in sixth, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

Getafe B are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Atlético Paso's form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' 11.
