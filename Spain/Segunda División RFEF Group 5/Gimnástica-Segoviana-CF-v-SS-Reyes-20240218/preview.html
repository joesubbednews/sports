Gimnástica Segoviana CF v SS Reyes

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Municipal de La Albuera
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Two of the Segunda División RFEF Group 5's meanest defences go head-to-head at Estadio Municipal de La Albuera on Sunday, when SS Reyes visit Gimnástica Segoviana CF.

Only one side – Atlético Paso – has conceded fewer goals than SS Reyes to date: the away side have let in just 15 goals in 22 games.

Gimnástica Segoviana have conceded 19 goals in 22 games, giving them the joint-fourth tightest back line so far this season.

SS Reyes are top of the table after 22 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn nine, earning 39 points.

Gimnástica Segoviana are four places behind the visitors in fifth, with nine wins and nine draws putting them on 36 points.

The home team are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, SS Reyes's form is better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Gimnástica Segoviana's nine.

Gimnástica Segoviana's last match was on February 11, a 1-0 win against CD Mensajero.

SS Reyes drew 1-1 with CP Cacereño last time out, also on February 11.
