Getafe B v Guadalajara

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Getafe B host Guadalajara on early Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

Getafe B have picked up 14 points from their last six games, and they face a Guadalajara side who have lost their last two matches, and collected nine points from the last possible 18.

With 40 goals in 31 games so far this season, Guadalajara are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 47 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Getafe B, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home team are fourth in the table after 32 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn 11, earning 53 points.

The visitors are seven places behind Getafe B in 11th, with 13 wins and four draws putting them on 43 points.

Getafe B's last match was on April 21, a 2-0 win against AD Unión Adarve, with Abdoulaye Keita Keita and Javier Carbonell Piserra getting the goals for Getafe B.

Guadalajara lost 2-1 against CF Villanovense last time out, on April 20, with Iván Moreno Rodríguez on the scoresheet.
