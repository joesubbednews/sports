Llerenense v Ursaria

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 4.30pm

Ursaria travel to Llerenense on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Montijo 4-1 at home, with goals from Pablo Villalón Rodríguez and Izan González Ruiz Peinado.

Llerenense also won their last match, 2-0 against UD San Fernando, with their goals scored by Michael Villajos Expósito and Victor Sánchez Morales.

With 24 goals in 28 games so far this season, Llerenense are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Ursaria are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The visitors are 13th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

The home side are one place ahead of Ursaria in 12th, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

Llerenense are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, Ursaria's form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
