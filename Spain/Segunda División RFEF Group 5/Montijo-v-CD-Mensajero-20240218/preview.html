Montijo v CD Mensajero

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Municipal Emilio Macarro Rodriguez
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Sunday's early match at the Estadio Municipal Emilio Macarro Rodriguez sees the league's two bottom sides face each other, as rock-bottom Montijo host CD Mensajero.

Montijo have picked up 19 points from their first 19 Segunda División RFEF Group 5 games, with five wins and four draws.

That is the same number of points as Mensajero have collected, having won three and drawn 10.

With 19 goals in 22 games so far this season, Montijo are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Mensajero are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

In the last three years, Montijo and Mensajero have played each other on three occasions. Mensajero won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when they played out a 1-1 draw.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Montijo's nine.

Montijo's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 loss against Getafe B.

Mensajero lost 1-0 against Gimnástica Segoviana CF last time out, also on Sunday.
