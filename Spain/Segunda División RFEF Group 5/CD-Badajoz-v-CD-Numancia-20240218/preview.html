CD Badajoz v CD Numancia

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.00am

CD Numancia travel for Sunday's early match against CD Badajoz looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

Numancia, who sit second in the league after 22 games, fell to a 3-2 home defeat to Illescas on February 11.

They face a Badajoz side who also lost their last match, a 2-1 defeat to UD San Fernando, and who sit 16th in the table.

With 37 goals in 22 games so far this season, Numancia are the league's highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Badajoz, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home team are in terrible form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Badajoz's two.
