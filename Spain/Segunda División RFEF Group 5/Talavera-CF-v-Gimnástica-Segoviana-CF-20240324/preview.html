Talavera CF v Gimnástica Segoviana CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio El Prado
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Gimnástica Segoviana CF travel to Talavera CF on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Ursaria 2-0 at home, with goals from Pedro Astray López and Juan Ignacio Pajín Silva.

Talavera, meanwhile, lost their last match, 4-0 against CP Cacereño.

With 32 goals in 26 games so far this season, Gimnástica Segoviana are scoring more than average in the league with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Talavera, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The away team are third in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn 10, earning 43 points.

The hosts are four places behind Gimnástica Segoviana in seventh, with 10 wins and eight draws putting them on 38 points.

Talavera are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Gimnástica Segoviana's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Talavera's five.
