Talavera CF v Guadalajara

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio El Prado
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Guadalajara face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Talavera CF defence on Sunday.

With 30 goals in 22 games, Guadalajara are the third-highest scorers in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5 ahead of the 4pm kick-off at the Estadio El Prado.

They face a Talavera side who have scored 23 in 22 matches, but conceded only 16 goals, putting them third among the league's tightest defences – only SS Reyes and Atlético Paso have conceded fewer goals.

Talavera are seventh in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn six, earning 33 points.

Guadalajara are two places behind the hosts in ninth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

Talavera are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.

Over the last two years, Talavera and Guadalajara have played each other twice. Talavera won both of them.

Their last meeting was on October 11, when Talavera won 2-1 away.

Talavera's last match was on February 11, a 1-0 loss against AD Unión Adarve.

Guadalajara beat Ursaria 1-0 last time out, also on February 11, with Iván Moreno Rodríguez on the scoresheet.
