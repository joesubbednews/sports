AD Unión Adarve v Llerenense

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Llerenense come to play AD Unión Adarve on early Sunday in poor form, having collected just four points from their last six games.

The visitors have won one and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Unión Adarve have two wins and three draws.

With 34 goals in 25 games so far this season, Unión Adarve are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Llerenense, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The away team are 12th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

The hosts are three places ahead of Llerenense in ninth, with eight wins and 11 draws putting them on 35 points.

Unión Adarve's last match was on March 3, a 2-1 loss against Montijo, with Gonzalo Expósito Verdejo getting the goal for Unión Adarve.

Llerenense drew 2-2 with SS Reyes last time out, also on Sunday, with Gustavo Mauricio Quezada Reinoso and Jacques Armand Thierry Bayo Mben on the scoresheet.
