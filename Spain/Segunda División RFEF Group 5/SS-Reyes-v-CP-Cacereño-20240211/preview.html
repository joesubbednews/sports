SS Reyes v CP Cacereño

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.00am

SS Reyes face CP Cacereño on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

SS Reyes are unbeaten in seven, with five wins and two draws, ahead of the 11am kick-off.

They face a CP Cacereño team who have won two and drawn three over the same number of games.

SS Reyes are top of the table after 21 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn eight, earning 38 points.

CP Cacereño are 10 places behind the hosts in 11th, with six wins and eight draws putting them on 26 points.

With 33 goals in 21 games so far this season, SS Reyes are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

SS Reyes's last match was on Sunday, a 4-1 win against Talavera CF.

CP Cacereño drew 0-0 with CD Mensajero last time out, also on February 4.
