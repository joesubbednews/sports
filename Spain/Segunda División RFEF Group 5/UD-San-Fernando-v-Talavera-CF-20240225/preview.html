UD San Fernando v Talavera CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 12.00pm

UD San Fernando face Talavera CF on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

San Fernando are unbeaten in five, with two wins and three draws, ahead of the 12pm kick-off.

They face a Talavera team who have won just one and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 19 goals in 23 games so far this season, San Fernando are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Talavera are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The hosts are 12th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won seven and drawn six, earning 27 points.

The away team are five places ahead of San Fernando in seventh, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

San Fernando's last match was on February 18, a 1-1 draw against Ursaria, with Kilian Aleman Vega getting the goal for San Fernando.

Talavera drew 0-0 with Guadalajara last time out, also on Sunday.
