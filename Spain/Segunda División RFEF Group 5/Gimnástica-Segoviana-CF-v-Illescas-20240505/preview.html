Gimnástica Segoviana CF v Illescas

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Municipal de La Albuera
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Gimnástica Segoviana CF host Illescas in Sunday's early match at Estadio Municipal de La Albuera looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

Gimnástica Segoviana, who sit third in the league after 33 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to Atlético Paso on April 28.

They face an Illescas side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with CP Cacereño, and who sit ninth in the table.

With 45 goals in 33 games so far this season, Gimnástica Segoviana are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Illescas, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to Gimnástica Segoviana's 15.
