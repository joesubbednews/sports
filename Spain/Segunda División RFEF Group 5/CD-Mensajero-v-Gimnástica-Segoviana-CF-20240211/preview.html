CD Mensajero v Gimnástica Segoviana CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Estadio Silvestre Carrillo
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.30am

CD Mensajero face Gimnástica Segoviana CF on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

Mensajero are unbeaten in six, with three wins and three draws, ahead of the 11.30am kick-off.

They face a Gimnástica Segoviana team who have won two and drawn three over the same number of games.

Mensajero are 17th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won three and drawn 10, earning 19 points.

Gimnástica Segoviana are 11 places ahead of the hosts in sixth, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

With 25 goals in 21 games so far this season, the away team are scoring more than average in the league with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Mensajero, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Mensajero's last match was on February 4, a 0-0 draw against CP Cacereño.

Gimnástica Segoviana drew 1-1 with Getafe B last time out, also on Sunday.
