CD Badajoz v Atlético Paso

Segunda División RFEF Group 5
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 11.00am

CD Badajoz face Atlético Paso on Sunday looking to secure a first win in eight Segunda División RFEF Group 5 games.

Badajoz have lost three and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against CP Cacereño on November 12.

They face an Atlético Paso side who have won three and drawn three over that time.

Atlético Paso are third in the table after 18 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Badajoz are 11 places behind the away team in 14th, with four wins and seven draws putting them on 19 points.

With 15 goals in 18 games so far this season, Atlético Paso are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in eight goals at a rate of 0.4 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

Badajoz's last match was on January 14, a 3-1 loss against AD Unión Adarve.

Atlético Paso beat Llerenense 1-0 last time out, also on Sunday.
