CD Numancia v UD San Fernando

Segunda División RFEF Group 5 | Nuevo Estadio Los Pajaritos
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 11.00am

CD Numancia host UD San Fernando in Sunday's early match at Nuevo Estadio Los Pajaritos looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5.

Numancia, who sit second in the league after 30 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Guadalajara on April 7.

They face a San Fernando side who secured a draw in their last match, a 2-2 tie with Illescas, and who sit 14th in the table.

With 47 goals in 30 games so far this season, Numancia are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

San Fernando, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 5, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Numancia's 12.
