Eibar Women v Sevilla Women

Liga F | Instalaciones de Unbe
Saturday, 13 April 2024 @ 1.00pm

Sevilla Women are strong favourites to take all three points despite Eibar Women's home advantage in Saturday lunchtime's match at Instalaciones de Unbe.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.92 on Sevilla sealing the win, with the visitors sitting seventh in Liga F table.

Eibar, who are 13th in the league and 16 points behind Sevilla, are priced at 3.52 to win. A draw is set at 3.45.

With 14 goals in 22 games so far this season, Eibar are the league's joint-second-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Sevilla, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 2.0 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

In Cristina Martín-Prieto Gutierrez, the away team have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. She has notched 14 goals in 22 appearances, to sit third in the scoring charts.

Her goal rate of one every 137 minutes is quicker than that of Andrea Abigail Alvarez Donis, the hosts' top scorer with a goal every 252 minutes, and a total of four goals in 20 games.

In the last five years, Eibar and Sevilla have played each other on five occasions. Eibar won two of them and Sevilla three.

On average, Eibar scored 1.2 goals and Sevilla 2.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 28, when Sevilla won 3-0 at home.

Eibar are in mixed form in Liga F, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Sevilla's form is similar – they have both taken seven points from 18.

Eibar's last match was on March 30, a 0-0 draw against Real Betis Women.

Sevilla beat Real Sociedad Women 4-2 last time out, also on March 30, with Antionette Oyedupe Payne, Eva Llamas Hernández, Martín-Prieto Gutierrez and Olatz Santana (own goal) on the scoresheet.
