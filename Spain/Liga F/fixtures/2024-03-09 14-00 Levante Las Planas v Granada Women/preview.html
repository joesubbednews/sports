Levante Las Planas v Granada Women

Liga F | Camp Municipal Les Planes
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 1.00pm

Levante Las Planas face Granada Women at Camp Municipal Les Planes on Saturday lunchtime looking to secure a first win in 11 Liga F games.

Levante Las Planas have lost six and drawn four matches since they last earned three points – against UD Granadilla Tenerife on November 12.

They face a Granada side who have won two and drawn three over that time.

Granada are 15th in the table after 18 games, of which they have won three and drawn three, earning 12 points.

Levante Las Planas are five places ahead of the away team in 10th, with four wins and seven draws putting them on 19 points.

With 17 goals in 18 games so far this season, Granada are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The hosts are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Levante Las Planas's last match was on February 18, a 2-1 loss against Madrid CFF, with Cristina Baudet Lucena getting the goal for Levante Las Planas.

Granada drew 2-2 with Sevilla Women last time out, on February 17, with Naima García Aguilar and Ornella María Vignola Cabot on the scoresheet.
