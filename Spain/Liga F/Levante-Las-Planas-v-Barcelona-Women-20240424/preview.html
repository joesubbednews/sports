Levante Las Planas v Barcelona Women

Liga F | Camp Municipal Les Planes
Wednesday, 24 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

Barcelona Women visit Levante Las Planas at Camp Municipal Les Planes on late Wednesday on the back of five consecutive wins in Liga F.

Barcelona have picked up 16 points from their last six games, and they face a Levante Las Planas side who lost their last match, and have collected two points from the last possible 18.

With 97 goals in 22 games so far this season, Barcelona are the league's highest scorers with 4.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in five goals at a rate of 0.2 per game.

Levante Las Planas, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The away side are top of the table after 22 games, of which they have won 21 and drawn one, earning 64 points.

The hosts are 11 places behind Barcelona in 12th, with four wins and eight draws putting them on 20 points.

In Caroline Graham Hansen, Barcelona have the league's sharpest shooter so far this season. She has notched 17 goals in 20 appearances.

Her goal rate of one every 81 minutes is much quicker than that of Jessica Dahiana Martínez Villagra, Levante Las Planas's top scorer with a goal every 231 minutes, and a total of seven goals in 20 games.

In the last three years, Levante Las Planas and Barcelona have played each other on three occasions. Barcelona won all of them.

Their last meeting was on January 6, when Barcelona won 9-1 at home.

Levante Las Planas's last match was on March 31, a 4-1 loss against Athletic Club Women, with Anissa Lahmari getting the goal for Levante Las Planas.

Barcelona beat Levante Women 5-0 last time out, also on March 31, with Bruna Vilamala Costa (two), Salma Paralluelo Ayingono (two) and Martina Fernández on the scoresheet.
