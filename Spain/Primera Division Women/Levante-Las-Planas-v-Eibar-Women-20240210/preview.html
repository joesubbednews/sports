Levante Las Planas v Eibar Women

Primera Division Women | Camp Municipal Les Planes
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Levante Las Planas face Eibar Women at Camp Municipal Les Planes on Saturday looking to secure a first win in nine Primera Division Women games.

Levante Las Planas have lost five and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against UD Granadilla Tenerife on November 12.

They face an Eibar side who have won three and lost five over that time.

Eibar are 13th in the table after 16 games, of which they have won four and drawn one, earning 13 points.

Levante Las Planas are three places ahead of the visitors in 10th, with four wins and six draws putting them on 18 points.

With 10 goals in 16 games so far this season, Eibar are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 34 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The home team are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

Levante Las Planas's last match was on Sunday, a 1-1 draw against UD Granadilla Tenerife, with Jessica Dahiana Martínez Villagra getting the goal for Levante Las Planas.

Eibar lost 2-0 against Real Sociedad Women last time out, also on Sunday.
