Granada Women v Sporting de Huelva

Primera Division Women | Ciudad Deportiva del Granada CF
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 1.00pm

Struggling Granada Women face Sporting de Huelva at Ciudad Deportiva del Granada CF on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-2 victory over Real Betis Women on January 7, Granada sit 15th in Primera Division Women.

They travel to play a Sporting side 16th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 3-1 against Sevilla Women.

Granada are in bad form in Primera Division Women, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

But with no wins and six losses over that period, Sporting's form is even worse – they have taken no points from 18, compared to the home team's three.

With eight goals in 13 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 2.4 per game.

Granada are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.6. They have conceded 2.1 goals per game.
