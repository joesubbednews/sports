Pradejón Women v Rayo Vallecano Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.30pm

Pradejón Women host Rayo Vallecano Women in Sunday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Pradejón, who sit zero in the league after 20 games, fell to a 2-0 away defeat to Espanyol II Women on February 11.

They face a Rayo side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Balears Women, and who sit zero in the table.

With 19 goals in 20 games so far this season, Rayo are the league's-10th-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Pradejón are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Pradejón's seven.
