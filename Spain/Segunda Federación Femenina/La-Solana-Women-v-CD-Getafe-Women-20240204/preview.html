La Solana Women v CD Getafe Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 11.00am

La Solana Women host CD Getafe Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Segunda Federación Femenina.

La Solana, who sit zero in the league after 18 games, fell to a 2-0 away defeat to UD Tenerife II Women on January 28.

They face a Getafe Women side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Córdoba Women, and who sit sixth in the table.

La Solana are in terrible form in Segunda Federación Femenina, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Getafe Women's form is much better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to la Solana's one.

With nine goals in 18 games so far this season, the hosts are the league's-15th-lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.
