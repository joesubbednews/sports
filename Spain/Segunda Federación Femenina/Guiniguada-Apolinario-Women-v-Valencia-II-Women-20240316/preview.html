Guiniguada Apolinario Women v Valencia II Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Valencia II Women travel for Saturday's early match against Guiniguada Apolinario Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Valencia II, who sit zero in the league after 22 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Fundación Tenerife Women on March 9.

They face a Guiniguada Apolinario side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against La Solana Women, and who sit 16th in the table.

Guiniguada Apolinario are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, Valencia II's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.
