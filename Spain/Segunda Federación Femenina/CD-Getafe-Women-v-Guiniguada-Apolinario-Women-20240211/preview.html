CD Getafe Women v Guiniguada Apolinario Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.30am

Guiniguada Apolinario Women travel for Sunday's early match against CD Getafe Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Guiniguada Apolinario, who sit zero in the league after 18 games, fell to a 2-0 home defeat to UD Tenerife II Women on February 3.

They face a Getafe Women side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with La Solana Women, and who sit eighth in the table.

With 17 goals in 18 games so far this season, Guiniguada Apolinario are the league's-11th-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Getafe Women, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Getafe Women's nine.
