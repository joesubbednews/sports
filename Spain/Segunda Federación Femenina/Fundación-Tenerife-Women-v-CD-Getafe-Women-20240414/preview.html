Fundación Tenerife Women v CD Getafe Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

CD Getafe Women visit Fundación Tenerife Women on Sunday on the back of six consecutive wins in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

It means Getafe Women have picked up the maximum 18 points from their last six games, and they face a Fundación Tenerife side who have lost their last two matches, and collected six points from the last possible 18.

With 44 goals in 26 games so far this season, Getafe Women are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Fundación Tenerife are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The away team are fourth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 16 and drawn seven, earning 55 points.

The hosts are four places behind Getafe Women in eighth, with 14 wins and five draws putting them on 47 points.

Fundación Tenerife's last match was on April 7, a 2-0 loss against UD Tenerife II Women.
