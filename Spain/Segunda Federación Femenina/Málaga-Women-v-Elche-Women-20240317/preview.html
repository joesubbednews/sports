Málaga Women v Elche Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Málaga Women host Elche Women on Sunday in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

In their last league match, on March 10, Málaga drew with Unión Viera Women 0-0 away.

Elche won, 1-0 at home against Juan Grande Women on March 9, with scoring their goals.

With 46 goals in 23 games so far this season, Elche are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Málaga are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are 15th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

The away team are five places ahead of Málaga in 10th, with 11 wins and five draws putting them on 38 points.

Málaga are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Elche's form is better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the home side's nine.
