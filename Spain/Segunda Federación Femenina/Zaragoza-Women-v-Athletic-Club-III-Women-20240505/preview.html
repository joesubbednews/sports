Zaragoza Women v Athletic Club III Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 10.00am

Athletic Club III Women travel for Sunday's early match against Zaragoza Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Athletic Club III, who sit zero in the league after 29 games, fell to a 4-0 home defeat to Viajes Interrías Women on April 28.

They face a Zaragoza side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Huesca Women, and who sit 10th in the table.

Zaragoza are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, Athletic Club III's form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

With 30 goals in 29 games so far this season, the away team are the league's-7rd-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 56 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Zaragoza, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.
