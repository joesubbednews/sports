Athletic Club III Women v Racing Féminas

Segunda Federación Femenina
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Athletic Club III Women host Racing Féminas in Saturday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Athletic Club III, who sit zero in the league after 24 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to Osasuna II Women on March 17.

They face a Racing Féminas side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Viajes Interrías Women, and who sit 11th in the table.

Athletic Club III are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with two wins and a draw over that period, Racing Féminas's form is identical – they have both taken seven points from 18.

With 26 goals in 24 games so far this season, the home team are the league's joint--2th-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.
