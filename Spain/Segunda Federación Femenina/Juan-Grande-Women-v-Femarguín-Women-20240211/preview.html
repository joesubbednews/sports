Juan Grande Women v Femarguín Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 12.00pm

Femarguín Women travel for Sunday lunchtime's match against Juan Grande Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Femarguín, who sit zero in the league after 19 games, fell to a 2-0 home defeat to Fundación Tenerife Women on February 4.

They face a Juan Grande side who also lost their last match, a 4-1 defeat to Real Unión de Tenerife Women, and who sit ninth in the table.

Juan Grande are in fantastic form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Femarguín's form is much worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' 15.

With 20 goals in 19 games so far this season, the away team are the league's joint--6th-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Juan Grande, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.
