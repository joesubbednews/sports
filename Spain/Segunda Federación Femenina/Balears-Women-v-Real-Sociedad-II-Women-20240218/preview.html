Balears Women v Real Sociedad II Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 2.00pm

Balears Women host Real Sociedad II Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Balears, who sit fourth in the league after 20 games, fell to a 1-1 away defeat to Rayo Vallecano Women on February 11.

They face a Sociedad II Women side who picked up a win in their last match, a 6-1 victory against Athletic Club III Women, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 39 goals in 20 games so far this season, Balears are the league's joint-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Sociedad II Women are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Balears's 11.
