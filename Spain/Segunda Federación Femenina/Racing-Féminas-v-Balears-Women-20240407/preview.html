Racing Féminas v Balears Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 10.00am

Balears Women face Racing Féminas on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

They face a Racing Féminas team who have won four and drawn one over the same number of games.
