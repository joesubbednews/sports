Real Madrid II Women v Espanyol II Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 10.00am

Real Madrid II Women host Espanyol II Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Madrid II Women, who sit top of the league after 29 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to Pradejón Women on April 27.

They face an Espanyol II side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Atlético Madrid III Women, and who sit zero in the table.

With 51 goals in 29 games so far this season, Madrid II Women are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Espanyol II, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in fantastic form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Madrid II Women's 15.
