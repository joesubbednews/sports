Viajes Interrías Women v Balears Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 10.00am

Balears Women travel for Sunday's early match against Viajes Interrías Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Balears, who sit fourth in the league after 29 games, fell to a 3-1 home defeat to Real Oviedo Women on April 28.

They face a Viajes Interrías side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-0 victory against Athletic Club III Women, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 51 goals in 29 games so far this season, Balears are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Viajes Interrías are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Viajes Interrías's seven.
