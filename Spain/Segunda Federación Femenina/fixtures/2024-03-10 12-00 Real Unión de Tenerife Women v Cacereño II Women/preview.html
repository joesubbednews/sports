Real Unión de Tenerife Women v Cacereño II Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Cacereño II Women travel for Sunday's early match against Real Unión de Tenerife Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.
