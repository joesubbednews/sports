Pradejón Women v Sporting Gijón Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 3.30pm

Sporting Gijón Women travel for Sunday's match against Pradejón Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Sporting, who sit zero in the league after 22 games, fell to a 2-1 home defeat to Balears Women on February 25.

They face a Pradejón side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Real Sociedad II Women, and who sit 16th in the table.

Pradejón are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and five losses over that period, Sporting's form is worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.

With 24 goals in 22 games so far this season, the away side are the league's joint--4th-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Pradejón are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.
