Real Madrid II Women v Atlético Madrid III Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Real Madrid II Women host Atlético Madrid III Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Madrid II Women, who sit top of the league after 22 games, fell to a 0-1 away defeat to Racing Féminas on February 25.

They face an Atlético Madrid III side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against Zaragoza Women, and who sit 14th in the table.

With 38 goals in 22 games so far this season, Madrid II Women are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 10 goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Atlético Madrid III, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home side are in fantastic form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with five wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Madrid II Women's 16.
