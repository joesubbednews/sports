Unión Viera Women v Málaga Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Unión Viera Women host Málaga Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Unión Viera, who sit zero in the league after 22 games, fell to a 0-0 away defeat to Juan Grande Women on February 25.

They face a Málaga side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against CFF Albacete, and who sit 13th in the table.

With 21 goals in 22 games so far this season, Unión Viera are the league's joint--9st-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Málaga, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with three wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is identical – they have both taken 11 points from 18.
