Elche Women v Juan Grande Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 10.00am

Elche Women face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Juan Grande Women defence on Saturday.

With 45 goals in 22 games, Elche are the top scorers in the Segunda Federación Femenina ahead of the 10am kick-off.

They face a Juan Grande side who have scored 31 in 22 matches, but conceded only 18 goals, putting them joint-fourth among the league's tightest defences – only Real Madrid II Women, UD Tenerife II Women and Fundación Tenerife Women have conceded fewer goals.

Elche are 10th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn five, earning 35 points.

Juan Grande are one place ahead of the home team in ninth, with 11 wins and six draws putting them on 39 points.

Elche are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Elche's 10.

Elche's last match was on February 28, a 3-2 win against Guiniguada Apolinario Women, with getting the goals for Elche.

Juan Grande drew 0-0 with Unión Viera Women last time out, on February 25.
