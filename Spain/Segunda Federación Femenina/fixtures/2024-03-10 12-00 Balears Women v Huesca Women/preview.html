Balears Women v Huesca Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Two of the Segunda Federación Femenina's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when third-placed Balears Women host zero-placed Huesca Women.

Balears have picked up 15 wins and three draws from 22 games so far this season, and sit 23 points above the visitors going into the 11am match.

Huesca, meanwhile, have won seven and drawn four, picking up 25 points.

With 43 goals in 22 games so far this season, Balears are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Huesca, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away side's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Balears's 11.

Balears's last match was on February 25, a 2-1 win against Sporting Gijón Women, with getting the goals for Balears.

Huesca drew 0-0 with Athletic Club III Women last time out, on February 24.
