Cacereño II Women v Levante II Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Tuesday, 2 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

Cacereño II Women host Levante II Women in Tuesday's match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Cacereño II, who sit zero in the league after 24 games, fell to a 0-0 away defeat to CFF Albacete on March 23.

They face a Levante II side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-0 victory against Guiniguada Apolinario Women, and who sit 15th in the table.

With 23 goals in 24 games so far this season, Cacereño II are the league's-10th-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Levante II, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have also conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with no wins and four draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Cacereño II's four.
