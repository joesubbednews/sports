Málaga Women v CD Getafe Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Relegation candidates Málaga Women face a challenge against high-flying CD Getafe Women on Sunday.

Málaga Women are rooted to the bottom of the Segunda Federación Femenina table, and have picked up 10 wins and eight draws in their 28 games to date.

Getafe Women, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 61 points, having won 18 and drawn seven, and are nine points behind table-toppers Real Madrid II Women.

Málaga are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With six wins and no losses over that period, Getafe Women's form is much better – they have taken 18 points from 18, compared to the hosts' eight.

With 49 goals in 28 games so far this season, the away team are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Málaga are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have also conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Málaga's last match was on Sunday, a 2-0 loss against UD Tenerife II Women.

Getafe Women beat Juan Grande Women 1-0 last time out, also on Sunday, with on the scoresheet.
