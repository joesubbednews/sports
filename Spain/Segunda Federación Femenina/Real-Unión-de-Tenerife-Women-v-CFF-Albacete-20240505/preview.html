Real Unión de Tenerife Women v CFF Albacete

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

CFF Albacete travel for Sunday's early match against Real Unión de Tenerife Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

CFF Albacete, who sit zero in the league after 29 games, fell to a 3-0 home defeat to Levante II Women on April 28.

With 14 goals in 29 games so far this season, CFF Albacete are the league's-15th-lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 54 goals at a rate of 1.9 per game.

Unión de Tenerife Women, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.
