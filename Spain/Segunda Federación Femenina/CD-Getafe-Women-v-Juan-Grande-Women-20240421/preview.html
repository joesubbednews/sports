CD Getafe Women v Juan Grande Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 3.00pm

Two of the Segunda Federación Femenina's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when CD Getafe Women host Juan Grande Women.

Only two sides – Real Madrid II Women and UD Tenerife II Women – have conceded fewer goals than Getafe Women to date: the home side have let in just 18 goals in 27 games.

Juan Grande have conceded 22 goals in 27 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Getafe Women are in exceptional form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with six wins and no losses from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Juan Grande's form is much worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home side's 18.

Getafe Women are third in the table after 27 games, of which they have won 17 and drawn seven, earning 58 points.

The visitors are five places behind Getafe Women in eighth, with 13 wins and eight draws putting them on 47 points.
