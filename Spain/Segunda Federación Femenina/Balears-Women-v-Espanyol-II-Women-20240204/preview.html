Balears Women v Espanyol II Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 4.30pm

Two of Segunda Federación Femenina's top sides face each other in Sunday's kick-off, when third-placed Balears Women host zero-placed Espanyol II Women.

Balears have picked up 12 wins and two draws from 18 games so far this season, and sit 33 points above the visitors going into the 4.30pm match.

Espanyol II, meanwhile, have won one and drawn two, picking up five points.

With 37 goals in 18 games so far this season, Balears are the league's highest scorers with 2.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Espanyol II, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 2.6 goals per game.

The home side are in good form in Segunda Federación Femenina, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to Balears's 13.

Balears's last match was on January 28, a 0-0 draw against Pradejón Women.

Espanyol II drew 2-2 with Athletic Club III Women last time out, also on January 28, with on the scoresheet.
