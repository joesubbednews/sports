Femarguín Women v Guiniguada Apolinario Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Femarguín Women host Guiniguada Apolinario Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Femarguín, who sit zero in the league after 26 games, fell to a 1-1 away defeat to La Solana Women on April 6.

They face a Guiniguada Apolinario side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-0 victory against Real Unión de Tenerife Women, and who sit 15th in the table.

With 26 goals in 26 games so far this season, Femarguín are the league's-9st-lowest scorers with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Guiniguada Apolinario are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in disappointing form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Femarguín's six.
