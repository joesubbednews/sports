Guiniguada Apolinario Women v Elche Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 15th-ranked Guiniguada Apolinario Women host 14th-placed Elche Women.

Guiniguada Apolinario have picked up 42 points from their first 42 Segunda Federación Femenina games, with 12 wins and six draws.

That is the same number of points as Elche have collected, having also won 12 and drawn six.

With 50 goals in 29 games so far this season, Elche are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Guiniguada Apolinario, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Guiniguada Apolinario's 11.
