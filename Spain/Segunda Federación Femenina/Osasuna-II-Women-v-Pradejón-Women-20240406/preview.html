Osasuna II Women v Pradejón Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Saturday, 6 April 2024 @ 11.15am

Osasuna II Women host Pradejón Women in Saturday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

Osasuna II, who sit zero in the league after 25 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Balears Women on March 24.

They face a Pradejón side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Bizkerre Women, and who sit zero in the table.

Osasuna II are in disappointing form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With 27 goals in 25 games so far this season, the away team are the league's joint--4th-lowest scorers with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.
