CFF Albacete v Levante II Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Sunday, 28 April 2024 @ 11.00am

CFF Albacete host Levante II Women in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda Federación Femenina.

CFF Albacete, who sit zero in the league after 28 games, fell to a 2-0 away defeat to Guiniguada Apolinario Women on April 21.

They face a Levante II side who picked up a win in their last match, a 3-2 victory against Femarguín Women, and who sit 15th in the table.

With 14 goals in 28 games so far this season, CFF Albacete are the league's-15th-lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 51 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Levante II, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have also conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away side's form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to CFF Albacete's four.
