Real Unión de Tenerife Women v UD Tenerife II Women

Segunda Federación Femenina
Saturday, 17 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Two of the Segunda Federación Femenina's meanest defences go head-to-head on Saturday, when UD Tenerife II Women visit Real Unión de Tenerife Women.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Tenerife II Women to date: the away side have let in just eight goals in 20 games.

Unión de Tenerife Women have conceded 17 goals in 20 games, giving them the joint-fourth tightest back line so far this season.

Unión de Tenerife Women are in fantastic form in the Segunda Federación Femenina, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

But with five wins and a draw over that period, Tenerife II Women's form is even better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to the home team's 15.

The visitors are third in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn seven, earning 43 points.

Unión de Tenerife Women are two places behind Tenerife II Women in fifth, with 12 wins and four draws putting them on 40 points.
