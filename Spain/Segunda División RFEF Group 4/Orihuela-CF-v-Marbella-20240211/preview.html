Orihuela CF v Marbella

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Orihuela CF host Marbella on Sunday in Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on February 4, Orihuela beat CD El Palo 2-1 at home.

Marbella drew, 0-0 at home against Union Estepona CF.

With 22 goals in 21 games so far this season, Orihuela are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Marbella are also average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The away side are fourth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn five, earning 35 points.

The hosts are five places behind Marbella in ninth, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Orihuela are in good form in Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, Marbella's form is worse – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to Orihuela's 13.
