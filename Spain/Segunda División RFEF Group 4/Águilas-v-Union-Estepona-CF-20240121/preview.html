Águilas v Union Estepona CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 11.00am

Two of Segunda División RFEF Group 4's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when Águilas host Union Estepona CF.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Águilas to date: the home side have let in just seven goals in 18 games.

Union Estepona have conceded 14 goals in 18 games, giving them the joint-third tightest back line so far this season.

Águilas are third in the table after 18 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

Union Estepona are two places behind the hosts in fifth, with nine wins and three draws putting them on 30 points.

Águilas are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Águilas's eight.

Águilas's last match was on January 13, a 0-0 draw against Manchego.

Union Estepona lost 2-0 against Yeclano Deportivo last time out, on January 14.
