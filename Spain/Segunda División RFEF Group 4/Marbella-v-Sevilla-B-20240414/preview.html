Marbella v Sevilla B

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal de Marbella
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

Marbella host Sevilla B in Sunday's late match at Estadio Municipal de Marbella looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

Marbella, who sit third in the league after 30 games, fell to a 5-1 away defeat to Real Betis B on April 7.

They face a Sevilla B side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against CD San Roque de Lepe, and who sit first in the table.

With 49 goals in 30 games so far this season, Sevilla B are the league's highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Marbella are also above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Marbella and Sevilla B have played each other on eight occasions. They won two each, and they drew four times.

On average, Marbella scored 1.2 goals and Sevilla B 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 3, when Sevilla B won 2-0 at home.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to Marbella's nine.
