Antoniano v Vélez

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Vélez travel to Antoniano on early Sunday in Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Cartagena B 2-0 at home.

Antoniano also lost their last match, 3-0 against Sevilla B.

With 15 goals in 20 games so far this season, Antoniano are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Vélez, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home side are 13th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won six and drawn five, earning 23 points.

The visitors are five places ahead of Antoniano in eighth, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

Antoniano are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Vélez's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.
