Yeclano Deportivo v Antoniano

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal La Constitución
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 5.00pm

Yeclano Deportivo host Antoniano on Sunday at the Estadio Municipal La Constitución in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on March 24, Yeclano Deportivo beat Cartagena B 2-1 away, with goals from José Manuel Naranjo Molina and José Ángel Ayala Hernández.

Antoniano also won, 2-1 at home against Real Betis B on March 23, with Álvaro Rivero Sánchez scoring their goals.

With 39 goals in 28 games so far this season, Yeclano Deportivo are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Antoniano, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are second in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 15 and drawn eight, earning 53 points.

The away team are nine places behind Yeclano Deportivo in 11th, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

Yeclano Deportivo are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Antoniano's form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home side's nine.
