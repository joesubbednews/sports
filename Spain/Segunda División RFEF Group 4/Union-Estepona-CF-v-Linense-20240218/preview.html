Union Estepona CF v Linense

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Linense face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Union Estepona CF defence on Sunday.

With 24 goals in 22 games, Linense are the joint-fourth-highest scorers in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4 ahead of the 11am kick-off.

They face an Union Estepona side who have scored 22 in as many matches, but conceded only 16 goals, putting them joint-third among the league's tightest defences – only Sevilla B and Águilas have conceded fewer goals.

Union Estepona are fifth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn five, earning 35 points.

Linense are two places behind the hosts in seventh, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

Union Estepona are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Union Estepona's six.

Union Estepona's last match was on February 10, a 1-1 draw against Cádiz B.

Linense drew 1-1 with Manchego last time out, on Sunday.
