Marbella v Union Estepona CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal de Marbella
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Two of Segunda División RFEF Group 4's meanest defences go head-to-head at Estadio Municipal de Marbella on Sunday, when Marbella host Union Estepona CF.

Only two sides – Águilas FC and Sevilla II – have conceded fewer goals than Marbella to date: the home side have let in just 15 goals in 20 games.

Union Estepona have conceded the same number of goals in 20 games, giving them the joint-third tightest back line so far this season.

Marbella are fourth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

Union Estepona are one place behind the home team in fifth, with 10 wins and three draws putting them on 33 points.

Marbella are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Marbella's eight.

Marbella's last match was on January 28, a 1-1 draw against CD El Palo.

Union Estepona beat Manchego 3-0 last time out, also on January 28.
