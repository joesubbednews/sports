Racing Cartagena MM v Águilas

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Águilas travel for Sunday's early match against Racing Cartagena MM looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

Águilas, who sit third in the league after 21 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Linense on February 4.

They face a Racing Cartagena MM side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Yeclano Deportivo, and who sit 11th in the table.

Racing Cartagena MM are in bad form in Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with no wins and four draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Águilas's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With 13 goals in 21 games so far this season, Racing Cartagena MM are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.4 goals per game.

In the last three years, Racing Cartagena MM and Águilas have played each other on three occasions. Águilas won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on October 1, when they played out a 0-0 draw.
