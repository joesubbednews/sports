La Unión Atlético v Manchego

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 10.30am

Manchego travel to La Unión Atlético on early Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Antoniano 2-1 at home.

La Unión Atlético also lost their last match, 2-0 against Águilas.

With 27 goals in 31 games so far this season, Manchego are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

La Unión Atlético, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away team are 14th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won eight and drawn 12, earning 36 points.

La Unión Atlético are three places ahead of Manchego in 11th, with 12 wins and four draws putting them on 40 points.

The hosts are in good form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Manchego's form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to La Unión Atlético's 13.
