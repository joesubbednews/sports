Cádiz B v Águilas

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 11.00am

Águilas travel to Cádiz B on early Sunday in Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Union Estepona CF 1-0 at home.

Cádiz B, meanwhile, lost their last match, 1-0 against Yeclano Deportivo.

With 22 goals in 19 games so far this season, Cádiz B are scoring more than average in the league with 1.2 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Águilas, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.4 goals per game.

The away team are third in the table after 19 games, of which they have won nine and drawn seven, earning 34 points.

The hosts are eight places behind Águilas in 11th, with six wins and six draws putting them on 24 points.

Cádiz B are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Águilas's form is slightly worse – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Cádiz B's nine.
