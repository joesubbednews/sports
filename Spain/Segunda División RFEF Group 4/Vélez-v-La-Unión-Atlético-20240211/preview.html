Vélez v La Unión Atlético

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Vivar Téllez
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

La Unión Atlético come to the Estadio Vivar Téllez to play Vélez on Sunday in terrible form, having collected just one point from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn just one of their last six fixtures, while Vélez have two wins and two draws.

La Unión Atlético are 17th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won six and drawn one, earning 19 points.

Vélez are nine places ahead of la Unión Atlético in eighth, with seven wins and nine draws putting them on 30 points.

With 24 goals in 21 games so far this season, the home side are scoring more than average in the league with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Vélez's last match was on February 4, a 0-0 draw against Antoniano.

La Unión Atlético lost 3-1 against Sevilla B last time out, also on February 4.
