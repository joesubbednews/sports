CD El Palo v La Unión Atlético

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio San Ignacio
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Sunday's early match at the Estadio San Ignacio sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 17th-ranked CD El Palo host 15th-placed La Unión Atlético.

El Palo have picked up 21 points from their first 21 Segunda División RFEF Group 4 games, with five wins and six draws.

That is two points less than la Unión Atlético have collected, having won seven and drawn two.

With 17 goals in 23 games so far this season, El Palo are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

La Unión Atlético, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

El Palo are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

And also with a win and two draws over that period, la Unión Atlético's form is identical – they have both taken five points from 18.

El Palo's last match was on Sunday, a 3-1 loss against Antoniano, with Roberto Sierra Gálvez getting the goal for el Palo.

La Unión Atlético drew 0-0 with UCAM Murcia CF last time out, also on February 18.
