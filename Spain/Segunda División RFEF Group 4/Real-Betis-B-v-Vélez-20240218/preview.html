Real Betis B v Vélez

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Ciudad Deportiva Luis de Sol
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Sunday's match between Real Betis B and Vélez promises to be one for the neutrals, as two of the Segunda División RFEF Group 4's most free-scoring sides go head-to-head.

Ahead of the game at Ciudad Deportiva Luis de Sol, Betis B and Vélez sit third and joint-fourth in the goal-scoring charts, with 29 and 24 goals respectively.

Betis B are sixth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won eight and drawn 10, earning 34 points.

Vélez are two places behind the hosts in eighth, with seven wins and nine draws putting them on 30 points.

Betis B are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and four draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Betis B's 10.

In the last three years, Betis B and Vélez have played each other on three occasions. Betis B won two of them and they drew once.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

Betis B's last match was on February 11, a 1-1 draw against Sevilla B, with Yanis Senhadji Chikhaoui getting the goal for Betis B.

Vélez lost 4-0 against La Unión Atlético last time out, also on February 11.
