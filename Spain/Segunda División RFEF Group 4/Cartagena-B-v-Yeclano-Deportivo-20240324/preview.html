Cartagena B v Yeclano Deportivo

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling Cartagena B face Yeclano Deportivo on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 0-1 victory over Real Betis B on March 17, Cartagena B sit bottom of the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

They travel to play a Yeclano Deportivo side second in the standings, who lost in their last match, 1-0 against Orihuela CF.

With 37 goals in 27 games so far this season, Yeclano Deportivo are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Cartagena B, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Cartagena B and Yeclano Deportivo have played each other on seven occasions. Cartagena B won one of them, Yeclano Deportivo three, and they drew three times.

On average, Cartagena B scored 0.6 goals and Yeclano Deportivo 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when Yeclano Deportivo won 2-1 at home.

The home side are in bad form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with no wins and four draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Cartagena B's four.
