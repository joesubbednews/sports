Águilas v CD El Palo

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Águilas are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday's kick-off against CD El Palo.

Águilas, who sit fifth in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4 with 26 games played, are priced at 1.6 to seal victory.

Sitting 12 places and 19 points behind them in the table, El Palo are 6.2 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 3.4.

Águilas are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

Águilas are also below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.4 goals per game.
