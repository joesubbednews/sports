Union Estepona CF v Vélez

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Vélez travel looking to secure a first win in eight Segunda División RFEF Group 4 games against Union Estepona CF on Sunday.

Vélez have lost four and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Orihuela CF on January 21.

They face an Union Estepona side who have won two and drawn four over that time.

With 27 goals in 26 games so far this season, Union Estepona are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Vélez are also average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are sixth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn seven, earning 40 points.

The visitors are six places behind Union Estepona in 12th, with seven wins and 11 draws putting them on 32 points.

Union Estepona's last match was on March 9, a 1-0 loss against Sevilla B.

Vélez drew 1-1 with Manchego last time out, on Sunday, with Kaya Makosso Faites Prevu on the scoresheet.
