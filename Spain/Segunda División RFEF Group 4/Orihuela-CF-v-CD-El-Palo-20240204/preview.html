Orihuela CF v CD El Palo

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Orihuela CF host CD El Palo on Sunday in Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on January 28, Orihuela beat UCAM Murcia CF 2-0 away.

El Palo drew, 1-1 at home against Marbella.

El Palo are 15th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won five and drawn five, earning 20 points.

Orihuela are five places ahead of el Palo in 10th, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

With 13 goals in 20 games so far this season, the away team are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Orihuela are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, El Palo's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Orihuela's 10.
