Orihuela CF v Linense

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

Linense travel to Orihuela CF on late Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with CD El Palo 0-0 at home.

Orihuela, meanwhile, won their last match, 3-2 against Cádiz B, with their goals scored by Juan Miguel Callejón Bueno and Adrián Revilla Valcarcel.

With 35 goals in 31 games so far this season, Orihuela are scoring more than average in the league with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Linense, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have also conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home side are fifth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn eight, earning 50 points.

The visitors are five places behind Orihuela in 10th, with 10 wins and 10 draws putting them on 40 points.

Orihuela are in good form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Linense's form is much worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to the hosts' 13.
