Yeclano Deportivo v Sevilla B

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal La Constitución
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Yeclano Deportivo host Sevilla B in Sunday's match at the Estadio Municipal La Constitución looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

Yeclano Deportivo, who sit second in the league after 22 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to CD San Roque de Lepe on February 11.

They face a Sevilla B side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Real Betis B, and who sit first in the table.

With 40 goals in 22 games so far this season, Sevilla B are the league's highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 0.5 per game.

Yeclano Deportivo are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Yeclano Deportivo and Sevilla B have played each other on seven occasions. They won three each, and they drew once.

On average, Yeclano Deportivo scored 1.0 goal and Sevilla B 1.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Sevilla B won 2-0 at home.

The home team are in good form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Yeclano Deportivo's 13.
