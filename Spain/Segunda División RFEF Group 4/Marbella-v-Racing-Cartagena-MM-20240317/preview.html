Marbella v Racing Cartagena MM

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal de Marbella
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Marbella host Racing Cartagena MM in Sunday's match at Estadio Municipal de Marbella looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

Marbella, who sit third in the league after 26 games, fell to a 3-0 away defeat to Antoniano on March 10.

They face a Racing Cartagena MM side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Linense, and who sit 11th in the table.

With 15 goals in 26 games so far this season, Racing Cartagena MM are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Marbella, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Marbella's nine.
