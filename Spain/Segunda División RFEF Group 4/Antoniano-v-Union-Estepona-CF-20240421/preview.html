Antoniano v Union Estepona CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 10.00am

Antoniano host Union Estepona CF on Sunday at the Estadio Municipal in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on April 13, Antoniano beat Manchego 2-1 away, with their goal scored by Álvaro Rivero Sánchez.

Union Estepona also won, 1-0 at home against Cartagena B.

With 27 goals in 31 games so far this season, Antoniano are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 37 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Union Estepona, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The away side are sixth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn eight, earning 47 points.

The hosts are three places behind Union Estepona in ninth, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

Antoniano are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Union Estepona's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Antoniano's 12.
