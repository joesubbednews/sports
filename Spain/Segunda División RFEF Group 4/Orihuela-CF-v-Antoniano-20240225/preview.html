Orihuela CF v Antoniano

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Orihuela CF host Antoniano on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on February 18, Orihuela beat Cartagena B 1-0 away, with their goal scored by DIergo Espejo de la Concepción.

Antoniano also won, 3-1 at home against CD El Palo, with Erik Daniel Aguado de León scoring their goals.

With 18 goals in 23 games so far this season, Antoniano are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Orihuela, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The away team are 12th in the table after 23 games, of which they have won seven and drawn seven, earning 28 points.

The hosts are four places ahead of Antoniano in eighth, with nine wins and six draws putting them on 33 points.

Orihuela are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with three wins and two draws over that period, Antoniano's form is identical – they have both taken 11 points from 18.
