Linense v Manchego

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal de La Línea de la Concepción
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Linense are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday's kick-off against Manchego.

Linense, who sit seventh in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4 with 21 games played, are priced at 1.6 to seal victory at Estadio Municipal de La Línea de la Concepción.

Sitting seven places and 11 points behind them in the table, Manchego are 5.6 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 3.5.

Linense are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Manchego's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the hosts' nine.

With 14 goals in 21 games so far this season, the away team are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Linense, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Linense's last match was on February 4, a 1-0 win against Águilas.

Manchego drew 1-1 with Cádiz B last time out, on February 3.
