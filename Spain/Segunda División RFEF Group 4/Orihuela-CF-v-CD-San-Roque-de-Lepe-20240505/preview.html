Orihuela CF v CD San Roque de Lepe

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Orihuela CF host CD San Roque de Lepe on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on April 28, Orihuela drew with Racing Cartagena MM 0-0 away.

San Roque de Lepe also drew, 0-0 at home against CD El Palo.

With 36 goals in 33 games so far this season, Orihuela are scoring more than average in the league with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

San Roque de Lepe, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The hosts are fifth in the table after 33 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn nine, earning 51 points.

The away side are nine places behind Orihuela in 14th, with 10 wins and eight draws putting them on 38 points.

Orihuela are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, San Roque de Lepe's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home team's 10.
