Cádiz B v Union Estepona CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Cádiz B host Union Estepona CF on Saturday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on February 3, Cádiz B drew with Manchego 1-1 away.

Union Estepona also drew, 0-0 away at Marbella on Sunday.

With 23 goals in 21 games so far this season, Cádiz B are scoring more than average in the league with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Union Estepona, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

The home team are seven places behind Union Estepona in 12th, with six wins and eight draws putting them on 26 points.

Cádiz B are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Union Estepona's form is slightly better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to Cádiz B's seven.
