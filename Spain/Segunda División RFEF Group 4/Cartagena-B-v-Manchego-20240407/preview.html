Cartagena B v Manchego

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling Cartagena B face Manchego on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-1 victory over Águilas on March 31, Cartagena B sit bottom of the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

They travel to play a Manchego side 15th in the standings, who also won their last match, 2-1 against Orihuela CF, on March 30.

Cartagena B are in bad form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with no wins and four draws from their last six games.

With two wins and four draws over that period, Manchego's form is much better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to the home team's four.

With 22 goals in 29 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Cartagena B are also below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.
