Cartagena B v Marbella

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Relegation candidates Cartagena B face a challenge against high-flying Marbella on Sunday.

Cartagena B are rooted to the bottom of the Segunda División RFEF Group 4 table, and have picked up three wins and seven draws in their 23 games to date.

Marbella, meanwhile, are third in the standings with 39 points, having won 11 and drawn six, and are seven points behind table-toppers Sevilla B.

With 19 goals in 23 games so far this season, Cartagena B are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Marbella, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The home side are in disappointing form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Cartagena B's six.

Cartagena B's last match was on February 18, a 1-0 loss against Orihuela CF.

Marbella beat Cádiz B 3-1 last time out, also on Sunday, with Ernest Ohemeng, Hugo Rodríguez Romero and Rafael Vicente Hernández on the scoresheet.
