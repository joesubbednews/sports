La Unión Atlético v Marbella

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 10.30am

La Unión Atlético host Marbella in Sunday's early match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

La Unión Atlético, who sit 15th in the league after 27 games, fell to a 1-2 home defeat to Antoniano on March 17.

They face a Marbella side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Racing Cartagena MM, and who sit third in the table.

With 29 goals in 27 games so far this season, Marbella are scoring more than average in the league with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

La Unión Atlético, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

La Unión Atlético are in reasonable form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with three wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the home team's 12.
