Real Betis B v Águilas

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Ciudad Deportiva Luis de Sol
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 10.30am

Real Betis B face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight Águilas defence on Sunday.

With 42 goals in 31 games, Betis B are the third-highest scorers in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4 ahead of the 10.30am kick-off at Ciudad Deportiva Luis de Sol.

They face a Águilas side who have scored 23 in 31 matches, but conceded only 16 goals, putting them top of the league's tightest defences.

Betis B are fourth in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn 11, earning 50 points.

Águilas are three places behind the home team in seventh, with 11 wins and 12 draws putting them on 45 points.

Betis B are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Betis B's 10.

Betis B's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 win against Yeclano Deportivo, with Ricardo Visus getting the goal for Betis B.

Águilas beat La Unión Atlético 2-0 last time out, also on Sunday, with Joel Rydstrand on the scoresheet.
