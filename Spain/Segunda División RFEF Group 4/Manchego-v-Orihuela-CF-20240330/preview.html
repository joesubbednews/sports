Manchego v Orihuela CF

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Polideportivo Juan Carlos I
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Orihuela CF face Manchego on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

Orihuela are unbeaten in nine, with six wins and three draws, ahead of the 4pm kick-off.

They face a Manchego team who have won one and drawn seven over the same number of games.

Manchego are 16th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won six and drawn 12, earning 30 points.

Orihuela are 11 places ahead of the hosts in fifth, with 12 wins and eight draws putting them on 44 points.

With 20 goals in 28 games so far this season, Manchego are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 32 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have also conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Manchego's last match was on March 23, a 0-0 draw against CD El Palo.

Orihuela beat Águilas 2-1 last time out, also on Saturday, with Hamza Bellari on the scoresheet.
