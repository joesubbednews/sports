Águilas v Linense

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Águilas host Linense on Sunday in Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on January 28, Águilas drew with Cádiz B 0-0 away.

Linense lost, 4-1 at home against Yeclano Deportivo.

With 17 goals in 20 games so far this season, Águilas are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in seven goals at a rate of 0.3 per game.

Linense, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The hosts are third in the table after 20 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

The away team are four places behind Águilas in seventh, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Águilas are in mixed form in Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Linense's form is similar – they have both taken nine points from 18.
