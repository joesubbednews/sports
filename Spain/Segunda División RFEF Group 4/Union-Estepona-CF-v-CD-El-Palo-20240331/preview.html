Union Estepona CF v CD El Palo

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Union Estepona CF host CD El Palo on Sunday in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on March 24, Union Estepona lost to UCAM Murcia CF 1-0 away.

El Palo drew, 0-0 at home against Manchego on March 23.

El Palo are 17th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won six and drawn eight, earning 26 points.

Union Estepona are 11 places ahead of el Palo in sixth, with 12 wins and seven draws putting them on 43 points.

With 20 goals in 28 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 33 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

Union Estepona are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, El Palo's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Union Estepona's eight.
