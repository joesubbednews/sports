La Unión Atlético v Antoniano

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 10.30am

La Unión Atlético face Antoniano on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

La Unión Atlético are unbeaten in five, with two wins and three draws, ahead of the 10.30am kick-off.

They face an Antoniano team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

La Unión Atlético are 15th in the table after 26 games, of which they have won eight and drawn four, earning 28 points.

Antoniano are two places ahead of la Unión Atlético in 13th, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

With 21 goals in 26 games so far this season, the away team are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

La Unión Atlético's last match was on Sunday, a 1-1 draw against Cartagena B, with Jorge García Zamora getting the goal for la Unión Atlético.

Antoniano beat Marbella 3-0 last time out, also on Sunday, with Erik Daniel Aguado de León and Javier Rodríguez Vega on the scoresheet.
