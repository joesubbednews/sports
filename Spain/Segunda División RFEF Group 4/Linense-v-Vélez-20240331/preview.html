Linense v Vélez

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal de La Línea de la Concepción
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Linense and Vélez both come into Sunday's kick-off at Estadio Municipal de La Línea de la Concepción in terrible form, having picked up only two points each in their last six games.

The two teams have each collected two draws and four losses from their last six outings.

Vélez are 15th in the table after 28 games, of which they have won seven and drawn 11, earning 32 points.

Linense are five places ahead of the visitors in 10th, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

With 26 goals in 28 games so far this season, the home side are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding at an average rate, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Vélez, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Linense's last match was on March 23, a 2-1 loss against Sevilla B, with Francisco Carbià Barrera getting the goal for Linense.

Vélez lost 3-0 against Cádiz B last time out, on March 24.
