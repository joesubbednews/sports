Antoniano v CD El Palo

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.30am

Antoniano host CD El Palo on Sunday at the Estadio Municipal in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

In their last league match, on February 11, Antoniano drew with UCAM Murcia CF 0-0 away.

El Palo also drew, 2-2 at home against Cartagena B, with Juanma Hernández (two) scoring their goals.

El Palo are 17th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won five and drawn six, earning 21 points.

Antoniano are four places ahead of el Palo in 13th, with six wins and seven draws putting them on 25 points.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Antoniano's eight.

With 15 goals in 22 games so far this season, Antoniano are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

El Palo are also below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.
