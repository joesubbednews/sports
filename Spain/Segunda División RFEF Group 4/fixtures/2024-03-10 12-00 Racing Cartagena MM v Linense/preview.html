Racing Cartagena MM v Linense

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Racing Cartagena MM face Linense on Sunday looking to secure a first win in 11 Segunda División RFEF Group 4 games.

Racing Cartagena MM have lost three and drawn seven matches since they last earned three points – against CD El Palo on December 10.

They face a Linense side who have won three and drawn three over that time.

With 14 goals in 25 games so far this season, Racing Cartagena MM are the league's lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Linense, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have also conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The hosts are 12th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won six and drawn 11, earning 29 points.

The away side are four places ahead of Racing Cartagena MM in eighth, with nine wins and eight draws putting them on 35 points.

Racing Cartagena MM's last match was on March 3, a 3-0 loss against Cádiz B.

Linense lost 1-0 against Marbella last time out, also on Sunday.
