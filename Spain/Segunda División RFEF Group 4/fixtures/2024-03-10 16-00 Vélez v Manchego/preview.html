Vélez v Manchego

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Vivar Téllez
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Manchego travel to play Vélez at the Estadio Vivar Téllez on Sunday hoping to break their five-match run of draws in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

Manchego have earned five points from their last six games.

They face a Vélez side who have collected two points from the last possible 18, and won their last game.

Manchego are 16th in the table after 25 games, of which they have won five and drawn 10, earning 25 points.

Vélez are five places ahead of the visitors in 11th, with seven wins and 10 draws putting them on 31 points.

With 17 goals in 25 games so far this season, Manchego are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 30 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The home team, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

Vélez's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 loss against Águilas.

Manchego drew 1-1 with Sevilla B last time out, on March 2, with Pablo Kawecki Barba on the scoresheet.
