CD El Palo v Yeclano Deportivo

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio San Ignacio
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Relegation candidates CD El Palo face a challenge against high-flying Yeclano Deportivo at the Estadio San Ignacio on Sunday.

CD El Palo are 17th in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4 table, and have picked up five wins and six draws in their 25 games to date.

Yeclano Deportivo, meanwhile, are top of the standings with 49 points, having won 14 and drawn seven.

With 36 goals in 25 games so far this season, Yeclano Deportivo are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

El Palo, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

El Palo are in terrible form in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4, with no wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home team's two.

El Palo's last match was on March 3, a 2-0 loss against Real Betis B.

Yeclano Deportivo beat UCAM Murcia CF 4-2 last time out, also on Sunday, with José Manuel Naranjo Molina (two), Pablo Pérez Juan and Víctor Olmedo Sellès on the scoresheet.
