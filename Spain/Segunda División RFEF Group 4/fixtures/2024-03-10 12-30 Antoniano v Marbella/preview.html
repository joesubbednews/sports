Antoniano v Marbella

Segunda División RFEF Group 4 | Estadio Municipal
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.30am

Marbella face Antoniano on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

Marbella are unbeaten in eight, with four wins and four draws, ahead of the 11.30am kick-off.

They face an Antoniano team who have won three and drawn three over the same number of games.

With 18 goals in 25 games so far this season, Antoniano are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

Marbella, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The visitors are third in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn seven, earning 43 points.

The home side are 10 places behind Marbella in 13th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

Antoniano's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against Cartagena B.

Marbella beat Linense 1-0 last time out, also on Sunday, with Aitor Puñal Rodríguez on the scoresheet.
