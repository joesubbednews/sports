Orihuela CF v Real Betis B

Segunda División RFEF Group 4
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 5.00pm

Orihuela CF face Real Betis B on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Segunda División RFEF Group 4.

Orihuela are unbeaten in six, with four wins and two draws, ahead of the 5pm kick-off.

They face a Betis B team who have won three and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 33 goals in 25 games so far this season, Betis B are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Orihuela, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 25 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn 10, earning 40 points.

The home side are three places behind Betis B in seventh, with 10 wins and seven draws putting them on 37 points.

Orihuela's last match was on March 3, a 0-0 draw against La Unión Atlético.

Betis B beat CD El Palo 2-0 last time out, also on March 3, with Carlos Reina Luengo and Yanis Senhadji Chikhaoui on the scoresheet.
