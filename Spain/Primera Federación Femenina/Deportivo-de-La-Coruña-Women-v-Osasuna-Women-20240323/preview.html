Deportivo de La Coruña Women v Osasuna Women

Primera Federación Femenina | Ciudad Deportiva de Abegondo - Campo 2
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Deportivo de La Coruña Women host Osasuna Women in Saturday's early match at Ciudad Deportiva de Abegondo-Campo 2 looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Primera Federación Femenina.

Deportivo de La Coruña, who sit top of the league after 21 games, fell to a 6-2 away defeat to Barcelona II Women on March 16.

They face an Osasuna side who picked up a win in their last match, a 4-1 victory against Madrid II Women, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 34 goals in 21 games so far this season, Deportivo de La Coruña are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Osasuna are also above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The hosts are in good form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly worse – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Deportivo de La Coruña's 13.
