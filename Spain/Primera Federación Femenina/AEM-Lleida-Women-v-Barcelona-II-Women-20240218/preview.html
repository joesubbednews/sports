AEM Lleida Women v Barcelona II Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Two of the Primera Federación Femenina's meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when AEM Lleida Women host Barcelona II Women.

Only one side – Deportivo de La Coruña Women – has conceded fewer goals than AEM Lleida to date: the home side have let in just 12 goals in 18 games.

Barcelona II have conceded 14 goals in 18 games, giving them the fourth tightest back line so far this season.

AEM Lleida are in mixed form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With five wins and a draw over that period, Barcelona II's form is much better – they have taken 16 points from 18, compared to the home side's 10.

The visitors are third in the table after 18 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn two, earning 38 points.

AEM Lleida are two places behind Barcelona II in fifth, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

AEM Lleida's last match was on February 11, a 1-0 loss against Osasuna Women.

Barcelona II drew 1-1 with Atlético Madrid II Women last time out, also on Sunday.
