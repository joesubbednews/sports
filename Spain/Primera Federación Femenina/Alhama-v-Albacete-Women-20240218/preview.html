Alhama v Albacete Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.30pm

Alhama host Albacete Women on Sunday in the Primera Federación Femenina.

In their last league match, on February 11, Alhama drew with Cacereño Women 0-0 away.

Albacete lost, 2-1 at home against Deportivo Alavés Women on February 10, with scoring their goals.

With 29 goals in 18 games so far this season, Alhama are the league's joint-third-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Albacete, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home side are fourth in the table after 18 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn four, earning 34 points.

The visitors are four places behind Alhama in eighth, with six wins and six draws putting them on 24 points.

Alhama are in mixed form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Albacete's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' 10.
