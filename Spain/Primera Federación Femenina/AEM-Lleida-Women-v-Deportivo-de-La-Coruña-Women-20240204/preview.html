AEM Lleida Women v Deportivo de La Coruña Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Primera Federación Femenina's two meanest defences go head-to-head on Sunday, when AEM Lleida Women host Deportivo de La Coruña Women.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than AEM Lleida to date: the home side have let in just 11 goals in 16 games.

Deportivo de La Coruña have conceded the same number of goals in 16 games, giving them the joint-first tightest back line so far this season.

Deportivo de La Coruña are top of the table after 16 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn four, earning 37 points.

AEM Lleida are four places behind the visitors in fifth, with eight wins and four draws putting them on 28 points.

The home team are in reasonable form in Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and two draws over that period, Deportivo de La Coruña's form is better – they have taken 14 points from 18, compared to AEM Lleida's 11.

AEM Lleida's last match was on January 28, a 1-0 loss against Cacereño Women.

Deportivo de La Coruña beat Espanyol Women 2-1 last time out, on January 27, with on the scoresheet.
