Europa Women v Cacereño Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Sunday's early match sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 12th-ranked Europa Women host 11th-placed Cacereño Women.

Europa have picked up 17 points from their first 17 Primera Federación Femenina games, with five wins and two draws.

That is three points less than Cacereño have collected, having won six and drawn two.

Europa are in bad form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Cacereño's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the hosts' four.

With 20 goals in 20 games so far this season, Europa are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 2.1 per game.

The away side are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

Europa's last match was on March 9, a 3-1 loss against Albacete Women.

Cacereño lost 1-0 against Espanyol Women last time out, on March 10.
