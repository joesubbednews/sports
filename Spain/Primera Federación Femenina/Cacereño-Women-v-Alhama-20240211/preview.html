Cacereño Women v Alhama

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Mid-season relegation candidates Cacereño Women face a challenge against high-flying Alhama on Sunday.

Cacereño Women are 11th in the Primera Federación Femenina table, and have picked up five wins and one draw in their 17 games to date.

Alhama, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 33 points, having won 10 and drawn three, and are five points behind table-toppers Deportivo de La Coruña Women.

With 29 goals in 17 games so far this season, Alhama are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Cacereño, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 2.0 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Cacereño's nine.

Cacereño's last match was on February 3, a 2-0 loss against Atlético Madrid II Women.

Alhama beat Madrid II Women 3-2 last time out, also on February 3, with on the scoresheet.
