Logroño Women v Deportivo Alavés Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 4.00pm

Logroño Women host Deportivo Alavés Women on Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in the Primera Federación Femenina.

Logroño have picked up 12 points from their last six games, and they face a Deportivo Alavés side who drew their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 25 goals in 23 games so far this season, Deportivo Alavés are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 24 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Logroño, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are ninth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn one, earning 31 points.

The visitors are two places ahead of Logroño in seventh, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on the same number of points.

Logroño's last match was on March 30, a 2-0 win against Madrid II Women.

Deportivo Alavés drew 0-0 with Espanyol Women last time out, also on March 30.
