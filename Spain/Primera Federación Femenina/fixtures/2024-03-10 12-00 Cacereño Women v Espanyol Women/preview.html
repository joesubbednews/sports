Cacereño Women v Espanyol Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Espanyol Women travel for Sunday's early match against Cacereño Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Primera Federación Femenina.

Espanyol, who sit third in the league after 19 games, fell to a 2-1 home defeat to Logroño Women on February 17.

They face a Cacereño side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against Athletic Club II Women, and who sit 11th in the table.

With 34 goals in 19 games so far this season, Espanyol are the league's joint-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Cacereño, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.8 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Cacereño's seven.
