Logroño Women v Athletic Club II Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 10 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Athletic Club II Women travel to Logroño Women on Sunday in the Primera Federación Femenina.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a defeat in their last match, having lost to Cacereño Women 1-0 at home.

Logroño, meanwhile, won their last match, 2-1 against Espanyol Women.

Athletic Club II are 13th in the table after 19 games, of which they have won two and drawn six, earning 12 points.

Logroño are three places ahead of the away side in 10th, with seven wins and one draw putting them on 22 points.

With 14 goals in 19 games so far this season, Athletic Club II are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 31 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Logroño are in mixed form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, Athletic Club II's form is worse – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Logroño's nine.
