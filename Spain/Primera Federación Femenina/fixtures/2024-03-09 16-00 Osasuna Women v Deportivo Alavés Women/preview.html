Osasuna Women v Deportivo Alavés Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Saturday, 9 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Osasuna Women face Deportivo Alavés Women on Saturday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in the Primera Federación Femenina.

Osasuna are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face a Deportivo Alavés team who have won three and lost two over the same number of games.

With 23 goals in 19 games so far this season, Osasuna are scoring at the league's average rate with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Deportivo Alavés are also average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are sixth in the table after 19 games, of which they have won eight and drawn six, earning 30 points.

The visitors are one place behind Osasuna in seventh, with seven wins and five draws putting them on 26 points.

Osasuna's last match was on February 17, a 4-2 win against Atlético Madrid II Women.

Deportivo Alavés lost 2-0 against Deportivo de La Coruña Women last time out, also on February 17.
