Deportivo de La Coruña Women v Logroño Women

Primera Federación Femenina | Ciudad Deportiva de Abegondo - Campo 2
Saturday, 10 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Deportivo de La Coruña Women face Logroño Women on Saturday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Primera Federación Femenina.

Deportivo de La Coruña are unbeaten in 12, with seven wins and five draws, ahead of the 11am kick-off.

They face a Logroño team who have won five and drawn one over the same number of games.

Deportivo de La Coruña are top of the table after 17 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn five, earning 38 points.

Logroño are nine places behind the home side in 10th, with six wins and one draw putting them on 19 points.

With 27 goals in 17 games so far this season, Deportivo de La Coruña are scoring more than average in the league with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 11 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

The visitors are also above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Deportivo de La Coruña's last match was on Sunday, a 0-0 draw against AEM Lleida Women.

Logroño beat Albacete Women 2-0 last time out, also on Sunday.
