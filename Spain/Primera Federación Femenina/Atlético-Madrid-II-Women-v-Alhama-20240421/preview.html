Atlético Madrid II Women v Alhama

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Relegation candidates Atlético Madrid II Women face a challenge against high-flying Alhama on Sunday.

Atlético Madrid II Women are 11th in the Primera Federación Femenina table, and have picked up seven wins and six draws in their 24 games to date.

Alhama, meanwhile, are fourth in the standings with 39 points, having won 11 and drawn six, and are 13 points behind table-toppers Barcelona II Women.

With 32 goals in 24 games so far this season, Alhama are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Atlético Madrid II, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The hosts are in bad form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the away side's form is slightly better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Atlético Madrid II's four.

Atlético Madrid II's last match was on Sunday, a 2-1 loss against Europa Women.

Alhama beat AEM Lleida Women 1-0 last time out, also on Sunday.
