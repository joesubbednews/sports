Osasuna Women v Logroño Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Logroño Women travel to Osasuna Women on early Sunday in the Primera Federación Femenina.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a draw in their last match, having tied with Deportivo Alavés Women 0-0 at home.

Osasuna, meanwhile, won their last match, 5-1 against Albacete Women.

With 36 goals in 24 games so far this season, Osasuna are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Logroño, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Osasuna's 11.

Osasuna are fifth in the table after 24 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn eight, earning 38 points.

Logroño are three places behind the home side in eighth, with 10 wins and two draws putting them on 32 points.
