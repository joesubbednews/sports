Alhama v Osasuna Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 6.00pm

Alhama host Osasuna Women on Sunday in the Primera Federación Femenina.

In their last league match, on April 27, Alhama beat Deportivo Alavés Women 2-1 at home.

Osasuna also won, 3-0 away at Europa Women.

With 39 goals in 26 games so far this season, Osasuna are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Alhama are also above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The home team are fifth in the table after 26 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn six, earning 42 points.

The visitors are one place behind Alhama in sixth, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

Alhama are in mixed form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Osasuna's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.

Over the last two years, Alhama and Osasuna have played each other twice. Osasuna won both of them.

Their last meeting was on January 21, when Osasuna won 1-0 away.
