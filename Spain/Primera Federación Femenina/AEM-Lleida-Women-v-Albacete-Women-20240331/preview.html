AEM Lleida Women v Albacete Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 11.00am

AEM Lleida Women host Albacete Women on Sunday in the Primera Federación Femenina.

In their last league match, on March 23, AEM Lleida beat Espanyol Women 2-0 away.

Albacete lost, 2-0 at home against Barcelona II Women.

With 20 goals in 22 games so far this season, AEM Lleida are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Albacete, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home team are fifth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

The visitors are two places behind AEM Lleida in seventh, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.

AEM Lleida are in mixed form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Albacete's form is similar – they have both taken seven points from 18.
