Albacete Women v Osasuna Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Albacete Women host Osasuna Women on Sunday in the Primera Federación Femenina.

In their last league match, on March 31, Albacete lost to AEM Lleida Women 1-0 away.

Osasuna drew, 2-2 at home against Cacereño Women on March 30.

With 31 goals in 23 games so far this season, Osasuna are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Albacete, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home team are in mixed form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Albacete's seven.

Osasuna are sixth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won nine and drawn eight, earning 35 points.

Albacete are two places behind Osasuna in eighth, with eight wins and seven draws putting them on 31 points.
