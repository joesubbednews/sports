Espanyol Women v AEM Lleida Women

Primera Federación Femenina | Ciutat Esportiva Dani Jarque
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 10.00am

The Primera Federación Femenina's two meanest defences go head-to-head at Ciutat Esportiva Dani Jarque on Saturday, when Espanyol Women host AEM Lleida Women.

No teams have conceded fewer goals than Espanyol to date: the home side have let in just 14 goals in 21 games.

AEM Lleida have conceded the same number of goals in 21 games, giving them the joint-first tightest back line so far this season.

Espanyol are third in the table after 21 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

AEM Lleida are three places behind the hosts in sixth, with eight wins and eight draws putting them on 32 points.

Espanyol are in mixed form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and four draws over that period, the away team's form is much worse – they have taken four points from 18, compared to Espanyol's 10.

Espanyol's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 win against Alhama.

AEM Lleida drew 1-1 with Deportivo Alavés Women last time out, on Saturday.
