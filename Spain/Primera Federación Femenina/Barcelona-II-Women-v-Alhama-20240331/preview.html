Barcelona II Women v Alhama

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 11.30am

Two of the Primera Federación Femenina's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when table-topping Barcelona II Women host fourth-placed Alhama.

Barcelona II have picked up 15 wins and three draws from 22 games so far this season, and sit 12 points above the visitors going into the 11.30am match.

Alhama, meanwhile, have won 10 and drawn six, picking up 36 points.

With 44 goals in 22 games so far this season, Barcelona II are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Alhama are also above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home side are in good form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Barcelona II's 14.

Barcelona II's last match was on March 23, a 2-0 win against Albacete Women.

Alhama drew 0-0 with Athletic Club II Women last time out, on Sunday.
