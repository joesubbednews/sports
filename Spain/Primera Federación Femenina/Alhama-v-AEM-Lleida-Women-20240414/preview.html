Alhama v AEM Lleida Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 4.30pm

Alhama face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form at home against a tight AEM Lleida Women defence on Sunday.

With 31 goals in 23 games, Alhama are the joint-fourth-highest scorers in the Primera Federación Femenina ahead of the 4.30pm kick-off.

They face an AEM Lleida side who have scored 21 in 23 matches, but conceded only 14 goals, putting them top of the league's tightest defences.

Alhama are in bad form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With two wins and three draws over that period, AEM Lleida's form is much better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the home team's three.

The visitors are fourth in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn eight, earning 38 points.

Alhama are one place behind AEM Lleida in fifth, with 10 wins and six draws putting them on 36 points.

Alhama's last match was on March 31, a 4-0 loss against Barcelona II Women.

AEM Lleida beat Albacete Women 1-0 last time out, also on March 31.
