Osasuna Women v Cacereño Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 10.30am

Osasuna Women host Cacereño Women on Saturday in the Primera Federación Femenina.

In their last league match, on March 23, Osasuna drew with Deportivo de La Coruña Women 2-2 away.

Cacereño won, 2-0 at home against Deportivo Alavés Women.

With 29 goals in 22 games so far this season, Osasuna are scoring more than average in the league with 1.3 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Cacereño, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The away side are 10th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won eight and drawn two, earning 26 points.

The hosts are four places ahead of Cacereño in sixth, with nine wins and seven draws putting them on 34 points.

Osasuna are in reasonable form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Cacereño's form is slightly worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Osasuna's 11.
