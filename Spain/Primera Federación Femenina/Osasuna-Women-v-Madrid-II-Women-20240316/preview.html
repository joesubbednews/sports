Osasuna Women v Madrid II Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 10.00am

Madrid II Women make the journey to play Osasuna Women on early Saturday looking to pick up points to end their eight-game losing streak.

Madrid II's struggles have left them with no points from their last six Primera Federación Femenina matches, while their opponents have earned 11 from a possible 18.

Madrid II are bottom of the table after 20 games, of which they have won two and drawn two, earning eight points.

Osasuna are eight places ahead of the visitors in sixth, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 13 goals in 20 games so far this season, Madrid II are the league's lowest scorers with 0.7 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 39 goals at a rate of 2.0 per game.

The home team are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Osasuna's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 loss against Deportivo Alavés Women.

Madrid II lost 2-0 against Barcelona II Women last time out, on Sunday.
