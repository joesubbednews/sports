Deportivo Alavés Women v AEM Lleida Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Saturday, 16 March 2024 @ 11.30am

AEM Lleida Women travel looking to secure a first win in six Primera Federación Femenina games against Deportivo Alavés Women on Saturday.

AEM Lleida have lost two and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Madrid II Women on January 21.

They face a Deportivo Alavés side who have won three and lost two over that time.

With 17 goals in 20 games so far this season, AEM Lleida are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 13 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Deportivo Alavés, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The visitors are fifth in the table after 20 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

The home team are two places behind AEM Lleida in seventh, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

Deportivo Alavés's last match was on Saturday, a 1-0 win against Osasuna Women.

AEM Lleida drew 0-0 with Atlético Madrid II Women last time out, on Sunday.
