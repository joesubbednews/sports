Barcelona II Women v Espanyol Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 11.00am

Two of the Primera Federación Femenina's top sides face each other in Sunday's early kick-off, when table-topping Barcelona II Women host third-placed Espanyol Women.

Barcelona II have picked up 16 wins and four draws from 24 games so far this season, and sit four points above the visitors going into the 11am match.

Espanyol, meanwhile, have won 14 and drawn six, picking up 48 points.

With 48 goals in 24 games so far this season, Barcelona II are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 17 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Espanyol are also above average scorers, with 1.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

The home team are in good form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Barcelona II's 14.

Barcelona II's last match was on April 14, a 0-0 draw against Cacereño Women.

Espanyol beat Madrid II Women 5-0 last time out, on April 13.
