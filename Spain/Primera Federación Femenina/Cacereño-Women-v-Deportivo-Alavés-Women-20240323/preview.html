Cacereño Women v Deportivo Alavés Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Saturday, 23 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling Cacereño Women face Deportivo Alavés Women on Saturday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-1 victory over Europa Women on March 17, Cacereño sit 11th in the Primera Federación Femenina.

They travel to play a Deportivo Alavés side eighth in the standings, who were held in their last match, 1-1 against AEM Lleida Women, on March 16.

With 23 goals in 21 games so far this season, Cacereño are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.7 per game.

Deportivo Alavés, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The home side are in mixed form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

And also with three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is identical – they have both taken 10 points from 18.
