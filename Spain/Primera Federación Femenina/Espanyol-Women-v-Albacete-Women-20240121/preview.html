Espanyol Women v Albacete Women

Primera Federación Femenina | Ciutat Esportiva Dani Jarque
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 10.00am

Espanyol Women face Albacete Women on Sunday seeking to protect their formidable unbeaten run in Primera Federación Femenina.

Espanyol are unbeaten in eight, with five wins and three draws, ahead of the 10am kick-off.

They face an Albacete team who have won three and drawn four over the same number of games.

With 28 goals in 14 games so far this season, Espanyol are the league's highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in nine goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Albacete, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game. They have conceded 1.1 goals per game.

The hosts are second in the table after 14 games, of which they have won nine and drawn four, earning 31 points.

The away team are four places behind Espanyol in sixth, with five wins and six draws putting them on 21 points.

In the last 10 years, Espanyol and Albacete have played each other on 11 occasions. Espanyol won six of them, Albacete four, and they drew once.

On average, Espanyol scored 1.9 goals and Albacete 1.6 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 7, when Espanyol won 2-1 away.
