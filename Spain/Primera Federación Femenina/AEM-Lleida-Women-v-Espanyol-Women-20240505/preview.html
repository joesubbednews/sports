AEM Lleida Women v Espanyol Women

Primera Federación Femenina
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Espanyol Women travel for Sunday's early match against AEM Lleida Women looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in the Primera Federación Femenina.

Espanyol, who sit third in the league after 26 games, fell to a 2-0 home defeat to Atlético Madrid II Women on April 27.

They face an AEM Lleida side who secured a draw in their last match, a 0-0 tie with Logroño Women, and who sit fourth in the table.

With 22 goals in 26 games so far this season, AEM Lleida are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Espanyol, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in the Primera Federación Femenina, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to AEM Lleida's 11.

Over the last two years, AEM Lleida and Espanyol have played each other twice. AEM Lleida won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on March 23, when AEM Lleida won 2-0 away.
