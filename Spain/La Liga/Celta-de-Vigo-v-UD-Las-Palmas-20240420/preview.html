Celta de Vigo v UD Las Palmas

La Liga | Abanca-Balaídos
Saturday, 20 April 2024 @ 1.00pm

UD Las Palmas come to Abanca-Balaídos to play Celta de Vigo on Saturday lunchtime in terrible form, having collected just two points from their last six games.

The visitors have drawn two and lost four of their last six fixtures, while Celta have two wins and two draws.

In the last 10 years, Celta and Las Palmas have played each other on nine occasions. Celta won four of them, Las Palmas three, and they drew twice.

On average, Celta scored 2.4 goals and Las Palmas 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 2, when Las Palmas won 2-1 at home.

Celta are 17th in the table after 31 games, of which they have won six and drawn 10, earning 28 points.

Las Palmas are five places ahead of the home side in 12th, with 10 wins and seven draws putting them on 37 points.

With 29 goals in 31 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 35 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Celta are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

Celta's last match was on April 12, a 2-1 loss against Real Betis, with Jørgen Strand Larsen getting the goal for Celta.

Las Palmas lost 2-0 against Sevilla last time out, on Sunday.
