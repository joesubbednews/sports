Almería v Deportivo Alavés

La Liga | Power Horse Stadium – Estadio de los Juegos Mediterráneos
Friday, 26 January 2024 @ 8.00pm

Almería are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Deportivo Alavés at Power Horse Stadium Estadio de los Juegos Mediterráneos on Friday, with just two points collected from their last six games.

Almería are bottom of the table after 21 games, of which they have won none and drawn six, earning six points.

Alavés are seven places ahead of the home team in 13th, with six wins and five draws putting them on 23 points.

Almería are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 2.2 goals per game.

Almería's last match was on Sunday, a 3-2 loss against Real Madrid, with Edgar González and Largie Ramazani getting the goals for Almería.

Alavés beat Cádiz 1-0 last time out, on January 19, with Luis Rioja on the scoresheet.
