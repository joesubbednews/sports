Sevilla v Granada CF

La Liga | Estadio Ramón Sánchez Pizjuán
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 8.00pm

Struggling Granada CF face Sevilla away at the Estadio Ramón Sánchez Pizjuán on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 3-0 victory over CA Osasuna on April 28, Granada sit 19th in La Liga.

They travel to play a Sevilla side 12th in the standings, who were held in their last match, 1-1 against Real Betis.

In the last 10 years, Sevilla and Granada have played each other on 15 occasions. Sevilla won nine of them, Granada five, and they drew once.

On average, Sevilla scored 1.9 goals and Granada 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on December 19, when Sevilla won 3-0 away.

With 36 goals in 33 games so far this season, Granada are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 61 goals at a rate of 1.8 per game.

Sevilla, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in reasonable form in La Liga, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Sevilla's 11.
