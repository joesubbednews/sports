Villarreal v Celta de Vigo

La Liga | Estadio de la Cerámica
Wednesday, 20 December 2023 @ 8.30pm

Struggling Celta de Vigo face Villarreal away at Estadio de la Cerámica on Wednesday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Granada CF on Saturday, Celta de Vigo sit 18th in La Liga.

They travel to play a Villarreal side 14th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 4-1 against Real Madrid, on Sunday.

In the last 10 years, Villarreal and Celta have played each other on 19 occasions. Villarreal won 10 of them, Celta six, and they drew three times.

On average, Villarreal scored 1.9 goals and Celta de Vigo 1.2 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on April 30, when Villarreal won 3-1 at home.

With 16 goals in 17 games so far this season, Celta are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Villarreal, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.3. They have conceded 1.9 goals per game.

The hosts' Álex Baena is the league's most creative player, racking up five assists in 15 appearances so far this season.

For Celta de Vigo, Iago Aspas has set up the most goals, having laid on four assists in 17 games.

Villarreal are in bad form in La Liga, with one win and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, the away team's form is better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Villarreal's four.
