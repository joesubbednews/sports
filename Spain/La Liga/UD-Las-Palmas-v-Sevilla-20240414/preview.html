UD Las Palmas v Sevilla

La Liga | Estadio de Gran Canaria
Sunday, 14 April 2024 @ 1.00pm

UD Las Palmas are on a terrible run ahead of hosting Sevilla at Estadio de Gran Canaria on Sunday, with just two points collected from their last six games.
