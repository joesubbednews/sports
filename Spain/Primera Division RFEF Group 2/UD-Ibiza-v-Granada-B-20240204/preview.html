UD Ibiza v Granada B

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Can Misses
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Mid-season relegation candidates Granada B face a challenge away against high-flying UD Ibiza at Estadio Municipal de Can Misses on Sunday.

Granada B are rooted to the bottom of the Primera Division RFEF Group 2 table, and have picked up just two wins and five draws in their 21 games to date.

Ibiza, meanwhile, are second in the standings with 50 points, having won 15 and drawn five, and are two points behind table-toppers CD Castellón.

With 42 goals in 21 games so far this season, Ibiza are the league's second-highest scorers with 2.0 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 16 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Granada B, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in good form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is much worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to Ibiza's 14.

Ibiza's last match was on January 28, a 1-0 win against Intercity, with Serge Patrick Njoh Soko getting the goal for Ibiza.

Granada B lost 2-0 against Málaga CF last time out, on January 27.
