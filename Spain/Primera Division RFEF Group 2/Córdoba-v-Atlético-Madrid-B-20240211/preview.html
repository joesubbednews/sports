Córdoba v Atlético Madrid B

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Nuevo Arcángel
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 7.00pm

Córdoba are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday's late kick-off against Atlético Madrid B.

Córdoba, who sit fourth in Primera Division RFEF Group 2 with 22 games played, are priced at 1.5 to seal victory at the Estadio Nuevo Arcángel.

Sitting nine places and 14 points behind them in the table, Atlético Madrid B are 5.0 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 3.4.

With 36 goals in 22 games so far this season, Córdoba are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.6 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Atlético Madrid B are also above average scorers, with 1.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and four draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly worse – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to Córdoba's nine.

Córdoba's last match was on February 3, a 2-0 win against Linares Deportivo, with Adilson Mendes Martins and Alberto Toril Domingo getting the goals for Córdoba.

Atlético Madrid B drew 1-1 with CD Castellón last time out, also on February 3, with Adrián Niño Heredia on the scoresheet.
