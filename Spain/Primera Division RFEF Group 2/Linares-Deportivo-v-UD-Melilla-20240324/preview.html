Linares Deportivo v UD Melilla

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Linarejos
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling UD Melilla face Linares Deportivo away at Estadio Municipal de Linarejos on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 4-3 victory over Granada B on March 17, Melilla sit 18th in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

They travel to play a Linares Deportivo side 17th in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-1 against Algeciras CF, on March 16.

Linares Deportivo are in reasonable form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With two wins and four losses over that period, Melilla's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the home team's 12.

With 17 goals in 28 games so far this season, the visitors are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 41 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Linares Deportivo are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.
