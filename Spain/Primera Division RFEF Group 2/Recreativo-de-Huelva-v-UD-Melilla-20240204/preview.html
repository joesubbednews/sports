Recreativo de Huelva v UD Melilla

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Nuevo Colombino
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Struggling UD Melilla face Recreativo de Huelva away at the Estadio Nuevo Colombino on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 2-0 victory over Ceuta on January 28, Melilla sit 19th in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

They travel to play a Recreativo de Huelva side fifth in the standings, who lost in their last match, 2-0 against CD Alcoyano, on January 27.

In the last 10 years, Recreativo de Huelva and Melilla have played each other on nine occasions. Melilla won five of them and they drew four times.

On average, Recreativo de Huelva scored 0.2 goals and Melilla 1.1 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on September 17, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

With 12 goals in 21 games so far this season, Melilla are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Recreativo de Huelva are also below average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home team are in good form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with four wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Recreativo de Huelva's 13.
