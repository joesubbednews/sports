Ceuta v Córdoba

Primera Division RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Córdoba face Ceuta on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

Córdoba are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 11am kick-off.

They face a Ceuta team who have won two and drawn two over the same number of games.

With 40 goals in 23 games so far this season, Córdoba are the league's third-highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Ceuta, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

The visitors are third in the table after 23 games, of which they have won 13 and drawn five, earning 44 points.

The home side are four places behind Córdoba in seventh, with eight wins and nine draws putting them on 33 points.

In the last five years, Ceuta and Córdoba have played each other on five occasions. Córdoba won two of them and they drew three times.

On average, Ceuta scored 1.4 goals and Córdoba 1.8 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 12, when they played out a 3-3 draw.

Ceuta's last match was on February 11, a 2-2 draw against CD Castellón.

Córdoba beat Atlético Madrid B 4-1 last time out, also on Sunday, with Antonio Manuel Casas Marín, Cristian Carracedo García, Enrique Márquez Climent and Mohamed Bouzaidi Diouri on the scoresheet.
