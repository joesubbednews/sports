UD Melilla v Ceuta

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Álvarez Claro
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 11.00am

UD Melilla host Ceuta on Sunday at the Estadio Álvarez Claro in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

In their last league match, on January 21, Melilla lost to Algeciras CF 1-0 away.

Ceuta drew, 0-0 at home against San Fernando CD.

Melilla are 19th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won three and drawn four, earning 13 points.

Ceuta are 11 places ahead of the home side in eighth, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

With 10 goals in 20 games so far this season, Melilla are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The visitors are also below average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Melilla are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Ceuta's form is slightly worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to the hosts' seven.
