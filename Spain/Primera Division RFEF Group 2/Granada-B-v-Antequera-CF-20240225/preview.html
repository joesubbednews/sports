Granada B v Antequera CF

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Miguel Prieto Garcia
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Antequera CF are strong favourites to take all three points despite Granada B's home advantage in Sunday's match at the Estadio Miguel Prieto Garcia.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.89 on Antequera sealing the win, with the visitors sitting eighth in Primera Division RFEF Group 2 table.

Granada B, who are 20th in the league and 24 points behind Antequera, are priced at 3.75 to win. A draw is set at 3.1.

Granada B are in terrible form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with no wins and a draw from their last six games.

With two wins and a draw over that period, Antequera's form is much better – they have taken seven points from 18, compared to the home team's one.

In Luis Miguel Redondo Fernandez, the visitors have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. He has notched 10 goals in 23 appearances, to sit second in the scoring charts.

His goal rate of one every 197 minutes is quicker than that of Julio Martínez Cortés, Granada B's top scorer with a goal every 273 minutes, and a total of three goals in 14 games.

With 12 goals in 24 games so far this season, Granada B are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.6 per game.

Antequera, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last three years, Granada B and Antequera have played each other on three occasions. Antequera won one of them and they drew the other.

Their last meeting was on November 4, when Antequera won 5-2 at home.

Granada B's last match was on Sunday, a 1-0 loss against Atlético Sanluqueño CF.

Antequera beat San Fernando CD 2-1 last time out, on February 17, with Jesús Alba Ramos and Luis Miguel Gutiérrez Ramírez on the scoresheet.
