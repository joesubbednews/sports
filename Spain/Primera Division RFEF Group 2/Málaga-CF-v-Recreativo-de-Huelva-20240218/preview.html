Málaga CF v Recreativo de Huelva

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio La Rosaleda
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 11.00am

Málaga CF host Recreativo de Huelva in Sunday's early match at the Estadio La Rosaleda looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

Málaga, who sit fourth in the league after 23 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to UD Melilla on February 11.

They face a Recreativo de Huelva side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with San Fernando CD, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 34 goals in 23 games so far this season, Málaga are scoring more than average in the league with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Recreativo de Huelva, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away side's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Málaga's seven.
