Mérida AD v Atlético Madrid B

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Romano
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

Struggling Mérida AD face Atlético Madrid B at the Estadio Romano on Sunday looking to build on a win in their last league outing.

After securing all three points with a 1-0 victory over Granada B on January 14, Mérida AD sit 17th in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

They travel to play an Atlético Madrid B side 11th in the standings, who also won their last match, 5-0 against Linares Deportivo.

With 32 goals in 19 games so far this season, Atlético Madrid B are scoring more than average in the league with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 1.5 per game.

Mérida AD, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.2. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with two wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to Mérida AD's eight.
