Intercity v Córdoba

Primera Division RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 5.00pm

Córdoba travel for Sunday's late match against Intercity looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

Córdoba, who sit second in the league after 32 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to CD Alcoyano on April 13.

They face an Intercity side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with San Fernando CD, and who sit 12th in the table.

With 58 goals in 32 games so far this season, Córdoba are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 28 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Intercity, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In Carlos Marín Tomás, the visitors can rely on one of the league's safest pair of hands. He has kept 10 clean sheets in his 21 appearances this season, and only one other 'keeper – Recreativo de Huelva's Rubén Ramos González – has been able to prevent the opposition scoring on more occasions in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

In the home team's net, Manuel Herrera Yagüe has four clean sheets in 12 games. He has conceded a goal every 98 minutes, 40% more often than the 135 minutes between goals for Marín Tomás.

Intercity are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Córdoba's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Intercity's seven.
