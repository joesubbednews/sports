Intercity v Córdoba

Primera Division RFEF Group 2
Sunday, 21 April 2024 @ 7.00pm

Córdoba come to play Intercity on late Sunday in excellent form, having collected 16 points from their last six games.

The visitors have won five and drawn one of their last six fixtures, while Intercity have two wins and four losses.

With 58 goals in 31 games so far this season, Córdoba are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.9 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 27 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Intercity, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The visitors are second in the table after 31 games, of which they have won 19 and drawn seven, earning 64 points.

The home side are 10 places behind Córdoba in 12th, with 11 wins and eight draws putting them on 41 points.

In Carlos Marín Tomás, Córdoba can rely on one of the league's safest pair of hands. He has kept 10 clean sheets in his 21 appearances this season, and only one other 'keeper – Recreativo de Huelva's Rubén Ramos González – has been able to prevent the opposition scoring on more occasions in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

In Intercity's net, Manuel Herrera Yagüe has four clean sheets in 12 games. He has conceded a goal every 98 minutes, 40% more often than the 135 minutes between goals for Marín Tomás.

Intercity's last match was on April 6, a 2-1 loss against CD Castellón, with Pol Roigé Rodriguez getting the goal for Intercity.

Córdoba beat Real Madrid B 2-0 last time out, on April 7, with Alberto Toril Domingo and José Manuel Calderón Portillo on the scoresheet.
