Real Murcia v Intercity

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Nueva Condomina
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 3.00pm

Real Murcia face Intercity on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

Murcia are unbeaten in five, with two wins and three draws, ahead of the 3pm kick-off.

They face an Intercity team who have drawn two and lost three over the same number of games.

With 21 goals in 22 games so far this season, Murcia are scoring at below the league average rate with 1.0 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 21 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Intercity, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In the last five years, Murcia and Intercity have played each other on five occasions. Murcia won two of them, Intercity one, and they drew twice.

On average, Murcia scored 1.0 goal and Intercity 0.4 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 18, when they played out a 0-0 draw.

The hosts are ninth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won eight and drawn seven, earning 31 points.

The away team are two places behind Murcia in 11th, with seven wins and eight draws putting them on 29 points.

Murcia's last match was on February 3, a 3-0 win against San Fernando CD, with and José Ángel Carrillo Casamayor getting the goals for Murcia.

Intercity lost 3-0 against Atlético Sanluqueño CF last time out, on February 4.
