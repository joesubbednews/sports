Recreativo de Huelva v Córdoba

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Nuevo Colombino
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 5.00pm

Córdoba face a challenge to maintain their high-scoring form away against a tight Recreativo de Huelva defence on Sunday.

With 33 goals in 19 games, Córdoba are the third-highest scorers in Primera Division RFEF Group 2 ahead of the 5pm kick-off at the Estadio Nuevo Colombino.

They face a Recreativo de Huelva side who have scored 21 in 19 matches, but conceded only 16 goals, putting them joint-top of the league's tightest defences.

Córdoba are third in the table after 19 games, of which they have won 11 and drawn three, earning 36 points.

Recreativo de Huelva are two places behind the visitors in fifth, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

The home team are in fantastic form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, Córdoba's form is slightly worse – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Recreativo de Huelva's 15.

Recreativo de Huelva's last match was on January 14, a 2-0 win against CD Atlético Baleares, with Antonio Domínguez Sacramento getting the goals for Recreativo de Huelva.

Córdoba beat Intercity 1-0 last time out, on January 13, with Alberto Toril Domingo on the scoresheet.
