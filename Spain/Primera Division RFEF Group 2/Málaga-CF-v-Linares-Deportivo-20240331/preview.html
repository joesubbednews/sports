Málaga CF v Linares Deportivo

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio La Rosaleda
Sunday, 31 March 2024 @ 3.00pm

Málaga CF are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday's kick-off against Linares Deportivo.

Málaga, who sit fourth in Primera Division RFEF Group 2 with 29 games played, are priced at 1.5 to seal victory at the Estadio La Rosaleda.

Sitting 12 places and 25 points behind them in the table, Linares Deportivo are 6.0 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 3.7.

With 41 goals in 29 games so far this season, Málaga are scoring more than average in the league with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

Linares Deportivo, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.3 goals per game.

In Roberto Fernández Jaén, the hosts have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. He has notched 10 goals in 23 appearances, to sit second in the scoring charts.

His goal rate of one every 185 minutes is quicker than that of Antonio Luis Díaz Sánchez, the away team's top scorer with a goal every 245 minutes, and a total of five goals in 22 games.

Málaga are in good form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with four wins and two draws from their last six games.

With three wins and a draw over that period, Linares Deportivo's form is worse – they have taken 10 points from 18, compared to Málaga's 14.

Málaga's last match was on March 23, a 0-0 draw against Algeciras CF.

Linares Deportivo drew 0-0 with UD Melilla last time out, on March 24.
