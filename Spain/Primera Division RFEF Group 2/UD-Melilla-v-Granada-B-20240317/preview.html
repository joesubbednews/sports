UD Melilla v Granada B

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Álvarez Claro
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 11.00am

Sunday's early match at the Estadio Álvarez Claro sees the league's two bottom sides face each other, as UD Melilla host rock-bottom Granada B.

Melilla have picked up 19 points from their first 19 Primera Division RFEF Group 2 games, with five wins and four draws.

That is seven points more than Granada B have collected, having won two and drawn six.

Melilla are in bad form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with one win and five losses from their last six games.

But with no wins and a draw over that period, Granada B's form is even worse – they have taken one point from 18, compared to the hosts' three.

In the last 10 years, Melilla and Granada B have played each other on 13 occasions. Melilla won four of them, Granada B five, and they drew four times.

On average, Melilla scored 0.9 goals and Granada B 1.5 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on January 4, when Melilla won 1-0 away.

With 13 goals in 27 games so far this season, Melilla are the league's lowest scorers with 0.5 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 38 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

The away team are also below average scorers, with 0.5 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

Melilla's last match was on March 10, a 1-0 loss against Intercity.

Granada B lost 1-0 against Ceuta last time out, also on March 10.
