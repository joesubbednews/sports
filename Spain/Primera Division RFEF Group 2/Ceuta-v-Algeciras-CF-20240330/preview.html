Ceuta v Algeciras CF

Primera Division RFEF Group 2
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 5.00pm

Ceuta host Algeciras CF on late Saturday on the back of four consecutive wins in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

Ceuta have picked up 13 points from their last six games, and they face an Algeciras side who drew their last match, and have collected 10 points from the last possible 18.

With 36 goals in 29 games so far this season, Ceuta are scoring more than average in the league with 1.2 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 29 goals at a rate of 1.0 per game.

Algeciras, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

In Pedro López Galisteo, the home side can rely on one of the league's safest pair of hands. He has kept nine clean sheets in his 23 appearances this season in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

In the visitors' net, Luis Alberto García Pacheco has five clean sheets in 18 games. He has conceded a goal every 101 minutes, only slightly more often than the 104 minutes between goals for López Galisteo.

Ceuta are sixth in the table after 29 games, of which they have won 12 and drawn 10, earning 46 points.

Algeciras are one place behind Ceuta in seventh, with 10 wins and 12 draws putting them on 42 points.

In the last three years, Ceuta and Algeciras have played each other on three occasions. Ceuta won one of them and Algeciras the other.

Their last meeting was on October 21, when Algeciras won 1-0 at home.

Ceuta's last match was on Sunday, a 2-1 win against UD Ibiza, with Sofiane El Ftouhi getting the goals for Ceuta.

Algeciras drew 0-0 with Málaga CF last time out, on March 23.
