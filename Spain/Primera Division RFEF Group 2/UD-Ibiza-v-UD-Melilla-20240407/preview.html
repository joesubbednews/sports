UD Ibiza v UD Melilla

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Can Misses
Sunday, 7 April 2024 @ 11.00am

UD Ibiza host UD Melilla in Sunday's early match at Estadio Municipal de Can Misses looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

Ibiza, who sit third in the league after 30 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to San Fernando CD on March 31.

They face a Melilla side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Atlético Sanluqueño CF, and who sit 18th in the table.

With 18 goals in 30 games so far this season, Melilla are the league's joint-third-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 42 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Ibiza, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.6 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The home team are in bad form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with no wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and two draws over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken five points from 18, compared to Ibiza's three.

Ibiza's Alejandro Gallar Falguera is the league's most creative player, racking up six assists in 20 appearances so far this season.

For Melilla, Alejandro Martín Masogo and Ousama Siddiki have set up the most goals, having laid on one assist apiece to date.
