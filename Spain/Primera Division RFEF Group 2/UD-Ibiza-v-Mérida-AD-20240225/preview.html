UD Ibiza v Mérida AD

Primera Division RFEF Group 2 | Estadio Municipal de Can Misses
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 11.00am

UD Ibiza host Mérida AD in Sunday's early match at Estadio Municipal de Can Misses looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Primera Division RFEF Group 2.

Ibiza, who sit second in the league after 24 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to Linares Deportivo on February 18.

They face a Mérida AD side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-1 victory against CD Castellón, and who sit 16th in the table.

With 44 goals in 24 games so far this season, Ibiza are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.8 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Mérida AD, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.2 goals per game.

The home team's Alejandro Gallar Falguera is the league's most creative player, racking up six assists in 20 appearances so far this season.

For the visitors, Daniel Sandoval Fernández and Javier Martín Contreras have set up the most goals, having laid on two assists apiece to date.

Ibiza are in reasonable form in Primera Division RFEF Group 2, with three wins and two draws from their last six games.

And also with three wins and two draws over that period, Mérida AD's form is identical – they have both taken 11 points from 18.
