Gimnàstic de Tarragona v Unionistas de Salamanca CF

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Nou Estadi de Tarragona
Sunday, 24 March 2024 @ 7.00pm

Gimnàstic de Tarragona host Unionistas de Salamanca CF on Sunday at Nou Estadi de Tarragona in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

In their last league match, on March 16, Gimnàstic de Tarragona drew with UE Cornellà 0-0 away.

Unionistas de Salamanca lost, 2-1 at home against Osasuna B, with Jon Etxaniz Llamas scoring their goals.

Gimnàstic de Tarragona are second in the table after 28 games, of which they have won 14 and drawn nine, earning 51 points.

Unionistas de Salamanca are nine places behind the home team in 11th, with eight wins and 12 draws putting them on 36 points.

In Alberto Varó Lara, Gimnàstic de Tarragona can rely on one of the league's safest pair of hands. He has kept 12 clean sheets in his 20 appearances this season, and only one other 'keeper – Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa's Miguel Bañuz Antón – has been able to prevent the opposition scoring on more occasions in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

In the visitors' net, Pablo Cacharrón Blanco has nine clean sheets in 19 games. He has conceded a goal every 155 minutes, only slightly more often than the 158 minutes between goals for Varó Lara.

With 22 goals in 28 games so far this season, Unionistas de Salamanca are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Gimnàstic de Tarragona, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with two wins and three draws from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Unionistas de Salamanca's form is worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Gimnàstic de Tarragona's nine.
