Deportivo La Coruña v SD Tarazona

Primera Division RFEF Group 1
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 6.00pm

Deportivo La Coruña host SD Tarazona on late Sunday on the back of three consecutive wins in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

Deportivo La Coruña have picked up 15 points from their last six games, and they face a Tarazona side who drew their last match, and have collected eight points from the last possible 18.

With 16 goals in 22 games so far this season, Tarazona are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.7 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Deportivo La Coruña, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

The home team are fourth in the table after 22 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn eight, earning 38 points.

The visitors are 11 places behind Deportivo La Coruña in 15th, with five wins and eight draws putting them on 23 points.

Deportivo La Coruña's last match was on February 4, a 4-1 win against CF Fuenlabrada, with Antonio David Álvarez Rey, Lucas Perez, Pablo Martinez and Yeremay Hernández Cubas getting the goals for Deportivo La Coruña.

Tarazona drew 0-0 with CD Teruel last time out, on February 3.
