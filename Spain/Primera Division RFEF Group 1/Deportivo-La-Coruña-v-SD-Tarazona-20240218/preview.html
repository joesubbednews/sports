Deportivo La Coruña v SD Tarazona

Primera Division RFEF Group 1
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 6.00pm

Deportivo La Coruña are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday's late kick-off against SD Tarazona.

Deportivo La Coruña, who sit third in Primera Division RFEF Group 1 with 23 games played, are priced at 1.2 to seal victory.

Sitting 12 places and 17 points behind them in the table, Tarazona are 9.5 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 5.2.

With 33 goals in 23 games so far this season, Deportivo La Coruña are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 18 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Tarazona, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.7 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The hosts' Lucas Perez is the league's most creative player, racking up eight assists in 21 appearances so far this season.

For the away team, Manuel Martín Cocca has set up the most goals, having laid on two assists in 13 games.

Deportivo La Coruña are in fantastic form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with five wins and one loss from their last six games.

With a win and three draws over that period, Tarazona's form is much worse – they have taken six points from 18, compared to Deportivo La Coruña's 15.

Deportivo La Coruña's last match was on February 10, a 5-0 win against SD Logroñés, with Pérez Martínez (two), David Mella Boullón, Iván Martínez Gonzálvez and Raúl Toni Alcaina Fenollosa getting the goals for Deportivo La Coruña.

Tarazona drew 0-0 with UE Cornellà last time out, also on February 10.
