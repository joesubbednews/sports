Sestao River Club v Rayo Majadahonda

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Las Llanas
Saturday, 30 March 2024 @ 5.00pm

Saturday's late match at the Estadio Las Llanas sees two relegation candidates play each other, as 18th-ranked Sestao River Club host bottom of the table Rayo Majadahonda.

Sestao River Club have picked up 28 points from their first 28 Primera Division RFEF Group 1 games, with six wins and 10 draws.

That is four points more than Rayo have collected, having won four and drawn 12.

Sestao River Club are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with two wins and a draw from their last six games.

With no wins and two draws over that period, Rayo's form is worse – they have taken two points from 18, compared to the home side's seven.

With 24 goals in 29 games so far this season, the visitors are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 40 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Sestao River Club are also below average scorers, with 0.9 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Sestao River Club's last match was on Sunday, a 4-3 loss against CD Lugo, with Jon Cabo Madariaga and Leandro Martínez Ortiz getting the goals for Sestao River Club.

Rayo lost 2-0 against Deportivo La Coruña last time out, on March 23.
