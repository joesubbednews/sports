CE Sabadell v Barcelona B

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadi de la Nova Creu Alta
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 5.00pm

CE Sabadell face Barcelona B on Sunday seeking to protect their long unbeaten run in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

CE Sabadell are unbeaten in five, with three wins and two draws, ahead of the 5pm kick-off.

They face a Barcelona B team who have won two and drawn one over the same number of games.

With 30 goals in 22 games so far this season, Barcelona B are the league's joint-second-highest scorers with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

CE Sabadell are also above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.6 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, CE Sabadell and Barcelona B have played each other on 16 occasions. CE Sabadell won five of them, Barcelona B nine, and they drew twice.

On average, CE Sabadell scored 1.4 goals and Barcelona B 2.0 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on November 11, when Barcelona B won 4-1 at home.

The hosts are 16th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won six and drawn four, earning 22 points.

The away team are 10 places ahead of CE Sabadell in sixth, with 10 wins and five draws putting them on 35 points.

CE Sabadell's last match was on February 4, a 1-1 draw against Rayo Majadahonda.

Barcelona B drew 1-1 with SD Logroñés last time out, also on February 4, with Pau Victor on the scoresheet.
