Arenteiro v Rayo Majadahonda

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadio de Espiñedo
Sunday, 17 March 2024 @ 4.00pm

Arenteiro are heavy favourites to keep all three points at home in Sunday's kick-off against Rayo Majadahonda.

Arenteiro, who sit seventh in Primera Division RFEF Group 1 with 27 games played, are priced at 1.6 to seal victory at Estadio de Espiñedo.

Sitting 13 places and 16 points behind them in the table, Rayo are 5.5 to win with *Betting Company*, while the draw is at 3.5.

With 22 goals in 27 games so far this season, Rayo are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 36 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

Arenteiro, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.9 goals per game.

The home side are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with three wins and three losses from their last six games.

With no wins and three draws over that period, the visitors' form is much worse – they have taken three points from 18, compared to Arenteiro's nine.

Arenteiro's last match was on March 9, a 2-1 win against SD Tarazona, with Christian Santos getting the goals for Arenteiro.
