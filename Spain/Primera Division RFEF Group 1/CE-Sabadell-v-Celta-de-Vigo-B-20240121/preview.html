CE Sabadell v Celta de Vigo B

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadi de la Nova Creu Alta
Sunday, 21 January 2024 @ 11.00am

Celta de Vigo B travel to Estadi de la Nova Creu Alta for Sunday's early match against CE Sabadell looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

Celta, who sit second in the league after 19 games, fell to a 1-0 home defeat to Gimnàstic de Tarragona on January 14.

They face a CE Sabadell side who picked up a win in their last match, a 1-0 victory against UE Cornellà, and who sit 17th in the table.

With 33 goals in 19 games so far this season, Celta are the league's highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

CE Sabadell are also above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.7 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with three wins and a draw from their last six games.

With four wins and two losses over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 12 points from 18, compared to CE Sabadell's 10.
