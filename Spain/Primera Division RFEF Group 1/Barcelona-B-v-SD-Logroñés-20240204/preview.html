Barcelona B v SD Logroñés

Primera Division RFEF Group 1
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 11.00am

SD Logroñés make the journey to play Barcelona B on early Sunday looking to pick up points to end their four-game losing streak.

Logroñés's struggles have left them with just three points from their last six Primera Division RFEF Group 1 matches, while their opponents have earned five from a possible 18.

With 27 goals in 20 games so far this season, Barcelona B are scoring more than average in the league with 1.4 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 23 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Logroñés, meanwhile, are below average scorers, with 0.8 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.5 goals per game.

The away side are 18th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won six and drawn one, earning 19 points.

The hosts are 11 places ahead of Logroñés in seventh, with nine wins and four draws putting them on 31 points.

In the last three years, Barcelona B and Logroñés have played each other on three occasions. Barcelona B won one of them and Logroñés the other.

Their last meeting was on August 27, when Logroñés won 1-0 at home.

Barcelona B's last match was on January 21, a 2-1 win against Real Sociedad B, with Diego Pertejo Canseco getting the goals for Barcelona B.

Logroñés lost 1-0 against Rayo Majadahonda last time out, also on January 21.
