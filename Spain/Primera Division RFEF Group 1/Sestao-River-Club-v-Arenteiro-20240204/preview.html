Sestao River Club v Arenteiro

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Las Llanas
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 7.00pm

Sestao River Club host Arenteiro on Sunday at the Estadio Las Llanas in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

In their last league match, on January 21, Sestao River Club drew with Unionistas de Salamanca CF 0-0 at home.

Arenteiro won, 2-1 away at Real Unión, with Agustín Emanuel Pastoriza Cacabelos and Luis Gonzaga Delage Cruzado on the scoresheet.

Sestao River Club are 19th in the table after 20 games, of which they have won three and drawn eight, earning 17 points.

Arenteiro are 11 places ahead of the home side in eighth, with eight wins and five draws putting them on 29 points.

With 12 goals in 20 games so far this season, Sestao River Club are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 25 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The visitors, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.4 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Sestao River Club are in disappointing form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with one win and three draws from their last six games.

With three wins and three losses over that period, Arenteiro's form is better – they have taken nine points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.
