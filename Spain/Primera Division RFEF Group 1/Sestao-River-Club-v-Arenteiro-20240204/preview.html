Sestao River Club v Arenteiro

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Las Llanas
Sunday, 4 February 2024 @ 7.00pm

Sestao River Club face Arenteiro at the Estadio Las Llanas on late Sunday looking to secure a first win in five Primera Division RFEF Group 1 games.

Sestao River Club have lost one and drawn three matches since they last earned three points – against Osasuna B on December 16.

They face an Arenteiro side who have won two and drawn one over that time.

Sestao River Club are 19th in the table after 21 games, of which they have won three and drawn nine, earning 18 points.

Arenteiro are 11 places ahead of the hosts in eighth, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

With 13 goals in 21 games so far this season, Sestao River Club are the league's second-lowest scorers with 0.6 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.2 per game.

The away team, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 1.0 goal per game.

Sestao River Club's last match was on January 27, a 1-1 draw against SD Ponferradina.

Arenteiro drew 1-1 with CD Teruel last time out, also on January 27, with Agustín Emanuel Pastoriza Cacabelos on the scoresheet.
