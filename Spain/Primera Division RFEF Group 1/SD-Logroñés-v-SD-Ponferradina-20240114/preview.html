SD Logroñés v SD Ponferradina

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Nuevo Municipal Las Gaunas
Sunday, 14 January 2024 @ 3.00pm

SD Logroñés host SD Ponferradina on Sunday at the Estadio Nuevo Municipal Las Gaunas in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

In their last league match, on January 4, Logroñés lost to Osasuna B 3-0 away.

Ponferradina won, 2-1 at home against Arenteiro on January 3.

In Alejandro Díez Salomón and Pol Llonch Puyalto, Ponferradina have the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. They have notched one goal each.

Logroñés's top scorer, with one goal in one game, is Jordi Escobar Fernández.

The visitors are second in the table after 18 games, of which they have won 10 and drawn six, earning 36 points.

The home side are 13 places behind Ponferradina in 15th, with six wins and one draw putting them on 19 points.

With 17 goals in 18 games so far this season, Logroñés are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.9 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 26 goals at a rate of 1.4 per game.

Ponferradina, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

Logroñés are in disappointing form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with two wins and four losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the away team's form is much better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to the hosts' six.
