Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa v SD Ponferradina

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Municipal Reino de León
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa host SD Ponferradina in Sunday's match at the Estadio Municipal Reino de León looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa, who sit fourth in the league after 23 games, fell to a 1-0 away defeat to Arenteiro on February 10.

They face a Ponferradina side who secured a draw in their last match, a 1-1 tie with Unionistas de Salamanca CF, and who sit second in the table.

In Miguel Bañuz Antón, Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa can rely on one of the league's safest pair of hands. He has kept 12 clean sheets in his 23 appearances this season, and no 'keeper has prevented the opposition scoring more often in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

In Ponferradina's net, Andrés Tomás Prieto Albert has 10 clean sheets in 23 games. He has conceded a goal every 138 minutes, 30% more often than the 173 minutes between goals for Bañuz Antón.

With 26 goals in 23 games so far this season, the away side are scoring more than average in the league with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 15 goals at a rate of 0.7 per game.

The hosts, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 0.7 goals per game.

Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with one win and four draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, Ponferradina's form is better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa's seven.
