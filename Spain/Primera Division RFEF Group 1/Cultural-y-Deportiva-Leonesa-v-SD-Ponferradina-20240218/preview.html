Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa v SD Ponferradina

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Municipal Reino de León
Sunday, 18 February 2024 @ 4.00pm

Two of Primera Division RFEF Group 1's top sides face each other at the Estadio Municipal Reino de León in Sunday's kick-off, when third-placed Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa host table-topping SD Ponferradina.

Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa have picked up 11 wins and seven draws from 22 games so far this season, and sit three points below the visitors going into the 4pm match.

Ponferradina, meanwhile, have won 12 and drawn seven, picking up 43 points.

With 25 goals in 22 games so far this season, Ponferradina are scoring more than average in the league with 1.1 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 14 goals at a rate of 0.6 per game.

Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa, meanwhile, are average scorers, with 1.0 goal per game. They have conceded 0.6 goals per game.

The hosts are in mixed form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with two wins and four draws from their last six games.

With three wins and two draws over that period, the away team's form is slightly better – they have taken 11 points from 18, compared to Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa's 10.

Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa's last match was on February 4, a 1-1 draw against Unionistas de Salamanca CF.

Ponferradina beat Osasuna B 1-0 last time out, also on February 4, with Nil Jiménez Berral on the scoresheet.
