Rayo Majadahonda v Osasuna B

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Miniestadio Cerro del Espino
Sunday, 5 May 2024 @ 11.00am

Osasuna B travel to Rayo Majadahonda on early Sunday in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

The visitors come into the game on the back of a win in their last match, having beaten Real Unión 2-1 at home, with goals from Max Svensson Río.

Rayo, meanwhile, lost their last match, 3-1 against CD Lugo, with their goal scored by Enrique Gómez Hermoso.

Rayo are 19th in the table after 33 games, of which they have won five and drawn 13, earning 28 points.

Osasuna B are eight places ahead of the hosts in 11th, with 10 wins and 11 draws putting them on 41 points.

With 26 goals in 33 games so far this season, Rayo are the league's third-lowest scorers with 0.8 goals per game. And they are conceding more than average, letting in 44 goals at a rate of 1.3 per game.

The away side, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.1. They have conceded 1.4 goals per game.

Rayo are in disappointing form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with one win and two draws from their last six games.

With two wins and two draws over that period, Osasuna B's form is better – they have taken eight points from 18, compared to the home team's five.
