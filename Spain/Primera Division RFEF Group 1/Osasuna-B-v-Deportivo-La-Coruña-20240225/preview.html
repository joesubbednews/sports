Osasuna B v Deportivo La Coruña

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Instalaciones Deportivas de Tajonar
Sunday, 25 February 2024 @ 7.00pm

Deportivo La Coruña are strong favourites to take all three points despite Osasuna B's home advantage in Sunday's late match at Instalaciones Deportivas de Tajonar.

*Betting Company* are offering odds of 1.83 on Deportivo La Coruña sealing the win, with the visitors sitting third in Primera Division RFEF Group 1 table.

Osasuna B, who are 13th in the league and 15 points behind Deportivo La Coruña, are priced at 4.41 to win. A draw is set at 3.28.

With 37 goals in 24 games so far this season, Deportivo La Coruña are the league's second-highest scorers with 1.5 goals per game. And they are conceding fewer than average, letting in 19 goals at a rate of 0.8 per game.

Osasuna B are also above average scorers, with 1.2 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have also conceded 1.2 goals per game.

In Ander Yoldi Aizagar, the home side have one of the league's most on-form strikers so far this season. He has notched eight goals in 23 appearances, to sit second in the scoring charts.

His goal rate of one every 226 minutes is quicker than that of Lucas Perez, the visitors' top scorer with a goal every 272 minutes, and a total of seven goals in 22 games.

Osasuna B are in bad form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with no wins and four draws from their last six games.

With five wins and one loss over that period, Deportivo La Coruña's form is much better – they have taken 15 points from 18, compared to Osasuna B's four.

Osasuna B's last match was on February 17, a 1-0 loss against Arenteiro.

Deportivo La Coruña beat SD Tarazona 4-1 last time out, on February 18, with Iván Martínez Gonzálvez, Pablo Vázquez Pérez, Pérez Martínez and Raúl Toni Alcaina Fenollosa on the scoresheet.
