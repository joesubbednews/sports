CD Lugo v Osasuna B

Primera Division RFEF Group 1 | Estadio Anxo Carro
Sunday, 11 February 2024 @ 6.00pm

CD Lugo are on a poor run ahead of hosting Osasuna B at the Estadio Anxo Carro on Sunday, with just four points collected from their last six games.

Lugo have picked up one win and one draw in their last six Primera Division RFEF Group 1 games, and face an Osasuna B side whose last six games have brought one win and three draws.

With 16 goals in 22 games so far this season, Lugo are scoring at below the league average rate with 0.7 goals per game. But they are conceding fewer than average too, letting in 20 goals at a rate of 0.9 per game.

Osasuna B, meanwhile, are above average scorers, with 1.3 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have also conceded 1.3 goals per game.

The visitors are 12th in the table after 22 games, of which they have won seven and drawn seven, earning 28 points.

The home side are two places ahead of Osasuna B in 10th, with eight wins and six draws putting them on 30 points.

Lugo's last match was on February 3, a 0-0 draw against Real Unión.

Osasuna B lost 1-0 against SD Ponferradina last time out, on Sunday.
