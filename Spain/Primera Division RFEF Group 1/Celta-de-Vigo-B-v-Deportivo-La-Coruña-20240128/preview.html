Celta de Vigo B v Deportivo La Coruña

Primera Division RFEF Group 1
Sunday, 28 January 2024 @ 8.00pm

Celta de Vigo B host Deportivo La Coruña in Sunday's late match looking to bounce back from defeat last time out in Primera Division RFEF Group 1.

Celta, who sit third in the league after 20 games, fell to a 2-1 away defeat to CE Sabadell on January 21.

They face a Deportivo La Coruña side who picked up a win in their last match, a 2-0 victory against SD Ponferradina, and who sit fifth in the table.

With 34 goals in 20 games so far this season, Celta are the league's highest scorers with 1.7 goals per game. But they are conceding more than average too, letting in 22 goals at a rate of 1.1 per game.

Deportivo La Coruña are also above average scorers, with 1.1 goals per game, compared to a league average of 1.0. They have conceded 0.8 goals per game.

In the last 10 years, Celta and Deportivo La Coruña have played each other on seven occasions. They won three each, and they drew once.

On average, Celta scored 0.9 goals and Deportivo La Coruña 1.9 in those matches.

Their last meeting was on October 8, when Celta won 1-0 away.

The home side are in reasonable form in Primera Division RFEF Group 1, with four wins and two losses from their last six games.

With four wins and a draw over that period, the visitors' form is slightly better – they have taken 13 points from 18, compared to Celta's 12.
